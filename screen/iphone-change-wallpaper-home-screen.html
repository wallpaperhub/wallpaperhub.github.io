How to Change Your iPhone Home Screen Wallpaper in Simple Steps

Changing your iPhone's home screen wallpaper is a quick and easy way to personalize your device. Start by going to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. You can select from Dynamic, Stills, or Live options. Once you've picked your favorite image, tap Set, then choose whether to apply it to your Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both.
