Easy Ways to Change Your Android Lock Screen Wallpaper

Changing your Android lock screen wallpaper is a simple process that can personalize your device. Go to your phone's "Settings," select "Display," then choose "Wallpaper." From there, pick "Lock Screen" and select an image from your gallery or a stock option. Don't forget to save your changes, and enjoy a fresh look every time you unlock your phone!
