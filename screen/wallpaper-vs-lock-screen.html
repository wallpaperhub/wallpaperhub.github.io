Wallpaper vs. Lock Screen: Which One to Choose?

When choosing between wallpaper and lock screen on your device, consider their roles. Wallpaper offers aesthetic appeal when the device is active, while the lock screen provides essential information and security. Customizing both can enhance your user experience, injecting personality into your device while ensuring practical functionality.
