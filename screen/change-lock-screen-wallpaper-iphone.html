How to Change Your iPhone Lock Screen Wallpaper

Changing your lock screen wallpaper on an iPhone is simple and fun! Start by opening the Settings app, then navigate to Wallpaper and tap "Choose a New Wallpaper." You can select from dynamic, stills, or your photos. Once you find the perfect image, set it as your lock screen wallpaper by tapping "Set" and choosing "Set Lock Screen."
