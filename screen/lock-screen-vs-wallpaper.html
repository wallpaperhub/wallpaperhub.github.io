Lock Screen vs. Wallpaper: What's the Difference?

Lock screens and wallpapers are essential elements of personalizing your device. The lock screen provides security, displaying notifications and shortcuts without unlocking. In contrast, wallpaper is the background image that reflects your style and mood. Together, they enhance the user experience and make devices uniquely yours.
