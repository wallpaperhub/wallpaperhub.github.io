Customize Your Device: Different Lock Screen and Wallpaper

Yes, you can have a different lock screen and wallpaper on most devices. This feature allows you to customize your phone to suit your style and preferences. To set it up, go to your device's settings, find the display or wallpaper options, and select your desired images for both the lock screen and home screen. Enjoy a personalized experience!
