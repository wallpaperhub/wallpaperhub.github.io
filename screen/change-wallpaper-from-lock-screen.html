Change Lock Screen Wallpaper: Quick Guide

Changing the wallpaper from your lock screen can personalize your device and refresh its look. For iOS, go to Settings, tap Wallpaper, and select a new image for your lock screen. On Android, long-press the home screen, select Wallpapers, and choose your desired lock screen image. Enjoy a new look that reflects your style!
