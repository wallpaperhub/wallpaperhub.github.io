Exploring the Aesthetics of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The "Yellow Wallpaper" is a haunting tale steeped in aesthetic nuances, reflecting mental turmoil through its vivid imagery and oppressive setting. The wallpaper itself, with its chaotic patterns and sickly color, symbolizes the constraints of a woman's mind. This poignant narrative explores themes of isolation, creativity, and the struggle for autonomy.
