Customize Your iPhone's Lock Screen Wallpaper Easily

Changing your iPhone's lock screen wallpaper is a simple way to personalize your device. Start by going to the Settings app, then select Wallpaper. Tap on "Choose a New Wallpaper" and choose from dynamic, stills, or your own photos. After selecting, set it as your lock screen wallpaper to refresh your device's look!
