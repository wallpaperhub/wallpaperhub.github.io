How to Change Your Android Lock Screen Wallpaper Easily

Changing your Android lock screen wallpaper is a simple yet effective way to personalize your device. To do this, open your device's Settings, select "Wallpaper," then choose the "Lock Screen" option. Browse your gallery for a favorite photo or select from pre-installed images. Hit "Set Wallpaper" to apply the changes!
