How to Change Your Mac's Lock Screen Wallpaper Easily

Changing the lock screen wallpaper on your Mac is a simple yet refreshing customization option. Start by clicking on the Apple menu, then select "System Preferences" and choose "Desktop & Screen Saver." Here, you can select a new wallpaper for your lock screen. Choose from Apple's images or upload your favorite photo for a personal touch!
