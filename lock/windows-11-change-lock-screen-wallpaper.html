How to Change Your Windows 11 Lock Screen Wallpaper

To change your lock screen wallpaper in Windows 11, right-click on the desktop and select "Personalize." Click on "Lock screen" in the menu. Here, you can choose a picture or slideshow for your lock screen. Adjust settings for more personalization, and enjoy a fresh look every time you wake your device!
