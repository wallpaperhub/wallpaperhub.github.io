iPhone Wallpaper vs Lock Screen: What's the Difference?

iPhone wallpaper and lock screen serve distinct purposes. The wallpaper is the backdrop for your home screen, reflecting your personality and style, while the lock screen serves as a protective barrier, displaying essential information like time and notifications. Choosing the right design is crucial for both aesthetics and functionality.
