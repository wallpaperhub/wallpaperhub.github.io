Understanding the Boston Massacre: A Turning Point in American History

The Boston Massacre was a pivotal event in American history that occurred on March 5, 1770. Tensions between British soldiers and colonists escalated, leading to the soldiers firing into a crowd, killing five people. This incident fueled anti-British sentiments and became a rallying point for colonial opposition to British rule.
