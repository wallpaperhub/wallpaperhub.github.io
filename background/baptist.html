Understanding the Baptist Faith: Core Beliefs & Practices

Baptists are a diverse group of Christian denominations known for their emphasis on baptism by immersion and the autonomy of local congregations. Originating in the early 17th century, they value individual faith, religious liberty, and the authority of the Scriptures. With a focus on personal relationships with God, Baptists promote active community involvement and evangelism, showcasing a rich history of social justice and community engagement.
