Dolph Lundgren: Action Movie Icon & Martial Arts Champion

Dolph Lundgren is a Swedish actor, director, and martial artist, best known for his iconic role as Ivan Drago in "Rocky IV". With a background in engineering and a black belt in karate, he has starred in numerous action films like "The Expendables" series and "Universal Soldier". Lundgren is also a champion of fitness and health.
