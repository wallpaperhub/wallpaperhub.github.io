Understanding the New World Order: Theories & Impacts

The New World Order (NWO) concept often refers to a theoretical global governance system that transcends national boundaries. It is frequently associated with conspiracy theories involving elite power structures aiming for control. This topic has evoked discussions on global cooperation, sovereignty, and the future of international relations.
