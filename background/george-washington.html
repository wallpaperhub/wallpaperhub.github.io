George Washington: The Founding Father of America

George Washington, the first President of the United States, was a pivotal figure in the founding of the nation. Born in 1732, he led the Continental Army during the American Revolutionary War, securing independence from British rule. Washington’s leadership and principles set enduring precedents for future presidents and the nation's governance.
