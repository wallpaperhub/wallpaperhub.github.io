What is the Kentucky Derby? | A Brief Overview

The Kentucky Derby, held annually in Louisville, Kentucky, is one of the most famous horse races in the U.S. Often called "The Run for the Roses," this prestigious race takes place on the first Saturday in May. With a rich history dating back to 1875, it features three-year-old thoroughbreds racing 1.25 miles at Churchill Downs.
