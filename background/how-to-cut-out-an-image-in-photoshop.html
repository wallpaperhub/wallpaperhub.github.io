Master Image Cutting in Photoshop: A Simple Guide

To cut out an image in Photoshop, start by opening your image. Use the Quick Selection Tool or the Pen Tool to carefully select the area you want to cut. Once selected, click on the 'Layer Mask' option to isolate the subject. Refine edges using feathering or smoothing for a clean finish. Save your cutout as a PNG or JPEG for use.
