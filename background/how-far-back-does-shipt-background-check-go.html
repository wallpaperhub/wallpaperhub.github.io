Understanding Shipt Background Check Duration

Shipt background checks typically review an applicant's history for the past seven years. This includes criminal records, driving history, and other relevant information to ensure safety and reliability for customers. Understanding this timeframe is essential for potential shoppers and drivers applying for Shipt.
