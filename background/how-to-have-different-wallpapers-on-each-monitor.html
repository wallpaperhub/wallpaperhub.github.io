Customize Dual Monitors: Unique Wallpapers for Each Screen

To set different wallpapers on each monitor, right-click on your desktop and select "Display settings." Identify your monitors and pick one to customize. Click "Background," choose a different wallpaper for each screen, and apply changes. Enjoy unique backgrounds that enhance your workspace!
