Understanding Home Health Aide Disqualifications

Home health aide disqualification can occur due to various reasons, including criminal convictions, failure to pass background checks, or inadequate training. Agencies may also disqualify individuals for neglecting client care or violating health regulations. Understanding these disqualifications is essential for maintaining care quality.
