Is Running Background Checks Without Consent Legal?

Running a background check on someone without their consent can violate privacy laws in many jurisdictions. While it varies by location, it's generally illegal to conduct such checks for employment or rental purposes without permission. Always ensure you understand the legal requirements in your area before proceeding.
