Understanding the Cardiovascular System: Key Insights

The cardiovascular system, comprising the heart, blood vessels, and blood, plays a crucial role in sustaining life by circulating oxygen and nutrients throughout the body. It regulates body temperature, maintains blood pressure, and removes waste products. Understanding its functions is essential for promoting heart health and preventing diseases.
