Change Background Color to Blue-Gray for All Slides

To change the background color for all slides in your presentation to blue-gray text 2, go to the 'Design' tab, select 'Format Background,' and choose the solid fill option. Pick the blue-gray text 2 color from the palette. Finally, click 'Apply to All' to ensure all slides reflect the new background color, enhancing your presentation's overall look.
