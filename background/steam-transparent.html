Understanding Steam Transparency: A Clear Insight

Steam transparent is a phenomenon that occurs when heat energy converts water into vapor, rendering it visible as a mist. This effect is especially seen in kitchens and baths, where hot water meets cooler air. Understanding steam's transparency helps manage humidity and enhance comfort in living spaces.
