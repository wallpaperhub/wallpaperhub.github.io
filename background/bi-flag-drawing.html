How to Draw the Bisexual Pride Flag

Drawing the bisexual pride flag is a simple yet meaningful way to celebrate and express bisexuality. The flag features a bold pink stripe at the top, representing attraction to the same sex, a blue stripe at the bottom for attraction to the opposite sex, and a purple stripe in the middle, symbolizing the blending of both. Use bright colors and straight lines to capture its essence!
