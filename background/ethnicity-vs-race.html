Understanding Ethnicity vs. Race: Key Differences

Ethnicity and race are often confused but represent distinct concepts. Race typically refers to physical characteristics, such as skin color, while ethnicity encompasses cultural aspects like language, traditions, and heritage. Understanding these differences promotes respect and appreciation for diverse backgrounds, fostering a more inclusive society.
