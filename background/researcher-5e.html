Maximize Your Adventure: Understanding the Researcher in D&D 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, the Researcher is a character who excels at gathering information and solving mysteries. This background allows players to tap into vast knowledge from libraries and archives, making them invaluable in uncovering secrets. With skills in Investigation and History, they can unravel puzzles and gain insight crucial for adventures.
