Unlocking the Secrets of Astrology: A Brief History

Astrology has ancient roots, tracing back to Babylon in the 2nd millennium BCE. It merges astronomy and mysticism, seeking to interpret celestial influences on human affairs. Over centuries, various cultures, including the Greeks and Indians, refined these practices, leading to diverse systems like Western astrology and Vedic astrology. Each offers insights into personality and destiny.
