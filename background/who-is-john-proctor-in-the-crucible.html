Who Is John Proctor in "The Crucible"?

John Proctor is a central character in Arthur Miller's play "The Crucible." A farmer in Salem, he struggles with guilt and morality after an affair with Abigail Williams. Proctor becomes a tragic hero as he challenges the hysteria of the witch trials and ultimately chooses integrity over his life, seeking redemption for his past sins.
