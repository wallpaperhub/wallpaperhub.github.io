Easy Background Drawing Tips for Beginners

Background drawing can be simple and enjoyable with the right techniques. Start by choosing a basic concept or scene, such as a landscape or cityscape. Use light sketching to outline key elements, then gradually add details and colors. Remember to focus on perspective and layering to create depth, making your background captivating and visually appealing.
