Monitor Your PC: Active Processes Made Easy

To check what is running on your PC, you can use the Task Manager. Press Ctrl + Shift + Esc to open it. Here, you’ll see a list of active processes along with their CPU and memory usage. You can also access the Task Manager by right-clicking the taskbar. This helps identify resource-heavy programs for better performance.
