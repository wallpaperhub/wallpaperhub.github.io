Fun Facts About Neptune: Discover the Mysteries of the Blue Planet

Neptune, the eighth planet from the Sun, is a gas giant known for its striking blue color caused by methane in its atmosphere. It has the strongest winds in the solar system, reaching up to 1,200 mph. With 14 moons, including Triton, Neptune is fascinating for its extreme temperatures and dynamic storms.
