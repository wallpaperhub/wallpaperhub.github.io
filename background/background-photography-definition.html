Understanding Background Photography: Definition & Techniques

Background photography refers to the art of capturing images where the background plays a significant role in the composition. It creates context and enhances the subject by either complementing or contrasting it. This style is vital in storytelling through images, as a well-crafted background can evoke emotions and add depth to the visual narrative.
