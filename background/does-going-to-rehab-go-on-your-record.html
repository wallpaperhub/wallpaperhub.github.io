  • does going to rehab go on your record

Going to rehab does not typically go on your criminal record or show up on background checks, as substance abuse treatment is confidential. However, if rehab is court-ordered as part of a legal case, it may appear in those records. Always check the specific laws in your state for detailed information on privacy protections.
