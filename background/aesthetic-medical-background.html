"Understanding Aesthetic Medicine: A Brief Overview"

Aesthetic medicine combines art and science, focusing on enhancing physical appearance through non-surgical procedures. Dermatologists, plastic surgeons, and trained specialists utilize techniques like Botox, fillers, and laser therapy to rejuvenate skin and improve overall aesthetics. Continuous education in emerging trends ensures top-quality care.
