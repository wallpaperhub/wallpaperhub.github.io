Does a 5150 Show Up on Gun Background Checks?

A 5150 hold, which indicates psychiatric hospitalization in California, can impact gun ownership eligibility. When applying for a firearm background check, any mental health issues resulting from a 5150 may be disclosed. This could disqualify individuals from purchasing a gun due to regulations surrounding mental health and firearm safety laws.
