Celebrate the Mid-Autumn Festival: Traditions & Joy

The Mid-Autumn Festival, also known as the Moon Festival, is celebrated on the 15th day of the 8th lunar month. Families gather to admire the full moon, share delicious mooncakes, and enjoy festive lantern displays. This cultural celebration symbolizes reunion and gratitude, marking the harvest season with joy and togetherness.
