History of Sports Medicine: Evolution of Athletic Care

Sports medicine traces its roots back to ancient civilizations, where athletes received rudimentary medical care. Over centuries, it evolved, gaining recognition in the 20th century with the establishment of specialized sports medicine fields. Today, it encompasses injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance enhancement, integrating science and athletics.
