Does Deferred Adjudication Affect Job Opportunities?

Deferred adjudication may raise questions during the job application process, as it can be perceived as a criminal charge. However, many employers focus on skills and qualifications over past legal issues. Transparency, demonstrating personal growth, and providing references can help mitigate concerns related to deferred adjudication.
