Beverly Hills: The Birth of Glamour

Beverly Hills was founded in 1914, originally as a Spanish ranching community. It was incorporated as a city in 1914 and later developed into a luxurious, star-studded destination, famous for its upscale lifestyle and iconic attractions like Rodeo Drive. The area quickly transformed into a symbol of glamour and wealth.
