Do Gun Ranges Conduct Background Checks?

Yes, gun ranges typically perform background checks on individuals renting firearms or participating in shooting activities. This process helps ensure safety and compliance with local, state, and federal laws. However, policies may vary between ranges, so it's advisable to check with the specific location for their exact requirements.
