What Disqualifies You from Owning a Suppressor?

Various factors can disqualify you from owning a suppressor, including felony convictions, domestic violence misdemeanors, restraining orders, mental health issues, and being a prohibited person under the National Firearms Act. Additionally, individuals under 18 or non-U.S. citizens may also face restrictions on suppressor ownership.
