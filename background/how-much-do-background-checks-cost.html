Understanding Background Check Costs: What to Expect

Background check costs can vary widely depending on the depth and type of check conducted. Basic checks may range from $10 to $50, while more comprehensive investigations, which include criminal, credit, and employment histories, can cost between $100 to $300 or more. Some services may charge monthly fees for continuous monitoring.
