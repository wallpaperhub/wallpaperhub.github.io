Discover the Fascinating World of Pandas

Pandas are large, black-and-white bears native to China, primarily found in bamboo forests. Renowned for their playful nature and distinctive coloration, pandas are also symbols of wildlife conservation. Their diet consists mainly of bamboo, and while they can eat other foods, they require a massive amount to survive.
