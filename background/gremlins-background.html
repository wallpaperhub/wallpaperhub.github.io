Unveiling Gremlins: Mischievous Creatures of Folklore

Gremlins, originally conceived in World War II folklore, became widely recognized through the 1984 film. These mischievous creatures, born from an unassuming pet called a Mogwai, thrive on chaos, transforming from cute to destructive if not properly cared for. Their antics blend horror with humor, captivating audiences across generations.
