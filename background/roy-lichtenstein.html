Discover the Art of Roy Lichtenstein: Pop Art Pioneer

Roy Lichtenstein was a pioneering American Pop artist known for his vibrant, comic book-inspired works. Utilizing techniques like Ben-Day dots, he transformed popular culture into fine art, challenging traditional artistic norms. His pieces often feature bold colors and satirical themes, making him a pivotal figure in modern art history.
