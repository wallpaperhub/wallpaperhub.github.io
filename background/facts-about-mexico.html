Fascinating Facts About Mexico: Culture, Cuisine, and More

Mexico is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning landscapes. It has a diverse range of climates and ecosystems, from deserts to tropical beaches. Home to ancient civilizations like the Maya and Aztecs, Mexico boasts incredible archaeological sites. The country celebrates over 100 distinct festivals every year, reflecting its deep-rooted traditions.
