Do Dogs Enjoy Background Noise? Discover the Facts!

Dogs have varying responses to background noise. Some may find it comforting, while others might feel anxious. Sounds like music or white noise can create a soothing environment, especially during stressful situations like thunderstorms. It's important to observe your dog's behavior to determine their preferences regarding sound.
