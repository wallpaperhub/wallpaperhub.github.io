Essential Guide to Physician Background Checks

A physician background check is a critical process designed to ensure the qualifications and integrity of medical professionals. It involves verifying education, training, licensure, work history, and any malpractice or criminal records. This helps healthcare facilities maintain high standards of patient care and safety.
