Top Reasons for Failing a Background Check

Several factors can lead to failing a background check, including a criminal history, inconsistent employment records, or discrepancies in educational qualifications. Other issues may include negative references, unresolved debts, or past bankruptcies. These findings can raise red flags and impact hiring decisions.
