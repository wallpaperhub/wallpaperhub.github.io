Best Colors for Headshots: Stand Out in Photos

Choosing the right colors for headshots is key to making a powerful impression. Opt for solid colors like navy blue, charcoal gray, or earth tones that convey professionalism. Soft pastels can evoke warmth, while bright hues add energy. Avoid busy patterns, as they distract from your face, ensuring you stand out in the frame.
