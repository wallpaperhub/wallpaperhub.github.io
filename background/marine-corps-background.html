Understanding the Marine Corps: History & Impact

The United States Marine Corps (USMC) is one of the country’s elite armed services, known for its rigorous training and commitment to excellence. Founded in 1775, the Corps has played a crucial role in various conflicts, emphasizing rapid response and adaptability. Marines are renowned for their discipline and the motto "Semper Fidelis," meaning "Always Faithful."
