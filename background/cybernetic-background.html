Understanding Cybernetic Background: An Overview

Cybernetic background refers to the study of communication and control in living organisms and machines. Originating in the 1940s, this interdisciplinary field combines elements of engineering, biology, and neuroscience. It seeks to understand complex systems and develop technologies that mimic biological processes, enhancing both human capabilities and machine efficiency.
