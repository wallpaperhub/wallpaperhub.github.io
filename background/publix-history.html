Discover the Rich History of Publix Super Markets

Publix Super Markets, founded in 1930 by George W. Jenkins in Winter Haven, Florida, has a rich history of growth and community commitment. From its humble beginnings as a single store, it evolved into one of the largest grocery chains in the U.S., emphasizing quality, customer service, and employee ownership. Today, Publix operates over 1,200 locations across several states, maintaining its reputation for excellence and dedication to customers.
