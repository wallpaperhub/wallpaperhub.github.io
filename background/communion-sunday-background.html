Understanding Communion Sunday: A Christian Tradition

Communion Sunday is a sacred observance in Christian communities, commemorating the Last Supper of Jesus Christ with his disciples. It unites congregants in a shared act of worship, reflecting on sacrifice and grace. Traditionally held monthly or quarterly, this service fosters connection, remembrance, and spiritual renewal among believers.
