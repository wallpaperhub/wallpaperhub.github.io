Understanding Alabama Background Check Laws

Alabama background check laws require employers to obtain consent before conducting checks on potential hires. The Alabama Fair Credit Reporting Act ensures individuals are informed if background checks affect employment decisions. Furthermore, certain records, such as arrests not leading to convictions, may not be disclosed.
