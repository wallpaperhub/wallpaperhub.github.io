Enhance Your Excel Sheets: Add a Background Picture

Excel allows you to customize your spreadsheets by adding a background picture. This feature enhances visual appeal and helps in creating engaging presentations. To do this, go to the ‘Page Layout’ tab, select ‘Background,’ and choose your desired image. Remember, using subtle images keeps your data readable while improving aesthetics.
