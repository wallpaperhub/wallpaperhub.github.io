Good Omens: The Comedic Battle Between Heaven and Hell

Good Omens is a comedic novel co-written by Neil Gaiman and Terry Pratchett, first published in 1990. It tells the story of an angel and a demon teaming up to prevent the apocalypse. With its whimsical take on good and evil, dynamic characters, and clever satire, it has garnered a cult following, inspiring adaptations and merchandise.
