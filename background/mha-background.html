Understanding the Background of My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia (MHA) is a popular Japanese manga and anime series set in a world where most people possess superpowers known as "Quirks." The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero. Through hard work and determination, he enrolls in U.A. High School, where he trains alongside aspiring heroes to fight villains.
