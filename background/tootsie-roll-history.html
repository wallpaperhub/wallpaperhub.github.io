The Sweet Legacy of Tootsie Roll: A Brief History

Tootsie Roll, invented in 1896 by Leo Hirschfield, is one of America's oldest confections. Employed as a treat for soldiers during World War II, its chewy texture and rich chocolate flavor have made it a beloved sweet. Named after Hirschfield's daughter, Tootsie, it remains a nostalgic favorite and an iconic candy in popular culture.
