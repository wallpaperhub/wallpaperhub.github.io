Macy's Legendary Journey: From 1858 to Today

Macy's, founded in 1858 by Rowland Hussey Macy in New York City, is an iconic department store known for its innovative marketing and grand flagship store in Herald Square. Macy's played a pivotal role in establishing Thanksgiving Day parades and remains a symbol of American retail culture, evolving with the times while maintaining its rich heritage.
