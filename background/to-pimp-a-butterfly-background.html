Understanding the Background of "To Pimp a Butterfly"

Released in 2015, Kendrick Lamar's "To Pimp a Butterfly" is a groundbreaking album blending hip-hop, jazz, and funk. Inspired by African American culture and struggles, it explores themes of personal identity, systemic racism, and resilience. Lamar's powerful lyrics and innovative sound create a profound commentary that resonates with listeners.
