"Explore the World of Sonic Forces: Speed, Customization, and Adventure"

Sonic Forces, released in 2017, is a fast-paced platformer that brings together iconic characters from the Sonic franchise. Players control Sonic, alongside customizable avatars and classic favorites like Tails and Knuckles, as they battle against Dr. Eggman and a new foe, Infinite. The game features vibrant worlds and engaging mechanics that emphasize speed and agility.
