Boost Your Immune System: Essential Tips & Insights

The immune system is the body's defense mechanism against infections and diseases. Comprising cells, tissues, and organs, it works continuously to identify and eliminate pathogens like bacteria and viruses. Maintaining a balanced immune system through a nutritious diet, regular exercise, and adequate sleep is essential for overall health.
