California Gun Background Check Requirements Explained

In California, gun background check requirements stipulate that all firearm purchases must undergo a thorough background check. This involves a review of the buyer's criminal history, mental health status, and other pertinent information. Checks are conducted through the California Department of Justice to ensure public safety.
