Discover the Heartwarming History of Teddy Bears

The teddy bear, a beloved companion for children and adults alike, has a rich history dating back to the early 20th century. Initially inspired by a hunting trip gone awry, these plush toys symbolize comfort and nostalgia. With diverse designs, they bring joy and warmth, making them a timeless gift for loved ones.
