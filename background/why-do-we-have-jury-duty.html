Understanding Jury Duty: Your Civic Responsibility

Jury duty is a civic responsibility that ensures a fair legal system. It allows citizens to participate in the justice process, providing a diverse perspective in trials. By serving, individuals help uphold the right to a jury of peers, support accountability, and contribute to community decision-making in legal matters.
