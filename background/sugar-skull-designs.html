Stunning Sugar Skull Designs for Día de los Muertos

Sugar skull designs, or "calaveras," are vibrant, artistic representations of skulls popular in Mexican culture, especially during Día de los Muertos. Characterized by colorful patterns, intricate details, and symbolic motifs, these designs celebrate life and honor departed loved ones. From face paint to art, their beauty lies in their cultural significance.
