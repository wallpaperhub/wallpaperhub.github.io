Essential Guide to Background Checks for Gun Buyers in Georgia

In Georgia, anyone looking to purchase a gun must undergo a background check conducted through the National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS). This process ensures that buyers do not have felony convictions, domestic violence charges, or mental health issues disqualifying them from firearm ownership.
