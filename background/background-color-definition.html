Understanding Background Color: A Quick Guide

Background color refers to the color that fills the behind elements on a webpage or graphic, providing depth and context. It's crucial for visual appeal and branding, as it can influence user experience, readability, and emotional response. Designers select background colors to enhance content engagement and highlight key areas.
