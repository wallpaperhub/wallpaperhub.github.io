Holy Spirit: The Symbolism of Fire and Dove

The Holy Spirit is often depicted as a dove, symbolizing peace, purity, and divine presence. The imagery of fire represents the Spirit's power, illuminating faith and igniting passion within believers. Together, the dove and fire symbolize a transformative experience, embodying God's love and guidance in our spiritual journeys.
