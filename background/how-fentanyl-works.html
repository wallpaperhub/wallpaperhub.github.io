Understanding How Fentanyl Works: A Comprehensive Guide

Fentanyl is a synthetic opioid that works by binding to the body's opioid receptors, mainly in the brain and nervous system. This interaction blocks pain signals and produces intense feelings of euphoria. Fentanyl is much more potent than morphine, making it highly effective for severe pain management, but also increasing the risk of addiction and overdose.
