How to Easily Remove Your Zoom Background

Removing Zoom backgrounds can enhance your virtual meetings. To do this, go to your Zoom settings, select "Background & Filters," and choose “None” to eliminate the current background. You can also clear any applied image or video backgrounds, allowing your actual surroundings to take the spotlight. Perfect for a more professional look!
