Explore the Roots & Rhythm of Blues Music

Blues music originated in the African American communities of the Deep South in the late 19th century. It combines elements of spirituals, work songs, and folk music, characterized by its soulful melodies and expressive lyrics. Influential artists like B.B. King and Muddy Waters helped popularize the genre, making it a cornerstone of modern music.
