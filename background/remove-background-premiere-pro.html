Effortlessly Remove Background in Premiere Pro

To remove the background in Premiere Pro, use the 'Ultra Key' effect found in the Effects panel. Simply apply it to your clip, select the key color using the eyedropper tool, and adjust settings like 'Matte Generation' and 'Spill Suppression' to refine your result. This technique is ideal for green screens and enhancing video aesthetics.
