Where Sitka Gear Began: A Glimpse into Its Origins

Sitka Gear was founded in 2005 in Bozeman, Montana. The company was established with a mission to create high-quality technical hunting apparel that meets the demands of serious hunters. Inspired by the state’s rugged landscape and changing weather conditions, Sitka Gear focuses on innovative designs and materials, ensuring functionality and durability.
