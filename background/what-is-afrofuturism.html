Understanding Afrofuturism: A Cultural Movement

Afrofuturism is a cultural movement that blends elements of science fiction, history, and African mythology. It envisions a future shaped by Black experiences, uplifting African heritage and exploring possibilities in technology and society. Through art, literature, and music, it creates a narrative that reimagines identity and fosters empowerment.
