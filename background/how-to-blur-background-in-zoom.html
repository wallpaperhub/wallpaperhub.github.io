How to Easily Blur Your Background in Zoom

To blur the background in Zoom, start by joining a meeting. Click on the upward arrow next to the "Stop Video" button and select "Choose Virtual Background." In the settings, choose the "Blur" option under backgrounds. This helps maintain privacy while keeping the focus on you during your virtual meetings.
