Apache Helicopter: A Powerful Flying Warrior

The Apache helicopter, officially known as the AH-64 Apache, is an American attack helicopter developed by McDonnell Douglas (now Boeing) in the late 1970s. Renowned for its agility and advanced technology, it features a tandem cockpit, powerful weapon systems, and sophisticated avionics, making it ideal for anti-tank missions and close air support.
