Cummins History: From Small Workshop to Global Leader

Cummins Inc. was founded in 1919 by Clessie Cummins and is renowned for its diesel engines and power solutions. Initially, the company focused on innovative engine designs, which revolutionized transportation. Over the decades, Cummins expanded globally, embracing sustainability, technology, and diverse power systems, solidifying its position in the industry.
