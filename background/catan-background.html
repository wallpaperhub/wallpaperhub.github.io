Discover the Exciting Background of Catan

Settlers of Catan, designed by Klaus Teuber, debuted in 1995 and quickly gained popularity. Set on the fictional island of Catan, players gather resources to build settlements and cities while trading with each other. Its unique blend of strategy, negotiation, and unpredictable gameplay makes it a staple in board gaming culture.
