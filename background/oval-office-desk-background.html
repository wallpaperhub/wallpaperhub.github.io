"Exploring the Iconic Oval Office Desk: A Symbol of Leadership"

The Oval Office desk, known as the Resolute Desk, is a symbol of leadership and power. Crafted from the timbers of the British Arctic ship HMS Resolute, this iconic piece has been used by numerous U.S. presidents since 1880. Its unique features, including its historical artifacts and presidential seals, reflect the grandeur of the office.
