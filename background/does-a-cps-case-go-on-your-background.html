CPS Cases and Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Yes, a Child Protective Services (CPS) case can appear on your background check, especially if it leads to legal action or if it's classified as substantiated. This can impact employment, housing, and even volunteer opportunities, especially in fields involving children. Always seek legal advice for specific situations.
