Understanding Nick Carraway: Key Character Traits in The Great Gatsby

Nick Carraway, the narrator of "The Great Gatsby," is marked by several distinct traits. He is observant, providing keen insights into the behaviors of those around him. His honesty and integrity set him apart in a corrupt society. Despite being reserved, Nick is empathetic, offering a deep understanding of the characters, especially Gatsby.
