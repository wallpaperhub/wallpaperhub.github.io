Unleashing the Mystery of Hellhounds

Hellhounds are mythical creatures often depicted as supernatural, black dogs associated with death and the underworld. In folklore, they serve as guardians of the gates to the afterlife, instilling fear and awe. Their blazing eyes and fierce demeanor make them symbolic of lost souls and punishment, appearing in various cultures and stories.
