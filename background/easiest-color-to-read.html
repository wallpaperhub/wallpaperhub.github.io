Best Readable Colors for Text: Maximize Clarity

The easiest color to read is often considered to be black text on a white background. This combination provides the highest contrast, making it visually accessible for most people. Other combinations like dark blue on light beige or yellow on black also work well, but it’s crucial to maintain sufficient contrast for clarity.
