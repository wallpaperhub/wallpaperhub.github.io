Federal Background Check: Essential Guide

A federal background check is a comprehensive review of an individual's criminal history, typically conducted by employers, licensing agencies, or organizations. This check involves searching databases managed by the FBI and other federal agencies to ensure safety and compliance. It's crucial for making informed hiring decisions in sensitive positions.
