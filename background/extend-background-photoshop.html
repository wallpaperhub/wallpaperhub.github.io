How to Easily Extend Backgrounds in Photoshop

Extending the background in Photoshop can enhance your images effortlessly. Start by selecting the area you want to extend using the Lasso or Marquee tool. Then, use Content-Aware Fill or clone tools for a seamless blend. Adjust layers and use the healing brush for perfect results. Practice makes perfect!
