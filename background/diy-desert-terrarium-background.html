DIY Desert Terrarium Background Ideas

Creating a DIY desert terrarium background is a fun and creative way to showcase your miniature desert ecosystem. Start with a sturdy glass container, then layer sand, rocks, and small succulents. Add decorative elements like small figurines or driftwood for an eye-catching effect. Ensure proper drainage with a layer of gravel and enjoy your personalized desert landscape!
