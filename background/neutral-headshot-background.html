"The Importance of a Neutral Headshot Background"

A neutral headshot background is essential for professional photography, helping to keep the focus on the subject. Ideal choices include solid colors like white, gray, or beige. These understated tones create a clean, distraction-free environment, perfect for business profiles, social media, or portfolios, emphasizing your features and attire.
