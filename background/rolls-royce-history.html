Discover the Rich History of Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce, founded in 1904, began as a luxury automobile manufacturer in the UK, known for its engineering excellence and iconic vehicles. In 1914, they expanded into aircraft engines, becoming a key player in aviation. Today, Rolls-Royce is recognized for its innovations in both automotive and aerospace sectors, maintaining a legacy of luxury and performance.
