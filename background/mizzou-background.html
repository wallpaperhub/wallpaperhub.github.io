Explore the Rich Heritage of Mizzou University

Mizzou, or the University of Missouri, is a renowned public research university located in Columbia, Missouri. Founded in 1839, it is the first university in the United States established west of the Mississippi River. Mizzou offers a vibrant campus life, diverse academic programs, and is known for its innovative research. The tiger mascot symbolizes school spirit and pride.
