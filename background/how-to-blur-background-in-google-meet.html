Blur Background in Google Meet: A Quick Guide

To blur the background in Google Meet, start a meeting and navigate to the bottom right corner. Click on the three vertical dots for more options, then select "Change background." Choose the blur option to obscure your surroundings, keeping the focus on you during the meeting. This feature enhances privacy and professionalism.
