The Majestic History of Lions: Kings of the Savanna

Lions, often dubbed the "king of the jungle," actually thrive in savannas and grasslands. Renowned for their majestic manes and social pride structures, lions live in groups that foster cooperation during hunting. Threatened by habitat loss and poaching, these magnificent big cats symbolize strength and courage, captivating our imaginations for centuries.
