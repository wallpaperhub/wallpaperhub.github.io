Do Traffic Violations Affect Your Driving Record?

Traffic violations typically get recorded on your driving record, affecting your insurance premiums and potentially leading to further penalties. Minor violations may stay on your record for a few years, while serious offenses could persist longer. Understanding how these violations impact you is crucial for safe driving.
