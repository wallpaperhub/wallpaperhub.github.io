Delicious Fried Chicken: A Culinary History

Fried chicken, a beloved dish, has roots in Southern US cuisine, influenced by African, Native American, and European cooking traditions. Its crispy coating, usually made from seasoned flour or batter, envelops juicy, tender meat. Originating as a special occasion meal, fried chicken is now a staple enjoyed worldwide, often served with sides like coleslaw and biscuits.
