The Transformative 1910s: War, Suffrage, and Innovation

The 1910s were a transformative decade marked by dramatic social, political, and technological changes. World War I reshaped nations, while movements for women's suffrage gained momentum. The era also saw the rise of jazz and modernist art, reflecting the innovative spirit of the time. A blend of chaos and creativity defined this pivotal period in history.
