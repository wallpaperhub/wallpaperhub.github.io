Unraveling the Rich History of Marathons

Marathon history dates back to ancient Greece, originating from the legendary run of Pheidippides in 490 BC, who carried news of victory over the Persians. The marathon was reintroduced in the modern Olympics in 1896. Over the years, it has evolved into a global sporting event, celebrated for endurance, spirit, and community.
