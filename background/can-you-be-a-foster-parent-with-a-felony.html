Can You Foster a Child with a Felony Record?

Yes, you can be a foster parent with a felony, but it often depends on the specific nature of the offense and the laws in your state. Many states conduct background checks and consider factors like the time elapsed since the felony and rehabilitation efforts. It's essential to check with your local foster care agency for specific guidelines.
