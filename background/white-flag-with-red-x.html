Understanding the Symbolism of the White Flag with Red X

The white flag with a red X is often used as a symbol of truce or surrender. Its bold contrast signifies a call for peace, marking a neutral position in conflicts. This emblem can be spotted in various contexts, from sports to historical battlefields, representing cooperation and the end of hostilities in a visually striking way.
