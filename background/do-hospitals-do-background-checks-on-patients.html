Do Hospitals Conduct Background Checks on Patients?

Hospitals typically do not conduct background checks on patients in the same way they do for employees. Instead, they focus on obtaining medical histories, consent for treatment, and verifying insurance details. The primary concern is patient health and safety, rather than criminal or personal background information.
