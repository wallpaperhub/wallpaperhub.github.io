Optimal Lighting for Chroma Key Backgrounds

When lighting a chroma key background, the most appropriate statement is to use even, diffused lighting without shadows. This ensures consistent color across the entire background, which is crucial for effective keying. Avoid hotspots and use softboxes or diffusers for best results.
