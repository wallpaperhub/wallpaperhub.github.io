Switzerland Flag: Symbol of Neutrality and Peace

The Switzerland flag features a bold red background with a white cross at its center, symbolizing neutrality and peace. This iconic design, adopted in 1889, represents the country's commitment to humanitarian values. The simplicity and striking contrast make it one of the oldest national flags still in use today.
