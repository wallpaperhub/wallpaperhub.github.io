Understanding 4473 Background Check Time: What You Need to Know

The 4473 background check time can vary significantly based on several factors, including the volume of requests received by the FBI and the applicant's personal history. Typically, checks are completed within minutes, but delays can occur, extending the process to several hours or even days in some cases. It's essential for applicants to allow for potential waiting times when planning to purchase firearms.
