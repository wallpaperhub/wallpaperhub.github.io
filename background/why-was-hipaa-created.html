Understanding the Purpose of HIPAA: Privacy in Healthcare

HIPAA, the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, was created to enhance patient privacy and secure healthcare data. Enacted in 1996, it aimed to streamline healthcare transactions, protect personal health information, and establish standards for electronic health records. This legislation safeguards patient rights and promotes trust in healthcare systems.
