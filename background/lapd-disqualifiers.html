LAPD Disqualifiers: Know What to Avoid

When applying for a position with the LAPD, certain disqualifiers can impact your eligibility. Common disqualifiers include felony convictions, serious misdemeanors, drug use, inconsistent employment history, and failure to meet physical fitness standards. Understanding these factors can help potential applicants prepare effectively.
