Change Background Transparency in PowerPoint: A Quick Guide

To change the background transparency in PowerPoint, right-click on the background image and select "Format Background." In the panel, choose "Fill" and then "Picture or texture fill." Adjust the "Transparency" slider to make your background more or less transparent. This is perfect for blending images with text or other elements.
