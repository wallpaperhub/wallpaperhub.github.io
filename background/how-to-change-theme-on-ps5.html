How to Change Your PS5 Theme in Minutes

To change the theme on your PS5, navigate to the Settings menu from the home screen. Scroll to "Themes," and select "Themes" again. Here, you'll find pre-installed themes and can access the PlayStation Store for more options. Choose your desired theme and press "Apply" to see the changes instantly!
