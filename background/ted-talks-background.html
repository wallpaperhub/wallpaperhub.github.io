The Inspiring Background of TED Talks

TED Talks revolutionized public speaking and communication by showcasing diverse ideas from various fields. Founded in 1984 as a conference about Technology, Entertainment, and Design, TED has evolved into a global platform where thought leaders share inspiration through powerful storytelling. Each talk aims to inspire and educate viewers worldwide.
