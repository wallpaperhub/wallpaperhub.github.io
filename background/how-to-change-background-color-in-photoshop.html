Change Background Color in Photoshop: A Quick Guide

To change the background color in Photoshop, first, select the layer you want to modify. Use the 'Magic Wand' or 'Quick Selection' tool to select the background area. Then, choose 'Edit' > 'Fill' and select your preferred color, or use the 'Paint Bucket' tool. Adjust opacity as needed and save your work for the final effect!
