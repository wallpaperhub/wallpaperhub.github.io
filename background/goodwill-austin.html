Shop & Support: Goodwill Austin's Mission

Goodwill Austin is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing employment opportunities and job training for individuals in the community. They offer a variety of services such as thrift stores, vocational rehabilitation, and skills training. By shopping or donating, you help support their mission of empowering people to achieve self-sufficiency.
