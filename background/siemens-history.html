Discover Siemens: A Rich History of Innovation

Siemens, founded in 1847 by Werner von Siemens and Johann Georg Halske, began as a telegraph company. Over the years, it evolved into a global technology powerhouse, innovating in electronics, automation, and transportation. With a rich legacy of engineering and sustainability, Siemens continues to shape industries and enhance lives worldwide.
