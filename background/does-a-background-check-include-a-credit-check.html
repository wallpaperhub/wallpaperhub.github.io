Does a Background Check Include a Credit Check?

A background check can include a credit check, but it depends on the purpose and the employer's policies. Typically, credit checks are conducted for positions that involve financial responsibilities. Candidates should be informed if a credit review is part of the background check and have the right to dispute any inaccuracies.
