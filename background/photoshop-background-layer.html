Mastering Photoshop Background Layers Made Easy

In Photoshop, the background layer is the foundation of your project. Unlike other layers, the background is locked by default, which prevents transparency and ensures your artwork is always visible. Understanding how to manipulate the background layer is essential for effective editing and design, giving you more creative control over your images.
