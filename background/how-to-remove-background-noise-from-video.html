Eliminate Background Noise in Video Easily

To remove background noise from video effectively, use audio editing software like Audacity or Adobe Premiere Pro. Import your video, isolate the audio track, and apply a noise reduction effect. Adjust parameters until the unwanted sounds are minimized. Finally, export your video with clear audio for a better viewing experience.
