Discovering the Beauty of White Stars

White stars are fascinating celestial bodies that emit a bright, pure light. Often associated with the main sequence of stellar evolution, these stars primarily burn hydrogen. Their surface temperatures range from 7,500 to 10,000 degrees Celsius, resulting in their distinctive color. Astronomers study white stars to understand the universe's structure and evolution.
