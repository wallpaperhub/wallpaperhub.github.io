Does DoorDash Search for Orders in the Background?

Yes, DoorDash operates in the background to continually seek orders. When a Dasher is online, the app syncs and refreshes in real-time, ensuring that they receive notifications for available deliveries. This constant searching helps Dashers maximize their earnings by quickly accepting new orders as they arise.
