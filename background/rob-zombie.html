Rob Zombie is an iconic figure in the world of horror and heavy metal. Originally the frontman of White Zombie, his transition to directing produced cult classics like "House of 1000 Corpses" and "The Devil's Rejects." With a unique blend of music and cinematic style, Zombie continues to influence the horror genre.
