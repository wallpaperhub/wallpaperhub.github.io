Discover the Spice: A Brief History of Chili

Chili, a staple in global cuisine, traces its origins to Central and South America, where it has been cultivated for thousands of years. This vibrant fruit, packed with capsaicin, adds spice and flavor to dishes worldwide. Its varieties range from mild bell peppers to fiery ghost peppers, each with unique characteristics and culinary uses.
