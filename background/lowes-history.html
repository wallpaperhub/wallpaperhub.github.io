Lowe's History: From Local Hardware Store to Home Improvement Giant

Founded in 1946 by Lucius Smith Lowe in North Wilkesboro, North Carolina, Lowe's began as a small hardware store. Over the decades, it expanded into a major home improvement retailer. By 1985, Lowe's was publicly traded, and today, it operates over 2,000 stores across the U.S., serving millions with quality products and services.
