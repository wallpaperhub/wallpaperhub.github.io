Empowering Erin Brockovich: The Woman Behind the Fight for Clean Water

Erin Brockovich is a celebrated environmental activist known for her relentless pursuit of justice against Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E) over water contamination in Hinkley, California. Her efforts, portrayed in the 2000 film starring Julia Roberts, brought awareness to toxic exposure and underscored the power of grassroots activism.
