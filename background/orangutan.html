Discover the Amazing Orangutan: Facts & Conservation

Orangutans are fascinating and intelligent primates native to the rainforests of Borneo and Sumatra. Known for their striking red fur and long arms, they spend most of their lives in trees, using tools and exhibiting complex social behaviors. Threatened by habitat loss and poaching, orangutans are vital to forest ecosystems.
