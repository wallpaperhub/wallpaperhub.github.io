Understanding Ripple: The Future of Digital Payments

Ripple is a digital payment protocol and cryptocurrency enabling fast, cross-border transactions. Launched in 2012, it aims to simplify international payments by using blockchain technology. Ripple's network facilitates real-time currency exchanges, offering lower transaction fees and enhanced security, making it a popular choice among financial institutions.
