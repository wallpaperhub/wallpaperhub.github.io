How Old is the Great Barrier Reef? Discover Its Ages!

The Great Barrier Reef, located off the coast of Australia, is estimated to be around 500,000 years old. However, its formation began over 20 million years ago. This natural wonder is the largest coral reef system in the world and is renowned for its biodiversity. Protecting it is crucial to preserving marine life.
