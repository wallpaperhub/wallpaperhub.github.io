Pot Leaf: Understanding Its Cultural and Medicinal Significance

The pot leaf, often recognized as a symbol of cannabis culture, represents both the plant itself and the wider movement for legalization and acceptance. Rich in history, these leaves are not only associated with recreational use but also with medicinal benefits and industrial applications. Understanding its significance helps demystify cannabis.
