Unraveling Toga's Backstory: The Bloodthirsty Villain

Toga Himiko, a character from "My Hero Academia," is a quirky villain with a tragic past. Born into a troubled family, she yearned for connection and acceptance. Her obsession with blood stems from a childhood trauma, driving her to join the League of Villains. Toga’s duality as a kind-hearted girl and ruthless villain captivates fans, making her a complex and unforgettable character.
