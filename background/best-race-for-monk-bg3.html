Best Monk Races in Baldur's Gate 3

When choosing the best race for a monk in Baldur's Gate 3, consider the Wood Elf or the Half-Orc. Wood Elves offer increased Dexterity and speed, enhancing a monk's agility and mobility. Half-Orcs provide a boost to Strength and unique abilities that augment damage, making them formidable fighters. Both races excel in agility, perfect for monks.
