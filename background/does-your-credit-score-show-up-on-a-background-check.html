Does Your Credit Score Appear in Background Checks?

A background check typically does not reveal your credit score, as credit information is considered sensitive data. However, some employers may run a credit check as part of their hiring process, which can access your credit report without disclosing your score. It's crucial to understand your rights regarding credit checks in employment.
