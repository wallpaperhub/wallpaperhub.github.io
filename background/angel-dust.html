Understanding Angel Dust: Effects and Risks

Angel dust, also known as PCP (phencyclidine), is a dissociative anesthetic that alters perceptions of reality, leading to hallucinations. Initially developed for medical use, it has gained notoriety as a recreational drug. Users may experience a sense of detachment and distorted sensory perceptions, posing significant health risks.
