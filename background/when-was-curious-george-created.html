When Was Curious George Created?

Curious George was created in 1941 by husband-and-wife duo Hans Augusto Rey and Margret Rey. The beloved little monkey first appeared in a book titled "Curious George," published by Houghton Mifflin. Since then, he has become a cultural icon, featured in numerous books, TV shows, and films, delighting generations of children.
