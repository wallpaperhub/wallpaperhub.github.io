Understanding Public Trust Clearance: A Quick Guide

Public trust clearance is a security designation for individuals working with sensitive government-related information. It ensures that employees can be trusted to handle specific responsibilities, safeguarding national interests. To obtain this clearance, thorough background checks assess financial, criminal, and personal history, reinforcing the integrity of public service.
