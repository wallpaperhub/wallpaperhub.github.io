Discover the Fascinating World of Tiger Stripes

Tiger stripes are not just a visual marvel; they serve a purpose in the wild. Each tiger has a unique pattern, aiding in camouflage while hunting in dense forests. This striking design, a mix of orange and black, also plays a role in social signaling and communication among tigers. Their beauty and functionality make tiger stripes remarkable.
