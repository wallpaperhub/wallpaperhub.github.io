Janet Yellen: Pioneering Economic Leadership

Janet Yellen is an influential American economist and the first woman to serve as the United States Secretary of the Treasury. Appointed by President Biden, she plays a crucial role in shaping economic policy, addressing inflation, and overseeing financial stability. Yellen's expertise significantly influences global economic trends.
