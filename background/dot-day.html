Celebrate Dot Day: Make Your Mark!

Dot Day is a global celebration held on September 15th, inspired by the beloved children's book "The Dot" by Peter H. Reynolds. It encourages creativity, self-expression, and the power of collaboration among children and adults alike. Join us in celebrating this special day by making your mark and inspiring others to do the same!
