Discover the Delicious History of Sandwiches

Sandwiches have a rich history, tracing back to the 18th century when John Montagu, the 4th Earl of Sandwich, popularized the idea of placing meat between bread for convenience. This simple yet innovative meal has evolved globally, featuring diverse ingredients and flavors, making sandwiches a beloved food choice for all ages.
