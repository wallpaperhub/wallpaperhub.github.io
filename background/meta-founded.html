Meta: Revolutionizing Social Connections and the Digital Future

Meta, formerly known as Facebook, was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg alongside his college roommates. Initially a social networking site, it has since evolved into a tech conglomerate encompassing various platforms, including Instagram and WhatsApp. This transformation emphasizes Meta's vision to build the metaverse, bridging digital and physical worlds.
