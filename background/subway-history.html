The Evolution of Subways: A Brief History

Subway history traces its roots back to the 19th century, when cities sought solutions to urban congestion. The first underground railway opened in London in 1863, marking a new era in public transit. Other cities followed suit, leading to the expansive subway systems we see today. Subways have transformed how we navigate urban landscapes.
