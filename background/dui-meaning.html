DUI Meaning: Understanding Driving Under the Influence

DUI stands for "Driving Under the Influence." It refers to the criminal offense of operating a vehicle while impaired by alcohol or drugs. Each state has laws defining legal limits for blood alcohol concentration (BAC) and the consequences of a DUI can include fines, license suspension, and even imprisonment. Understanding DUI is crucial for responsible driving.
