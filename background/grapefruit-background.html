Discover the History and Benefits of Grapefruit

Grapefruit, a hybrid of the sweet orange and pomelo, is native to tropical regions. Known for its tangy flavor and vibrant pink or yellow flesh, it has a rich history dating back to 18th century Barbados. Grapefruit is packed with nutrients, including vitamins C and A, and offers numerous health benefits, making it a popular choice for healthy diets.
