How to Add a Background Image on One Page in Word

To insert a background image in Word on just one page, first open your document and navigate to the desired page. Click on the "Design" tab, then select "Page Color." Choose "Fill Effects," and in the "Picture" tab, click "Select Picture" to upload your image. Adjust the transparency if needed and click "OK." Your image will now be the background for that specific page.
