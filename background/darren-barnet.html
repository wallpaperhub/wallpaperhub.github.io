Darren Barnet: Rising Star of Netflix's "Never Have I Ever"

Darren Barnet is an American actor, best known for his role as Paxton Hall-Yoshida in Netflix's hit series "Never Have I Ever." Born on April 27, 1991, in Los Angeles, Barnet's charm and talent have captivated audiences. With a background in theatre and a passion for storytelling, he continues to rise in Hollywood.
