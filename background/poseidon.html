Poseidon: God of the Sea and Earthquakes

Poseidon, the Greek god of the sea, is a powerful deity known for his trident, which he uses to create storms, earthquakes, and tidal waves. As the brother of Zeus and Hades, he rules over water and horses, symbolizing both creation and destruction. Poseidon's mythology consists of numerous tales involving heroism, love, and vengeance.
