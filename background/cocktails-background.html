Discover the Fascinating History of Cocktails

Cocktails have a rich history, dating back to the 18th century. Originally a way to mask the taste of poor-quality alcohol, these drinks evolved into sophisticated concoctions enjoyed worldwide. Ingredients range from spirits to fresh fruits, with techniques and styles varying by culture. Today, cocktails symbolize social gatherings and creativity.
