Green Day: The Punk Rock Revolutionaries

Green Day is a pivotal punk rock band formed in 1986, known for their energy and socially conscious lyrics. Their hit albums, including "Dookie" and "American Idiot," revolutionized the genre, addressing issues like political dissent and personal struggles. With an enduring legacy, they continue to inspire new generations of musicians.
