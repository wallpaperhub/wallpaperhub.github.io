How to Confirm Your Background Check Status

To find out if you passed your background check, wait for the employer to contact you. They typically will reach out to confirm your employment status. Alternatively, you can inquire directly with the HR department about your status. If you haven't heard back after a couple of weeks, it's acceptable to follow up.
