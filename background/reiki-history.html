Explore the Origins of Reiki: A Brief History

Reiki originated in early 20th century Japan, founded by Mikao Usui. It combines spiritual practices with healing techniques, emphasizing energy balance and relaxation. After Usui's teachings, the practice spread worldwide, adapting various styles and philosophies. Today, Reiki is widely recognized for promoting wellness and reducing stress.
