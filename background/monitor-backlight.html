Enhancing Your Viewing: Understanding Monitor Backlight

Monitor backlight is a critical component that enhances your viewing experience by illuminating the screen. It impacts brightness, contrast, and overall color accuracy. Proper calibration can reduce eye strain and enhance clarity. Understanding your monitor’s backlight technology—like LED, OLED, or LCD—can help you choose the right settings for optimal performance.
