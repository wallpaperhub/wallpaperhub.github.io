Customizing Your Windows 11 Lock Screen: Tips & Tricks

Windows 11 offers a customizable lock screen that enhances both security and personalization. Users can choose from stunning backgrounds, display useful widgets like weather and calendar, and set custom login messages. Additionally, the dynamic lock screen can showcase a slideshow of images, ensuring your device remains visually engaging even in sleep mode.
