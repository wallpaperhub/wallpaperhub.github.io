Kimora Lee Simmons: Fashion Icon & Entrepreneur

Kimora Lee Simmons is a multifaceted entrepreneur, former supermodel, and fashion mogul. Born on May 4, 1975, she rose to fame as the face of high-profile fashion brands, including Baby Phat, which she co-founded. Beyond her modeling career, Kimora is known for her lavish lifestyle, philanthropy, and as a television personality.
