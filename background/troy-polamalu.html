Troy Polamalu: NFL Legend and Safety Icon

Troy Polamalu, a legendary safety for the Pittsburgh Steelers, revolutionized the position with his dynamic playmaking ability and unmatched agility. Known for his signature long hair and powerful hits, he was a two-time Super Bowl champion and eight-time Pro Bowler. Polamalu's instincts on the field made him a defensive icon in the NFL.
