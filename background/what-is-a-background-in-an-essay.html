Understanding Background in Essays: A Quick Guide

In an essay, the background provides context for the topic discussed, setting the stage for the argument or narrative. It often includes historical, cultural, or situational information that informs the reader’s understanding. A well-crafted background enriches the essay, connecting the reader to the central ideas and themes.
