Unraveling the Legends of the Wild West: History & Myths

The Wild West represents a tumultuous period in American history, spanning the late 19th century. It was characterized by lawlessness, cowboys, outlaws, and clashes with Native Americans. As settlers moved westward, towns sprung up and notorious figures like Billy the Kid and Calamity Jane became legends. The landscape featured vast plains, rugged mountains, and ghost towns, creating an iconic backdrop for countless stories.
