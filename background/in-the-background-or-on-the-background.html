Understanding 'In the Background' vs. 'On the Background'

In the background refers to something that exists out of sight or is not the main focus, while on the background implies that something is placed or layered on top of a given backdrop. Understanding these nuances in usage enhances clarity in communication, especially in design and art contexts.
