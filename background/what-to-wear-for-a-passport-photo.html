Essential Tips for Passport Photo Outfits

When preparing for a passport photo, opt for solid colors that contrast with the background. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors. A collared shirt or blouse is ideal, and ensure your outfit is modest and reflects your personal style. Keep accessories minimal to maintain focus on your face. Good grooming is essential!
