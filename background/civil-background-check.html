Essential Guide to Civil Background Checks

A civil background check is a process used to review an individual's civil court records, revealing any history of lawsuits, evictions, or bankruptcies. Employers and landlords often conduct these checks to assess risks associated with hiring or renting. Understanding civil records helps make informed decisions, ensuring trust and safety.
