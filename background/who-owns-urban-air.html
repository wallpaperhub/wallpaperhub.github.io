Who Owns Urban Air Adventure Park?

Urban Air Adventure Park is owned by Urban Air Trampoline & Adventure Park, which operates a growing number of indoor adventure parks across the United States. The company was founded in 2011 in Dallas, Texas, and has since expanded to numerous locations, offering various attractions like trampolines, climbing walls, and obstacle courses.
