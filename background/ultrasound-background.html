Understanding Ultrasound: A Comprehensive Guide

Ultrasound, a non-invasive imaging technology, utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create real-time visualizations of the body's internal structures. It plays a crucial role in prenatal care, guiding medical diagnostics and procedures, and provides valuable insights into conditions affecting the heart, organs, and soft tissues through detailed images.
