Best Backgrounds for Selling Items: Tips for Stunning Product Photos

When selling items, choosing the right background is crucial. A clean, uncluttered space enhances focus on the product, while neutral colors like white or soft pastels create a calm, professional look. Incorporate texture with wooden surfaces or fabric backdrops to add depth, and ensure good lighting for optimal visibility.
