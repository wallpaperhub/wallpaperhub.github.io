Essential Guide to Background Apps on iPhone

Background apps on iPhone can slow down performance and drain battery life. Managing these apps is crucial for efficient usage. You can close unused apps by swiping up from the bottom of the screen and bringing up the app switcher, then swiping away the apps you wish to close. Regular maintenance ensures a smooth experience!
