John Carlos Stanton: MLB Powerhouse

John Carlos Stanton is a professional baseball outfielder known for his immense power and performance in Major League Baseball (MLB). Playing for the New York Yankees, he has earned multiple All-Star selections and won the National League MVP award in 2017. Stanton continues to be a significant force on the field with his remarkable home run capabilities.
