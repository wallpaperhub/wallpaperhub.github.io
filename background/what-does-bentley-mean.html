Bentley Meaning Explained: Origin & Significance

Bentley is a name of English origin, derived from the Old English words 'bent' (meaning 'bent grass') and 'leah' (meaning 'woodland clearing'). Historically, it is associated with a town in England and has become synonymous with luxury automobiles, specifically the British brand renowned for its craftsmanship and performance.
