Understanding Ofrenda: Honoring the Departed

Ofrendas, vibrant altars adorned with photos, food, and mementos, honor loved ones who have passed away, particularly during the Day of the Dead celebrations in Mexico. These sacred spaces reflect a deep cultural connection to ancestors, inviting their spirits back to celebrate. Each element holds significance, offering comfort and remembrance.
