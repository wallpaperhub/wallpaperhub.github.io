Calvin Coolidge's Educational Journey: From Public Schools to Presidency

Calvin Coolidge, the 30th President of the United States, had an education rooted in traditional values. He attended public schools and later graduated from Amherst College in 1895. Coolidge's academic focus was on law and politics, which shaped his pragmatic approach to governance and economic policy, emphasizing responsibility and simplicity.
