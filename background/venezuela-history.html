Discovering Venezuela's Vibrant History

Venezuela has a rich history, beginning with indigenous tribes long before Spanish colonization in the 15th century. Simón Bolívar led the fight for independence in the early 19th century. Throughout the 20th century, Venezuela navigated through dictatorships and democratic governments, driven by oil wealth and social issues, culminating in the current political crisis.
