How to Mute Keyboard Sounds on Android

To turn off keyboard sounds on your Android device, open the "Settings" app. Navigate to "Sound" or "Sound & vibration," then look for "Advanced." Here, find "Other sounds" or "Keyboard sound" and toggle it off. This will silence key presses, ensuring a quieter typing experience while using your Android device.
