Discover the Fascinating History of Apples

Apples have a rich history, believed to have originated in Central Asia thousands of years ago. They were cultivated by ancient civilizations, including the Greeks and Romans. With over 7,500 varieties globally, apples are a staple fruit, known for their health benefits and versatility in cooking, from pies to salads. Enjoyed fresh or cooked, they symbolize knowledge and temptation in various cultures.
