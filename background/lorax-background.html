"The Lorax: A Tale of Environmental Wisdom"

The Lorax, a beloved character from Dr. Seuss’s book, is an emblem of environmental advocacy. Originally published in 1971, the story highlights the impact of industrialization on nature. Through The Lorax’s voice, readers learn about the importance of conservation, sustainability, and protecting our planet’s resources for future generations.
