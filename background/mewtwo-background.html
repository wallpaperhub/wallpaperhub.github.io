Mewtwo: The Legendary Psychic Pokémon Explained

Mewtwo is a powerful psychic-type Pokémon created through genetic manipulation. As a clone of the legendary Pokémon Mew, Mewtwo was designed to be the ultimate Pokémon, boasting immense abilities and intelligence. Featured in games, anime, and films, Mewtwo’s story delves into themes of identity, ethics, and the consequences of human ambition.
