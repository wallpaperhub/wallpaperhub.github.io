Discover the Sweet History of Ben & Jerry's

Ben & Jerry's was founded in 1978 by childhood friends Ben Cohen and Jerry Greenfield in Burlington, Vermont. Initially starting as a ice cream parlor, they began experimenting with unique flavors. Their commitment to sustainability and social justice has defined their brand ethos, making them a beloved choice for ice cream lovers worldwide.
