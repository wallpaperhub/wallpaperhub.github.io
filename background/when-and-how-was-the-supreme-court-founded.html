Founding of the Supreme Court: Key Dates & Facts

The Supreme Court of the United States was founded on September 24, 1789, with the establishment of the Judiciary Act. It began operating on February 2, 1790, becoming the country's highest judicial authority. The Court was designed to ensure justice, interpret laws, and uphold the Constitution, solidifying the rule of law.
