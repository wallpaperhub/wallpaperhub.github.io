Unveiling Philippians: A Journey Through Its Historical Background

The Philippians, an early Christian community in the city of Philippi, were established by the Apostle Paul during his second missionary journey around AD 49-51. This area was significant as it was a Roman colony in Macedonia, with a diverse population. Paul’s letter to them emphasizes themes of joy, unity, and perseverance in faith.
