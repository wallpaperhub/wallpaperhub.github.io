Discover the History and Varieties of Soup

Soup has a rich history, originating from ancient times when it served as a nourishing way to utilize leftovers and enhance flavors. Traditionally made by simmering ingredients like vegetables, grains, and meats, soup reflects cultural variations and personal tastes. Today, it remains a comforting staple worldwide, offering countless recipes and nutritional benefits.
