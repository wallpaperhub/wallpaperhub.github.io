Exploring Voltaire's Beliefs: Reason, Tolerance, and Freedom

Voltaire, a key figure of the Enlightenment, championed reason, freedom of speech, and religious tolerance. He criticized dogma and advocated for individual rights, believing in the power of rational thought to challenge oppression. His wit and satire aimed to prompt societal reform, reflecting his commitment to justice and liberty.
