Grants for Students: Support Based on Background

Student grants based on personal background are known as "need-based" or "merit-based" grants. These financial aids consider factors like ethnicity, socioeconomic status, or life circumstances, providing support to underrepresented groups or those facing economic hardships. They aim to promote educational access and equity.
