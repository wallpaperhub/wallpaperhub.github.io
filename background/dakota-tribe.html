Dakota Tribe: Culture, History, and Heritage

The Dakota Tribe, indigenous to the Northern Plains, is known for its rich culture and history. Historically, they relied on bison hunting and agriculture. Their traditional social structure features family clans, and they have deep spiritual connections to the land. Today, the Dakota continue to preserve their language and cultural heritage.
