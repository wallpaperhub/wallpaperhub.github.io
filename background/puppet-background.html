Discover the Fascinating World of Puppetry

Puppetry is an ancient art form that dates back thousands of years, originating in various cultures around the world. It involves the manipulation of puppets—either handcrafted or digital—by a puppeteer to tell stories, convey emotions, and entertain. From shadow puppets in Asia to marionettes in Europe, each style reflects unique cultural expressions.
