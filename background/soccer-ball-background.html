Soccer Ball Background: History & Design

Soccer balls have a rich history and unique design that make them a crucial element of the game. Traditionally, they are made of leather or synthetic materials and come in the iconic black and white pentagonal pattern. The evolution of soccer balls has enhanced durability and performance, reflecting advances in technology while keeping the spirit of the sport alive.
