Discover the Refreshing Taste of Barada

Barada, a traditional beverage from the Middle East, is a refreshing blend made from steeped herbs and spices, often enjoyed cold. It serves as a delightful palate cleanser or a health booster, rich in antioxidants. Perfect for summer gatherings or a cozy night in, barada can be tailored using various local ingredients for a unique twist.
