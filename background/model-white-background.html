Model Photography: Why a White Background Works

A model against a white background can enhance the visual impact of a photograph, emphasizing the subject's features and attire without distractions. This technique is widely used in fashion photography, e-commerce, and portraiture to create a clean, professional look. The simplicity of a white backdrop allows for focus and clarity, making the model the star of the image.
