Understanding UVA: Protect Your Skin

UVA (ultraviolet A) light is a type of solar radiation that is present year-round and can penetrate deeply into the skin. It is known for contributing to skin aging and increasing the risk of skin cancer. Understanding UVA background is crucial for developing effective sun protection and promoting skin health awareness.
