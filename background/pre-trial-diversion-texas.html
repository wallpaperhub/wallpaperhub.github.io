Understanding Pre-Trial Diversion in Texas

Pre-trial diversion in Texas is a legal program that allows eligible defendants to resolve their criminal charges outside of the traditional courtroom process. By completing specific requirements, such as community service or counseling, individuals can avoid a criminal record. This option promotes rehabilitation and reduces the burden on the judicial system.
