Origin of the Name Riley: Meaning & History

Riley is a name of Irish origin, derived from the Gaelic surname "O'Reilly." It originally meant "descendant of Raghallach," which translates to "courageous." The name has evolved into a popular first name for both genders in English-speaking countries. It embodies a playful and adventurous spirit, making it a favorite among parents.
