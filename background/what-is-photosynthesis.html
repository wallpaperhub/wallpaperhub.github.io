Understanding Photosynthesis: The Key to Life

Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants, algae, and some bacteria convert light energy into chemical energy. Using sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide, these organisms produce glucose and oxygen. This essential process not only fuels the plant's growth but also supplies oxygen, supporting life on Earth.
