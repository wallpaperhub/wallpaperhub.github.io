Understanding Story Exposition: Key Elements Explained

The exposition of a story introduces the setting, characters, and the basic situation, providing essential background information and context. It establishes the atmosphere and reveals key details that set the stage for the narrative. This crucial part of storytelling helps readers understand the plot’s stakes and characters’ motivations.
