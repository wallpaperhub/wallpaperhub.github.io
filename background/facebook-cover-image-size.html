Facebook Cover Image Size: A Quick Guide

When creating a Facebook cover image, the ideal size is 820 pixels wide by 312 pixels tall for desktop. For mobile devices, it appears at 640 pixels wide by 360 pixels tall. To ensure clarity, use high-resolution images and avoid placing important text near the edges, as they may get cropped on different screens.
