Understanding Bacteria: The Role of Microorganisms

Bacteria are single-celled microorganisms that exist in various environments, from soil to human bodies. They play essential roles in nutrient cycling, fermentation, and even in human health. Despite their negative reputation for causing diseases, many bacteria are beneficial, aiding digestion and producing vitamins. Understanding bacteria is crucial for advances in medicine, agriculture, and biotechnology.
