Explore Philadelphia's Historic Legacy

Philadelphia's history is rich and diverse, marking it as a pivotal city in the founding of the United States. Established in 1682 by William Penn, it served as the site of the Continental Congress and the signing of the Declaration of Independence. Today, it celebrates its heritage through historic landmarks and vibrant culture.
