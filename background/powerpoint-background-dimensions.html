Perfect PowerPoint Background Dimensions Explained

PowerPoint background dimensions are crucial for creating visually appealing presentations. The standard slide size is typically 16:9, ideal for modern screens. For custom dimensions, you can adjust the width and height in the 'Design' tab. Always ensure your design fits the selected size to avoid cropping and maintain clarity.
