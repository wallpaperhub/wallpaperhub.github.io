Stunning Dragon Fruit Images: Colors & Benefits

Dragon fruit, also known as pitaya, is a visually stunning fruit with vibrant pink or yellow skin and unique green scales. Its sweet, mildly tangy flesh is speckled with tiny black seeds. Perfect for smoothies, salads, or as a refreshing snack, dragon fruit is not only tasty but also packed with nutrients and antioxidants, making it a trendy addition to healthy diets.
