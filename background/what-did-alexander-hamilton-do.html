Alexander Hamilton: Founding Father & Financial Visionary

Alexander Hamilton was a Founding Father of the United States, serving as the first Secretary of the Treasury. He established the country's financial system, laid the groundwork for the Federal Reserve, and shaped economic policies through the Hamiltonian economic plan. His influence endures in American finance and governance.
