Who Founded Wingstop: The Story Behind the Iconic Wing Chain

Wingstop was founded in 1994 by Antonio Swad in Garland, Texas. The restaurant specializes in chicken wings with various flavors and sides, rapidly gaining popularity. It became known for its casual dining atmosphere and commitment to high-quality ingredients, leading to national and international expansion over the years.
