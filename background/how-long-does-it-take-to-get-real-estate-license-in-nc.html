How Long to Get Your Real Estate License in NC: A Quick Guide

In North Carolina, obtaining a real estate license typically takes around 3 to 6 months. Prospective agents must complete 75 hours of pre-licensing education, pass the state exam, and undergo a background check. The time frame can vary depending on how quickly you complete your coursework and exam preparation.
