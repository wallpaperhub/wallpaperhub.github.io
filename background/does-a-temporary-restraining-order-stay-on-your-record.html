Understanding Temporary Restraining Orders: Their Impact on Your Record

A temporary restraining order (TRO) may not stay on your record forever. In many cases, if the order is dismissed or expires without being converted into a permanent order, it typically won’t appear on your criminal record. However, it can still affect background checks and may be visible in some civil court records.
