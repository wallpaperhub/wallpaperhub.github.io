Understanding Inflation: Causes & Effects

Inflation is the economic phenomenon where the overall price levels of goods and services rise over time, leading to a decrease in purchasing power. It can result from demand exceeding supply or increased production costs. Understanding inflation is essential for making informed financial decisions and planning for the future.
