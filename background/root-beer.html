Discover the Sweet World of Root Beer

Root beer is a beloved, non-alcoholic beverage with a unique, creamy flavor. Originating in North America, it’s traditionally brewed from roots like sassafras and sarsaparilla. Enjoyed chilled, root beer is a popular choice for floats, mixing with vanilla ice cream for a nostalgic treat. A sweet sip of history!
