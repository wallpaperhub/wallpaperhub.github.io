Ukraine Flag: Symbol of Freedom and Heritage

The flag of Ukraine features two horizontal stripes: a vibrant blue top half symbolizing the sky and a golden-yellow bottom half representing the wheat fields. This iconic design reflects the nation’s agricultural heritage and rich landscapes. Historically, the colors are associated with freedom, unity, and resilience.
