Do I Need to Disclose a Felony After 7 Years?

In many cases, you may not be required to disclose a felony after 7 years, but this varies by state and the circumstances of your conviction. Certain jobs, especially in law enforcement or finance, may require full disclosure regardless of time. Always check specific local laws and employer requirements for clarity.
