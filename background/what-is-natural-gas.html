Understanding Natural Gas: Benefits and Uses

Natural gas is a fossil fuel formed from decomposed organic materials, primarily consisting of methane. It's a clean-burning energy source used for heating, electricity generation, and as a feedstock in chemical production. As a more environmentally friendly alternative to coal and oil, natural gas plays a key role in today's energy transition.
