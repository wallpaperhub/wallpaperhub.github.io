History of Medtronic: Pioneering Medical Innovation

Medtronic was founded in 1949 by Earl Bakken and his brother-in-law, Art Fry, initially as a medical equipment repair company. It grew rapidly, pioneering the development of the first battery-operated pacemaker in 1957. Over the decades, Medtronic expanded its product line to address a range of medical conditions, becoming a global leader in medical technology.
