Easy Ways to Blur Backgrounds in Photos

To blur the background of a picture, you can use photo editing software or apps. Start by selecting the subject and isolating it using a lasso tool or a selection brush. Once your subject is selected, apply a Gaussian blur or lens blur to the background. Adjust the blur intensity for a professional effect. Save your final image!
