Discovering the Amish: Tradition Meets Simplicity

The Amish are a group of traditionalist Christians, known for their simple living, plain dress, and reluctance to adopt modern technology. Originating in Europe in the 16th century, they emphasize community, family values, and self-sufficiency. Primarily found in the United States, they uphold a distinctive lifestyle rooted in faith and tradition.
