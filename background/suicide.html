Understanding Suicide: Insights & Prevention

Suicide is a critical issue impacting individuals and communities worldwide. It can arise from factors like mental health disorders, trauma, or life crises. Creating a supportive environment, encouraging open conversations, and offering accessible mental health resources are essential to preventing suicide and promoting well-being.
