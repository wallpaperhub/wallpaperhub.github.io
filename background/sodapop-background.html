"Understanding Sodapop Curtis: The Heart of The Outsiders"

Sodapop Curtis is a popular character from S.E. Hinton's novel "The Outsiders." He is the middle Curtis brother known for his good looks and charm. Working at a gas station, he is a caring and supportive figure to his brothers, Ponyboy and Darry. Sodapop represents youthful rebellion and the struggles of growing up, embodying a blend of innocence and maturity.
