Understanding Addison: A Comprehensive Overview

Addison is a term that may refer to various subjects, such as the Addison's Disease, a disorder of the adrenal glands, or the name of a city, like Addison, Texas. The name "Addison" has English origins and means "son of Adam." It has gained popularity as a first name and is also associated with prominent historical figures and locations.
