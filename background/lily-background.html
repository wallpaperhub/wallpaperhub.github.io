Explore the Beauty and Significance of Lilies

Lilies, known for their stunning blooms and enchanting fragrance, hold a rich cultural and botanical history. These perennials belong to the Lilium genus, with various species symbolizing purity, renewal, and transformation. Found in diverse habitats, they thrive in well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight, making them a favorite in gardens worldwide.
