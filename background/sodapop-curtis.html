Sodapop Curtis: The Heart of The Outsiders

Sodapop Curtis is one of the beloved characters from S.E. Hinton's novel "The Outsiders." He is the middle Curtis brother, known for his charming personality and good looks. Sodapop dreams of a better life beyond the struggles of their working-class background and serves as a crucial emotional support for his brothers, Ponyboy and Darry.
