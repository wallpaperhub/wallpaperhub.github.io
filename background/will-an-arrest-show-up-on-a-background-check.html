"Do Arrests Show Up on Background Checks?"

Yes, an arrest can show up on a background check, depending on the type of check conducted. Criminal background checks typically reveal arrests and convictions unless expunged. Employers, landlords, and other organizations may access this information, impacting hiring decisions, rental applications, or licensing.
