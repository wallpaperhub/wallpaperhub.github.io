Remove Background Noise in Audacity: A Quick Guide

To remove background noise in Audacity, start by selecting a section of the audio track that contains only the noise. Go to 'Effect' and choose 'Noise Reduction,' then click 'Get Noise Profile.' Next, select the entire track, return to 'Noise Reduction,' and adjust the settings before applying. Finally, listen and fine-tune as needed.
