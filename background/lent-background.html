Understanding Lent: A Spiritual Journey

Lent is a solemn observance in the Christian calendar, lasting 40 days, leading up to Easter. It commemorates the 40 days Jesus spent fasting in the desert. Traditionally, the season involves prayer, fasting, and almsgiving, encouraging self-reflection and spiritual growth. Lent begins on Ash Wednesday and concludes on Holy Saturday.
