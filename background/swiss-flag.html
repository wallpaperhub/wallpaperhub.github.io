Discover the Swiss Flag: Symbol of Unity

The Swiss flag is a striking emblem characterized by a white cross on a red background, symbolizing peace and neutrality. Its origins date back to the 13th century when it represented the Swiss Confederation. The flag is a prominent national symbol, representing Swiss unity, democracy, and the country's rich heritage.
