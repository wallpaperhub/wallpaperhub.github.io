Transform Your Black Background to White: A Step-by-Step Guide

To change a black background to white, start by accessing the settings or properties of the application or software you're using. Look for the 'Background' or 'Theme' option. Select 'White' or a lighter color. In graphic editing tools, use the paint bucket or fill tool. Save changes and enjoy the refreshed look.
