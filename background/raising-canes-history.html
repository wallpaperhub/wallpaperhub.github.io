Raising Cane's: A Brief History of the Iconic Chicken Chain

Raising Cane's was founded in 1996 by Todd Graves and Craig Silvey in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. The restaurant quickly became popular for its focus on quality chicken fingers, served with their signature Cane's Sauce. Today, it operates over 600 locations across the U.S. and beyond, emphasizing simplicity and fresh ingredients.
