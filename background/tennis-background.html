Discover the History of Tennis: From Origins to Modern Game

Tennis has a rich history, dating back to the late 12th century when it originated as a handball game in France. Evolving into lawn tennis in the late 19th century, it gained popularity worldwide. The sport offers physical challenge, strategy, and camaraderie, with grass, clay, and hard courts supporting diverse playing styles.
