System of a Down: A Musical Revolution

System of a Down, formed in 1994, is an American rock band known for their unique fusion of metal, punk, and Armenian folk music. With distinctive vocals from Serj Tankian and aggressive instrumentation, they gained fame with hits like "Chop Suey!" and "Toxicity." Their politically charged lyrics offer powerful social commentary, making them influential in the music scene.
