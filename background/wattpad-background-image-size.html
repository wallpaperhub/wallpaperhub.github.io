Optimal Wattpad Background Image Sizes Explained

When designing a background image for Wattpad, it's essential to know the ideal size for optimal display. The recommended dimensions are 2560 pixels in width and 1440 pixels in height. This resolution ensures your image looks sharp and clear on various devices, enhancing reader engagement and maintaining a professional appearance for your story.
