Understanding the Longstanding Issue of Food Waste

Food waste has been a persistent issue for centuries, originating from agricultural practices and increasing with urbanization and industrialization. Historically, overproduction and poor storage led to significant spoilage. Today, this problem is exacerbated by modern consumption habits, impacting food security and the environment.
