Unlocking Background Graphics in PowerPoint

When using PowerPoint, you might find that your background graphics are locked. This can be frustrating when you need to modify or remove them. The issue usually arises from the slide master view, where elements are anchored. To unlock, go to the slide master, select the graphic, and adjust the settings accordingly.
