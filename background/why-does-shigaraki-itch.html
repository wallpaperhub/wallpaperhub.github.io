Why Shigaraki Itches: The Pain Behind Decay

Shigaraki, a character from "My Hero Academia," experiences intense itching due to his Quirk, Decay. This power inflicts decay on his surroundings, causing physical and emotional distress. His itch symbolizes the deep-rooted pain and turmoil from his tragic past, reflecting his desire for acceptance amid chaos and destruction.
