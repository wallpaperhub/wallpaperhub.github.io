The Importance of a Good Background in Visual Design

Missing backgrounds can significantly affect the aesthetics of any visual project, making elements appear disconnected. This absence can lead to confusion, where the focal point struggles to establish context. Addressing this issue involves integrating appropriate backdrops that enhance and unify the overall design, ensuring clarity and visual appeal.
