Understanding Grief: A Compassionate Guide

Grief is a profound emotional response to loss, often manifesting as sadness, anger, or confusion. It can arise from various experiences, such as the death of a loved one, the end of a relationship, or significant life changes. Understanding grief's background helps individuals navigate their emotions and find healing through support and coping mechanisms.
