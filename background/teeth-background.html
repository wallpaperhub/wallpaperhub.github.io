Essential Insights About Teeth: Health & Care

Teeth are vital for our overall health and well-being, playing integral roles in digestion, speech, and appearance. Composed of enamel, dentin, pulp, and cementum, they vary in types including incisors, canines, premolars, and molars. Understanding teeth can help maintain oral hygiene and prevent dental issues for a brighter smile.
