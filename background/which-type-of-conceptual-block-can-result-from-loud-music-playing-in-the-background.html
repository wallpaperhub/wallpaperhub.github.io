"Overcoming Conceptual Blocks: The Impact of Loud Music on Focus"

Loud music playing in the background can create a conceptual block known as "distraction." This occurs when external stimuli interfere with our cognitive processes, making it difficult to focus on tasks. It hampers creative thinking and problem-solving, as the brain struggles to filter out competing sounds, leading to decreased productivity and clarity.
