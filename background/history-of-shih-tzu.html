Discovering the History of the Shih Tzu: From Royal Companionship to Beloved Pets

The Shih Tzu, known as a "Lion Dog," originates from Tibet and was bred by Chinese royalty. They were favored companions in ancient palaces, revered for their loyalty and beauty. Initially bred from the Pekingese and the Tibetan Mastiff, these dogs have a rich history intertwined with Chinese culture, symbolizing status and prestige.
