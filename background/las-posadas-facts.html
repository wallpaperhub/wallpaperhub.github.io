Discover Las Posadas: Important Facts and Traditions

Las Posadas is a traditional Mexican celebration that commemorates Mary and Joseph's search for lodging before Jesus' birth. It takes place from December 16-24. Participants reenact the nativity, visit homes to seek "posada" (shelter), sing carols, and share food. The festive customs combine religion, culture, and community spirit.
