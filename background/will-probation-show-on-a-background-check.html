Will Probation Show Up on a Background Check?

Probation may appear on a background check, depending on the type of check and the policies of the company conducting it. Generally, criminal background checks can reveal probation status, especially if it involved a felony or serious offense. It's important to understand local laws regarding disclosure and how they may affect employment opportunities.
