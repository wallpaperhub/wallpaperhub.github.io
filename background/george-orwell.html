Discover George Orwell: Master of Dystopian Literature

George Orwell was a British novelist and essayist renowned for his keen insights into social injustice and totalitarianism. His most famous works, "1984" and "Animal Farm," critique oppressive regimes and advocate for individual freedom. Orwell's writing remains relevant as it explores themes of power, corruption, and the human condition.
