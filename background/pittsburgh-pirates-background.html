Pittsburgh Pirates: A Rich Baseball Legacy

The Pittsburgh Pirates are a professional baseball team founded in 1881, based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. They are a member of Major League Baseball's National League Central Division. The team has a storied history, with five World Series championships and a passionate fan base that fiercely supports them through highs and lows.
