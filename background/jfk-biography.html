JFK: A Brief Biography of America's Iconic President

John F. Kennedy, the 35th President of the United States, was born on May 29, 1917, in Brookline, Massachusetts. He served from 1961 until his assassination in 1963. A decorated WWII veteran, Kennedy championed civil rights, the space race, and the establishment of the Peace Corps. His iconic speeches and charisma left a lasting legacy.
