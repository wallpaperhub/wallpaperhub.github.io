Understanding Background Music: Definition & Impact

Background music refers to the musical accompaniment that enhances the ambiance in various settings without dominating the scene. Commonly used in films, videos, and social gatherings, it subtly influences emotions and perceptions, creating a more engaging experience for the audience. Its role is to support rather than overshadow the primary focus.
