Uber Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Yes, Uber conducts background checks on all potential drivers before they can start driving. These checks include a review of driving records and criminal history to ensure passenger safety. The process varies by region to comply with local laws, but maintaining a safe environment is a priority for the company.
