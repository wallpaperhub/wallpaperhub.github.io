Unlock Opportunities with the T-BON Blue Card

The T-BON blue card is a biometric residence permit designed for immigrants in certain countries. It facilitates easier mobility across EU nations and allows holders to reside and work in the issuing state. With an emphasis on skilled labor, the blue card aims to attract talent and address labor shortages in various sectors.
