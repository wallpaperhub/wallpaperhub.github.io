Enhancing Cell Visibility: Staining Techniques

Staining cells to contrast with their background enhances visibility, making cellular structures more discernible. This technique aids in the examination of cell morphology, behavior, and interactions. By utilizing specific stains, researchers can differentiate cell types, track cell changes, and conduct more accurate assessments during studies and diagnostics.
