Does a DUI Show Up on Background Checks?

Yes, a DUI can appear on a background check. Depending on the type of check and local laws, it may show up in criminal history reports. Employers or organizations may review this information when making hiring decisions, impacting job prospects. It's crucial to understand how a DUI may influence background assessments.
