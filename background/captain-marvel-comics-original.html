Explore the Original Captain Marvel Comics: A Hero's Journey

Captain Marvel, originally known as Mar-Vell, debuted in Marvel Comics in 1967. Created by Roy Thomas and Gene Colan, he embodies the spirit of heroism and galactic justice. Over the years, the character has evolved, with Carol Danvers taking on the mantle. Capturing readers' imagination, Captain Marvel represents empowerment and resilience.
