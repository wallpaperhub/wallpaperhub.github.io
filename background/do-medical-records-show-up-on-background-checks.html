Do Medical Records Appear in Background Checks?

Medical records typically do not show up on background checks for employment or housing, as these records are protected under privacy laws like HIPAA. However, certain jobs in healthcare or positions requiring security clearance may involve a review of medical history. Always check local laws, as regulations can vary.
