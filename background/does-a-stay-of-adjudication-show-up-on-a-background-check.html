Understanding Stay of Adjudication and Background Checks

Yes, a stay of adjudication can appear on a background check, but its visibility may depend on the state laws and the specific type of check performed. Typically, this type of legal outcome might not result in a conviction but could still be disclosed in certain circumstances, potentially impacting employment opportunities.
