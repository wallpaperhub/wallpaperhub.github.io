Understanding Literature Background: A Comprehensive Overview

Literature background refers to the historical and cultural context in which literary works emerge. It encompasses various genres and movements, reflecting societal values, norms, and issues. Understanding this background enriches readers' interpretations by providing insight into authors' motivations and the influences shaping their narratives.
