Understanding Energy Drinks: Origins and Impact

Energy drinks emerged in the late 20th century, designed to enhance energy, alertness, and physical performance. Initially popularized by brands like Red Bull, they typically contain caffeine, taurine, and sugars. Over time, these beverages have garnered both enthusiasts and critics due to their health effects and marketing practices.
