History of Amazon: From Bookstore to E-Commerce Giant

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 as an online bookstore. It rapidly expanded into a vast e-commerce platform offering a variety of products and services. In the 2000s, Amazon introduced innovations like Prime Membership and AWS. Today, it's a global leader in e-commerce, cloud computing, and digital streaming.
