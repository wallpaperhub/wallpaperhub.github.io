Understanding CBI: India's Key Investigative Agency

CBI, or the Central Bureau of Investigation, is India's premier investigating agency, known for tackling high-profile cases, corruption, and serious crimes. Established in 1941, it plays a crucial role in upholding the law, ensuring justice, and maintaining public trust. The agency is vital in coordinating with state police forces and international agencies.
