Understanding Overlapping Employment in Background Checks

When conducting a background check, overlapping employment is a crucial factor to consider. It highlights transparency and accountability in a candidate's work history. Employers should verify any gaps or overlaps in employment dates to ensure accuracy. This can prevent issues related to job performance and integrity in the hiring process.
