Norse Mythology: Legends of the Gods

Norse mythology is a rich tapestry of ancient tales featuring gods, goddesses, and legendary creatures. Central figures include Odin, the all-father; Thor, the god of thunder; and Loki, the trickster. These stories explore themes of fate, heroism, and the cosmos, offering profound insights into the Viking age and shaping modern fantasy.
