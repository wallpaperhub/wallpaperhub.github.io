The Origins of Pole Dancing: A Historical Overview

Pole dancing originated in India as a form of traditional entertainment called Mallakhamb, date back to the 12th century. It later evolved in the West through stages of burlesque and circus performances during the late 20th century, gaining popularity as a fitness trend. Its rich history blends culture, art, and athleticism.
