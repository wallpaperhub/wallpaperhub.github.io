Understanding Connecticut Background Check Laws

Connecticut background check laws regulate how employers and organizations conduct screenings for prospective employees and volunteers. Under Connecticut law, individuals are entitled to know if a background check is performed and must provide written consent. Employers must also avoid discrimination when using background information.
