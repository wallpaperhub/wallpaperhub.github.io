The Evolution of Cars: A Brief History

Cars have evolved significantly since their inception in the late 19th century. Early models were simple and slow, powered by steam or electricity. The introduction of the internal combustion engine revolutionized the industry, leading to mass production. Today, we see advanced technology, electric vehicles, and a push towards sustainability.
