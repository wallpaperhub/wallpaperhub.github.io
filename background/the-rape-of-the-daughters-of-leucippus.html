The Rape of the Daughters of Leucippus is a poignant tale from Greek mythology, highlighting themes of desire and violence. The beautiful daughters of Leucippus, Phoebe and Hilaeira, captured the hearts of the twin brothers Castor and Pollux. The story unfolds with their abduction, symbolizing the darker aspects of love and fate.
