Blueground Cancellation Policy Explained

Blueground's cancellation policy offers flexibility to guests, allowing changes up to 24 hours before arrival without penalties. For long-term stays, guests can cancel or modify bookings with a notice period outlined in their contracts. Always review cancellation terms specific to your booking for optimal planning.
