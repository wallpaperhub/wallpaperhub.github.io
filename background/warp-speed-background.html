Understanding Warp Speed: A Dive into Faster-than-Light Travel

Warp speed is a theoretical concept in physics and science fiction representing faster-than-light travel. It gained popularity through media like "Star Trek," where spacecraft traverse vast distances in space instantaneously. In real-world physics, concepts like wormholes or Alcubierre drives explore these possibilities, pushing the limits of human understanding.
