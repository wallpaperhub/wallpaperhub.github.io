Understanding Beer Transparency: A Guide

Beer transparency refers to the visual clarity and purity of the brew, showcasing its quality and craftsmanship. A transparent beer often indicates proper filtration and brewing techniques. Breweries strive for a crystal-clear appearance, which can enhance the drinking experience by highlighting the beer's color and carbonation.
