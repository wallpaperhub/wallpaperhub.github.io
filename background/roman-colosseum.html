Explore the Wonders of the Roman Colosseum

The Roman Colosseum, an iconic symbol of Ancient Rome, boasts a captivating history as a grand arena for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles. Completed in AD 80, it could hold up to 80,000 spectators. Today, it remains a UNESCO World Heritage site, attracting millions of visitors eager to explore its impressive architecture and rich past.
