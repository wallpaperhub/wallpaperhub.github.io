Discover the Rich Legacy of the Byzantine Empire

The Byzantine Empire, lasting over a millennium, was a hub of art, culture, and politics. Nestled between Europe and Asia, its capital, Constantinople, thrived on trade and scholarship. Known for its iconic mosaics and architectural marvels like Hagia Sophia, Byzantine society was a blend of Roman legacy and Greek influence, profoundly impacting Western civilization.
