How to Set a Video as Your Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

To make a video your wallpaper, start by selecting a video that you love. Convert it to a suitable format if necessary (like MP4). On your device, access the settings for wallpaper or home screen. Choose the option for a live wallpaper and select your video. Adjust the settings to fit your screen, then apply it. Enjoy!
