Understanding Signal-to-Noise Ratio: A Key to Clarity

Signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) is a fundamental measure used in various fields, including telecommunications and audio engineering. It compares the level of a desired signal to the level of background noise, indicating clarity and quality. Higher SNR values signify clearer signals, while lower values indicate more noise interference, affecting performance.
