Denim: The Timeless Fabric Revolutionizing Fashion

Denim, a classic fabric originating in the late 19th century, is known for its durability and timeless style. Traditionally made from cotton twill, denim comes in various washes and colors. It has evolved from workwear to a fashion staple, suited for jeans, jackets, and skirts, embodying both casual and high-fashion aesthetics.
