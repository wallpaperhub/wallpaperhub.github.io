Enhance Your Google Slides with Text Box Background Colors

To change the background color of a text box in Google Slides, select the text box and click on the "Fill color" icon in the toolbar. Choose your desired color from the palette. This enhancement aids in better visibility and aesthetic appeal of your slides, ensuring your message stands out effectively during presentations.
