Optimal Discord PFP Size: A Quick Guide

When setting a profile picture (PFP) on Discord, it's essential to know the optimal size for the best appearance. The recommended dimensions are 128 x 128 pixels. A square image works best, ensuring your profile is sharp and clear across all devices. Remember to keep your file size under 8 MB and use formats like PNG, JPEG, or GIF.
