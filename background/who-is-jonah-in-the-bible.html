Who is Jonah? The Biblical Prophet Explained

Jonah is a significant figure in the Bible, known as a prophet who received a divine mission from God to warn the city of Nineveh to repent for its wickedness. Instead, Jonah initially fled, leading to his famous encounter with a great fish that swallowed him. After three days, he was released, ultimately fulfilling God's command and leading Nineveh to repentance.
