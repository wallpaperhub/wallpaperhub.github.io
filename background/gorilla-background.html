Gorilla Facts: Explore Their Background and Habitat

Gorillas are large primates belonging to the family Hominidae, primarily found in the forests of central and western Africa. They exhibit immense strength and intelligence, living in family groups led by a dominant silverback male. Their diet mainly consists of leaves, stems, and fruit, with a social structure that emphasizes strong familial bonds.
