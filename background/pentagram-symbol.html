Understanding the Pentagram Symbol: History & Meaning

The pentagram symbol, a five-pointed star, has deep historical significance across cultures. Often associated with mysticism and spirituality, it represents the elements—earth, air, fire, water—and the spirit. In modern contexts, it is linked to Wicca and Paganism, symbolizing protection and balance. Its geometric harmony captivates many.
