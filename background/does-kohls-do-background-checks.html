Kohl's Background Check Process: What You Need to Know

Kohl's does conduct background checks as part of their hiring process. This typically includes checking criminal history and verifying employment records. Applicants should be aware that certain positions may require more extensive checks, especially those involving financial responsibilities. It's advisable to be transparent during the application process.
