Blur Background in Lightroom: Easy Guide

To blur the background in Lightroom, start by selecting the subject using the Masking tool. Utilize the AI-powered tools to refine your selection. Once your subject is isolated, apply a reduction in clarity and sharpness to the background, adjusting the amount of blur to achieve your desired effect. Finally, fine-tune the colors and light for a polished look.
