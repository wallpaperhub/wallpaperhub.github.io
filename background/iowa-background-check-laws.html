Iowa Background Check Laws: What You Need to Know

Iowa background check laws regulate the use of criminal history information for employment, housing, and other purposes. Employers can request background checks, but applicants must be notified and consent is required. Certain records may be sealed, influencing what information is accessible. Understanding these laws is crucial for both employers and applicants.
