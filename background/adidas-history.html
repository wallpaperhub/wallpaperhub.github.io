“A Brief History of Adidas: From 1949 to Global Sports Leader”

Adidas, founded in 1949 by Adolf Dassler in Germany, revolutionized sports footwear with innovative designs and advanced technology. The iconic three stripes were introduced to symbolize performance and quality. Today, Adidas stands as a global leader in sports apparel, merging fashion with function while endorsing sustainability.
