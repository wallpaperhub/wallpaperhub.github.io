Mona Lisa's Background: An Enigmatic Landscape

The background of the Mona Lisa, painted by Leonardo da Vinci, features a dreamy landscape comprising winding paths, distant mountains, and a river. This ethereal setting enhances the enigmatic expression of the subject, drawing viewers into the painting. The use of sfumato technique creates soft transitions between colors, adding to the depth and realism.
