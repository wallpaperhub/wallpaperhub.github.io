Does Adjudication Withheld Appear on Background Checks?

Adjudication withheld refers to a legal decision where charges are not pursued after a plea deal or probation, meaning no conviction is recorded. However, it can still appear in background checks, depending on state laws and the specific checks performed. It's essential to understand how this may affect employment or housing opportunities.
