How to Fade Background Images for Better Design

Fading a background image can enhance your website's aesthetic by creating a smooth transition between elements. To achieve this, you can use CSS opacity settings or a gradient overlay. This technique helps improve readability by softening the image behind text, making your content stand out while maintaining visual appeal.
