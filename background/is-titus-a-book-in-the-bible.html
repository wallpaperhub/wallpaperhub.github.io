Exploring the Book of Titus in the Bible

Yes, Titus is a book in the New Testament of the Bible. It is one of the Pauline Epistles, written by the Apostle Paul to Titus, a fellow worker and church leader. The letter addresses important theological issues and provides guidance on church leadership, sound doctrine, and Christian living, emphasizing grace and good works.
