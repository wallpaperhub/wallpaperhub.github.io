Top Reasons You May Be Disqualified from Law School

Several factors can disqualify an individual from becoming a lawyer. These include a felony conviction, a lack of moral character, failing the bar exam, and misrepresenting information on applications. Additionally, mental health issues that impair performance or integrity may also lead to disqualification.
