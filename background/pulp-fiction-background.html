Pulp Fiction Background: The Iconic Film Unveiled

Pulp Fiction, directed by Quentin Tarantino, revolutionized the film industry with its unique narrative structure and eclectic dialogue. Set in Los Angeles, the film intertwines the lives of hitmen, a boxer, and a gangster's wife, showcasing crime, redemption, and pop culture references. Its 1994 release garnered critical acclaim and a devoted following.
