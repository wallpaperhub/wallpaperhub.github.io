Understanding Bear Habitat and Behavior

Bears are powerful and adaptable mammals found in diverse habitats across North America, Europe, and Asia. With eight species like the grizzly, black, and polar bear, they display a range of behaviors and diets, from omnivorous feeding to hibernation. Distinct for their size and strength, bears symbolize wilderness and conservation efforts.
