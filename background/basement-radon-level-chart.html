Essential Guide to Basement Radon Levels

Understanding basement radon levels is crucial for home safety. Use a radon level chart to monitor and assess risks in your space. Typically, levels are measured in picocuries per liter (pCi/L). A level below 2 pCi/L is considered safe, while 4 pCi/L or higher poses health risks. Regular testing can safeguard your family!
