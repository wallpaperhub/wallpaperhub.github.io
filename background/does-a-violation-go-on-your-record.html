Do Violations Affect Your Record? Find Out!

When you receive a violation, it can affect your record depending on its nature. Traffic violations often appear on driving records, while criminal violations can impact criminal records. Not all violations are permanent; some may be expunged after a certain period, but it's essential to understand the specific laws in your area.
