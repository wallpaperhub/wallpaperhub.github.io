Criminal Background Check Errors: What to Know

Criminal background check errors can lead to severe consequences for individuals, including job rejections and unwarranted stigma. Such inaccuracies may arise from outdated information, clerical mistakes, or identity mix-ups. It's essential to regularly review your background report, dispute errors promptly, and stay informed about your rights to ensure accuracy.
