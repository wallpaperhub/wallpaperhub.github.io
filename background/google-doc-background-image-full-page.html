How to Set a Full-Page Background Image in Google Docs

To set a full-page background image in Google Docs, open your document, go to "File," and select "Page setup." Change the page color to match your image for transparency. Insert the image by clicking "Insert" > "Image," then choose "Upload from computer" or other options. Resize to fill the page and send it to the back.
