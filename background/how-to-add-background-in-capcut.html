Add Backgrounds in CapCut: Simple Guide

To add a background in CapCut, begin by importing your video clip into the project. Tap on the "Background" option in the bottom menu. Choose a solid color, gradient, or image from your gallery. Once selected, adjust the size and position as needed. Finally, preview your video to ensure the background complements the content.
