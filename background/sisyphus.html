Sisyphus: The Endless Struggle

Sisyphus, a figure from Greek mythology, embodies the struggle against the inevitable. Condemned to roll a boulder up a hill only to watch it roll back down, he symbolizes perseverance in the face of futility. His tale resonates with human experiences of relentless effort and the search for meaning, reflecting life's challenges.
