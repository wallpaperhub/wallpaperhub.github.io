Bill Gates: From Tech Titan to Philanthropist

Bill Gates, co-founder of Microsoft, revolutionized personal computing in the 1970s. Born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, he exhibited an early passion for programming. Gates' innovative vision led Microsoft to become the world's largest software company. Today, he is also known for his philanthropic efforts through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
