Enhance Your Walk: The Importance of Sidewalks in Urban Design

Sidewalks are vital components of urban landscapes, serving as pathways for pedestrians and enhancing community interactions. They not only promote safety but also encourage walking, connecting neighborhoods and fostering a sense of place. The materials and design of sidewalks can influence aesthetics and functionality, making them crucial to urban planning.
