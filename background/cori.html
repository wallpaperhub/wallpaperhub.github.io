Explore the Flavorful World of Cori: Benefits & Uses

Cori, also known as cilantro or coriander, is an aromatic herb widely used in culinary dishes around the globe. With vibrant green leaves and distinctive flavor, it enhances salsas, salads, and soups. Cori also offers numerous health benefits, including antioxidants and vitamins, making it a popular choice for nutritious cooking.
