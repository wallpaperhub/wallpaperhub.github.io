Colin Kaepernick: Activism and NFL Legacy

Colin Kaepernick is an influential figure known for his NFL career and activism. Best known for kneeling during the national anthem to protest racial injustice, Kaepernick sparked a nationwide conversation about policing and racial inequality. His actions have made him a symbol of the fight for social justice and a catalyst for change.
