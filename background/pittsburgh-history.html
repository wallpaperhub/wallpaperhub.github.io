Discover Pittsburgh: A Journey Through History

Pittsburgh, known as the "Steel City," played a pivotal role in America's industrial revolution. Founded in 1758, its strategic location at the confluence of three rivers facilitated trade and growth. The city's rich history includes its transformation from steel production to technology and education, symbolizing resilience and innovation.
