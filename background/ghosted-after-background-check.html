Why Employers Ghost Candidates After Background Checks

Being ghosted after a background check can be frustrating and confusing. Applicants often wonder why they never received feedback, especially after investing time and effort. This silence may stem from various reasons, such as company policy, hiring freezes, or undisclosed issues found during the check. Understanding this process can lessen the sting of being left in the dark.
