Understanding Barotrauma: Causes & Prevention

Barotrauma occurs when there's a pressure imbalance between the inside and outside of the body, often affecting the lungs, ears, and sinuses. It commonly arises during diving, flying, or any rapid changes in altitude. Understanding barotrauma is crucial for prevention and effective treatment to avoid serious health risks.
