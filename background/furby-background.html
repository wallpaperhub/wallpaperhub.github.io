Discover the Fascinating Background of Furby: A Timeless Toy

Furby, a popular interactive toy launched in 1998, was created by Tiger Electronics. With its unique blend of robotic technology and entertainment, Furby quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Designed to communicate in its own language, Furbish, it could mimic human speech and respond to touch, captivating children and collectors alike.
