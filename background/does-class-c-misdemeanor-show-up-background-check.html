Do Class C Misdemeanors Show on Background Checks?

Yes, a Class C misdemeanor can show up on a background check, particularly if the check includes court records. While these offenses are less severe, they still appear in public records and may be visible to potential employers or landlords. It's always wise to understand how these records could impact opportunities.
