Change Artboard Color in Illustrator: Easy Guide

To change the artboard color in Illustrator, select the artboard tool and choose your desired artboard. Go to the "Artboard Options" in the control panel or double-click the artboard. Under "Background Color," pick a new color to create a vibrant workspace. This adjustment enhances visibility and can inspire creativity in your designs.
