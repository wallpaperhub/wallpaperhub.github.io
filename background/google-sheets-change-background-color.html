Change Background Color in Google Sheets: A Quick Guide

To change the background color in Google Sheets, select the cells you want to modify. Click on the paint bucket icon in the toolbar, then choose your desired color from the palette. For more options, click on “Custom” to create a specific shade. This feature allows for better organization and visual appeal in your spreadsheets.
