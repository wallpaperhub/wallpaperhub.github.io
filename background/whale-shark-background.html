Discovering the Majestic Whale Shark

Whale sharks are the largest fish in the ocean, reaching lengths of up to 40 feet. These gentle giants are filter feeders, primarily consuming plankton, and are known for their unique spotted patterns. Found in warm waters worldwide, they are migratory and often gather in large numbers at rich feeding grounds, making them a marvel of marine life.
