Exploring the Origins and Benefits of Botox

Botox, derived from the bacterium Clostridium botulinum, was initially used for medical conditions like muscle spasms and excessive sweating. Its cosmetic use began in the 1990s, primarily for reducing wrinkles and fine lines. The treatment works by temporarily paralyzing facial muscles, resulting in a smoother appearance.
