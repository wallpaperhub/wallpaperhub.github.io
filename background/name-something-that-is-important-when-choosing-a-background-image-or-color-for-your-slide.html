Essential Tips for Choosing Slide Backgrounds

When choosing a background image or color for your slide, it's crucial to consider contrast. A high contrast between the background and text ensures readability and draws the audience's attention. Selecting colors that align with your theme can enhance visual appeal, making your presentation more engaging and effective.
