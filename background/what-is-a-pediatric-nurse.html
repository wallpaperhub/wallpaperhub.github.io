Understanding Pediatric Nursing: A Special Care for Kids

A pediatric nurse is a specialized healthcare professional who focuses on the medical needs of infants, children, and adolescents. They provide essential care, administer medications, and support families through various health challenges. Pediatric nurses play a crucial role in promoting healthy growth and development in young patients.
