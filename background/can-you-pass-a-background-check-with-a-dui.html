Can You Pass a Background Check with a DUI?

Passing a background check with a DUI on your record is possible but depends on various factors. Employers may consider the time elapsed since the offense, the nature of the job, and your overall record. It's crucial to be honest about your DUI and demonstrate personal growth since then to improve your chances.
