George Washington: The Founding Father

George Washington, born on February 22, 1732, in Virginia, was a pivotal figure in American history. He served as the commander-in-chief of the Continental Army during the American Revolutionary War and became the first President of the United States. His leadership, vision, and commitment to democracy laid the foundation for the nation.
