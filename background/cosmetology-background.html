Understanding Cosmetology: A Comprehensive Overview

Cosmetology is the study and application of beauty treatments, encompassing skin care, hair styling, and makeup artistry. This profession blends artistry with science, requiring extensive training in techniques, safety, and client consultation. Aspiring cosmetologists often pursue formal education to gain skills and certification in this dynamic field.
