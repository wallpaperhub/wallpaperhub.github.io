Discover Abraham: The Father of Faith in the Bible

Abraham, a central figure in the Bible, is revered as the father of faith. His story, found mainly in Genesis, illustrates his covenant with God, showcasing his journey from Ur to Canaan. Known for his unwavering faith and obedience, Abraham's significance extends to Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, highlighting his enduring legacy.
