Embrace the Power of Ambient Sound for Better Focus

Ambient sound refers to the background noise present in any environment, often unnoticed until it's absent. It sets the mood, enhances experiences, and can influence emotions. From the rustling of leaves to city traffic, ambient sound plays a crucial role in our perception of space, promoting relaxation or focus in various settings.
