Is Kia a Japanese Car Brand? Discover the Truth!

Kia is not a Japanese brand; it is a South Korean automobile manufacturer. Founded in 1944, Kia operates through its headquarters in Seoul, South Korea. The company has made a name for itself by producing reliable and affordable vehicles, gaining a significant presence globally and becoming part of the Hyundai Motor Group.
