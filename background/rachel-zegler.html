Rachel Zegler: Rising Star of Hollywood

Rachel Zegler is an American singer and actress, celebrated for her role as Maria in Steven Spielberg's 2021 adaptation of "West Side Story." Born on May 3, 2001, she first gained fame through her viral YouTube performances. Zegler’s talent and charisma have quickly made her a rising star in Hollywood, captivating audiences worldwide.
