Understanding Level 2 Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A Level 2 background check is a comprehensive screening process typically used to assess individuals for employment or sensitive positions. It includes criminal history, fingerprinting, and checks against federal databases. This level of scrutiny helps employers ensure candidates pose no threat and uphold workplace safety and integrity.
