Charming Gingham Patterns: Style and Versatility

Gingham pattern is a timeless textile design characterized by its checks, alternating between two colors, often white and another vibrant hue. Originating from the 17th century, it has been a staple in fashion and home decor, evoking a sense of rustic charm and casual elegance. Gingham is perfect for summer picnics, shirts, and tablecloths!
