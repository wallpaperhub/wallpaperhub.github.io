Discover the Basics of the Baseball Diamond

A baseball diamond is a meticulously designed field that forms the heart of the game. Shaped like a diamond, it consists of four bases: first, second, third, and home plate. Players take turns batting and fielding, creating an exciting dynamic. Its grass infield and dirt base paths offer a unique challenge, making baseball a beloved sport worldwide.
