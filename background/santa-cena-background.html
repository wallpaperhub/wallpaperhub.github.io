The Significance of Santa Cena: An Insight into the Last Supper

Santa Cena, or the Last Supper, is a significant biblical event depicting Jesus’ final meal with his apostles before his crucifixion. This moment is captured in various art forms, notably in Leonardo da Vinci's renowned mural. The narrative explores themes of betrayal, sacrifice, and communion, embodying the essence of Christian faith.
