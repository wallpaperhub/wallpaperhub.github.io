Will They Find Out About Your Background Check?

If you conduct a background check on someone, they may or may not find out, depending on how discreet you are. Most people are unaware that checks can occur, especially if done by employers or landlords. However, if the person has legitimate reasons to suspect a check, they might inquire about it.
