"Discover Tan France: Fashion Icon & 'Queer Eye' Star"

With his unique sense of style and warm personality, he helps people transform their wardrobes and self-confidence. Born in England to Pakistani parents, Tan's journey inspires many as he advocates for diversity in fashion.
