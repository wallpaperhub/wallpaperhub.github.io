Understanding Public Mugshots: Ethics & Impact

Public mugshots are official photographs taken by law enforcement during the booking process of a suspect. These images are often made available to the public through various online databases, leading to discussions about privacy, ethics, and accountability in the criminal justice system. They're used by media and individuals alike for various purposes, including background checks and personal research.
