Fascinating Penguin Facts: Uncovering the World of These Flightless Birds

Penguins are fascinating flightless birds, primarily found in the Southern Hemisphere, especially in Antarctica. Known for their distinctive black and white plumage, these social creatures thrive in cold environments. They are excellent swimmers, using their flippers to navigate through icy waters while hunting for fish and krill.
