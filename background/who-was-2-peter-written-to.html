Who Was 2 Peter Written To? Insights & Analysis

2 Peter is traditionally believed to have been written to a broad audience of Christians, particularly those facing challenges in their faith. The letter warns against false teachers and encourages believers to grow in their knowledge of Jesus Christ. It was likely addressed to a network of early Christian communities.
