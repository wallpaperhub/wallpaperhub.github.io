Delicious Duo: The History of Peanut Butter and Jelly

Peanut butter and jelly (PB&J) has a rich history dating back to the 19th century. Peanut butter, invented in the 1880s, gained popularity as a protein source. Jelly, made from fruit preserves, complemented it perfectly. The classic sandwich emerged in American lunches by the 1900s, symbolizing comfort and nostalgia for many.
