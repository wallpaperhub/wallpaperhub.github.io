Exploring the Evolution of Instagram: From Launch to Social Media Giant

Instagram launched in October 2010, co-founded by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger. Initially focused on photo sharing, it gained rapid popularity, reaching 1 million users in just two months. Facebook acquired Instagram in 2012, expanding features to include Stories, Reels, and IGTV, solidifying its position as a leader in social media.
