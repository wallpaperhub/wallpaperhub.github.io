Unraveling Money Heist: The Story Behind the Series

Money Heist, originally titled "La Casa de Papel," is a Spanish heist crime drama series created by Álex Pina. Set in Madrid, it follows a masterful criminal known as "The Professor" who orchestrates a meticulously planned heist on the Royal Mint of Spain, involving a group of skilled robbers. The show's complex characters and thrilling plot twists have captivated audiences worldwide.
