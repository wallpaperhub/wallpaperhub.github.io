Discover the Meaning Behind the Toyota Logo

The Toyota logo is a well-recognized emblem featuring three overlapping ovals, symbolizing the unification of mind, body, and heart. The design represents Toyota's commitment to innovation and quality, reflecting its global reach and responsibility. The logo has evolved since its inception in 1936, becoming iconic in the automotive industry.
