Discover Nicaragua: Geography & Highlights

Nicaragua is a central American country bordered by Honduras to the north and Costa Rica to the south. It boasts stunning natural landscapes, including lakes, volcanoes, and beautiful beaches along the Pacific and Caribbean coasts. The capital city is Managua, which serves as the political and cultural center of the nation.
