Understanding the Marine Corps: History & Values

The U.S. Marine Corps is one of the most prestigious military branches, founded in 1775. Known for their rigorous training and commitment to excellence, Marines are often the first to respond in crises. Their rich history is steeped in valor and honor, with many significant engagements showcasing their bravery and tactical expertise.
