Dive into Tiki Culture: A Tropical Escape

Tiki culture originated in the mid-20th century, inspired by Polynesian art and the allure of tropical islands. It gained popularity through tiki bars, decor, and cocktails, evoking a sense of escape and adventure. The vibrant carvings, lush motifs, and exotic drinks celebrate a carefree lifestyle, making tiki a beloved theme for parties and gatherings.
