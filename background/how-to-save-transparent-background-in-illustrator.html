How to Save an Image with Transparent Background in Illustrator

To save an image with a transparent background in Illustrator, first, ensure your artwork is on a layer without a background. Then, go to 'File' > 'Export' > 'Export As'. Choose PNG format, check the 'Use Artboards' option and select 'Transparent' for the background. Click 'OK' to save your file preserving transparency.
