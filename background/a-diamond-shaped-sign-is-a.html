Understanding Diamond-Shaped Traffic Signs

A diamond-shaped sign is a critical traffic signal that indicates caution or warning. Often seen at intersections or in areas requiring drivers to slow down, these signs guide motorists to be aware of potential hazards or changes in road conditions. Understanding these signs enhances safety for all road users.
