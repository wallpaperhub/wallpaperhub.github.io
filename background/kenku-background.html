“Exploring Kenku Background: Secrets of the Avian Rogue”

Kenku are a fascinating race in Dungeons & Dragons, known for their raven-like appearance and mimicry skills. Typically shunned by society, they embrace a life of adaptability and stealth, often becoming skilled thieves or spies. Their unique culture revolves around oral tradition, as they cannot speak original words but replicate sounds they’ve heard.
