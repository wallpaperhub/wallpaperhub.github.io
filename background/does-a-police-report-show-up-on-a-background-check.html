Does a Police Report Appear in Background Checks?

A police report may show up on a background check depending on the type of check conducted and the content of the report. Typically, standard background checks for employment or rental purposes may not include minor incidents. However, more comprehensive checks, especially those for specific professional licenses, might reveal police reports related to criminal activity.
