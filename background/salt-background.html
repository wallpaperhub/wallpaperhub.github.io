Understanding the Significance of Salt: A Historical Perspective

Salt, a vital mineral, has been essential to human civilization for thousands of years. Its use spans from culinary to preserving food, and it plays a critical role in health as a source of sodium. The history of salt mining and trade has profoundly influenced economies and cultures globally, making it a remarkable and multifaceted substance.
