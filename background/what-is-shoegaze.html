What is Shoegaze Music? Discover the Sound!

Shoegaze is a subgenre of alternative rock that emerged in the late 1980s, characterized by its ethereal soundscapes, heavy use of guitar effects, and introspective lyrics. Bands like My Bloody Valentine and Slowdive create a dreamy, immersive experience, often blending vocals with layers of distorted guitars, evoking a heightened emotional atmosphere.
