How to Change Background Color in DaVinci Resolve: A Step-by-Step Guide

To change the background color in DaVinci Resolve, first select the clip in your timeline. Navigate to the “Color” workspace and open the “Nodes” panel. Use the “Solid Color” effect by adding a new node. Adjust the color to your preference. Finally, ensure the new background is visible behind your main clip for a seamless look.
