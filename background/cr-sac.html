Understanding CR Sac: Key Insights

CR sac refers to a critical structure within our body, particularly associated with the brain's cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) system. It plays a vital role in supporting the brain, cushioning it against trauma, and maintaining intracranial pressure. Understanding the CR sac's function is essential for comprehending various neurological conditions.
