Walmart: A Brief History of Retail Innovation

Walmart was founded by Sam Walton in 1962 in Rogers, Arkansas, revolutionizing retail with a focus on low prices and exceptional customer service. Expanding rapidly, Walmart became a leader in discount merchandising, pioneering the use of technology for inventory management. Today, it operates thousands of stores worldwide, remaining a key player in global retail.
