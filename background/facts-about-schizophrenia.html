Understanding Schizophrenia: Key Facts & Insights

Schizophrenia is a chronic mental disorder affecting 1 in 100 people globally. Symptoms include hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking, often emerging in late adolescence or early adulthood. Treatment usually combines medication and therapy, promoting recovery and improving quality of life for many affected individuals.
