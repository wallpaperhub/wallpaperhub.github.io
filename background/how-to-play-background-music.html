How to Play Background Music Effectively

To play background music, choose a music source like a streaming service or playlist. Adjust the volume to a low, unobtrusive level that complements the main activity. Ensure the music genre aligns with the setting—soft for relaxation, upbeat for gatherings. Test the sound to maintain the right balance for engagement.
