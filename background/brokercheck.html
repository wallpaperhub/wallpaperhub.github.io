“Know Your Broker: Explore BrokerCheck for Investor Safety”

BrokerCheck is a free tool provided by FINRA that allows investors to assess the backgrounds and qualifications of brokers and broker-dealers. With BrokerCheck, users can access important information such as employment history, regulatory actions, and customer complaints, helping them make informed decisions before investing.
