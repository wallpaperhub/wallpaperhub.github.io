Optimize Your Background Images with Srcset

Using the "srcset" attribute for background images allows developers to specify multiple resolution images for different screen sizes. This enhances load times and image quality on varying devices, ensuring a responsive design. By tailoring images to the viewing environment, websites can provide an optimal user experience across all platforms.
