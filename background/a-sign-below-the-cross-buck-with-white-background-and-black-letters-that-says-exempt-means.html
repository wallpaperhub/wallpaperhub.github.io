Understanding the 'Exempt' Railroad Sign

A sign with a white background and black letters that says 'exempt' below the cross buck indicates that certain vehicles, such as those belonging to emergency services or government agencies, are not required to stop at a railroad crossing. This exemption helps streamline traffic for specific authorized vehicles while maintaining safety for others.
