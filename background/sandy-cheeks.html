Sandy Cheeks: The Brave Texas Squirrel of Bikini Bottom

Sandy Cheeks is a beloved character from the animated series SpongeBob SquarePants. A brainy squirrel from Texas, she lives underwater in a treedome and is known for her adventurous spirit, karate skills, and scientific know-how. Sandy's unique perspective brings humor and lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of staying true to oneself.
