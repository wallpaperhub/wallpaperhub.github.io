Tory Burch: Fashion Designer & Philanthropist

Tory Burch is an American fashion designer known for her preppy-bohemian style, luxury handbags, and iconic logo. Born on May 17, 1966, she launched her eponymous brand in 2004, quickly gaining popularity with her classic yet modern designs. Burch is also committed to philanthropy, supporting women's empowerment through the Tory Burch Foundation.
