Understanding the Marine Corps Flag: Significance and History

The Marine Corps flag, adorned with the emblem of an eagle, globe, and anchor, represents the legacy and values of the United States Marine Corps. Its crimson background symbolizes courage, while the gold signifies honor. This flag is not just a symbol of military might but also of camaraderie, dedication, and the enduring spirit of Marines.
