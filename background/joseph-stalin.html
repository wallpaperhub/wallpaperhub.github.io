Understanding Joseph Stalin: Impact and Legacy

Joseph Stalin was the General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union from the mid-1920s until his death in 1953. He transformed the USSR through industrialization and collectivization, but his regime was marked by widespread repression, purges, and the Great Terror. Stalin's legacy remains controversial, shaping global politics significantly.
