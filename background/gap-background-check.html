Understanding Gap Background Checks: Essential Insights

A gap background check is a process used to investigate an individual's history during periods of employment gaps. Employers may conduct these checks to ensure the candidate's honesty and integrity. It can include verifying past employment, education, and reasons for any unexplained breaks. Understanding how to navigate and present these gaps is essential for job seekers.
