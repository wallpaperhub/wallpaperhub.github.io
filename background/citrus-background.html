The Rich History of Citrus Fruits

Citra fruits, like oranges, lemons, and limes, belong to the Rutaceae family, revered for their vibrant flavors and health benefits. Originating in Southeast Asia, citrus has expanded worldwide, enriching cuisines and cultures. Packed with vitamin C and antioxidants, they promote wellness and add zest to dishes and beverages.
