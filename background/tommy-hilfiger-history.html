Tommy Hilfiger: A Brief History of an American Fashion Icon

Tommy Hilfiger, founded in 1985, revolutionized American fashion with his preppy, classic designs that blend pop culture and sportswear. The brand quickly gained global recognition, becoming an icon for casual luxury. Over the years, it expanded into various categories, including denim, accessories, and fragrances, maintaining its commitment to quality and style.
