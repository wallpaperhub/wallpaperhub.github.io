Celebrate Pi Day: Fun Activities & Background

Pi Day, celebrated on March 14th, honors the mathematical constant π (pi), approximately 3.14. This day is used to promote interest in mathematics and highlight the importance of pi in various fields. Schools and math enthusiasts celebrate with activities like pie-eating contests, lectures, and math challenges, making learning fun.
