Understanding the Background of Shark Tank: A Guide

Shark Tank is a popular reality TV series that premiered in 2009, showcasing aspiring entrepreneurs pitching their business ideas to a panel of wealthy investors known as "sharks." The goal is to secure investment and guidance. Each episode features diverse businesses, demonstrating innovation and entrepreneurship in action.
