"Essential Guide to California ID Card: Front & Back Details"

The California ID card features vital information on both the front and back. The front displays your name, date of birth, ID number, and a photo, while the back contains a barcode and additional data for verification. Understanding its layout is essential for identification purposes and smooth transactions in daily life.
