Essential Guide to Conducting a Literature Review

To conduct a literature review, start by defining a clear research question or topic. Gather relevant sources, such as academic journals, books, and articles. Analyze and synthesize the findings, noting key themes and gaps. Organize your review logically, summarizing each source’s contribution and relevance to your research. Finally, cite your sources correctly.
