Explore the Fascinating History of Apples

Apples have a rich history dating back thousands of years, originating in the Central Asian mountains. Cultivated for their taste and versatility, they spread globally through trade and exploration. Varieties thrived in different climates, influencing culinary traditions. Today, apples symbolize health and are a staple fruit worldwide.
