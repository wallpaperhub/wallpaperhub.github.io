Ignite Your Adventure: Folk Heroes in D&D 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, Folk Heroes are characters ingrained in the culture of their communities. Often celebrated for their humble origins and courageous deeds, they inspire others through tales of bravery. With the ability to foster bonds and stand against tyranny, Folk Heroes embody the spirit of the people, carving out a legacy that echoes through the ages.
