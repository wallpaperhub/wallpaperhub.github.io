Understanding Peter Pan Syndrome: Why Some Adults Refuse to Grow Up

Peter Pan Syndrome refers to adults who resist growing up and refuse to take on adult responsibilities. Often characterized by a carefree attitude, these individuals may struggle with commitment, maturity, and self-reliance. The syndrome can impact personal relationships and career advancement, creating a cycle of dependence and avoidance.
