Discovering R&B: The Evolution of Rhythm and Blues

R&B (Rhythm and Blues) has a rich history, emerging in the 1940s from African American music genres like jazz and blues. It combines soulful melodies with emotive lyrics, often exploring love and personal experiences. Over the decades, R&B has evolved, influencing pop and hip-hop, giving rise to modern artists while retaining its core essence.
