Wiener Dog: History, Traits, and Care Tips

The Wiener Dog, or Dachshund, originated in Germany, named for its distinctive long body and short legs. Bred as hunting dogs, they were excellent at burrowing into badger dens. Their playful and stubborn nature makes them popular companions today. Recognized for their loyalty and affectionate demeanor, Wiener Dogs are cherished family pets.
