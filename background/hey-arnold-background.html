"Discovering the World of Hey Arnold!"

"Hey Arnold!" is an iconic animated series that aired from 1996 to 2004, created by Craig Bartlett. Set in a vibrant, urban neighborhood, the show follows Arnold, a kind-hearted schoolboy with a football-shaped head, as he navigates childhood adventures and friendships. Its unique art style combines retro and contemporary elements, resonating with both children and adults alike.
