Can Someone Run a Background Check on You Without Consent?

Yes, someone can run a background check without your permission, depending on the purpose and jurisdiction. Employers, landlords, or others may conduct checks for legitimate reasons, but they often need to comply with laws like the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). Always be aware of your rights regarding privacy and consent.
