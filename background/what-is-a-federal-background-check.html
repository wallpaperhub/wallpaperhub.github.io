Understanding Federal Background Checks: A Complete Guide

A federal background check is a thorough screening process that verifies an individual's criminal history and other relevant information at the federal level. Conducted by agencies like the FBI, it examines records for arrests, convictions, and other legal matters. Employers often use these checks to ensure a safe and trustworthy workforce.
