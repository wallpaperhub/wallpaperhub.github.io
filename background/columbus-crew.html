Columbus Crew: Heart of Ohio Soccer

Columbus Crew, an iconic soccer team in Major League Soccer (MLS), boasts a rich history, passionate fanbase, and a distinctive yellow-and-black color scheme. Established in 1996, they play at Lower.com Field in Columbus, Ohio, and have won multiple championships. Their exciting matches and community involvement make them a beloved part of the city.
