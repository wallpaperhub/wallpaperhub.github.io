The Wiz: A Bold Reimagining of Oz

The Wiz is a groundbreaking 1975 musical that reimagines L. Frank Baum's classic "The Wonderful Wizard of Oz" with an all-Black cast. Set in a vibrant, urban environment, it infuses elements of soul, R&B, and funk into its soundtrack. The story follows Dorothy’s journey to find her way home, encountering memorable characters along the way.
