Understanding the Significance of Lei in Hawaiian Culture

Lei, a traditional Hawaiian garland, symbolizes love, friendship, and celebration. Made from a variety of materials like flowers, leaves, and shells, leis are worn during special occasions such as graduations, weddings, and luaus. Their vibrant colors and fragrant aromas embody the spirit of aloha, creating lasting memories for both givers and recipients.
