Customize Your Mac's Login Screen: A Quick Guide

To change the login screen on your Mac, go to System Preferences and select "Users & Groups." Click the lock icon to make changes, then select "Login Options." From here, you can customize the display name, set a different login window appearance, or even change your profile picture. Make your Mac feel unique!
