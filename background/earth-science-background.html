Understanding Earth Science: Key Insights

Earth science encompasses the study of the Earth's structure, processes, and the interactions within its systems, including the atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere, and geosphere. This field investigates phenomena such as weather patterns, climate change, geological formations, and natural resources, playing a crucial role in understanding our planet's past, present, and future.
