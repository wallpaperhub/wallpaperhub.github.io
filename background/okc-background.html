Explore the Vibrant Background of Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City, the state capital of Oklahoma, has a rich history intertwined with Native American cultures and the Westward Expansion era. Founded during the Land Run of 1889, it blossomed as a hub for oil and agriculture. Its vibrant arts scene, sports teams, and diverse neighborhoods reflect a dynamic urban landscape.
