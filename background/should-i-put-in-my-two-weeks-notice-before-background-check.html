Should I Quit Before a Background Check?

Putting in your two weeks' notice before a background check can be a tricky decision. While it's tempting to signal your commitment to leaving, consider if the job offer is secure. Background checks can take time, and should issues arise, you may lose both your old and new job. Weigh your options carefully before proceeding.
