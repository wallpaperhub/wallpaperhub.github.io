Essential Background: Setting the Scene

Giving background is essential for contextualizing a subject or story. It provides the necessary information to help the audience understand the setting, relevancy, and significance. By outlining key elements such as history, culture, or previous events, a strong background fosters engagement and enriches comprehension, guiding the audience through the narrative smoothly.
