Juneteenth: Celebrating Freedom and Equality

Juneteenth, celebrated on June 19, commemorates the emancipation of enslaved African Americans in the United States. Marking the day when the Union soldiers announced the end of slavery in Texas in 1865, it honors freedom, resilience, and the ongoing fight for equality. The day is celebrated with parades, educational events, and cultural festivities.
