How to Fix AirPods Picking Up Background Noise

AirPods are popular for their convenience, but many users report issues with background noise. The built-in microphones can inadvertently pick up ambient sounds, affecting call clarity and listening experiences. To mitigate this, consider using noise-cancellation features, adjusting fit, or finding quieter environments.
