Understanding Conviction vs. Arrest: Key Legal Differences

Conviction and arrest are key terms in the criminal justice system. An arrest occurs when law enforcement detains an individual suspected of a crime, while a conviction follows a trial where it's proven the person committed that crime. Understanding the difference is crucial for navigating legal procedures and rights.
