Understanding Motor Vehicle Reports: What You Need to Know

A motor vehicle report (MVR) is a document that provides a comprehensive history of a driver's records, including driving violations, accidents, and license status. Often requested by employers, insurance companies, and legal entities, the MVR helps assess an individual's driving behavior and risk level, ensuring informed decisions are made around driving privileges and safety.
