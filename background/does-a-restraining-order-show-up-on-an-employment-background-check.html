Do Restraining Orders Appear on Employment Background Checks?

A restraining order can potentially show up on an employment background check, depending on the type of check conducted. Employers typically review criminal records, which may include restraining orders. However, not all checks are the same, and some may focus more on job-related inquiries than on personal history.
