What Landlords Seek in a Tenant Background Check

Landlords primarily look for reliable tenants through background checks. They assess credit history to gauge financial responsibility, criminal records for safety concerns, and rental history for previous landlord relations. Employment verification is also crucial, ensuring tenants can meet monthly rent obligations.
