Endangered Animals: 10 Must-Know Facts

Endangered animals are species at risk of extinction due to habitat loss, poaching, and climate change. Notable examples include the Amur leopard, with only 100 left in the wild, and the Vaquita, a small porpoise nearing extinction with just 30 remaining. Protecting them is vital for biodiversity and ecological balance.
