Discover the Rich History of Wendy's Fast Food

Wendy's was founded by Dave Thomas in 1969 in Columbus, Ohio. Renowned for its square hamburgers and fresh ingredients, the brand revolutionized the fast-food industry with its focus on quality. Over the years, it expanded globally, introducing innovative menu items and a lovable mascot, Wendy, becoming a household name.
