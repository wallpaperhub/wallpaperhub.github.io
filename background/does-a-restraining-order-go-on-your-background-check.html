Does a Restraining Order Show Up on Background Checks?

A restraining order can appear on a background check, especially if it leads to criminal charges or is a part of the public record. However, the visibility can depend on various factors including the type of background check conducted. It's crucial to understand how these orders might impact your legal and employment status.
