Understanding Tics: Background and Insights

Tics are sudden, repetitive movements or sounds that can be categorized into motor and vocal tics. They are common in childhood and are often associated with conditions like Tourette syndrome. Understanding their background is crucial for effective management, as tics can be influenced by genetics, environment, and stress.
