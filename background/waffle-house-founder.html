The Legacy of Waffle House Founders: Joe Rogers Sr. & Tom Forkner

The Waffle House was founded by Joe Rogers Sr. and Tom Forkner in 1955 in Avondale Estates, Georgia. Their vision was to create a restaurant that served affordable, quality food 24/7. With a focus on simple, delicious waffles and a welcoming atmosphere, Waffle House has grown into a beloved American dining staple.
