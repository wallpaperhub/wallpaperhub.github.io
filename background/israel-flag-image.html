Stunning Israel Flag Image: Symbol of Heritage

The Israel flag features a blue Star of David between two horizontal blue stripes on a white background. Symbolizing Jewish identity and heritage, the flag was officially adopted in 1948. Its colors are said to represent the sky and sea, connecting the Jewish people to their historical and spiritual roots.
