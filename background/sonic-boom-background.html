Understanding Sonic Booms: What You Need to Know

Sonic booms occur when an object travels faster than the speed of sound, creating a shock wave that produces a loud noise. This phenomenon is often associated with supersonic aircraft like fighter jets. Factors influencing sonic booms include altitude, speed, and atmospheric conditions. Understanding these factors is key to mitigating their impact in populated areas.
