Discover the Rich History of Glacier National Park

Glacier National Park, established in 1910, showcases stunning landscapes shaped by ancient glaciers. With over 700 miles of trails, it attracts adventurers worldwide. The park's rich history includes Native American heritage and early exploration, preserving unique ecosystems and diverse wildlife for future generations.
