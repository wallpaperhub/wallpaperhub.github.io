Unleashing the Game: Jessica Pegula's Rise in Tennis

Jessica Pegula is an American professional tennis player born on February 24, 1994. Known for her powerful baseline game and competitive spirit, Pegula has achieved significant success on the WTA tour. She's also recognized for her philanthropic efforts and comes from a wealthy sports background, as her parents own the NFL's Buffalo Bills.
