Discover the Visionary Art of Pablo Picasso

Pablo Picasso was a Spanish painter, sculptor, and one of the most influential artists of the 20th century. His pioneering contributions to modern art include co-founding the Cubist movement and exploring various styles. Notable works like "Guernica" and "Les Demoiselles d'Avignon" showcase his innovative techniques and bold visions.
