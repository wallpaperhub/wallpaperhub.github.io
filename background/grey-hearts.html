Understanding the Meaning of Grey Hearts

Grey hearts are a symbol of neutrality and balance, often used to convey feelings of love that are complex or undefined. They can represent friendship, emotional support, or the blending of emotions. In digital communication, a grey heart adds a layer of depth, suggesting subtlety and a spectrum of feelings beyond mere romance.
