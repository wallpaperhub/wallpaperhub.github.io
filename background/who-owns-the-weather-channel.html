Who Owns The Weather Channel? Insights & Details

The Weather Channel is owned by a subsidiary of the entertainment conglomerate, Warner Bros. Discovery. Originally launched in 1982, the channel has undergone several ownership changes, with major companies like NBCUniversal and The Weather Company also being involved. It continues to provide national and local weather updates and forecasts.
