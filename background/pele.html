Pele: The King of Football

Pele, known as "The King of Football," is regarded as one of the greatest soccer players of all time. His dazzling skills, unmatched goal-scoring ability, and three World Cup victories (1958, 1962, and 1970) solidified his legacy. Beyond the pitch, Pele's influence transcends sports, inspiring generations worldwide.
