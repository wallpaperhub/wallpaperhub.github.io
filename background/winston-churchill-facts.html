Essential Facts About Winston Churchill

Winston Churchill, the British Prime Minister during WWII, was known for his fierce oratory and leadership. Born in 1874, he served in both World Wars and played a crucial role in the Allies' victory. Churchill was awarded the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1953 for his historical writings. He passed away in 1965.
