How to Make Image Background Transparent in Google Slides

To make an image background transparent in Google Slides, select the image you want to edit. Click on "Format options" in the toolbar, then choose "Adjustments." Use the transparency slider to modify the transparency level according to your needs. This allows you to create a clean and polished presentation by highlighting key elements.
