Hells Canyon Massacre: A Dark Chapter in History

The Hells Canyon Massacre refers to a tragic incident in 1887 where eight Chinese miners were brutally murdered in Oregon's Hells Canyon. This event highlighted the rampant racism and violence faced by Chinese immigrants during the era. The murders remain a haunting reminder of injustice and the struggle for equality in American history.
