Understanding Light Sleeping in Dogs: Nature's Calm

Light sleeping dogs often exhibit a serene expression, their gentle breathing providing a rhythmic backdrop to quiet moments. This behavior is common among many breeds, as dogs feel safe and secure in their environment. Light sleep is crucial for their mental and physical health, allowing them to recharge and remain playful when awake.
