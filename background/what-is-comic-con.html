What is Comic-Con? Ultimate Guide to the Pop Culture Odyssey

Comic-Con is an annual event celebrating comic books, movies, TV shows, and pop culture. Fans gather to meet creators, attend panels, and explore a vast array of merchandise. It's a vibrant hub for cosplay, networking, and viewing sneak peeks of upcoming releases. Ideal for enthusiasts, it's a must-visit for any geek culture lover!
