Discover the Unique White Skunk: Nature's Snowy Marvel

White skunks, often mistaken for common skunks, are unique due to their striking white fur, which serves as camouflage in snowy environments. These fascinating creatures are less common than their black counterparts. Their behavior, diet, and social interactions can vary, but they are primarily nocturnal foragers, playing a vital role in their ecosystems.
