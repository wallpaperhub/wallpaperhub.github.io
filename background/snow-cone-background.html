Discover the Sweet History of Snow Cones

Snow cones, a delightful summer treat, have a history rooted in ice harvesting. Originating in the early 19th century, street vendors began shaven ice topped with flavored syrups. Their vibrant colors and refreshing taste made them a favorite at fairs and parties. Today, snow cones symbolize summer fun and nostalgia, evoking memories of childhood joy.
