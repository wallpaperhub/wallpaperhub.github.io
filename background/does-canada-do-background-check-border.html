Background Checks at Canada’s Borders: What You Need to Know

Yes, Canada conducts background checks at its borders. When entering Canada, travelers are screened for criminal records, previous immigration violations, and other security risks. This process helps ensure public safety and maintain the integrity of Canada's immigration system. It's essential for travelers to be aware of this to avoid complications.
