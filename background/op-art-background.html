Mesmerizing Op Art Backgrounds: Explore Visual Illusions

Op art, or optical art, utilizes visual effects to create illusions of movement and three-dimensionality. Artists manipulate shapes, lines, and colors to deceive the eye, resulting in mesmerizing backgrounds that engage viewers. This art form often plays with perception, challenging conventions and inviting interpretation.
