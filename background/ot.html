Unlocking Potential: The Power of Occupational Therapy

OT, or occupational therapy, focuses on enabling individuals to participate in daily activities despite physical, emotional, or cognitive challenges. By using therapeutic techniques and adaptive equipment, OTs help people recover, improve, and maintain their independence. This holistic approach enhances quality of life for all ages.
