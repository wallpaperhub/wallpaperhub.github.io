Discover Fascinating Facts About Queen Elizabeth I

Queen Elizabeth I, the last Tudor monarch, reigned from 1558 to 1603, known as the Elizabethan Era. She was the daughter of Henry VIII and Anne Boleyn. Famous for her intelligence and political acumen, she never married, earning the title “The Virgin Queen.” Elizabeth’s reign saw flourishing arts, with Shakespeare at the fore.
