When Was the FAA Created? Key Dates in Aviation

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) was created on August 23, 1958, through the Federal Aviation Act. This significant legislation was established to ensure safe and efficient air travel in the United States, overseeing civil aviation practices, air traffic control, and aviation safety measures. The FAA continues to play a vital role in aviation management.
