Discover the Exciting History of Field Days

Field day origins trace back to agricultural fairs, promoting local farming and community spirit. Initially aimed at showcasing crops, livestock, and farming innovation, these events now encompass various recreational activities and educational workshops. Today, field days serve as a platform for families and friends to connect while celebrating traditional practices.
