Unlocking Learning: Activate Background Knowledge

Activating background knowledge is essential for effective learning. By connecting new information to prior experiences, learners enhance comprehension and retention. Techniques include asking questions, using graphic organizers, and facilitating discussions that encourage learners to relate concepts to their existing knowledge, making lessons more meaningful.
