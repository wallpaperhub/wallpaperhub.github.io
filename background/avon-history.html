Discover Avon: A Legacy of Beauty and Empowerment

Avon was founded in 1886 by David H. McConnell in New York City as a door-to-door selling company. Initially focused on perfumes, it quickly evolved, offering a diverse range of beauty and personal care products. Avon's innovative marketing strategy empowered women as sales representatives and continues to thrive globally, celebrating empowerment and entrepreneurship.
