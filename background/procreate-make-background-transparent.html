How to Make Background Transparent in Procreate

To make a background transparent in Procreate, begin by selecting the layer containing your artwork. Tap on the layer, then choose "Alpha Lock" or "Select" to isolate the desired area. Use the eraser tool or fill the background with a transparent color. Finally, export your image as a PNG to retain the transparency.
