Bible Church vs. Baptist Church: Key Differences Explained

Bible churches and Baptist churches differ primarily in their approach to Scripture and governance. Bible churches prioritize teaching straight from the Bible with a less formal structure, while Baptist churches emphasize baptism by immersion and typically have a congregational form of governance. Both evoke strong community values but vary in tradition and practice.
