Understanding Exposition: The Key to Great Storytelling

Exposition is a crucial element in storytelling that introduces essential characters, settings, and conflicts. It lays the groundwork for the narrative, helping audiences understand the context and stakes involved. A well-crafted exposition engages readers or viewers, drawing them into the story and setting the stage for unfolding events.
