Comprehensive Tenant Background Check Guide

To conduct a thorough background check on a tenant, start by obtaining their consent to run the check. Collect necessary personal information, including Social Security number and previous addresses. Use reputable screening services to check criminal records, credit scores, and eviction histories. Review the findings carefully to make an informed decision.
