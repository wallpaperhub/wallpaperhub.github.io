Understanding Gun Form: A Guide to Firearm Design

Gun form refers to the distinct configuration or design elements of firearms, impacting their function and use. It encompasses aspects like size, shape, and ergonomics, which are crucial for handling, control, and performance. Understanding gun form aids in selecting the right firearm for specific applications, ensuring safety and efficacy.
