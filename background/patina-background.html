Stunning Patina Backgrounds: Enhance Your Decor

Patina background refers to the unique, often colorful finish that develops on metals or surfaces over time due to oxidation. This natural aging process creates a visually stunning appearance, rich in character. Commonly seen in aged brass, copper, and bronze, patina adds an artistic touch to decor, art, and furniture, enhancing their aesthetic appeal.
