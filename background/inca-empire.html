Discover the Inca Empire: Culture and Achievements

The Inca Empire, flourishing in the 15th and early 16th centuries, was South America’s largest empire, centered in modern-day Peru. Known for its intricate architecture, including Machu Picchu, the Incas developed a sophisticated road system and advanced agricultural techniques. Their society thrived on trade, culture, and innovative governance.
