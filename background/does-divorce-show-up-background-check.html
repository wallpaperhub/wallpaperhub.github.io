Does Divorce Appear on Background Checks? Know the Facts!

Yes, a divorce may appear on a background check, depending on the type of check conducted. Criminal background checks typically do not include marital history, while civil records checks may reveal divorce proceedings. Employers or organizations that require thorough checks might view this information as part of assessing stability and reliability.
