Discover the Fascinating Background of Olivia

Olivia is a name of Latin origin meaning "olive tree." It symbolizes peace and beauty. Widely popular in literature and television, Olivia has been associated with various notable figures and characters. The name also represents qualities such as intelligence, creativity, and charm, making it a favored choice among parents worldwide.
