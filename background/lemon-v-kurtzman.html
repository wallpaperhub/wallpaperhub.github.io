Lemon v. Kurtzman: Key Supreme Court Case Explained

In Lemon v. Kurtzman (1971), the Supreme Court evaluated the constitutionality of state funding for private religious schools. The Court decided such funding violated the Establishment Clause of the First Amendment. This landmark case set a precedent for the separation of church and state, impacting school funding policies nationwide.
