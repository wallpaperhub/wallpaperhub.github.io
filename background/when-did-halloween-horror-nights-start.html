History of Halloween Horror Nights

Halloween Horror Nights started in 1991 at Universal Studios Hollywood as a special event designed to create a terrifying atmosphere with haunted houses, scare zones, and live entertainment. Since then, it has evolved into an annual tradition across multiple Universal parks, renowned for its elaborate mazes inspired by horror films and pop culture.
