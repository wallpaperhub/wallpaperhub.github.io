Exploring the Rich History of India

India's history spans thousands of years, marked by the rise and fall of empires such as the Maurya, Gupta, Mughal, and British. It showcases a rich tapestry of cultures, religions, and philosophies, with significant milestones including the Indus Valley Civilization, the birth of Hinduism and Buddhism, and the struggle for independence led by figures like Mahatma Gandhi.
