Understanding Radar Technology: A Comprehensive Overview

Radar technology originated during World War II for detecting aircraft, evolving since then into crucial applications in aviation, meteorology, and automotive industries. It uses radio waves to identify objects' distance, speed, and direction, enabling features like weather tracking and collision avoidance. This innovation continues to shape various sectors.
