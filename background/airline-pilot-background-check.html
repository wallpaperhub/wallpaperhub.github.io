Essential Airline Pilot Background Checks Explained

Airline pilot background checks are crucial for ensuring passenger safety. This process includes verifying a pilot's flight history, criminal record, and medical examinations. Airlines also conduct interviews and reference checks to assess candidates' qualifications and character. A thorough background check helps maintain industry standards and trust.
