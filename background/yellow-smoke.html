Understanding Yellow Smoke: Causes & Effects

Yellow smoke often signals the presence of pollutants or chemicals, raising concerns in various contexts such as industrial waste or environmental contamination. It's crucial to investigate the sources of this discoloration to safeguard public health and the environment. Awareness and actions can help mitigate risks associated with yellow smoke.
