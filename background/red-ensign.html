Understanding the Red Ensign: History and Significance

The Red Ensign is a flag traditionally associated with the United Kingdom's maritime history. Primarily used on civilian ships, it features a red background with the Union Jack in the canton. It symbolizes British maritime heritage and continues to be flown by numerous merchant vessels today, representing Britain in international waters.
