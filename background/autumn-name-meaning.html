Understanding the Meaning of Autumn: Symbolism & Significance

Autumn, derived from the Latin "autumnus," signifies the season of harvest and change. It symbolizes a time of transition, both in nature and in life, where leaves turn vibrant hues and crops are gathered. This season invites reflection, gratitude, and preparation for the quiet of winter, embodying beauty and transformation.
