Discover the Rich History of Minneapolis

Minneapolis, founded in the 1850s, grew rapidly due to its location along the Mississippi River and the milling industry. Known for its flour production, the city rose to prominence with the Mill District. Through industrialization and immigration, Minneapolis developed a diverse culture. Today, it embraces arts, nature, and history.
