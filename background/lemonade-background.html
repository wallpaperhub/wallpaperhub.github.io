Refreshing Lemonade: A Timeless Classic

Lemonade has a rich history, originating from ancient Egypt where it was enjoyed as a refreshing drink. Over centuries, it evolved, becoming popular in various cultures. In the 19th century, lemonade stands emerged in the U.S., creating a cherished childhood tradition. Today, it's a symbol of summer, refreshment, and joy, enjoyed globally.
