Buddhists and the Concept of God: What You Need to Know

Buddhism does not center around the belief in a creator god as seen in many theistic religions. Instead, it focuses on personal enlightenment, moral conduct, and the nature of suffering. Some Buddhists see divine beings or bodhisattvas, but they are not gods in the traditional sense, rather enlightened beings who aid in the path to Nirvana.
