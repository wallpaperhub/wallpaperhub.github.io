"Exploring the Goonies: Behind the Scenes of a Classic Adventure"

"The Goonies," a beloved adventure film released in 1985, was directed by Richard Donner and produced by Steven Spielberg. Set in Astoria, Oregon, the story follows a group of kids known as the Goonies who embark on a quest to find pirate treasure. The film explores themes of friendship, bravery, and childhood imagination and has become a cult classic over the years.
