Explore the Rich History of Medieval Towns

Medieval towns flourished during the Middle Ages, characterized by stone buildings, narrow winding streets, and marketplaces. Often fortified with walls and towers, they served as centers of trade and community life. Architecture featured churches, guild halls, and residences, reflecting the social hierarchy and bustling economic activity of the era.
