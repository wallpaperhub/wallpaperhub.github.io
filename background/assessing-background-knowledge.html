Assessing Background Knowledge: A Key to Effective Learning

Assessing background knowledge is essential in tailoring educational content to meet learners' needs. This process involves evaluating what students already know, helping educators identify gaps and build on existing knowledge. Effective assessments can foster engagement, enhance learning experiences, and drive student success.
