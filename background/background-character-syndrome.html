Understanding Background Character Syndrome: Overcoming Feelings of Invisibility

Background character syndrome refers to the phenomenon where individuals feel overlooked or undervalued in social settings, akin to a background character in stories. This can lead to feelings of isolation and low self-esteem, stemming from a lack of recognition or engagement in personal relationships or group dynamics. Awareness and proactive communication are key to overcoming these feelings.
