Toro: Behind the Brand and Its Legacy

Toro is a brand renowned for its innovation in outdoor equipment, particularly lawnmowers and other gardening tools. Established in 1914, it has become a staple for both residential and commercial users, emphasizing durability and performance. The company's heritage lies in delivering quality products for lawn care enthusiasts.
