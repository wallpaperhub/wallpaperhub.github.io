CORI Check vs. Background Check: What's the Difference?

A CORI check (Criminal Offender Record Information) and a background check are not the same, although they often overlap. A CORI check specifically examines an individual’s criminal history in Massachusetts, while a background check can encompass a broader range of information, including employment history, credit checks, and more, depending on the context.
