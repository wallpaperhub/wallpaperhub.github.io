Discover the Transformative Journey of Scrooge

Scrooge, a character from Charles Dickens' "A Christmas Carol," embodies the spirit of greed and isolation. Initially, he is a miser who rejects joy and generosity, but after being visited by three ghosts on Christmas Eve, he undergoes a profound transformation. By embracing kindness, Scrooge learns the true meaning of Christmas and redemption.
