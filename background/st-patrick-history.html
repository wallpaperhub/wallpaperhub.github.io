Discover the Rich History of St. Patrick

St. Patrick's Day, celebrated on March 17th, commemorates the life of St. Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland. Born in Roman Britain, he was kidnapped and brought to Ireland as a slave. After escaping, he returned to convert the Irish to Christianity. Symbols like the shamrock represent his teachings, making the day a celebration of Irish culture.
