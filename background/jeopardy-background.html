Explore the Rich History of Jeopardy!

Jeopardy! is an iconic American quiz show that first aired in 1964, known for its unique format where contestants respond to clues phrased as questions. Created by Merv Griffin, it features a distinctive game board, exciting trivia across various categories, and a competitive atmosphere. Its legacy includes numerous adaptations and dedicated fans.
