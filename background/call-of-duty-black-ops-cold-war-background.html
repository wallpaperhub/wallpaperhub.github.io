Explore the Background of Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War

Call of Duty: Black Ops Cold War is set during the early 1980s, amid the Cold War tensions between the U.S. and Soviet Union. Players engage in covert operations across various global locales, unraveling a conspiracy linked to the infamous Perseus. The game blends historical events with thrilling gameplay, offering both multiplayer and single-player experiences.
