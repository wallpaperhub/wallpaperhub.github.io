Understanding Orthodox Images: A Sacred Tradition

Orthodox images, or icons, hold profound spiritual significance in the Orthodox Christian tradition. These sacred artworks depict Jesus Christ, the Virgin Mary, and various saints, serving as windows into the divine. They are characterized by their intricate details, vibrant colors, and adherence to specific iconographic styles that connect believers to their faith.
