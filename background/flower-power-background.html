Embrace the Spirit of Flower Power: A Vibrant Background

Flower power, originating from the 1960s counterculture, symbolizes peace, love, and social change. It emerged as a vibrant response to war and societal issues, primarily associated with the hippie movement. Bright colors and floral motifs represent the belief in nature’s healing powers and the desire for a harmonious world, making it timelessly relevant.
