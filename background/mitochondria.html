Mitochondria: The Powerhouse of Cells Explained

Mitochondria, often referred to as the powerhouse of the cell, are essential organelles that generate adenosine triphosphate (ATP), the energy currency of cells. They also play a vital role in regulating metabolism, apoptosis, and heat production. Their health is linked to various diseases, making them a focus in cellular biology and medicine.
