"Understanding Script Background: Contexts that Shape Narratives"

A script’s background sets the stage for its narrative depth, providing context that shapes characters and plot. It involves the world, era, and underlying themes at play, enriching the storyline. Understanding this background allows actors and directors to bring authenticity and emotional resonance to performances, making the script more engaging.
