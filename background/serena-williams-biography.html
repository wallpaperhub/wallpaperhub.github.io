Discover the Inspiring Life of Serena Williams

Serena Williams, born on September 26, 1981, is an iconic American tennis player known for her powerful serve and remarkable achievements. Growing up in Compton, California, she began playing tennis at age four, rising through the ranks to become a 23-time Grand Slam champion. Her legacy extends beyond sports, advocating for gender equality and social justice.
