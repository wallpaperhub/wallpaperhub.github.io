Henry Ford: Pioneer of the Automotive Industry

Henry Ford, born in 1863, revolutionized the automotive industry by introducing assembly line production. As the founder of Ford Motor Company, he made cars affordable for the masses. His innovative methods, such as the $5 workday, transformed labor practices. Ford's legacy includes not just cars, but economic impact and a vision for a prosperous America.
