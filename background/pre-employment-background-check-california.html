Essential Guide to Pre-Employment Background Checks in California

In California, pre-employment background checks are crucial for employers. These checks can include criminal history, employment verification, and education confirmation. California law mandates that job candidates are informed and give consent before a background check. Understanding regulations and conducting fair checks fosters trust and transparency in the hiring process.
