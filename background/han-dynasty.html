Exploring the Han Dynasty: China's Golden Age

The Han Dynasty, spanning from 206 BCE to 220 CE, was a pivotal period in Chinese history marked by significant advancements in technology, culture, and trade. It established the Silk Road, connecting China to the West. The dynasty is known for its contributions to literature, philosophy, and governance, laying the foundation for future dynasties.
