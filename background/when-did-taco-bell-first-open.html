Taco Bell: A Brief History of Its First Opening

Taco Bell first opened its doors in Downey, California, on March 21, 1962. Founded by Glen Bell, the fast-food chain quickly became known for its unique twist on Mexican cuisine, serving items like tacos, burritos, and nachos. Its success led to rapid expansion across the United States and beyond.
