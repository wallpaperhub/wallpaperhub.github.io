Understanding ADHD Noise Sensitivity: Tips & Strategies

Individuals with ADHD often experience noise sensitivity, which can overwhelm their senses and make focusing difficult. This heightened awareness of sound can lead to anxiety, irritability, and frustration in noisy environments. Understanding and managing noise sensitivity is crucial for improving daily functioning and overall well-being in those with ADHD.
