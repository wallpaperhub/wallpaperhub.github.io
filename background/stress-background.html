Understanding Stress: Causes and Management

Stress is an emotional and physical response to challenging situations. It arises from various sources, including work pressure, personal issues, and environmental factors. While some stress can be motivating, excessive stress may lead to health problems like anxiety, depression, and cardiovascular issues. Understanding its causes and effects helps in managing stress effectively.
