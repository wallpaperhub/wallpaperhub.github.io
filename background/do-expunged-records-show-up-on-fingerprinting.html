Do Expunged Records Appear in Fingerprinting? Find Out!

Expunged records generally do not show up on fingerprint checks conducted for employment or licensing purposes. This means that if a record has been officially expunged, it should not be available to employers or licensing boards. However, some specific legal situations may allow certain agencies to access these records.
