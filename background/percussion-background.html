"The Heartbeat of Music: Exploring Percussion Backgrounds"

Percussion is a fundamental element of music, encompassing a variety of instruments that produce sound through striking, shaking, or scraping. From drums to tambourines, percussionists add rhythm and texture to compositions, enriching the overall sound. Emphasizing dynamics and timing, they are essential in both ensemble and solo performances.
