Spice Girls: The Iconic Pop Group That Changed Music

The Spice Girls, a British pop sensation formed in 1994, revolutionized music with their catchy hits and girl-power message. Comprising Mel B, Mel C, Emma, Geri, and Victoria, they took the world by storm with chart-toppers like "Wannabe" and "Spice Up Your Life." Their legacy continues to inspire generations!
