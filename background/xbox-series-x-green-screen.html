Fixing Xbox Series X Green Screen Issues

Experiencing a green screen on your Xbox Series X can be frustrating. This issue often occurs due to software glitches or hardware malfunctions. To troubleshoot, try restarting the console, checking the HDMI connections, or resetting display settings. If the problem persists, consider seeking professional support or updates from Xbox.
