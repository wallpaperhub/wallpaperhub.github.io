Unleashing the Power of Light in Destiny 2

In Destiny 2, Light represents the power of the Guardians, providing them with immense abilities to combat darkness across the galaxy. This energy is derived from the Traveler, a mysterious celestial entity. Players harness Light to regain strength, unlock subclasses, and wield various weapons, enhancing their gameplay experience. Understanding Light is crucial for mastering the game's intricate mechanics and engaging in epic battles.
