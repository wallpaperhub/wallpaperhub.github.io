Rick Astley: The Legend Behind 'Never Gonna Give You Up'

Rick Astley is a British singer-songwriter best known for his hit song "Never Gonna Give You Up," released in 1987. This catchy track topped charts around the world and became a cultural phenomenon in the 2000s due to the "Rickrolling" meme, where unsuspecting users are led to the music video. Astley's continued influence combines nostalgia with modern pop culture.
