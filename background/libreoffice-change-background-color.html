Change Background Color in LibreOffice: A Quick Guide

To change the background color in LibreOffice, open your document and navigate to the "Format" menu. Select "Page Style" and go to the "Area" tab. Here, you can choose a fill color for the background; simply click on the color box and make your selection. This customization enhances your document’s appearance and readability.
