Best Presentation Background Colors for Impact

When selecting the best color for a presentation background, opt for shades that enhance readability and engagement. Light backgrounds with dark text (like white or light gray with black lettering) are classic choices. Consider calming blues or greens to evoke trust, while avoiding overly bright colors that can distract from your message.
