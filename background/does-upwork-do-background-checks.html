Do Background Checks on Upwork? Here’s What You Need to Know

Upwork does not perform background checks on freelancers or clients. Instead, it relies on the community of users to report issues and maintain trust. Users can verify their identity, but it's up to clients and freelancers to conduct their due diligence through reviews, ratings, and communication before starting a project.
