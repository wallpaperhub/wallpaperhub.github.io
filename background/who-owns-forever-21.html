Who Owns Forever 21? Discover the Current Owners!

Forever 21, a popular fast-fashion retailer, is owned by Authentic Brands Group (ABG) and Simon Property Group. After filing for bankruptcy in 2019, the brand was purchased by these firms, aiming to revitalize its presence in the industry. The new ownership focuses on reducing debt and expanding e-commerce capabilities.
