Dollar Tree conducts background checks to ensure a safe workplace and maintain corporate integrity. These checks typically assess criminal history and employment verification. Potential employees should be prepared for this step in the hiring process, as it reflects Dollar Tree's commitment to personnel safety and legal compliance.
