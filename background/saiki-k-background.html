Unraveling Saiki K: The Life of a Psychic

Saiki K, short for "The Disastrous Life of Saiki K," is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Shuichi Asou. It follows Kusuo Saiki, a high school student with psychic powers who aims to live a normal life despite the chaos that his abilities bring. The humor and quirky character dynamics make it a fan favorite.
