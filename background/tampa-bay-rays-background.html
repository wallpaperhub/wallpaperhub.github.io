Tampa Bay Rays: A Brief Team Overview

The Tampa Bay Rays, established in 1998, are a Major League Baseball team based in St. Petersburg, Florida. Known for their innovative approach and strong farm system, the Rays have made multiple playoff appearances. Their home field, Tropicana Field, hosts a passionate fanbase, eager for championship success.
