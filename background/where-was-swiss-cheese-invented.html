Discovering the Origin of Swiss Cheese: Where It All Began

Swiss cheese, with its characteristic holes, originated in Switzerland, particularly in the cantons of Emmental and Appenzell. The cheese-making tradition dates back to the Middle Ages, and its unique flavor and texture come from the use of specific bacteria and the climate of the region. Today, Swiss cheese is loved worldwide.
