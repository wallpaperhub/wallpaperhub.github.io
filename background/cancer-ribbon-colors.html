Understanding Cancer Ribbon Colors: A Quick Guide

Cancer ribbon colors symbolize various cancer types, creating awareness and support for patients and their loved ones. Each color represents a specific cancer: pink for breast cancer, purple for pancreatic cancer, and gold for childhood cancer, among others. By wearing these ribbons, we unite against cancer and honor those affected.
