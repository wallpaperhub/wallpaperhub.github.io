Capturing Nature: Marsh Drawing Techniques

Marsh drawing is a captivating art form that captures the serene beauty of wetlands. Artists utilize various techniques to sketch vibrant flora and fauna found in marshes, highlighting their unique textures and colors. These drawings not only celebrate nature but also raise awareness about the importance of marsh ecosystems.
