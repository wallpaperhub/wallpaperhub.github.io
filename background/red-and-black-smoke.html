Understanding Red and Black Smoke: Safety Insights

Red and black smoke often symbolize danger and alarm in various contexts. Red smoke can indicate fire or alertness, while black smoke typically raises concerns about toxic fumes, suggesting incomplete combustion. Understanding these smoke colors is crucial for safety in industrial settings, fire management, and emergency protocols.
