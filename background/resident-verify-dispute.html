Resolving Resident Verify Disputes: Your Guide

Resident verify disputes occur when residents challenge the accuracy of their verification status. These disputes can arise due to inconsistencies in provided documents, identity theft, or clerical errors. Resolving these disputes requires clear communication and supporting evidence to rectify any misconceptions about residency status.
