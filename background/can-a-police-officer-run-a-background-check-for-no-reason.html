Can Police Officers Conduct Background Checks Without Just Cause?

Police officers typically cannot run a background check without a valid reason, such as investigating criminal activity or ensuring safety. Unauthorized checks may violate privacy laws and regulations. Citizens have protections under the law, and officers must adhere to guidelines when accessing sensitive personal data.
