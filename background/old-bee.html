Understanding the Role of Old Bees in the Hive

Old bees, often overlooked, are vital to their colonies. As they age, they shift from foraging to nurturing tasks. These seasoned workers possess extensive knowledge of floral resources, enhancing pollen collection. Their experience is crucial for the hive's survival, showcasing the importance of every bee, regardless of age.
