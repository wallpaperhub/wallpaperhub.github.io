Procreate: How to Use Transparent Backgrounds

Procreate allows artists to create stunning illustrations with ease. One essential feature is the ability to work with a transparent background. This enables your designs to seamlessly integrate into any project. To achieve this, simply select a new canvas, enable transparency, and let your creativity flow while maintaining crisp edges and perfect layering.
