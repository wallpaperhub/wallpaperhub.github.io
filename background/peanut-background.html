Explore the Fascinating Background of Peanuts

Peanuts, native to South America, are legumes popular worldwide for their rich flavor and nutritional benefits. They thrive in warm, sandy soils and are a staple in many diets. Beyond snacks, they contribute to various dishes and products like peanut butter and oil, highlighting their versatility and importance in cuisine.
