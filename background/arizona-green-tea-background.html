Arizona Green Tea: A Refreshing Beverage for Every Moment

Arizona Green Tea is a refreshing beverage that originated in 1992 as a blend of green tea, ginseng, and honey. Made by AriZona Beverages, it quickly gained popularity for its unique flavor and vibrant packaging. The brand promotes a healthy lifestyle and has become synonymous with casual, on-the-go drinking culture.
