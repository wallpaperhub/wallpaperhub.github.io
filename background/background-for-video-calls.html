Perfecting Your Video Call Background: Tips & Ideas

When setting up a background for video calls, consider simplicity and professionalism. A clean, clutter-free space enhances focus while minimizing distractions. Use soft colors that convey warmth or creativity, and avoid busy patterns. Adding personal touches like plants or artwork can enhance your presence without being intrusive.
