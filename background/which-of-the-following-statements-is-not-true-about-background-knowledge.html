Common Misconceptions About Background Knowledge

Background knowledge is essential for learning, but one misconception is that it is fixed and unchangeable. In reality, background knowledge can evolve through experience and exposure to new information. This flexibility allows individuals to build upon existing knowledge, making it dynamic rather than static.
