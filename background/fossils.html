Discovering Fossils: Insights into Earth's History

Fossils are remnants or traces of ancient life preserved in rock. They provide critical insights into Earth's history, showcasing evolution and past environments. Ranging from bone fragments to imprints of plants, fossils help scientists reconstruct the past, offering a window into the age of dinosaurs, early mammals, and more.
