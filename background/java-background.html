Understanding Java: A Beginner's Guide

Java, an object-oriented programming language, was developed by Sun Microsystems in the mid-1990s. Known for its versatility and ‘write once, run anywhere’ capability, it powers a vast array of applications from mobile apps to large-scale enterprise systems. Its rich ecosystem and community support make it a top choice among developers.
