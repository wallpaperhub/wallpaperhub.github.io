How Old is Nico Robin in One Piece?

Nico Robin, a character from the popular manga and anime series "One Piece," is currently 30 years old as of the latest arcs. Born on February 6, Robin is known as the archaeologist of the Straw Hat Pirates. Her age has remained consistent since the time skip, where she joined the crew in pursuit of adventure and knowledge.
