Do High School Diplomas Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, high school diplomas can show up on background checks, but it depends on the depth of the check and the policies of the employer. Typically, verifications may include educational history, which can reveal if a candidate graduated high school. It's crucial for job seekers to be truthful about their education.
