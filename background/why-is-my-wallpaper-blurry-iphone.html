Why Is My iPhone Wallpaper Blurry? Fix It Now!

If your iPhone wallpaper appears blurry, it could be due to several factors. The image resolution may be lower than your screen's resolution, leading to pixelation. Additionally, using an image that has been overly compressed can affect clarity. Try selecting a high-resolution image specifically designed for your device.
