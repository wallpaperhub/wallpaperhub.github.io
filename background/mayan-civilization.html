Unveiling the Mysteries of the Mayan Civilization

The Mayan civilization, flourishing from 2000 BC to 1500 AD, was renowned for its advanced architecture, sophisticated mathematics, and a complex writing system. Spanning present-day Mexico, Belize, Guatemala, and Honduras, the Mayans created monumental cities like Tikal and Chichen Itza, showcasing their rich cultural heritage and astronomical knowledge.
