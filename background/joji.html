Joji: Rising Star in R&B Music

Joji, born George Miller, is a Japanese-Australian singer, songwriter, and producer. Known for his unique blend of R&B, lo-fi, and alternative sounds, Joji rose to fame through his viral internet videos and heartfelt music. His emotional lyrics and captivating melodies have garnered a dedicated fanbase, making him a prominent figure in contemporary music.
