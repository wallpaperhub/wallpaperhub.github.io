Disqualifications in Level 2 Background Checks Explained

Several factors can disqualify an individual from passing a level 2 background check. These include felony convictions, certain misdemeanors, criminal offenses against children, or any history of abuse or violence. Furthermore, inconsistencies in provided information can lead to disqualification. Each case is assessed individually based on the nature of the offense and the time elapsed since the crime.
