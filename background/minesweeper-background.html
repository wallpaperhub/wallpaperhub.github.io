Minesweeper: Uncovering the Secrets of the Grid

Minesweeper is a classic puzzle video game, where players uncover squares on a grid while avoiding hidden mines. Originating in the 1960s, it challenges logic and strategy. Players must deduce the locations of mines based on numerical clues, creating a blend of patience and tactical thinking that has captivated gamers for decades.
