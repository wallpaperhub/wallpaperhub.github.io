Convert EPS to PNG: Simple Guide

Converting EPS (Encapsulated PostScript) files to PNG (Portable Network Graphics) formats is essential for web use. EPS files are vector-based and best for print, while PNG files are raster images ideal for online visuals. Use graphic design software or online converters to easily switch formats without losing quality.
