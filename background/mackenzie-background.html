Mackenzie: Uncovering Its Rich Heritage and Modern Appeal

Mackenzie, a popular name with Scottish roots, has evolved into a modern cultural icon. Initially derived from the Gaelic "MacCoinnich," meaning "son of Coinneach," it embodies a rich heritage. Today, Mackenzie symbolizes strength, versatility, and charm, making it a beloved choice for individuals and brands alike.
