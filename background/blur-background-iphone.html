Blur Background on iPhone: Tips & Tricks

To blur the background on your iPhone, use the Portrait Mode in the Camera app. This feature allows you to create stunning photos with a sharp subject and a smooth, blurred background. Adjust the blur intensity in the Photos app post-capture for enhanced effects. It's perfect for portraits and adds a professional touch!
