Discovering the World of Milk: Nutrition & History

Milk has been a vital part of human nutrition for centuries, originating from domesticated animals like cows, goats, and sheep. It is a rich source of calcium, vitamins, and proteins, playing an essential role in bone health and overall well-being. Today, milk is enjoyed worldwide in various forms, including fresh, powdered, and fermented products.
