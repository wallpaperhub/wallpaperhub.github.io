Discovering the Rich History of Scrabble

Scrabble, the beloved word game, has a rich history dating back to the 1930s when architect Alfred Butts designed it as "Lexiko." Its unique blend of strategy, vocabulary, and chance captures players of all ages. Over the decades, Scrabble has evolved into a worldwide phenomenon, fostering competition and fun in homes and tournaments alike.
