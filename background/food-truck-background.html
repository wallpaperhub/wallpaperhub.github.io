Delicious on Wheels: The Rise of Food Trucks

Food trucks have evolved from simple mobile kitchens to a vibrant culinary phenomenon. Originating in the 19th century as horse-drawn wagons, they now offer diverse cuisines worldwide. These mobile eateries reflect cultural trends, encourage entrepreneurship, and foster community engagement, serving gourmet meals and local favorites alike.
