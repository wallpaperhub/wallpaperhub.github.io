Ansel Adams: Fascinating Facts About the Iconic Photographer

Ansel Adams was a renowned American photographer and environmentalist, celebrated for his black-and-white landscapes of the American West, particularly Yosemite National Park. Born in 1902, he was instrumental in developing the Zone System, a photographic technique that allows for precise control of exposure and development. His work championed conservation.
