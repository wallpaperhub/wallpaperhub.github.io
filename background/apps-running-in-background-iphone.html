Manage iPhone Background Apps for Optimal Performance

Apps running in the background on your iPhone can drain battery life and impact performance. To manage these apps, visit Settings > General > Background App Refresh. Here, you can choose which apps can refresh content when not in use. Disabling unnecessary background activity can optimize your iPhone's efficiency and extend battery life.
