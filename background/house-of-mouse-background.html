Discover the Magic of the House of Mouse

The "House of Mouse" is an animated television series created by Disney, which first aired in 2001. Set in a magical club, it features beloved Disney characters who gather to experience various comedic sketches and short films. Mickey Mouse serves as the host, providing a nostalgic blend of classic and new characters while entertaining audiences of all ages.
