Understanding Background Literature: Key Concepts & Importance

Background literature refers to the existing body of work on a topic, providing context and foundation for further research. It helps identify gaps in knowledge, informs methodology, and frames the significance of new studies. A thorough review ensures that research builds on prior findings, enhancing credibility and scholarly contribution.
