How to Easily Change Your Zoom Background

To change your Zoom background, start a meeting and click the arrow next to "Stop Video." Select "Choose Virtual Background." You can pick from the default images or upload your own by clicking the "+" icon. Ensure "I have a green screen" is checked if you use one. Adjust your video settings for the best effect.
