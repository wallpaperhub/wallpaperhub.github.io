Understanding Background Characters: Their Significance in Storytelling

Background characters play a crucial role in storytelling by enriching the narrative and supporting main characters. They provide depth to the plot, create a more immersive world, and can reflect themes and emotions. While not in the spotlight, these characters often resonate with audiences, adding layers to the story's journey.
