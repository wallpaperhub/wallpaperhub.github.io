How Many Questions Are on the RBT Exam?

The RBT exam consists of 75 multiple-choice questions that assess your knowledge and skills related to behavior analysis. Of these, 60 questions are scored, while the remaining 15 are unscored pre-test questions used to evaluate their effectiveness for future exams. Proper preparation is crucial for passing the exam.
