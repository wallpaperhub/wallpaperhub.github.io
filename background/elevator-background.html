Elevator Background: A Brief History and Evolution

Elevators have revolutionized vertical transportation since their inception in the 19th century. Initially powered by steam, they evolved into electrical systems, enhancing building accessibility and urban growth. Today, elevators come equipped with advanced technology, ensuring safety and efficiency, making them indispensable in modern architecture.
