Discover the Delicious History of French Fries

French fries, beloved worldwide, have a rich history that dates back to the late 1600s. Although their origin is debated, many believe they were invented in Belgium, where thinly sliced potatoes were first fried. They gained popularity in France and later in the United States, becoming a staple fast food item enjoyed by millions.
