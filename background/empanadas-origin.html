Discover the Origin of Empanadas: A Culinary Journey

Empanadas, delicious pastry pockets, trace their origins back to the Galician region of Spain. They were introduced to Latin America in the 16th century, evolving into local varieties. Each culture adds its twist, filling them with meats, cheeses, or vegetables, showcasing a blend of culinary traditions that delight food lovers worldwide.
