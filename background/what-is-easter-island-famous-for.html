Easter Island: Discover the Mysteries of Moai Statues

Easter Island, or Rapa Nui, is renowned for its enigmatic moai statues, massive stone figures carved by the indigenous Rapa Nui people. These iconic monoliths, often weighing several tons and standing over 30 feet tall, reflect the island's rich cultural heritage, history, and the mysteries surrounding their construction and transportation.
