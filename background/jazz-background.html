Discover the Roots of Jazz: A Cultural Journey

Jazz originated in the late 19th century in African American communities, blending elements of blues and ragtime. Known for its spontaneity, jazz emphasizes improvisation and individual expression. Iconic artists like Louis Armstrong and Duke Ellington revolutionized the genre, making it a staple of American culture and influencing countless musical styles worldwide.
