Understanding Speckle Background in Imaging

Speckle background refers to the grainy or dotted visual effect often seen in digital imaging and photography. This phenomenon occurs due to interference patterns produced by light waves, resulting in a texture that can enhance or detract from an image's clarity. Understanding speckle can improve techniques in imaging and applications in medical ultrasound or remote sensing.
