Coors Brewing Company: Quality Beer Since 1873

Coors Brewing Company, established in 1873, is renowned for its classic American lagers and commitment to quality. Based in Golden, Colorado, it upholds sustainable practices while innovating with new flavors. As part of Molson Coors Beverage Company, Coors continues to be a popular choice among beer enthusiasts worldwide.
