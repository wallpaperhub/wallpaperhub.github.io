Hershey History: The Sweet Legacy of Chocolate

Hershey History traces back to 1894 when Milton S. Hershey founded the Hershey Chocolate Company in Pennsylvania. After perfecting milk chocolate, he launched the iconic Hershey bar in 1900, eventually building a factory in Hershey, PA. The company introduced famous candies like Kisses and Reese's, shaping sweet treats globally.
