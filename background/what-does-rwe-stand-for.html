Understanding RWE: What It Means and Its Importance

RWE stands for Real-World Evidence, a concept that refers to the clinical evidence derived from the analysis of real-world data outside of traditional clinical trials. This data includes findings from everyday healthcare practices, patient registries, and electronic health records, providing insights into treatment effectiveness and safety in varied patient populations.
