Discover Claude Monet: The Master of Impressionism

Claude Monet, born on November 14, 1840, in Paris, France, was a pivotal figure in the Impressionist movement. Renowned for his innovative approach to light and color, Monet's works, such as "Water Lilies" and "Impression, Sunrise," captured the essence of nature. His legacy continues to influence artists worldwide.
