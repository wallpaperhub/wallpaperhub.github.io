Do Police Reports Appear on Background Checks?

Police reports can show up on background checks, but it largely depends on the nature of the report and the type of background check being conducted. Criminal reports, particularly those that have led to convictions, are more likely to appear. However, non-criminal reports or those without charges may not. Always check local laws.
