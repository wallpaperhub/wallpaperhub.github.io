Explore the Thrilling History of Mountain Biking

Mountain biking began in the late 1970s in Marin County, California, when cyclists modified old bikes for off-road use. The sport grew in popularity through the '80s and '90s, leading to dedicated trails, competitions, and the development of specialized mountain bikes. Today, it's a global phenomenon, enjoyed for recreation and competition alike.
