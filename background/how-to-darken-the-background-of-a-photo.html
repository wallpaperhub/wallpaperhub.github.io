Darken Your Photo Background: Easy Steps

To darken the background of a photo, use editing software like Adobe Photoshop or online tools like Canva. Begin by selecting the background with a lasso tool or magic wand. After selection, adjust the brightness and contrast settings or apply a dark overlay. Finally, fine-tune until satisfied with the result.
