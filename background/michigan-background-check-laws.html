Understanding Michigan Background Check Laws: What You Need to Know

Michigan background check laws dictate how and when employers, landlords, and others can obtain an individual's criminal history or other personal information. The law requires consent before conducting checks and has guidelines on what information can be considered. Understanding these laws is crucial for both individuals and entities involved.
