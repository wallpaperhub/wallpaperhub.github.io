Quick Guide to Removing Background in Premiere Pro

To remove the background in Premiere Pro, use the Ultra Key effect. First, add your clip to the timeline, then navigate to the "Effects" panel and search for "Ultra Key." Drag it onto your clip. In the “Effect Controls” panel, click the eyedropper tool and select the background color you want to remove. Fine-tune settings for optimal results!
