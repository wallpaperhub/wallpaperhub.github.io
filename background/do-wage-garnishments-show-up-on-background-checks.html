Do Wage Garnishments Appear on Background Checks?

Wage garnishments can potentially show up on background checks, particularly during financial or employment screenings. While they are not usually disclosed on a criminal background check, they may appear in credit reports. Employers may consider them when assessing financial responsibility, impacting job eligibility.
