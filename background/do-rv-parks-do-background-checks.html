Background Checks at RV Parks: What You Need to Know

Many RV parks implement background checks to ensure the safety and security of their guests and property. These checks may include criminal history, prior tenancy, and financial stability assessments. Policies vary among parks, so it’s best to inquire directly when making reservations for your stay.
