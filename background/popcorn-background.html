Popcorn: A Delicious Snack with a Rich History

Popcorn has a rich history, originating from ancient civilizations in Central America. It is made by heating corn kernels until they explode, creating fluffy snacks loved worldwide. Today, popcorn is a staple at cinemas and homes, often enjoyed with butter, cheese, or caramel. Its versatility makes it a favorite for all ages.
