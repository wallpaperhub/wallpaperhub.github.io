How to Disable Dark Mode on Google

To turn off dark mode on Google, open the Google app, tap on your profile picture at the top right, and select "Settings." Choose "Theme," and then select "Light" to disable dark mode. For Chrome, go to "Settings," find "Appearance," and switch the theme to "Light" to enjoy a brighter browsing experience.
