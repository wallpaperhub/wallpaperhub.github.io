Who Owns Kidz Bop? Discover the Brand's Origins

Kidz Bop is owned by Kidz Bop LLC, a division of Concord Music Group. Launched in 2001, it produces family-friendly cover versions of popular songs sung by children. The brand has become a household name, appealing to kids and parents alike with its catchy tunes and kid-centric themes, offering entertainment while promoting positivity.
