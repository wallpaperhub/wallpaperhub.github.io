Understanding Soft Background Checks: A Quick Guide

A soft background check is a preliminary assessment that doesn't affect an individual's credit score. It involves basic information verification, such as employment history and education. Companies use it to ensure a candidate's suitability without the invasiveness of a full background check. It's often done discreetly and efficiently.
