Can Felons Work for USPS?

Yes, the United States Postal Service (USPS) does hire felons, but the decision is typically case-by-case. Factors such as the nature of the offense, how long ago it occurred, and the position applied for play important roles. Applicants are encouraged to be honest during the application process.
