Does Second Chance Probation Appear on Background Checks?

Second chance probation may not always show up on background checks, but it depends on the jurisdiction and the type of check being conducted. Some employers or agencies may only see felony or misdemeanor convictions, while others may uncover specific probation statuses in their searches. Always check local laws for details.
