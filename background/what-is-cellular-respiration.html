Understanding Cellular Respiration: The Energy Process

Cellular respiration is a biochemical process where cells convert glucose and oxygen into energy (ATP), carbon dioxide, and water. This vital process occurs in various stages, including glycolysis, the Krebs cycle, and the electron transport chain. It supplies energy necessary for cellular activities and maintaining life.
