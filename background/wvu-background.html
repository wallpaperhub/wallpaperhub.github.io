Discovering the Legacy of West Virginia University

West Virginia University (WVU), founded in 1867, is a land-grant institution located in Morgantown, West Virginia. Known for its vibrant campus culture and academic excellence, WVU offers diverse programs across various disciplines, with a focus on research, innovation, and community engagement, fostering leaders for the future.
