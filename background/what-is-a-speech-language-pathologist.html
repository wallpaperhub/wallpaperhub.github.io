Understanding Speech-Language Pathology: A Brief Guide

A speech-language pathologist (SLP) is a trained professional specializing in diagnosing and treating communication and swallowing disorders. SLPs work with individuals of all ages to improve speech clarity, language skills, and cognitive-communication abilities. Their goal is to enhance functional communication and foster better social interactions.
