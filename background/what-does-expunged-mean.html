What Does Expunged Mean? Clear Your Record Today!

Expunged refers to the legal process of erasing or sealing a criminal record, effectively removing it from public access. This process allows individuals to start fresh without the stigma of past offenses affecting their future opportunities. Expungement varies by jurisdiction, affecting eligibility and procedures.
