Does a Diversion Show on Background Checks?

When it comes to background checks, diversion programs may or may not show up depending on the specifics of the check. In general, successful completion of a diversion program can lead to the charges being dismissed, and these records sealed. However, certain employers or agencies may still have access to these records.
