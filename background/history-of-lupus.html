Understanding the History of Lupus

Lupus has a long and complex history, tracing back to ancient civilizations that first described similar skin conditions. The name "lupus," meaning "wolf" in Latin, emerged in the 13th century due to the rash resembling a wolf bite. Over the centuries, medical understanding evolved, with significant advancements in diagnosis and treatment by the 20th century.
