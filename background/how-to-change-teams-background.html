How to Change Your Teams Background in Minutes

To change your Teams background, start a meeting and click on the 'More actions' (three dots) in the toolbar. Select 'Apply background effects.' You can choose a preset, blur your background, or upload a custom image. Remember to preview your selection before applying it to ensure it looks just right!
