Effects of Background TV on Babies

Many parents enjoy having TV on in the background while attending to their babies. However, this practice can impact an infant's development and attention span. It’s essential to consider engaging with your baby through interaction and play instead, promoting healthier cognitive growth while minimizing passive screen time.
