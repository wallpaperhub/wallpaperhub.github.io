Discover the Inspiring History of Under Armour

Under Armour was founded in 1996 by Kevin Plank, a former University of Maryland football player. The brand started with the innovative idea of moisture-wicking fabric, designed to keep athletes dry. Over the years, it grew into a global sportswear powerhouse, offering a wide range of performance apparel, footwear, and accessories for athletes.
