When Did Ramen Noodles First Appear?

Ramen noodles were first introduced to the world in 1958 by Momofuku Ando in Japan. He developed instant noodles as a quick and convenient meal solution, leading to the creation of Nissin's Chicken Ramen. This revolutionary product transformed global eating habits, making ramen a beloved staple worldwide.
