Discovering the Classic Pong Game: A Retro Journey

Pong, one of the earliest arcade games, revolutionized the gaming world in the 1970s. With its simple yet captivating mechanics, it features two paddles and a bouncing ball, simulating table tennis. The black-and-white graphics evoke nostalgia, while its sound effects and gameplay style laid the groundwork for modern video games.
