Understanding Pi: The Circle's Essential Ratio

Pi, denoted by the Greek letter π, represents the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter. An irrational number, its decimal representation is infinite and non-repeating, approximately equal to 3.14159. Pi has rich historical significance, appearing in ancient civilizations' calculations and continues to fascinate mathematicians today.
