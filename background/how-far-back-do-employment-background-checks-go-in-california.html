How Far Back Do Employment Background Checks Go in California?

In California, employment background checks typically look back seven years for criminal records. However, certain records, like convictions leading to incarceration, may be retrieved beyond this limit. It's important to note that California law restricts employers from considering arrests not resulting in conviction, enhancing fair hiring practices.
