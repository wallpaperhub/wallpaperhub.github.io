Molecular Biology Explained: A Brief Overview

Molecular biology is the branch of science that focuses on the molecular basis of biological activity. It studies the interactions between various systems of a cell, including interactions between DNA, RNA, proteins, and their biosynthesis. This field combines aspects of genetics and biochemistry to understand cellular functions.
