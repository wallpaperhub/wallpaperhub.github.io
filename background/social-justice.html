Empowering Equality: A Deep Dive into Social Justice

Social justice is the pursuit of equality, fairness, and respect for all individuals, regardless of their background. It addresses systemic inequalities related to race, gender, age, and socioeconomic status. Advocating for social justice helps build a more inclusive society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and contribute uniquely.
