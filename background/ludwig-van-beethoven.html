Discover Ludwig van Beethoven: The Musical Genius

Ludwig van Beethoven, a pivotal figure in Western classical music, revolutionized the genre with his innovative compositions. Born in Bonn in 1770, he overcame deafness to create masterpieces like the Fifth Symphony and Moonlight Sonata. His work bridged the Classical and Romantic eras, influencing countless musicians and composers.
