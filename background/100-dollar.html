Understanding the Power of a $100 Bill

The $100 bill, often called "C-note," symbolizes American currency's highest standard value. Known for its distinct design featuring Benjamin Franklin, it represents not just wealth but history and trust in the U.S. financial system. Understanding its significance can enhance your appreciation of money management and financial planning.
