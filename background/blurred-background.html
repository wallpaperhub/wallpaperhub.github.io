Mastering Blurred Backgrounds in Photography

Blurred backgrounds enhance your photos by drawing attention to the subject. This technique, known as bokeh, creates a pleasing visual effect, making your images more dynamic. Ideal for portraits, it adds depth and focus, while softening distractions. Mastering this art can elevate your photography skills significantly.
