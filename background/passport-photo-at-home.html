How to Take a Professional Passport Photo at Home

Taking a passport photo at home is convenient and cost-effective. Start by using a plain white background. Ensure good lighting—natural light works best—to avoid shadows. Position the camera at eye level, and keep your face centered and expression neutral. Take several shots to choose the best one, and follow guidelines for sizing!
