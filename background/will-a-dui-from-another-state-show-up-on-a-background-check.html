DUI Records from Other States: What You Need to Know

Yes, a DUI from another state can show up on a background check. Most comprehensive checks include nationwide databases that track criminal records across states. Employers and agencies often access these databases, meaning out-of-state DUIs can affect job applications, licensing, and more.
