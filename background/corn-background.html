Exploring Corn: A Fundamental Crop's History and Uses

Corn, a staple crop native to Mesoamerica, has played a crucial role in agriculture for thousands of years. It is a versatile plant, used for food, livestock feed, and industrial products. Corn’s vibrant yellow kernels and towering stalks symbolize abundance, making it a significant agricultural commodity worldwide, with various adaptations to suit different climates.
