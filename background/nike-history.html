Nike: A Journey Through History

Nike, founded in 1964 by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight as Blue Ribbon Sports, became Nike, Inc. in 1971. The iconic Swoosh logo, designed by Carolyn Davidson, symbolizes speed and motion. Innovating with products like the Air Jordan line, Nike transformed sports culture, becoming a global leader in athletic footwear and apparel.
