Explore the Mysteries of Venus: Earth's Twin Planet

Venus, often called Earth's sister planet, is the second planet from the Sun and is known for its thick, toxic atmosphere and extreme surface temperatures. With a surface obscured by clouds of sulfuric acid, it presents a hellish environment. Venus rotates slowly backward, showcasing its unique qualities among inner planets.
