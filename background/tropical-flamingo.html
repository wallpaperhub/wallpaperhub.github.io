Vibrant Tropical Flamingos: Nature's Pink Marvels

Tropical flamingos are vibrant wading birds known for their stunning pink plumage, long legs, and graceful necks. Found in warm coastal regions, these social birds gather in large flocks and feed on algae and small invertebrates. Their unique coloration comes from a diet rich in carotenoids found in their food.
