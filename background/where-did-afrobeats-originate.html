Discover the Origins of Afrobeats

Afrobeats originated in West Africa, primarily Nigeria and Ghana, in the early 2000s. It blends various musical styles, including traditional African rhythms, hip-hop, dancehall, and highlife. Artists like Fela Kuti laid the groundwork, while contemporary stars like Wizkid and Burna Boy have propelled the genre onto the global stage.
