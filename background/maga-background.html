MAGA Background: Understanding the Movement

MAGA (Make America Great Again) is a political slogan popularized by Donald Trump during his 2016 presidential campaign. It symbolizes a nationalistic movement aimed at prioritizing American interests in economics, immigration, and trade. The slogan evokes a sense of nostalgia and aims to unite supporters around a vision of a stronger, prosperous America.
