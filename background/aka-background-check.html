Understanding Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A background check, often referred to as a pre-employment screening, is a process used by employers to verify the personal and professional history of potential employees. This includes checking criminal records, employment history, education, and references. It's essential for ensuring safety and compliance within the workplace.
