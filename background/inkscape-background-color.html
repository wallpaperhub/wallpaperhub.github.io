Change Background Color in Inkscape: A Quick Guide

Inkscape allows you to customize the background color of your canvas easily, enhancing your design experience. To change the background color, go to 'File' > 'Document Properties', and under the 'Page' tab, you'll find the 'Background color' option. Adjust it to suit your project's needs, making your designs pop and improving visual contrast.
