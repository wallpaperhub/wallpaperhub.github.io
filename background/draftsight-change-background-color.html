Customizing Background Color in Draftsight: A Simple Guide

To change the background color in Draftsight, navigate to the 'Options' menu. Click on 'Display' settings, where you can find 'Background Color.' Select your desired color from the palette or enter a specific color code. Click 'OK' to apply changes. This customization enhances visual comfort and helps differentiate drawing elements more effectively.
