Discovering Wikipedia's Fascinating History

Wikipedia was launched in January 2001 by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger as a free, collaboratively edited online encyclopedia. It rapidly grew, driven by volunteer contributions, and became one of the largest reference websites in the world. Its unique model of open editing has revolutionized information sharing and accessibility.
