Discover the Essence of Bourbon Barrels

Bourbon barrels, traditionally made from American white oak, play a crucial role in whiskey aging. The charred interior enhances flavor, with notes of vanilla, caramel, and spice developing over time. Understanding the craftsmanship and history behind these barrels reveals why they’re essential for creating rich, complex bourbon.
