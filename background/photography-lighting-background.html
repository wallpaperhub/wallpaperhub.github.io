Mastering Photography Lighting and Background

Good lighting is crucial in photography, as it dramatically impacts the mood, depth, and clarity of your images. The background plays a vital role too; a well-chosen background can enhance your subject or create visual distractions. Use natural light, reflectors, and diffusers, and ensure the background complements your subject for stunning results.
