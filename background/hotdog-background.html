Discover the Delicious History of Hot Dogs

Hot dogs originated in Germany in the 19th century, with influences from sausages. They gained popularity in the U.S. during the early 20th century, especially in ballparks and street vendors. The classic combination of a sausage in a bun, often topped with mustard, ketchup, and onions, has become an iconic American food, enjoyed at barbecues and festivals.
