Understanding the Role of Thorns in Nature

Thorns have long been recognized as nature's defense mechanism, evolving over centuries to protect plants from herbivores. Found in a variety of species, each thorn tells a story of survival and adaptation. Their intricate structures serve practical purposes while also captivating us with their beauty, symbolizing resilience and strength.
