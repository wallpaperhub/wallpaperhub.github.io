Understanding the Origins of Yellow Fever

Yellow fever originated in Africa and is caused by the yellow fever virus, transmitted primarily by Aedes mosquitoes. Historical records trace outbreaks back to the 17th century. The disease spread to the Americas through the transatlantic slave trade, leading to devastating epidemics in cities like Philadelphia and New Orleans.
