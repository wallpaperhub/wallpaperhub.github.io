AAPI Month: Celebrate Diversity and Heritage

AAPI Month celebrates the rich history, culture, and contributions of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders. This month-long observance serves as a reminder to honor the diverse voices and stories of these communities. Activities include cultural exhibitions, educational programs, and community events that promote understanding and unity.
