Understanding Libra: The Diplomatic Sign of the Zodiac

Libra, the seventh sign of the zodiac, spans from September 23 to October 22. Represented by the scales, it symbolizes balance, harmony, and justice. Libras are known for their diplomatic nature, strong social skills, and love for beauty and aesthetics. Ruled by Venus, they thrive in partnerships and seek fairness in all aspects of life.
