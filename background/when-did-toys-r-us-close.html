Toys "R" Us Closure: Key Dates and Insights

Toys "R" Us officially closed its doors in 2018 after declaring bankruptcy in 2017. The company struggled to compete with online retailers and shifting consumer preferences, leading to the shutdown of its stores. While Toys "R" Us once dominated the toy industry, its decline marked a significant change in retail landscape.
