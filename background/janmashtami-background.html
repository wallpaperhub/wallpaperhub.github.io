Janmashtami: Celebrate Lord Krishna's Birth

Janmashtami celebrates the birth of Lord Krishna, an incarnation of Vishnu. This festival, marked by joyous celebrations, devotional singing, and traditional dances, occurs on the eighth day of the dark fortnight in the month of Bhadrapada. Devotees engage in fasting and re-enactments of Krishna's early life, emphasizing themes of love and divine play.
