Does a Medical Marijuana Card Show Up on Background Checks?

Medical marijuana cards generally do not appear on standard background checks, as these checks primarily focus on criminal records and financial history. However, specific employers or agencies may conduct more in-depth searches where such information could potentially be revealed. Always verify local laws and employer policies.
