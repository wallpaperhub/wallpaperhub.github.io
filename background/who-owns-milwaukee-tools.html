Who Owns Milwaukee Tools? Discover Its Parent Company!

Milwaukee Tools is owned by Techtronic Industries Co. Ltd. (TTI), a global leader in power tools and outdoor products. TTI acquired Milwaukee Electric Tool Corporation in 2005, allowing them to innovate and expand the Milwaukee brand. Known for professional-grade tools, Milwaukee focuses on durability and advanced technology to meet industry needs.
