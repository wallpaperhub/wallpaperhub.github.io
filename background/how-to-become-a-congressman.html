Steps to Becoming a Congressman

To become a congressman, start by understanding the role and responsibilities of Congress. Gain relevant experience through public service or community involvement. Obtain a degree in political science, law, or related fields. Campaign actively, focusing on issues important to your community, and build a strong network. Finally, run for office and secure votes!
