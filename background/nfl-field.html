Everything You Need to Know About NFL Fields

NFL fields are meticulously designed to meet league standards, measuring 100 yards long with 10-yard end zones and a width of 53.3 yards. The grass or turf surface ensures optimal player safety and performance. The iconic end zones showcase team colors and logos, creating an exhilarating atmosphere for fans and athletes alike.
