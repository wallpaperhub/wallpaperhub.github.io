Understanding Solar Panels: A Sustainable Energy Solution

Solar panels, originating from the late 19th century, harness sunlight and convert it into electricity through photovoltaic cells. As populations grow, the need for sustainable energy sources amplifies. Their effectiveness and decreasing costs make solar panels a vital part of advancing green energy solutions worldwide.
