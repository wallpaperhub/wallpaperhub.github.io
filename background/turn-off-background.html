Maximize Performance: Turn Off Background Apps

To enhance your device's performance and battery life, turning off background apps is essential. This feature prevents applications from using resources when not actively in use. By deactivating background processes, you can enjoy smoother performance and longer battery life, resulting in a more efficient and enjoyable experience.
