How to Draw a Delicious Caramel Apple

Drawing a caramel apple is a fun, creative endeavor! Start with a round apple shape, then add a glossy caramel coating that drips subtly down the sides. Highlight the texture with light and shadow to make it look luscious. For added flair, sprinkle nuts or colorful toppings on top, enhancing your art's sweetness!
