Do Sealed Records Show Up on Background Checks?

Sealed records generally do not show up on background checks, as they are legally restricted from public view. However, specific circumstances may allow certain employers or agencies access to this information. It's essential to understand your state's laws to ensure privacy and comply with hiring requirements.
