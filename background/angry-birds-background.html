Angry Birds: History & Fun Facts

Angry Birds is a popular mobile game franchise launched in 2009 by Rovio Entertainment. The game features a group of colorful, cartoonish birds seeking revenge on the mischievous pigs that stole their eggs. With physics-based gameplay, vibrant graphics, and engaging levels, Angry Birds has become a cultural phenomenon, spawning numerous sequels, merchandise, and even animated series.
