Understanding Adjudication Background Checks

Adjudication background check refers to the process of evaluating an individual's history to determine their suitability for a particular role, especially in sensitive jobs. This includes reviewing criminal records, employment history, credit reports, and other relevant information to assess the person's qualifications and potential risks associated with hiring them.
