La Llorona: The Weeping Woman's Haunting Tale

La Llorona, or "The Weeping Woman," is a chilling Mexican folklore tale about the ghost of a woman who mourns her lost children. Legend says she wanders rivers, weeping and searching for them, luring unsuspecting souls to their doom. Her tragic story serves as a cautionary tale about the consequences of betrayal and loss.
