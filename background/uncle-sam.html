Uncle Sam: The Face of American Patriotism

Uncle Sam is a personification of the United States government, often depicted as a tall man in a top hat and striped suit. Emerging during the War of 1812, he symbolizes patriotism and national pride. His iconic image has influenced recruiting posters and public propaganda, embodying the federal identity and appealing to American citizens.
