Understanding Animal Testing: Ethics, Impact & Alternatives

Animal testing refers to the use of non-human animals in research to assess the safety and effectiveness of products, primarily in medicine. While it has contributed to many medical breakthroughs, it remains controversial due to ethical concerns regarding animal welfare, necessitating a balance between scientific advancement and humane treatment.
