Can You Legally Conduct Background Checks on Minors?

Background checks on minors can be complex, as they are protected by various privacy laws. Generally, organizations can conduct checks if they have parental consent or if it's for specific purposes like employment or volunteering. Always ensure compliance with local regulations and understand the ethical implications.
