Understanding Cooper Background: The Art of Barrel-Making

Cooper background refers to the historical and professional lineage associated with cooperage, the craft of making wooden barrels and casks. Traditionally, coopers were skilled artisans vital for industries such as winemaking and brewing. Their expertise not only ensured the quality of storage but also contributed to flavor development, making them integral to the beverage industry.
