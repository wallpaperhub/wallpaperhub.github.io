The History of Xanax: When It First Launched

Xanax, the brand name for alprazolam, was first approved by the FDA in 1981. It quickly became popular as an effective treatment for anxiety and panic disorders. Its calming effects led to widespread use, making it one of the most prescribed medications in the United States. Over the years, Xanax has sparked discussions around dependency and misuse.
