Essential Guide to Background Checks in Ontario

In Ontario, background checks are crucial for employers to ensure the safety and integrity of their workplaces. These checks usually include criminal record checks, employment history, and education verification. It's vital for candidates to provide consent, and organizations must comply with privacy laws while performing these checks.
