Discover Richard Gere: Iconic Actor & Activist

Richard Gere is a celebrated American actor known for his charismatic performances in films like "Pretty Woman" and "Chicago." He was born on August 31, 1949, and has captivated audiences with his charm and talent. In addition to acting, Gere is an activist, advocating for human rights and the Tibetan cause.
