How to Enable Google Dark Mode: A Quick Guide

To enable Google Dark Mode, go to your device's settings and select "Display." From there, toggle on "Dark theme." If you're using Google Chrome, navigate to the settings, click on "Appearance," and select "Dark" under the theme options. For Google apps, check the app settings for a dark mode toggle.
