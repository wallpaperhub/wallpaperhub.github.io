Understanding Demographic Segmentation for Targeted Marketing

Demographic segmentation divides a market based on specific characteristics such as age, gender, income, and education. For example, a luxury car brand might target high-income professionals aged 30-50, focusing on their desire for status and comfort. This approach helps tailor marketing strategies to meet the unique needs of different groups.
