Understanding Felony Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A felony background check is a process used to investigate an individual's criminal history, specifically any felony convictions. Employers often conduct these checks to ensure workplace safety and maintain compliance with regulations. It's crucial for applicants to understand their rights and the implications of a felony on employment opportunities.
