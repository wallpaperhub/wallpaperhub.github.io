Fingerprinting vs. Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Fingerprinting and background checks are distinct processes. Fingerprinting involves capturing an individual's unique fingerprints for identification, often used in criminal background checks. In contrast, a background check encompasses a broader review of a person's history, including criminal records, employment, and credit checks.
