Why Is My Zoom Background Backwards?

When your Zoom background appears backwards, it's typically due to your camera's mirror effect being enabled. This feature makes it easier for you to adjust your appearance as it mimics a mirror, but it can confuse viewers who see text or logos reversed. To fix this, check your video settings and disable mirroring.
