What is Included in a USCIS Background Check?

USCIS background checks involve several key components, including checks against criminal records, immigration history, and security databases. The process ensures applicants do not pose a threat to national security or public safety. It includes fingerprinting and may review tax and employment history to verify identity and eligibility.
