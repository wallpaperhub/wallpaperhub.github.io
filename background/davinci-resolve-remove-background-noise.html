Eliminate Background Noise in DaVinci Resolve

To remove background noise in DaVinci Resolve, first select your audio clip. Navigate to the Fairlight tab, then use the Noise Reduction effect by adding it to your audio track. Adjust settings like threshold and ratio until the unwanted noise is minimized. Use the playback feature to preview your adjustments for optimal clarity.
