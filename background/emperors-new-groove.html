Discover the Hilarity of The Emperor's New Groove

The Emperor's New Groove is a comedic animated film that follows the self-absorbed Emperor Kuzco, who is transformed into a llama by his power-hungry advisor, Yzma. With the help of a kind villager named Pacha, Kuzco embarks on a hilarious journey to regain his throne while learning valuable lessons about friendship and humility.
