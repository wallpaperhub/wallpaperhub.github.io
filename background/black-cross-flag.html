Understanding the Black Cross Flag: History & Symbolism

The black cross flag, often associated with historical military units and maritime history, symbolizes strength and resilience. Its stark design, featuring a black cross on a contrasting background, has been used in various contexts, from pirate ships to national emblems, representing unity and courage in times of conflict.
