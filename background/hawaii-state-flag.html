Hawaii State Flag: Symbolism and Design

The Hawaii state flag features a unique design that showcases the Union Jack alongside eight horizontal stripes representing the eight main islands. The flag was adopted in 1898 and symbolizes Hawaii's royal heritage, British influence, and its place as a U.S. state. Each color stripe signifies the islands' diverse culture and history.
