Peter Navarro: Architect of America’s Trade Policy Shift

Peter Navarro is an American economist and political advisor known for his role as Director of the National Trade Council under President Donald Trump. A staunch advocate for economic nationalism, he has been a vocal critic of China’s trade practices. Navarro is also recognized for his writings on trade policy and economic strategy.
