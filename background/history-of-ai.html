The Evolution of AI: From Origins to Today

The history of AI dates back to the 1950s when pioneers like Alan Turing and John McCarthy began exploring machine intelligence. Early developments focused on symbolic operations and theorem proving. In the 1980s, neural networks regained popularity, leading to breakthroughs in the 21st century, including deep learning. Today, AI impacts numerous sectors, from healthcare to entertainment, revolutionizing how we interact with technology.
