Explore the History and Evolution of Basketball

Basketball has a rich history, originating in the late 19th century. Invented by Dr. James Naismith in 1891, it was created to keep students active indoors. The game quickly gained popularity and evolved into a global phenomenon, with professional leagues established worldwide, culminating in the NBA's dominance today.
