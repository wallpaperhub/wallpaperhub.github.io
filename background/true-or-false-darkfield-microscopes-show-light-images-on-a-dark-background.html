Darkfield Microscopes: Bright Images on a Dark Background

True. Darkfield microscopes utilize specialized optics to scatter light, producing bright images on a dark background. This technique enhances the contrast of transparent and unstained specimens, making details more visible. It's an essential tool in biological and material sciences for observing live organisms and intricate structures.
