Understanding Police Lineups: Identification Process Explained

A police lineup is a crucial procedure in criminal investigations, allowing witnesses to identify suspects among a group of individuals. Typically, a suspect stands alongside others who match the description, aiding the victim or witness in recognizing the perpetrator. Proper protocols ensure fairness to avoid misidentification and uphold justice.
