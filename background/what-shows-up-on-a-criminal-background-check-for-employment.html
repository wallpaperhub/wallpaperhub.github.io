What Employers See in Your Criminal Background Check

A criminal background check for employment typically reveals felony and misdemeanor convictions, arrests, sex offender status, and sometimes civil judgments. Employers may also see pending charges and a history of drug-related arrests, which can significantly affect hiring decisions. It's vital to check your own record for accuracy.
