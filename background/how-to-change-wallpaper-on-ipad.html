How to Change Wallpaper on iPad in Simple Steps

To change the wallpaper on your iPad, go to "Settings" > "Wallpaper." Tap "Choose a New Wallpaper," then select from Apple's dynamic, stills, or your photo library. Choose an image, adjust it as needed, and tap "Set" to apply your new wallpaper to the Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both. Enjoy your fresh look!
