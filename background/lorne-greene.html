Discover the Life of Lorne Greene: Iconic Actor and Broadcaster

Lorne Greene was a renowned Canadian actor, broadcaster, and singer, best known for his role as Ben Cartwright on the classic TV series "Bonanza." Born in 1915, Greene's deep voice and commanding presence made him a beloved figure in entertainment. He also hosted "Front Page Challenge" and enjoyed success in music, showcasing his versatility.
