Andrew Carnegie: The Steel Baron Who Shaped America

Andrew Carnegie, born in 1835 in Scotland, was a key figure in America's steel industry. Emigrating to the U.S. as a child, he rose from modest beginnings to become one of the richest men of his time. By innovating production methods, he revolutionized steel manufacturing and laid the foundation for modern industrial America.
