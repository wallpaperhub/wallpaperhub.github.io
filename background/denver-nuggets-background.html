Denver Nuggets: History and Highlights

The Denver Nuggets, established in 1967, are a professional basketball team based in Denver, Colorado. Part of the NBA's Western Conference, they have a rich history marked by passionate fan support and notable players. The Nuggets are known for their high-scoring games and energetic playing style, contributing to the team's growing legacy.
