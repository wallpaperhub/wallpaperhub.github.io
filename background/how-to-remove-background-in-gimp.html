Effortless Background Removal in GIMP

To remove the background in GIMP, open your image, select the Fuzzy Select Tool or the Free Select Tool from the toolbox. Click on the area you want to remove. Adjust the selection as needed by adding or subtracting areas. Once satisfied, press the Delete key to remove the background. Export your image to save the changes.
