Explore Azur Lane: The Action-Packed Naval RPG

Azur Lane is a mobile game released in 2017, blending action and strategy with RPG elements. Set in a maritime universe, players collect and command anthropomorphic warships known as "shipgirls" to battle enemy fleets. The game's captivating backstory features alternate-history naval battles and character-driven narratives, appealing to fans of the genre.
