Discovering Alexander Hamilton: America's Revolutionary Financier

Alexander Hamilton was a founding father of the United States, known for being the first Secretary of the Treasury. He played a pivotal role in creating the country's financial system and establishing the national bank. His life story is filled with ambition, intellect, and drama, culminating in a famous duel with Aaron Burr.
