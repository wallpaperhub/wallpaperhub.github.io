What is Celebrate Recovery? A Path to Healing

Celebrate Recovery is a biblical-based program designed for individuals struggling with habits, hurts, and hang-ups. It provides a safe environment for healing through a 12-step approach rooted in Christian principles. Participants share their stories, support one another, and find hope and restoration in a community-focused setting.
