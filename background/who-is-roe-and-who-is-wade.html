Understanding Roe v. Wade: The Landmark Abortion Case

Roe and Wade refer to the landmark U.S. Supreme Court case Roe v. Wade (1973), which legalized abortion nationwide. "Roe" is a pseudonym for Norma McCorvey, who challenged Texas laws prohibiting abortion, while "Wade" represents Henry Wade, the district attorney. This case has been pivotal in the ongoing debate over reproductive rights.
