BET Awards: Celebrating Black Excellence

The BET Awards, established in 2001 by the Black Entertainment Television network, celebrate African American achievements in music, acting, sports, and other fields. The ceremony highlights diverse talent across genres, featuring live performances and honorifics like the Lifetime Achievement Award, reflecting the rich cultural contributions of the Black community.
