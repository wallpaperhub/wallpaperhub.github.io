How to Draw Electric Guitars: A Step-by-Step Guide

Drawing an electric guitar can be a fun and creative process. Start by sketching the body shape, which is often sleek and curved. Focus on details like the pickguard, pickups, and neck. Use vibrant colors to represent your favorite styles. Don’t forget to add strings and tuning pegs for realism, making your art come alive!
