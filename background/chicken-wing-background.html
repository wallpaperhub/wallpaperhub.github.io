Delicious Chicken Wing History: From Buffalo to Your Plate

Chicken wings have a rich culinary heritage, originating from the United States in the 1960s. Buffalo, New York, is credited with creating the iconic Buffalo wing, which combines deep-frying and a spicy sauce. Today, chicken wings are a staple in bars and restaurants, celebrated for their versatility and appeal at parties and sports events.
