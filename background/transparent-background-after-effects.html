Transparent Background in After Effects: A Quick Guide

Creating a transparent background in After Effects is essential for seamless video editing and compositing. To do this, set up your workspace to show the transparency grid. You can easily remove backgrounds using the Roto Brush Tool, or by layering and masking techniques. This will help you achieve a clean and professional look for your projects.
