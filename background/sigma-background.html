Understanding Sigma: A Key to Quality Improvement

Sigma, a letter in the Greek alphabet, represents mathematical concepts like summation in statistics. Its symbolism extends into various fields, including engineering and management, notably in Six Sigma methodology, which focuses on quality improvement through data analysis. Understanding Sigma's background is essential for grasping its significance across disciplines.
