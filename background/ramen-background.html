Discover the Fascinating History of Ramen

Ramen, a beloved Japanese noodle soup dish, has roots in Chinese wheat noodles. Its popularity surged in Japan post-World War II, with the introduction of instant noodles in the 1950s revolutionizing its accessibility. Today, ramen serves as a comfort food staple, celebrated for its variety and rich flavors across the globe.
