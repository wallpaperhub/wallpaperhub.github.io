Understanding the Vet Profession: Background and Education

Veterinarians play a vital role in animal health care and well-being. Their background includes extensive education, typically a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree, along with hands-on clinical training. This expertise enables them to diagnose, treat, and prevent health issues in animals, ultimately enhancing pet and livestock care.
