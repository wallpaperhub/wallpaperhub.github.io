When Was Prohibition in the U.S.?

Prohibition in the United States began with the 18th Amendment, which took effect on January 17, 1920. This nationwide constitutional ban on the sale, production, and transportation of alcoholic beverages was enforced until its repeal by the 21st Amendment on December 5, 1933. The era was defined by organized crime and social change.
