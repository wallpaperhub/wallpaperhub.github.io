Understanding Dark Kitchens: The Future of Food Delivery

Dark kitchens, also known as ghost kitchens, are food preparation spaces that solely focus on delivery and takeout. These kitchens have gained rapid popularity due to the rise of food delivery apps, enabling entrepreneurs to launch restaurants without traditional dine-in spaces. Emphasizing culinary innovation, dark kitchens can offer diverse menus from various cuisines while minimizing overhead costs.
