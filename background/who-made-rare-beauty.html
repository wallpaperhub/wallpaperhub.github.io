Who Created Rare Beauty? Selena Gomez's Inspiring Makeup Brand

Rare Beauty was founded by pop artist Selena Gomez in 2020. The brand was born out of her desire to challenge unrealistic beauty standards and promote self-acceptance. Rare Beauty emphasizes the importance of inner beauty and includes a diverse range of products designed to celebrate individuality and enhance natural features.
