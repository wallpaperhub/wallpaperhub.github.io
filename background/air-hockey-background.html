Discover the Exciting World of Air Hockey

Air hockey, a popular table game, combines elements of hockey and billiards. Originating in the 1970s, it features a smooth, low-friction table where players use paddles to hit a lightweight puck into the opponent's goal. Its fast-paced nature and simple rules make it a favorite in arcades and game rooms worldwide.
