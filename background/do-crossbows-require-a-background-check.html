Do Crossbows Need a Background Check? Understand the Rules

Crossbows do not typically require a background check in most states, as they are generally classified as archery equipment rather than firearms. However, regulations may vary based on local laws, so it's essential to check your specific state's regulations regarding ownership, purchase, and use of crossbows. Always stay informed!
