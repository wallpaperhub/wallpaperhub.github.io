How to Update Your Public Profile on Snapchat

To edit your public profile on Snapchat, open the app and tap on your Bitmoji. Select 'View My Profile,' then click 'Edit Profile.' Here, you can update your profile picture, add a bio, and choose visibility settings. Don't forget to save your changes before exiting the menu to ensure your updates are applied!
