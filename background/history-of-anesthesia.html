"The Evolution of Anesthesia: A Brief History"

The history of anesthesia dates back to ancient times when natural substances like opium and alcohol were used to dull pain. In the 19th century, advancements led to the introduction of ether and nitrous oxide, revolutionizing surgery. By the early 20th century, techniques evolved, culminating in modern anesthetics, allowing for safer and more effective medical procedures.
