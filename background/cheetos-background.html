Discover the Crunchy History of Cheetos

Cheetos, a popular cheese-flavored snack, made its debut in 1948, created by Frito-Lay. The brand is known for its iconic orange color and cheesy flavor, which stems from a combination of cornmeal and real cheese. Cheetos has evolved over the years, introducing various flavors and products while retaining its playful mascot, Chester Cheetah.
