Prayer Breakfasts: A Tradition of Unity and Reflection

Prayer breakfasts began as gatherings where individuals come together to share a meal and pray, promoting unity and gratitude. These events often attract political, community, and religious leaders, fostering dialogue and collaboration. Originating in the early 20th century, they provide a platform for reflection and collective purpose.
