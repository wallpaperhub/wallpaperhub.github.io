NERC CIP Background Checks: Ensuring Grid Security

The NERC CIP background check is a crucial step in ensuring the reliability and security of the North American power grid. NERC (North American Electric Reliability Corporation) mandates these checks to prevent insider threats and promote compliance with critical infrastructure standards. They help identify potential risks by vetting individuals seeking access to sensitive systems.
