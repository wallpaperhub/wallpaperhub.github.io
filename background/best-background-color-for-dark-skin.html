Best Background Colors for Dark Skin: A Quick Guide

When selecting background colors for dark skin tones, consider bold and vibrant shades like deep blues, rich greens, or warm earth tones. These colors create a striking contrast that enhances the beauty of darker skin. Pastel shades can also work but should be carefully chosen to avoid blending in too much.
