Discover the Original 13 Colonies

The original 13 colonies, established in the 17th and early 18th centuries, were British settlements along the Atlantic Coast of North America. These colonies included Virginia, Massachusetts, New York, and Pennsylvania, each contributing unique cultures and economies. Their quests for freedom and self-governance laid foundations for the United States.
