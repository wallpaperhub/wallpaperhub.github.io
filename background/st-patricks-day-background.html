Celebrate St. Patrick's Day: History & Traditions

St. Patrick's Day, celebrated on March 17th, honors Ireland's patron saint, St. Patrick. Originating in the 17th century, it marks the arrival of Christianity in Ireland. Traditionally associated with wearing green, shamrocks, and festive parades, the day symbolizes Irish culture and heritage worldwide, uniting communities in joyful celebration.
