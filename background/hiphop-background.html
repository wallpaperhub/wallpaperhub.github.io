Discover the Roots and Evolution of Hiphop Culture

Hiphop, originating from the African American and Latino communities in the Bronx during the late 1970s, is more than just music. It encompasses a vibrant culture, including graffiti art, breakdancing, and DJing. This genre reflects social issues and has evolved, influencing global music, fashion, and lifestyle, uniting diverse audiences.
