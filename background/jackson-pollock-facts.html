10 Fascinating Jackson Pollock Facts You Need to Know

Jackson Pollock, a leading figure in abstract expressionism, is known for his unique drip painting technique. Born in 1912 in Wyoming, Pollock's art emphasizes spontaneity and movement, often using house paints and canvases laid on the ground. His most famous work, "No. 5, 1948," showcases his revolutionary approach. Pollock's legacy endures in contemporary art.
