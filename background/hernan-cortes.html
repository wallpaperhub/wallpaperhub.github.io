Discover Hernán Cortés: Conquistador of the Aztec Empire

Hernán Cortés was a Spanish conquistador known for leading the expedition that resulted in the fall of the Aztec Empire in the early 16th century. Born in 1485, he played a crucial role in the Spanish colonization of the Americas, driven by ambitions of wealth and power. Cortés' actions had lasting impacts on indigenous cultures and European expansion.
