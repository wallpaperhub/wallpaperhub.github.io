Change iPhone Text Message Background Easily

To change the background on text messages on your iPhone, open the Messages app, select a conversation, and tap on the "i" info icon. Then, choose "Background" or "Send with Effect," depending on your iPhone's iOS version. Select a new background or effect, and tap "Send" to see your updates in action!
