Discover the Fascinating History of Lotteries

Lottery has a rich history, dating back to ancient times when selections were made by chance for various purposes, including funding infrastructure. Modern lotteries emerged in the 15th century, enhancing public services by generating revenue. Today, they are a popular form of entertainment, offering players the chance to win substantial prizes through random draws.
