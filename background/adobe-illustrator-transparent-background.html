How to Create Transparent Backgrounds in Adobe Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a powerful design tool that allows users to create stunning graphics. To create a transparent background, simply select your artwork, go to the "Object" menu, and choose "Clipping Mask" to isolate your design. Export your work as PNG or SVG to maintain transparency, perfect for web and print applications.
