Explore Milwaukee's Rich History

Milwaukee's history begins with Native American tribes, primarily the Menomonee, who inhabited the area for centuries. In the 1830s, European settlers arrived, leading to rapid growth as a key shipping port. The city's brewing industry flourished in the 19th century, becoming famous for its beer. Milwaukee embraces its cultural diversity, evident in its festivals and architecture.
