Essential Dental Assistant Disqualifications Explained

Dental assistant disqualifications may include a lack of required educational credentials, failure to pass background checks, or inadequate certification. Additionally, any criminal history related to healthcare can hinder job eligibility. Poor communication skills or inability to handle patient records may also lead to disqualification.
