Essential CMS Background Check Requirements Explained

CMS background check requirements ensure that individuals working with vulnerable populations meet safety standards. These checks often include criminal history, professional licenses, and employment verification. It is vital for organizations to comply with federal and state regulations to maintain trust and provide a secure environment.
