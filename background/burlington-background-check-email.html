Burlington Background Check Process Explained

When applying for a position at Burlington, you may receive an email regarding your background check. This email usually outlines what to expect, the necessary steps you'll need to follow, and where to find more information to complete the process smoothly. Be sure to respond promptly to any requests to avoid delays in your application.
