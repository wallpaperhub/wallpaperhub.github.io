How to Change MUI Tooltip Background Color

When customizing MUI tooltips, changing the background color is a simple but effective way to enhance your design. Use the `sx` prop to specify colors directly or use the `theme` object for cohesive branding. This lets you keep your tooltips consistent and visually appealing, ensuring they stand out and provide clear information to users.
