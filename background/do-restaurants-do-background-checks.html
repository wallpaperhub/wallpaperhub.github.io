Do Restaurants Perform Background Checks on Employees?

Many restaurants conduct background checks as part of their hiring process to ensure the safety and reliability of their staff. These checks may include verifying previous employment, criminal records, and references. By doing this, restaurants aim to create a safe environment for both employees and customers.
