Kite History: From Ancient Roots to Modern Fun

Kites have a rich history, tracing back to ancient China, where they served various purposes, from military communication to entertainment. Traditionally made from paper and bamboo, modern kites come in diverse materials and designs, evolving into popular recreational activities. Flying kites fosters creativity and offers a delightful connection with nature.
