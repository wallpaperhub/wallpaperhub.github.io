Discover the Origins of Scentsy: A Journey of Fragrance

Scentsy was founded in 2004 in Meridian, Idaho. It started as a candle and fragrance business, focusing on safe, wickless wax warmers. With a diverse range of scents and home fragrance products, Scentsy quickly grew into a global brand, emphasizing quality and customer satisfaction. The company operates on a direct sales model, empowering independent consultants.
