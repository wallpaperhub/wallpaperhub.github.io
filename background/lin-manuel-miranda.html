Explore Lin-Manuel Miranda: Broadway's Revolutionary Talent

Lin-Manuel Miranda is a renowned American composer, lyricist, and actor, best known for creating and starring in the groundbreaking musical "Hamilton." His innovative blend of hip-hop and theater has redefined Broadway, capturing diverse audiences. Miranda's work extends to film, television, and activism, showcasing his versatile talent and commitment to cultural representation.
