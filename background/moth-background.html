"Discovering Moths: Nature's Nocturnal Wonders"

Moths are fascinating creatures of the order Lepidoptera, closely related to butterflies. Found nearly everywhere on Earth, they exhibit a wide variety of sizes, colors, and behaviors. Many moths are nocturnal and play essential roles in pollination and as a food source for other animals. Understanding their background helps us appreciate their ecological significance and diverse adaptations.
