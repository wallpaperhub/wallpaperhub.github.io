SFPD Disqualifications: What You Need to Know

SFPD disqualifications refer to the criteria that prevent individuals from becoming police officers in the San Francisco Police Department. Factors include felony convictions, serious misdemeanors, drug use, dishonorable military discharge, and any history of domestic violence or abuse. These regulations ensure the integrity and safety of the force.
