TREC Background Check Requirements: All You Need to Know

When applying for a position in Texas, the TREC (Texas Real Estate Commission) background check requirements must be met. Applicants must provide a valid photo ID, disclose any criminal history, and submit fingerprints for a criminal background screening. Meeting these requirements ensures compliance and helps maintain industry standards.
