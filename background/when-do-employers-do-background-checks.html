When Do Employers Conduct Background Checks?

Employers typically conduct background checks during several key stages of the hiring process. Commonly, checks are performed after an initial interview, before extending a job offer, or sometimes even when considering promotions within the company. This practice helps ensure employee safety, verify qualifications, and maintain organizational integrity.
