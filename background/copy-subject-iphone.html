How to Easily Copy Text and Images on iPhone

Copying a subject on your iPhone is easy! Simply tap and hold the text or image you want to copy until you see the “Copy” option appear. After you select that, you can paste it wherever you like, whether it’s a message, note, or social media app. Mastering this will streamline your sharing experience!
