Understanding District of Columbia v. Heller: A Landmark Second Amendment Case

District of Columbia v. Heller (2008) is a landmark Supreme Court case that affirmed an individual's right to possess firearms for self-defense in their home. The Court struck down a Washington D.C. law that restricted handgun ownership, ruling it violated the Second Amendment. This decision significantly impacted gun control laws nationwide.
