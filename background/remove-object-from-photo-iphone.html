Effortlessly Remove Objects from Photos on iPhone

To remove an object from a photo on your iPhone, use the built-in Photos app. Open the image, tap "Edit," and select the "Markup" tool. Choose the eraser or lasso to select the object and remove it. For more complex edits, consider third-party apps like TouchRetouch or Photoshop Express for more advanced features. Save your edits!
