Understanding Radiation Levels: What You Need to Know

Radiation levels refer to the amount of ionizing radiation present in a specific environment, measured in units like microsieverts. These levels can vary due to natural sources such as cosmic rays, radon gas, and human activities like medical procedures and nuclear power. Understanding radiation levels is crucial for health and safety.
