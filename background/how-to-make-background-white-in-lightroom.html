How to Achieve a White Background in Lightroom

To make the background white in Lightroom, start by selecting the photo in the Develop module. Use the Adjustment Brush or Graduated Filter to isolate the background. Increase exposure, reduce shadows, and adjust the whites and highlights sliders to achieve a pure white background. Adjust color temperature as necessary for a clean look.
