When Was the Premier League Founded?

The Premier League was created on February 20, 1992, when clubs in the Football League First Division broke away from the Football League. This marked a new era in English football, allowing clubs to maximize their revenue through television rights and sponsorships. The league officially began its first season in August 1992.
