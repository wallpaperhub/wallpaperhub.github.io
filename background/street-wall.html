Urban Expressions: The Impact of Street Walls

Street walls tell stories of urban life, often adorned with vibrant murals, graffiti, and posters. They serve as a canvas for artistic expression and social commentary, transforming mundane roads into dynamic galleries. Beyond art, these walls work as barriers, offering safety and privacy, while enhancing the character of neighborhoods.
