Effortlessly Remove Backgrounds in Photoshop

To remove the background in Photoshop, start by opening your image. Use the Quick Selection Tool or the Magic Wand Tool to select the background. Once selected, press the Delete key. For finer adjustments, utilize the Layer Mask feature to paint away unwanted areas. Save your image in PNG format to preserve transparency.
