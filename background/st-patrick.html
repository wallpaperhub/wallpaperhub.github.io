Celebrate St. Patrick's Day: Traditions & Festivities

St. Patrick's Day is celebrated on March 17th, honoring the patron saint of Ireland, St. Patrick. This festive occasion is marked by parades, wearing green, and Irish cultural festivities. People enjoy traditional food, drink, and music, while shamrocks symbolize good luck. It's a day of joy, unity, and appreciation for Irish heritage.
