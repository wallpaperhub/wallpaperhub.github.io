Understanding ABC: Optimize Costs & Increase Profitability

ABC, or Activity-Based Costing, is a management accounting method that assigns costs to products and services based on the resources they consume. This approach helps businesses understand profitability and inefficiencies, enabling informed decision-making and strategic planning. Its origins date back to the 1980s, focusing on refining cost allocation.
