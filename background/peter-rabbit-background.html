Discover the Adventures of Peter Rabbit

Peter Rabbit, created by Beatrix Potter in 1902, is a classic children's character known for his mischief and adventures in Mr. McGregor's garden. The story, inspired by Potter's own childhood experiences in the English countryside, explores themes of curiosity and courage. Peter's iconic blue coat and love for veggies make him an enduring symbol of childhood innocence.
