Understanding Reverend Hale: A Crucial Character in The Crucible

Reverend Hale is a pivotal character in Arthur Miller's play "The Crucible," representing transformation and moral conflict. Initially, he arrives to root out witchcraft in Salem with fervor. However, as the events unfold, he becomes disillusioned by the hysteria and injustice, ultimately advocating for truth and redemption in a corrupted society.
