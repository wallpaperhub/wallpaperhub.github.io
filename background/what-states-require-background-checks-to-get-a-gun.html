"States Requiring Gun Background Checks: Key Insights"

In the United States, several states require background checks for gun purchases to enhance safety and regulate firearm access. States like California, New York, and Illinois mandate universal background checks, whereas others have more lenient policies. Always check local laws to understand the requirements where you reside.
