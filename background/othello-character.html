Othello: A Tragic Hero's Journey

Othello, a tragic hero of Shakespeare's play, is a Moorish general in the Venetian army. He is noble, honorable, and deeply in love with Desdemona. However, his insecurities and vulnerability to manipulation lead him to jealousy and tragic downfall. Othello's complex character explores themes of race, love, and betrayal.
