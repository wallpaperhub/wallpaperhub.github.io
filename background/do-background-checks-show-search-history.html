Do Background Checks Reveal Your Search History?

Background checks typically do not reveal an individual's search history. These checks focus on criminal records, credit history, employment verification, and sometimes social media activity. However, personal online data like search history is generally private and not accessible unless authorized or if specific legal conditions are met.
