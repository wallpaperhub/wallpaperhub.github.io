Understanding the Marvels of Red Lightning

Red lightning is a rare and captivating atmospheric phenomenon that occurs during thunderstorms, characterized by a striking red hue. This unusual occurrence is caused by the interaction of electrical discharges with particles in the atmosphere, emitting a vivid glow. Witnessing red lightning is both eerie and mesmerizing, offering a stunning spectacle to storm chasers and nature enthusiasts alike.
