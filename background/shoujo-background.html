Discover the Allure of Shoujo Backgrounds

Shoujo background refers to the artistic styles and themes often found in shoujo manga and anime, typically aimed at young female audiences. Characterized by soft colors, romantic motifs, and expressive characters, these backgrounds create an inviting atmosphere. They often complement storylines that focus on love, friendship, and personal growth.
