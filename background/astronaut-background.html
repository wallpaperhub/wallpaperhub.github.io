Astronaut Background: Education & Experience Explained

Astronauts come from diverse backgrounds, often holding degrees in engineering, science, or mathematics. They typically have experience as pilots, scientists, or medical doctors. Physical fitness and teamwork skills are essential. Many undergo rigorous training, including simulations, spacewalks, and survival training, to prepare for unique challenges in space missions.
