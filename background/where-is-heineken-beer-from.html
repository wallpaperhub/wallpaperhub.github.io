Heineken: Discover the Origins of Your Favorite Beer

Heineken beer originates from Amsterdam, Netherlands, where it was first brewed in 1864 by Gerard Adriaan Heineken. Known for its distinctive green bottle and a refreshing taste, Heineken has become a global symbol of quality lager. Today, it is enjoyed in over 190 countries worldwide, making it one of the most recognizable brands in beer.
