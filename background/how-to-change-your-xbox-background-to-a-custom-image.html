How to Change Your Xbox Background to a Custom Image

Changing your Xbox background to a custom image is easy! First, save the desired image to your console or OneDrive. Go to "Settings," then "General," and select "Personalization." Click "My Background," choose "Custom Image," and locate your image. Adjust it as needed, and enjoy your personalized home screen!
