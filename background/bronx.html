Explore the Bronx: Culture, Nature & History

The Bronx, one of New York City's five boroughs, is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant street art, and diverse communities. Home to the Bronx Zoo and the New York Botanical Garden, it offers breathtaking natural beauty amidst urban life. The borough is also the birthplace of hip-hop, shaping global music culture.
