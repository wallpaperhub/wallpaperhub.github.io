Remove White Background in Illustrator: A Quick Guide

To remove a white background in Illustrator, select the object with the white backdrop. Use the "Magic Wand Tool" to click on the white area, then press Delete. If the object has fine details, try the "Image Trace" feature for better results. Lastly, export your design in PNG format to maintain transparency.
