Discover the Magic of Quidditch in Harry Potter

Quidditch, the magical sport in Harry Potter, features four balls and seven players on broomsticks. Originating from a mix of traditional sports like rugby and soccer, it captivates fans with its fast pace and strategy. The game includes scoring through hoops and the elusive Golden Snitch, which adds excitement to the Hogwarts experience.
