What Does Harper Mean? Uncover the Origin and Significance

Harper is a name of English origin, traditionally meaning "harp player." It's become popular as both a first and last name. Known for its melodic connotation, the name evokes creativity and artistry. It has gained traction in recent years, often symbolizing a charming and strong personality in both boys and girls.
