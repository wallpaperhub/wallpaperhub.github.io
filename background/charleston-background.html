Explore the Rich History and Culture of Charleston

Charleston, South Carolina, is a historic city known for its charming architecture, cobblestone streets, and rich cultural heritage. Founded in 1670, it played a significant role in America's history, especially during the Civil War. Renowned for its beautiful gardens, vibrant arts scene, and renowned cuisine, Charleston continues to attract visitors worldwide.
