Yasmeen Ghauri: Iconic Supermodel and Fashion Trailblazer

Yasmeen Ghauri is an iconic model known for her stunning beauty and captivating presence on the runway. Born in Pakistan and later moving to Canada, she gained fame in the 1990s, becoming one of the first South Asian models to achieve international success. Ghauri's journey inspires many and highlights diversity in fashion.
