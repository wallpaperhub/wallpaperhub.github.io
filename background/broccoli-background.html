Discover the Fascinating Background of Broccoli

Broccoli, a cruciferous vegetable, has a rich history dating back over 2,000 years to the Mediterranean region. Believed to be first cultivated by the Romans, it gained popularity in the 16th century, particularly in Italy. Packed with nutrients, it's now celebrated globally for its health benefits and versatility in cuisine.
