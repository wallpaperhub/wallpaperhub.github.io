Sofia Vergara: The Star Behind "Modern Family"

Sofia Vergara is a Colombian-American actress and model, renowned for her vibrant personality and stunning looks. Born on July 10, 1972, she catapulted to fame through her role in the hit TV series "Modern Family." Vergara is also a successful entrepreneur, with her own line of products, and a prominent figure in Hollywood, admired for her talent and charisma.
