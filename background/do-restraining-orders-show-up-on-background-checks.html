Do Restraining Orders Impact Background Checks?

Restraining orders can appear on background checks, depending on the type of check performed. Criminal background checks typically include civil matters like restraining orders, while basic checks may not. It's crucial to be aware that these orders reflect legal issues and can impact employment, housing, and more.
