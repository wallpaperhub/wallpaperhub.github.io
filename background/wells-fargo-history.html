Discovering Wells Fargo: A Historical Overview

Wells Fargo has a rich history dating back to 1852 when it was founded by Henry Wells and William Fargo in San Francisco. Initially providing banking and express services during the Gold Rush, the company expanded across the West, playing a crucial role in American finance. Today, Wells Fargo is a leading global bank with diverse financial services.
