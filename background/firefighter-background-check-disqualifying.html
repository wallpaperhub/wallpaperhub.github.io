Essential Insights: Firefighter Background Check Disqualifications

Firefighter background checks often disqualify applicants based on criminal history, substance abuse, and certain behavioral issues. A felony conviction, especially related to violence or theft, can be a dealbreaker. Additionally, lacking a clean driving record or any flags for dishonesty may also lead to disqualification in this rigorous selection process.
