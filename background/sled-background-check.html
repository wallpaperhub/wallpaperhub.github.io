SLED Background Checks: Ensuring Safety & Trust

SLED background checks are thorough investigations conducted by the state to ensure the safety and reliability of individuals in sensitive positions. These checks include criminal history, employment verification, and more. They play a vital role in safeguarding vulnerable populations and maintaining a trustworthy environment.
