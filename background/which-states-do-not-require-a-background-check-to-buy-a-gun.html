States Without Gun Background Checks: What You Should Know

In the United States, several states do not require background checks for private gun sales. These include states like Alaska, Arizona, and Wyoming, where individuals can purchase firearms without undergoing a federal background check. This lack of regulation raises concerns about gun safety and access for individuals with criminal backgrounds.
