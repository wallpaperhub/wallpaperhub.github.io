Eliminate Background Noise in Premiere Pro Easily

To reduce background noise in Premiere, first, select your audio clip and navigate to “Effects.” Apply the “DeNoise” effect to reduce unwanted sounds. Adjust the effect settings until you find the right balance. Additionally, use the “Multiband Compressor” to smooth out audio levels. Finally, always preview changes to ensure clarity.
