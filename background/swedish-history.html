Discovering Swedish History: From Vikings to Modern Times

Swedish history is a rich tapestry that weaves together Viking heritage, medieval monarchies, and modern innovations. From the early Norse sagas to the establishment of a constitutional monarchy, Sweden has evolved significantly. Key periods include the Viking Age, the Kalmar Union, and its role in the Thirty Years' War, shaping its unique identity.
