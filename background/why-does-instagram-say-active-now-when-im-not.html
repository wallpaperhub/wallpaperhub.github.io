Why Does Instagram Show “Active Now” When I’m Offline?

Instagram may show you as "active now" even when you're not due to several factors like background app activity, notifications, or even slight delays in server updates. Sometimes, if you have a stable internet connection or are using multiple devices, it could create confusion in your activity status.
