Eliminate Background Noise in GarageBand Easily

To reduce background noise in GarageBand, start by selecting the track you want to clean. Use the Noise Gate effect to cut out low-level sounds. Alternatively, apply a compressor to even out volume levels. Additionally, consider using EQ to filter out unwanted frequencies. Finally, ensure your recording environment is as quiet as possible.
