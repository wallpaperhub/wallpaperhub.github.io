Discover the Sweet World of Skittles

Skittles, the colorful fruit-flavored candy, was first introduced in 1974 by the British company Mars, Incorporated. Initially only available in the UK, Skittles quickly gained popularity and expanded into international markets. Their iconic tagline, "Taste the Rainbow," has become synonymous with the brand, celebrated for its vibrant colors and various fruity flavors.
