Exploring the North Pole: Teams & Their Mission

North Pole teams generally consist of scientists, researchers, and explorers focused on studying climate change, wildlife, and the environment. These teams work collaboratively in extreme conditions to gather crucial data that contributes to our understanding of the polar region's impact on global ecosystems and climate systems.
