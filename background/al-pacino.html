Al Pacino is an iconic American actor known for his powerful performances and intense charisma. With a career spanning over five decades, he has delivered unforgettable roles in films like "The Godfather," "Scarface," and "Scent of a Woman." Pacino's artistic journey is marked by numerous accolades, including an Academy Award and multiple Golden Globes.
