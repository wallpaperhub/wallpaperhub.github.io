Understanding Movie Extras: The Unsung Heroes of Film

In movies, background characters are often referred to as "extras." They add depth to scenes, enhance the story's authenticity, and populate the environment, yet they typically do not have speaking roles. Extras play a crucial part in creating realism, making the on-screen world feel more dynamic and relatable to the audience.
