Discover Key West: Size & Charm of the Southernmost City

Key West, the southernmost point in the continental U.S., spans about 7.4 square miles. This small yet vibrant island boasts a rich history, stunning sunsets, and a laid-back atmosphere. With a population of around 25,000, it feels like a close-knit community where locals and tourists alike enjoy the tropical charm and heritage.
