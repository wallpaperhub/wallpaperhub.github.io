Stunning Geometric Patterns: A Visual Guide

Geometric patterns are visually striking designs formed by repeating shapes, lines, and colors. They are found in various art forms, from textiles to architecture, and serve both aesthetic and functional purposes. These patterns stimulate the eye, create harmony, and can reflect cultural significance, making them a vital element in design.
