Understanding Sunday School: A Brief History

Sunday school has a rich history, providing children and adults with a foundation in biblical teachings. Originating in the late 18th century, it nurtures faith through structured lessons, engaging activities, and community building. Sundays have become a time not just for worship but also for fostering a love of learning within a Christian context.
