Understanding America's Obesity Crisis: A Historical Overview

Obesity became a pressing issue in America during the late 20th century. By the 1980s, data from the CDC began showing significant increases in obesity rates among adults and children. Factors like the rise of processed foods, sedentary lifestyles, and economic changes contributed to this growing public health crisis.
