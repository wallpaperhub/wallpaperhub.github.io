History of Forensic Science: From Ancient Roots to Modern Techniques

Forensic science has roots dating back to ancient civilizations, where techniques like fingerprinting and toxicology began. The discipline evolved significantly in the 19th century with developments by pioneers like Alphonse Bertillon and Hans Gross. Today, forensic science encompasses various fields, aiding law enforcement in solving crimes through scientific evidence.
