Understanding Gravitational Wave Background: Cosmic Insights

Gravitational wave background refers to the persistent rumble of gravitational waves produced by numerous astrophysical events, like merging black holes and neutron stars. These waves, detected by observatories such as LIGO and Virgo, provide insight into the universe's history and the nature of space and time, enhancing our understanding of cosmology.
