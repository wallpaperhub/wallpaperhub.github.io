Remove Checkered Background in Photoshop: A Simple Guide

To remove a checkered background in Photoshop, use the Magic Wand Tool or Quick Selection Tool to select the checkered area. Press the Delete key to remove it. For a more precise edit, use Layer Masks to hide the unwanted areas without permanently deleting them. Adjust the selection as needed for a clean finish.
