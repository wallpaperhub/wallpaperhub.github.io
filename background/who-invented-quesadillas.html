Who Really Invented Quesadillas?

Quesadillas, a beloved Mexican dish, likely trace their origins to the indigenous peoples of Mesoamerica, who filled tortillas with various ingredients. The exact individual who invented quesadillas remains unknown, but they became popularized in the 16th century. Today, they feature diverse fillings and are enjoyed worldwide.
