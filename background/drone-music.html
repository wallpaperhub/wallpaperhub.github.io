Discovering the World of Drone Music

Drone music is a mesmerizing genre characterized by sustained tones and minimal changes over extended periods. It creates immersive soundscapes, often inspired by ambient, experimental, and electronic elements. Artists like La Monte Young and Brian Eno have popularized this style, inviting listeners into a meditative auditory experience.
