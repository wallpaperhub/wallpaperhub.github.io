Understanding Virginia's 7-Year Background Check Law

In Virginia, a 7-year background check law limits what employers can consider during hiring. This law mandates that employers can only look back 7 years for felony convictions, promoting fair employment practices. However, some offenses, like serious crimes, can still be disclosed beyond this timeframe, ensuring safety and compliance.
