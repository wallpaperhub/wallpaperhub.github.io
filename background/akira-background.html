Explore the Impact and Legacy of Akira

Akira, a groundbreaking anime film directed by Katsuhiro Otomo, premiered in 1988. Set in a post-apocalyptic Neo-Tokyo, it follows Kaneda and his friend Tetsuo as they navigate motorcycle gangs and government conspiracies. Known for its stunning visuals and complex themes, Akira has influenced global pop culture and the sci-fi genre.
