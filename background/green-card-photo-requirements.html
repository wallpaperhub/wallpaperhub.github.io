Essential Green Card Photo Requirements Explained

When applying for a green card, your photo must meet specific requirements: it should be 2x2 inches in size, in color, taken within the last six months, with a plain white background. Your face must be visible and centered, without glasses or head coverings (unless for religious reasons). Ensure the photo is high quality and properly formatted.
