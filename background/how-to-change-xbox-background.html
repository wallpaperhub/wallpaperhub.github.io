How to Change Your Xbox Background: A Step-by-Step Guide

To change your Xbox background, start from the home screen and press the Xbox button to access the guide. Navigate to "Profile & system," then select "Settings." Choose "General," then "Personalization," followed by "My background." Here, you can select a game, USB photo, or preset theme to customize your experience.
