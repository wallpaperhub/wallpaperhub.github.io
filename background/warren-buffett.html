Warren Buffett: The Oracle of Omaha's Investment Wisdom

Warren Buffett, often called the "Oracle of Omaha," is one of the most successful investors in history. Known for his value investing strategy, Buffett emphasizes long-term growth over quick gains. He leads Berkshire Hathaway, which owns diverse businesses, and believes in the power of compounding wealth through prudent decisions.
