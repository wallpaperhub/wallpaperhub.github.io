The Essentials of Neuroscience: Understanding the Brain and Behavior

Neuroscience is the scientific study of the nervous system, focusing on the brain's structure, function, and development. It connects biology, psychology, and medicine, exploring how neural processes affect behavior and cognition. By understanding the brain, neuroscience aims to uncover the mechanisms of disorders and enhance overall mental health.
