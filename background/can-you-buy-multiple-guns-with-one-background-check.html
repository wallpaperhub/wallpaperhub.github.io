"Buying Multiple Guns: One Background Check Explained"

In the U.S., it's generally possible to buy multiple firearms in a single transaction with one background check. However, each purchase is subject to the same regulations, and dealers may conduct separate checks depending on the local laws. Understanding these regulations is essential for responsible gun ownership.
