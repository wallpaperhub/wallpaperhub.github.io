Discover the Fascinating Background of Carnations

Carnations, with their ruffled petals and rich fragrance, boast a fascinating background in culture and history. Known for their longevity, these flowers symbolize a range of emotions from love to gratitude. Originating in the Mediterranean, carnations were prized by ancient Greeks and Romans, and today, they are popular in various celebrations.
