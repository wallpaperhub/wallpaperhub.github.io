Maximize Value with Project-Based Service Agreements

PBSA, or Project-Based Service Agreements, are contracts that focus on the delivery of specific outcomes rather than traditional service metrics. This innovative approach promotes collaboration, allowing companies to align their goals with service providers for optimal performance and value. PBSA enhances accountability and fosters long-term partnerships.
