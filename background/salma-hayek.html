Salma Hayek: A Journey Through Cinema and Activism

Salma Hayek is a prominent Mexican-American actress, producer, and director known for her stunning performances and charismatic presence. Born on September 2, 1966, in Coatzacoalcos, Mexico, she gained worldwide fame for her role in "Frida." Beyond acting, Hayek is an advocate for women's rights and an influential figure in Hollywood.
