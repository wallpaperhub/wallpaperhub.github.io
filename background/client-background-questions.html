Essential Client Background Questions for Effective Engagement

Client background questions are crucial in understanding their needs and expectations. Start by asking about their business history, industry, and target audience. Inquire about previous experiences with similar services and their specific goals. This information helps in tailoring strategies that align with their vision and enhances communication.
