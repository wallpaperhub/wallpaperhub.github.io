"Understand Your Driving Record: Key Insights & Impact"

A driving record is a summary of a driver's history and behavior on the road. It includes information on traffic violations, accidents, and the status of the driver's license. Employers, insurers, and authorities often review driving records to assess responsibility and risk. Maintaining a clean record is essential for safer driving and better insurance rates.
