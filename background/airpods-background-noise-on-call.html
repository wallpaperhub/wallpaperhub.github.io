"Fixing AirPods Background Noise During Calls"

AirPods have revolutionized listening, but background noise during calls can be a nuisance. It often occurs due to external sounds interfering with the microphone quality. To improve call clarity, ensure a good fit, keep the microphone clear from obstructions, and utilize noise-cancellation features available in newer models.
