"The Wolf of Wall Street," directed by Martin Scorsese, is a biographical drama depicting the rise and fall of stockbroker Jordan Belfort. Based on Belfort's memoir, the film showcases themes of excess and moral decay as it reveals the dark side of the financial world during the 1990s, highlighting Belfort's corrupt practices and extravagant lifestyle.
