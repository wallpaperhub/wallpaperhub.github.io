Discover the Fascinating Background of Oysters

Oysters are bivalve mollusks found in marine or brackish waters. They have been harvested for food and pearls for thousands of years, dating back to ancient civilizations like the Romans and Greeks. Known for their unique flavor and nutritional benefits, these shellfish play a critical role in marine ecosystems by filtering water and providing habitat.
