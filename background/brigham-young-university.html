Discover Brigham Young University: Excellence in Education

Brigham Young University (BYU), located in Provo, Utah, is a private research university affiliated with the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. Renowned for its strong academic programs, vibrant campus life, and commitment to faith, culture, and community service, BYU offers a unique educational experience with a rigorous curriculum.
