Meaning of the Statue of Liberty: A Symbol of Freedom

The Statue of Liberty symbolizes freedom, democracy, and hope. Gifted by France to the United States in 1886, it stands as a welcoming beacon to immigrants arriving in New York City. This iconic monument embodies the spirit of liberty and serves as a reminder of the enduring values of equality and opportunity, inspiring generations worldwide.
