Understanding DOJ MBI: A Guide to Business Ethics

DOJ MBI refers to the Department of Justice's Model Business Initiative, aimed at enhancing transparency and ethical practices in various industries. This initiative encourages businesses to adopt best practices in compliance, transparency, and accountability, focusing on fostering an environment of integrity and trust across all sectors.
