Stunning Bokeh Backgrounds for Your Photos

Bokeh background refers to the artistic blur created in photographs, emphasizing the subject by softening the surrounding elements. This technique enhances visual aesthetics, giving depth and making images more engaging. Often found in portraits and macro photography, bokeh highlights colors and shapes, offering an enchanting backdrop that enhances storytelling.
