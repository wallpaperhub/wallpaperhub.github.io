How Long Has Chick-fil-A Been Serving Delicious Chicken?

Chick-fil-A was founded by Truett Cathy in 1946 when he opened the Dwarf Grill in Hapeville, Georgia. The restaurant specialized in a simple chicken sandwich that gained popularity over time. In 1967, the first Chick-fil-A restaurant was opened in a mall in Atlanta, marking the beginning of the brand we know today.
