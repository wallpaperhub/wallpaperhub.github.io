“Understanding Chicken Transparency: A Consumer's Guide”

Chicken transparency refers to the clarity and traceability of chicken products in the market. Consumers today are increasingly interested in where their food comes from, how it's raised, and processed. Transparency ensures accountability, better farming practices, and higher quality meat, promoting consumer trust and informed choices.
