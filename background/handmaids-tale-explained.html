"Understanding The Handmaid's Tale: A Dystopian Overview"

"The Handmaid's Tale" is a dystopian narrative set in a totalitarian society where women, particularly Handmaids, are subjugated to bear children for elite couples. It explores themes of power, gender roles, and resistance through Offred, the protagonist, who navigates a world stripped of freedom, battling both oppression and her yearning for autonomy.
