Mona Lisa: The Enigmatic Masterpiece

The Mona Lisa, painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century, is one of the most iconic works of art in history. Known for her enigmatic smile and masterful use of sfumato, the painting resides in the Louvre Museum, captivating millions. Its allure lies in its beauty, mystery, and da Vinci's extraordinary technique.
