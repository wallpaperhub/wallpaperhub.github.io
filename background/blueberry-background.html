Blueberry Background: Origins, Benefits, and Culinary Uses

Blueberries, native to North America, are small, round berries known for their vibrant blue hue and sweet-tart flavor. Packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, they are celebrated not just for their delightful taste but also for their numerous health benefits. From smoothies to desserts, blueberries are a nutritious, versatile favorite.
