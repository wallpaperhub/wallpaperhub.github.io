The Art of the Courtier in D&D 5E

In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, the Courtier is a fascinating subclass that emphasizes social interaction and diplomacy. Utilizing charisma, courtiers excel in negotiating alliances, gathering information, and influencing powerful figures. They blend combat prowess with the art of persuasion, making them invaluable in both courtly and adventurer settings.
