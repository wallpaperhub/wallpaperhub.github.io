Jackie Chan: Cinematic Legend and Martial Arts Icon

Jackie Chan is a legendary martial artist and actor known for his unique blend of action and comedy. Born in Hong Kong, he rose to fame with films like "Drunken Master" and "Rush Hour." His dedication to performing stunts and innovative choreography has influenced generations. Beyond cinema, Chan is a philanthropist and cultural ambassador.
