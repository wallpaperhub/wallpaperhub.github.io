Jennifer Granholm: Champion of Clean Energy and Economic Growth

Jennifer Granholm is a prominent American political figure and the current Secretary of Energy. She served as the governor of Michigan from 2003 to 2011 and is known for her leadership in renewable energy and economic development. Granholm advocates for clean energy policies and aims to tackle climate change while boosting job growth.
