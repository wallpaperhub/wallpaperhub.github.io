How to Easily Blur Videos in iMovie

Blurring a video in iMovie is a simple yet effective way to focus on specific elements or add a creative touch. To blur a clip, select it in your timeline, navigate to the "Effects" panel, and apply the "Blur" effect. Adjust the intensity as needed to achieve your desired look, enhancing your video without distracting from the main content.
