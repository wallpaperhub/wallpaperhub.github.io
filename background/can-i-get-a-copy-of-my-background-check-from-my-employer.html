How to Request a Copy of Your Background Check from Your Employer

You can request a copy of your background check from your employer, as the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) entitles you to access this information. It’s important to contact your HR department or the specific provider used for the check. Be aware that some employers may have their own policies regarding these requests.
