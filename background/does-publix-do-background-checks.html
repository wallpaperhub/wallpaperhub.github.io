Understanding Publix Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Yes, Publix does conduct background checks as part of their hiring process. This is to ensure the safety and integrity of the workplace and to verify the qualifications of potential employees. Background checks may include criminal records, employment history, and education verification, depending on the position applied for.
