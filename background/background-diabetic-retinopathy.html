Understanding Background Diabetic Retinopathy: Key Insights

Background diabetic retinopathy is a common complication of diabetes, characterized by damage to the retina's blood vessels. This condition often develops unnoticed and can lead to vision impairment. Early detection through regular eye exams is crucial, as managing diabetes effectively can slow the progression of retinopathy.
