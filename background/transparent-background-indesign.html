How to Create Transparent Backgrounds in InDesign

Creating a transparent background in Adobe InDesign enhances your design's versatility. To achieve this, navigate to the Effects panel, select the object, and adjust the opacity or use the export options to save as PNG. This technique is invaluable for logos and graphics, allowing seamless incorporation into various projects.
