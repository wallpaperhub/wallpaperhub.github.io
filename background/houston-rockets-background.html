Houston Rockets: A Legacy of Excellence in the NBA

The Houston Rockets, founded in 1967, have a rich history in the NBA. Originally based in San Diego, the team relocated to Houston in 1971. The Rockets gained fame for their back-to-back championships in the mid-1990s, led by legends Hakeem Olajuwon and Clyde Drexler. Known for their dynamic playing style, the Rockets continue to be a significant force in the league.
