Understanding Acolyte Background in D&D 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, an Acolyte is a character who has dedicated their life to the service of a temple, deity, or religious order. They often possess strong connections to their faith, providing them with clerical abilities, access to spells, and a unique backstory that enhances their role in the party. Acolytes are not just followers; they can become powerful allies in any campaign.
