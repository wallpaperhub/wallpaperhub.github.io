Understanding Wires: The Essential Guide

Wires are essential components found in various applications, ranging from electrical wiring to electronics. They serve as conductive pathways for electricity and signals, made typically from copper or aluminum due to their excellent conductivity. Understanding the background of wires helps in appreciating their role in technology and safety standards.
