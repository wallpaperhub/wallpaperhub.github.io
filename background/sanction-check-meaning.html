Understanding Sanction Checks: Importance & Process

Sanction check refers to the process of reviewing individuals or organizations against government-imposed sanctions lists. This is essential in preventing transactions with entities that may pose legal or financial risks. These checks help ensure compliance with laws designed to promote national security and prevent criminal activities.
