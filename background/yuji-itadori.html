Yuji Itadori: The Hero of Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori is the main protagonist of the popular anime and manga series "Jujutsu Kaisen." A high school student with extraordinary physical abilities, he becomes a jujutsu sorcerer after swallowing a cursed object—Ryomen Sukuna's finger. Yuji's journey is marked by incredible battles, friendships, and a quest to protect humanity from curses.
