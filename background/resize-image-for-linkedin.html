Resize Images for LinkedIn: Easy Guide

To resize an image for LinkedIn, use an image editor to ensure it meets the platform's recommended dimensions: 400x400 pixels for profile pictures and 1200x627 pixels for cover images. Maintain a 1:1 aspect ratio for profiles to ensure clarity. Save in JPEG or PNG format for the best quality when uploading.
