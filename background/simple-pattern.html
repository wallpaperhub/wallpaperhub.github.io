Simple patterns are foundational designs that can be observed in everyday life, art, nature, and architecture. These patterns often consist of repeated shapes, colors, or lines that create visual harmony and structure. Understanding simple patterns enhances creativity, fosters problem-solving skills, and shapes our perceptions of the world.
