Gherkins: The Crunchy Pickle Delight

Gherkins are small, pickled cucumbers often used in salads and sandwiches. Known for their tangy crunch, they are a key ingredient in many dishes, adding flavor and texture. Rich in antioxidants and low in calories, gherkins are a healthy choice. These tiny veggies can also be enjoyed as snacks or in a charcuterie board.
