Does a Misdemeanor DUI Appear on Background Checks?

A misdemeanor DUI typically does show up on background checks, especially during employment or housing applications. The duration it stays on your record can vary by state, but many will look back at criminal records for several years. It's important to understand how it may impact opportunities and consider legal advice for expungement.
