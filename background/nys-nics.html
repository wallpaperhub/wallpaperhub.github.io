Understanding NYS NICS: A Guide to Background Checks

NY's NICS, or the New York State National Instant Criminal Background Check System, is essential for ensuring public safety by screening individuals before firearm purchases. It aids in identifying those prohibited from owning firearms due to criminal records or mental health issues. Understanding its importance can enhance community awareness.
