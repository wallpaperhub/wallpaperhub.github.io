Understanding Light Speed: The Universe's Fastest Phenomenon

Light speed, approximately 299,792 kilometers per second, is the fastest speed at which energy and information can travel in the universe. This fundamental constant plays a critical role in the theories of relativity, impacting how we understand space and time. Its implications extend to cosmology, quantum mechanics, and the nature of reality.
