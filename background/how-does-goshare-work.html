How GoShare Works: Your Local Moving Solution

GoShare is a user-friendly app that connects people needing moving help with local drivers who have trucks or vans. Users can request assistance for deliveries or moving items by entering their pickup and drop-off locations. Once a driver accepts the request, users can track the journey and pay securely through the app.
