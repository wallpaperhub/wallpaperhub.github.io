Missouri Firearms Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Missouri law does not require universal background checks for most firearm purchases. Private sales between individuals typically do not entail background checks, although licensed dealers are mandated to conduct them. This has sparked debates on gun safety and the need for stricter regulations to prevent unauthorized access to firearms.
