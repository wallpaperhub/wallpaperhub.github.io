Understanding Different Types of Saws and Their Uses

A saw is an essential cutting tool used in various trades, including construction, woodworking, and metalworking. Saw backgrounds vary widely depending on their type—manual or powered—and intended use. Understanding the different saws, from hand saws to table saws, informs best practices and safety measures for effective cutting.
