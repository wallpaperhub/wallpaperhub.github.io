Bonnie Blue Flag: A Symbol of Southern Heritage

The Bonnie Blue Flag is a historical flag that symbolizes Southern pride and independence, originally used in the early 1860s during the American Civil War. Featuring a blue field with a single white star, it represents the Confederacy's struggle and has been embraced by various groups over the years, evoking both nostalgia and controversy.
