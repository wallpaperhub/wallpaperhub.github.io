Change Background Color in Blender: Easy Step-by-Step Guide

To change the background color in Blender, start by navigating to the 'World' tab in the properties panel. Under the 'Surface' section, select 'Color' and choose your desired color. Alternatively, for 3D views, go to the 'View' menu, select 'Viewport Shading,' and adjust the 'World' or 'Object' settings to customize your scene's backdrop as needed.
