Understanding Carl Rogers: The Father of Humanistic Psychology

Carl Rogers was a renowned psychologist and one of the founding figures of humanistic psychology. His client-centered therapy emphasized the importance of empathy, genuineness, and unconditional positive regard in facilitating personal growth. Rogers believed that individuals have an inherent drive toward self-actualization and understanding themselves.
