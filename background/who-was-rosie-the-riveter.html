Unveiling Rosie the Riveter: Icon of Women’s Empowerment

Rosie the Riveter is a cultural icon representing women who worked in factories during World War II. Her image symbolized women's economic power and their ability to perform traditionally male roles in the workforce. The phrase "We Can Do It!" became a rallying cry for female empowerment and was instrumental in changing societal norms about women's roles.
