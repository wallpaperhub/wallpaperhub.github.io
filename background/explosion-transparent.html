Understanding Explosion Transparency in Visual Effects

Explosion transparency refers to the ability to visually showcase explosion effects while maintaining clarity in the underlying scene. This technique is often used in graphics, gaming, and visual effects to create dramatic impacts without obscuring vital elements. By combining realism with transparency, it enhances user experience and engagement.
