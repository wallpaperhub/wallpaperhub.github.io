Keep Background Apps Running After Closing Chrome

To continue running background apps when Chrome is closed, you can adjust your settings in the Chrome browser. Go to Settings, scroll down to "Advanced," and find the "System" section. Toggle the switch for "Continue running background apps when Google Chrome is closed" to enable it. This allows apps like extensions and notifications to keep functioning even after your browsing session ends.
