Caution Backgrounds: Safety in Design

Caution backgrounds are essential elements in design that signify warning or alertness. These backgrounds typically utilize bold colors, like yellow or orange, and often include symbols or text that capture attention quickly. They play a crucial role in safety signage, online notifications, and product labels, conveying important messages effectively.
