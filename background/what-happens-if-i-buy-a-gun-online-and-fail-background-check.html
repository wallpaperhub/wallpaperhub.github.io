What Happens If You Fail a Gun Background Check Online?

If you buy a gun online and fail a background check, the transaction cannot be completed, and you will not receive the firearm. Depending on state laws, the seller may be required to report the failure to authorities. Additionally, you may face legal consequences if you attempted to bypass regulations.
