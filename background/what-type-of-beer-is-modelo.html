What Type of Beer is Modelo? Discover the Flavors!

Modelo is a popular Mexican beer known for its rich flavor and smooth finish. It's primarily a Pilsner-style lager, specifically "Modelo Especial," characterized by its light golden color and refreshing taste, making it a great choice for casual sipping or pairing with various foods. The brewery also produces "Negra Modelo," a dark Munich-style lager with a slightly sweeter and maltier profile.
