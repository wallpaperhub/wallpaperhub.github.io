When Did Oreos First Hit the Shelves?

Oreos were invented in 1912 by the National Biscuit Company, known today as Nabisco. The original cookie featured a cream filling sandwiched between two chocolate wafers. Over the years, it has evolved into one of the most popular cookies worldwide, spawning numerous flavors and variations, delighting cookie lovers everywhere.
