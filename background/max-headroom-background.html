Max Headroom: The Iconic Cultural Phenomenon

Max Headroom is a fictional character created in the 1980s, known for his digital, glitchy persona and satirical commentary on media and consumerism. He originated from a British TV movie and later had his own series, blending sci-fi with comedy. His iconic appearance and catchphrases made him a cultural icon, reflecting the evolving landscape of technology and youth culture.
