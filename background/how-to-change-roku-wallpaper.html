Change Roku Wallpaper: A Step-by-Step Guide

To change your Roku wallpaper, start by navigating to the Home screen. Select 'Settings', then 'Theme', and choose ' wallpapers'. You can select from the available options or upload your own by connecting to the Roku mobile app. Follow the prompts to adjust your desired wallpaper and enjoy a personalized home screen experience!
