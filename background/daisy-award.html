Daisy Award: Celebrating Exceptional Nursing Care

The DAISY Award is an internationally recognized program honoring compassionate and skillful nursing care. Nurses are nominated by patients, families, and colleagues for their exceptional contributions, highlighting the impact of dedicated care in healthcare settings. Each award presentation celebrates these heroes and their unwavering commitment to patient well-being.
