The Beatles: Meet the Legendary Members

The Beatles, one of the most iconic bands in history, consisted of four talented members: John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. John, the visionary leader, brought innovative songwriting, while Paul contributed melodic brilliance. George added depth with his guitar skills, and Ringo's drumming provided the perfect backbone to their sound. Together, they revolutionized music and culture.
