Quick Guide: Change Background Color in GIMP

To change the background color in GIMP, start by opening your image. Use the "Select" tool to outline the area you want to change. Next, go to the "Edit" menu and choose "Fill with Color." Select your desired color from the color dialog box. Finally, save your image with the new background color applied for a vibrant transformation.
