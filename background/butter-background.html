Delicious Butter Background: A Culinary Essential

Butter has been a staple in kitchens for centuries, celebrated for its rich flavor and versatility. This creamy dairy product is created by churning cream, separating buttermilk from the fat. Its golden hue is derived from carotenoids in the milk. Beyond cooking, it symbolizes indulgence and comfort in various cultures, enhancing both savory and sweet dishes.
