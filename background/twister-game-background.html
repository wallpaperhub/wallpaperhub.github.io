Discover the Fun Origins of the Twister Game

Twister game, created in 1966 by Charles Foley and Neil Rabens, combines elements of a physical challenge and a board game. Players balance on a colorful mat marked with circles of different colors while following instructions to place their hands or feet on specific colors. This party favorite promotes laughter, fun, and friendly competition.
