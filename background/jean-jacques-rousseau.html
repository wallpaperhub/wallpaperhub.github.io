Exploring Jean-Jacques Rousseau: Philosopher of Freedom

Jean-Jacques Rousseau was an influential philosopher of the 18th century, known for his ideas on education, democracy, and individual freedom. His works, like "The Social Contract" and "Emile," challenged societal norms, advocating for natural human goodness and the importance of community. Rousseau's thoughts continue to inspire modern political and educational theories.
