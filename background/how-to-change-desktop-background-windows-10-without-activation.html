Change Windows 10 Desktop Background Without Activation

To change your desktop background in Windows 10 without activation, right-click on the desktop and select "Personalize." Choose "Background," then pick your desired image from "Background" settings. You can also select a solid color or a slideshow. Enjoy your new background without needing to activate Windows!
