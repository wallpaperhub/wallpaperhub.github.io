Midsommar: Embrace the Magic of Summer Solstice

Midsommar, or "Midsummer," refers to the summer solstice celebration in various cultures, notably in Scandinavian traditions. It symbolizes fertility, growth, and light, marking the longest day of the year. Celebrations typically include music, dancing, and the iconic maypole, embodying community spirit and the joy of summer.
