Explore the Evolution of UPS: From Local Courier to Global Giant

UPS (United Parcel Service) was founded in 1907 in Seattle as a bicycle delivery service. It grew rapidly, expanding its services and fleet throughout the 20th century. The introduction of air delivery in 1953 and the launch of their tracking system in 1994 revolutionized shipping. Today, UPS is a global leader in logistics and supply chain solutions.
