Discovering the Life and Art of Vincent van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh, a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter, is renowned for his vivid colors and emotive brushwork. His masterpieces, like "Starry Night" and "Sunflowers," capture intense feelings and the beauty of nature. Despite battling mental health issues, van Gogh's legacy endures, influencing countless artists and art lovers worldwide.
