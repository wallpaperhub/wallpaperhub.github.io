Boston Terrier History: From Ratters to Beloved Companions

Boston Terriers originated in the late 19th century in Boston, Massachusetts, through the crossbreeding of Bulldogs and Terriers. Initially bred for ratting and fights, their friendly demeanor and intelligence soon made them popular family pets. Today, they are celebrated for their distinctive tuxedo-like coat and affectionate nature.
