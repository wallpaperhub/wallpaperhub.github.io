Change Background Color in Bluebeam: Quick Guide

Looking to change the background color in Bluebeam? Simple! Open your document, navigate to the 'View' menu, select 'Background', and click 'Add Background'. Choose the color you desire, and adjust the opacity if needed. This quick change enhances readability and can make your PDF stand out beautifully!
