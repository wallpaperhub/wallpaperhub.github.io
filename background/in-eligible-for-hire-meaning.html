Understanding "In-Eligible for Hire" Explained

"In-eligible for hire" refers to individuals who do not meet the necessary qualifications or criteria for employment. This can arise from various factors such as criminal records, lack of required skills, or failure to pass background checks. Understanding this term is crucial for both employers and job seekers in the recruitment process.
