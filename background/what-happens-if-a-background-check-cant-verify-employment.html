What If Your Employment Can't Be Verified?

If a background check can't verify employment, it can raise red flags for potential employers. They may question the accuracy of your resume or concerns about dishonesty. This can lead to delayed hiring decisions or even withdrawal from the hiring process. Applicants should ensure they provide accurate information and be prepared to explain any discrepancies.
