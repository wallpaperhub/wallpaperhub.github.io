Essential Robert Frost Facts: A Brief Overview

Robert Frost is a celebrated American poet known for his depictions of rural New England life. Born on March 26, 1874, he wrote famous works like "The Road Not Taken" and "Stopping by Woods on a Snowy Evening." He won four Pulitzer Prizes for Poetry and had a unique ability to explore complex themes through simple language.
