Discover the Legacy of Langston Hughes: A Harlem Renaissance Icon

Langston Hughes was a central figure of the Harlem Renaissance, known for his impactful poetry, essays, and novels that explored African American identity and culture. His works celebrate the spirit and struggles of the Black community, addressing themes of hope, resilience, and social justice, making him a timeless voice of American literature.
