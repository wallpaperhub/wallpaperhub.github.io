Effortlessly Remove Image Backgrounds in Google Slides

To remove a background in Google Slides, select the image you want to edit. Click on "Format options" in the toolbar, then choose "Adjustments." Look for "Remove background" and click it. Google Slides will highlight the background; use the tools to refine your selection. Save by clicking outside the image when satisfied.
