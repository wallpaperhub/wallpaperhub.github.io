"The Fascinating Origin of Jollibee"

Jollibee, the iconic Filipino fast-food chain, originated in 1978 when Tony Tan Caktiong transformed his ice cream parlor into a burger restaurant in Quezon City. With a menu blending western fast food and local flavors, Jollibee quickly gained popularity, becoming a symbol of Filipino culture and identity, now with global presence.
