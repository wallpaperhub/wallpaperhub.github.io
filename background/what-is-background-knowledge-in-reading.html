Understanding Background Knowledge in Reading

Background knowledge in reading refers to the information and experiences a reader brings to a text. This knowledge enhances comprehension, allowing readers to make connections, infer meanings, and engage with the material on a deeper level. It plays a crucial role in understanding context, themes, and the author's intent.
