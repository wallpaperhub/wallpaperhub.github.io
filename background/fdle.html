Discover the Role of the Florida Department of Law Enforcement

The Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) plays a crucial role in maintaining public safety and ensuring justice in the state. It provides various services, including criminal investigations, forensic science, and the management of criminal history records. FDLE collaborates with local law enforcement agencies to safeguard communities and enhance crime prevention efforts.
