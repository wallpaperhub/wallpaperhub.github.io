Understanding Transparent Images: No Background Explained

When an image has no background, it is referred to as a "transparent image" or "PNG image." This type of image allows the subject to stand out without any distractions, making it perfect for logos, graphics, and overlays. Transparent images enhance design aesthetics by blending seamlessly into various backgrounds.
