Who Invented Hot Sauce? Discover Its Spicy Origins

Hot sauce, known for its fiery kick, has roots tracing back to ancient civilizations. While the exact inventor remains unknown, the use of spicy peppers dates back to the Aztecs and Incas. Modern hot sauce emerged in the 19th century, with brands like Tabasco popularizing it. Today, it’s a staple worldwide, enhancing flavors in diverse cuisines.
