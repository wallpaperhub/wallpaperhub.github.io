Discovering the Fascinating Journey of Salmon Runs

Salmon run refers to the migratory journey of salmon from freshwater rivers to the ocean and back again to spawn. This natural phenomenon is crucial for maintaining healthy ecosystems and supports various wildlife. Understanding salmon runs helps us appreciate their role in biodiversity and the importance of conservation efforts in preserving these habitats.
