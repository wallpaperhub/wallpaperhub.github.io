Discover Fascinating Facts About John Lewis

John Lewis is a renowned British retailer founded in 1864, known for its employee ownership model. The first store opened in Oxford and now operates over 40 shops across the UK. John Lewis is celebrated for its quality products and exceptional customer service, along with its iconic annual Christmas advert.
