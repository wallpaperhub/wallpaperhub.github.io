Do Medical Assistants Undergo Drug Testing?

Medical assistants may be subject to drug testing, especially when applying for jobs in healthcare settings. Employers often require tests to ensure a safe and compliant workplace. The specific policies can vary by facility and state regulations, but it’s essential for candidates to be aware of potential testing procedures during the hiring process.
