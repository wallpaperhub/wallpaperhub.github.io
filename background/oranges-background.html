All About Oranges: History, Benefits & Uses

Oranges are a vibrant fruit, rich in history and nutrition. Believed to have originated in Southeast Asia, they have spread globally, becoming a staple in diets. Known for their bright color and refreshing taste, oranges are packed with vitamin C and antioxidants, promoting health and wellness. Perfect for juices, snacks, or desserts!
