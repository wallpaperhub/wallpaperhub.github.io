Creative Collage Art: Express Yourself!

Collage is an art form that combines various materials, including photographs, paper, and found objects, to create a unified composition. It encourages creativity by allowing artists to express their ideas scrappily and dynamically. Whether digital or traditional, collages can tell powerful stories or evoke emotions through their layered visuals.
