“Discover the Rich History of Sushi: From Tradition to Global Delight”

Sushi, originating from Japan, dates back to the 8th century as a method of preserving fish in fermented rice. Over time, this evolved into the modern sushi we know today, featuring fresh ingredients, vinegared rice, and various rolls. Sushi has transcended borders, becoming a beloved global cuisine.
