How to Change Instagram Chat Theme Easily

To change the theme on Instagram chat, open the app and go to the conversation you want to customize. Tap on the name at the top of the chat. From there, select "Theme" and choose your favorite option from the available themes. Your new selection will instantly change the chat's appearance, making conversations more fun!
