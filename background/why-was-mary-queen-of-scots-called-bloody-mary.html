Understanding Mary, Queen of Scots: The Misconception of "Bloody Mary"

Mary, Queen of Scots, was not called "Bloody Mary"; that title actually refers to Mary I of England. Mary I earned it due to her persecution of Protestants during her reign in the 16th century. Confusion arises because both queens faced significant challenges and executed rivals, fueling their controversial legacies.
