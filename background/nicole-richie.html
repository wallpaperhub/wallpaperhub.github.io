Nicole Richie: Fashion Icon & Media Personality

Nicole Richie is a renowned fashion designer, author, and television personality. Best known for her role in "The Simple Life" alongside Paris Hilton, she has become a style icon and entrepreneur. Richie founded the fashion brand House of Harlow 1960 and has made significant contributions to the fashion and entertainment industries.
