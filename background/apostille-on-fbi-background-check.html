Apostille Your FBI Background Check: A Quick Guide

Obtaining an apostille on an FBI background check is essential for international use. This process verifies the authenticity of the document, making it valid in countries that are part of the Hague Apostille Convention. Start by getting your FBI background check, then apply for an apostille through your state's Secretary of State office to ensure your document meets international standards.
