Enhance Your Text with Photoshop Background Colors

Your text background color in Photoshop can greatly enhance your design's visual appeal. To change it, select the text layer, go to the 'Layer Style' section, and choose 'Color Overlay' to customize your background. This feature allows for vibrant colors and easy adjustments, ensuring your text stands out effectively against the design.
