J.K. Rowling: The Untold Story of the Harry Potter Author

J.K. Rowling, born on July 31, 1965, in Yate, England, is a renowned author best known for the Harry Potter series. Raised in a modest background, she faced numerous challenges before publishing her first book in 1997. The series gained immense popularity, making her one of the wealthiest writers globally and a powerful philanthropist.
