Exploring the Zine Revolution: A Creative Journey

Zines, or self-published magazines, emerged in the 1930s as a medium for personal expression and counterculture. Originating in marginalized communities, they provide a platform for alternative voices and art. Over decades, zines have evolved, embracing diverse topics and fostering DIY culture. They remain vital in today's digital age, offering tangible creativity.
