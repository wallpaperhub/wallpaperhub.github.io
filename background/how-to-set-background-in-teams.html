How to Set a Background in Microsoft Teams: A Quick Guide

To set a background in Microsoft Teams, join a meeting and click on the three dots (More actions) in the toolbar. Select "Show background effects." You can blur your background or choose from various images. To add your own image, click "Add new." Select the image and then apply it by clicking "Apply." Enjoy a personalized Teams experience!
