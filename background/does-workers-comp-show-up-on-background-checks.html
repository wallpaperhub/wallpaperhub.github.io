Do Workers Comp Claims Show Up on Background Checks?

Workers' compensation claims generally do not appear on background checks, as they are not considered public records. However, employers may be informed of a worker's comp history during the hiring process if it is relevant to job duties. Always review local laws and company policies for specific guidelines.
