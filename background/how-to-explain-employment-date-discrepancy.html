How to Address Employment Date Discrepancies Effectively

When addressing an employment date discrepancy, transparency is key. Start by acknowledging the difference and provide a clear explanation, such as overlapping jobs or site closure. Ensure to present supporting documentation if available. Conclude by expressing commitment to honesty and the importance of clear communication.
