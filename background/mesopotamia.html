Discover Mesopotamia: The Cradle of Civilization

Mesopotamia, often called the "Cradle of Civilization," was a historical region located between the Tigris and Euphrates rivers. It witnessed the rise of some of the world's first cities and empires, such as Sumer, Akkad, Babylon, and Assyria. This area fostered advancements in writing, law, and agriculture, shaping human history significantly.
