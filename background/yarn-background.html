Discovering the Rich History of Yarn

Yarn has a rich history dating back to ancient civilizations, where it played a crucial role in textile production. Made from fibers like wool and cotton, yarn has evolved over centuries, influencing various cultures and crafts. Today, it remains vital in knitting, crocheting, and weaving, allowing artists to express creativity through unique patterns and textures.
