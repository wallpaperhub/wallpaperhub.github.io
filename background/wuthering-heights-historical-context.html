Historical Context of Wuthering Heights: A Deep Dive

Wuthering Heights, published in 1847, reflects the social and cultural milieu of early 19th-century England. The Industrial Revolution, class struggles, and evolving gender roles shape its narrative. Set on the Yorkshire moors, the novel explores themes of passion, revenge, and nature, revealing stark contrasts between rural and industrial life.
