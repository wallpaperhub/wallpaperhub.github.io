American Gothic: Iconic Art Explored

American Gothic is a renowned painting created by Grant Wood in 1930, depicting a stern farmer and his daughter in front of a Gothic-style house. This iconic image symbolizes rural American strength, resilience, and the complexities of modern life. Its lasting influence reflects both art and cultural identity, captivating viewers worldwide.
