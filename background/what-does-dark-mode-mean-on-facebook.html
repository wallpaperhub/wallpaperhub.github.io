What is Dark Mode on Facebook? Discover its Benefits!

Dark mode on Facebook is a feature that changes the app's color scheme to darker tones, reducing the amount of blue light emitted by screens. This mode is designed to be easier on the eyes, especially in low-light environments. Users can enjoy a more comfortable browsing experience while maintaining battery life on their devices.
