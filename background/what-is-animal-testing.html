Understanding Animal Testing: Facts and Ethics

Animal testing involves using animals in experiments to assess the safety and efficacy of products, primarily in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and research. While it can provide valuable data on biological processes, ethical concerns and animal welfare advocates argue for alternative methods. This ongoing debate seeks a balance between scientific advancement and humane considerations.
