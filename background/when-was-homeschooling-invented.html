When Was Homeschooling Invented? History & Evolution

Homeschooling has roots that trace back to ancient times, but it became more formally recognized in the late 20th century. The modern homeschooling movement gained momentum in the 1970s, driven by a desire for personalized education and a critique of traditional schooling. Today, it offers diverse educational approaches tailored to families.
