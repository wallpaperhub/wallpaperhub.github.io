How to Change Background Color in Affinity Designer

Affinity Designer allows users to easily change the background color of their artwork, enhancing visual appeal. To change the background color, select the background layer in the Layers panel, then choose the 'Fill' option from the right sidebar. Pick your desired color, and watch your design transform!
