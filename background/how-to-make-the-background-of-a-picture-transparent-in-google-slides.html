Make Picture Background Transparent in Google Slides

To make a picture's background transparent in Google Slides, first select the image. Right-click and choose "Format Options." Under "Adjustments," find the "Transparency" slider. Move it to increase transparency, making the background clear while keeping the main subject visible. Click outside to see the changes.
