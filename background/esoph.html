Understanding the Esophagus: Health, Function, and Disorders

Esoph, short for esophagus, is a crucial muscular tube connecting the throat to the stomach. It plays a vital role in digestion by transporting food and liquids. The esophagus can be affected by various conditions, such as reflux, strictures, and cancer. Maintaining esophageal health is essential for overall well-being.
