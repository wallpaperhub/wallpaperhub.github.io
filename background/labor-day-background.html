Understanding Labor Day: A Tribute to Workers

Labor Day, celebrated on the first Monday in September, honors the contributions of American workers and the labor movement. Originating in the late 19th century, it symbolizes the fight for fair labor practices, workers' rights, and improved working conditions. Today, it serves as a tribute to all who contribute to our economy.
