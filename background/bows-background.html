Bows: A Brief History and Cultural Symbolism

Bows have a rich history, transcending cultures and eras. Originally used for hunting and warfare, bows have evolved into symbols of artistry and decoration. Their design varies from simple wooden types to intricate modern styles, often incorporated in fashion, gift-wrapping, and events. Bows continue to charm and inspire creativity worldwide.
