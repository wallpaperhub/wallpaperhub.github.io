Silence the Noise: Tips to Reduce Background Sound in Earphones

To reduce background noise in earphones, consider using noise-cancelling technology or high-quality ear tips that provide a snug fit. Alternatively, play your audio at a reasonable volume to drown out ambient sounds. Using apps designed for sound enhancement can also help improve your listening experience.
