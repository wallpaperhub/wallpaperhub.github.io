Discover the Vibrant World of Pop Art

Pop art emerged in the 1950s and is characterized by vibrant colors, bold lines, and imagery from popular culture. It celebrates mass media, consumerism, and everyday objects, transforming them into art. Artists like Andy Warhol and Roy Lichtenstein pushed boundaries, making art accessible and reflective of contemporary life.
