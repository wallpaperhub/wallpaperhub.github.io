Understanding Vermont's Background Check Laws

Vermont's background check laws focus on protecting privacy while ensuring safety. The state mandates background checks for firearm purchases, requiring sellers to perform checks through licensed dealers. Employers must adhere to regulations when conducting employee background checks, including obtaining consent and providing access to results.
