Worship Hands: The Power of Reverence and Praise

Worship hands are a powerful symbol of reverence and connection to the divine. They extend in gratitude, open in surrender, or uplifted in praise. Through various traditions, hands become instruments of prayer, touching hearts and sharing community. Engaging in worship with hands deepens faith and fosters a collective spiritual experience.
