Discover the Delicious World of Concha Pastry

Concha, also known as "pan de muerto" in some regions, is a traditional Mexican pastry characterized by its shell-like appearance and sweet flavor. Its name derives from the Spanish word for shell, reflecting its unique design. Often enjoyed with a hot beverage, this delightful treat has cultural significance, especially during Day of the Dead celebrations.
