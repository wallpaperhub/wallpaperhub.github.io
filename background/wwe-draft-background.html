WWE Draft: The Ultimate Brand Showdown

The WWE Draft, first introduced in 2002, was designed to enhance competition by dividing the roster between two brands, Raw and SmackDown. This annual event allows superstars to be selected, creating new rivalries and storylines. Over the years, the Draft has become a key element of WWE, shaping its future and engaging fans.
