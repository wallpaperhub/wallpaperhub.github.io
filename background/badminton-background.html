Explore the Rich History of Badminton

Badminton originated in ancient India, evolving from a game called "Poona." Introduced to Britain in the 19th century, it gained popularity quickly. The sport combines agility, strategy, and athleticism, played with a shuttlecock and rackets. Today, badminton enjoys global recognition, with major events like the Olympics spotlighting elite players.
