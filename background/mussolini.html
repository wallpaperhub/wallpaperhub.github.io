Mussolini: The Rise and Fall of Italy's Fascist Leader

Benito Mussolini was an Italian politician who led the National Fascist Party and served as Prime Minister from 1922 to 1943. He championed authoritarian rule, promoting nationalism and militarism while suppressing opposition. Mussolini's regime played a significant role in World War II, aligning with Nazi Germany before his eventual downfall.
