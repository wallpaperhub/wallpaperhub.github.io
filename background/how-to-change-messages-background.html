Customize Your Messaging App: Change Message Backgrounds Easily

To change your messaging app's background, open the app and go to settings. Look for 'Themes' or 'Background', then select a new image or color from the available options. Save your changes to personalize your messaging experience. Enjoy a fresh, customized look as you chat with friends!
