Who Was Jean-Michel Basquiat? Understanding His Art and Impact

Jean-Michel Basquiat was a groundbreaking American artist known for his raw, neo-expressionist style. Emerging from the New York City graffiti scene in the late 1970s, he gained fame for his vibrant paintings that explored themes of race, identity, and social issues. His unique fusion of text and imagery continues to influence contemporary art.
