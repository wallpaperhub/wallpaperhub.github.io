Six the Musical: Queens Reimagined

The musical "Six" reimagines the lives of the six wives of Henry VIII, presenting them as fierce, modern-day pop stars. Written by Toby Marlow and Lucy Moss, it combines rich historical context with vibrant music and engaging choreography. Each queen shares her story, transforming their struggles into a compelling celebration of female empowerment.
