Fix Background Issues on Remote Desktop Connection

When using a remote desktop connection, your background may be turned off to optimize performance and reduce bandwidth usage. This setting ensures smoother operation, but can also lead to a less engaging user experience. To enhance your remote desktop sessions, consider adjusting your settings to enable the background if bandwidth allows.
