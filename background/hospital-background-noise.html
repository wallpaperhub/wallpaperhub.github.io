Understanding Hospital Background Noise: Impact & Solutions

Hospital background noise significantly impacts patient recovery and staff efficiency. Common sounds include beeping machines, conversations, and footsteps, which can cause stress and disrupt sleep. With an increase in awareness, many hospitals are implementing sound management strategies to create a quieter, more healing environment for patients and caregivers alike.
