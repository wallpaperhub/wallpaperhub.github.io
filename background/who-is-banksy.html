Who is Banksy? The Mysterious Street Artist Explained

Banksy is a pseudonymous England-based street artist, political activist, and film director known for his provocative and socially charged graffiti art. His works, often laden with satire and dark humor, address themes like war, consumerism, and social inequality. Despite his fame, Banksy's true identity remains a mystery, contributing to his allure and intrigue.
