Understanding Background Data Usage: A Quick Guide

Background data usage refers to the data consumed by apps and services running in the background of your device, even when you're not actively using them. This includes updates, notifications, and syncing. Understanding background data can help you manage your data limits, improve battery life, and enhance performance on mobile devices.
