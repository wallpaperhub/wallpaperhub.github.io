Discover the Power of Odin: The Allfather of Norse Mythology

Odin, a major deity in Norse mythology, is revered as the god of wisdom, poetry, war, and death. He is often depicted with one eye, having sacrificed the other for knowledge. Odin leads the Valkyries and rules over Valhalla, welcoming fallen warriors. His legendary quests and pursuit of wisdom define him as a complex, multi-faceted figure.
