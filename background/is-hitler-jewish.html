Is Hitler Jewish? Debunking the Myths

Adolf Hitler, the infamous leader of Nazi Germany, is not Jewish. This myth stems from various conspiracy theories and misconceptions about his ancestry. Historically, Hitler was known for his vehement anti-Semitic beliefs and policies that led to the Holocaust, making any claim of Jewish heritage unlikely and unfounded.
