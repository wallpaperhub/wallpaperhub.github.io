Discover the Fascinating History of Water Polo

Water polo originated in the late 19th century in England as a competitive sport resembling rugby, played in water. It evolved in the early 20th century, gaining popularity in Europe and the U.S. The sport became an Olympic event in 1900 for men and 2000 for women, showcasing teamwork, strategy, and athleticism in challenging aquatic conditions.
