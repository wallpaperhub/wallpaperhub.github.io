Understanding Socioeconomic Status: Key Insights

Socioeconomic status (SES) encompasses a person's social and economic position in relation to others, based on income, education, and occupation. It influences access to resources, quality of life, and opportunities, shaping individuals' health, education, and overall well-being. Understanding SES is crucial for addressing inequality and fostering social mobility.
