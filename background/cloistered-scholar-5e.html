“Unlocking the Cloistered Scholar: Mastering Knowledge in D&D 5e”

In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, a Cloistered Scholar is a variant of the wizard or knowledge-focused class, who spends years in isolation for study. This character thrives on learning, often gaining proficiency in valuable skills like Arcana or History. They may possess unique spells and abilities, showcasing their profound understanding of the arcane and scholarly pursuits.
