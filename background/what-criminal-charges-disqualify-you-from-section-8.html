Criminal Charges That Disqualify You from Section 8

Certain criminal charges can disqualify individuals from receiving Section 8 housing assistance. These charges typically include drug-related offenses, violent crimes, sexual offenses, and other felonies. Responding to these concerns, housing authorities often perform background checks. Stay informed to avoid disqualification.
