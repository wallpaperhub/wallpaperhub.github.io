Discover the Healing Power of Asana: Yoga Postures Explained

Asana, rooted in ancient Indian traditions, refers to a physical posture or position during yoga practice. The term is derived from the Sanskrit word meaning "seat." Various asanas contribute to physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual growth. They are fundamental in yoga, promoting flexibility, strength, and mindfulness.
