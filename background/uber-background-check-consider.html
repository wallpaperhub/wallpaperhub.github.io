Understanding Uber's Background Check Process

When applying to drive for Uber, background checks are a critical step in ensuring safety and trust. Uber reviews criminal history, driving records, and personal identification to assess driver eligibility. These checks ensure that riders can feel secure and protected during their travels, reinforcing Uber's commitment to safety.
