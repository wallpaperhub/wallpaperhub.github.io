How to Change Your Instagram Story Background Color

Changing the background color of your Instagram Story is easy and fun! To do this, simply create a new story and tap on the color palette icon. Choose your preferred color and then press and hold the screen to fill the entire background. You can also use gradient colors for a stylish effect. Enhance your stories instantly!
