Listen to YouTube with Screen Off: Easy Tips

To listen to YouTube with the screen off, you can use the YouTube Premium subscription, enabling background play. Alternatively, use a web browser like Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android). Open YouTube, play your video, then exit the app; continue listening. Ensure desktop mode is enabled for smooth playback.
