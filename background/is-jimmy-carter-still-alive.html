Is Jimmy Carter Alive? Updates on the 39th President

As of October 2023, Jimmy Carter, the 39th President of the United States, is still alive. He celebrated his 99th birthday on October 1, 2023, making him the longest-living U.S. president in history. Carter is known for his humanitarian work and post-presidency efforts, particularly through the Carter Center.
