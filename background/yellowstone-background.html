Discover Yellowstone: Nature's First National Park

Yellowstone National Park, established in 1872, is the first national park in the world, renowned for its stunning geothermal features, diverse wildlife, and breathtaking landscapes. Spanning over 3,400 square miles across Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho, it showcases magnificent geysers like Old Faithful and picturesque lakes, making it a must-visit destination.
