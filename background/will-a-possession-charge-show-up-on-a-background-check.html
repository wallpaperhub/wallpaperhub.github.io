Do Possession Charges Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, a possession charge can show up on a background check. If the charge is still on your criminal record, employers, landlords, and other entities may see it when they conduct a background check. The specifics can vary based on the state laws and the type of check being performed. Expunging or sealing the record can help mitigate this issue.
