Exploring the Rich Tapestry of Folklore

Folklore encompasses the traditional beliefs, myths, legends, and practices of a culture. It reflects the values and traditions passed through generations, serving as a connection between the past and present. Folklore can manifest in stories, songs, rituals, and art, enriching cultural identity and providing insight into human experiences.
