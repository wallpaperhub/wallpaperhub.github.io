Explore the Intriguing Background of Fire Emblem

Fire Emblem is a tactical role-playing game series developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo. Set in a fantasy world, the franchise features a mix of strategic combat, character development, and deep narratives. Players control a roster of characters, while key gameplay elements include permadeath, class systems, and relationships.
