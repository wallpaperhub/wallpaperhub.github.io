What is a Live Scan Background Check?

A live scan background check is a digital method of capturing fingerprints that are then checked against state and federal databases for criminal records. It's commonly used in various job applications, particularly in fields like education, healthcare, and law enforcement. This efficient process enhances security and safety for employers and communities.
