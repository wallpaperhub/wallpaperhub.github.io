When Was Ray Bradbury Born? Discover His Birthdate!

Ray Bradbury was born on August 22, 1920, in Waukegan, Illinois. He became one of the most celebrated American authors, renowned for his distinctive contributions to science fiction and fantasy. His works, such as "Fahrenheit 451" and "The Martian Chronicles," have left a profound impact on literature and continue to influence readers worldwide.
