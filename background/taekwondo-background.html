Discover the Origins and Principles of Taekwondo

Taekwondo, a Korean martial art, combines self-defense, sport, and philosophy. Originating from ancient Korean warfare and influenced by Japanese karate, it emphasizes high kicks, speed, and agility. Taekwondo promotes physical fitness, discipline, and respect while encouraging personal growth and confidence through its structured training.
