Exploring the Background of 1 John: Key Insights

1 John is an epistle in the New Testament, traditionally attributed to the Apostle John. It addresses early Christian communities facing internal conflicts, heretical teachings, and ethical dilemmas. Written to affirm the truth of Jesus Christ and promote love among believers, this letter emphasizes the importance of faith and communal unity.
