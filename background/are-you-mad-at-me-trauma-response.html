Understanding the "Are You Mad at Me?" Trauma Response

Feeling anxious after someone asks "Are you mad at me?" often signals a trauma response rooted in past experiences. This can lead to fear of abandonment or rejection, regardless of the current situation. It's crucial to recognize these feelings and seek understanding, allowing for healthier communication and emotional healing in relationships.
