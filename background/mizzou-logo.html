Mizzou Logo: Strength & Tradition

The Mizzou logo, representing the University of Missouri, showcases a proud tiger, symbolizing strength and determination. Its distinctive black and gold color scheme embodies school spirit and tradition. Used in athletics and branding, the logo unites students and alumni, making it a powerful emblem of community pride and achievement.
