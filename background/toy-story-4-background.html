"Background of Toy Story 4: A Pixar Adventure"

"Toy Story 4" expands the beloved Pixar universe, introducing new characters and deeper themes of friendship and self-identity. Released in 2019, it follows Woody, Buzz, and their friends on a road trip adventure after Bonnie creates a new toy, Forky. The film explores concepts of belonging, purpose, and moving on, resonating with audiences young and old.
