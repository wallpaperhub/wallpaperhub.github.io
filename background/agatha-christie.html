Explore the World of Agatha Christie: Queen of Crime

Agatha Christie is renowned as the "Queen of Crime," having penned over 60 detective novels, including classics like "Murder on the Orient Express" and "And Then There Were None." Her intricate plots and unforgettable characters, notably Hercule Poirot and Miss Marple, have captivated readers for generations, making her one of the best-selling authors in history.
