Crafting Captivating Hooks & Strong Thesis Statements

In writing, a hook captures the reader's attention, sparking curiosity or emotion. A thesis statement presents the main argument or point of the piece, guiding the reader through your perspective. Together, they create a compelling introduction that motivates readers to continue exploring your ideas. Both are essential for engaging writing.
