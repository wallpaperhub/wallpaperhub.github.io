How to Disable Virtual Background in Zoom

To turn off the virtual background in Zoom, start by joining a meeting. Click on the arrow next to the "Stop Video" button. Select "Video Settings." In the settings window, navigate to the "Background & Filters" tab. Here, choose "None" under the "Virtual Background" options. Your background will revert to the original one.
