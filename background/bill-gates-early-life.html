Bill Gates: The Early Years of a Tech Pioneer

Bill Gates was born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington. Growing up in an affluent family, he exhibited an early passion for computers and programming. At just 13, he wrote his first software program. Gates attended Harvard University but dropped out to start Microsoft, which would revolutionize personal computing.
