Discover the Legendary Background of King Arthur

Arthur, a character steeped in legend, has roots in medieval British folklore. Often regarded as a noble king, he is best known for wielding the magical sword Excalibur and leading the Knights of the Round Table. His adventures symbolize bravery, honor, and chivalry, inspiring countless tales and adaptations over centuries.
