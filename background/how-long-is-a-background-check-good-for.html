"How Long Are Background Checks Valid?"

A background check is typically valid for anywhere from one to seven years, depending on the type of check and the specific laws in your area. Employers usually conduct them at the time of hiring, but updates may be necessary if required for certain positions. Always check local regulations for your specific situation.
