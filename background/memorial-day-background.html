Memorial Day: Honoring Heroes and Remembering Sacrifice

Memorial Day, observed in the United States on the last Monday of May, honors the brave men and women who sacrificed their lives serving in the military. This day of remembrance emerged after the Civil War, initially called Decoration Day, as families adorned graves with flowers. It's a poignant time to reflect on courage and honor.
