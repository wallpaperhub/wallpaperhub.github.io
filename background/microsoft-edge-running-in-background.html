Optimize Microsoft Edge: Manage Background Running

Microsoft Edge running in the background can enhance your browsing experience by allowing updates, syncing data, and enabling features like notifications to work seamlessly. However, it may consume system resources and impact battery life. To manage this, check your settings and disable background activity if necessary.
