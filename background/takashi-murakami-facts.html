Fascinating Takashi Murakami Facts You Need to Know

Takashi Murakami is a renowned Japanese contemporary artist known for his colorful, playful style that merges fine art with commercial culture. He coined the term "superflat," reflecting post-war Japanese society. Notable works include the smiling flowers and his collaborations with brands like Louis Vuitton.
