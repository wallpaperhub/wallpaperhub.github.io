The Evolution of Lean: From Toyota to Global Industry

The history of lean methodology traces back to post-World War II Japan, particularly with Toyota's production system. Streamlining processes to eliminate waste, improve efficiency, and foster continuous improvement, lean principles gained traction globally in the late 20th century. Organizations adopted these practices to enhance productivity and quality across various industries.
