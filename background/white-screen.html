Troubleshooting White Screen Issues on Your Device

A "white screen" often refers to a blank display that can appear on devices when software malfunctions or during booting. It may indicate issues such as application errors, hardware faults, or corrupted files. Troubleshooting might involve restarting the device, checking connections, or resetting software settings to resolve the problem.
