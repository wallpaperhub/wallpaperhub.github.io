What to Do If Your Background Check Contains Errors

If a background check provided incorrect information, act promptly. First, review the report for errors. Gather supporting documents to prove inaccuracies. Contact the background check company to dispute the findings and request a correction. If necessary, notify potential employers about the discrepancy for clarity.
