Enhance Your Modeling Background: Tips & Techniques

Modeling background refers to the visual and artistic elements that support the subject in photography and design. This includes textures, colors, and lighting that enhance the modeling subject's features. A well-crafted background not only highlights the model but also creates an engaging narrative, adding depth and context to the overall composition.
