Delicious Smores: A Sweet Campfire Treat

Smores, a beloved campfire treat, blend graham crackers, chocolate, and toasted marshmallows. Originating in the United States, their name reflects the craving they inspire—“s'more.” Traditionally enjoyed during camping trips, they symbolize warmth, nostalgia, and the joy of sharing sweet moments with friends and family.
