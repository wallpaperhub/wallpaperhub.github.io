Norway Flag: History, Colors, and Symbolism

The Norway flag, featuring a red backdrop adorned with a blue cross outlined in white, symbolizes the country's rich Viking heritage and connection to Nordic culture. Each color has historical significance, representing the landscapes and history of Norway, while the cross reflects the nation's Christian heritage, uniting its people.
