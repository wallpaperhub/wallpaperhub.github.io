Xavier Becerra: Champion of Health and Social Justice

Xavier Becerra is an accomplished American politician and attorney, currently serving as the Secretary of Health and Human Services. A former Congressman from California, he has been a strong advocate for healthcare reform, immigrant rights, and social justice. Becerra's leadership focuses on expanding access to healthcare and addressing public health challenges.
