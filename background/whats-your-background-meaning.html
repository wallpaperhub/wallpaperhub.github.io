What Does Your Background Mean? Unpacking Identity

Understanding "background" means looking at the experiences, education, and culture that shape an individual. It encompasses personal history, societal influences, and significant events that contribute to one's identity. Knowing someone's background helps us appreciate their perspective and values in various contexts, including work and relationships.
