A house fire is a devastating event, often caused by electrical malfunctions, cooking accidents, or negligent use of flammable materials. The rapid spread of flames can lead to significant damage, loss of personal belongings, and even loss of life. Fire safety measures are essential to prevent such tragedies and protect families.
