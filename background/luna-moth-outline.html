Discover the Fascinating Luna Moth: Nature's Green Marvel

The Luna Moth, known for its striking green wings and delicate appearance, is a captivating nocturnal insect. Found in North America, it boasts a wingspan of up to 4.5 inches. The moth is notable for its short adult lifespan, during which it focuses solely on reproduction. Understanding their life cycle, habitat, and conservation status is essential.
