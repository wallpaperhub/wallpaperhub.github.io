Explore Wicca: A Modern Pagan Path

Wicca is a modern Pagan spiritual practice that emphasizes nature worship, rituals, and witchcraft. It draws on ancient traditions, celebrating the cycles of the moon and seasons. Practitioners, known as Wiccans, often focus on personal empowerment, healing, and connecting with the divine feminine and masculine energies.
