Fascinating Facts About Insects

Insects are the most diverse group of organisms on Earth, with roughly one million known species. They play crucial roles in ecosystems as pollinators, decomposers, and as a food source for other animals. Their unique anatomy, including exoskeletons and compound eyes, enables them to thrive in various environments, making them essential to biodiversity.
