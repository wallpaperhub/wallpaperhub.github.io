Who Owns Workday? Understanding Its Shareholders

Workday Inc. is a publicly traded company specializing in enterprise cloud applications for finance and human resources. Founded in 2005 by Aneel Bhusri and Dave Duffield, it has grown significantly and is now owned by its shareholders. Major institutional investors and mutual funds hold substantial stakes in the company.
