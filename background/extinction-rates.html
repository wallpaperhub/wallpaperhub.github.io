Rising Extinction Rates: What You Need to Know

Extinction rates are rising alarmingly due to habitat loss, climate change, and human activity. Scientists estimate that species are disappearing at 1,000 times the natural rate, with many unable to adapt to rapidly changing environments. Protecting biodiversity is crucial for ecosystem health and resilience.
