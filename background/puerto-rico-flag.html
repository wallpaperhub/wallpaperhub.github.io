Discover the Rich History of the Puerto Rico Flag

The Puerto Rico flag features five horizontal stripes: three red and two white, with a blue triangle containing a white five-pointed star on the left. The red symbolizes the blood shed by independence fighters, the white represents peace, and the blue stands for the sky and coastal waters. It’s a proud emblem of Puerto Rican identity.
