Celebrating Black Women in History

Black women have made significant contributions throughout history, from trailblazing activists like Harriet Tubman and Sojourner Truth to influential figures in art and science such as Zora Neale Hurston and Marie Maynard Daly. Their strength, resilience, and achievements have shaped culture and society, inspiring future generations.
