Gatorade: The Origins and Evolution of Sports Hydration

Gatorade was developed in 1965 by University of Florida researchers to help athletes rehydrate and replenish electrolytes lost during strenuous activity. Initially serving the university's football team, it gained popularity and became a national phenomenon. Today, Gatorade is synonymous with sports hydration, featuring various flavors and formulations.
