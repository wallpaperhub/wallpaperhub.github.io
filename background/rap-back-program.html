Understanding the RAP Back Program: A Quick Guide

The RAP Back Program is a vital initiative that enhances public safety by allowing law enforcement agencies to receive real-time notifications about individuals with criminal histories. When a person with a previous charge commits a new offense, the program ensures prompt communication, helping authorities make informed decisions swiftly and effectively.
