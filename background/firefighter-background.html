Understanding the Firefighter Background: Training & Skills

Firefighters are essential professionals trained to respond to emergencies, such as fires, accidents, and natural disasters. Typically, they undergo extensive training, including physical fitness, firefighting techniques, and emergency medical response. Many have backgrounds in related fields or volunteer experience, emphasizing teamwork and quick decision-making.
