Yale Logo: A Symbol of Tradition and Excellence

The Yale logo, a symbol of excellence and tradition, features a bold serif typeface that prominently displays "Yale University." It incorporates an ancient seal featuring the university's founding year, 1701. With its deep blue and white colors, the logo epitomizes the institution's prestigious heritage and its commitment to education.
