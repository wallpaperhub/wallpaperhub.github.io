Discovering the Elizabethan Era: Art, Culture & Legacy

The Elizabethan Era, spanning from 1558 to 1603, marked a vibrant period in English history. It was characterized by the flourishing of arts, including the works of Shakespeare and advancements in exploration. Notable for its cultural achievements, this era also faced challenges like political intrigue and the Spanish Armada. The legacy remains influential today.
