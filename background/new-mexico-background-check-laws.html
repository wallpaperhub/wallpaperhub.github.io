Understanding New Mexico's Background Check Laws

New Mexico's background check laws require checks for firearms purchases through licensed dealers. These laws help ensure public safety by screening individuals for criminal history and mental health issues. Certain exemptions may apply; however, it's crucial for buyers to understand their obligations to comply with state regulations.
