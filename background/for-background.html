Understanding Backgrounds: Importance in Design

In design and art, "background" refers to the space behind the main subject, shaping perception and context. It establishes mood, adds depth, and enhances storytelling. Thoughtfully chosen backgrounds can evoke emotions, create focus, and enrich the viewer's experience, making them essential in visual compositions.
