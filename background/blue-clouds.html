Understanding Blue Clouds: A Visual Wonder

Blue clouds, often a result of scattering light, present a unique visual spectacle in the sky. When sunlight interacts with atmospheric particles, it can create various cloud colors, and under certain conditions, clouds can appear bluish. This phenomenon is not only beautiful but also intriguing, inviting observers to explore the science behind it.
