Discover the Delicious History of Shaved Ice

Shaved ice has a rich history, originating in Japan as Kakigori, where finely shaven ice is topped with sweet sauces. Over time, it evolved globally, with variations like Hawaiian shave ice featuring vibrant syrups and condensed milk. Its popularity soared, especially in tropical regions, becoming a refreshing summer favorite.
