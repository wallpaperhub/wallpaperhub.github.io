Understanding Child Dedication: A Commitment to Faith

Child dedication is a spiritual practice wherein parents commit to raising their child in alignment with their faith and values. Traditionally observed in many religious communities, this ceremony symbolizes gratitude, responsibility, and a hope for the child's future. It fosters a supportive community, encouraging spiritual growth and guidance.
