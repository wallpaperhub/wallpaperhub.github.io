Roe v. Wade: Impact on Reproductive Rights

Roe v. Wade was a landmark 1973 Supreme Court case that established a woman's legal right to have an abortion under the Constitution's right to privacy. This decision intensified debates over reproductive rights and continues to influence American politics and public opinion on women's health and autonomy.
