Massachusetts Fingerprint Background Check: What You Need to Know

In Massachusetts, a fingerprint background check is a crucial process used to ensure the safety and security of individuals in various sectors, especially in education, healthcare, and childcare. This involves capturing fingerprints electronically and checking them against state and federal databases for criminal history, helping employers make informed hiring decisions.
