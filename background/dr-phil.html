Understanding Dr. Phil: Advice, Insights, and Impact

He gained fame through his appearances on "The Oprah Winfrey Show," later launching his own talk show. Dr. Phil addresses topics ranging from relationship struggles to mental health, making him a trusted source for many seeking guidance.
