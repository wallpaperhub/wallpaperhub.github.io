Who Creates Milwaukee Tools? Discover the Manufacturer!

Milwaukee Tools is manufactured by the Milwaukee Electric Tool Corporation, an American company founded in 1924. Known for their rugged design and high performance, Milwaukee tools are popular among professionals in the construction and trades industries. Their commitment to innovation ensures reliable and efficient tools for every job.
