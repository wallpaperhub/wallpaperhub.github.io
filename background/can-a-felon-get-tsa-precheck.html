Can Felons Obtain TSA PreCheck? Explained

Yes, a felon can apply for TSA PreCheck, but approval is not guaranteed. Eligibility largely depends on the type and severity of the felony. Applicants must complete a background check, and certain convictions may disqualify them from the program. It's best to check the TSA's official guidelines for specific disqualifications.
