Understanding Stems: The Plant's Lifeline

A stem is a vital part of a plant that supports leaves, flowers, and fruits, playing a critical role in transporting nutrients and water. Stems can vary in thickness and length, and can be herbaceous or woody. They also serve as storage organs in some plants, showcasing their adaptability in various environments.
