Understanding the Louisiana Purchase: A Historical Overview

The Louisiana Purchase was a landmark agreement in 1803, where the United States acquired approximately 828,000 square miles of territory from France, effectively doubling the nation's size. This deal, orchestrated by President Thomas Jefferson, facilitated westward expansion and played a crucial role in shaping America's future.
