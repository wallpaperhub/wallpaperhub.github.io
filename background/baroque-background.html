Exploring the Richness of Baroque Art and Architecture

The Baroque period, spanning the 17th and early 18th centuries, is characterized by its grandiosity and emotional intensity in art and architecture. Originating in Italy, Baroque style features dynamic compositions, lavish decorations, and dramatic lighting. This movement reflects the complexities of human emotion and the power of the Catholic Church during its time.
