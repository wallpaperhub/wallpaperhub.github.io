Who Owns CBS Network? Insights & Ownership

CBS Network is owned by Paramount Global, which was formerly known as ViacomCBS. The company oversees a variety of media and entertainment assets, including broadcasting, cable, and digital content, making CBS one of the prominent networks in the United States. Its extensive programming includes news, sports, and entertainment shows.
