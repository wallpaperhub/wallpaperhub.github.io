Optimize Downloads with Background Intelligent Transfer Service

Background Intelligent Transfer Service (BITS) is a powerful technology developed by Microsoft that allows for the transfer of files in the background. It's particularly useful for applications that need to download or upload files without significantly impacting system performance or user experience. BITS efficiently manages bandwidth, ensuring that transfers occur without interrupting other network activities.
