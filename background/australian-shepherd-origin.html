Discover the Origins of the Australian Shepherd

The Australian Shepherd, despite its name, originated in the United States during the 19th century. Developed as a herding dog for livestock, particularly in the western states, it was influenced by various breeds, including the Basque shepherd dogs brought by Australian immigrants. This breed is known for its intelligence, agility, and strong work ethic.
