Understanding Background Checks: A Comprehensive Guide

A background check is a process used to verify an individual's identity and assess their suitability for a specific role, particularly in employment. It often includes criminal records, credit history, employment verification, and education checks. Organizations use these checks to ensure safety and trustworthiness.
