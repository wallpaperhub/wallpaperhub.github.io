Discover the Iconic Penn State Logo: Facts and History

The Penn State logo, a simple yet iconic design, features a white lion's head silhouette on a blue background, representing strength and unity. This emblem symbolizes pride and tradition for the university, embodying its commitment to excellence in education, athletics, and community. Recognized nationwide, it inspires belonging among students and alumni.
