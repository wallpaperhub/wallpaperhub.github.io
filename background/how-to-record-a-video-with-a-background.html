How to Record Video with an Engaging Background

To record a video with a background, first choose a clean, clutter-free space. Consider using a green screen for added creativity or a virtual background with software. Ensure adequate lighting and position the camera at eye level. Adjust the framing to include both you and the background, making it visually appealing.
