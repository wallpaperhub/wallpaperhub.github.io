"Understanding Loom Backgrounds: A Guide to Textured Designs"

A loom background is a textured, woven design that provides visual depth and interest to various projects. It refers to the backdrop created by a loom, showcasing intricate patterns and colors. This background can be used in textile art, weaving, and digital designs to enhance aesthetics and create unique visual narratives.
