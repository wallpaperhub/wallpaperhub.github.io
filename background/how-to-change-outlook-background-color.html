Change Your Outlook Background Color Effortlessly

Changing the Outlook background color is simple! Go to File > Options, then select "General." Click on "Office Theme" to choose your preferred background color. You can customize it further by going to the "View" tab, selecting "View Settings," then "Conditional Formatting" to apply specific color settings to emails. Enjoy your personalized Outlook!
