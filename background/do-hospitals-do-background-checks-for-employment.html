Do Hospitals Conduct Employee Background Checks?

Hospitals prioritize safety, which often means conducting thorough background checks on potential employees. These checks typically include criminal history, employment verification, and credentialing to ensure a trustworthy and qualified workforce. Such measures help maintain a secure environment for patients and staff alike.
