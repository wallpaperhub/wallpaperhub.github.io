Understanding Ben Day Dots: A Retro Art Technique

Ben Day dots are a classic printing technique that involves using small, distinct dots to create shading and texture in illustrations. Originating in the 19th century, this style gained prominence in pop art, particularly in the works of Roy Lichtenstein. Today, Ben Day dots continue to influence modern design, adding a playful and retro aesthetic to various visual media.
