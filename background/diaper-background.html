Diaper Evolution: A Brief History

Diapers have evolved significantly from their early roots. Historically, they were made from cloth or natural fibers, designed primarily for function over form. Today, advancements in materials and technology have led to disposable diapers that offer convenience and comfort, making them a staple in modern parenting. Understanding their background helps appreciate their role in child care.
