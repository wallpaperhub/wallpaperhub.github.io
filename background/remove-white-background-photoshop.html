Effortless White Background Removal in Photoshop

To remove a white background in Photoshop, start by opening your image in the program. Use the Magic Wand Tool to select the white area, then press Delete to remove it. If needed, refine your selection with the Lasso Tool. Save your image in PNG format to maintain transparency. This technique is perfect for creating clean visuals!
