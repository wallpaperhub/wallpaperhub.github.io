Understanding Tertullian's Doctrine of the Trinity

Tertullian, an early Christian theologian, was pivotal in developing the doctrine of the Trinity. He coined the term "Trinity" to describe the coexistence of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit as three distinct persons in one God. His writings laid the foundation for later theological debates and formulations in Christianity.
