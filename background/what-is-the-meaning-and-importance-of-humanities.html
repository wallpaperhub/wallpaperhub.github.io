Understanding Humanities: Meaning & Importance

Humanities encompass the study of human culture, history, and values, providing insights into the human experience. Their importance lies in fostering critical thinking, empathy, and ethical reasoning, essential for navigating societal challenges. By understanding diverse perspectives, humanities help us connect with others and enrich our lives.
