Is Dark Mode Better for Your Eyes? Pros and Cons Explained

Dark mode may reduce eye strain in low-light conditions by decreasing glare and blue light exposure. However, its effectiveness can vary by individual preference and lighting situations. It's essential to consider your environment and comfort when choosing between dark and light modes for screen use.
