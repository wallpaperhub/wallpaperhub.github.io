Yale University: Location in New Haven, CT

Yale University is located in New Haven, Connecticut. Established in 1701, it is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the United States. Nestled in a vibrant city known for its rich history, cultural institutions, and lively atmosphere, Yale's campus features stunning architecture and a diverse community, making it a remarkable place to study.
