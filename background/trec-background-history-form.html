"Understanding TREC: A Brief History of the Text Retrieval Conference"

The TREC (Text REtrieval Conference) has evolved since its inception in 1992, aiming to advance the state of information retrieval through large-scale evaluations. It fosters collaboration among researchers and practitioners to refine algorithms, datasets, and metrics, enriching the development of robust search technologies.
