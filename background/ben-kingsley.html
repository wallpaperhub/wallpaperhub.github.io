Ben Kingsley: The Versatile Actor Behind Iconic Roles

Sir Ben Kingsley is an acclaimed British actor known for his versatile performances in film, television, and theater. Born on December 31, 1943, in Scarborough, England, he rose to fame with his portrayal of Mahatma Gandhi in the 1982 film "Gandhi," earning an Academy Award for Best Actor. Kingsley's career spans decades, showcasing his talent across genres.
