Mastering Triangle Patterns in Trading

The triangle pattern is a popular chart formation in technical analysis, often signaling potential price movements. It consists of converging trendlines, forming a triangle shape. Traders analyze the breakout direction to make informed decisions. Understanding this pattern can enhance trading strategies and improve market predictions.
