Understanding 10-Year Background Check Laws by State

Many states in the U.S. allow employers to conduct background checks spanning up to 10 years. These checks typically include criminal records, employment history, and educational verification. However, regulations vary by state regarding what can be reported and for how long, affecting potential employment opportunities for individuals.
