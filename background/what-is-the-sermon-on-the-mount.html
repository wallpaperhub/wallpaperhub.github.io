Sermon on the Mount: Jesus' Guide to Living Well

The Sermon on the Mount is a collection of teachings by Jesus found in the Gospel of Matthew (chapters 5-7). It includes the Beatitudes, moral guidelines, and principles like love, forgiveness, and humility. This pivotal sermon emphasizes inner virtue over mere external compliance, shaping Christian ethical principles.
