Understanding "Decisional" in Background Checks

Decisional on a background check refers to the assessment of information that influences hiring decisions. It indicates whether an applicant poses a risk based on their history, such as criminal records or employment gaps. Employers use this term to clarify which findings directly impact their choice to hire or reject a candidate.
