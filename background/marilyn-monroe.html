Marilyn Monroe: The Timeless Icon of Glamour

Marilyn Monroe, an iconic actress and cultural icon, mesmerized audiences with her charm and beauty. Rising to fame in the 1950s, she starred in classics like "Some Like It Hot" and "The Seven Year Itch." Known for her trademark blonde hair and sultry voice, Monroe became a symbol of glamor and complicated femininity, leaving a lasting legacy.
