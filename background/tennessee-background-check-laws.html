Tennessee Background Check Laws: What You Need to Know

Tennessee background check laws mandate that employers perform background checks on potential employees for certain positions, particularly in education and childcare. The laws protect the privacy of individuals while allowing employers to ensure a safe workplace. Applicants have rights regarding the information gathered during these checks.
