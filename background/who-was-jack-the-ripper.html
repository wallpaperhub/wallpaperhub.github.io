Unveiling the Mystery: Who Was Jack the Ripper?

Jack the Ripper was an unidentified serial killer active in London's Whitechapel district in 1888. His gruesome murders, primarily targeting female sex workers, were marked by extreme violence. The case sparked widespread media coverage and intense public fascination, leading to countless theories and investigations. Despite extensive inquiries, his identity remains a mystery.
