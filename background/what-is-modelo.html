Discover Modelo: Mexico's Iconic Beer Brand

Modelo refers to Grupo Modelo, a renowned Mexican brewery established in 1925, famous for producing beers like Corona, Modelo Especial, and Negra Modelo. The brand is celebrated for its high-quality brews, combining traditional brewing methods with modern techniques, and has gained global popularity, making it a staple in many bars and restaurants.
