History of PCR: From Invention to Innovation

Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) was developed in 1983 by Kary Mullis, revolutionizing molecular biology. This technique allows for the amplification of specific DNA sequences, making it essential for genetic research, forensic analysis, and medical diagnostics. PCR's innovative approach has led to breakthroughs in genomics and biotechnology.
