Discover the Thrills of BMX: A Brief History

BMX, or Bicycle Motocross, originated in the late 1970s in California, evolving from motocross racing. It quickly gained popularity, emphasizing tricks and stunts. Today, BMX combines racing with freestyle events, showcasing athlete creativity and skill. The sport continues to grow, inspiring riders worldwide with its vibrant culture and community.
