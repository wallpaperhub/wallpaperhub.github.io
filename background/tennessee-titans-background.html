Tennessee Titans: Team History & Highlights

The Tennessee Titans are a professional football team based in Nashville, Tennessee. Established in 1960 as the Houston Oilers, they became the Titans in 1999 after relocating. Known for their blue, navy, and white colors, they play at Nissan Stadium and have made multiple playoff appearances, including a Super Bowl appearance in 2000.
