Exploring the Evolution of 3D Printing: From Concept to Reality

The history of 3D printing began in the 1980s with Charles Hull's invention of stereolithography, a process using UV light to solidify resin layer by layer. In the following decades, various technologies emerged, including FDM and SLS. 3D printing has evolved from prototyping to creating intricate designs in industries like healthcare, aerospace, and automotive.
