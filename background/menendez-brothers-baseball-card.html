The Menendez brothers' baseball card has become a collector's item and a piece of dark pop culture history. After their infamous trial in the early '90s, these cards, often featuring their childhood images, gained notoriety among collectors. The mixed feelings surrounding their story have made these cards particularly intriguing for true crime enthusiasts and baseball card collectors alike.
