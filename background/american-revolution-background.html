American Revolution: Origins and Impact

The American Revolution was a pivotal conflict (1775-1783) that transformed the thirteen American colonies' struggle for independence from British rule. Fueled by rising tensions over taxation without representation, oppressive laws, and Enlightenment ideals, it united colonists to fight for liberty, setting the foundation for a new democratic nation.
