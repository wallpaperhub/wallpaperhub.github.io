Unveiling Ralph Waldo Emerson: Life & Legacy

Ralph Waldo Emerson (1803-1882) was an influential American essayist, philosopher, and poet, known for leading the transcendentalist movement. Born in Boston, he graduated from Harvard Divinity School and became a minister. Emerson emphasized individualism and nature in his works. Notable essays include "Self-Reliance" and "The Over-Soul." His ideas profoundly impacted American literature and philosophy.
