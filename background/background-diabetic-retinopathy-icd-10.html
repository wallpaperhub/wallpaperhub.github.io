Understanding Background Diabetic Retinopathy: ICD 10 Insights

Background diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects the eyes, characterized by damage to the blood vessels in the retina. It can lead to vision impairment and even blindness if not managed properly. The ICD-10 code for background diabetic retinopathy is E11.359. Early detection and regular eye exams are crucial for prevention and management.
