The Colorful History of M&M's: A Sweet Evolution

M&M's, the colorful candy-coated chocolates, were created in 1941 by Forrest Mars and Bruce Murrie. Inspired by the need for a durable chocolate snack during wartime, they quickly gained popularity. Each M&M features the iconic "M" printed on one side and is sold in a variety of flavors and colors, making them a beloved treat worldwide.
