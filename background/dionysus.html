Dionysus: God of Wine & Revelry

Dionysus, the Greek god of wine, fertility, and merriment, embodies the spirit of revelry and transformation. Often depicted with a vine crown and accompanied by sweet nymphs and satyrs, he represents the balance between civilization and the wild. His mysteries symbolize liberation and the joy of creation, inspiring art and culture throughout history.
