Discover the Origin of the Parker Last Name

The surname Parker originates from the Old French word "parquier," meaning "keeper of the park." It began as a status name for individuals responsible for managing parks or enclosed lands, primarily during the Middle Ages. Over time, it evolved into a common surname in England, spreading to other regions.
