History of Physical Therapy: A Journey Through Time

Physical therapy has a rich history, originating in ancient civilizations like Greece and China, where massage and manual therapy were practiced. In the 19th century, the modern field emerged with the development of electrotherapy and early rehabilitation techniques. Today, it combines science and creativity to promote healing and enhance physical function.
