Understanding Background Check Levels: A Quick Guide

Levels of background checks vary based on purpose and depth. Basic checks might include employment verification and criminal records, while comprehensive checks encompass credit history, education verification, and social media review. Employers typically choose the level based on the role's requirements and the potential risks involved.
