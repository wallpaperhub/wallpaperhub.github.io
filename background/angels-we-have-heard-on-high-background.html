"The Heartwarming History of 'Angels We Have Heard on High'"

"Angels We Have Heard on High" is a traditional Christmas carol that originated in France in the 18th century. It celebrates the announcement of Christ’s birth by angels to shepherds in the fields. The song’s joyful refrain emphasizes the glory of God and invites listeners to rejoice. It remains a beloved staple of holiday music worldwide.
