FDLE Firearm Background Check: What You Need to Know

The Florida Department of Law Enforcement (FDLE) conducts firearm background checks to ensure the safety of gun purchases. These checks evaluate the buyer's criminal history, mental health status, and other criteria mandated by state and federal laws. Understanding the background check process is vital for responsible gun ownership.
