Effortlessly Blur Backgrounds in Lightroom

To blur the background in Lightroom, start by selecting the subject using the Select Subject tool. Once highlighted, create a mask and then adjust the background using the brush tool. Decrease clarity, sharpness, and exposure to achieve a soft blur effect. This helps to focus the viewer's attention on the subject!
