Captivating Star Patterns: Design Inspirations

A star pattern is a geometric design consisting of points radiating outward, often forming a symmetrical shape. Used in various contexts like art, textiles, and architecture, star patterns can symbolize guidance, mystery, and beauty. They capture the eye and inspire creativity, making them popular in both modern and traditional designs.
