Discovering the Vibrant 1960s: A Decade of Change

The 1960s was a transformative decade, marked by social upheaval, a quest for civil rights, and a counterculture revolution. The Vietnam War escalated, provoking protests, while the civil rights movement sought equality. Music flourished with icons like The Beatles and Bob Dylan, symbolizing rebellion and change, establishing a legacy that still resonates today.
