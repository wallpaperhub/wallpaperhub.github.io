Remembering Christ: A Communion Reflection

In the sermon "Do This in Remembrance of Me," we reflect on the profound significance of communion. Jesus invites us to remember His sacrifice, fostering deeper connections with Him and each other. This act of remembrance is not only a tradition but a transformative encounter, where faith is renewed, and community strengthened.
