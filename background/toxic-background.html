"Understanding Toxic Backgrounds: Recognize and Overcome"

A toxic background can significantly affect mental health and personal development. It encompasses harmful relationships, unstable environments, or histories of abuse that create psychological scars. Recognizing the signs of a toxic background is crucial, as healing can lead to healthier future relationships and improved self-esteem.
