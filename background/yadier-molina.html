Yadier Molina: Legendary Catcher of the Cardinals

Yadier Molina, an iconic Puerto Rican catcher, has been a cornerstone of the St. Louis Cardinals since debuting in 2004. Known for his exceptional defensive skills and leadership, he has earned multiple Gold Glove awards and participated in several All-Star Games. Molina's impact on baseball extends beyond the field, making him a beloved figure in the sport.
