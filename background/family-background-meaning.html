Understanding Family Background: Meaning & Impact

Family background refers to the familial and social context in which a person grows up. It includes aspects such as socioeconomic status, cultural heritage, education, and family dynamics. Understanding one's family background helps in shaping identity, values, and perspectives, influencing life choices and opportunities.
