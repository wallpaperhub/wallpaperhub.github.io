Step Brothers: A Comedy of Chaos and Brotherhood

Step Brothers is a 2008 comedy film directed by Adam McKay, starring Will Ferrell and John C. Reilly as two middle-aged, immature men who become stepbrothers when their parents marry. The film explores themes of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth, showcasing the absurdity of their antics as they navigate adulthood together.
