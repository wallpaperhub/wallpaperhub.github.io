Discover the Flavorful History of Tacos

Tacos have a rich history, rooted in Mexican culture, where they originated as a convenient meal for workers. Traditionally made with corn tortillas, they are filled with various ingredients such as meats, beans, and vegetables. Over time, tacos have become a beloved dish worldwide, with diverse regional variations reflecting local tastes.
