W.E.B. Du Bois: Champion of African American Education

W.E.B. Du Bois emphasized the importance of education in achieving racial equality. He believed that a "Talented Tenth" of the African American population should receive higher education to lead social change. Du Bois advocated for a liberal arts education, arguing it would empower African Americans to challenge oppression and uplift their communities.
