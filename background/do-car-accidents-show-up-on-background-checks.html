Do Car Accidents Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, car accidents can show up on background checks, especially if they involve legal consequences like citations or claims. Typically, background checks focus on criminal records, but insurance claims related to serious accidents may be noted in driving records. Always review your state's laws for specifics.
