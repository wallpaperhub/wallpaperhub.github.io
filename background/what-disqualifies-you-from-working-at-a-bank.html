What Disqualifies You from Bank Employment?

Several factors can disqualify you from working at a bank, including a criminal record, particularly for financial crimes or fraud. Poor credit history may raise concerns about your trustworthiness. Furthermore, lack of relevant qualifications or experience, and failing background or drug tests can also hinder your eligibility.
