Whack-A-Mole Origins: The Fun Behind the Game

Whack-a-Mole is a classic arcade game that originated in the 1970s, gaining popularity for its engaging, fast-paced action. Players use a mallet to hit mechanical moles that randomly pop up from their holes, aiming for high scores. Its fun mechanics and competitive nature have made it a beloved pastime in arcades worldwide.
