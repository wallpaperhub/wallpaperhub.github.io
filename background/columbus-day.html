Celebrate Columbus Day: History & Heritage

Columbus Day, observed on the second Monday in October, honors Christopher Columbus's arrival in the Americas in 1492. The day celebrates Italian-American heritage but also sparks discussions about colonialism and indigenous peoples. Many cities hold parades, events, and educational initiatives to reflect on this complex history.
