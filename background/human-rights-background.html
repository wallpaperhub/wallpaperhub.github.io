Understanding Human Rights: A Comprehensive Overview

Human rights are the basic rights and freedoms inherent to all individuals, regardless of nationality, gender, ethnicity, religion, or any other status. They embody principles of dignity, respect, and equality, stemming from centuries of philosophical, legal, and social advocacy. Rooted in documents like the Universal Declaration of Human Rights, these rights aim to protect individuals from injustice and promote freedoms globally.
