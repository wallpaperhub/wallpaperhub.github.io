Is Elon Musk a U.S. Citizen? Discover the Facts!

Elon Musk is a U.S. citizen, having obtained citizenship after moving to the U.S. from South Africa. While he was born in Pretoria, South Africa, he later became a naturalized citizen, allowing him to contribute to American innovation through companies like Tesla and SpaceX. His citizenship status is significant in understanding his influence in the tech industry.
