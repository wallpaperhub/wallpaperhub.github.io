Explore the Life of Jacob: A Biblical Journey

Jacob, a key figure in the Bible, is known for his complex journeys and profound experiences. He was the son of Isaac and Rebecca, and twin brother of Esau. Jacob's story includes his famous ladder dream, his wrestling with God, and his twelve sons, who became the founders of the twelve tribes of Israel. His life exemplifies struggle, faith, and transformation.
