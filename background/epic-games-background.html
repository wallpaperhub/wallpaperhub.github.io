Epic Games: The Rise of a Gaming Powerhouse

Epic Games, founded in 1991 by Tim Sweeney, revolutionized the gaming world with titles like "Unreal" and "Fortnite." Initially known for their innovative game engine technology, they later shifted focus to digital distribution through the Epic Games Store. Their commitment to creators and gamers alike continues to shape the industry.
