Sam Walton: Pioneer of Walmart and Retail Innovation

Sam Walton, the founder of Walmart, transformed retail with his visionary approach to business. Born in 1918, he emphasized low prices, customer focus, and innovative management strategies. Walton's dedication to efficiency and community involvement helped Walmart grow into the world's largest retailer, redefining shopping for millions.
