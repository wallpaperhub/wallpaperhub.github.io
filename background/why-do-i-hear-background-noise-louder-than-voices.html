Understanding Why Background Noise Drowns Out Voices

Hearing background noise louder than voices can be attributed to several factors. Issues like hearing loss, auditory processing disorders, or the environment itself can impact how we perceive sounds. Additionally, distraction and stress may heighten the perception of noise. Understanding these factors can help improve communication in noisy settings.
