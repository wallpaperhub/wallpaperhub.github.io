Yellow Lightning: Nature's Striking Wonder

Yellow lightning captivates with its vivid hue, often associated with storms and powerful electrical discharges. This unique phenomenon can create stunning visuals in the night sky, sometimes illuminating the landscape in eerie, golden shades. Enthusiasts and photographers alike seek to capture its fleeting beauty, making it a popular subject in nature and weather photography.
