In Unity, changing the background color of your scene can greatly enhance the visual appeal of your game. To do this, access the 'Camera' component and modify the 'Background' field. You can choose any color using the color picker. Additionally, consider creating ambiance by adjusting lighting settings, adding gradients, or using skyboxes.
