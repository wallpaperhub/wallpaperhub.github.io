How to Add Background Music in Premiere Pro: A Step-by-Step Guide

To add background music in Premiere Pro, first import your audio file by selecting "File" > "Import." Drag the audio file to the timeline below your video clips. You can adjust the length by trimming or extending it. Use the audio mixer to balance levels and ensure the music enhances your video without overpowering dialogue.
