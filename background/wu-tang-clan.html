Discover Wu-Tang Clan: Hip-Hop Legends

Wu-Tang Clan is a legendary hip-hop group from Staten Island, known for their innovative sound and lyricism. Founded in 1992, their debut album "Enter the Wu-Tang (36 Chambers)" forever changed the genre. The group is renowned for blending East Coast rap with martial arts themes, creating a unique cultural impact that resonates today.
