Do All States Require Firearm Background Checks?

Not all states in the U.S. require background checks for firearm purchases. While federal law mandates checks for sales through licensed dealers, private sales, including gun shows, can bypass this requirement in certain states. Consequently, the regulations vary widely, highlighting the importance of understanding local laws before buying a firearm.
