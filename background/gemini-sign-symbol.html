Understanding the Gemini Sign Symbol: Meaning & Traits

The Gemini sign symbol, represented by the Twins, reflects duality, communication, and adaptability. In astrology, Geminis are known for their quick-witted nature and versatility. The symbol embodies their sociable spirit, curiosity, and constant quest for knowledge. This air sign thrives on interaction, making it a dynamic and lively emblem.
