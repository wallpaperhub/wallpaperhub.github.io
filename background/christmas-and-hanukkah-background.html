Christmas vs. Hanukkah: Celebrating Traditions

Christmas and Hanukkah are two significant holidays celebrated in December. Christmas, rooted in Christianity, marks the birth of Jesus Christ and is celebrated with festive decorations, gift-giving, and family gatherings. Hanukkah, a Jewish festival, commemorates the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem, celebrated with lighting the menorah, games, and traditional foods.
