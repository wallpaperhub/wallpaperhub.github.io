Understanding the Native American Swastika: A Symbol of Harmony

The swastika, an ancient symbol used by Native American cultures, represents harmony, balance, and the cycle of life. Unlike its later negative connotation, the swastika is a sacred emblem in many Indigenous tribes, symbolizing prosperity and good fortune, reflecting their deep spiritual beliefs and connection to nature.
