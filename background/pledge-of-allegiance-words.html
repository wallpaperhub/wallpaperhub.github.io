Understanding the Pledge of Allegiance: Words and Meaning

The Pledge of Allegiance is an expression of loyalty to the United States, recited by millions. It begins with "I pledge allegiance to the flag," emphasizing unity and patriotism. The pledge highlights ideals of liberty and justice, serving as a reminder of the nation's values and the sacrifices made to uphold them.
