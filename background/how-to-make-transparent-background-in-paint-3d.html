How to Create Transparent Background in Paint 3D

To create a transparent background in Paint 3D, begin by opening your image. Select the "Magic Select" tool to outline your subject. Adjust the selection as necessary, then click "Next." Now, click the "Remove" button to erase the background. Finally, save your picture as a .PNG file to maintain transparency.
