How to Create Stunning Space Paintings

To paint space, start with a dark canvas and apply a base coat of black. Layer bright colors for stars, using a sponge or toothbrush technique for splatters. Blend blues and purples for galaxies. Add white for stars and nebulae; use soft brushes for smooth transitions. Finish with highlights and details for depth!
