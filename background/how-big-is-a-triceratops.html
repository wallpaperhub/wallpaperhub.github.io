Discover the Size of Triceratops: Facts & Figures

Triceratops, a massive ceratopsian dinosaur, measured up to 30 feet long and stood about 10 feet tall at the hips. Weighing around 6 to 12 tons, this herbivore had a distinct three-horned face and a large bony frill behind its head. Its impressive size made it one of the largest land animals during the Late Cretaceous period.
