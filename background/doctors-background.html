Understanding Doctors' Backgrounds: Education & Experience

Doctors come from diverse backgrounds, typically spanning years of education and training. Most complete a bachelor's degree, followed by medical school, and several years of residency. This rigorous path ensures they acquire expertise in their specialty, as well as skills in patient care, communication, and ethical decision-making.
