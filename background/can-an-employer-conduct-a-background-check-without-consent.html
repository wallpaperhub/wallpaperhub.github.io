Can Employers Run Background Checks Without Consent?

Employers typically need consent to conduct background checks under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). Without consent, an employer may face legal issues. However, in some cases, such as certain positions in law enforcement, background checks can be conducted without employee consent. Always check local laws for specifics.
