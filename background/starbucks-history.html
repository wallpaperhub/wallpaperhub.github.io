Discover the Rich History of Starbucks

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, originally selling high-quality coffee beans and equipment. The company transformed under Howard Schultz, who introduced the coffeehouse concept in the 1980s, expanding rapidly. Today, Starbucks is a global leader, known for its specialty coffees and community-focused stores.
