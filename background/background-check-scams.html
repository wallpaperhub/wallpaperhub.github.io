Spotting Background Check Scams: Protect Yourself

Background check scams exploit individuals seeking employment or rental opportunities. Scammers may request personal information or fees for fake services, claiming to conduct background checks. Always verify the legitimacy of companies before providing sensitive data, and report suspicious activity to authorities to protect yourself.
