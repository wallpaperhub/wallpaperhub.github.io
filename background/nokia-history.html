"The Evolution of Nokia: From Pulp Mill to Tech Giant"

Nokia, founded in 1865, initially started as a pulp mill in Finland. Over the years, it diversified into various industries including rubber and cable manufacturing. By the 1990s, Nokia became a global leader in mobile telecommunications, revolutionizing the industry with iconic phones. Today, Nokia focuses on 5G technology and digital transformation.
