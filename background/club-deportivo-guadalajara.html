Discover Club Deportivo Guadalajara: Chivas, Mexico's Legendary Football Team

Club Deportivo Guadalajara, commonly known as Chivas, is one of the most iconic football clubs in Mexico. Founded in 1906, the team is renowned for its rich history, passionate fanbase, and commitment to fielding only Mexican players. Chivas plays its home games at Estadio Akron and is famous for its red and white stripes.
