Noah's Ark: A Tale of Faith and Renewal

The story of Noah in the Bible is a powerful tale of faith and obedience. God, dismayed by humanity's wickedness, chose Noah, a righteous man, to build an ark. Noah followed God's instructions, gathering two of each animal. When the flood came, Noah and his family were saved, symbolizing hope and renewal for future generations.
