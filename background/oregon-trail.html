Explore the Oregon Trail: A Journey to the West

The Oregon Trail, a historic route in the 19th century, was crucial for westward expansion in the United States. Spanning over 2,000 miles, it connected Missouri to Oregon's Willamette Valley. Pioneers traveled in covered wagons, facing challenges like rough terrain and harsh weather, while forging a path to new opportunities and adventure.
