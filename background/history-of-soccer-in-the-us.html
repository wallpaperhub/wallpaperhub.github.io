History of Soccer in the U.S.: From Origins to Popularity

Soccer in the U.S. has a rich history that dates back to the 19th century, with the first known match played in 1869. Initially overshadowed by sports like baseball and American football, soccer gained traction in the late 20th century, particularly with the establishment of Major League Soccer in 1993, leading to increasing popularity and youth involvement across the nation.
