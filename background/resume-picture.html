Should You Include a Photo on Your Resume? Tips & Guidelines

Including a professional picture on your resume can enhance your job application. It helps create a memorable first impression and showcases your personality. Ensure your photo is high-quality, with good lighting and a neutral background. Dress appropriately for your industry to convey professionalism and confidence.
