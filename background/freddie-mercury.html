Freddie Mercury: The Iconic Voice of Queen

Freddie Mercury, the legendary frontman of Queen, was known for his powerful voice, flamboyant stage presence, and timeless songs. Born Farrokh Bulsara in Zanzibar, he became an icon of rock music, delivering hits like "Bohemian Rhapsody" and "We Will Rock You." His unique style and charisma left an indelible mark on music history.
