Understanding Ronald Reagan: A Political Legacy

Ronald Reagan, the 40th President of the United States, served from 1981 to 1989. A former actor and California governor, he was known for his charismatic leadership, Cold War policies, and economic reforms, emphasizing tax cuts and deregulation. Reagan's legacy continues to shape American politics today.
