Does Amazon Perform Background Checks for Warehouse Jobs?

Yes, Amazon does conduct background checks for warehouse positions. These checks generally include criminal history, employment verification, and sometimes drug screenings. The process aims to ensure a safe and reliable workforce. Applicants can expect to provide personal information and consent to these checks as part of the hiring process.
