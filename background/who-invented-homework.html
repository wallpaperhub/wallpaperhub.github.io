Who Invented Homework? Discover the History!

Homework, as we know it today, doesn't have a single inventor. It evolved over centuries, with its roots in ancient civilizations. The practice gained popularity in the early 20th century, especially due to educational reformers like Horace Mann. Homework's aim is to reinforce learning and instill discipline in students.
