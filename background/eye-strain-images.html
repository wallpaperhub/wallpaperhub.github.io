Combat Eye Strain: Causes & Solutions

Eye strain images depict the discomfort and visual fatigue experienced from prolonged screen time or focusing on objects. Common symptoms include blurred vision, dry eyes, and headaches. These images often illustrate the causes, such as excessive device use, improper lighting, or poor posture. Understanding eye strain is crucial for prevention.
