Exploring Carrie White: The Iconic Horror Character

Carrie White, a character from Stephen King's chilling novel, embodies the struggles of adolescence and bullying. As an outcast with telekinetic powers, her story unfolds in a harrowing tale of revenge and self-discovery. Carrie's journey reflects the consequences of isolation, trauma, and the quest for acceptance, making her a compelling figure in horror literature.
