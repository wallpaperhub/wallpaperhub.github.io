Do Dollar General Employees Undergo Background Checks?

Dollar General does conduct background checks as part of their hiring process to ensure a safe working environment for employees and customers. This includes checking criminal history and, in some cases, employment verification. Candidates should be prepared for this step to ensure a smoother onboarding experience.
