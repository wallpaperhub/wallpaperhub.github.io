Top Tips for Choosing the Best Video Conferencing Background

Choosing the best background for video conferencing can enhance your professionalism and engagement. Opt for a clean, clutter-free space with good lighting. A simple bookshelf or a neutral wall works well. Virtual backgrounds can also add a touch of creativity, but ensure they don’t distract. Test beforehand for the best results.
