Uncommon Traffic Sign Shapes: What’s Not Used?

Traffic signs utilize specific shapes to convey important messages to drivers. Common shapes include circles for speed limits, triangles for yield signs, and rectangles for informational signs. Interestingly, shapes like stars and hexagons are not used, as they do not provide clear instructions or universally recognized signals needed for safe driving.
