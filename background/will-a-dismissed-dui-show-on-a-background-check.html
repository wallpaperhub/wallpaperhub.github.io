Will a Dismissed DUI Show Up on Background Checks?

A dismissed DUI may still appear on a background check depending on the type of check. Generally, criminal records, including dismissed charges, can be accessed by employers and institutions conducting thorough background investigations. However, some checks may not reveal dismissed charges, especially if sealed or expunged.
