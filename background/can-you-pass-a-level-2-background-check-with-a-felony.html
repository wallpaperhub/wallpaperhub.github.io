Can You Pass a Level 2 Background Check with a Felony?

Passing a level 2 background check with a felony is challenging, but not impossible. Factors like the nature of the felony, time since the conviction, and any rehabilitation efforts can influence outcomes. Some employers may be willing to overlook past offenses for qualified candidates, especially if years have passed.
