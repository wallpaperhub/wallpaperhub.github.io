Jobs That Require Fingerprinting: A Complete Guide

Many jobs require fingerprinting to ensure the safety and security of vulnerable populations. Positions in healthcare, education, law enforcement, and childcare typically mandate background checks involving fingerprinting. This process helps employers verify an individual's identity and check for any criminal history before hiring them.
