All About Pete Alonso: MLB's Power Hitting First Baseman

Pete Alonso is a professional baseball player known for his power hitting and competitive spirit. He plays as a first baseman for the New York Mets in Major League Baseball (MLB). Alonso gained fame by winning the Home Run Derby in 2019, showcasing his incredible swing. His dedication on the field inspires countless fans and players alike.
