Does Disability Appear on Background Checks?

Disability typically does not show up on a background check, as these checks focus on criminal history, employment verification, and other relevant personal information. Employers are generally prohibited from discriminating based on disability under the ADA. It's best to inquire about specific policies during the hiring process.
