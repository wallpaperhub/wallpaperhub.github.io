How Long Does Reckless Driving Stay on Your Record?

Reckless driving typically stays on your driving record for a duration of 3 to 5 years, depending on your state's laws. However, the exact time can vary based on factors such as the severity of the offense and prior driving history. After this period, the incident may be eligible for expungement or removal from your record.
