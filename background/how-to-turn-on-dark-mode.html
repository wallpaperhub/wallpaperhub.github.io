Quick Guide to Enable Dark Mode on Any Device

To turn on dark mode, open your device settings. For most smartphones, go to "Display" or "Screen" settings, then look for "Dark Mode" or "Night Mode." Toggle it on. For apps, check individual app settings like Facebook or YouTube. This mode reduces eye strain and saves battery life by using darker colors.
