Explore Michigan's Rich History

Michigan state history is rich and diverse, marked by the founding of Detroit in 1701, its role in the fur trade, and the rise of the automobile industry in the early 20th century. The state played a vital role in American education and labor movements, making significant contributions that shaped the nation. Today, Michigan continues to evolve while honoring its storied past.
