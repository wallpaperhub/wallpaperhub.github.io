Explore the Rich History of Guatemala

Guatemalan history is rich and complex, stemming from ancient Mayan civilization, which flourished in the region before the Spanish conquest in the 16th century. Post-independence in 1821, the country faced political instability, including civil wars. Recent decades have seen democratic progress despite ongoing challenges.
