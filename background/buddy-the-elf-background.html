Discover Buddy the Elf: A Heartwarming Holiday Icon

Buddy the Elf is a beloved character from the 2003 film "Elf," portrayed by Will Ferrell. He is a human raised by Santa's elves at the North Pole, who brings joy and innocence to his adventures in New York City. Armed with his childlike enthusiasm and a passion for Christmas, Buddy navigates the bustling city, spreading holiday cheer while searching for his biological father.
