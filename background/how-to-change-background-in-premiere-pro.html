How to Easily Change Backgrounds in Premiere Pro

To change the background in Adobe Premiere Pro, first import your video. Use the 'Ultra Key' effect from the Effects panel to remove the original background. Adjust the settings to fine-tune the keying. Next, place your new background layer beneath the video in the timeline. Adjust its position and scale as needed.
