Simple Church Flyer Design Ideas

When designing a church flyer, opt for a plain background to ensure clarity and focus on your message. Simple textures or soft colors can enhance readability without distraction. Utilize ample white space for a clean, inviting look and highlight key details such as event times and dates. This approach fosters community engagement effectively.
