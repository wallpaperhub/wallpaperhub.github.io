How to Add Music to Your PowerPoint Presentation

Add music to your PowerPoint presentation by going to the 'Insert' tab, selecting 'Audio,' and choosing 'Audio on My PC' or 'Record Audio.' Browse and select your desired audio file, then click 'Insert.' You can adjust playback options by selecting the audio icon and using the 'Playback' tab to set it to play automatically or on click.
