Essential Guide to Softball Fields: Layout & Design

A softball field is specifically designed for the sport, featuring a diamond layout with bases and a pitcher's mound. The surface usually consists of grass or turf, offering a smooth, safe playing area. Key elements include a 60-foot base path and a 43-foot pitching distance. This iconic layout fosters excitement and teamwork in every game.
