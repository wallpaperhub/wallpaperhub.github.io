Does a Medical Card Appear on Background Checks?

No, having a medical card typically does not show up on standard background checks. These checks usually focus on criminal records and employment history, not medical information. However, laws can vary by state, and employers may have different policies regarding medical marijuana use. Always check local regulations and company policies.
