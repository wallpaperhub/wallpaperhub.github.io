Discover the World of Tequila: A Guide to Agave's Finest

Tequila is a vibrant Mexican spirit made from the blue agave plant, primarily in the region of Jalisco. Known for its distinct flavors ranging from fruity to earthy, it’s enjoyed neat, in cocktails, or as part of traditional celebrations. With various types like blanco, reposado, and añejo, tequila offers diverse tasting experiences.
