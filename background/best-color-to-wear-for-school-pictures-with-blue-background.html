Best Colors to Wear for School Pictures with Blue Background

For school pictures with a blue background, consider wearing colors that create contrast, like white, yellow, or coral. These shades will pop against the blue, ensuring you stand out. Pastel colors like lavender or peach can also work well for a soft look. Ultimately, choose a color that makes you feel confident and comfortable!
