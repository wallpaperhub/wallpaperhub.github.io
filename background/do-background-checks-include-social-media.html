Do Background Checks Cover Social Media Insights?

Background checks often include a review of social media profiles to assess a candidate's character and judgment. Employers may look for evidence of inappropriate behavior or harmful opinions that could affect their company’s reputation. Social media scrutiny can reveal insights beyond traditional background information.
