Exploring Merle Oberon's Unique Ethnicity

Merle Oberon, a celebrated actress of the 1930s and 1940s, had a complex ethnicity that often sparked interest. Born in India to an English father and an Indian mother, she was often misidentified as purely British. Her mixed heritage contributed to her unique appearance and helped propel her to Hollywood stardom.
