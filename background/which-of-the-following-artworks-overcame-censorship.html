Artworks That Defied Censorship

Throughout history, several artworks have overcome censorship through bold expression or public outcry. Notable examples include Picasso's "Guernica," which confronted the horrors of war, and Marina Abramović's "The Artist is Present," challenging boundaries of performance art. These pieces not only sparked conversations but also reshaped perceptions.
