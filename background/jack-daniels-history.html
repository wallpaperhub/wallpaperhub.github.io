Explore the Legacy of Jack Daniel's Whiskey

Jack Daniel's history dates back to 1866 when Jasper Newton "Jack" Daniel established his distillery in Lynchburg, Tennessee. It is renowned for its distinctive sour mash whiskey and unique charcoal filtering process known as the Lincoln County Process. Over the decades, Jack Daniel's has become a global icon synonymous with quality and tradition.
