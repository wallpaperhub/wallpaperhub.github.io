Demeter: Goddess of Agriculture and Seasons

Demeter, the ancient Greek goddess of harvest and agriculture, symbolizes fertility and the cycle of life. She is revered for nurturing the earth, ensuring bountiful crops, and maintaining balance in nature. Demeter's mythology intertwines with the seasons, marking the relationship between her daughter Persephone and the changing of the seasons.
