Essential First Aid: Be Prepared for Emergencies

First aid is the immediate assistance provided to individuals who experience an illness or injury. It encompasses essential techniques for stabilizing a person's condition until professional medical help arrives. Originating from military practices, first aid has evolved into a vital skill for everyday emergencies, enhancing survival rates and promoting safety across communities.
