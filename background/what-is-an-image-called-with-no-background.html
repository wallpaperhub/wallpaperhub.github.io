What is a Transparent Image? Understanding Background-Free Images

An image with no background is commonly referred to as a "transparent image" or "PNG image." These images allow the subject to be placed over various backgrounds without any visible borders, making them ideal for graphic design and digital presentations. They enhance visual appeal by blending seamlessly with the surrounding content.
