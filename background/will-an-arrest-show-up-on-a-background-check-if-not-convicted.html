Do Arrests Show on Background Checks Without a Conviction?

Even if a person is not convicted, an arrest can still appear on a background check. Arrest records are typically public information and can be accessed by employers and organizations conducting checks. However, the impact of this record may vary based on local laws and the type of background check being conducted.
