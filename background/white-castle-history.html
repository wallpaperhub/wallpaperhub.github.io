Discover the Rich History of White Castle

White Castle, founded in 1921 by Billy Ingram and Walter Anderson, revolutionized fast food with its small, square burgers known as "sliders." Initially a humble burger stand in Wichita, Kansas, White Castle focused on consistency and affordability, growing into an iconic brand that paved the way for the fast-food industry.
