Understanding MVR: What You Need to Know

MVR stands for "Motor Vehicle Record," a document that details an individual’s driving history. It includes information on traffic violations, accidents, license status, and more. MVRs are often used by employers for background checks, insurance companies for risk assessment, and individuals to review their own driving records for accuracy.
