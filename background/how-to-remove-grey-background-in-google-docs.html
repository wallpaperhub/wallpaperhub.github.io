Remove Grey Background in Google Docs: A Simple Guide

To remove a grey background in Google Docs, begin by selecting the text or area with the background. Navigate to the toolbar, click on "Format," then "Paragraph styles," and select "Borders and shading." In the window that appears, look for the "Background color" option and set it to "None" or choose white to remove the grey background.
