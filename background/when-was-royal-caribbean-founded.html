When Was Royal Caribbean Founded? Discover the History!

Royal Caribbean was founded in 1968 by Anders Wilhelmsen, Knut Kloster, and I. S. Lindblad. Initially, it began operations with the acquisition of a single ship, the "Song of Norway." Over the years, the company has expanded its fleet and services, becoming a leader in the cruise industry, known for innovation and large, modern ships.
