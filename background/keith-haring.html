Exploring the Vibrant Art of Keith Haring

Keith Haring was a groundbreaking American artist known for his vibrant pop art and graffiti-inspired style. Emerging in the 1980s, his works often featured bold lines and playful imagery, tackling social issues such as AIDS awareness and LGBTQ+ rights. Haring's legacy continues to inspire through public murals and collaborations around the world.
