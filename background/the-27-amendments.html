Understanding the 27 Amendments: A Complete Guide

The 27 amendments to the U.S. Constitution outline fundamental rights and government powers. From the Bill of Rights, which guarantees individual freedoms, to the recent 27th Amendment addressing congressional pay, each amendment reflects societal changes and values. Understanding these amendments helps grasp America's legal landscape and democracy.
