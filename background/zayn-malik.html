Zayn Malik: Solo Star and One Direction Legend

Zayn Malik, former One Direction member, is a British singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and captivating performances. He launched a successful solo career with hits like "Pillowtalk" and "Dusk Till Dawn." His unique style, blending R&B and pop, has garnered a massive global following, making him a prominent figure in contemporary music.
