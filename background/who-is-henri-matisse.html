Who is Henri Matisse? Discover the Fauvist Master

Henri Matisse (1869-1954) was a French artist known for his vibrant use of color and innovative techniques in painting, drawing, and sculpture. A leading figure of Fauvism, he emphasized bold, expressive forms, capturing emotion through color. Matisse's works, such as "The Dance" and "Woman with a Hat," remain influential in modern art.
