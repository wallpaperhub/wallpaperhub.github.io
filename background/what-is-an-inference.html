Understanding Inference: The Key to Critical Thinking

Inference is a cognitive process where conclusions are drawn based on evidence and reasoning rather than explicit statements. It involves using observed facts, experiences, and background knowledge to fill gaps or make predictions. Inferences are crucial for critical thinking, problem-solving, and understanding deeper meanings in various contexts.
