Understanding Project Background: Key Insights

Project background outlines the context and rationale behind initiating a project. It encompasses key factors such as existing problems, target audience, and objectives. Understanding the project's background is crucial for aligning stakeholders' expectations and ensuring efficient resource allocation, ultimately guiding the project's success.
