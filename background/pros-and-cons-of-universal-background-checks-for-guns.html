Universal Background Checks: Pros and Cons Explained

Universal background checks for guns have notable pros and cons. Pros include enhanced public safety, reduced gun violence, and preventing firearms from falling into the wrong hands. Conversely, critics argue these checks could infringe on personal freedoms, create delays in legal purchases, and impose financial burdens on buyers. Balancing safety and rights is key.
