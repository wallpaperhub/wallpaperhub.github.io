Discovering the Wonders of Snowflakes

Snowflakes are intricate ice crystals formed when water vapor freezes in the atmosphere. Each snowflake has a unique structure, influenced by temperature and humidity. These delicate formations showcase nature's artistry in winter landscapes, creating a breathtaking spectacle in snow-covered environments around the globe.
