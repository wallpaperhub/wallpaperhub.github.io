Who is Hakeem Jeffries: A Rising Political Leader

Hakeem Jeffries is a prominent American politician serving as the U.S. Representative for New York's 8th congressional district. He is a member of the Democratic Party and has held office since 2013. Known for his advocacy on social justice, economic equity, and progressive policies, Jeffries has gained national attention as a leading voice in Congress.
