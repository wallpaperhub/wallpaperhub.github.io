The Birth of Zorro: A Hero's Creation in 1919

Zorro, the iconic character, was created in 1919 by writer Johnston McCulley. He made his debut in the story "The Curse of Capistrano," which was serialized in magazines. Zorro, known for his masked identity and heroic deeds, has since become a beloved symbol of justice and rebellion, inspiring countless adaptations in film and literature.
