Understanding the History and Impact of Cannabis

Weed, or cannabis, has a rich history dating back thousands of years, used for medicinal, recreational, and industrial purposes. Ancient civilizations revered it for its therapeutic properties, while modern legislation has shifted toward legalization. Today, cannabis culture is vibrant, encompassing diverse strains and uses, reflecting its evolving role in society.
