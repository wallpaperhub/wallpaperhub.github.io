Understanding Depression: Causes, Symptoms & Solutions

Depression is a common mental health disorder characterized by persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a lack of interest or pleasure in daily activities. It affects how a person thinks, feels, and functions, and can lead to emotional and physical problems. It's essential to seek help, as effective treatments are available.
