Understanding Civil War History: Key Insights

The Civil War (1861-1865) was a pivotal conflict in American history, fought between the Northern states (Union) and Southern states (Confederacy) over issues like slavery, states' rights, and economic differences. It resulted in significant social and political changes, leading to the abolition of slavery and strengthening the federal government.
