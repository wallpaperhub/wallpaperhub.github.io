Fixing SSN Errors in Background Checks

Yes, you can fix an SSN error on your background check. Start by contacting the background check company to report the mistake. Gather documentation, such as your Social Security card, to verify your identity and correct the information. Follow up until the corrections are made to avoid issues with employment or loans.
