The Sweet History of Dairy Queen: A Journey from Ice Cream Stand to Global Brand

Dairy Queen, founded in 1940 in Joliet, Illinois, began as a soft-serve ice cream stand. Originally, it sold a variety of frozen treats, quickly gaining popularity. The iconic Blizzard debuted in 1985, revolutionizing the brand. Today, Dairy Queen is known for its tasty treats and fast-food options, with thousands of locations worldwide.
