How to Create a Stunning Zoom Background

To create a stylish Zoom background, start by choosing a high-quality image that reflects your personality or profession. Use design tools like Canva or Adobe Spark to customize it. Ensure the resolution is 1920x1080 pixels for clarity. Finally, upload your image in Zoom settings under "Virtual Background." Enjoy a fresh look for your meetings!
