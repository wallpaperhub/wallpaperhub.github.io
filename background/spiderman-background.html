Spider-Man: The Iconic Hero's Journey

Spider-Man, a Marvel superhero, originated in Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962), created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. His real name is Peter Parker, a teenager bitten by a radioactive spider, gaining superhuman abilities like wall-crawling and enhanced strength. Struggling with adolescence and crime-fighting, he embodies the "with great power" mantra.
