Discover the Fascinating Background of Little Einsteins

Little Einsteins is an educational animated series that premiered in 2005 on Disney Junior. It follows four preschool-aged friends—Leo, Annie, Quincy, and June—who embark on musical adventures in a rocket ship. The show fosters a love for music, art, and exploration, introducing children to classical compositions and famous artworks.
