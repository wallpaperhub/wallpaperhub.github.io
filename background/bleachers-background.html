Understanding Bleachers: A Guide to Sports Seating

Bleachers are elevated seating areas commonly found in sports venues, auditoriums, and gyms. They provide spectator comfort and clear visibility during events. Constructed from metal or wood, bleachers are designed for durability and safety, accommodating large crowds while enhancing the spectator experience in various public events.
