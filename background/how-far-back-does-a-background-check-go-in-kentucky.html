Background Check Duration in Kentucky: What You Need to Know

In Kentucky, background checks can typically go back seven years for most records. However, certain convictions may remain indefinitely visible, especially for serious crimes. It's important to understand that the depth of a check can depend on the type of inquiry and specific employer policies, as some may adhere to stricter guidelines.
