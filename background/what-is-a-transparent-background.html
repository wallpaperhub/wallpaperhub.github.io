Understanding Transparent Backgrounds in Images

A transparent background is an image feature where the background is clear rather than filled with color or a pattern. This allows the subject of the image to stand out and blend seamlessly with various backgrounds when used in designs or presentations. Transparent backgrounds are crucial in graphic design, web design, and branding.
