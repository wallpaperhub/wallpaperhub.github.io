Discover the Iconic Target Logo: A Visual Identity

The Target logo, a bold red bullseye, symbolizes the brand's commitment to quality and value. Since its introduction in 1962, this iconic design has become synonymous with Target’s innovative retail approach. The simplicity and clarity of the logo make it easily recognizable, resonating with consumers seeking style and affordability.
