Discovering Rip Clouds: Nature's Weather Indicators

Rip clouds, or "arcus clouds," are fascinating atmospheric formations that appear as horizontal bands or rolls in the sky. Often associated with thunderstorms, they indicate turbulent weather and can create dramatic visuals during storms. Observing rip clouds can provide insights into upcoming weather changes.
