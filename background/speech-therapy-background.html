Understanding Speech Therapy: A Comprehensive Guide

Speech therapy is a specialized field that focuses on diagnosing and treating communication disorders. It incorporates various techniques to enhance speech, language, and swallowing abilities. Speech therapists assess individual needs and tailor interventions, helping clients overcome challenges related to articulation, fluency, and social communication.
