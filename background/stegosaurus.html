"Discover the Fascinating Stegosaurus: A Jurassic Marvel"

The Stegosaurus, a fascinating dinosaur from the late Jurassic period, is renowned for its distinctive plates and spiked tail. This herbivore roamed North America and had a unique combination of features, including a small brain relative to its size. Its iconic appearance makes it a favorite among dinosaur enthusiasts and kids alike.
