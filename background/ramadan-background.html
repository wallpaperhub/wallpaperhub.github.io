Understanding Ramadan: Significance and Traditions

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar, commemorating the first revelation of the Quran to the Prophet Muhammad. It's a time of fasting, prayer, and reflection, where Muslims abstain from food and drink from dawn until sunset. This sacred month fosters spiritual growth, empathy for the less fortunate, and community bonding.
