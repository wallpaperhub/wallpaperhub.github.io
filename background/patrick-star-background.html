Explore the Fun Background of Patrick Star from SpongeBob SquarePants

Patrick Star is a beloved character from the animated series "SpongeBob SquarePants." He is an enthusiastic yet dim-witted sea star who lives under a rock in Bikini Bottom. Known for his pink color, round shape, and carefree attitude, Patrick adds humor to the show and often joins SpongeBob in his adventures, showcasing their strong friendship.
