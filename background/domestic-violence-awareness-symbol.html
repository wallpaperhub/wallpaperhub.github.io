Domestic Violence Awareness Symbol: Understanding Its Importance

The domestic violence awareness symbol, often represented by the purple ribbon, highlights the urgent need to address and prevent domestic abuse. It serves as a visual reminder of the struggles faced by survivors and advocates. Raising awareness through this symbol is crucial in fostering support, education, and a united front against violence in homes.
