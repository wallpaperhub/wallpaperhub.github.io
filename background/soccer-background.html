Discover the Rich History of Soccer

Soccer, known as football in most of the world, has roots dating back to ancient civilizations. The modern game emerged in the 19th century in England, evolving from various forms of football. Today, it's the world's most popular sport, captivating millions with its blend of skill, strategy, and teamwork in stadiums and homes alike.
