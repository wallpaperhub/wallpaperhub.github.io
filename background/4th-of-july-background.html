Celebrate Independence Day: 4th of July Essentials

The 4th of July, also known as Independence Day, commemorates the adoption of the Declaration of Independence in 1776. This pivotal document marked the birth of the United States, symbolizing freedom and democracy. Celebrated nationwide, the holiday features fireworks, parades, and barbecues, bringing families and communities together in patriotic spirit.
