Experience the Thrill of Jaripeo: A Cultural Rodeo

Jaripeo is a traditional Mexican rodeo, deeply rooted in the culture of rural communities. Originating in the 16th century, it combines horseback riding, bull riding, and cultural festivities. Jaripeo is not just a sport; it’s a celebration of community, showcasing agility, bravery, and traditional music, bringing families together.
