Discover Fascinating Manatee Backgrounds: The Sea Cows

Manatees, often referred to as "sea cows," are gentle marine mammals that thrive in warm coastal waters and freshwater rivers. They possess a robust, rotund body and paddle-like flippers, allowing them to gracefully navigate through aquatic environments. These herbivores primarily feed on submerged vegetation, playing a crucial role in maintaining healthy ecosystems.
