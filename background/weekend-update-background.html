Weekend Update: The Satirical Spin on Current Events

Weekend Update is a comedic segment on "Saturday Night Live," delivering sharp commentary on current events, politics, and pop culture. Launched in 1975, it features a news desk format with hosts who utilize humor and wit to engage viewers. The segment has become a cultural staple, reflecting societal sentiments through satire.
