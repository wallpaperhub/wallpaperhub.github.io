Understanding Weather Stations: A Complete Overview

Weather stations are pivotal for monitoring local meteorological conditions. They gather data on temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation. These instruments have evolved from rudimentary tools to sophisticated digital systems, providing vital information for agriculture, disaster management, and daily weather forecasting.
