Celtic Patterns: Timeless Artistry and Intricate Designs

Celtic patterns are intricate designs inspired by the art and mythology of ancient Celtic cultures. Characterized by their loops, knots, and interwoven motifs, these patterns symbolize eternity and interconnectedness. Often found in manuscripts, stone carvings, and textiles, they capture the essence of Celtic heritage and artistic expression.
