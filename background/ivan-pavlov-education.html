Uncovering Ivan Pavlov's Educational Journey

Ivan Pavlov was a Russian physiologist best known for his work in classical conditioning. He graduated from Saint Petersburg University in 1870, majoring in natural science. Pavlov later studied at the Institute of Experimental Medicine, where his research on the digestive system led to breakthroughs in behaviorism. His education laid the foundation for his revolutionary experiments.
