Discover the World of Beans: History, Types & Benefits

Beans are versatile legumes that have been cultivated for thousands of years. Originating in ancient civilizations, they are rich in protein, fiber, and essential nutrients. Various types include black, pinto, and kidney beans, each with unique flavors and uses in cuisines worldwide. Beans are not just a staple food; they're also key in sustainable farming.
