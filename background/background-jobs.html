Enhance Your Workflow with Background Jobs

Background jobs are processes that run in the background without user interaction, allowing applications to perform tasks asynchronously. They improve efficiency by offloading resource-intensive operations, like data processing or notifications, ensuring a seamless user experience. Popular in web development, they support scalability and enhance performance.
