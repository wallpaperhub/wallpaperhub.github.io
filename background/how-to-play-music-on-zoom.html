How to Play Music on Zoom: A Quick Guide

To play music on Zoom, start by opening your meeting and clicking on the 'Share Screen' button. Select the 'Advanced' tab and then choose 'Computer Audio.' Make sure to play the music from your device. Adjust volume settings for a balanced sound and ensure participants can hear it clearly. Enjoy sharing your tunes!
