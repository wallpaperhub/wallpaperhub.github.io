Understanding the Regency Era: Key Historical Insights

The Regency Era, spanning from 1811 to 1820, was a period of significant cultural change in Britain, marked by the rule of the Prince Regent. It was characterized by advancements in literature, fashion, and architecture. Prominent figures such as Jane Austen emerged, influencing social norms and romantic ideals that still resonate today.
