Discovering the World of Transparent Plankton

Plankton are microscopic organisms drifting in oceans, rivers, and lakes, playing a crucial role in aquatic ecosystems. Their transparency serves as a survival adaptation, making them less visible to predators. This unique characteristic also allows them to efficiently utilize sunlight for photosynthesis, supporting food webs underwater.
