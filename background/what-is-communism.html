Understanding Communism: An Overview

Communism is a socio-economic ideology advocating for a classless society where all property is publicly owned. In this system, goods and services are distributed based on need rather than profit, aiming to eliminate inequality. Rooted in the works of Karl Marx and Friedrich Engels, communism envisions a world free from capitalism's constraints.
