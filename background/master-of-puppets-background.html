Master of Puppets: A Thrash Metal Classic

Released in 1986, Metallica's "Master of Puppets" is a landmark thrash metal album that showcases the band's intricate sound and lyrical depth. Themes of addiction and manipulation resonate through powerful tracks like "Battery" and the title song. It solidified Metallica's place in music history and influenced countless bands.
