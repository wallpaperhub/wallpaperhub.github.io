The Irresistible Ikettes: Legends of Soul Music

Ikettes are iconic background singers that rose to fame with the legendary Ike and Tina Turner in the 1960s. Known for their dynamic performances and powerful vocals, the Ikettes contributed significantly to the unique sound of soul music. Their vibrant style blended rhythm and blues, leaving a lasting impact on the music industry.
