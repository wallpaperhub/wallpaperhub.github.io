Discover the Origins of Pepperoni Pizza: A Culinary Delight

Pepperoni pizza, a beloved icon of American cuisine, has origins tracing back to Italian salami. With its vibrant flavors and crispy crust, it quickly became a favorite in pizzerias across the U.S. This delectable blend of spicy, savory pepperoni atop rich tomato sauce and melted cheese continues to delight pizza lovers everywhere.
