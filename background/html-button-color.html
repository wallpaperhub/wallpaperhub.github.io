Best HTML Button Colors for User Engagement

When designing a website, the color of HTML buttons plays a crucial role in user experience and engagement. Selecting the right button color can enhance visibility and encourage clicks. Use contrasting colors for prominence, and consider psychological effects—blue for trust, red for urgency, and green for action. Optimize colors to align with your brand for consistency.
