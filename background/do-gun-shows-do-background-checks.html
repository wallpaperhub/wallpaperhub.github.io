Do Gun Shows Require Background Checks? Clarifying the Rules

Gun shows in the U.S. often raise questions about background checks. While federal law requires licensed dealers to perform background checks, private sellers at gun shows may not be obligated to do so. This loophole varies by state, leading to a patchwork of regulations and concerns regarding firearm access without thorough vetting.
