Fascinating Facts About India

India, the seventh-largest country, boasts diverse cultures, languages, and religions. Home to over 1.4 billion people, it features the world's second-most populous city, Mumbai. India is known for its historical landmarks like the Taj Mahal and diverse wildlife. Spices, Bollywood, and yoga are also integral to its global identity.
