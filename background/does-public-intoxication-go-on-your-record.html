Does Public Intoxication Stay on Your Record?

Public intoxication can lead to legal consequences, but whether it goes on your record depends on the jurisdiction and the specifics of the case. In some areas, it may result in a misdemeanor charge, which could appear on your criminal record. However, first-time offenders may have options for expungement or diversion programs.
