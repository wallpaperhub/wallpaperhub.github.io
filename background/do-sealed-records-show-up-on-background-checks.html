Do Sealed Records Appear on Background Checks?

Sealed records typically do not appear on background checks, as their primary purpose is to limit public access to sensitive information. However, certain exceptions may apply, depending on the type of check conducted and the laws in your jurisdiction. It's crucial to understand how each check works and any potential access to sealed records.
