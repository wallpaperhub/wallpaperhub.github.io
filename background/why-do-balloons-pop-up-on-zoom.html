Why Do Balloons Pop Up on Zoom?

On Zoom, virtual balloons pop up during celebratory moments, creating a festive atmosphere. These balloons serve to enhance participant engagement during meetings, webinars, or events, signaling achievements or milestones. Their popping adds an element of surprise and enjoyment, helping to lighten the mood and foster a sense of community among participants.
