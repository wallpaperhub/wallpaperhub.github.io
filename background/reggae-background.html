Discover the Roots of Reggae Music

Reggae originated in Jamaica in the late 1960s, blending ska, rocksteady, and African rhythms. It reflects the island's culture, addressing themes of love, resistance, and social justice. Key figures like Bob Marley and Peter Tosh popularized the genre globally, using it as a platform for political and spiritual expressions.
