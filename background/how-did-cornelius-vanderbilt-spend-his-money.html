How Cornelius Vanderbilt Invested His Wealth

Cornelius Vanderbilt, a titan of industry, spent his fortune on various ventures, including railroads and shipping. He made significant investments in infrastructure, often funding the construction of new lines. Vanderbilt also donated to educational institutions, notably funding Vanderbilt University, leaving a lasting legacy in philanthropy.
