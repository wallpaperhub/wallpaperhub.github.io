ImageJ is a powerful image processing program that enables users to subtract background from images effectively. By utilizing the “Subtract Background” function, you can enhance the visibility of features in your microscopy or other scientific images. This technique helps in noise reduction, making critical details clearer and aiding in accurate analysis.
