Understanding the Roots of Rap Music

Rap music originated in the 1970s in the Bronx, New York City, blending rhythm and insightful lyrics. Influenced by disco, funk, and spoken word, it emerged as a powerful cultural movement. Pioneers like DJ Kool Herc and Grandmaster Flash shaped the genre, addressing social issues and life experiences through artful storytelling.
