Discover the Native Origins of Dahlias

Dahlias are native to Mexico and Central America, thriving in diverse environments ranging from mountains to lowland areas. These vibrant flowers were cultivated by the Aztecs for centuries before becoming popular across the globe. Today, dahlias are celebrated for their stunning variety and rich colors, making them a favorite among gardeners.
