Can 16-Year-Olds Be Drug Tested for Jobs?

Yes, a 16-year-old can be drug tested for a job, especially in industries where safety is critical. However, laws vary by state and employer policies may influence this practice. It's crucial for young applicants to understand their rights and the conditions under which testing may occur.
