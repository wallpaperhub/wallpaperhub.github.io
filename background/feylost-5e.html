Understanding the Feylost: The Enigmatic Characters of 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, "feylost" refers to characters who have ventured into the Feywild and emerged changed by their experiences. These individuals often retain remnants of their time in the enchanting realm, such as heightened senses or peculiar abilities. Their strange nature can lead to unique roleplaying opportunities and make them fascinating allies or unpredictable foes.
