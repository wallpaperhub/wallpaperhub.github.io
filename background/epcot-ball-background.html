Exploring Epcot Ball: A Symbol of Innovation

The Epcot ball, officially known as Spaceship Earth, is an iconic geodesic sphere located at Epcot in Walt Disney World. Completed in 1982, this architectural marvel stands 180 feet tall and symbolizes innovation and human achievement. Visitors enjoy a journey through time, exploring the history of communication within its attractions.
