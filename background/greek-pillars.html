Exploring the Iconic Greek Pillars: Styles & Significance

Greek pillars are an iconic symbol of ancient architecture, showcasing the artistry and engineering of classical Greece. They come in three main styles: Doric, Ionic, and Corinthian, each distinguished by unique designs and proportions. These majestic structures, found in temples and public buildings, represent stability, beauty, and democracy.
