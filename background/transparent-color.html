Understanding Transparent Colors: A Guide

Transparent colors are hues that allow light to pass through, creating a unique blend of transparency and vibrancy. They are often used in design to convey depth and layering, enhancing visual interest. Commonly seen in acrylic paints and glass, transparent colors can evoke emotions and add sophistication to any project.
