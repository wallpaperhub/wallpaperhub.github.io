Discovering Alexander Graham Bell: The Inventor of the Telephone

Alexander Graham Bell was a Scottish-born inventor who revolutionized communication with the invention of the telephone in 1876. Beyond telecommunications, Bell's work in aeronautics and hydrofoils showcased his innovative spirit. His legacy continues to inspire advancements in sound technology and communication today.
