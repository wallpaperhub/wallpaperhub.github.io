Is Intelius Free? Understanding Its Costs

Intelius is not a free service. While it offers some basic information at no charge, detailed reports, background checks, and other features require a subscription or one-time payment. Users can access various public records, but they should expect to pay for more comprehensive data. Always check their site for current pricing.
