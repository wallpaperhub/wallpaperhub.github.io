The Pinkertons: Pioneers of Private Investigation

The Pinkertons, founded in 1850 by Allan Pinkerton, were America's first private detective agency. Renowned for their role in solving crimes and protecting railroads, they became infamous for tracking down criminals like Jesse James. Their legacy influences modern investigative practices and popular culture.
