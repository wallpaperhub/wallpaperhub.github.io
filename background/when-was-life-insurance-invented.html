When Was Life Insurance Invented? A Brief History

Life insurance can trace its origins back to ancient civilizations, with early proto-insurance practices in Babylon and Rome. However, the modern concept began in the 17th century in England, with the establishment of the first life insurance company, the Amicable Societies, in 1706, marking a significant milestone in financial security.
