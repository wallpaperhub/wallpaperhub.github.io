Discovering the Connecticut Colony: History & Heritage

Connecticut Colony was established in the early 17th century by Puritan settlers seeking religious freedom. A significant early settlement was Hartford, founded in 1636. It became known for its Fundamental Orders in 1638, considered the first written constitution. The colony prospered through farming, trade, and maritime activities, shaping America's early governance.
