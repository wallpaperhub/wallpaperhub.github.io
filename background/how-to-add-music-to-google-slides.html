How to Easily Add Music to Google Slides

To add music to Google Slides, open your presentation and select the slide where you want the music. Click on "Insert" in the top menu, then choose "Audio." You can upload audio from your Google Drive or choose from available options. Adjust playback settings and volume in the sidebar to customize your experience.
