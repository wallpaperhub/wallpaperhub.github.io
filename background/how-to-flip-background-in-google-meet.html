How to Flip Your Background in Google Meet

To flip your background in Google Meet, start a meeting and click on the three dots (More Options) in the bottom right corner. Select "Apply visual effects" from the menu. Here, you can choose to flip your background horizontally. This feature helps you create a personalized environment and enhances your video call experience.
