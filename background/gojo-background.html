Discover the Intriguing Background of Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru, a prominent character in "Jujutsu Kaisen," is known for his playful personality and unmatched combat skills. Born into the prestigious Gojo family, he possesses the rare Six Eyes and Limitless techniques. As a teacher at Jujutsu High, he aims to protect humanity from curses while shaping the next generation of Jujutsu sorcerers.
