Ramadan Kareem! As the holy month of Ramadan begins, it's a time for reflection, prayer, and community. Muslims around the world observe fasting from dawn until sunset, deepening their spirituality and compassion for those in need. This blessed month encourages generosity and strengthens the bonds within families and communities.
