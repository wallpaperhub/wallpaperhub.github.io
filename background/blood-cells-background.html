Understanding Blood Cells: Types & Functions

Blood cells are essential components of our bloodstream, primarily categorized into three types: red blood cells (erythrocytes), white blood cells (leukocytes), and platelets (thrombocytes). Red blood cells transport oxygen, white blood cells defend against infections, and platelets aid in clotting. Together, they maintain vital bodily functions.
