"The Evolution of Nuclear Energy: A Brief History"

The history of nuclear energy began in the early 20th century with discoveries by scientists like Marie Curie and Albert Einstein. The first controlled nuclear reaction occurred in 1942. Nuclear energy was harnessed for electricity in the 1950s, transforming power generation. By the late 20th century, safety and waste management became critical issues, leading to ongoing developments in nuclear technology today.
