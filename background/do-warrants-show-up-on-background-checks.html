Do Warrants Appear on Background Checks?

Warrants can show up on background checks depending on the type of check conducted. Typically, criminal background checks will reveal active warrants. However, some employment checks may not detail warrants unless they pertain to a criminal conviction. It's essential to understand what type of background check is being carried out.
