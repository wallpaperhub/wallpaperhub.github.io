Understanding Universal Background Checks for Gun Purchases

A universal background check is a system that ensures all individuals attempting to purchase firearms undergo a thorough evaluation, regardless of where the purchase is made. This process checks criminal history, mental health status, and other factors to promote responsible gun ownership while enhancing public safety by preventing potentially dangerous individuals from acquiring weapons.
