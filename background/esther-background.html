Understanding Esther: The Brave Jewish Queen of Persia

Esther is a significant figure in the Hebrew Bible, revered for her courage and intelligence. As a Jewish queen of Persia, her story unfolds during a period of adversity for her people. Esther's bravery in revealing her identity and advocating for the Jewish community highlights themes of faith, resilience, and the importance of standing up against injustice.
