Can Employers Access Private Instagram Accounts?

Employers cannot directly view private Instagram accounts unless they are granted access by the account owner. However, if your profile is public, all content, including photos and stories, is visible to anyone, including employers. It's crucial to manage your online presence and privacy settings to maintain the desired level of security.
