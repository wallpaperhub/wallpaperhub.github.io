Understanding Background Charts in Cells

In spreadsheet applications, a chart that appears in the background of a cell is known as a "sparkline." Sparklines are miniature graphs that provide a visual representation of data trends within a single cell, allowing users to quickly assess important information without cluttering the spreadsheet with larger charts.
