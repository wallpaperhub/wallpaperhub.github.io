The Evolution of Social Work: A Historical Overview

The history of social work dates back to the late 19th century, evolving from charitable efforts and religious missions to a profession focused on systemic social change. Early pioneers like Jane Addams emphasized social justice and welfare. Over the decades, social work has adapted to meet the needs of marginalized communities, shaping public policy and providing essential services.
