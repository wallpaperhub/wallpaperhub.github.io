How to Easily Blur Backgrounds in Photoshop

To blur the background in Photoshop, first, open your image and select the subject using the Quick Selection Tool or the Pen Tool. After isolating the subject, create a mask. Then, apply the Gaussian Blur filter to the background layer. Adjust the blur level to achieve the desired effect. Finally, save your work.
