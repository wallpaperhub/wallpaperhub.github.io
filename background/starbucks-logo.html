Starbucks Logo: A Symbol of Quality and Allure

The Starbucks logo, featuring a twin-tailed siren, has evolved since its inception in 1971. The green circle symbolizes freshness and growth, while the siren represents allure and temptation. This iconic emblem reflects Starbucks' commitment to quality coffee and a welcoming atmosphere, making it a recognizable brand worldwide.
