Unraveling the Roots of Tortured Poets

The "Tortured Poets Department" delves into the lives of poets who have faced extreme emotional and psychological struggles. Often characterized by themes of despair, love, and existential angst, these poets channel their pain into profound works. This niche explores the intersection of creativity and suffering, highlighting their unique contributions to literature.
