How to Disable Virtual Backgrounds in Zoom

To turn off the virtual background in Zoom, start by opening the Zoom application and heading to the settings menu. Click on "Background & Filters" on the left panel. Here, you can select "None" to disable the virtual background feature. This will revert your image to the original, allowing a more natural appearance during meetings.
