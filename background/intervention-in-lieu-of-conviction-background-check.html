Understanding Intervention in Lieu of Conviction

Intervention in lieu of conviction allows individuals facing charges to avoid a formal conviction by participating in rehabilitation programs. This legal option can significantly impact background checks, enabling individuals to pursue employment or housing opportunities without the stigma of a criminal record, provided they fulfill program requirements.
