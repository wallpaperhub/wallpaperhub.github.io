Explore the Rich History of Hookah: From Tradition to Trend

Hookah, also known as shisha, has a rich history originating in the Middle East and South Asia. It involves smoking flavored tobacco through a water pipe that cools and filters the smoke. Traditionally a social activity, hookah bars have gained popularity worldwide, creating a vibrant community around shared experiences and diverse flavors.
