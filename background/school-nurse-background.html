School Nurse Essentials: Background & Role

School nurses play a vital role in promoting student health and wellness. Typically Registered Nurses (RNs) with specialized training, they address daily healthcare needs, provide emergency care, and manage chronic conditions. School nurses also educate students and staff on health issues, ensuring a safe learning environment.
