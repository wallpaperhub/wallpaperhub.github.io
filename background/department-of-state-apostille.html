Apostille Services by the Department of State

The Department of State apostille facilitates the authentication of public documents for international use. It ensures that documents such as birth certificates, diplomas, and court orders are recognized abroad, making them legally valid. This process streamlines foreign transactions and is essential for official matters that cross borders.
