Do Short-Term Jobs Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, a short-term job can show up on a background check, as many employers have different screening processes. Generally, most checks look at employment history, which includes both short and long-term positions. However, some employers focus mainly on recent jobs. It's essential to be honest about your work history.
