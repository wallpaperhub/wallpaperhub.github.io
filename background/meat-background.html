Understanding the History and Evolution of Meat

The background of meat consumption dates back thousands of years, evolving with human civilization. From early hunting practices to modern farming techniques, meat has become a staple in diets worldwide. Cultural, religious, and environmental factors have shaped how societies view and use meat, influencing culinary traditions and dietary choices.
