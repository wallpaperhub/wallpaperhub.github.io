Do Non-Convictions Show Up in Background Checks?

Non-convictions generally do not appear on background checks, as these checks typically focus on criminal convictions. However, the specific details can vary by state and type of check. Some employers may still view arrest records or other non-conviction information, depending on their policies and legal guidelines.
