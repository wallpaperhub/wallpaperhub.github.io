Easy Steps to Change Your PowerPoint Background

To change the background in PowerPoint, start by selecting the slide you want to modify. Navigate to the "Design" tab. Click on "Format Background," where you can choose a solid fill, gradient fill, picture, texture, or pattern. Make your selection, and apply it to all slides if desired by clicking “Apply to All.”
