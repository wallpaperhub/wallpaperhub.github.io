Essential Ingredients of a Martini: Gin, Vermouth, and More

Martinis are classic cocktails traditionally made with gin and dry vermouth, often garnished with an olive or a lemon twist. Variants like the vodka martini swap gin for vodka, appealing to different tastes. The perfect martini balances the spirit with vermouth, creating a smooth, sophisticated drink enjoyed worldwide.
