When Was UFC Founded? A Brief History of the Championship

The Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) was founded on November 12, 1993. The first event took place in Denver, Colorado, showcasing various martial arts styles in a no-holds-barred competition. The organization aimed to determine the most effective martial art in real combat. Since its inception, the UFC has evolved into a global phenomenon.
