Discover Hecate: The Enigmatic Goddess of Magic and Mysteries

Hecate, the ancient Greek goddess, embodies magic, witchcraft, and the moon. Often depicted at crossroads, she symbolizes choices and transitions. Revered as a protector of the household, she guides souls and offers wisdom. Celebrated during the full moon, Hecate represents empowerment and mystery, connecting the spiritual and physical realms.
