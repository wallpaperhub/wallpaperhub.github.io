Discover Shaquille O'Neal's Inspiring Early Life Journey

Shaquille O'Neal, born on March 6, 1972, in Newark, New Jersey, faced a challenging childhood. Raised by his mother, Lucille O'Neal, after his father, Joe Toney, was absent, Shaq found solace in sports. His height and talent emerged early, leading him to excel in basketball. He later moved to San Antonio, Texas, where he completed high school.
