Lil Yachty: The Rising Star of Hip-Hop

Lil Yachty, born Miles Parks McCollum, is an influential American rapper known for his distinctive style and vibrant personality. Emerging from Atlanta, his unique blend of melodic flows and youthful energy has captivated fans worldwide. Hits like "Broccoli" and "One Night" have solidified his status in hip-hop, while collaborations with top artists highlight his versatility.
