Founders of Delta Sigma Theta: Legacy of Leadership

Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc. was founded on January 13, 1913, by 22 remarkable women at Howard University. These visionaries aimed to promote academic excellence, community service, and sisterhood. The founders, including the esteemed Sadie Tanner Mossell Alexander, laid the foundation for a legacy of social activism that continues to inspire today.
