Understanding the Esthetician Background: Skills & Training

Estheticians are skincare professionals trained in various beauty techniques, including facials, hair removal, and makeup application. Many begin their journey with formal education in cosmetology or esthetics, often supplemented by certification programs. Their expertise helps clients achieve healthier skin and enhances overall well-being.
