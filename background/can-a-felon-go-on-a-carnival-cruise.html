Carnival Cruises and Felons: What You Need to Know

Yes, a felon can go on a Carnival cruise, but there are some limitations. If the felony is recent or serious, it may hinder boarding, especially if there are outstanding warrants. It's advisable to check with Carnival's customer service regarding specific policies, keeping in mind that legal restrictions vary by individual circumstances.
