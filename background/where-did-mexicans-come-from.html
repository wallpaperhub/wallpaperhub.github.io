Origins of Mexicans: A Cultural Journey

Mexicans primarily descended from indigenous peoples, primarily the Aztecs and Maya, combined with later Spanish settlers who arrived in the 16th century. This blending created a rich cultural heritage. Today, Mexico's population reflects this diverse history, enriched by various ethnic groups and cultural influences over the centuries.
