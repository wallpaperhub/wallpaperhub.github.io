DUI Reduced to Reckless Driving: What You Need to Know

When a DUI charge is reduced to reckless driving, it can significantly impact background checks. While a DUI may lead to severe penalties, reckless driving often has less serious ramifications. However, some employers or agencies may still see the reckless driving on your record, so it's important to consult a legal expert.
