Pedro Pascal: Rising Star in Hollywood

Pedro Pascal is a Chilean-American actor known for his captivating performances in popular series and films. He gained fame with his roles in "Game of Thrones" as Oberyn Martell and "Narcos" as Javier Peña. More recently, he has captured hearts as Din Djarin in "The Mandalorian" and Joel in "The Last of Us," showcasing his versatility and charm.
