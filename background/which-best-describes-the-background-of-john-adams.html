Unveiling the Background of John Adams: Founding Father

John Adams was born on October 30, 1735, in Braintree, Massachusetts. His background as a farmer's son shaped his values. He pursued education vigorously, attending Harvard College, which led to his role as a lawyer. A key figure in the American Revolution, he championed independence and laid foundations for the U.S. government.
