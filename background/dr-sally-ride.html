Discover Sally Ride: America’s First Woman in Space

Dr. Sally Ride was an American astronaut, physicist, and educator who made history as the first American woman in space. She flew on the Space Shuttle Challenger in 1983, paving the way for future female astronauts. Ride was also a passionate advocate for science education, inspiring young minds to explore STEM fields.
