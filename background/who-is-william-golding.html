William Golding: Exploring the Author of Lord of the Flies

William Golding was a British novelist, playwright, and poet, best known for his novel "Lord of the Flies," published in 1954. Born in 1911, Golding explored themes of human nature and societal breakdown. His work often reflects a deep understanding of the darker aspects of humanity. In 1983, he won the Nobel Prize in Literature.
