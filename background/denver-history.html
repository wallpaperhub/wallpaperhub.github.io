Explore Denver History: From Gold Rush to Modern City

Denver's history began in the mid-19th century when gold was discovered, leading to a boomtown atmosphere. Originally a mining camp named "Montana City," it was later renamed Denver in honor of Kansas Territorial Governor James Denver. Over the years, Denver evolved into a bustling city known for its vibrant culture, diverse communities, and role as a gateway to the Rocky Mountains.
