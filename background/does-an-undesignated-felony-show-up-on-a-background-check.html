Do Undesignated Felonies Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, an undesignated felony can show up on a background check. When employers or agencies conduct these checks, they often access criminal records containing all felony charges. Even if the conviction hasn't been formally designated, its presence on the record may still impact employment opportunities or other screenings.
