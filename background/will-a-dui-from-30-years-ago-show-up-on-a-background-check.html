Will a 30-Year-Old DUI Appear on Your Background Check?

Yes, a DUI from 30 years ago may show up on a background check, depending on the laws in your state and the type of check being conducted. Some checks are more comprehensive and may reveal older offenses, while others focus on recent history. It's important to know how long your record is kept based on local regulations.
