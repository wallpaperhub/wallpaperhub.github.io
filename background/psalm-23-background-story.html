Understanding the Background of Psalm 23

Psalm 23, often attributed to King David, highlights themes of trust and divine guidance. Written during a time of personal struggle, it illustrates the relationship between God and believers, using pastoral imagery. The shepherd metaphor signifies protection and care, reassuring followers of God's presence in life's adversities.
