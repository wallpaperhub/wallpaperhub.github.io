Understanding OV-1: A Guide to Operational View 1

OV-1, or Operational View 1, is a key element in systems architecture, detailing high-level operational concepts and processes of a system. It outlines interactions among various components, ensuring clarity in the operational environment. This visual representation aids in understanding system capabilities and functionalities, benefiting stakeholders.
