Exploring the Vibrant Background of Pop Music

Pop music, a genre characterized by its catchy melodies and widespread appeal, has roots in the 1950s and 1960s. It evolved from rock and roll and incorporated various elements from jazz, R&B, and electronic music. Iconic artists like Michael Jackson and Madonna shaped its landscape, making it a dominant force in global entertainment.
