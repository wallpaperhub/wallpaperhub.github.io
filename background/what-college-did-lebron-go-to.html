What College Did LeBron James Attend?

LeBron James did not attend college; he skipped college basketball altogether and went directly to the NBA from high school. After an impressive career at St. Vincent-St. Mary High School in Akron, Ohio, he was the first overall pick in the 2003 NBA Draft, launching an illustrious professional career.
