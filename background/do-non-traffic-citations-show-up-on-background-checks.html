Do Non-Traffic Citations Appear in Background Checks?

Non-traffic citations can appear on background checks, but it largely depends on the type of check conducted. Employment screenings usually focus on significant offenses, while personal checks might reveal lesser citations. It's important to understand the specifics of the background check being performed.
