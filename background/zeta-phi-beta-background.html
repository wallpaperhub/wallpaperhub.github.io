Zeta Phi Beta: A Legacy of Excellence

Zeta Phi Beta Sorority, Inc. was founded on January 16, 1920, at Howard University in Washington, D.C. As a historically Black sorority, it emphasizes scholarship, service, sisterhood, and civil rights. Known for its commitment to community service and empowerment, Zeta Phi Beta strives to promote harmony among its members and the broader community.
