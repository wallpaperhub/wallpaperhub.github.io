Gigi Hadid: Iconic Model & Fashion Entrepreneur

Gigi Hadid is a renowned American model and television personality celebrated for her stunning looks and impressive runway presence. Born on April 23, 1995, in Los Angeles, she rose to fame through her work with high-profile fashion brands and appearances in major fashion campaigns. Gigi is also known for her activism and entrepreneurial spirit.
