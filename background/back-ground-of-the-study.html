Understanding the Background of Research Studies

The background of the study provides the essential context for understanding the research's significance and scope. It outlines previous work, identifies gaps in existing knowledge, and presents the rationale for the current investigation. By highlighting the relevance of the study, it sets the stage for the research objectives and questions to be addressed.
