Discover What Philadelphia Is Known For: History & Culture

Philadelphia, known as Philly, is famous for its rich history, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture. It's home to the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art, where you can find the famous "Rocky Steps." Additionally, Philly is renowned for its delicious cheesesteaks and is a hub for arts and music.
