Where Is Nestlé From? Discover Its Swiss Roots

Nestlé is a Swiss multinational food and beverage company, founded in 1866 by Henri Nestlé in Vevey, Switzerland. Initially focused on infant nutrition, it has since expanded to a wide range of products, including dairy, coffee, and pet food. Today, Nestlé operates globally, making it one of the largest food companies in the world.
