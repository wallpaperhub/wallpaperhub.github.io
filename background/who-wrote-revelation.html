The Author of Revelation: Unveiling the Mystery

The Book of Revelation, the final book of the New Testament, is traditionally attributed to John the Apostle. Many scholars debate whether this John is indeed the same disciple known for his close relationship with Jesus or another early Christian prophet named John of Patmos. The text is a vivid projection of apocalyptic visions and prophecies.
