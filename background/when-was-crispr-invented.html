When Was CRISPR Invented? Discover Its Origins!

CRISPR, a revolutionary gene-editing technology, was invented in 2012 by scientists Jennifer Doudna and Emmanuelle Charpentier. They discovered the potential of CRISPR-Cas9 as a precise tool for modifying DNA, which has since transformed genetic research, medicine, and biotechnology, making gene editing more accessible and efficient.
