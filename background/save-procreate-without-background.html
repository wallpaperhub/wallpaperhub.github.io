How to Save Procreate Artwork Without Background

To save your Procreate artwork without a background, go to the Actions menu, tap on "Share," and select the format you prefer, such as PNG. Ensure the "Background color" is set to "transparent" before saving. This way, your artwork retains its quality and can be easily used on various platforms without an unwanted background.
