Understanding the Impact of a Gross Background

Gross background refers to the context or environment in which something is perceived as unpleasant, negative, or disturbing. It encompasses influences from social, cultural, and economic factors that shape perceptions. Understanding this background is crucial for recognizing biases and addressing challenges effectively.
