Tina Turner: Rock 'n' Roll Legend

Tina Turner, often called the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll," was an iconic singer and performer whose career spanned over six decades. Known for her powerful voice, dynamic stage presence, and hits like "What's Love Got to Do with It," she overcame adversity and became a symbol of resilience and empowerment in music history.
