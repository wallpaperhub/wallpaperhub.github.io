Who Invented Horchata? Discover Its Origins!

Horchata has ancient origins, predominantly traced back to the Valencia region of Spain. Initially made from tiger nuts (chufas), its popularity spread to Latin America where variations emerged. Its delightful, nutty flavor has made horchata a beloved drink across cultures, enjoyed for centuries as a refreshing beverage.
