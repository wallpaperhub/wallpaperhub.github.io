Discover the Renaissance: Art, Innovation & Culture

The Renaissance, a vibrant cultural movement from the 14th to the 17th century, birthed new ideas in art, science, and philosophy. It marked a rebirth of classical learning and humanism, leading to masterpieces from artists like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo. This period transformed Europe, shaping modern thought and creativity.
