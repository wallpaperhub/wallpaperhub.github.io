When Did Christianity Start? An Overview

Christianity began in the 1st century AD, rooted in the teachings of Jesus of Nazareth, who is considered the central figure of the faith. His life, death, and resurrection laid the groundwork for a new movement among Jews and Gentiles. By the end of the century, Christianity had spread across the Roman Empire.
