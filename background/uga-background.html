Discover UGA: History & Impact

UGA, or the University of Georgia, is a prestigious public research university located in Athens, Georgia. Established in 1785, it is one of the oldest public universities in the United States. UGA is known for its rich history, vibrant campus life, and strong academic programs, contributing significantly to the local community and state economy.
