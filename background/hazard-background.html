Understanding Hazard Background: A Quick Guide

Hazard background refers to the historical and contextual risks associated with specific environments or activities. Understanding these hazards is vital for effective risk assessment and management. By examining past incidents and recognizing patterns, organizations can implement safety measures to prevent future accidents and protect lives.
