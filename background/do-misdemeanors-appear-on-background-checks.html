Do Misdemeanors Show Up on Background Checks?

Misdemeanors can appear on background checks, depending on the type of check being conducted. Many employers or agencies use comprehensive checks that include criminal records, which can show misdemeanor convictions. Petty misdemeanors may be excluded from certain reports, but serious ones typically remain visible for years.
