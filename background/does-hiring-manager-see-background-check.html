Do Hiring Managers See Background Check Results?

When an employer conducts a background check, the hiring manager typically receives the results. This process helps them verify your qualifications, work history, and criminal records. However, candidates must be notified before the check occurs, and any discrepancies should be clarified, ensuring a fair assessment in the hiring process.
