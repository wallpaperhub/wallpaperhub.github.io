Understanding California Ammo Laws: A Quick Guide

California has some of the strictest ammunition laws in the United States. In 2016, the state enacted Proposition 63, which requires background checks for ammunition purchases. Buyers must also present valid ID and undergo a verification process, with limits on online sales and specific regulations for storage and transportation.
