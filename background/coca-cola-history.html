The Fascinating History of Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola, created in 1886 by Dr. John Stith Pemberton, began as a medicinal tonic in Atlanta, Georgia. Its unique flavor quickly gained popularity, and in the 1890s, Asa Candler transformed it into a global brand. Through innovative marketing and memorable slogans, Coca-Cola became a symbol of happiness and refreshment, leading to its iconic status today.
