Embrace the Peace Sign: A Symbol of Unity and Hope

The peace sign, a universal symbol of tranquility and harmony, emerged in the 1950s during anti-war movements. Its simple design, a vertical line intersected by two diagonal lines, represents a commitment to non-violence and unity. Today, it continues to inspire generations in the pursuit of peace and social justice worldwide.
