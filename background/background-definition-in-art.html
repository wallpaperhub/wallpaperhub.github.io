Understanding Background in Art: Definition & Importance

In art, "background" refers to the space or scenery behind the main subjects of a composition. It plays a crucial role in setting the mood, context, and depth, enhancing the overall narrative. Elements in the background can influence the viewer's perception of the foreground while contributing to the harmony of color and form.
