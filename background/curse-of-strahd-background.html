Unraveling the Curse of Strahd: A Dark Horror Adventure

In the "Curse of Strahd" campaign, players are drawn into the dark realm of Barovia, a land enveloped in mist and ruled by the vampire lord Strahd von Zarovich. With a tragic past and unending thirst for power, Strahd seeks to ensnare adventurers in his web of despair. Survival hinges on uncovering Barovia's secrets while confronting its malevolent forces.
