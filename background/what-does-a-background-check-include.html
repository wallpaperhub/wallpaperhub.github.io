What Does a Background Check Include?

A background check typically includes a review of criminal history, employment history, educational verification, credit reports, and reference checks. It may also involve a check of social media profiles and other public records to assess a candidate's character and suitability for a role or rental. Understanding this process is crucial for informed decisions.
