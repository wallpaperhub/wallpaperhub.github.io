Inspiring Life of Mother Teresa: A Biography

Mother Teresa, born in 1910 in Albania, dedicated her life to serving the poor and sick. She founded the Missionaries of Charity in 1950, providing care to the destitute worldwide. Known for her compassion, she received numerous awards, including the Nobel Peace Prize in 1979. Her legacy inspires compassion and charitable work globally.
