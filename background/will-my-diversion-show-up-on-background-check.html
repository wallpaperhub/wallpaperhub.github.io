Do Diversions Show Up on Background Checks?

When facing a background check, many wonder if a diversion will appear in their record. Generally, if a diversion was successfully completed, it may not show up on most background checks. However, laws vary by state and type of screening, so it's important to check local regulations to understand the potential impact on your record.
