Customize Multi-Monitor Backgrounds Easily

To set different backgrounds on multiple monitors, right-click on your desktop and select "Display settings." Choose the monitor you want to customize. Under "Background," select an image or color for that monitor. Repeat for each display. Enjoy a personalized workspace with unique backgrounds on all your screens!
