John Pierpont Morgan: The Titan of American Finance

John Pierpont Morgan (1837–1913) was a prominent American banker and financier who played a crucial role in the consolidation of major industries in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. He was instrumental in founding General Electric and U.S. Steel, and his influence helped to shape modern corporate finance and Wall Street.
