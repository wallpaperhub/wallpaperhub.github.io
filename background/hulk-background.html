Discover the Incredible Hulk: Origin & Background

Hulk, a Marvel Comics character, debuted in 1962, created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby. Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk, a green behemoth, when angered. His struggle with duality and personal turmoil echoes the human experience. The Hulk symbolizes strength and anger, becoming an icon in popular culture and blockbuster films.
