Unveiling Hekate: The Ancient Goddess of Magic and Mysteries

Hekate is an ancient Greek goddess associated with magic, witchcraft, the moon, and the night. Often depicted at crossroads, she serves as a guide to the unseen realms and the spirit world. Hekate is revered as a protector of the household and an ally in dark times, embodying the duality of light and shadow in her mysteries.
