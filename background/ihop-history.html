Discovering the Delicious History of IHOP

IHOP, or the International House of Pancakes, was founded in 1958 in Los Angeles, California. Known for its breakfast offerings, especially pancakes, IHOP has expanded its menu over the years to include a range of American diner favorites. The chain has grown to become a beloved staple across the United States and internationally.
