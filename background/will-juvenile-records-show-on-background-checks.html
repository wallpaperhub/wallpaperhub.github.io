Do Juvenile Records Appear on Background Checks?

Juvenile records usually do not appear on background checks for employment or certain legal situations, as many states seal or expunge these records upon reaching adulthood. However, exceptions exist, particularly for serious offenses. Individuals can check local laws for specifics on their juvenile record privacy.
