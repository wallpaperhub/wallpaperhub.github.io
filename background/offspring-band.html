The Offspring: Iconic Punk Rock Band

Formed in 1984, The Offspring is an iconic American punk rock band known for its energetic sound and catchy hooks. Their breakthrough album, "Smash," produced hits like "Come Out and Play," establishing them as punk icons. With a unique blend of humor and social commentary, they continue to influence the music scene today.
