Essential Guide to MLA Works Cited

MLA Works Cited is a crucial element of academic writing that lists all sources referenced in a paper. Proper formatting, including author names, publication titles, and publication dates, ensures clarity and credibility. Adhering to the MLA style promotes academic integrity and helps readers locate the original sources easily.
