Y2K: Understanding the Year 2000 Crisis

The Y2K problem, also known as the Year 2000 problem, arose from fears that computer systems would misinterpret the year 2000 as 1900 due to their use of two-digit year formats. This sparked global panic about potential failures in utilities, banking, and critical infrastructure. However, extensive preparations successfully averted major crises.
