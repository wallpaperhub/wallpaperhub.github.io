Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade: A Holiday Tradition

The Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade is an annual tradition held in New York City, featuring giant character balloons, dazzling floats, and performances from renowned artists. This beloved event marks the beginning of the holiday season and draws millions of spectators, both in-person and via television. Join the excitement and celebrate with family and friends!
