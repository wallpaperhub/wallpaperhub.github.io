What Was the Declaration of Independence Written On?

The Declaration of Independence was originally drafted on parchment, a durable writing surface made from animal skin. This document announced the American colonies' separation from British rule, articulating the principles of freedom and self-governance. Parchment’s longevity symbolizes the enduring importance of these ideals.
