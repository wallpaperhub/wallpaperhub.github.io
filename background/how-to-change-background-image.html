How to Easily Change Your Background Image

Changing your background image is easy! First, right-click on your desktop and select 'Personalize' or 'Display settings.' Then, choose 'Background' and either pick a preset image, browse your computer for a new one, or select a solid color. Adjust settings as desired, save, and enjoy your fresh new look!
