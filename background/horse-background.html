Discovering the Rich History of Horses

Horses have a rich and diverse history, serving as companions, work animals, and symbols of freedom across cultures. Originally domesticated around 4000 BC, these majestic creatures have influenced human society through transportation, agriculture, and sport. Understanding their background helps us appreciate their role in shaping civilizations.
