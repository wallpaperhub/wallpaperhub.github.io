Discover the Benefits of White Noise for Sleep and Focus

White noise is a type of sound that masks other noises, providing a constant, soothing background hum. It's beneficial for enhancing sleep quality, increasing focus, and even promoting relaxation. Many people use white noise machines or apps to drown out distractions, creating a calming atmosphere ideal for studying, working, or unwinding.
