Warning Signs: Understanding Their Shapes and Meanings

Most warning signs have a distinctive shape that helps convey their messages quickly and effectively. Often, they are rectangular or triangular, with bright colors like yellow or red to grab attention. These shapes are designed to alert people to potential hazards, ensuring safety in various environments and promoting quick recognition.
