"The First Electric Car: A Historical Breakthrough"

The first electric car, developed in the 1830s, was a groundbreaking innovation that sparked the evolution of sustainable transportation. Pioneering inventors like Robert Anderson created early models using rechargeable batteries. Despite early promise, electric vehicles faded until the late 20th century, reviving interest in eco-friendly solutions today.
