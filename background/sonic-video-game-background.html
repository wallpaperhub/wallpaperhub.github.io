Sonic the Hedgehog: A High-Speed Video Game Legacy

Sonic the Hedgehog debuted in 1991, created by SEGA as a response to Nintendo's Mario. This iconic blue hedgehog became a symbol of speed and adventure, featuring colorful worlds and memorable characters like Tails and Knuckles. Sonic's influence spans games, cartoons, and films, solidifying his legacy as a beloved video game icon.
