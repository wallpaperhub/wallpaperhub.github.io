Unveiling the Paladin Background: Chronicles of Honor and Valor

Paladins are noble warriors sworn to uphold justice and righteousness. Their backgrounds often feature a blending of martial prowess and divine favor, stemming from their oath to protect the innocent and combat evil. Many paladins hail from diverse walks of life, including knights, clerics, or common folk inspired by faith.
