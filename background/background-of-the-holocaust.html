Understanding the Holocaust: A Dark Chapter in History

The Holocaust was the systematic, state-sponsored persecution of six million Jews by the Nazi regime and its collaborators during World War II. Rooted in deep-seated anti-Semitism, it unfolded from 1941 to 1945, culminating in the genocide of Jews and other marginalized groups, dramatically altering the course of history.
