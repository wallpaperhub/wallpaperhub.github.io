Cornelius Vanderbilt: The Commodore of Industry

Cornelius Vanderbilt, known as the "Commodore," was an influential American industrialist in the 19th century. He began his career in the steamboat industry and later transformed the railroad sector. Vanderbilt is credited with pioneering innovations that greatly improved transportation efficiency and played a major role in shaping the modern economy.
