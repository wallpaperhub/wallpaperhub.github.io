Understanding MLG: The Rise of Major League Gaming

MLG, or Major League Gaming, is a prominent esports organization known for hosting competitive video game tournaments. It revolutionized the gaming landscape, bringing together players and fans from around the world. With its roots in the early 2000s, MLG has evolved, covering various genres like FPS, MOBA, and fighting games, and fostering a vibrant community.
