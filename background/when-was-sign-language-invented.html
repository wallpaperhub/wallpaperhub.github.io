When Was Sign Language Invented? Discover Its Origins!

Sign language, as a means of communication for the deaf and hard of hearing, has roots that trace back to ancient history. Evidence suggests that forms of sign language were used as early as the 5th century BC in Greece. The modern development of sign languages began in the 18th century with the establishment of formal schools for the deaf.
