Does Freelance Work Appear on Background Checks?

Freelance work typically does not show up on traditional background checks, which often focus on criminal history, employment history, and credit reports. However, some background checks may include freelance work if the employer specifically looks for it. To enhance credibility, freelancers should maintain clear records and gather client testimonials.
