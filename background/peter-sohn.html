Explore the Creative Journey of Peter Sohn: Pixar's Visionary

Peter Sohn is a talented American animator and director, known for his work at Pixar Animation Studios. He was born on October 24, 1977, and made significant contributions to films like "The Incredibles" and "Ratatouille." Sohn directed the acclaimed film "The Good Dinosaur," showcasing his storytelling and artistic vision.
