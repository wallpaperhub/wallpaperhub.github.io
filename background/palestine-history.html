Explore the Rich History of Palestine

Palestine's history is rich and complex, with roots tracing back to ancient civilizations. It has been a crossroads for empires, enduring foreign rule from the Egyptians to the Ottomans. The 20th century marked significant upheaval, notably the impact of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict, shaping its modern identity and quest for sovereignty.
