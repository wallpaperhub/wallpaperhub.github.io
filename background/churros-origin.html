Discover the Delicious Origin of Churros

Churros originated in Spain, where they were traditionally eaten for breakfast with chocolate sauce. The unique dough is shaped into long, ridged sticks and deep-fried until golden. Over time, churros spread to Latin America and gained popularity worldwide, becoming a beloved treat enjoyed at fairs and food stalls.
