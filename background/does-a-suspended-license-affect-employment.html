How a Suspended License Impacts Employment Opportunities

Having a suspended license can significantly affect your employment opportunities, especially if your job requires driving. Many employers conduct background checks, which may reveal your driving status, impacting their hiring decision. Additionally, your ability to commute to work may be hindered, limiting job options.
