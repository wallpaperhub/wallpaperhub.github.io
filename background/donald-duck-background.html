Discovering Donald Duck: A Legacy of Laughter

Donald Duck, created by Carl Barks, made his debut in 1934. With a feisty personality and iconic sailor outfit, he quickly became a beloved Disney character. His adventures often revolve around his bad luck and volatile temper, but he always retains a lovable charm. Donald's family, including Uncle Scrooge and Daisy, enrich his stories.
