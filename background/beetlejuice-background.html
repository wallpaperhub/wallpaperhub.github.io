Discover the Quirky World of Beetlejuice: Film Background

Beetlejuice is a 1988 cult classic film directed by Tim Burton that blends horror and comedy. It features a recently deceased couple who recruit the help of the eccentric ghost, Beetlejuice, to haunt the obnoxious new owners of their home. With its unique visual style, memorable characters, and iconic catchphrases, the film has become a staple in pop culture.
