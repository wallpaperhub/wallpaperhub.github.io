Do Hotels Conduct Employee Background Checks?

Yes, hotels often conduct background checks on employees to ensure safety and security for guests and staff. These checks typically include criminal history, employment verification, and sometimes credit checks, depending on the role. Thorough screening helps maintain a trustworthy environment in the hospitality industry.
