Ernesto Miranda: The Man Behind Miranda Rights

Ernesto Miranda was a pivotal figure in American legal history, best known for the landmark Supreme Court case Miranda v. Arizona (1966). His arrest on charges of kidnapping and rape led to the establishment of Miranda rights, ensuring that individuals detained by law enforcement are informed of their rights to silence and legal counsel.
