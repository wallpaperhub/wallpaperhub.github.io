What Does YMCA Stand For? Discover Its Meaning!

The YMCA stands for the Young Men's Christian Association. Founded in 1844 in London, its mission is to promote the well-being of individuals and communities through programs focused on youth development, healthy living, and social responsibility. The Y serves people of all ages, backgrounds, and walks of life, fostering a spirit of inclusion and community.
