Discover the Rich History of Tea

Tea, a beloved beverage worldwide, has a rich history dating back to ancient China around 2737 BC. It was initially used for medicinal purposes before evolving into a cultural ritual. Tea culture spread through Asia and, later, to Europe, where it gained popularity. Today, tea varieties range from green to black, each with unique flavors.
