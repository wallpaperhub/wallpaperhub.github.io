Understanding the Difference: Introduction vs. Background

The introduction and background sections of a paper serve distinct purposes. The introduction captures the reader's attention and states the main thesis or research question. In contrast, the background provides context, elaborating on relevant literature and historical information that supports the introduction and enhances understanding of the topic.
