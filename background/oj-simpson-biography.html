O.J. Simpson: Life, Legacy, and Controversies

O.J. Simpson, born July 9, 1947, is a former professional American football player, actor, and broadcaster. He gained fame as a running back for the Buffalo Bills and later starred in films and television. Simpson's life took a dramatic turn with a highly publicized murder trial in the 1990s, which captivated the nation.
