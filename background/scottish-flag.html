Discover the Iconic Scottish Flag: The Saltire

The Scottish flag, known as the Saltire, features a white diagonal cross on a blue field representing the Patron Saint of Scotland, St. Andrew. Its striking design symbolizes Scotland's rich heritage and cultural identity. The Saltire is prominently displayed during national holidays, sporting events, and as a symbol of pride for Scots worldwide.
