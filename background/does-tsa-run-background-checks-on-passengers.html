Do TSA Background Checks Include Passengers?

Yes, the TSA conducts background checks on certain individuals, especially for those applying for trusted traveler programs like TSA PreCheck. While the general public might not undergo extensive checks, specific criteria can lead to additional screening. This helps enhance security measures at airports and ensures passenger safety.
