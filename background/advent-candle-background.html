Advent Candle History: Symbolism and Significance

Advent candles have a rich history rooted in Christian traditions, symbolizing the countdown to Christmas. Each candle represents a week of Advent and emphasizes themes like hope, peace, joy, and love. Traditionally lit during family gatherings or church services, these candles enhance the reflective and spiritual atmosphere of the season.
