Unveiling the Whimsy of Alice in Wonderland

Alice in Wonderland, penned by Lewis Carroll, emerged from a whimsical journey of imagination. First published in 1865, the story follows young Alice as she tumbles down a rabbit hole into a fantastical realm filled with quirky characters, delightful nonsense, and thought-provoking themes. This captivating tale has inspired generations, leading to countless adaptations and cultural references.
