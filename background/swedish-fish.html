Sweet & Chewy Swedish Fish: A Candy Delight

Swedish Fish are chewy, gummy candies shaped like fish, originally created in Sweden but loved worldwide. Their unique texture and fruity flavors, particularly the iconic red raspberry, make them a popular treat for all ages. Perfect for snacking, movie nights, or festive occasions, Swedish Fish add a splash of fun to any candy bowl!
