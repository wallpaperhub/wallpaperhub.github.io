Delta Airlines: A Journey Through History

Delta Airlines, founded in 1925 as a crop-dusting service in Macon, Georgia, transformed into a major airline by the 1950s. Known for innovations like the first commercial jet service and the introduction of wide-body aircraft, Delta expanded globally through strategic mergers. Today, it stands as one of the largest airlines in the world, committed to customer service and sustainability.
