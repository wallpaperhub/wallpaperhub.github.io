The Fascinating Evolution of Pole Dancing: From Ancient Art to Modern Fitness

The history of pole dancing dates back to ancient times, originally rooted in various cultures across the globe. In India, it was part of traditional performances known as Mallakhamb. In the 1920s, it began evolving in the United States, gradually merging with burlesque and nightlife, ultimately leading to its modern-day interpretations in fitness and entertainment.
