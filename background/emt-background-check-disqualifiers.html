EMT Background Check Disqualifiers You Should Know

When undergoing an EMT background check, certain disqualifiers can hinder eligibility. Key disqualifiers include felony convictions, particularly violent crimes, drug offenses, and any history of misconduct related to patient care. Additionally, issues such as dishonesty on applications or previous license revocations may lead to disqualification.
