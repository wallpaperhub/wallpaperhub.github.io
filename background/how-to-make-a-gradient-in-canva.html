Mastering Gradient Creation in Canva

To create a gradient in Canva, start by opening your design and selecting the element you want to apply the gradient to. Click on the 'Color' option and choose 'Gradient.' Select the color stops and adjust the angle for your desired look. Customize it further by layering shapes or using transparency for a unique effect!
