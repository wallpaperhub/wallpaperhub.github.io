Change Your Messenger Video Call Background Easily

To change the background during a Messenger video call, start a call with your contact. Once connected, swipe up from the bottom of the screen to reveal options. Tap on "Effects" and select "Background." Choose a preset or upload your own image. Adjust it to fit your video frame and enjoy your personalized backdrop!
