Hardy: Thriving in Tough Conditions

Hardy refers to beings or elements that exhibit resilience and strength, thriving in challenging conditions. This term is often used in botany to describe plants capable of withstanding harsh climates or poor soil. In a broader sense, it symbolizes endurance and the ability to persist through adversity, embodying the spirit of survival.
