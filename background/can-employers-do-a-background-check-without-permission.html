Can Employers Conduct Background Checks Without Consent?

Employers often conduct background checks to ensure the suitability of candidates. However, legality varies by location. In many places, employers typically need permission to perform these checks, particularly for sensitive information. Always check local laws and company policies regarding consent and notifications.
