The Color Purple: A Journey of Empowerment and Resilience

The Color Purple is a powerful novel by Alice Walker, recounting the life of Celie, an African American woman in the early 20th century American South. Through letters to God and later to her sister, Celie documents her struggles against oppression, abuse, and her journey towards empowerment and self-discovery, ultimately celebrating love and resilience.
