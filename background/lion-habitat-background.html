Discover Lion Habitats: Where These Majestic Creatures Roam

Lions primarily inhabit grasslands, savannas, dense bush, and scrublands, thriving in regions where prey is abundant. Their natural habitat spans across sub-Saharan Africa and parts of India. The environment plays a crucial role in their hunting strategies and social structures, as pride dynamics heavily depend on the available resources.
