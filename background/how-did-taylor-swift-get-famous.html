How Taylor Swift Launched Her Iconic Career

Taylor Swift's rise to fame began as a teenager in Nashville, where she signed with Big Machine Records. Her self-titled debut album featured hits like "Tim McGraw," capturing country music fans. Swift's talent for storytelling and relatable lyrics resonated widely, leading her to crossover into pop and become a global superstar.
