Quiet Your iPhone: Easy Steps to Disable Background Noise

To turn off background noise on your iPhone, go to Settings > Accessibility > Audio/Visual. Toggle off the "Phone Noise Cancellation" option. For FaceTime and video calls, enable the "Spatial Audio" feature to enhance clarity. You can also use third-party apps like Krisp to filter out noise during calls.
