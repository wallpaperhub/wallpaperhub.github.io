Understanding City Watch in D&D 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, city watches serve as local law enforcement, ensuring order in urban settings. Composed of guards and watchmen, they respond to crimes, patrol neighborhoods, and maintain peace. Players can interact with them for quests, information, or consequences of their actions, adding depth to city adventures.
