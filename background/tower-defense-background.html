Mastering Tower Defense: Strategy and Gameplay Guide

The tower defense genre has roots in strategy games, where players build defensive structures to thwart waves of enemies. Typically set in fantasy or sci-fi worlds, these games challenge players to strategically position towers, manage resources, and upgrade defenses to protect their base from relentless attacks. Engaging gameplay fosters critical thinking and planning.
