Unraveling the Legacy of Ray Kroc: Fast Food Pioneer

Ray Kroc was a visionary entrepreneur who transformed McDonald's into a global fast-food empire. He joined the company in 1954 and established the franchise model, emphasizing speed, consistency, and quality. Kroc's innovative marketing strategies and dedication to customer service revolutionized the industry, making McDonald's a household name worldwide.
