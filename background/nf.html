Understanding NF: A Guide to Neurofibromatosis

NF (Neurofibromatosis) is a genetic disorder causing tumors to form on nerve tissue. It affects various body systems, potentially impacting skin, bones, and the nervous system. Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial for managing symptoms. Awareness and research are key to improving the lives of those affected by NF.
