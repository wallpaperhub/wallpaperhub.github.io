Understanding the Lacrosse Field Layout

A lacrosse field features a large rectangular area, typically 110 by 60 yards, with distinct markings. The field includes two goal areas, located at each end, and a center circle for face-offs. Grass or turf surfaces are preferred for optimal play. Familiarizing with the layout enhances gameplay and strategy.
