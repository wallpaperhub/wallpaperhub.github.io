Explore the Ancient Roots of Yoga

Yoga dates back over 5,000 years, originating in ancient India as a spiritual practice. Early texts, such as the Vedas and Upanishads, outline yoga's philosophical roots. By the time of Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, its principles were codified, influencing various schools. Today, yoga blends physical exercise with mental well-being globally.
