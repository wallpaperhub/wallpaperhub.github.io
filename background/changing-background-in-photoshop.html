Mastering Background Changes in Photoshop

Changing the background in Photoshop is a powerful technique to enhance your images. Start by selecting the subject using tools like Quick Selection or the Pen Tool. Refine the edges for a clean cut, then isolate the background layer. Finally, insert a new background layer and adjust colors for a seamless blend. Experiment with blending modes for stunning results!
