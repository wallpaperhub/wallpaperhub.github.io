Essential Guide to OIG Background Checks

OIG background checks are crucial for ensuring compliance and integrity in healthcare organizations. These checks help identify individuals and entities excluded from federal healthcare programs due to various reasons such as fraud or abuse. Utilizing OIG background checks fosters a safe environment and protects vulnerable populations.
