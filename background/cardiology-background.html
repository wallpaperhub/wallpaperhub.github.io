Understanding Cardiology: Heart Health Essentials

Cardiology is a specialized field of medicine focusing on diagnosing and treating heart-related conditions. It encompasses various aspects, including heart disease prevention, surgery, and rehabilitation. Cardiologists utilize advanced techniques and technology to manage ailments such as arrhythmias, heart attacks, and hypertension, aiming to improve patient outcomes.
