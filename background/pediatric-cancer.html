Pediatric Cancer: Understanding, Treatment, & Support

Pediatric cancer is a rare but serious condition affecting children, requiring specialized care and treatment. Early detection is crucial for effective treatment options, which may include chemotherapy, radiation, and surgery. Support for families navigating this challenging journey is vital, encompassing emotional, psychological, and financial assistance.
