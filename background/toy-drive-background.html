Toy Drive: Bring Joy to Children in Need

A toy drive is a charitable initiative aimed at collecting toys for children in need, especially during the holiday season. It fosters community spirit and brings joy to underprivileged kids by ensuring they receive gifts. These drives often involve local businesses, schools, and organizations working together to make a difference in children's lives.
