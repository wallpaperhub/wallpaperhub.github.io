Understanding Earth Slides: Causes and Impact

Earth slides, also known as landslides or earth movements, are natural phenomena where masses of soil, rock, or debris descend down slopes due to gravity. Triggered by factors like heavy rainfall, earthquakes, or human activities, they can devastate landscapes, disrupt ecosystems, and pose threats to human structures and safety.
