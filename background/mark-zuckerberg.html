Mark Zuckerberg: The Visionary Behind Facebook

Mark Zuckerberg, co-founder and CEO of Facebook, revolutionized social networking, shaping how people connect online. Born in 1984, he launched Facebook in 2004 while at Harvard University. His vision transformed communication, leading to billions of users globally. Beyond tech, Zuckerberg advocates for education and philanthropy.
