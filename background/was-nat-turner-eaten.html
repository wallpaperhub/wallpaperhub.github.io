Was Nat Turner Eaten? Myth vs. Reality

Nat Turner's fate was gruesome as he was executed for leading a slave rebellion in 1831. After his death, rumors circulated about inhumane treatment of his body. However, there is no historical evidence or credible accounts to suggest that he was eaten. Instead, Turner remains a symbol of resistance against slavery.
