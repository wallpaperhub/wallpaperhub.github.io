All About Thunder: Understanding the Sound of Nature

Thunder is a fascinating atmospheric phenomenon caused by the rapid expansion of heated air during a lightning strike. The rumbling sound creates a dramatic backdrop, evoking feelings of awe and fear. Understanding thunder's origins helps us appreciate nature's power and beauty, while reminding us to respect the elements.
