Discovering General Grievous: The Warrior Before the Cyborg

Before becoming the infamous cyborg General Grievous, he was a feared warrior on the planet Kalee. As a fierce leader of the planet's resistance against the Huk class, Grievous showcased his strategic mind and martial prowess. His transformation into a cyborg was driven by the desire for power and revenge against the Jedi, who he detested for their role in his planet's suffering.
