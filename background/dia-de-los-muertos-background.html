Embrace Día de los Muertos: A Celebration of Life and Death

Día de los Muertos, or Day of the Dead, is a vibrant Mexican celebration honoring deceased loved ones. Rooted in ancient Aztec traditions, it blends indigenous customs with Catholicism. Families create altars adorned with photos, flowers, and offerings, inviting spirits to join the living in joyful remembrance each November 1st and 2nd.
