Discover the Vitruvian Man: Art Meets Science

The Vitruvian Man, created by Leonardo da Vinci around 1490, is an iconic drawing that epitomizes the blend of art and science. It depicts a nude male figure in two superimposed positions within a circle and square, symbolizing the harmony of human proportions and the universe. This masterpiece reflects the Renaissance ideals of beauty, balance, and humanism.
