All About Dandelions: Nature's Resilient Flower

Dandelions, known scientifically as Taraxacum, are resilient flowering plants found worldwide. These bright yellow blooms are often seen in lawns and fields, yet they are more than just weeds. Dandelions have a rich history in herbal medicine and are packed with vitamins and minerals. Their lifecycle, from flower to seed, fascinates nature enthusiasts.
