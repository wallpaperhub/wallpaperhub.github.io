Discover the Legacy of Blue Beetle: DC's Iconic Hero

Blue Beetle is a popular DC Comics superhero known for his unique connection to an alien artifact called the Scarab. The character has evolved over the years, with different incarnations, but the most recognized is Jaime Reyes, a Latino teen who gains extraordinary powers and a high-tech suit. Blue Beetle's adventures often explore themes of identity, family, and heroism.
