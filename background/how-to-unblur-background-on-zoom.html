How to Unblur Background on Zoom: A Simple Guide

To unblur the background on Zoom, first, start or join a meeting. Click on the arrow next to the "Start Video" button and select "Video Settings." In the "Background & Filters" tab, choose "None" or select a preferred virtual background to replace the blur. This will allow your real background to appear clearly during the call.
