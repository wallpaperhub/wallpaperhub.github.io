Understanding Erik Erikson: The Man Behind Developmental Psychology

Erik Erikson was a prominent developmental psychologist known for his theory of psychosocial development. He proposed eight stages of human development that outline the challenges faced throughout life, from infancy to late adulthood. His work emphasizes the role of social interaction in personal identity formation.
