How to Create a Transparent Background in GIMP

GIMP makes it easy to create a transparent background for your images. Simply open your image, use the 'Fuzzy Select Tool' to select the background, and press 'Delete' to remove it. You can then export your image in PNG format to preserve the transparency. This technique is essential for graphic designers and web developers.
