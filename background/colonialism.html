Understanding Colonialism: Its Impact & Legacy

Colonialism significantly impacted global history, resulting in the dominance of European powers over vast territories. This practice exploited resources, imposed foreign cultures, and disrupted local societies. The legacy of colonialism persists today, influencing economics, politics, and identity in formerly colonized nations.
