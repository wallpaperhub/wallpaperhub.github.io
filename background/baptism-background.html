Baptism: Exploring Its Significance and Traditions

The background of baptism in Christianity traces back to the New Testament, symbolizing purification and the initiation into the faith. Traditionally, it involves water, representing cleansing from sin and rebirth. Different denominations practice baptism in various forms, either through immersion or sprinkling, reflecting their theological beliefs.
