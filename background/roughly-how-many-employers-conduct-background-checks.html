How Many Employers Conduct Background Checks?

Approximately 70% to 80% of employers in the United States conduct background checks as part of their hiring process. These checks help employers verify candidates' qualifications, criminal histories, and credit information, contributing to informed hiring decisions that enhance workplace safety and culture.
