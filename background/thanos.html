Understanding Thanos: The Mad Titan's Quest for Infinity

Thanos, the iconic Marvel supervillain, is known for his quest to obtain the Infinity Stones to enact his vision of balance in the universe. With his immense strength and intelligence, he poses a significant threat to heroes like Thor, Iron Man, and the Avengers. His complex motivations challenge the idea of good versus evil, making him a compelling character.
