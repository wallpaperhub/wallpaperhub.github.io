Who Is Tim Scott? | U.S. Senator from South Carolina

Tim Scott is an American politician serving as the junior U.S. Senator from South Carolina since 2013. A member of the Republican Party, he is known for his focus on economic opportunity, education reform, and social justice. Scott is notable for being the first African American senator from the South since Reconstruction.
