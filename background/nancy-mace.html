Nancy Mace: Trailblazing Politician & Businesswoman

Nancy Mace is a prominent American politician and businesswoman, known for her role as the U.S. Representative for South Carolina's 1st congressional district. A trailblazer, she gained attention as the first woman to graduate from The Citadel. Mace is recognized for her active stance on various issues, including veterans' affairs and economic development.
