Add Motion Blur to Your Photos Easily

To add motion blur to a photo, use an editing software like Photoshop or online tools. Select the subject and duplicate the layer. Apply the motion blur effect, adjusting the angle and distance for desired intensity. Blend the layers and erase parts where you want to keep sharp details. Save your final image for a dynamic, professional look.
