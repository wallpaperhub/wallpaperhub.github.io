Top Secret Clearance Requirements: What You Need to Know

Top secret clearance is a security designation that requires several key requirements. Applicants must be U.S. citizens, undergo thorough background checks, and demonstrate a need for access to classified information. Factors include financial responsibility, personal conduct, and loyalty to the United States.
