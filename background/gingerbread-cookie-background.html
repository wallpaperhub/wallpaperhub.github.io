Delicious Gingerbread Cookies: A Sweet Holiday Tradition

Gingerbread cookies trace their origins to ancient civilizations, with the modern version popularized in Europe during the Middle Ages. Initially made with spices and honey, they became famous for their intricate designs during Christmas. Today, they symbolize the holiday spirit, often decorated and enjoyed during festive celebrations.
