Does Probation Before Judgment Appear on Background Checks?

Probation before judgment (PBJ) typically does not appear on a standard background check, as it is a disposition that can be deemed non-conviction. However, certain employers may request deeper checks that could reveal it. Always clarify the type of background check being done to understand what may show up.
