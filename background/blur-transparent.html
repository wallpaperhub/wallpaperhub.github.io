Blur transparency is a powerful design technique that involves applying a blur effect to transparent elements, creating a soft, diffused appearance. This method enhances depth and focus, allowing background visuals to merge seamlessly with foreground elements. Ideal for modern web and graphic design, it adds elegance and sophistication.
