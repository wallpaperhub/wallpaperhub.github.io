Understanding the Evolution of Buses

Buses have evolved from simple horse-drawn carriages to modern transportation marvels. Designed for public use, their history reflects advances in engineering and urban planning. Today, buses are a critical element in reducing traffic congestion and promoting sustainable transportation while providing accessibility for countless passengers worldwide.
