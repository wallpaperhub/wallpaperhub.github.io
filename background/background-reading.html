Essential Background Reading for Effective Research

Background reading is the essential practice of reviewing existing literature and resources related to a specific topic before diving deeper. This process helps gather insights, identifies key themes, and establishes a foundation for further exploration. It enriches understanding and informs research or study, making it a vital step in any academic or professional work.
