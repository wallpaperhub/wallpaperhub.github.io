Delicious Journey: The History of Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese sandwiches date back to ancient times when bread and cheese were baked together. In the 1920s, the modern version emerged with sliced bread and processed cheese, becoming a staple during the Great Depression. Today, grilled cheese has evolved into a comfort food, inspiring gourmet variations and endless creativity in kitchens around the world.
