Discover the Fascinating History of Salad

Salads have a rich history dating back to ancient civilizations. The word "salad" originates from the Latin "salata," meaning salted. These healthy dishes have evolved, incorporating various ingredients from leafy greens to fruits and nuts, appealing to diverse tastes. Today, they symbolize freshness and nutrition in global cuisine.
