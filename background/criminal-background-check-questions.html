Essential Questions for Criminal Background Checks

When conducting a criminal background check, it's important to ask the right questions to get a clear understanding of an individual's history. Key questions may include: Have you ever been convicted of a crime? What types of offenses are on your record? How long ago did the incidents occur? These inquiries help assess risk and ensure informed decisions.
