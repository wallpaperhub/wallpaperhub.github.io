Is a Muzzleloader a Firearm? Understanding the Classification

Yes, a muzzleloader is considered a firearm. According to legal definitions, firearms are weapons that can expel a projectile through the action of an explosive. Muzzleloaders fit this definition, as they require the ignition of gunpowder to fire a projectile. Regulations vary by region, impacting ownership and usage.
