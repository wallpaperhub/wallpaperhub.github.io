Aquaman: King of Atlantis & Defender of the Seas

Aquaman, the half-human, half-Atlantean superhero, is a key member of the Justice League. With powers to communicate with marine life and exceptional swimming abilities, he defends both land and sea. His journey explores themes of identity, leadership, and environmentalism, making him a captivating character in the DC universe.
