Turn Off Background Apps on iPhone: Quick & Easy Guide

To improve your iPhone's performance and battery life, consider turning off background apps. Go to Settings > Background App Refresh and toggle the feature off for specific apps or entirely. This prevents apps from refreshing in the background, conserving battery power and enhancing overall speed and efficiency.
