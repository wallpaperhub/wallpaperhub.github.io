Understanding the Meaning of Humpty Dumpty

Humpty Dumpty is a nursery rhyme character often depicted as an egg-shaped figure. The story conveys themes of fragility and the inevitability of consequence, illustrating how sometimes, once something is broken (or a situation is irreparably harmed), it can't be fixed no matter the efforts made to resolve it.
