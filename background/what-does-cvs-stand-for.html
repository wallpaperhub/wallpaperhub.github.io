What Does CVS Stand For? Find Out Here!

CVS stands for "Convenience Value Store." It's a popular pharmacy chain in the United States, primarily offering pharmaceuticals, health and beauty products, and convenience items. Founded in 1963, it has grown to incorporate walk-in clinics, making healthcare accessible while focusing on customer convenience and value.
