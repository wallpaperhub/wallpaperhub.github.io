Chinese New Year: Celebrating Traditions and Culture

Chinese New Year, also known as Lunar New Year, is a significant celebration that marks the beginning of the lunar calendar. It symbolizes renewal, family reunions, and the arrival of spring. Traditionally, families honor ancestors and deities, share festive meals, and engage in various cultural practices, such as lion dances and fireworks.
