Essential Guide to Background Checks for Buying a Gun in SC

In South Carolina, purchasing a firearm requires a background check. This process, mandated by federal law, ensures that individuals with felony convictions, domestic violence restraining orders, or severe mental health issues are prohibited from buying guns. The background check is conducted through the National Instant Criminal Background Check System (NICS) for safety and accountability.
