What Are Tamales Made Of? A Quick Guide

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish made primarily from masa (corn dough) which is filled with a variety of ingredients, such as meats, cheeses, vegetables, or chilies. The filled masa is wrapped in corn husks or banana leaves and then steamed, resulting in a delicious, flavorful meal often enjoyed during celebrations.
