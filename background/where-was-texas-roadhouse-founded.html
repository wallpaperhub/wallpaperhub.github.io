Discover the Origins of Texas Roadhouse

Texas Roadhouse was founded in Clarksville, Indiana, in 1993. The restaurant was created by Kent Taylor, who envisioned a casual dining experience specializing in hand-cut steaks and a vibrant atmosphere. Today, Texas Roadhouse has grown into a popular chain across the United States, known for its inviting ambiance and delicious, quality food.
