Best Headshot Backgrounds: A Complete Guide

When choosing the best background for headshots, opt for neutral tones like white, gray, or beige to ensure focus remains on your face. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that can distract. Natural settings or blurred outdoor scenes can create warmth, while solid colors convey professionalism. Adapt your background to your brand!
