D&D Backgrounds Demystified: Enhance Your Character's Story

In Dungeons & Dragons, backgrounds offer characters a rich backstory and unique skills. They define a character's life prior to adventuring, shaping motivations and abilities. Each background provides specific proficiencies, languages, and equipment, enhancing gameplay. By choosing a background, players add depth and creativity, personalizing their hero's journey.
