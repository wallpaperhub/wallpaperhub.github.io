Understanding NICU Nurse Background: Roles & Qualifications

NICU nurses are specialized healthcare professionals who care for critically ill newborns in Neonatal Intensive Care Units. They typically possess a Bachelor’s degree in Nursing (BSN) and have completed additional training in neonatal care. Their expertise helps monitor vital signs, administer medications, and provide emotional support to families during challenging times.
