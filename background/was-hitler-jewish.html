Was Hitler Jewish? Debunking the Myth

There is a persistent myth that Adolf Hitler had Jewish ancestry, but extensive historical research has debunked this claim. Hitler, who led the Nazi regime responsible for the Holocaust, had a clear anti-Semitic ideology. His beliefs were rooted in a false notion of Aryan superiority, making the idea of him being Jewish implausible and unfounded.
