Explore the World of Herbs: History & Uses

Herbs have been treasured for centuries, revered for their culinary, medicinal, and aromatic properties. Originating from diverse climates worldwide, these plants are often categorized into culinary herbs like basil and thyme, and medicinal herbs such as ginseng and echinacea. Their rich history and versatility make them essential in various cultures.
