Pittsburgh Steelers: A Legacy of Excellence

The Pittsburgh Steelers, established in 1933, are a professional American football team based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. They are a member of the National Football League (NFL) and the AFC North division. Known for their legendary defense, "Steel Curtain," and their iconic black and gold colors, the Steelers have won six Super Bowl titles, making them one of the most successful franchises in NFL history. Their loyal fan base and storied rivalries, especially with the Baltimore Ravens, contribute to their rich legacy.
