Reduce Background Processes for Optimal Device Performance

Having too many background processes running can significantly slow down your device, causing lag and inefficiencies. These processes consume valuable resources, including RAM and CPU, leading to slower load times and reduced performance. Managing and limiting background tasks can help improve overall speed and responsiveness.
