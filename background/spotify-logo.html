Discover the Meaning Behind the Spotify Logo

The Spotify logo features a simple yet recognizable design that consists of a green circle with three black sound wave lines radiating from the center. This logo symbolizes music streaming and the brand's mission to connect artists with audiences. Its minimalist style makes it versatile for various applications, from app icons to promotional material.
