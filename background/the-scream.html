The Scream: Understanding the Iconic Masterpiece

The Scream is an iconic painting by Edvard Munch, created in 1893, that captures deep emotion and existential angst. The figure's haunting expression against a turbulent sky evokes feelings of anxiety and despair, making it a powerful symbol of modern art. The piece continues to resonate with viewers, reflecting the human condition.
