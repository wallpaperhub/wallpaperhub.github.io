How to Change PS5 Theme: A Quick Guide

To change your PS5 theme, start from the main home screen. Navigate to "Settings," then select "Themes." From there, you can choose between the default themes or select "Custom" to upload your own. Follow the prompts to apply your chosen theme and enjoy a fresh look for your gaming experience!
