Understanding Green Gas: A Sustainable Energy Source

Green gas, also known as biogas, is produced from organic matter decomposition. It primarily consists of methane and carbon dioxide. As a renewable energy source, green gas can be harnessed from agricultural waste, landfills, and wastewater, contributing to reduced greenhouse gas emissions and promoting sustainability in energy production.
