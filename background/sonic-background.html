Enhancing Your Experience: The Power of Sonic Background

Sonic background refers to the auditory environment in which sounds occur, integrating layers of audio that create a rich listening experience. It encompasses music, sound effects, and ambient noise, all designed to enhance storytelling or gameplay. Whether in film, video games, or art, a well-crafted sonic background adds depth and immersion.
