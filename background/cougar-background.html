Understanding Cougars: A Comprehensive Guide

Cougars, also known as mountain lions or pumas, are large, powerful carnivorous mammals native to the Americas. They are found in a variety of habitats, from mountains to forests and deserts. With sleek, muscular bodies and adaptable behavior, cougars are solitary and territorial, primarily hunting deer and smaller animals.
