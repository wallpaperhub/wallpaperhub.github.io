Tips to Avoid Distracting Backgrounds in Photos

Photos with distracting backgrounds can take away from the subject's impact, making the image less engaging. To enhance your photography, focus on using simple, uncluttered backdrops. Alternatively, utilize techniques such as depth of field to blur the background, drawing attention to your subject and creating a more polished composition.
