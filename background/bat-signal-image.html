Iconic Bat Signal: A Symbol of Hope in Gotham

The Bat Signal, a legendary image in the Batman universe, symbolizes hope and justice. When Gotham faces danger, this iconic spotlight, bearing the Batman emblem, illuminates the sky, calling upon the Dark Knight to save the day. It represents the bond between the city and its vigilante, embodying the fight against crime and despair.
