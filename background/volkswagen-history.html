Discovering Volkswagen: A Legacy of Innovation

Volkswagen, founded in 1937 in Germany, began with the iconic Beetle designed by Ferdinand Porsche. The brand became known for affordable, reliable cars, gaining popularity post-WWII. Over the decades, it expanded globally, introducing models like the Golf and Passat. Volkswagen remains a leader in the automotive industry, emphasizing innovation and sustainability.
