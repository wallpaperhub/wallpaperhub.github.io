i9 vs W2: Key Differences Explained

When comparing i9 and W2 forms, it's essential to understand their purposes. The i9 form verifies identity and eligibility to work in the U.S., while the W2 form reports employee wages and tax withholdings. Employers complete these forms for compliance, impacting both the employer and employee's tax responsibilities.
