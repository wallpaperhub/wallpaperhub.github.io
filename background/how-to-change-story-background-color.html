How to Change Your Story Background Color in Just a Few Steps

Changing the background color of a story is easy! First, access your story editing tool and select the background option. You can choose colors from the palette or input a hex code for a custom shade. Preview your changes to ensure it fits your theme, then save. Your new background will make your story pop!
