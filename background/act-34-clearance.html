Essential Guide to Act 34 Clearance

Act 34 clearance is a critical process for individuals seeking employment in specific fields, particularly in education, healthcare, and social services. It ensures that applicants have no criminal background that could pose a risk to vulnerable populations. Obtaining this clearance involves a thorough background check and is mandatory for compliance with state regulations.
