Essential Guide to Level 2 Background Checks in Florida

In Florida, a Level 2 background check is comprehensive, involving fingerprinting and review of national and state databases. It's often required for positions with vulnerable populations, such as children and the elderly. The process ensures employers can make informed hiring decisions while maintaining safety and compliance with state laws.
