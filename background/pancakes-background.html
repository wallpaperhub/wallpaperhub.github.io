The Delicious History of Pancakes: A Global Favorite

Pancakes have a rich history dating back thousands of years, with variations across cultures. Originating from ancient Greece and Rome, they were made from flour, water, and sometimes milk and spices. Today, pancakes are enjoyed worldwide, from fluffy American stacks to delicate French crêpes, each reflecting local flavors and traditions.
