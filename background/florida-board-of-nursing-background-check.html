Essential Guide to Florida Board of Nursing Background Check

To become a licensed nurse in Florida, individuals must undergo a thorough background check by the Florida Board of Nursing. This process ensures applicants meet professional standards and helps maintain public safety. It involves criminal history checks and verification of past nursing credentials, essential for maintaining the integrity of healthcare.
