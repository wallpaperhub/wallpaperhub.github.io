Do Cats Prefer Background Noise? Understanding Their Preferences

Many cats have varied responses to background noise. Some may enjoy soft sounds like music or a washing machine, finding comfort in the gentle hum. Others may be startled or anxious by louder noises, like TV volume or construction sounds. Observing your cat's reactions will help you understand their preferences better.
