Zion National Park: A Journey Through History

Zion National Park, established in 1919, has a rich history rooted in both Native American culture and Western exploration. The Paiute people inhabited the area millennia ago, relying on its resources. The park's stunning canyons and rock formations attracted European settlers in the 1800s, leading to its preservation as a national park for future generations.
