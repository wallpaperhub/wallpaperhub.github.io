Essential Education for Aspiring Theatre Critics

To become a successful theatre critic, a background in theatre studies, literature, or journalism is recommended. Familiarity with different genres, dramatic theory, and an understanding of performance techniques can enhance critiques. Experience in theatre production or acting can also provide valuable insights into the art form.
