Discover the Vibrant Beauty of Talavera Pottery

Talavera refers to a style of pottery from Mexico, known for its vibrant colors and intricate designs. Originating from the town of Talavera de la Reina in Spain, this craft flourished in Puebla, blending indigenous and Spanish influences. Talavera tiles and pottery are celebrated for their beauty and craftsmanship, often used in home decor.
