Understanding North Carolina Background Check Laws

North Carolina background check laws regulate how employers and organizations can access and utilize criminal history information. Employers must obtain written consent before conducting a background check and must follow the Fair Credit Reporting Act. Certain offenses can be expunged, which may affect hiring decisions.
