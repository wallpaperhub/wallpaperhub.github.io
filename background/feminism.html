Empowering Equality: Understanding Feminism

Feminism is a movement advocating for women's rights on the grounds of political, social, and economic equality to men. It aims to dismantle the systemic barriers and societal norms that perpetuate gender inequality. By promoting empowerment, challenging stereotypes, and striving for equal opportunities, feminism shapes a fairer society for all.
