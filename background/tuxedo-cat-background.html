Tuxedo Cats: The Charming Companions

Tuxedo cats, distinguished by their black and white coats resembling formal wear, are charming and playful companions. They are not a specific breed, but rather a color pattern seen in various breeds. Often associated with a blend of curiosity and intelligence, these cats are known for their affectionate nature and lively antics, making them beloved pets.
