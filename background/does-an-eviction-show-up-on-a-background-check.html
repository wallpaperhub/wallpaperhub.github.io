Do Evictions Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, an eviction can show up on a background check. Landlords typically conduct background checks to assess a tenant's rental history, and evictions are part of that. They are publicly available records, which means they may influence potential landlords' decisions if a tenant has a history of eviction.
