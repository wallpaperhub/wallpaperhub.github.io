"Exploring the Jungle Book: Origins & Themes"

"The Jungle Book," originally a collection of stories by Rudyard Kipling published in 1894, is set in the lush jungles of India. It follows Mowgli, a boy raised by wolves, as he navigates the dangers of the jungle, including encounters with the fierce tiger Shere Khan and wise Baloo the bear. Themes of survival, friendship, and identity abound.
