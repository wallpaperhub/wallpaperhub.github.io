Discover the Charm of Sterling Silver: Quality & Elegance

Sterling, often referring to sterling silver, is an alloy primarily made of silver and copper, known for its durability and shine. It is the standard for silver quality, with a composition of 92.5% silver and 7.5% other metals. Sterling silver is popular in jewelry, utensils, and decorative items due to its beautiful luster and resistance to tarnish.
