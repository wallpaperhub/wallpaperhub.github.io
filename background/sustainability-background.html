Maximizing Sustainability: A Background Overview

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs without compromising future generations' ability to meet their own. It encompasses environmental, social, and economic dimensions, aiming for a balance that preserves ecosystems while fostering economic growth and social equity. Education and innovation play crucial roles in promoting sustainable practices.
