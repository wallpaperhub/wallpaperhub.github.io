Discover the World of Warrior Cats: Background & Lore

Warrior Cats is a popular book series by Erin Hunter, set in a fictional feline universe where clans of wild cats live in the forests. Each clan has its own territory, customs, and struggles, creating an intricate social structure. Themes of loyalty, bravery, and survival are central, as cats battle for resources while navigating complex relationships.
