Misdemeanor Records: Duration and Impact

A misdemeanor typically stays on your record for several years, often between 1 to 5 years, depending on state laws. In some cases, it may be possible to expunge or seal the record, which can help mitigate the long-term effects. Knowing the jurisdiction's regulations is crucial for understanding your options.
