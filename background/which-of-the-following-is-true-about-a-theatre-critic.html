Understanding Theatre Critics: Insights & Reviews

A theatre critic is a professional who reviews and evaluates live performances, often providing insights into acting, direction, and stage design. They influence public opinion and can impact a show's success. A good critic balances subjective taste with objective analysis and helps audiences navigate the rich landscape of theatrical offerings.
