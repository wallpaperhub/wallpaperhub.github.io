Understanding Alcohol: A Psychoactive Depressant

Alcohol is a psychoactive substance classified as a depressant, which affects the central nervous system. It slows down brain function, leading to relaxed inhibitions and impaired coordination. While commonly consumed in various beverages, excessive use can lead to addiction, health issues, and negative social impacts.
