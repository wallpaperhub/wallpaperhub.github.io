Stunning Japanese Waves: Nature's Artistry

Japanese waves, or "Nihon no nami," are renowned for their beauty and significance in art, especially within woodblock prints. These waves, often depicted as swirling forms and dynamic lines, symbolize nature's power. From Hokusai's iconic "Great Wave off Kanagawa" to tranquil beach scenes, they convey serenity and strength.
