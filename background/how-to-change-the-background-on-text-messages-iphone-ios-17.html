Customize Your iPhone Text Message Background in iOS 17

To change the background on text messages in iOS 17, open the Messages app and navigate to a conversation. Tap on the contact's name or profile photo at the top. Select "Info," then choose "Change Background." Here, you can select a photo from your library or choose from the available options. Save the changes!
