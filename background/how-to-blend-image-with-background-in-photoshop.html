Blend Images Seamlessly in Photoshop

To seamlessly blend an image with the background in Photoshop, start by using the Quick Selection Tool to isolate the subject. Refine the edges with "Select and Mask." Once your selection is perfect, place it over your background layer. Adjust opacity and use layer masks to blend edges smoothly, achieving a cohesive look.
