Discover the Fascinating Background of Shrek

Shrek, the iconic animated character from DreamWorks, debuted in 2001 in his own feature film. Created as a parody of fairy tales, Shrek's background is rooted in being an ogre who loves his swampy solitude. The film uniquely combines humor, heart, and adventure as Shrek embarks on a quest to rescue Princess Fiona, discovering friendship and self-acceptance along the way.
