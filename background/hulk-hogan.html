Hulk Hogan: Wrestling Legend & Cultural Icon

Hulk Hogan, born Terry Gene Bollea, is a legendary professional wrestler known for his charismatic persona and mustache. Rising to fame in the 1980s, he became a cultural icon, headlining numerous WrestleMania events and starring in various films and television shows. Hogan's larger-than-life character and catchphrases like "Hulkamania" made him a household name.
