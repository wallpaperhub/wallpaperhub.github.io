Understanding Jean Piaget: Pioneer of Child Development

Jean Piaget was a pioneering Swiss psychologist known for his work in child development and cognitive theory. He proposed that children progress through four stages of cognitive development: sensorimotor, preoperational, concrete operational, and formal operational. His theories have profoundly influenced education and psychology.
