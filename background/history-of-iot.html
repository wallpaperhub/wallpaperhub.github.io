Unveiling the History of IoT: A Brief Overview

The history of IoT (Internet of Things) began in the late 20th century, with the term coined in 1999. Early developments included connected devices like toasters and refrigerators. The rise of wireless technology and cloud computing in the 2000s propelled IoT into various sectors, facilitating smart homes, industries, and cities, revolutionizing connectivity.
