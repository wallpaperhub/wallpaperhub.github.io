Mastering Aperture Priority for Stunning Photos

Aperture priority is a camera setting that allows photographers to control the aperture (f-stop) while the camera automatically selects the appropriate shutter speed. This mode is perfect for achieving the desired depth of field, making it ideal for portraits or landscape photography. Mastering aperture priority enhances creative expression.
