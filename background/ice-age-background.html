Understanding the Ice Age: Key Facts & Impact

The Ice Age, spanning from about 2.4 million to 11,700 years ago, was marked by significant climatic changes leading to large ice sheets covering vast areas of the Earth. This period saw the evolution and extinction of various species, including mammoths and saber-toothed cats. The Ice Age significantly shaped our planet's landscapes and ecosystems.
