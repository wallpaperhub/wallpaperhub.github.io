Understanding Geology: Insights into Earth's Processes

Geology is the scientific study of the Earth, focusing on its materials, processes, and history. It encompasses the analysis of rocks, minerals, and geological formations, revealing how Earth's structure has evolved over time. Understanding geology is crucial for natural resource management, environmental protection, and hazard assessment.
