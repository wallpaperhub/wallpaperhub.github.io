Understanding Passover: Meaning & Significance

Passover, or Pesach, is a significant Jewish holiday commemorating the Exodus of the Israelites from slavery in Egypt. It symbolizes freedom, faith, and the importance of remembering one's heritage. Observed with rituals like the Seder meal, Passover teaches the values of liberation and gratitude, uniting families in reflection and celebration.
