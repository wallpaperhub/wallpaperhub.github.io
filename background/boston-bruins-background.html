Boston Bruins: A Legacy of Excellence in Hockey

The Boston Bruins, established in 1924, are one of the Original Six NHL teams. Known for their iconic black and gold colors, they have a rich history filled with legendary players like Bobby Orr and Phil Esposito. The team's passionate fanbase and historic home at TD Garden make the Bruins a central figure in Boston sports culture.
