Understanding Skinner: The Pioneer of Behaviorism

Skinner, an influential American psychologist, is best known for his work on behaviorism and operant conditioning. By focusing on observable behaviors rather than internal thoughts, he revolutionized psychology. His experiments with animals demonstrated how rewards and punishments shape actions, establishing foundational principles for modern behavioral therapy.
