Left 4 Dead 2: Survive the Zombie Apocalypse

Left 4 Dead 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter game developed by Valve, set in a post-apocalyptic world plagued by a zombie infection. Players assume the roles of survivors battling hordes of infected in various locales, utilizing teamwork and strategy. The game's immersive atmosphere, diverse environments, and memorable characters make it a classic in the genre.
