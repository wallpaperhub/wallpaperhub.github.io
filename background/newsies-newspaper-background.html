Newsies: The Inspiring Story of Young Newspaper Sellers

The Newsies newspaper movement, originating in the late 19th century, involved young boys selling newspapers on the streets of New York City. Facing exploitation from publishers, they organized strikes to demand fair wages and better working conditions. Their struggle highlighted labor rights and the impact of youth activism, inspiring future generations.
