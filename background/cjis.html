Understanding CJIS: Your Guide to Criminal Justice Information

The Criminal Justice Information Services (CJIS) Division is a critical component of the FBI, managing the nation’s criminal justice data. It provides valuable information to law enforcement agencies, ensuring efficient crime-solving and public safety. CJIS encompasses a range of services, including background checks, fingerprinting, and crime statistics analysis.
