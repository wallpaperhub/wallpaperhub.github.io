Pearl Jam: The Iconic Rock Band That Redefined Grunge

Pearl Jam is an iconic American rock band formed in 1990 in Seattle. Known for their powerful lyrics and passionate performances, they played a pivotal role in the grunge movement of the 1990s. With hits like "Alive" and "Jeremy," the band has inspired countless fans. Their commitment to social issues and authentic sound sets them apart in the music scene.
