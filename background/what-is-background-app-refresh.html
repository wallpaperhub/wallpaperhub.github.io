Understanding Background App Refresh: Optimize Your Device

Background App Refresh is a feature on smartphones and tablets that allows apps to update their content in the background while you use other apps. This ensures you have the latest information available as soon as you open them, enhancing the user experience. However, it can impact battery life and data usage.
