How to Tackle High Background in Western Blot

High background in western blotting can obscure specific protein signals and lead to misinterpretation of results. This issue may arise from non-specific antibody binding, excess protein loading, or inadequate wash steps. To address high background, optimizing antibody concentrations, adjusting incubation times, and enhancing wash buffers can vastly improve clarity.
