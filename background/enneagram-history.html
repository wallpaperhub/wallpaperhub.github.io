Uncovering the History of the Enneagram: A Journey Through Time

The Enneagram's history dates back to ancient traditions, with roots in Greek philosophy, early Christian mysticism, and the teachings of Gurdjieff in the 20th century. It evolved into a personality framework that categorizes nine distinct types. Today, the Enneagram is widely used for personal growth, relationships, and organizational development, blending spirituality and psychology.
