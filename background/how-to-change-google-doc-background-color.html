Change Google Docs Background Color: A Quick Guide

To change the background color in Google Docs, open your document and navigate to the "File" menu. Select "Page setup," then click on the "Background color" option. Choose your desired color from the palette. Finally, click "OK" to apply the change to your document's background and enjoy a personalized touch!
