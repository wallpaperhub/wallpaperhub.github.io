Key Development Theorists: Insights Into Human Growth

Development theorists are key figures in understanding human growth and learning. They explore various stages and aspects of development, including cognitive, social, and emotional domains. Notable theorists such as Piaget, Vygotsky, and Erikson provide valuable insights into how individuals progress and adapt across different life stages.
