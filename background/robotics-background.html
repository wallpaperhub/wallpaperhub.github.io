Exploring the Foundations of Robotics: A Brief Overview

Robotics, a fusion of engineering and computer science, focuses on designing, building, and programming machines capable of performing tasks autonomously or semi-autonomously. With roots in artificial intelligence and control systems, it has applications ranging from manufacturing to healthcare, enhancing productivity and precision across various fields.
