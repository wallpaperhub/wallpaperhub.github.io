Can You Take the Hazmat Test Before Your Background Check?

Yes, you can take the hazmat test before your background check is completed. Many individuals choose to study for the test and complete it to expedite the process. However, keep in mind that passing the hazmat test does not guarantee approval; you must still pass the background check to obtain your hazmat endorsement.
