Transform Your iPhone Photos with a Black Background

To make the background black on your iPhone photo, open the Photos app and select the image you want to edit. Tap "Edit," then choose "Filters." Select "Mono" or "Silvertone" to convert the image to black and white. You can then adjust the brightness and contrast to enhance the black background for a dramatic effect.
