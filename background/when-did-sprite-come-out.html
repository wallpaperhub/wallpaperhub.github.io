When Did Sprite Come Out? A Brief History

Sprite, the popular lemon-lime flavored soft drink, made its debut in 1961. Initially launched as a competitor to 7 Up, it was developed by the Coca-Cola Company and quickly gained popularity for its refreshing taste. Over the years, Sprite has become a staple in the soda market, known for its iconic green packaging and tagline, "Obey Your Thirst."
