Essential Guide to Sunscreen: Protect Your Skin

Sunscreen is a topical product designed to protect the skin from harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation emitted by the sun. It contains active ingredients like zinc oxide or chemical absorbers that prevent sunburn, skin aging, and reduce the risk of skin cancer. Regular use is vital for maintaining skin health and preventing damage.
