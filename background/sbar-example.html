Effective SBAR Communication in Healthcare

SBAR is a critical communication tool used in healthcare settings to ensure clear and concise information exchange. It stands for Situation, Background, Assessment, and Recommendation. An example would be: "Situation: Patient has elevated blood pressure. Background: History of hypertension. Assessment: Likely unstable. Recommendation: Administer medication and monitor." This method enhances clarity and patient safety.
