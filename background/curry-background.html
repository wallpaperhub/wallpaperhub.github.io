Exploring the Rich History of Curry

Curry has a rich history that spans continents, originating from South Asia over 4,000 years ago. Initially used for medicinal purposes, it evolved into a flavorful blend of spices. Its popularity spread through trade routes to Southeast Asia, the Caribbean, and beyond, leading to various regional adaptations that enhance its global appeal.
