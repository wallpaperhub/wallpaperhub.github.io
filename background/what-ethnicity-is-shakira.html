What Ethnicity is Shakira? Discover Her Roots

Shakira, the Colombian singer and songwriter, has a rich ethnic background. She is primarily of Lebanese and Spanish descent, with her Lebanese roots tracing back to her paternal grandparents. This diverse heritage influences her music and dance, which reflects a blend of various cultural elements, making her a global icon.
