Jasmine: The Fragrant Flower with a Rich History

Jasmine, a fragrant flowering plant, thrives in warm climates and is cherished for its delicate white or yellow blossoms. Historically significant in various cultures, it symbolizes purity and love. Jasmine has medicinal properties and plays a vital role in perfumery, making it a beloved choice in gardens worldwide.
