Understanding the Geosphere: Earth's Solid Foundation

The geosphere comprises the solid parts of Earth, including rocks, minerals, landforms, and the processes that shape them. It interacts with the atmosphere, hydrosphere, and biosphere, playing a crucial role in Earth’s systems. Understanding the geosphere helps us comprehend geological processes, climate change, and natural resources.
