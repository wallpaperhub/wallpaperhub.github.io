Understanding Vitiligo: Causes, Symptoms, and Treatment

Vitiligo is a long-term skin condition that causes patches of skin to lose their pigment, resulting in lighter areas. This occurs when melanocytes, the cells responsible for skin color, are destroyed. Although vitiligo can affect anyone and at any age, it is often associated with autoimmune disorders. Treatments focus on skin care and cosmetic solutions.
