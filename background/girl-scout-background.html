Empowering Girls: The Girl Scouts Journey

Girl Scouts, founded in 1912 by Juliette Gordon Low, aims to empower girls through leadership, community service, and skill development. This program encourages participants to engage in various activities, including camping, fundraising, and public speaking. Girls build confidence and friendships while fostering a spirit of teamwork and resilience.
