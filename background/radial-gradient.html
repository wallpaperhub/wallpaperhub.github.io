Discover the Beauty of Radial Gradients

A radial gradient is a visual design technique where colors radiate outward from a central point. This versatility allows it to create depth and dimension in backgrounds, illustrations, and web design. Radial gradients enhance aesthetic appeal by blending multiple hues, producing a smooth transition that draws the viewer's eye toward the center.
