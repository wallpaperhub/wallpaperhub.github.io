Unveiling the History of Professional Wrestling

Professional wrestling has a rich history, evolving from early carnivals and traveling shows in the late 19th century to the mainstream entertainment spectacle it is today. Key milestones include the formation of major promotions like WWE in the 1980s, the rise of televised wrestling, and its cultural impact through global fandom and various forms of media.
