Understanding Microbiology: The Invisible World

Microbiology is the study of microscopic organisms, including bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa. These tiny life forms are crucial to numerous natural processes, from decomposition to human health. Understanding microbiology helps in disease control, biotechnology, and environmental science, spotlighting its impact on our daily lives.
