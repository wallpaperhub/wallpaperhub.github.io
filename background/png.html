Mastering PNG: The Best Image Format for Digital Graphics

PNG stands for Portable Network Graphics, a popular image format known for its lossless compression and transparency support. Ideal for web graphics, PNG files retain image quality without degrading color information. They are widely used for logos, icons, and images requiring clear backgrounds, making them perfect for digital applications.
