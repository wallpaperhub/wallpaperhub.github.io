Who Wrote the Corinthians? A Look at Apostle Paul's Letters

The Apostle Paul is credited with writing the letters known as Corinthians in the Bible. Addressed to the Christian community in Corinth, these epistles—1 Corinthians and 2 Corinthians—offer guidance, correction, and teachings on faith. Paul's insights address moral issues, unity within the church, and the essence of love.
