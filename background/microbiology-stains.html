Essential Microbiology Stains for Microbe Identification

Microbiology stains are essential tools in the study of microorganisms, aiding in their identification and characterization. They enhance visibility under a microscope by coloring cells or structures, helping to distinguish between types of bacteria, fungi, and other microbes. Common techniques include Gram staining, acid-fast staining, and special stains for capsules and spores.
