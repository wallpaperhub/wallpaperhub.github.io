Explore the World of Public Domain Music

Public domain music consists of melodies and compositions that are no longer protected by copyright, allowing anyone to use and share them freely. This includes works by famous composers like Bach and Beethoven, as well as traditional folk songs. Accessing public domain music is a great way to enrich your projects without licensing fees.
