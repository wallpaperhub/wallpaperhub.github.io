Should You Leave Music On for Your Dog? Find Out!

Leaving music on for your dog can have positive effects. Soothing tunes can help reduce anxiety, especially during stressful situations like thunderstorms or being alone. Choose calming music specifically designed for dogs, and monitor their reactions. Every dog is unique; some may love it, while others may prefer silence.
