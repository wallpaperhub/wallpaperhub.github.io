Colorful Sprinkles: A Sweet History & Fun Facts

Sprinkles are small, colorful confectionery toppings often used to add a decorative touch and sweetness to desserts like cakes, cookies, and ice cream. They come in various shapes, sizes, and flavors, making them a popular choice for celebrations. From birthday parties to weddings, sprinkles elevate the joy of any treat!
