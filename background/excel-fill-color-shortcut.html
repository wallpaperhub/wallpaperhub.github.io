Master Excel Fill Color Shortcuts for Efficient Formatting

Using the fill color shortcut in Excel can enhance your spreadsheet's visual appeal. To quickly fill a cell or range with color, simply select the cells and press "Alt + H + H" to open the fill color menu. Then, use the arrow keys to choose your desired color and hit Enter. This efficient method saves time and streamlines your workflow!
