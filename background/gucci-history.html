Discover the Fascinating History of Gucci

Gucci, founded in 1921 in Florence by Guccio Gucci, began as a leather goods shop. The brand became renowned for its craftsmanship and innovation, integrating Italian luxury with artisanal quality. Throughout the decades, it grew into a global fashion powerhouse, known for iconic products like the GG logo and unique prints, reflecting style evolution.
