Airbnb Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Airbnb does conduct background checks, primarily for hosts to ensure a safe environment for guests. While these checks vary by location and can include criminal history, they are not foolproof. Guests are encouraged to also check reviews and ratings to ensure they choose reliable properties and hosts.
