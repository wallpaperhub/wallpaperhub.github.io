Discover the Background of Basketball Legend Allen Iverson

Allen Iverson, born June 7, 1975, in Hampton, Virginia, is a former professional basketball player and Hall of Famer. Known for his incredible scoring ability and tenacious playing style, he played the majority of his career with the Philadelphia 76ers. Iverson's influence extended beyond the court, impacting the culture of basketball worldwide.
