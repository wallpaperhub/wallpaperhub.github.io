Wheat: A Global Staple and Its Rich History

Wheat, one of the world's oldest cultivated grains, dates back thousands of years, flourishing in various climates. It's a staple food source, rich in nutrients and versatility, forming the basis of many diets globally. From bread to pasta, wheat’s significance in agriculture, economy, and cuisine continues to grow, making it essential for food security.
