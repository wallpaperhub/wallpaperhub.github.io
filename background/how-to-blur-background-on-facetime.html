How to Blur Background on FaceTime: Step-by-Step Guide

To blur the background on FaceTime, ensure you're using an iPhone or iPad with iOS 15 or later. Start your FaceTime call, then swipe up from the bottom of the screen or tap the effects button (star icon). Select the "Blur Background" option. This feature helps keep the focus on you while obscuring distractions behind.
