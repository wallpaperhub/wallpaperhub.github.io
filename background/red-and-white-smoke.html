Red and White Smoke: Symbolism and Significance

Red and white smoke can evoke powerful emotions and symbolize various meanings depending on its context. Often seen in celebrations, sports events, or protests, red can symbolize passion and energy, while white represents purity and peace. Together, they create a striking visual contrast that captures attention and stirs emotions.
