Understanding Justice Background: A Brief Overview

Justice background refers to the historical and philosophical foundations of the concept of justice in societies. It encompasses various theories, such as distributive, restorative, and procedural justice, shaped by cultural and social influences. Understanding this background is crucial for navigating contemporary legal systems and advocating for fairness.
