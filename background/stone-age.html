Discover the Stone Age: Humanity's Dawn

The Stone Age marks humanity's early period, characterized by the use of primitive tools made from stone. This era is divided into three phases: Paleolithic, Mesolithic, and Neolithic. Key developments during this time included the mastery of fire, the creation of art, and the beginnings of agriculture, shaping human civilization.
