Understanding Pipe Background: History and Use

Pipes have been a fundamental part of human civilization, dating back to ancient times when they were used for irrigation and plumbing. Made from various materials such as clay, metal, and plastic, pipes facilitate the transport of fluids and gases. Their design and construction have evolved significantly, influencing industries from construction to sanitation.
