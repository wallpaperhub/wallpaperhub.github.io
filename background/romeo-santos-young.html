Young Romeo Santos: Rise of a Bachata Legend

Romeo Santos, born July 21, 1981, in the Bronx, New York, grew up surrounded by music, deeply influenced by his Dominican heritage. As a young boy, he began singing in local talent shows and formed the popular bachata group Aventura. His rich vocals and charismatic stage presence set the foundation for his stellar solo career.
