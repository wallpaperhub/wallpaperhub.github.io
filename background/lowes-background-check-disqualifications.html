Understanding Lowe's Background Check Disqualifications

Lowes conducts thorough background checks on potential employees, considering various disqualifications. Major offenses like felonies, theft, or fraud can lead to automatic disqualification. Additionally, a poor driving record may impact positions requiring driving. Understanding these factors can help applicants prepare more effectively.
