Understanding Ohio Background Check Laws: A Quick Guide

Ohio background check laws govern the use of criminal and personal history checks for various purposes, including employment and housing. Under Ohio law, individuals have the right to request their own background check for accuracy and organizations must obtain consent before conducting checks. Certain offenses can be sealed, enhancing privacy.
