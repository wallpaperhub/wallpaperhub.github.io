Change Background in Google Drawings: A Quick Guide

To change the background in Google Drawings, open your drawing and click on "Background" in the toolbar or right-click on the canvas. Select a color or choose "Image" to upload a picture. Adjust the opacity if needed to make your background blend seamlessly with your design. Save to apply the changes.
