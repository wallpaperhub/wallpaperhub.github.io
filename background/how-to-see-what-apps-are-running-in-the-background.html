How to Check Background Apps on Your Device

To check which apps are running in the background, open your device’s settings and navigate to the “Apps” or “Application Manager” section. Here, you can see active apps. On Android, swipe up from the bottom and hold to access the app switcher. For iOS, double-click the Home button or swipe up from the bottom (iPhone X and later).
