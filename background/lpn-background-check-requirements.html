Essential LPN Background Check Requirements Explained

When applying for Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) positions, conducting a background check is essential. Requirements typically include a criminal history check, verification of nursing credentials, and examination of any prior disciplinary actions. Each state may have specific regulations, so always refer to local guidelines when preparing.
