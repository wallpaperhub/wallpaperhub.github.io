Ultimate Guide to Green Screen Magic

Green screens are a powerful tool in video production, enabling creators to replace backgrounds effortlessly. By filming in front of a green backdrop, you can superimpose any image or video behind your subject, enhancing storytelling. This technique is widely used in film, gaming, and live streaming for seamless visual effects.
