How Landlords Verify Evictions: A Quick Guide

Landlords typically check for evictions through background checks, which may include searching public records and tenant screening services. These checks provide insights into previous rental behaviors, including any past evictions, payment history, and criminal records. This thorough process helps landlords make informed decisions before renting to new tenants.
