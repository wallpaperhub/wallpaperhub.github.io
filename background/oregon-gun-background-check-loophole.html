Addressing Oregon's Gun Background Check Loophole

Oregon's gun background check loophole allows private sellers to bypass mandatory checks, raising concerns about safety and crime. Critics argue this creates an avenue for prohibited individuals to obtain firearms easily. Advocates for reform push for closing the loophole to ensure comprehensive background checks for all gun transactions.
