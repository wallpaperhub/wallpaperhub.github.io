Clean Up Your Background: Tips for a Professional Look

A dirty background can undermine the professionalism of any image, whether it's a photograph or a presentation. It distracts the viewer from the main subject and can convey a lack of attention to detail. Cleaning up the background can enhance clarity and focus, giving a polished impression that resonates positively with audiences.
