Elon Musk: The Journey from Childhood to Tech Titan

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He showcased an early interest in technology, coding his first video game at age 12. After moving to Canada and later to the U.S., Musk co-founded Zip2, and his entrepreneurial journey began. His early life was marked by ambition, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge.
