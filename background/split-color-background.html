Split Color Backgrounds: A Dynamic Design Trend

Split color backgrounds are a vibrant design trend that enhances visual appeal by dividing the canvas into two or more contrasting colors. This technique adds depth and character to websites, posters, and social media graphics. Using complementary tones can draw attention and create a dynamic visual flow, perfect for engaging audiences effectively.
