Who Owns Scrub Daddy? Discover the Brand's Origins

Scrub Daddy, the popular smiley-faced cleaning sponge, was created by entrepreneur Aaron Krause. The company, Scrub Daddy Inc., gained immense fame after appearing on Shark Tank, where it secured an investment from Lori Greiner. Today, Scrub Daddy remains a privately owned business, with Krause still at the helm, overseeing innovation.
