Exploring the Rich Legacy of Green Lantern

Green Lantern is a hero within the DC Comics universe, first appearing in 1940. The character is part of an intergalactic police force known as the Green Lantern Corps, wielding a power ring that can create anything the wearer imagines. The legacy includes several individuals, such as Hal Jordan and John Stewart, each facing unique challenges and villains.
