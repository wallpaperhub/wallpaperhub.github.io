Mastering Switch Tasking: Boost Productivity

Switch tasking refers to the process of rapidly shifting focus from one task to another, often leading to decreased productivity and increased errors. Unlike multitasking, which involves doing multiple tasks simultaneously, switch tasking can cause cognitive overload, making it harder to achieve quality results. Finding strategies to minimize switch tasking can improve efficiency and focus.
