Understanding Background Data Usage: What It Means

Allowing background data usage means permitting apps to use data even when not actively open. This enables timely updates, notifications, and syncing, but it can also lead to higher data consumption and potentially affect battery life. Users should manage this feature based on their data plans and usage patterns.
