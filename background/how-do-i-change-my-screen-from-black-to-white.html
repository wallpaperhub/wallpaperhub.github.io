How to Switch Your Screen from Black to White

To change your screen from black to white, go to your device's settings. For most devices, select 'Display' or 'Accessibility', then look for 'High Contrast' or 'Color Inversion' options. Disable any "dark mode" or similar settings. You can also adjust the theme in appearance settings to switch your screen to a light background.
