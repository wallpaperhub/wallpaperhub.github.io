Understanding Night Court: A Quick Guide

Night court, a unique judicial phenomenon, typically occurs after regular hours, allowing certain minor cases to be processed efficiently. It often focuses on cases involving misdemeanors, traffic violations, and minor criminal offenses. This alternative scheduling improves access to justice, easing the burden on overloaded daytime courts.
