Malala: The Brave Advocate for Girls' Education

Malala Yousafzai is a Pakistani activist known for her advocacy of girls' education. Surviving an assassination attempt by the Taliban at just 15, she transformed her struggle into an international movement, winning the Nobel Peace Prize in 2014. Malala inspires millions to champion education rights and fight against oppression.
