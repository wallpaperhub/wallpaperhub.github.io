Understanding Habakkuk: A Prophet’s Dialogue with God

Habakkuk is a unique book in the Old Testament, showcasing the prophet's dialogue with God. Concerned about injustice and the suffering of his people, Habakkuk questions divine timing and plans. God responds, emphasizing faith and the eventual triumph of righteousness. The book ends with a powerful prayer, expressing deep trust in God amidst turmoil.
