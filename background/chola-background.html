Explore the Rich History of the Chola Dynasty

The Chola dynasty, one of the longest-ruling dynasties in South India, flourished between the 9th and 13th centuries. Renowned for their advances in art, architecture, and Tamil literature, the Cholas established a vast empire spanning Southeast Asia. Their iconic bronze sculptures and majestic temples, like Brihadeeswarar, reflect their rich cultural heritage.
