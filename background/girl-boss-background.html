Empowering Women: The Rise of the Girl Boss

The term "girl boss" has its roots in the entrepreneurial spirit of women who break barriers and redefine success in the business world. This movement, popularized by the book "Girlboss" by Sophia Amoruso, encourages women to embrace leadership roles, champion their ambitions, and inspire others. It's about empowerment, resilience, and creating a supportive network for aspiring female leaders.
