Donnie Darko: A Mind-Bending Cult Classic

Donnie Darko is a cult classic film that intertwines psychological thriller and sci-fi. Set in the 1980s, it follows a troubled teenager, Donnie, who experiences bizarre visions, including a mysterious giant rabbit named Frank. As he navigates time travel and teenage angst, the film explores themes of fate, mental illness, and existentialism.
