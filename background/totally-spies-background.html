Totally Spies: Explore the Adventures of Teenage Spy Girls

Totally Spies! is an animated series that debuted in 2001, created by Vincent Chalvon-Demersay and David Michel. Set in Los Angeles, it follows three teenage girls—Sam, Clover, and Alex—who double as undercover spies for WOOHP, an international agency. The show blends action, humor, and friendship, showcasing their adventures while juggling school life.
