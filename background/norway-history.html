Explore Norway's Rich History: From Vikings to Modern Times

Norway's history is rich and varied, dating back to the Viking Age when seafaring Norsemen dominated trade and exploration. Over the centuries, it has evolved through union with Denmark, followed by a brief period under Swedish control, before achieving full independence in 1905. Modern Norway is known for its democracy and commitment to social welfare.
