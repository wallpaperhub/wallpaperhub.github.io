Understanding Heterogeneous Background Echotexture in Ultrasound

Heterogeneous background echotexture refers to the diverse patterns and variations in ultrasound imaging that indicate different tissue types and conditions. Recognizing these variations aids in accurate diagnoses and improves understanding of pathological processes. It plays a crucial role in distinguishing normal from abnormal tissue characteristics during medical evaluations.
