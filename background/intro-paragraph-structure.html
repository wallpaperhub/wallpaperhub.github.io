Perfect Intro Paragraph Structure: Crafting Engaging Beginnings

The introductory paragraph serves as the foundation of your writing. It should start with a hook to capture the reader's attention, followed by some background information to set the context. Finally, it should culminate in a clear thesis statement that outlines the main argument or purpose of the piece, guiding readers on what to expect.
