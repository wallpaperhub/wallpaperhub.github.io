Understanding Germs: Visualizing the Invisible Threat

Germs are microscopic organisms, often depicted in images as colorful, abstract forms that represent bacteria, viruses, and other pathogens. These illustrations help us visualize the unseen threats that lurk around us, emphasizing the importance of hygiene and sanitation in our daily lives to prevent illness.
