Discover the Genius of Van Gogh

Vincent van Gogh, the iconic Dutch painter, is renowned for his vivid colors and emotional depth. His masterpieces, such as "Starry Night" and "Sunflowers," reflect his turbulent life and passion for art. Despite struggling with mental health, his innovative brushwork and unique style have left a lasting legacy, influencing generations of artists.
