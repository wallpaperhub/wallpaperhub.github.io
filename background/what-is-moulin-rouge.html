Moulin Rouge: Parisian Cabaret History & Experience

Moulin Rouge is a historic cabaret in Paris, established in 1889, known for its vibrant performances and iconic can-can dance. This legendary venue has inspired countless artists and filmmakers, symbolizing the spirit of Parisian nightlife. Visitors flock to experience its rich cultural heritage and unforgettable entertainment.
