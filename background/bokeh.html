Mastering Bokeh: A Guide to Stunning Photography Effects

Bokeh is a stunning photographic effect that showcases the aesthetic quality of the blur in out-of-focus areas. It draws attention to the subject, creating a dreamy and ethereal atmosphere. This technique enhances portraits and macro shots, adding depth and visual interest. Explore the beauty of bokeh to elevate your photography!
