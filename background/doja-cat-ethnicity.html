Doja Cat's Ethnicity: A Fusion of Cultures

Doja Cat, born Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, has a mixed ethnic background that influences her music and artistry. She is of South African descent on her mother’s side and Jewish-American heritage from her father. This diverse cultural heritage is reflected in her unique musical style and vibrant persona.
