Ballet Background: Discovering the Art Form's Roots

Ballet, a classical dance form, has roots dating back to the Italian Renaissance in the 15th century. It evolved into a theatrical art during the 17th century in France and Russia, blending grace, strength, and storytelling. Characterized by its precise movements and formalized steps, ballet is renowned for its elegance and discipline, captivating audiences worldwide.
