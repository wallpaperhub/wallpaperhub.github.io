How to Change YouTube Background to Black: Easy Guide

To change your YouTube background to black, open the YouTube app or website. Click on your profile icon, then select "Dark theme" from the menu. Toggle the switch to enable the dark mode. This will change the background to black, making it easier on the eyes, especially in low light. Enjoy your new viewing experience!
