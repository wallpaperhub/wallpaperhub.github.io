Understanding Lavender Town Syndrome: The Dark Legend of Pokémon

Lavender Town Syndrome is a popular urban legend associated with the Pokémon video game series, particularly the original Pokémon Red and Green versions. It reportedly involves a haunting melody played in Lavender Town that led to psychological effects on players, including headaches and even suicidal thoughts, sparking widespread concern and mythos.
