Mild Diffuse Encephalopathy: Understanding the Basics

Mild diffuse encephalopathy is a neurological condition characterized by a generalized dysfunction of brain activity. Often detected through MRI, it can result from various factors including metabolic disturbances, infections, or trauma. Symptoms may vary, including cognitive decline, altered consciousness, and behavioral changes, warranting thorough evaluation and management.
