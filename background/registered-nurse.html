Essential Guide to Registered Nurses

Registered nurses (RNs) play a vital role in healthcare, providing patient care, educating patients, and collaborating with healthcare teams. They monitor patient health, administer medications, and advocate for patients' needs. With diverse specialties, RNs contribute significantly to improving patient outcomes and ensuring a safe healing environment.
