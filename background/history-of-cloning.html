The Fascinating History of Cloning: From Theory to Dolly

Cloning has a long and complex history, starting with early scientific theories in the 19th century. In 1996, the first successful cloning of a mammal, Dolly the sheep, marked a revolutionary moment. Since then, cloning technology has evolved, leading to advances in medicine, agriculture, and ethical debates about its implications.
