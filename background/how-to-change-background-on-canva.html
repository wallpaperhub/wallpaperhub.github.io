Change Background on Canva: A Step-by-Step Guide

To change the background on Canva, start by selecting the design you want to edit. Click on the background area or the "Background" tab in the left panel. Choose a color, texture, or an image from Canva's library or upload your own. Adjust the opacity and layer settings as needed, then save your design for sharing!
