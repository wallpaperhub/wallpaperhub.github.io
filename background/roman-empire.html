The Legacy of the Roman Empire: A Brief Overview

The Roman Empire, one of history's greatest civilizations, spanned from 27 BC until the fall of the Western Empire in AD 476. Renowned for its architectural achievements, extensive road networks, and governance, it influenced law, politics, and culture across Europe. The Empire's legacy continues to shape modern society.
