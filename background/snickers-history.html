Discover the Sweet History of Snickers

Snickers, the iconic chocolate bar, was first introduced in 1930 by the Mars Company. Named after the Mars family's favorite horse, it features nougat, caramel, peanuts, and milk chocolate. Initially marketed as a nutritious snack, it gained popularity for its delicious combination. Over decades, Snickers has become a beloved treat worldwide.
