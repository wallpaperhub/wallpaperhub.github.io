When Did Universal Orlando Open? Key Dates & History

Universal Orlando Resort officially opened its doors on June 7, 1990. Originally launched as Universal Studios Florida, the park featured attractions based on popular movies and shows. Over the years, it has expanded significantly, adding new themed areas, hotels, and attractions that have made it a premier destination for entertainment seekers.
