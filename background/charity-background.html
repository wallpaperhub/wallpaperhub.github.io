Understanding Charity: A Historical Overview

Charity has a rich background rooted in altruism, spanning centuries across various cultures. Initially emerging from religious and community-oriented practices, it evolved into organized efforts aimed at addressing social issues. Today, charities focus on poverty alleviation, education, health, and disaster relief, highlighting humanity's collective responsibility.
