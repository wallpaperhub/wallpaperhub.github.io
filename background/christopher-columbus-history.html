Christopher Columbus: The Controversial Discoverer

Christopher Columbus, an Italian explorer, is credited with 'discovering' the Americas in 1492 while sailing under the Spanish flag. His voyages opened the way for the widespread European exploration and colonization of the continent. However, his expeditions also brought significant hardships to indigenous populations.
