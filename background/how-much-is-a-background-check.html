How Much Do Background Checks Cost?

The cost of a background check varies widely depending on factors like the type of check (employment, criminal, credit), the depth of information required, and the state or agency conducting it. Generally, fees range from $10 to $100 or more. Comprehensive checks may cost upwards of $200. Always compare services for the best value.
