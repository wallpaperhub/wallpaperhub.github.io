Optimizing MacBook Wallpaper Dimensions for Perfect Fit

When choosing a wallpaper for your MacBook, it's important to know the correct dimensions to ensure a perfect fit. Most MacBook models use a resolution of 2560x1600 pixels for the Retina display. For older models, dimensions may vary, but common sizes include 1920x1080 pixels and 1440x900 pixels. Always check your model for the best results.
