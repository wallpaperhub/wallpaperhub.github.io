Do Unions Hire Felons? Understanding Opportunities

Unions generally do not have a blanket policy against hiring felons. Criteria can vary significantly by union and location. Many unions consider the nature of the crime, the time passed since the conviction, and the applicant's overall qualifications. Some unions actively support reintegration efforts, promoting second chances for individuals with criminal records.
