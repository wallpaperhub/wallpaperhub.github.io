What is Human Trafficking? Understanding the Crisis

Human trafficking is a serious crime involving the illegal trade of people for forced labor, sexual exploitation, or other forms of modern slavery. It violates fundamental human rights and affects millions globally. Victims often suffer from abuse and oppression, making it crucial to raise awareness and combat this heinous issue.
