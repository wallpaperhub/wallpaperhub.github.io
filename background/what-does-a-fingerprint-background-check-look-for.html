Understanding Fingerprint Background Checks

A fingerprint background check primarily looks for a person's criminal history by examining their unique fingerprints against databases. It searches for any past felonies, misdemeanors, or outstanding warrants. This type of check can also help verify identity and ensure the individual is not misrepresenting their background.
