Amal Clooney: Human Rights Advocate and Legal Luminary

Amal Clooney is a renowned human rights lawyer, activist, and author. Born in Beirut and raised in London, she has dedicated her career to advocating for justice and equality globally. A graduate of Columbia Law School, Amal has represented high-profile clients and co-founded an NGO to combat human trafficking, making a significant impact worldwide.
