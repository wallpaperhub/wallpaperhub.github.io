Mango: The King of Fruits - History, Varieties & More

Originating from South Asia, the mango is often referred to as the "king of fruits." With over 1,000 varieties, this juicy, sweet stone fruit offers rich flavors and vibrant colors, ranging from green to yellow. Revered in cultures worldwide, it symbolizes love, prosperity, and fertility, while being featured in countless culinary delights.
