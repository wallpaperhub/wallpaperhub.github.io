Celebrate Friendsgiving: A Modern Thanksgiving Twist

Friendsgiving is a modern celebration where friends gather to enjoy a festive meal, typically around the same time as Thanksgiving. Originating in the United States, it emphasizes friendship and community, enabling people to come together, share gratitude, and create lasting memories. It's an inclusive twist on the traditional family gathering.
