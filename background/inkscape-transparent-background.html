How to Set a Transparent Background in Inkscape

Inkscape allows you to create stunning graphics with a transparent background, perfect for web design and digital artwork. To achieve this, simply create or open your design, go to File > Document Properties, and adjust the background settings to transparency. This feature helps layer images seamlessly without any white or colored boxes.
