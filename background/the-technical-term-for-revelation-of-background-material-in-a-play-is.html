Understanding Exposition in Plays: Definition & Importance

The technical term for the revelation of background material in a play is "exposition." Exposition serves to inform the audience about key elements such as characters, setting, and plot context, helping them understand the story's foundation and purpose. It is crucial in establishing the narrative framework and enhancing audience engagement.
