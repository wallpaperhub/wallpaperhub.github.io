When Was Paris Founded? Discover the History!

Paris, the capital of France, traces its origins back to around 250 BC when a Celtic tribe known as the Parisii settled on the banks of the Seine River. This area eventually grew into a thriving urban center. Over the centuries, Paris evolved into a major cultural, political, and economic hub, known today for its rich heritage and iconic landmarks.
