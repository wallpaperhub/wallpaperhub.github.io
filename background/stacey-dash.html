Stacey Dash: Actress, Commentator & Controversy

Stacey Dash is an American actress and political commentator, known for her role in the iconic film "Clueless." Born on January 20, 1967, she gained fame for her beauty and wit. Over the years, she has been a controversial figure in politics, voicing her views on various social issues, often changing the trajectory of her public image.
