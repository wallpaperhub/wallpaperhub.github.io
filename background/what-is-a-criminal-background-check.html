Understanding Criminal Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A criminal background check is a process used to review an individual's criminal history. This check typically includes examining records from law enforcement agencies, court documents, and other databases to assess any past offenses or convictions. It's commonly required for employment, housing, and licensing purposes, ensuring safety and trust.
