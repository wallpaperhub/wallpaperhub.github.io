Brown Noise vs White Noise: Which is Better for You?

Brown noise features deeper tones, akin to a waterfall or thunder, promoting relaxation and focus. In contrast, white noise delivers a consistent, soothing sound across all frequencies, ideal for masking distractions. Both types can aid sleep, concentration, and stress relief, but preferences vary based on individual sensitivities to sound.
