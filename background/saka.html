Discover Saka: The Vibrant Harvest Festival Celebrating Nature

Saka, the traditional harvest festival, celebrates the bounty of nature with vibrant rituals and cultural significance. Originating from agrarian customs, it showcases local communities' connections to the land, featuring colorful processions, traditional dances, and culinary delights. This festival fosters unity, gratitude, and cultural pride.
