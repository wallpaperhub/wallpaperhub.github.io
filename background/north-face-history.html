The North Face: A Legacy of Adventure and Innovation

Founded in 1966 by Douglas Tompkins and Susie Tompkins Buell, The North Face began as a small retail store in San Francisco, specializing in climbing gear and outdoor clothing. Its name represents the coldest, most unforgiving side of a mountain, symbolizing adventure. Over the years, it evolved into a leading brand in outdoor gear, pioneering innovative technologies for explorers and outdoor enthusiasts worldwide.
