Explore the Rich Biodiversity of the Forest Floor

The forest floor is a diverse ecosystem, rich in life and nutrients. It serves as a habitat for countless species, including insects, fungi, and small mammals. Layers of fallen leaves, decomposing plant matter, and natural materials create a nourishing ground, facilitating the growth of young plants and trees, while supporting vital ecological processes.
