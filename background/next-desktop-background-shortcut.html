Next Desktop Background Shortcut: Quick Change Tips

Changing your desktop background can refresh your workspace. To quickly cycle through your backgrounds, use the shortcut: right-click on the desktop, select "Personalize," then "Background." Here, you can choose a slideshow option for automatic changes or select a specific image. Enjoy a fresh view with ease!
