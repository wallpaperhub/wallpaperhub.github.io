Understanding McDonald v. Chicago: Landmark Supreme Court Case

In "McDonald v. Chicago," the Supreme Court addressed the Second Amendment's applicability to state laws, stemming from Chicago's strict gun regulations. Otis McDonald challenged these laws, asserting they infringed on his right to bear arms. The 2010 ruling affirmed that the Second Amendment applies at both state and federal levels.
