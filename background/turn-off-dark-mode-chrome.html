How to Disable Dark Mode in Chrome

To turn off dark mode in Chrome, open the browser settings by clicking the three dots in the top right corner. Navigate to 'Settings', then 'Appearance'. From there, find the 'Theme' section, and select 'Light' or 'System Default' to disable dark mode. Restart Chrome to see changes take effect.
