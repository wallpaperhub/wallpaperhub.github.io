Optimal Image Size for Microsoft Teams Backgrounds

For the best Microsoft Teams background, use an image size of 1920x1080 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio). This resolution ensures your background looks clear and professional during video calls. Avoid cluttered images; instead, choose options that reflect your personality or brand while maintaining a subtle and non-distracting ambiance.
