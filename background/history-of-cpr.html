Essential CPR History: Lifesaving Techniques Through the Ages

Cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) has a rich history dating back to ancient times, with roots in methods to revive the drowned. In the 18th century, more structured techniques emerged, leading to the development of modern CPR in the 1960s. Today, CPR is a standardized life-saving procedure taught worldwide, emphasizing its importance in emergencies.
