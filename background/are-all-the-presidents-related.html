Are All U.S. Presidents Related?

While not all U.S. presidents are directly related, many share distant ancestry due to intermarriages among families over generations. Research has revealed connections among several presidents, showing that political dynasties have influenced American leadership. Exploring these ties unveils intriguing aspects of political history.
