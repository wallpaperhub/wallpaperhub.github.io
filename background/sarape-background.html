"Explore the Vibrant World of Sarapes"

The sarape, a traditional Mexican textile, features vibrant colors and intricate patterns, reflecting rich cultural heritage. Often used as shawls, blankets, or decorative items, these woven fabrics symbolize warmth and comfort. Their striking designs often incorporate regional motifs, making each piece unique and valued in folk art.
