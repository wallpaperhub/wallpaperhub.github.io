Understanding Neutral Gradients: A Design Essential

Neutral gradient refers to the gradual transition between colors or tones that maintain a balance between light and dark. This effect often appears in design, photography, and art, offering a harmonious backdrop that enhances the overall composition. It creates a soft visual flow, blending hues naturally without abrupt changes.
