Ace the EA Exam: Your Path to Becoming an Enrolled Agent

The EA exam, or Enrolled Agent exam, is a key assessment for tax professionals seeking to represent clients before the IRS. It consists of three parts: Individuals, Businesses, and Representation, Practices, and Procedures. Passing the EA exam demonstrates expertise in tax issues and opens doors to new career opportunities in the accounting field.
