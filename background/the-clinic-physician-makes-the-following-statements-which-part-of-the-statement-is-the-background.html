Understanding the Physician’s Statement: Finding the Background

In any medical statement made by a clinic physician, the background provides essential context, such as patient history, previous diagnoses, or treatments. Identifying this background helps in understanding the current health situation and guides any medical decisions or recommendations moving forward.
