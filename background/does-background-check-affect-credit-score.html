Do Background Checks Impact Your Credit Score?

Background checks do not directly affect your credit score. However, when a potential employer or landlord checks your credit history as part of a background check, they may see your credit report, which can influence their decision. It's important to maintain a good credit score to ensure positive outcomes in these checks.
