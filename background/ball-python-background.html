Explore the Fascinating Background of Ball Pythons

Ball pythons, native to West and Central Africa, are popular pet snakes known for their docile nature and striking patterns. They thrive in various environments, often found in grasslands, savannas, and forests. Their ability to curl into a tight ball when threatened adds to their charm. Understanding their background helps in providing proper care.
