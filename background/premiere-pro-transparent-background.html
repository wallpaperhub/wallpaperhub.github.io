How to Create a Transparent Background in Premiere Pro

In Premiere Pro, creating a transparent background is essential for overlaying videos or graphics. Start by selecting the clip you want to edit, go to the Effects Control panel, and apply the "Color Key" or "Ultra Key" effect. Adjust the settings to remove the background and achieve a seamless transparent look, perfect for any project.
