Change Your Discord Background: Step-by-Step Guide

To change your Discord background, go to User Settings by clicking the gear icon at the bottom. Choose "Appearance" from the left menu. Scroll to find the "Themes" section and select a new background option. You can also customize the colors or apply a custom CSS for a more personalized touch. Enjoy a fresh look!
