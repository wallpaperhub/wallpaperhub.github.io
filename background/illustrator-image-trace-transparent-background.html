Mastering Image Trace with Transparent Backgrounds in Illustrator

Illustrator's Image Trace feature allows you to convert raster images into vector art while maintaining transparency in the background. This is ideal for creating clean designs or logos without the hassle of a solid backdrop. Simply select your image, choose the trace options, and adjust settings for the perfect result!
