The Invention of Vapes: A Brief History

Vapes, or electronic cigarettes, were invented in 2003 by Hon Lik, a Chinese pharmacist. He aimed to create a smoking alternative that eliminated the harmful effects of traditional cigarettes. Since then, vapes have evolved significantly, gaining popularity worldwide, especially among young adults and smokers seeking alternatives.
