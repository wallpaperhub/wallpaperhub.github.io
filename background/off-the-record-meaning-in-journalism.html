Understanding 'Off the Record' in Journalism

In journalism, "off the record" refers to information shared by a source that is not intended for publication or attribution. Journalists use this to gain insights without revealing the source's identity or comments publicly. It fosters trust between reporters and sources, allowing deeper discussions on sensitive topics while maintaining confidentiality.
