Understanding Migration Backgrounds: A Comprehensive Guide

Migration background refers to the historical and personal contexts of individuals or groups who have relocated from one place to another. It encompasses factors such as economic opportunities, conflicts, education, and family ties that drive people to migrate. Understanding migration backgrounds helps us appreciate cultural diversity and foster inclusivity.
