Understanding Environmental Science: Key Concepts & Importance

Environmental science is the study of the interactions between the natural environment and human activities. It combines principles from ecology, geology, meteorology, and social sciences to understand environmental issues like climate change, pollution, and biodiversity loss. This interdisciplinary field aims to find solutions for sustainable living.
