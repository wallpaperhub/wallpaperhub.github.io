Discover the Impact of 4-H: Empowering Youth Through Hands-On Learning

4-H is a youth development organization that empowers young people to develop skills in leadership, citizenship, and life through hands-on learning. Originating in the early 20th century, 4-H has grown to encompass various projects, from agriculture to technology, fostering personal growth and community involvement.
