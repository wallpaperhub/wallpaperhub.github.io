Understanding CPG: Key Insights and Trends

Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) encompass everyday products like food, beverages, toiletries, and cleaning items. These goods are essential for daily living and are sold through various retail channels. CPG companies focus on branding and marketing strategies to stand out in a competitive market, impacting consumer choices and driving sales.
