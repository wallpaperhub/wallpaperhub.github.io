The Great Wave: Hokusai's Masterpiece

The Great Wave off Kanagawa is an iconic woodblock print created by Japanese artist Hokusai in the early 19th century. It depicts a massive blue wave threatening boats near Mount Fuji, symbolizing the power of nature and humanity's struggle against it. This masterpiece remains a defining image of Japanese art and culture.
