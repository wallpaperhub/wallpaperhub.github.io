Quickly Switch Foreground and Background Colors in Photoshop

In Photoshop, switching foreground and background colors is simple. Use the "X" key on your keyboard to swap them instantly. Alternatively, click the double-headed arrow icon in the toolbar, or adjust colors in the Color Picker. This feature is vital for adjustments, painting, and adding effects efficiently.
