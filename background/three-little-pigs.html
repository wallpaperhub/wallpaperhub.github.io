The Three Little Pigs: A Timeless Tale of Hard Work

Once upon a time, three little pigs built their own houses. The first pig chose straw, the second wood, and the third sought strength with bricks. When a fierce wolf arrived, he easily blew down the straw and wooden houses. However, the brick house stood firm, teaching them the value of hard work and resilience.
