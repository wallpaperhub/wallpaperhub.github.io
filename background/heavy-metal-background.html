Unleashing the Power: The Heavy Metal Revolution

Heavy metal music, originating in the late 1960s, is characterized by its loud, aggressive sound and complex guitar riffs. Influenced by blues rock and psychedelic rock, it explores themes of rebellion, fantasy, and existential angst. Subgenres like thrash, death, and black metal further diversify the genre, appealing to a global audience of dedicated fans.
