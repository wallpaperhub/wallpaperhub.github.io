Discover the Rich History of Soccer

Soccer, known as football outside North America, dates back over 2,000 years, originating in ancient China, Greece, and Rome. The modern game began in England during the 19th century, leading to the formation of the Football Association in 1863. Soccer has since evolved into a global phenomenon, captivating millions with its rich tradition and competitive spirit.
