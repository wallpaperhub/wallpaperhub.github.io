JB Mauney: The Bull Riding Legend

JB Mauney, an iconic figure in professional bull riding, has captivated audiences with his fearless style and impressive skill. Born on January 9, 1987, in Mooresville, North Carolina, he has become a two-time PBR World Champion. Mauney's determination and passion for the sport have made him a legend, inspiring both fans and aspiring riders.
