Understanding USF: A Brief Background

USF, or the University of South Florida, was founded in 1956. With a rich history and a diverse community, it has grown into a top-tier research university. USF offers a broad range of programs and engages students in innovative research while fostering an inclusive environment that reflects Florida's vibrant culture.
