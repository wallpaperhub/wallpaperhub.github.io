Ideal Facebook Cover Photo Size Explained

To ensure your Facebook cover photo looks great, use the optimal size of 820 pixels wide by 312 pixels tall on desktop. For mobile, it displays at 640 pixels wide by 360 pixels tall. Keep in mind that important elements should be centered to avoid cropping on different devices.
