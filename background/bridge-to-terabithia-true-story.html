True Story Behind Bridge to Terabithia

"Bridge to Terabithia," while a fictional story by Katherine Paterson, was inspired by her experiences growing up and her friendship with a real-life young boy. The themes of friendship, imagination, and loss hold deep emotional resonance. It reflects the journey of coping with loss and cherishing childhood innocence.
