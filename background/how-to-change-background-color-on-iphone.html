Change iPhone Background Color: A Quick Guide

To change the background color on your iPhone, go to Settings > Wallpaper. Choose ‘Choose a New Wallpaper’ and select a solid color or image from your library. After selecting, adjust the image if required, and tap 'Set' to apply it to your Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both. Enjoy your refreshed look!
