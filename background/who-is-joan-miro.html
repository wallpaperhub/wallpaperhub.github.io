Discovering Joan Miró: The Master of Surrealism

Joan Miró was a renowned Spanish painter, sculptor, and ceramicist, born in 1893 in Barcelona. Known for his vibrant use of color and abstract forms, Miró’s work embodies elements of surrealism and expressionism. His iconic pieces often depict whimsical figures and landscapes, captivating art lovers worldwide until his passing in 1983.
