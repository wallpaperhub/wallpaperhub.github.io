Delicious History of Pop-Tarts: A Sweet Evolution

Pop-Tarts were introduced by Kellogg's in 1964, designed as a breakfast pastry that could be eaten on-the-go. Inspired by a method of preserving fruit, the first flavors included strawberry and blueberry. Over the years, Pop-Tarts have evolved, featuring a wide range of flavors, seasonal editions, and even collaborations, solidifying their place in pop culture.
