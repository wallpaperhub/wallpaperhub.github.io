Discover Louisville, KY: A Historic Overview

Louisville, KY, established in 1778, has a rich history influenced by its position on the Ohio River. Known for the Kentucky Derby, it became a vital shipping hub in the 19th century. The city played a significant role during the Civil War and has evolved into a cultural hotspot, known for its diverse communities and vibrant arts scene.
