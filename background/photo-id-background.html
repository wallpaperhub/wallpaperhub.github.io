Perfect Photo ID Backgrounds: Tips & Tricks

When taking a photo for an ID, the background plays a crucial role. A neutral, light-colored backdrop ensures the subject stands out, making identification easier. Avoid patterns or distractions that may detract from the main focus. Proper lighting also enhances clarity, so choose a location with even, soft illumination to achieve the best results.
