Inspiring Facts About Simone Biles: The Gymnast Legend

Simone Biles, an American artistic gymnast, is a four-time Olympic champion known for her incredible talent and groundbreaking skills. Born on March 14, 1997, in Columbus, Ohio, she has won a record 32 medals at World Championships. Biles also advocates for mental health awareness, inspiring many with her courage.
