Does Shoplifting Appear on Background Checks?

Shoplifting can show up on a background check, depending on whether the offense was classified as a misdemeanor or felony and if it resulted in a conviction. Arrest records may also be included in some checks. Employers or organizations may have access to this information, impacting job applications and opportunities.
