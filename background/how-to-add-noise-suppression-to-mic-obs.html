Boost Your Mic Quality: Add Noise Suppression in OBS

To add noise suppression to your mic in OBS, follow these steps: Open OBS and select your audio input source. Click on the gear icon, then choose "Filters." In the Filters window, click the "+" button and select "Noise Suppression." Adjust the suppression level to your liking and click "Close." Test your audio to ensure clarity!
