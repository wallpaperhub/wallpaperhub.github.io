"Mastering the Bodyguard Background in D&D 5e"

In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition (5e), a bodyguard is a character specialized in protecting their allies, typically a noble or a high-profile target. They excel in defensive tactics, often using abilities to shield their charge from harm. This background balances combat prowess with high social skills, making them invaluable in both battles and negotiations.
