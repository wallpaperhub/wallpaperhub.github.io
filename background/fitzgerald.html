Exploring the Genius of F. Scott Fitzgerald

F. Scott Fitzgerald was an influential American author known for his depiction of the Jazz Age in the 1920s. His most famous work, "The Great Gatsby," explores themes of wealth, love, and the American Dream. Fitzgerald's lyrical prose and keen observations make him a timeless literary figure, resonating with readers across generations.
