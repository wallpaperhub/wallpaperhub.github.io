Discover the Unique World of Golf Ball Design

Golf balls are small, spherical objects used in the game of golf, designed for optimal flight and performance. Typically made from a core of rubber or plastic, they feature a dimpled surface that reduces air resistance and enhances control. Understanding their design helps players improve their game and achieve better results on the course.
