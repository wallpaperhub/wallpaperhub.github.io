Do Airports Conduct Background Checks on Passengers?

Airports do not perform background checks on all passengers routinely. However, security measures, including passenger screening through TSA, involve checks against various watchlists. Enhanced scrutiny may occur for individuals deemed suspicious. While common for the travel industry, full background checks are not standard for each traveler.
