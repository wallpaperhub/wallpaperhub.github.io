Fun and Fascinating Facts About Jazz Dance

Jazz dance is an exciting and expressive dance form that originated in the African-American communities of the 20th century. Characterized by its improvisational nature, jazz dance blends elements of ballet, African dance, and popular dance styles. It emphasizes rhythm, style, and energy, often featuring syncopated movements and lively music.
