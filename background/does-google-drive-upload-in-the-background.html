Google Drive: Background Uploads Made Easy

Yes, Google Drive can upload files in the background. When you initiate an upload, it continues seamlessly even if you switch to other applications or lock your device. This feature ensures that your files are being uploaded without requiring your constant attention, making it convenient for users who multitask.
