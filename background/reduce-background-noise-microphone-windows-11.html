How to Reduce Microphone Background Noise in Windows 11

To reduce background noise on your microphone in Windows 11, navigate to 'Settings' > 'System' > 'Sound'. Under the 'Input' section, select your microphone, click on 'Device Properties', and find 'Additional device properties'. In the 'Enhancements' tab, enable 'Noise Suppression' or similar options to improve sound clarity.
