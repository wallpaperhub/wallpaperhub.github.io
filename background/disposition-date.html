Understanding Disposition Date: What You Need to Know

Disposition date refers to the date when a property or asset is officially released or sold, signaling the completion of a transaction. This date is crucial for both buyers and sellers as it marks the transfer of ownership and can have tax implications. Understanding the disposition date helps in planning and managing financial obligations effectively.
