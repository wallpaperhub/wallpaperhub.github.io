Understanding Gun Background Checks: What They Reveal

A gun background check reveals crucial information about a buyer's history. It typically shows criminal records, mental health issues, and previous gun purchases. This process helps ensure that firearms are sold responsibly, preventing access to high-risk individuals and enhancing public safety.
