“Hairspray: The Evolution of a Styling Essential”

Hairspray, a popular hair product, was first developed in the 1960s and quickly became a staple for achieving voluminous, styled hair. Known for its strong hold and flexibility, it revolutionized hairstyling. Hairspray gained cultural significance through music and film, notably the hit musical "Hairspray," celebrating individuality and self-expression.
