Who Wrote the Book of Joel?

The Book of Joel, part of the Minor Prophets in the Old Testament, is traditionally attributed to the prophet Joel himself. Scholars estimate it was written around 835-796 BC, during a time of social and religious upheaval in Judah. Joel's prophetic messages focus on themes of repentance and divine judgment.
