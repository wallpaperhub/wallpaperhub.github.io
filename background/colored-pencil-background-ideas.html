Creative Colored Pencil Backgrounds: Tips & Ideas

Colored pencil backgrounds can transform your artwork with depth and vibrancy. Experiment with gradients by layering colors for a soft transition. Try stippling for texture, or create a wash effect by blending with a solvent. Adding patterns like stripes or dots can enhance creativity. Let nature inspire you for color choices!
