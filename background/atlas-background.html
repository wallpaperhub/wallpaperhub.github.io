Discovering the Atlas: A Look into its Mythical and Cartographic Significance

Atlas, the titan from Greek mythology, is often depicted holding up the heavens or the Earth. His story symbolizes endurance and strength, as he bears the weight of the world. The name 'Atlas' has transcended mythology, becoming synonymous with maps and exploration, with the modern atlas compiling global geographies.
