Effortlessly Remove Backgrounds from Photos

To remove a background from a picture, use online tools or software like Photoshop. Select the background removal feature, and carefully outline the subject. Once selected, delete or mask the background. Save your image in a file format that supports transparency (like PNG) for a clean result. Experiment with edges for a natural look.
