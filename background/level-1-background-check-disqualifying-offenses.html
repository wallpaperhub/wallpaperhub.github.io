Understanding Level 1 Background Check Disqualifications

Level 1 background checks often disqualify individuals based on specific offenses. Common disqualifying issues include felony convictions, particularly violent crimes, sexual offenses, and certain drug-related charges. Misdemeanors can also be flagged, especially those involving dishonesty, theft, or domestic violence. These criteria help ensure safety and trustworthiness in sensitive positions.
