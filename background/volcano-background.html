Understanding Volcanoes: Nature’s Fiery Giants

Volcanoes are geological formations resulting from the eruption of molten rock, ash, and gases from beneath the Earth's crust. They can be classified into various types such as shield, stratovolcano, and cinder cone. Volcanoes play a crucial role in shaping landscapes and influencing ecosystems, but they can also pose significant hazards to nearby communities.
