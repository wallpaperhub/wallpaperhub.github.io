Discovering the Rich History of Los Angeles

Los Angeles, known as the City of Angels, has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1781. Originally established as a Spanish settlement, it evolved through various cultural influences, including Mexican and American. Today, LA is renowned for its entertainment industry, diverse population, and iconic landmarks like Hollywood and the Griffith Observatory.
