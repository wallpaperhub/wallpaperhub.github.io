Background Colors: Enhancing Design Aesthetics

Background colors are essential in design, affecting mood and readability. They can enhance visual appeal and brand identity or convey emotions. When selecting a background color, consider color theory principles, contrast with text, and overall aesthetics. A well-chosen background can significantly impact user experience and engagement.
