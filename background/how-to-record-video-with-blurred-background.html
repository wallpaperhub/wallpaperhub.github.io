Blurred Background Video Recording Tips

To record video with a blurred background, use a camera with a wide aperture to create depth of field, focusing on the subject while softening the background. Alternatively, use video editing software or apps with a portrait mode feature. Ensure good lighting for the subject and experiment with camera settings for best results.
