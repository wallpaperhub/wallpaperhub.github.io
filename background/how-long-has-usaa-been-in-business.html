How Long Has USAA Been in Business? Discover Its History

USAA, or the United Services Automobile Association, has been in business since 1922. It was founded to provide insurance and financial services to military members and their families. Over the decades, USAA has grown to serve millions, expanding its offerings to include banking, investments, and more, all with a focus on the unique needs of its clients.
