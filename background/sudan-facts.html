10 Fascinating Facts About Sudan You Need to Know

Sudan is the third-largest country in Africa, known for its rich history and cultural diversity. It is home to numerous ancient pyramids, more than any other country, and has diverse ecosystems ranging from deserts to wetlands. While it faces challenges like conflict and economic issues, Sudan's heritage and people showcase resilience and strength.
