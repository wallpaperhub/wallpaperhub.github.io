Does ShopRite Conduct Background Checks for Employment?

ShopRite conducts background checks on potential employees as part of their hiring process. These checks typically include criminal history, employment records, and sometimes credit checks, depending on the position. This policy helps ensure a safe shopping environment and a trustworthy workforce.
