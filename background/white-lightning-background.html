Uncovering the Myths of White Lightning: A Brief History

White lightning, often synonymous with moonshine, captures the essence of clandestine distilling. This potent spirit has deep roots in American history, often associated with Prohibition and the Southern Appalachian culture. The term evokes images of backwoods stills and the strong, unaged whiskey that has both fascinated and inspired countless stories.
