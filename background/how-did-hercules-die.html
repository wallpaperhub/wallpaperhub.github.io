How Hercules Met His Tragic End

Hercules, the legendary Greek hero, met his end due to a tragic twist of fate. After being poisoned by a garment tainted with hydra blood, sent by his wife Deianira, he suffered immense pain. To escape, he built his own funeral pyre, and upon his death, he was granted immortality in the afterlife as a hero among gods.
