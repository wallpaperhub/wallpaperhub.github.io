How to Eliminate Microphone Background Static

Static in the background of a microphone can be disruptive, impacting audio quality during recordings or live performances. This noise often stems from electrical interference, poor cables, or inadequate grounding. To minimize static, ensure proper equipment setup, use quality cables, and check for sources of interference.
