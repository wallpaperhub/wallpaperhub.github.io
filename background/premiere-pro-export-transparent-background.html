Exporting Transparent Backgrounds in Premiere Pro

To export a video with a transparent background in Premiere Pro, start by ensuring your sequence is set to an alpha channel format. Go to File > Export > Media, choose QuickTime format, and select Animation or ProRes 4444 codec. Ensure you check "Render at Maximum Depth" and "Include Alpha Channel" before exporting. This captures transparency effectively.
