Understanding Sigmund Freud: Pioneer of Psychoanalysis

Sigmund Freud, the father of psychoanalysis, revolutionized our understanding of the human mind. His theories on the unconscious, defense mechanisms, and dream interpretation laid the groundwork for modern psychology. Freud's ideas about personality structures—id, ego, and superego—continue to influence therapeutic practices today.
