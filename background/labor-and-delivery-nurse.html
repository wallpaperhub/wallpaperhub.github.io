Essential Guide to Labor and Delivery Nursing

Labor and delivery nurses play a vital role in the childbirth process, providing critical support to expectant mothers. They monitor the health of both mother and baby, offer emotional encouragement, and assist with pain management. Skilled in various medical procedures, these nurses ensure safe deliveries, promoting a comforting environment during labor.
