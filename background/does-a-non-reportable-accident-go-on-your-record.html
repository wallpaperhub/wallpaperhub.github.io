Do Non-Reportable Accidents Impact Your Driving Record?

Non-reportable accidents typically do not appear on your driving record, as they usually involve minor damages or injuries that do not exceed a specific monetary threshold. However, it’s essential to check your state's laws, as regulations may vary. Always consult your insurance provider for clarity regarding your personal situation.
