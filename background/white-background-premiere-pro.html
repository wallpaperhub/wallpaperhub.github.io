How to Create a White Background in Premiere Pro

Creating a white background in Premiere Pro can enhance your video projects. Start by using the "Color Matte" feature to add a solid white layer. This technique offers a clean and bright backdrop, perfect for showcasing graphics or text. Customize opacity and blend settings for a polished look, ensuring your content stands out effectively.
