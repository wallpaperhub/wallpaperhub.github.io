Understanding Albert Bandura: The Pioneer of Social Learning Theory

Albert Bandura was a renowned psychologist known for his contributions to social learning theory and self-efficacy. His famous Bobo doll experiment demonstrated that children learn behaviors through observation and imitation. Bandura's work emphasized the role of social influences on behavior, shaping educational and psychological practices.
