New Texas Law: Background Checks Explained

Texas has introduced a new law mandating enhanced background checks for certain occupations and firearm purchases. This legislation aims to promote public safety and prevent individuals with criminal histories from accessing sensitive positions or weapons. Businesses must comply, ensuring thorough vetting processes to protect communities.
