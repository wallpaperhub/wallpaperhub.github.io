Who Owns Dave & Buster's? A Quick Insight

Dave & Buster's is a publicly traded company, which means it is owned by its shareholders. However, the founding visionaries, Dave Corriveau and Buster Corley, were instrumental in establishing the brand. Today, the company's operations are overseen by an executive team that focuses on enhancing the guest experience at their entertainment venues.
