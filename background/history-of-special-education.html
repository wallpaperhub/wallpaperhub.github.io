The Evolution of Special Education: A Brief History

The history of special education dates back to the 18th century, with pioneers like Jean-Marc Itard advocating for the education of children with disabilities. Progress continued in the 20th century, leading to formal legislation. The 1975 Education for All Handicapped Children Act marked a significant milestone, ensuring access to education for all.
