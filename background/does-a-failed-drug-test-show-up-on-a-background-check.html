Does a Failed Drug Test Appear on Background Checks?

A failed drug test typically does not appear on a standard background check. Employers usually only see felony or misdemeanor convictions, credit history, and employment records. However, if a company specifically conducts drug screening as part of their hiring process, the results may influence hiring decisions. Always check local laws regarding privacy and employment.
