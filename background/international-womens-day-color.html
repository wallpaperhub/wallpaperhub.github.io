Celebrate International Women's Day: The Meaning of Colors

International Women's Day, celebrated on March 8, is symbolically represented by the color purple. This color signifies justice, dignity, and the fight for gender equality. Beyond purple, green and white are also associated with the movement, reflecting hope and purity. Wearing these colors honors women's achievements and advocates for their rights worldwide.
