Understanding the Grimace: Expression and Emotion

Grimace is a facial expression often associated with discomfort, disapproval, or distress. It can indicate various emotions, from pain to disgust. This physical reaction often serves as a non-verbal communication tool, revealing feelings that words may not convey. Understanding grimaces in social interactions can improve empathy and connection.
