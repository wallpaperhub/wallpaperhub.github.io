Exploring the Background of "Mean Girls"

“Mean Girls” is a teen comedy film released in 2004, written by Tina Fey and based on the non-fiction book "Queen Bees and Wannabes" by Rosalind Wiseman. Set in a high school, it explores the social dynamics and cliques among adolescents. The film became a cultural phenomenon, highlighting themes of friendship, rivalry, and self-identity.
