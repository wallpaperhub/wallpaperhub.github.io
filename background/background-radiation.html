Understanding Background Radiation: Sources & Effects

Background radiation is the omnipresent ionizing radiation originating from various natural and artificial sources. It includes cosmic rays from outer space, radioactive materials in the earth, and even human activities such as nuclear testing. Understanding background radiation is crucial for assessing exposure risks and ensuring safety.
