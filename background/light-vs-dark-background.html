Light vs Dark Background: Which is Better?

Choosing between light and dark backgrounds can significantly impact design and user experience. Light backgrounds often enhance readability and provide a clean, minimal look, while dark backgrounds can reduce eye strain and create a modern aesthetic. Consider your audience and context when making the choice for optimal engagement.
