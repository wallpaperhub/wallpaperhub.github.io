Essential Passport Photo Requirements: A Quick Guide

When taking a passport photo, ensure that it's in color and measures 2x2 inches. The background should be plain white or off-white. You must face the camera directly with a neutral expression, keeping both eyes open. Glasses are allowed but should not create glare. Head coverings for religious purposes are permitted, but facial features must be visible.
