Cowboy Hat History: The Iconic Western Accessory

Cowboy hats have a rich history rooted in the American West, symbolizing ranching and frontier life. Originally designed for practicality, their wide brims protect against sun and rain. Today, cowboy hats are not only functional but also stylish, representing a unique cultural heritage while remaining popular in fashion and music.
