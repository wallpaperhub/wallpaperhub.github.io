Understanding the Symbolism of Fasting

Fasting symbols vary across cultures, often representing self-discipline and spiritual growth. Commonly associated with various religions, such as Ramadan in Islam, these symbols highlight the importance of restraint, reflection, and community. Fasting acts as a reminder of the strength found in sacrifice, fueling personal transformation.
