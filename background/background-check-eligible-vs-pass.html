Background Check Eligibility vs Passing: What You Need to Know

When hiring, understanding the difference between "background check eligible" and "pass" is crucial. Being eligible means your information will be reviewed, whereas passing indicates no disqualifying issues were found. Employers rely on these distinctions to make informed decisions, ensuring a safe and trustworthy workplace.
