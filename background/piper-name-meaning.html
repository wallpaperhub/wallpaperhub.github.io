Piper Name Meaning: Discover the Origin & Significance

The name "Piper" has roots in Old English, meaning "flute player" or "pipe player." Traditionally associated with musical talent and artistry, Piper has gained popularity as a modern given name, symbolizing creativity and free spirit. It evokes images of harmony and playful melodies, making it a charming choice for a child.
