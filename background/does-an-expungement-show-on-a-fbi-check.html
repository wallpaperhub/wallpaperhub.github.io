Does Expungement Show on FBI Background Checks?

Expungement generally removes a conviction from public records, meaning it should not show up on an FBI background check. However, certain exceptions exist, and the FBI may still have access to sealed records. It's essential to confirm your expungement status and understand its implications on background checks for employment or other purposes.
