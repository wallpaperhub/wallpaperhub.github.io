Will Employment Background Checks Uncover Undisclosed Jobs?

An employment background check typically reveals previous jobs through verification with employers, but it may not uncover all undisclosed positions. Many checks focus on official records, and if a job was not disclosed, it may not appear unless the employer is contacted directly. Honesty in applications is essential for trust.
