Essential Guide to the TWIC Card

A TWIC card (Transportation Worker Identification Credential) is essential for anyone working in secure maritime environments. It serves as a government-issued ID, validating the holder's identity and allowing access to restricted port areas. Obtaining a TWIC card requires background checks and fingerprinting for security compliance.
