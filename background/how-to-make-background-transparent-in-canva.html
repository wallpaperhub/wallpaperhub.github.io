How to Make Background Transparent in Canva

To make the background transparent in Canva, first, select the image or element you want to edit. Click on the ‘Background’ tab, and then select the ‘Transparency’ slider. Adjust the slider to your desired opacity level until the background is fully transparent. Finally, download your design as a PNG for best results!
