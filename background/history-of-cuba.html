Discover the Intriguing History of Cuba: From Colonization to Revolution

Cuba's history is rich and complex, marked by indigenous cultures, Spanish colonization, and a fight for independence in the 19th century. The 20th century saw the rise of communism under Fidel Castro, leading to significant social change and economic challenges. The US embargo and recent reforms continue to shape Cuba's unique identity.
