Discover the Native Origins of Olive Oil

Olive oil is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly in countries like Greece, Italy, and Spain. The cultivation of olive trees dates back thousands of years in these areas, where the warm climate and suitable soil conditions contribute to the growth of high-quality olives. This heritage significantly influences Mediterranean cuisine today.
