Understanding Atoms: The Building Blocks of Matter

Atoms are the fundamental building blocks of matter, consisting of a nucleus of protons and neutrons surrounded by a cloud of electrons. They are incredibly small, with sizes measured in picometers. Understanding atomic structure is crucial in fields like chemistry, physics, and materials science, forming the basis for all chemical reactions and interactions.
