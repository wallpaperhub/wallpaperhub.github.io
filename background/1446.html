Key Events of 1446: Gutenberg's Printing Revolution

In 1446, a year marked by significant events, Johannes Gutenberg introduced the printing press in Europe, revolutionizing the spread of knowledge and culture. This innovation laid the groundwork for the Renaissance and the Reformation, reshaping society and enhancing literacy. The year also saw ongoing conflicts, particularly in Europe and Asia.
