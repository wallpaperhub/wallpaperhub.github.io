History and Evolution of Pie: A Delicious Journey

Pie has a rich history that dates back to ancient times, with origins in Greece and Rome, where it was originally made with meat. Over centuries, pie evolved into a popular dessert, featuring sweet fillings like fruit and custard. Today, pies are beloved worldwide, celebrated for their versatility and comforting flavors.
