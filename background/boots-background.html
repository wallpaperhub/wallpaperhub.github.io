Stylish Boots: History, Types, and Trends

Boots have a rich history, originating as protective footwear for military and labor use. Over centuries, they evolved into a fashion staple, reflecting individual style and culture. From rugged work boots to chic ankle and knee-high styles, boots offer versatility, durability, and comfort, making them essential for various occasions.
