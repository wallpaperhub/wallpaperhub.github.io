Do Dispensaries Share Information with the Government?

Dispensaries must comply with local and state regulations, which may involve sharing certain information with the government. This typically includes sales data, inventory levels, and customer details for tax purposes and compliance checks. However, patient privacy is protected under various laws, limiting the amount of personal information shared.
