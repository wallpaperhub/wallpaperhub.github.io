Fixing Black Background Issues on iPhone

A black background on your iPhone could be due to several reasons. Your device may be in Dark Mode, a feature designed to reduce eye strain. Alternatively, it could be a result of a wallpaper setting or accessibility options. If it persists, try restarting your phone or checking for software updates to fix any glitches.
