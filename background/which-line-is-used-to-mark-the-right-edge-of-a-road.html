Right Edge Road Markings: Understanding the Solid White Line

To mark the right edge of a road, a solid white line is typically used. This line serves as a boundary for vehicles, helping drivers maintain safe positioning and lane discipline. It indicates that changing lanes is discouraged in that area, enhancing overall road safety for all users.
