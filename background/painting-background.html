Mastering Painting Backgrounds: Tips & Techniques

When painting backgrounds, consider color harmony, texture, and layering techniques. Start with a base color, gradually adding depth with shades and highlights. Incorporating elements like gradients or patterns can enhance the visual impact. Experimentation is key; let your creativity flow to bring dynamic backgrounds to life.
