Understanding Momentum: A Key Concept in Physics

Momentum is the product of mass and velocity, representing the quantity of motion an object possesses. In physics, it's a key concept for understanding how objects move and interact. When a force is applied, it changes an object's momentum, influencing its speed and direction. This principle is vital in various fields, including engineering and sports.
