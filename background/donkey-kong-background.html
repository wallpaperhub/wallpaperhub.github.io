Exploring the Classic Donkey Kong Background

Donkey Kong, introduced by Nintendo in 1981, quickly became a classic, revolutionizing arcade gaming. The game features a giant ape who abducts a damsel in distress, with players controlling Jumpman (later known as Mario) to save her. Due to its innovative gameplay and iconic characters, Donkey Kong laid the groundwork for future platformers.
