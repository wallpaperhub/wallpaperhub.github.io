How Employers Verify Your Education: The Complete Guide

Employers verify education by contacting schools or universities directly to confirm degrees, diplomas, and attendance dates. They may utilize third-party verification services that specialize in background checks. Additionally, employers may require transcripts or other documentation to ensure candidates meet educational qualifications for the job.
