Understanding Atmospheric Background: A Key to Environmental Awareness

Atmospheric background refers to the natural levels of gases and particles present in the atmosphere, influencing air quality and climate. It serves as a baseline for environmental monitoring, aiding in determining the impact of pollution and other human activities. Understanding atmospheric background is essential for effective climate change strategies.
