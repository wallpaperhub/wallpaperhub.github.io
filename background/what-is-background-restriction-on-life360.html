Understanding Life360's Background Restriction Feature

Background restriction on Life360 is a feature that limits the app's functionality to the foreground, meaning users may not receive real-time updates or alerts when the app is not actively open. This can affect location sharing and notifications for safety but helps save battery life. Users can adjust this setting for optimal performance.
