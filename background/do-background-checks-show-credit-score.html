Do Background Checks Include Your Credit Score?

Background checks typically do not show a person's credit score. Instead, they focus on criminal history, employment verification, and education. However, some landlords or employers may obtain a credit report as part of the screening process, which can include credit score information if permissible. Always check local laws regarding this practice.
