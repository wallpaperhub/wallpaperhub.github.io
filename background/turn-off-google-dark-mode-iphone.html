How to Disable Google Dark Mode on iPhone

To turn off Google Dark Mode on your iPhone, open the Google app and tap on your profile picture in the top right corner. Select "Settings," then find "Theme." Here, choose "Light" to disable Dark Mode. You can also adjust your device's general appearance in the iPhone's settings under "Display & Brightness."
