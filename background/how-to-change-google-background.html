How to Change Your Google Background: A Quick Guide

To change your Google background, open your Chrome browser and go to the Google homepage. Click on the "Customize" button at the bottom right corner. Select "Background" and choose an image from the library, or upload your own. Once you've made your selection, click "Done" to save your new background. Enjoy your personalized Google experience!
