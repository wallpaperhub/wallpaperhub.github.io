Chrysanthemum: A Bloom of Joy and Color

Chrysanthemums, often called "mums," are beloved flowers known for their vibrant colors and diverse forms. Native to Asia and northeastern Europe, they symbolize joy and optimism. These hardy blooms thrive in various climates, making them popular in gardens worldwide. With a rich cultural history, chrysanthemums hold significance in celebrations like the Japanese festival of Kiku.
