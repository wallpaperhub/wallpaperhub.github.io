Customize Your Outlook: Understanding Office Backgrounds

Office background in Outlook refers to the visual theme that users can apply to enhance their email and calendar interface. It allows for personalization, making the workspace more inviting and comfortable. Users can choose from various pre-set backgrounds or upload their own images, creating a distinctive look that reflects their style or company branding.
