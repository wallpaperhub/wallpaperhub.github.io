Discovering the Evolution of Chromatography Techniques

Chromatography, developed in the early 20th century by Mikhail Tsvet, revolutionized the separation of mixtures. Initially used for plant pigments, it evolved into various forms like gas, liquid, and thin-layer chromatography. This technique is now essential in chemistry, biology, and pharmaceuticals for analyzing and purifying complex substances.
