Optimize Apple Watch Background App Refresh

Background App Refresh on your Apple Watch allows apps to update in the background, ensuring you receive the latest information without delay. This feature enhances functionality, but it can impact battery life. To manage it, access the Watch app on your iPhone, navigate to General, and toggle Background App Refresh on or off as needed.
