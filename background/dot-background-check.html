"Essential Guide to DOT Background Checks"

A DOT background check is essential for employers in the transportation industry, ensuring compliance with federal regulations. It verifies an individual's driving history, criminal records, and safety performance. This process helps maintain safe workplaces and protect public safety by screening potential employees before hiring.
