Understanding Machining: Techniques and Innovations

Machining is a vital manufacturing process that involves the removal of material from a solid workpiece to shape it into desired geometries. Historically rooted in precision engineering, it encompasses various techniques like milling, turning, and grinding. As technology advances, automation and CNC (Computer Numerical Control) have revolutionized the field, enhancing accuracy and efficiency.
