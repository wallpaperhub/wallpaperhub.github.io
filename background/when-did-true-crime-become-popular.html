True Crime: The Rise of a Genre

True crime gained massive popularity in the 1990s with the rise of shows like "America's Most Wanted" and books by authors like Ann Rule. However, its roots trace back to earlier decades. The genre exploded in the 2010s with podcasts, documentaries, and series exploring real cases, captivating audiences and sparking discussions on societal issues.
