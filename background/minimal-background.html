Embrace Minimalism: The Power of Simple Backgrounds

Minimal background refers to a design approach that emphasizes simplicity and clarity. By using clean lines, ample white space, and muted colors, minimal backgrounds help focus attention on the main content. This style enhances readability and creates a modern, sophisticated look, making it a popular choice for websites and presentations.
