How to Easily Create a White Background in Photoshop

To create a white background in Photoshop, first, open your image. Use the 'Magic Wand' tool to select the background. Adjust the tolerance as needed to ensure a clear selection. Press 'Delete' to remove the selected area. To fill, choose 'Edit,' then 'Fill,' and select 'White.' Finally, save your image to keep the changes.
