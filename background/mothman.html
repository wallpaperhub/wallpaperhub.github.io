Discovering Mothman: Lore and Legends Unveiled

Mothman, a legendary creature first spotted in Point Pleasant, West Virginia, has captivated imaginations since the 1960s. Described as a large, humanoid figure with glowing red eyes and massive wings, sightings often coincide with disasters. Many view Mothman as an omen, adding to its mystique and drawing paranormal enthusiasts to explore its legacy.
