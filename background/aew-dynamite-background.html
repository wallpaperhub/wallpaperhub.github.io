AEW Dynamite: The Revolution in Wrestling

AEW Dynamite is a weekly professional wrestling television show produced by All Elite Wrestling (AEW). Premiering on October 2, 2019, it quickly gained popularity for its engaging storylines, high-quality matches, and diverse roster. The show showcases a blend of seasoned veterans and newcomers, emphasizing competition and athleticism.
