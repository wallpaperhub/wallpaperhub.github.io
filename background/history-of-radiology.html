Unlocking the Past: A Brief History of Radiology

Radiology, a pivotal branch of medicine, began in 1895 with Wilhelm Conrad Roentgen's discovery of X-rays. This breakthrough revolutionized diagnostics, allowing for non-invasive visualization of internal structures. Over the decades, advancements like CT and MRI evolved, improving diagnostic accuracy and treatment planning, and establishing radiology as a cornerstone of modern healthcare.
