The Flintstones: A Prehistoric Comedy Classic

The Flintstones, created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera, debuted in 1960 as the first prime-time animated television series. Set in the prehistoric town of Bedrock, it follows the lives of the Flintstone family and their neighbors, the Rubbles. The show humorously portrays modern-day life with a Stone Age twist, focusing on their daily lives, adventures, and friendship.
