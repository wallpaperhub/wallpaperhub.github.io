Discover Beautiful Pan de Muerto PNG Images

Pan de muerto is a traditional Mexican bread associated with the Day of the Dead celebrations. Its unique shape and sweet flavor make it a beloved treat during this time. Featured often in altars, this bread, adorned with bone-like shapes, symbolizes the cycle of life and death.
