Understanding AHCA Background Screening Disqualifying Offenses

AHCA background screening disqualifying offenses include a range of serious crimes that can prevent individuals from working in healthcare settings. Offenses such as felony convictions for abuse, neglect, fraud, and certain types of misdemeanors may lead to disqualification. Understanding these offenses is essential for compliance and employment in the healthcare sector.
