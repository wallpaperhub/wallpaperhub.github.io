Discover the Delicious History of Bagels

Bagels have a rich history that dates back to the 17th century in Poland, where they were created by Jewish communities. Initially known as "bajgiel," the dough is boiled before baking, giving bagels their distinctive chewy texture. Today, bagels come in various flavors and styles, symbolizing cultural diversity and delectable breakfast options worldwide.
