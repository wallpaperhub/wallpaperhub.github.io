Vanessa Hudgens: From High School Sweetheart to Hollywood Star

Vanessa Hudgens is an American actress and singer, famously known for her role as Gabriella Montez in the "High School Musical" series. Born on December 14, 1988, in Salinas, California, she began her career in theater before making her mark in film and television. Beyond acting, she pursues a music career, showcasing her versatile talent.
