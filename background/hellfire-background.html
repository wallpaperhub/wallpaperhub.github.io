Understanding Hellfire: Origins & Symbolism

The concept of hellfire is rooted in various religious texts, symbolizing eternal punishment for the wicked. Often depicted as an inferno, it serves as a deterrent against immoral behavior. This imagery can be found in numerous cultural references, illustrating humanity's fear of repercussions in the afterlife and the moral boundaries we navigate.
