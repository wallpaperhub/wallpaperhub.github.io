Tedy Bruschi: NFL Legend & Stroke Survivor

Tedy Bruschi, a former NFL linebacker, is renowned for his incredible career with the New England Patriots. Known for his fierce playing style, he was a key component in the team's three Super Bowl victories. After retiring, Bruschi became an advocate for stroke awareness, inspiring many with his story of resilience and recovery.
