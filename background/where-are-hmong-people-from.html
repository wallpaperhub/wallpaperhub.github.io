Discovering the Origins of Hmong People

Hmong people originated from the mountainous regions of southern China, Laos, Vietnam, and Thailand. They are an ethnic group with a rich cultural heritage and history. Over time, many Hmong have migrated to various countries, including the United States, seeking better opportunities and escaping conflict.
