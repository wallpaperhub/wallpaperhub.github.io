Top Facts About Rick Riordan: The Master of Modern Mythology

Rick Riordan is a celebrated American author known for popularizing mythology among young readers. He is best known for the "Percy Jackson & the Olympians" series, which spins modern tales from Greek mythology. Born on June 5, 1964, Riordan also writes the "Heroes of Olympus" series and other mythological retellings. His engaging storytelling has inspired millions!
