Dolly Parton: Country Music Legend and Philanthropist

Dolly Parton is a legendary country music icon, known for her captivating voice and songwriting talent. With hits like "Jolene" and "9 to 5," she has influenced generations. Beyond music, she's an actress, businesswoman, and philanthropist, notably founding the Imagination Library, promoting literacy among children.
