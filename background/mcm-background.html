Understanding the MCM Background: Craftsmanship & Influence

MCM (Modern Craftsmanship Movement) emphasizes unique artistry, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design. It champions handmade items that reflect individual expression and sustainable practices. Originating in the late 20th century, MCM aims to revive the appreciation for craftsmanship in a digital world, celebrating the beauty of imperfection and creativity.
