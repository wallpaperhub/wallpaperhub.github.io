Understanding Forest Fires: Causes & Impacts

Forest fires are natural or human-caused events that rapidly consume vegetation, threatening ecosystems, wildlife, and human settlements. Factors like drought, high temperatures, and lightning often ignite these blazes, which can spread quickly. Understanding their causes and impacts is crucial for prevention and management strategies.
