Exploring Marine Biology: Understanding Ocean Life

Marine biology is the study of ocean ecosystems, focusing on the life forms inhabiting them, from microscopic phytoplankton to the largest whales. It encompasses research on marine organisms, their behaviors, interactions, and habitats, providing insights into biodiversity and the health of our oceans, essential for conservation efforts.
