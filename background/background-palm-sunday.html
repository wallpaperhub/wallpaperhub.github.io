Understanding Palm Sunday: Significance and Traditions

Palm Sunday marks the beginning of Holy Week in Christian tradition, commemorating Jesus’ triumphant entry into Jerusalem. Held on the Sunday before Easter, it symbolizes the fulfillment of prophecy as crowds welcomed Him with palm branches, celebrating His arrival as the Messiah. This day serves as a reminder of both joyous celebration and impending sacrifice.
