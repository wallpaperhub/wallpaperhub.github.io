"Understanding the Meaning of the White Question Mark"

The white question mark, often used in design and web interfaces, symbolizes inquiry and information. Its minimalist aesthetic draws attention without overwhelming, making it ideal for user interaction cues. Whether in apps or graphic design, the white question mark prompts users to seek clarification or additional details, enhancing overall user experience.
