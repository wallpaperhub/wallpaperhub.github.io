Discover Radiohead: Iconic Sound & Impact

Radiohead, the British alternative rock band formed in 1985, is known for its experimental sound and thought-provoking lyrics. With albums like "OK Computer" and "Kid A," they have redefined modern music, exploring themes of alienation and technology. Their influence extends across genres, inspiring countless artists globally.
