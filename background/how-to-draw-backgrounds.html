Mastering Background Drawing: Tips & Techniques

To draw captivating backgrounds, start by selecting a focal point to lead the viewer's eye. Use perspective techniques to create depth, incorporating elements like trees, mountains, or buildings in layers. Experiment with colors to set the mood and use light and shadow for realism. Practice different styles and keep your compositions balanced.
