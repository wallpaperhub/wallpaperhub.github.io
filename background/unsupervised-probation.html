Understanding Unsupervised Probation: A Guide

Unsupervised probation allows offenders to serve their sentences while not under direct supervision, provided they adhere to specific conditions. This alternative aims to reduce incarceration rates, promote rehabilitation, and ease the burden on correctional facilities. It grants individuals more freedom while emphasizing responsibility.
