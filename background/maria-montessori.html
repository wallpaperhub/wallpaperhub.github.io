Discovering Maria Montessori: Pioneer of Child-Centered Education

Maria Montessori was an Italian physician and educator known for developing the Montessori method of education, which emphasizes hands-on learning, independence, and respect for a child's natural development. Her innovative approach transformed early childhood education and continues to influence teaching practices globally.
