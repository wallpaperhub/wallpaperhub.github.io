Customize Your Roku Background in Minutes!

To change the Roku background, navigate to the main menu and select "Settings." From there, choose "Themes" and then "My Themes" to explore your options. You can select a different theme or download new ones from the Roku Store. Just follow the on-screen instructions to set your preferred background. Enjoy customizing your Roku!
