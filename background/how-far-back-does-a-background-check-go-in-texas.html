Understanding Background Check Duration in Texas

In Texas, a typical background check can reveal information for up to seven years, depending on the type of check and specific circumstances. Criminal records can be accessed indefinitely, while civil cases may have a different time frame. Employment, credit, and driving history also play a role in how far back checks may go.
