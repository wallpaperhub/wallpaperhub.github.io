Cillian Murphy: Versatile Actor & Star of 'Peaky Blinders'

Cillian Murphy is an acclaimed Irish actor known for his versatility and compelling performances. Rising to fame with roles in films like "28 Days Later" and "Inception," Murphy has captivated audiences with his portrayal of complex characters. Notably, his role as Thomas Shelby in the hit series "Peaky Blinders" solidified his status as a leading man in Hollywood.
