"Understanding CPS Checks: Ensuring Child Safety"

A CPS check, or Child Protective Services check, is a crucial background investigation assessing an individual’s history related to child abuse or neglect. Used primarily for childcare employment, adoption, and foster care placements, it ensures a safe environment for children. Understanding the CPS check process is key to safeguarding vulnerable populations.
