Captivating Memento Mori Images: Life & Death in Art

Memento mori images serve as poignant reminders of mortality, encouraging reflection on life’s transience. These evocative visuals often feature skulls, withering flowers, and hourglasses, blending artistry with philosophical depth. They inspire viewers to cherish each moment and consider their own existence while appreciating the beauty of life.
