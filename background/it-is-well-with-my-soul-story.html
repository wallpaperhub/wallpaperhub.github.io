It Is Well With My Soul: The Heartfelt Story Behind the Hymn

The story behind "It Is Well With My Soul" is deeply inspiring. Written by Horatio Spafford in the late 19th century, it emerged from profound personal tragedy, including the loss of his children. Despite his heartbreak, Spafford found solace in faith, creating a hymn that resonates with many for its themes of peace and hope amidst sorrow.
