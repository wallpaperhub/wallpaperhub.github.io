Does a Background Check Mean You've Got the Job?

A background check is often a standard part of the hiring process, but it doesn't automatically mean you've secured the job. It typically signifies that the employer is seriously considering you as a candidate. It's important to remain patient during this phase, as references and checks can take time to complete.
