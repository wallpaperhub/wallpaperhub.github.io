How to Effectively Blur Backgrounds in Photos

To blur the background in pictures, use photo editing software or apps like Photoshop, Snapseed, or Lightroom. Select the subject using selection tools, then apply a blur effect to the background. Adjust blur intensity for a natural look. This technique enhances focus on the subject and adds depth to your images.
