Exploring the Rich History of Bread

Bread has a rich history that dates back thousands of years, originally emerging from ancient civilizations like the Egyptians. Initially made from simple grains and water, bread quickly evolved through various cultures, incorporating diverse ingredients and techniques. Its significance spans not only sustenance but also rituals and traditions globally.
