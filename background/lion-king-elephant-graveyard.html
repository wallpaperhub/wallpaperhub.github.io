The Elephant Graveyard: A Forbidden Realm in The Lion King

In "The Lion King," the Elephant Graveyard is a haunting, desolate place filled with the remains of long-gone giants. It serves as a forbidden area where young Simba and Nala venture, lured by curiosity. This eerie location symbolizes the darker aspects of the circle of life and showcases the importance of respecting boundaries in the animal kingdom.
