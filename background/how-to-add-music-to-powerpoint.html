Add Music to PowerPoint: A Quick Guide

To add music to a PowerPoint presentation, first, open your slide where you want the music. Click on the "Insert" tab, then select "Audio" and choose "Audio on My PC." Browse for your music file and click "Insert." You can adjust playback settings in the "Playback" tab, allowing the music to play across slides or during specific timings.
