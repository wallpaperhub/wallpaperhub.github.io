Discover Gilligan's Island: Classic TV Fun!

"Gilligan's Island" is a classic American TV show that aired from 1964 to 1967. It follows the misadventures of seven castaways stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. The comedic mix of personalities, including the bumbling Gilligan, the wealthy couple, and the professor, created timeless scenarios filled with humor and heart.
