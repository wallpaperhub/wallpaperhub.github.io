Understanding Free Fall: The Basics of Gravity

Free fall occurs when an object is subjected only to the forces of gravity, experiencing acceleration due to gravity alone. It’s a critical concept in physics and is observable in various phenomena, such as skydiving and the acceleration of falling objects. Free fall helps illustrate the laws of motion and gravitational forces.
