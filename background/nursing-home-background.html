Understanding Nursing Home Care: A Comprehensive Overview

Nursing homes, also known as skilled nursing facilities, provide a supportive environment for elderly individuals who require assistance with daily living activities or medical care. These institutions are designed to ensure safety, health, and social engagement among residents. With trained staff, nursing homes focus on enhancing the quality of life for seniors, addressing their unique needs.
