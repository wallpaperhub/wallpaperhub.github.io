Gingerbread has a rich history, dating back to ancient civilizations where sweet baked goods were enjoyed in various forms. Traditionally made with spices like ginger, cinnamon, and cloves, these festive treats evoke warmth and nostalgia during the holiday season. Their intricate designs and aromas make gingerbread a cherished symbol of celebration and joy.
