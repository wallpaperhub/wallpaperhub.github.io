Understanding 1 Peter: A Guide to Faith and Perseverance

1 Peter is a New Testament epistle addressing early Christians facing persecution. It emphasizes hope, suffering, and the importance of faith in Jesus. The letter encourages believers to live righteously, to remain steadfast in trials, and to embody love and humility in their relationships, reflecting Christ’s character amid adversity.
