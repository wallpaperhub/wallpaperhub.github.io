Mastering SwiftUI: Create Stunning Blur Backgrounds

SwiftUI allows you to create stunning user interfaces with ease, and incorporating a blur background is a powerful design technique. By using the `.blur(radius:)` modifier, you can seamlessly enhance your views, drawing attention to foreground elements while creating a soft, ethereal background. This adds depth and focus, elevating your app's aesthetic appeal.
