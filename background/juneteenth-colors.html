Meaningful Colors of Juneteenth: Celebrate Freedom

Juneteenth colors, primarily red, black, and green, symbolize freedom, resilience, and cultural pride. The red represents the struggles and sacrifices made for freedom, black honors the African heritage and identity, while green signifies hope and prosperity. Together, these colors reflect the spirit of unity and celebration during Juneteenth.
