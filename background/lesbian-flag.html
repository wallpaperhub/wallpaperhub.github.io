Understanding the Lesbian Flag: A Symbol of Pride

The lesbian flag, a vibrant symbol of pride, features shades of pink, purple, and white. The darker pink at the top represents femininity, while the lighter pink symbolizes love and community. The purple stripe stands for diversity. Celebrating LGBTQ+ identity, the flag is a powerful reminder of visibility and acceptance.
