Do Restraining Orders Appear on Background Checks?

A restraining order can indeed show up on a background check, especially if it has led to legal actions, such as a criminal case. Civil restraining orders might not always appear, but many agencies include them in their records. Thus, it's important to understand how your legal history may influence employment or other opportunities.
