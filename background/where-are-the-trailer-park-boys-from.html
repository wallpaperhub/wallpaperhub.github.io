Where Are the Trailer Park Boys From?

Trailer Park Boys is set in the fictional Sunnyvale Trailer Park in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia, Canada. This mockumentary series follows the misadventures of Ricky, Julian, and Bubbles as they navigate a life of crime and chaos among an eccentric cast of characters. The show's unique charm highlights the humor found in everyday struggles.
