Optimal Twitter Banner Size Explained

When designing a Twitter banner, it's crucial to follow the ideal dimensions to ensure optimal display. The recommended size is 1500 x 500 pixels. This format ensures your banner looks sharp and professional across devices. Keep crucial elements within the safe zone (around 1260 x 408 pixels) to avoid being cropped on mobile views.
