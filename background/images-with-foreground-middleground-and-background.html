Understanding Foreground, Middleground, and Background in Images

Images can be categorized into three layers: foreground, middleground, and background. The foreground includes elements closest to the viewer, providing depth and context. The middleground serves as a transition, while the background sets the scene's tone. Understanding these layers enhances composition, creating more engaging visuals.
