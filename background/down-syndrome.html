Understanding Down Syndrome: A Brief Guide

Down syndrome is a genetic condition caused by the presence of an extra chromosome 21, leading to developmental challenges and cognitive impairments. Children and adults with Down syndrome can lead fulfilling lives with early intervention, inclusive education, and support. Awareness and understanding are essential to promote acceptance and integration within communities.
