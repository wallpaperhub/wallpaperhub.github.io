Discovering Theodore Roosevelt: America's Dynamic President

The 26th President of the United States, Theodore Roosevelt, served from 1901 to 1909. A key figure in the Progressive Era, he championed reforms, including trust-busting and consumer protection. Known for his vigorous personality, he also expanded national parks and fought for workers' rights, leaving a lasting legacy on American society.
