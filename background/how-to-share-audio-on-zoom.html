How to Share Audio on Zoom: A Quick Guide

To share audio on Zoom, start your meeting and click on the "Share Screen" button. Choose the screen or application you want to share. Before clicking "Share," check the box for "Share computer sound" at the bottom left. This allows participants to hear any audio from your device, ensuring a seamless experience.
