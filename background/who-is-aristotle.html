Who Was Aristotle? Discover His Impact on Philosophy

Aristotle was a Greek philosopher and polymath born in 384 BC. He made significant contributions to various fields, including metaphysics, ethics, politics, and science. As a student of Plato, he later founded his own school, the Lyceum. Aristotle's works laid the groundwork for Western philosophy and still influence modern thought today.
