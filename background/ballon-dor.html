Ballon d'Or: The Ultimate Football Award

The Ballon d'Or is a prestigious annual football award recognizing the best male and female players worldwide. Established in 1956, it celebrates excellence by combining votes from journalists globally. Iconic legends like Messi and Ronaldo have dominated, sparking debates on talent and legacy in the beautiful game.
