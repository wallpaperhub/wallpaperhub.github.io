Tennessee Background Check Law: 7-Year Rule Explained

In Tennessee, background check laws mandate that employers can generally only consider criminal records dating back seven years. This limitation is designed to promote fair employment practices and help individuals reintegrate into the workforce. Understanding this law can assist both job seekers and employers in navigating hiring processes.
