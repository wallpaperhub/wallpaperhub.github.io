The Evolution of OSHA: A Brief History

OSHA, the Occupational Safety and Health Administration, was established in 1970 to ensure safe working conditions across the United States. The agency arose from growing concerns about workplace hazards and workers' rights. Through regulations and inspections, OSHA has significantly reduced workplace fatalities and injuries, promoting a culture of safety.
