Discovering the Vibrant Hippie Culture of the 70s

The 1970s were a vibrant era characterized by the rise of the hippie movement, which embraced peace, love, and freedom. Fueled by opposition to the Vietnam War, the counterculture celebrated individuality through psychedelic art, music festivals like Woodstock, and a rejection of materialism. Colorful fashion and communal living defined this revolutionary decade.
