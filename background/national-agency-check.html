Understanding National Agency Checks: Your Guide to Security Backgrounds

A National Agency Check (NAC) is a thorough security background investigation conducted by government authorities. It aims to assess an individual’s suitability for access to classified information or positions of trust. The process typically includes checks on criminal history, employment, and credit records, ensuring the individual meets necessary security standards.
