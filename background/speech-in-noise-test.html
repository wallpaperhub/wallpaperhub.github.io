Speech in Noise Test: Understanding Hearing in Noisy Environments

The speech in noise test evaluates a person's ability to understand speech amid background noise. It is essential for diagnosing hearing impairments, particularly in challenging listening environments. During the test, participants listen to sentences or words while various noise levels play, helping audiologists assess auditory processing capabilities and guide treatment options.
