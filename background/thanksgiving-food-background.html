Delicious Origins of Thanksgiving Food

Thanksgiving food has deep historical roots, originating from the harvest feasts of early settlers in America. Traditional dishes like turkey, stuffing, cranberry sauce, and pumpkin pie symbolize gratitude and family unity. Over centuries, these customs evolved into a festive occasion, celebrating abundance and community.
