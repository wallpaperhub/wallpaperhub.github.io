Understanding the Significance of Semataries

Sematary refers to sacred burial grounds where the dead are laid to rest. These sites often carry deep cultural and spiritual significance, varying widely across different societies. Historically, semataries serve as both a final resting place for loved ones and a space for the living to honor their memories and reflect on life.
