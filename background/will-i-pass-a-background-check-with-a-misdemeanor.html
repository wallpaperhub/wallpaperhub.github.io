Can You Pass a Background Check with a Misdemeanor?

Having a misdemeanor on your record can impact background checks, but it doesn’t automatically mean you won’t pass. Employers often consider the nature of the offense, how long ago it occurred, and your overall qualifications. Being honest and showcasing your skills can help mitigate concerns during the hiring process.
