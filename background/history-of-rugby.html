The Rich History of Rugby: From 19th Century Roots to Global Phenomenon

Rugby originated in England during the early 19th century, evolving from traditional football games. In 1823, William Webb Ellis famously picked up the ball during a game, leading to the sport's distinct rules. It gained popularity, culminating in the establishment of the Rugby Football Union in 1871, and expanded globally with various formats, including rugby union and rugby league.
