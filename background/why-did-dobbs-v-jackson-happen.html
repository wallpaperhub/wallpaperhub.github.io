Understanding Dobbs v. Jackson: Abortion Rights Explained

Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization arose from a Mississippi law banning most abortions after 15 weeks of pregnancy. The case challenged the precedent set by Roe v. Wade, focusing on states' rights and the extent to which they can regulate abortion. It sparked national debates over reproductive rights and health care access.
