Marcus Garvey: Champion of Black Nationalism

Marcus Garvey was a Jamaican political leader, publisher, journalist, and activist, who became a prominent figure in the early 20th-century Black nationalism and Pan-Africanism movements. He founded the Universal Negro Improvement Association (UNIA) to promote Black self-empowerment. Garvey's legacy inspires movements for racial pride and unity.
