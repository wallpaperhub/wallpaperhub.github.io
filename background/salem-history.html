Explore the Fascinating History of Salem

Salem, a historic city in Massachusetts, is known for its infamous witch trials of 1692, where fear led to the persecution of innocent people. Founded in 1626, Salem was a significant maritime center in colonial America, thriving on trade and commerce. Today, it celebrates its rich history with museums, festivals, and preserved sites.
