Experience the Vibrant Celebration of Holi: Festival of Colors

Holi, also known as the Festival of Colors, originates from ancient Hindu mythology. Celebrated predominantly in India and Nepal, it marks the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil. Traditionally, participants throw colored powders, sing, dance, and indulge in festive foods, fostering community and joy.
