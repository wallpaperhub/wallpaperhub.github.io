Miami Vice: The Iconic 80s Crime Drama

Miami Vice, which aired from 1984 to 1990, revolutionized television with its stylish blend of crime drama and vibrant aesthetics. Set in the lively backdrop of Miami, the show depicted the lives of undercover detectives Sonny Crockett and Ricardo Tubbs as they tackled drug trafficking and organized crime, all while showcasing the city’s nightlife and iconic visuals.
