How to Render with a Transparent Background in Blender

Creating a transparent background in Blender is a straightforward process. First, go to the Render Properties tab and enable 'Transparent' under the Film section. Adjust your scene lights and camera settings as needed. Upon rendering, your output will feature a clean, transparent background, ideal for compositing and design projects.
