How to Change Your Background to White: A Step-by-Step Guide

To make a background white, start by selecting the area you wish to change. For images, use editing software like Photoshop or GIMP, and apply a fill layer with pure white. In web design, adjust the CSS background color property to #FFFFFF. This simple change enhances clarity and focus on the primary content.
