Enhance Your PowerPoint: Edit Background Graphics

Editing background graphics in PowerPoint can transform your presentation's look. To do this, right-click on the slide, select "Format Background," choose between solid fill, gradient fill, or picture fill, and adjust transparency as necessary. This simple step enhances visual appeal and keeps your audience engaged.
