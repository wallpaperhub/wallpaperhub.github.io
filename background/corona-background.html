Understanding COVID-19: Background and Impact

The COVID-19 pandemic, caused by the novel coronavirus SARS-CoV-2, emerged in late 2019 and significantly impacted global health, economies, and daily life. It led to widespread illness, lockdowns, and a push for vaccine development. Understanding the pandemic's background is vital for grasping its ongoing effects and importance of public health measures.
