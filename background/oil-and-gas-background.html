Understanding Oil and Gas Industry Dynamics

The oil and gas industry plays a crucial role in the global economy, powering transportation, heating, and electricity. It involves exploration, extraction, refining, and distribution of fossil fuels, which have been vital for industrial development since the 19th century. However, environmental concerns are driving a shift toward sustainable energy sources.
