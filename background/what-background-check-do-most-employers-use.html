Essential Background Checks Employers Use

Most employers conduct background checks to verify a candidate's identity, employment history, and criminal record. Common checks include social security verification, education verification, and reference checks. Some employers also check credit reports or use services that provide comprehensive reports to ensure a safe and reliable workforce.
