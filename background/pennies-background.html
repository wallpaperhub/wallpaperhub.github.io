The Rich History of Pennies: A Look Back

Pennies have a rich history, originating in ancient Rome as "denarius," with the term evolving over time. The modern penny, often seen as a trivial coin, represents the 1-cent denomination in the U.S. Mint. Despite debates about its utility, the penny’s background reveals its role in trade and culture through centuries.
