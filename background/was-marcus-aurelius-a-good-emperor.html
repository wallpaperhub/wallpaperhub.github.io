Was Marcus Aurelius a Good Emperor? Explore His Legacy

Marcus Aurelius is often hailed as a good emperor due to his philosophical approach and commitment to Stoicism. His reign from 161 to 180 AD was marked by significant challenges, including military conflicts and the Plague. Despite these, he prioritized the welfare of his people and aimed to embody virtue, making strides in leadership and governance.
