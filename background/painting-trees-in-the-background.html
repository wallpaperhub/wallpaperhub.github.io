“Tips for Painting Trees in the Background”

Painting trees in the background can bring depth and serenity to any artwork. When capturing their essence, focus on varying shades of green and brown to represent light and shadow. Experiment with different brush techniques, such as stippling or blending, to create realistic textures. Trees not only enhance the scenery but also evoke emotions, grounding your piece in nature's beauty.
