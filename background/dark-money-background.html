Understanding Dark Money in Politics

Dark money refers to political spending by organizations that do not disclose their donors. This practice allows significant financial influence over elections and legislation without transparency. Critics argue it undermines democracy, as voters are unable to know who is funding political messages, potentially skewing public policy in favor of hidden interests.
