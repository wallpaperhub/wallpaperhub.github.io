How to Change the Background in Google Docs: A Quick Guide

Customizing the background of your Google Doc can enhance its visual appeal and make your documents stand out. To change the background, go to "File," select "Page setup," and choose "Page color." You can opt for solid colors or even upload images. This feature is perfect for personalized documents, presentations, or creative projects.
