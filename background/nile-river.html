Discover the Wonders of the Nile River

The Nile River, one of the longest rivers in the world, flows through eleven countries in northeastern Africa, including Egypt and Sudan. Known for its crucial role in ancient civilizations, the river supports diverse ecosystems and agriculture. With stunning landscapes and rich history, it remains a vital resource for millions today.
