Simple Background Ideas for Stunning Digital Art

When creating digital art, a simple background can enhance your subject without distracting from it. Consider using soft gradients, abstract shapes, or textured brushes to add depth. For a minimalist approach, solid colors or subtle patterns work well. Experiment with opacity and layering to create depth without overwhelming your focal point.
