Recycling: A Key to Sustainability

Recycling has its roots in the conservationist movements of the 1960s and 70s, aimed at reducing waste and preserving natural resources. With rising environmental concerns, it has become a vital practice. Today, recycling encompasses various materials—from paper to plastics—promoting sustainability and reducing landfill reliance.
