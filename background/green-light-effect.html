Understanding the Green Light Effect: Boost Your Motivation and Clarity

The green light effect refers to the phenomenon where a green light signal signifies the go-ahead for action or decision-making, often used in various contexts from traffic signals to project management. This influence can boost motivation and confidence, encouraging individuals to pursue goals with clarity and purpose, fostering a positive mindset.
