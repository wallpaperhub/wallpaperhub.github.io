Who Owns Oreo Cookies? Discover the Brand Behind the Treat!

Oreo cookies, the iconic sandwich cookie known for its distinctive cream filling, are owned by Mondelez International. Originally introduced in 1912, Oreos have become a beloved snack around the world. Mondelez oversees the production and global distribution of these cookies, ensuring they remain a household favorite.
