Understanding Criminal History: What You Need to Know

Criminal history refers to the record of a person's criminal offenses, including arrests, convictions, and sentences. It plays a crucial role in various legal contexts, such as employment screening and background checks. Understanding one's criminal history is essential for rehabilitation and ensuring accountability within society.
