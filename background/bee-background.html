Understanding Bees: Importance & Conservation

Bees play a crucial role in our ecosystem, primarily through pollination, which supports biodiversity and food production. Their social structure showcases a queen, workers, and drones, each with vital roles. Unfortunately, factors like habitat loss, pesticides, and climate change threaten their populations, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
