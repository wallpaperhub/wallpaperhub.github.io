How to Activate Noise Cancelling on AirPods

To turn on noise cancelling on your AirPods, ensure they are connected to your device. For AirPods Pro, press and hold the force sensor on the stem of an earbud until you hear a chime. Alternatively, go to your device’s settings, select Bluetooth, tap on your AirPods, and choose “Noise Cancellation.” Enjoy immersive sound!
