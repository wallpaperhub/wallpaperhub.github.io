What Does BGM Mean? Understanding Background Music

BGM stands for "Background Music," often used in various media such as films, games, and advertisements. It enhances the overall atmosphere and emotional impact without overshadowing the main content. BGM is essential in creating immersive experiences, guiding audience emotions, and maintaining engagement throughout the viewing or listening experience.
