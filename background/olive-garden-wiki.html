Explore Olive Garden: Your Guide to Italy's Flavors

Olive Garden is a popular American restaurant chain known for its Italian-American cuisine, featuring dishes like pasta, breadsticks, and salads. Founded in 1982 in Orlando, Florida, Olive Garden emphasizes a warm, family-friendly atmosphere. It is well-known for its unlimited soup and salad offerings, making it a favorite for casual dining.
