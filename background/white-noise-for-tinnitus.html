Relief for Tinnitus: How White Noise Can Help

White noise can be a soothing solution for those affected by tinnitus, a condition characterized by persistent ringing or buzzing in the ears. Utilizing white noise machines or apps can mask these intrusive sounds, providing relief and improving sleep quality. Many find that consistent exposure helps condition their auditory system, offering a semblance of calm.
