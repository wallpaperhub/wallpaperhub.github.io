Discover the Legacy of Duke Ellington: Jazz Icon

Duke Ellington, an iconic figure in jazz history, was a composer, pianist, and bandleader. Born in 1899, he elevated big band music with his innovative compositions and arrangements, blending various genres. His timeless works, including "Mood Indigo" and "It Don't Mean a Thing," continue to inspire musicians worldwide.
