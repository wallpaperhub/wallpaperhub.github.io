Understanding Background IQ: Uncovering Your Unique Intelligence

Background IQ refers to the intelligence measured by ability tests or assessments taken in a more relaxed environment, often outside traditional academic settings. This concept highlights the impact of social and cultural influences on cognitive performance, illustrating how unique backgrounds shape individual potential and abilities in diverse contexts.
