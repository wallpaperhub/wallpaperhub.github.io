How to Navigate the Maryland Correctional Officer Hiring Process

The Maryland correctional officer hiring process involves several key steps. Applicants must complete an online application, pass a background check, and undergo physical and psychological evaluations. Candidates also face a written exam and an interview. Training at the Maryland "Correctional Academy" is mandatory upon selection.
