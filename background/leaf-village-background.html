Discover the Fascinating Background of Leaf Village in Naruto

The Leaf Village, also known as Konohagakure, is one of the most prominent hidden villages in the Naruto universe. Nestled in a lush, green forest, it symbolizes harmony between nature and ninjutsu. Home to legendary ninjas like Naruto and Sasuke, its rich history is filled with battles, alliances, and the pursuit of peace, making it vital to the series' narrative.
