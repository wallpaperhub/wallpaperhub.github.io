Understanding Character Background: Essential Insights

Character background provides depth and dimension, shaping a character's motivations, fears, and desires. It encompasses their history, upbringing, and key experiences that influence their behavior in the story. These elements are crucial for relatability and engagement, allowing readers to connect with characters on a more emotional level.
