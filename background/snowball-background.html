Discover the Fun of Snowballs: History & Uses

Snowballs have a rich history that dates back to ancient times, symbolizing winter fun and childhood memories. Formed by compacting snow, these frosty spheres can be simple or elaborate. They are often associated with festive winter activities and are a favorite in snowball fights, representing playful competition and joy in cold weather.
