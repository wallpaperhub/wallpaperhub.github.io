Do Dismissed Evictions Appear on Background Checks?

Dismissed evictions generally do not show up on background checks. While an eviction record can raise red flags for landlords, dismissed cases indicate that the court ruled in favor of the tenant. However, it's crucial to clarify with potential landlords about their criteria since practices can vary. Always check your own report for accuracy.
