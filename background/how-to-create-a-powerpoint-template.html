How to Design a Custom PowerPoint Template

To create a PowerPoint template, start by opening a blank presentation. Set your preferred slide size via Design > Slide Size. Design a master slide with your desired fonts, colors, and layouts under View > Slide Master. Save your template by going to File > Save As and choose PowerPoint Template (*.potx) to ensure it's reusable.
