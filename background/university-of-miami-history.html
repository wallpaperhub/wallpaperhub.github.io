University of Miami: A Brief History

Founded in 1925, the University of Miami has evolved into a prominent research institution. Initially established as a small liberal arts college, it has grown to include diverse programs across numerous fields. The campus in Coral Gables, Florida, reflects a vibrant cultural atmosphere, contributing significantly to Miami's educational landscape.
