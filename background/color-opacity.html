Mastering Color Opacity: A Guide to Design Transparency

Color opacity refers to the transparency level of a color, determining how much of the underlying layer or background is visible. This property enhances designs by allowing for layering effects, creating depth, and achieving a desired aesthetic. Understanding color opacity is essential in graphic design, web design, and art.
