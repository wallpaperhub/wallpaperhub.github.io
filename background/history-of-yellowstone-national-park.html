Explore the Rich History of Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone National Park, established in 1872, was the first national park in the United States and is often regarded as the first in the world. Spanning over 3,400 square miles across Wyoming, Montana, and Idaho, it protects unique geothermal features, wildlife, and beautiful landscapes. Its rich history includes Indigenous peoples' presence and fascinating geological formations.
