Background Check Duration for Insurance Licenses

When applying for an insurance license, a background check typically goes back 7 to 10 years. This duration can cover criminal records, credit history, and employment verification. Factors like state regulations and the type of insurance license can influence the exact timeframe. It's essential to be aware of what may be revealed during this process.
