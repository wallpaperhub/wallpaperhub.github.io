Colorful Peeps: A Sweet History

Peeps, the iconic marshmallow candies, have a colorful history dating back to the 1950s. Initially hand-made, these sugary treats became a springtime favorite due to their chick shape. Over the years, Peeps have expanded their flavor and color range, evolving into a year-round snack beloved by all ages, and sparking creative recipes and crafts.
