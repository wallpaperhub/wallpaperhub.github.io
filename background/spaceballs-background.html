Spaceballs: The Ultimate Sci-Fi Parody You Can't Miss

Spaceballs, directed by Mel Brooks, is a 1987 satirical comedy that parodies the sci-fi genre, especially the Star Wars franchise. The film follows hero Lone Starr and his sidekick Barf as they battle the evil Dark Helmet to rescue Princess Vespa and save Planet Druidia. Its clever jokes and memorable characters have made it a cult classic.
