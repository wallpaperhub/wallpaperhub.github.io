Exploring the Dark History of Insane Asylums

Insane asylums, once places of confinement for those deemed mentally ill, evoke a haunting image of the past. Often characterized by harsh conditions and outdated treatments, they symbolize society's struggle to understand mental illness. Today's approach emphasizes compassion and treatment, shifting from punishment to healing.
