Explore the Underground Adventures of Dig Dug

Dig Dug is a classic arcade game developed by Namco in 1982, where players control a character named Dig Dug who tunnels through the earth, inflating and defeating enemies like Pookas and Fygars. Its unique gameplay and charming graphics have made it a beloved retro title, showcasing early examples of strategy and skill in gaming.
