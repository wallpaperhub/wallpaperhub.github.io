Napoleon Bonaparte: A Brief Biography

Napoleon Bonaparte, born on August 15, 1769, on the island of Corsica, rose from modest beginnings to become a military leader and Emperor of France. Renowned for his strategic prowess, he led France through a series of significant campaigns during the Napoleonic Wars. His life was marked by ambition, reform, and eventual exile.
