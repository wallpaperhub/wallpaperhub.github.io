Essential Insights into Geography

Geography is the study of the Earth's landscapes, environments, and the relationships between people and their environments. It encompasses physical features like mountains and rivers, as well as human elements such as cities and cultures. Understanding geography enriches our awareness of global diversity and interconnectivity.
