Discover the Rich History of Delis: A Culinary Journey

Deli, short for delicatessen, originated in Germany and has become a staple in cultures worldwide. It offers a variety of meats, cheeses, and specialty foods. Traditionally focused on high-quality ingredients and artisanal preparations, delis serve as social hubs where customers enjoy fresh sandwiches and prepared meals.
