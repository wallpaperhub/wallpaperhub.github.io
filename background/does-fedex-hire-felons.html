Does FedEx Hire Felons? Here’s What You Need to Know

FedEx has a policy of conducting background checks on potential employees, which includes looking for felony convictions. However, they do consider the nature of the offense, how much time has passed, and the relevance to the job. Many positions may still be available for those with a criminal record, encouraging reentry into the workforce.
