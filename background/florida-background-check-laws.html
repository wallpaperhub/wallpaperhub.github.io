Understanding Florida Background Check Laws: Essential Insights

Florida background check laws require employers to obtain consent before running checks and must comply with the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). Certain regulations exist for specific industries, limiting the use of criminal history for employment decisions. Citizens can access their own records to ensure accuracy.
