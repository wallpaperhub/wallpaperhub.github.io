Carolina Panthers Logo: Symbol of Strength and Pride

The Carolina Panthers logo features a fierce, stylized panther's head in black, blue, and white. It symbolizes strength, speed, and agility, perfectly representing the team's identity. The logo has evolved since its inception in 1995 but remains a powerful emblem of the team's spirit and fan loyalty in the NFL.
