Understanding First Fruits in the Bible: Significance & Practice

In the Bible, "first fruits" refers to the initial harvest offerings dedicated to God, symbolizing gratitude and acknowledgment of His provision. This ancient practice is rooted in the agricultural laws of Israel and is often seen as a spiritual metaphor for dedicating our best to God, recognizing Him as the source of all blessings.
