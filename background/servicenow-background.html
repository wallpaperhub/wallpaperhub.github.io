Understanding ServiceNow: A Comprehensive Background

ServiceNow is a cloud-based platform designed for automating IT service management (ITSM). It enables organizations to streamline their operations by providing a suite of tools for IT workflows, including incident management, problem resolution, and change management. ServiceNow enhances productivity by improving service delivery and transparency.
