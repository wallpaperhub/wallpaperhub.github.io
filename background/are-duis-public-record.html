Are DUIs Public Record? Understanding Your Rights

Yes, DUIs (Driving Under the Influence) are considered public records. This means that they can be accessed by anyone through court records or law enforcement databases. This transparency is meant to maintain public safety and accountability, but it can also have lasting impacts on an individual's personal and professional life.
