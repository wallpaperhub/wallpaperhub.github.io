How to Become a Sonographer: A Step-by-Step Guide

To become a sonographer, start by earning a high school diploma and pursuing a degree in sonography or a related field. Complete a clinical internship to gain hands-on experience, and obtain certification from a recognized board. Stay updated on advancements in technology and continue professional development for career growth.
