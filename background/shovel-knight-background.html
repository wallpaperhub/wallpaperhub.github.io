Discover the Adventures of Shovel Knight: A Retro Indie Game

Shovel Knight is a retro-style indie game developed by Yacht Club Games, released in 2014. It combines elements of classic platformers with modern design. Players take on the role of the titular knight, equipped with a shovel as their primary weapon, battling through diverse levels filled with enemies, puzzles, and treasures.
