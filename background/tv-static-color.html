Understanding TV Static Colors: A Visual Guide

TV static colors manifest as a mix of random hues, predominantly gray, black, and white. This flickering display occurs when a television receives no signal, leading to an electromagnetic disturbance. It’s a nostalgic sight for many, triggering memories of cathode-ray tube televisions and the joy of channel surfing for something exciting.
