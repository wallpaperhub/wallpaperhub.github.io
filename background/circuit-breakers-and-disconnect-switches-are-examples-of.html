Understanding Circuit Breakers and Disconnect Switches

Circuit breakers and disconnect switches are examples of essential electrical components that enhance safety and control in power systems. While circuit breakers automatically interrupt current flow during faults, disconnect switches provide a manual means to isolate equipment. Both are vital for preventing damage and ensuring reliable operation.
