Christine Lagarde: A Leader in Global Finance

Christine Lagarde, an influential figure in global finance, has served as the President of the European Central Bank since 2019. Prior to this, she held the position of Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund. Known for her expertise in economic policy and leadership, Lagarde continues to shape financial strategies in Europe and beyond.
