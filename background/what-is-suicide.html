Understanding Suicide: Causes, Prevention, and Support

Suicide is the act of intentionally ending one's own life. It often stems from mental health issues, trauma, or overwhelming despair. The decision to commit suicide is influenced by complex emotional and psychological factors, and it highlights the urgent need for compassion, support, and effective mental health resources to prevent such tragedies.
