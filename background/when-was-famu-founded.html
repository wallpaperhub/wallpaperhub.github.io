FAMU: A Historical Overview of Its Founding

Florida A&M University (FAMU) was founded on October 3, 1887. It began as a private institution for the education of African Americans and has since grown into a renowned public research university. FAMU is located in Tallahassee, Florida, and is known for its commitment to academic excellence and community service.
