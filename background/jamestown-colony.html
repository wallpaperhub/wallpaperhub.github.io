Explore the Historic Jamestown Colony: America's First Settlement

Founded in 1607, Jamestown Colony was the first permanent English settlement in North America. It faced numerous challenges, including harsh winters, food shortages, and conflicts with Indigenous peoples. Despite these struggles, it laid the foundation for future colonization and the development of what would become the United States.
