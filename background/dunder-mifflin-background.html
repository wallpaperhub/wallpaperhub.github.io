Dunder Mifflin: The Iconic Paper Company from "The Office"

Dunder Mifflin is a fictional paper company featured in the U.S. version of the television series "The Office." Founded by Charles Miner and later managed by Michael Scott, the company becomes a hub for quirky personalities and comedic antics. Set in Scranton, Pennsylvania, it serves as the backdrop for exploring workplace dynamics and relationships.
