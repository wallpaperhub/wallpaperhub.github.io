"A Nightmare on Elm Street: Horror's Iconic Dream Demon"

"A Nightmare on Elm Street" is a seminal horror film released in 1984, directed by Wes Craven. It introduced the terrifying character Freddy Krueger, a vengeful spirit who haunts teenagers in their dreams. The film explores themes of fear, trauma, and guilt, making it a cornerstone of the slasher genre and spawning numerous sequels and remakes.
