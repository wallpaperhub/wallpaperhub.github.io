When Was 2 Timothy Written? A Quick Insight

2 Timothy is widely believed to have been written around A.D. 67, just before the Apostle Paul's martyrdom. This letter, a personal epistle to Timothy, serves as a final exhortation, encouraging him to remain steadfast in faith and sound teaching, reflecting Paul's deep concern for the future of the Church and his beloved protégé.
