Unveiling the Viking Legacy: Explorers and Warriors

Vikings, seafaring Norse warriors from Scandinavia, thrived from the late 8th to the early 11th century. They were known for their longships, exploration, and raids across Europe. Viking society was complex, with a rich mythology and advanced craftsmanship. Their legacy influences modern culture with tales of heroism and adventure.
