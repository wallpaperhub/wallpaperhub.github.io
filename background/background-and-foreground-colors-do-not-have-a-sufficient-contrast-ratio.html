Enhancing Readability: Importance of Color Contrast Ratio

Background and foreground colors that lack sufficient contrast can create readability issues, particularly for individuals with visual impairments. It's essential to ensure adequate contrast ratios to enhance accessibility and user experience. A clear distinction between colors not only improves visibility but also conveys content effectively.
