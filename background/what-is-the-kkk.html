Understanding the Ku Klux Klan: History & Impact

The Ku Klux Klan (KKK) is a white supremacist hate group formed in the United States during the Reconstruction era post-Civil War. Known for its racist ideologies and violent actions against African Americans and other minorities, the Klan has a notorious history of intimidation, lynching, and acts of terrorism to promote white supremacy.
