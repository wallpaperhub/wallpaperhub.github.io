Discover Thomas Jefferson: Founding Father & President

Thomas Jefferson was the third President of the United States and a founding father, renowned for drafting the Declaration of Independence in 1776. A proponent of individual liberties, Jefferson's vision for America emphasized democracy, education, and religious freedom. His legacy continues to influence American society today.
