Change Background on Your LG TV: Step-by-Step Guide

To change the background on your LG TV, press the Home button on your remote. Navigate to 'Settings', then select 'Picture'. Here, find 'Wallpapers' or 'Screensaver'. Choose from the provided options or upload your own images via USB. Save your settings, and enjoy a personalized backdrop while your TV is idle.
