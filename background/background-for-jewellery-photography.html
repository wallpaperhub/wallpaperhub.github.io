Perfect Backdrops for Stunning Jewellery Photography

When photographing jewellery, the background is crucial for highlighting the piece's details and colors. Opt for a simple, non-distracting backdrop to ensure the jewellery stands out. Soft, neutral tones like white, grey, or pastel shades work well. Additionally, consider textures like wood or fabric for added depth and interest.
