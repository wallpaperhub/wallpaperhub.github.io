Is Niagara Falls Man-Made? Uncover the Truth

Niagara Falls is a natural wonder, not man-made. Formed over 12,000 years ago, its breathtaking beauty is the result of geological processes involving glaciers and erosion. While humans have harnessed its power for hydroelectricity and tourism, the falls themselves are created by nature's forces, making them a true marvel of the earth.
