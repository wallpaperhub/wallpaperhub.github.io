Discover the Story Behind PetSmart: A Pet Care Leader

PetSmart, founded in 1986, emerged as a leader in pet supplies and services. With over 1,600 stores across the U.S., Canada, and Puerto Rico, it provides a wide range of products and services, including pet adoption, grooming, and training. PetSmart is dedicated to improving the lives of pets and their owners.
