Legacy of Norma McCorvey: The Jane Roe Story

Norma McCorvey was the woman behind the landmark Supreme Court case Roe v. Wade, which legalized abortion in the United States in 1973. Known by the legal pseudonym "Jane Roe," her life story reflects the complexities of personal, social, and legal issues surrounding abortion. McCorvey's later conversion to Christianity sparked further debate on her legacy.
