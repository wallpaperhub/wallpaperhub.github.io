"Understanding NFT Background: Essentials of Non-Fungible Tokens"

NFTs, or Non-Fungible Tokens, represent unique digital assets verified using blockchain technology. Unlike cryptocurrencies, which are interchangeable, NFTs signify ownership of specific items like art, music, and collectibles. Their rise has transformed digital ownership, enabling artists and creators to profit directly from their work in the digital realm.
