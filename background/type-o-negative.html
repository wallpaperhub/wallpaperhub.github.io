Discover Type O Negative: The Gothic Metal Legends

Type O Negative was an iconic American gothic metal band known for their heavy sound and themes of love, loss, and despair. Formed in 1990, the band, led by the charismatic Peter Steele, gained a cult following with their unique blend of doom metal and romanticism. Their albums, like "Bloody Kisses," remain influential, showcasing their talent and distinctive style.
