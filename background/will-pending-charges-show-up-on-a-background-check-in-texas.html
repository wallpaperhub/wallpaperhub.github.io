Do Pending Charges Show Up on a Texas Background Check?

Your background check in Texas may include pending charges, depending on the nature of the check. If conducting a criminal history review, these charges can be visible. However, if the check is limited to convictions only, pending charges might not appear. It's crucial to know the type of check being performed.
