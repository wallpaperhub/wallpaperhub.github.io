Understanding Newspaper Background: A Historical Overview

Newspapers have a rich history, evolving from handwritten news bulletins to printed broadsheets. They played a crucial role in shaping public opinion, fostering literacy, and providing a platform for diverse voices. With the advent of digital media, newspapers now face challenges but continue to adapt and remain integral to informed societies.
