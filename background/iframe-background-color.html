Enhance Iframe Background Color for Better Design

When working with iframes, setting a background color can enhance the visual appearance of your embedded content. Use CSS styles to define the background color, ensuring it complements your website’s theme. Note that some iframes may inherit the parent’s background, so testing is essential for optimal results.
