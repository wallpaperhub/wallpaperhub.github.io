How to Export Illustrator Files with Transparent Background

Exporting your Illustrator artwork with a transparent background is simple. First, select your artwork and go to 'File', then 'Export', and choose 'Export As.' In the dialog box, select PNG or SVG format. Ensure the 'Use Artboards' option is checked for precise results, then set the background to transparent. Now you're ready to go!
