Subway Logo: Iconic Design Explained

The Subway logo, a dynamic symbol of the popular sandwich chain, features bold green and yellow colors. The green represents freshness and healthiness, while the yellow signifies optimism. Its unique design includes the word "Subway" along with an arrow pointing right, signifying progress and movement, perfectly encapsulating the brand's commitment to quick service and quality food.
