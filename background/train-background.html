Trains have a rich history, serving as a vital mode of transportation across continents. Their background is rooted in the Industrial Revolution, evolving from steam engines to modern electric systems. Today, trains symbolize connectivity and efficiency, offering a sustainable alternative for long-distance travel and cargo transport.
