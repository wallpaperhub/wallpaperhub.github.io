How to Set a Custom Xbox One Background from Your Phone

To set a custom background on your Xbox One using your phone, first, ensure you have the Xbox app installed. Sign in, go to 'My Library,' then select 'Screenshots' to upload your image. Once uploaded, navigate to 'Settings' on your Xbox, select 'Personalization,' then 'My background,' and choose your image. Enjoy your custom look!
