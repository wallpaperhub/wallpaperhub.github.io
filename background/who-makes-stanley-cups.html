Who Crafts the Stanley Cup? Unveiling the Makers

Stanley Cups, awarded annually to the NHL playoff champion, are crafted by the famous silversmiths at Tiffany & Co. The trophy, originally made of 925 sterling silver, is renowned for its iconic design and history since 1893. Each year, new rings are added to commemorate the winning teams, making it a lasting symbol of excellence in hockey.
