Discover the Art of Georgia O'Keeffe

Georgia O'Keeffe was a pioneering American artist known for her contributions to modernism and her iconic flower paintings. Her works captured the beauty of the American landscape and the female form. O'Keeffe's distinctive style, characterized by vibrant colors and bold forms, continues to inspire artists and art lovers today.
