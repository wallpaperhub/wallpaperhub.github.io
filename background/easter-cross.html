Easter Cross: A Symbol of Resurrection and Hope

The Easter cross symbolizes the resurrection of Jesus Christ, a cornerstone of Christian faith. Adorned with flowers and decorations during Easter celebrations, it represents hope, renewal, and the promise of eternal life. Many churches and homes display the cross as a reminder of the sacrifice and victory over death, embodying the true essence of Easter.
