Understanding Caveman Life: A Glimpse into Prehistoric Humanity

Cavemen, the prehistoric humans of the Paleolithic era, lived in small groups and depended on hunting and foraging. They created tools from stone and bone, harnessed fire for warmth and cooking, and developed early forms of art, as seen in cave paintings. Their survival strategies laid the groundwork for modern society.
