Discovering Katrina Law: Actress & Icon

Katrina Law is an American actress known for her dynamic roles in television and film. Born on September 30, 1985, in Berlin, Germany, she gained fame for her portrayal of Nyssa al Ghul in the DC Comics series "Arrow" and for her lead role in "The Warrior's Way." With diverse talents, Law continues to impress audiences worldwide.
