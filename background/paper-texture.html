Understanding Paper Texture: A Comprehensive Guide

Paper texture refers to the tactile quality and surface characteristics of paper, influencing how it feels in hand and how it interacts with ink. Different textures, such as smooth, laid, or linen, can enhance visual appeal and affect printing techniques. Understanding paper texture is essential for selecting the right paper for artistic or professional projects.
