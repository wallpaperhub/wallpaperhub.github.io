What Does AMC Stand For in the Movie Industry?

AMC commonly stands for "American Multi-Cinema," one of the largest movie theater chains in the U.S. It provides a wide range of films, including blockbusters and independent features, along with amenities like reclining seats and in-theater dining. AMC is a popular destination for moviegoers seeking an enhanced cinema experience.
