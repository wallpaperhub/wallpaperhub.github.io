How to Check for Evictions: A Step-by-Step Guide

To check for evictions, start by accessing public records at your local courthouse or online database. You can also request a tenant screening report from a credit bureau, which provides eviction history. Additionally, ask landlords for references and verification of rental history to uncover any past eviction issues.
