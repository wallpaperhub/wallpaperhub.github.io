Elvis Presley: The King of Rock & Roll

Elvis Presley, known as the "King of Rock and Roll," was born on January 8, 1935, in Tupelo, Mississippi. He rose to fame in the 1950s with hits like "Heartbreak Hotel" and "Jailhouse Rock." His unique blend of genres and charismatic performances revolutionized music. Presley also starred in films and became a cultural icon before his untimely death in 1977.
