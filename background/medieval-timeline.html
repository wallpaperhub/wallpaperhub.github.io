The Complete Medieval Timeline: Key Events Uncovered

The medieval timeline spans from the 5th to the late 15th century, marking the Middle Ages' development in Europe. Key events include the fall of the Western Roman Empire (476 AD), the rise of feudalism, the Crusades (1096-1291), and the Black Death (1347-1351). The era ends with the Renaissance's onset and the discovery of new lands.
