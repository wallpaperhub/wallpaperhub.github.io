How to Make Videos with Transparent Backgrounds

To create a video with a transparent background, use video editing software like Adobe After Effects or Camtasia. Start by importing your footage, then use the chroma key tool to remove the background color. Export your video in a format that supports transparency, such as MOV with Alpha Channel.
