Blink-182: The Iconic Pop-Punk Band That Shaped a Generation

Blink-182 is a legendary pop-punk band formed in 1992, known for their catchy melodies and relatable lyrics. With hits like "All the Small Things" and "What's My Age Again?", they defined a generation's sound. Their energetic performances and humorous personas have left a lasting impact on the music scene, continuing to inspire new artists.
