Explore the Rich History of Islam

Islam, founded in the 7th century by the Prophet Muhammad in Mecca, swiftly spread across the Arabian Peninsula. Key events include the hijra (migration) to Medina, the establishment of a vast caliphate, and significant cultural advancements during the Golden Age. The faith is now practiced by over a billion people worldwide.
