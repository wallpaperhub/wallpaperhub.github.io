Discover the Fascinating History of the Everglades

The Everglades, a unique subtropical wilderness in Florida, has a rich history shaped by natural and human influences. Originally inhabited by Indigenous peoples, it later faced extensive drainage for agriculture and urban development in the 20th century. Conservation efforts have since aimed to restore its delicate ecosystem.
