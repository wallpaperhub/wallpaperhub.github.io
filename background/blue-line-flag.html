Understanding the Blue Line Flag: A Symbol of Support

The blue line flag is a poignant symbol representing law enforcement and their dedication to protecting communities. It features a black and white American flag with a single blue stripe, honoring the sacrifices made by police officers. This flag promotes awareness and support for those who risk their lives for public safety.
