How to Easily Select Backgrounds in Photoshop

Selecting a background in Photoshop can enhance your design. Start by opening your image and using the Quick Selection Tool to outline the area you want to keep. Refine the selection with Select and Mask to smooth edges. Once satisfied, simply delete the background or replace it with a new layer for a fresh look.
