The Difference Between Off the Record and On Background

When discussing journalism, "off the record" means the information cannot be used in any form, while "on background" allows the information to be used but without attributing it to the source. Understanding these distinctions is vital for reporters, as they shape how sensitive information is handled and shared in the media landscape.
