Discover the Fascinating World of Spinosaurus

Spinosaurus, one of the largest carnivorous dinosaurs, roamed the Earth around 85 million years ago during the Late Cretaceous period. Its distinctive sail-like structure on its back is formed by elongated neural spines. Believed to be semi-aquatic, Spinosaurus likely hunted fish and is known for its unique adaptations that set it apart from other theropods.
