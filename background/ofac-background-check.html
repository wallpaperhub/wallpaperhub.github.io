Essential Guide to OFAC Background Checks

An OFAC background check is a critical process used to ensure compliance with U.S. sanctions and regulations. It involves screening individuals and entities against the Office of Foreign Assets Control's lists to identify any potential risks associated with financial transactions. Essential for businesses, this check helps prevent engaging with sanctioned parties.
