Background Check Expansion Act: Enhancing Firearm Safety

The Background Check Expansion Act aims to enhance safety by requiring background checks for all firearm sales and transfers. This bipartisan proposal seeks to close loopholes that allow unlicensed sellers to bypass checks, ultimately reducing gun violence and ensuring responsible ownership. Communities deserve safer environments.
