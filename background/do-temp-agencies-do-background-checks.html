Do Temp Agencies Conduct Background Checks? Discover the Facts!

Temp agencies often conduct background checks as part of their hiring process. This ensures that candidates meet the qualifications and trustworthiness required by their client companies. Background checks may include criminal history, employment verification, and educational credentials, helping agencies match reliable candidates with suitable positions.
