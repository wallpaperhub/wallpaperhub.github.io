Mastering the Art of Blurry Backgrounds in Photography

Blurry backgrounds, or bokeh effects, enhance the focus on the main subject in photography and videography. This technique creates a dreamy atmosphere by softening details in the background while keeping the subject sharp. It adds depth, emphasizing emotions and storytelling in visual media, making your images more compelling and professional.
