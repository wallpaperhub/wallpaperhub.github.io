Understanding Immigration Background Checks

Immigration background checks typically include verification of identity, criminal history, immigration status, and any prior applications for visas or residencies. Checks often assess employment, education records, and financial stability to ensure compliance with immigration laws and regulations, promoting national security and public safety.
