How to Change Your Apple Watch Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

To change the Apple Watch wallpaper, press and hold the watch face until you see the gallery of faces. Swipe left or right to choose a style, then tap "Customize." Select the background, and use the Digital Crown to scroll through options. Tap "Add" when satisfied, and your new wallpaper is set!
