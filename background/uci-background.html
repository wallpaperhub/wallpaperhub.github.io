Discover UCI: Academic Excellence & Innovation

The University of California, Irvine (UCI) is renowned for its distinguished academic programs, vibrant campus life, and commitment to research and innovation. Established in 1965, UCI is a leading public university that fosters diversity, engages in community service, and promotes environmental sustainability. With a strong focus on STEM, arts, and humanities, UCI prepares students for a dynamic future.
