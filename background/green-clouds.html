Understanding Green Clouds: Nature's Weather Signals

Green clouds are a rare atmospheric phenomenon often linked to severe weather conditions. These clouds may appear during thunderstorms, indicating possible hail or a tornado. Some research suggests they can also occur from sunlight scattering in unique environmental conditions. Understanding their formation helps in predicting severe weather.
