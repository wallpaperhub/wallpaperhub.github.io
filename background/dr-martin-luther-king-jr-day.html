Celebrate Martin Luther King Jr. Day: Honoring a Legacy

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Day is celebrated annually on the third Monday in January, honoring the legacy of the civil rights leader. It serves as a day of reflection, education, and community service, encouraging people to engage in activism and promote equality and justice for all. Embrace his vision of peace and unity.
