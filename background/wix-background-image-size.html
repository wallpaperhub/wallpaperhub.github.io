Optimal Wix Background Image Size Tips

When using Wix, background image size is crucial for achieving a polished and professional look. Ideally, choose images that are at least 1920 pixels wide for full-width displays. This ensures clarity on all devices. Remember to optimize images for faster loading times by compressing them without losing quality.
