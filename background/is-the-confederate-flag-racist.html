Is the Confederate Flag a Symbol of Racism?

The Confederate flag is often seen as a symbol of racism due to its historical ties to the Confederacy and its use by white supremacist groups. It represents a legacy of slavery and segregation for many Americans. While some claim it symbolizes Southern pride, its connection to racism remains a contentious debate.
