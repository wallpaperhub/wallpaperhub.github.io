Who Founded Ghost Energy Drink? Discover the Creators!

Ghost Energy Drink was created by the company Ghost Lifestyle, co-founded by entrepreneurs Dan Lourenco and Ryan Hughes in 2016. Ghost is known for blending innovation with lifestyle branding, providing unique flavor profiles and high-quality ingredients in their energy drinks. The brand seamlessly integrates fitness and fun.
