When Did Netflix Start? A Brief History

Netflix started its journey on August 29, 1997, founded by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph in Scotts Valley, California. Initially, it operated as a DVD rental service, allowing customers to rent DVDs online and have them delivered by mail. Over the years, Netflix evolved into a streaming giant, revolutionizing how we consume entertainment.
