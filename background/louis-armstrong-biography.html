Explore the Life of Louis Armstrong: Jazz Legend

Louis Armstrong, born on August 4, 1901, in New Orleans, was a groundbreaking jazz musician, trumpeter, and singer. He revolutionized jazz with his improvisational skills and charismatic performances. Famous for hits like "What a Wonderful World" and "Hello, Dolly!," Armstrong's influence extends beyond music, making him a cultural icon until his death in 1971.
