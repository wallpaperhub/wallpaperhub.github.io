Explore Background Needlepoint Stitches

Background needlepoint stitches serve as the foundation for many needlework projects, providing depth and aesthetic appeal. These stitches fill spaces, enhance designs, and can be simple or complex, depending on the piece. Common techniques include basketweave, tent, and diagonal stitches, allowing for creativity and variation in texture.
