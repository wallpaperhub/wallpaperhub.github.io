Understanding the Pisces Zodiac Sign: Traits & Characteristics

Pisces, symbolized by two fish swimming in opposite directions, embodies duality and depth. Those born under this sign (February 19 - March 20) are often known for their empathetic nature, creativity, and intuitive abilities. Pisces individuals are dreamers and visionaries, often drawn to the arts and spirituality.
