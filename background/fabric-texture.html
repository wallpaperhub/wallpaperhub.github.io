Explore the Art of Fabric Texture

Fabric texture refers to the visual and tactile qualities of a material, shaping how it's perceived and experienced. From the soft, plush feel of velvet to the crispness of cotton, texture influences aesthetics and functionality. It plays a crucial role in fashion and interior design, impacting comfort, durability, and style.
