Crafting a Compelling Character Background

Choosing your character background is a crucial step in defining who they are. It shapes their motivation, skills, and relationships within your story. Consider their upbringing, key life events, and personal beliefs. A well-crafted background adds depth, allowing players or readers to connect emotionally and invest in the character's journey.
