Streamline Your I-485 USCIS Background Check Process

The USCIS background check is a crucial part of the I-485 application process for adjusting your status to that of a lawful permanent resident. This check involves the examination of your criminal history, immigration status, and any potential security risks. Delays can occur, depending on the complexity of your case or issues found during the screening.
