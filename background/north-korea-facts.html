10 Fascinating Facts About North Korea You Should Know

North Korea, officially known as the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, is one of the world's most secretive nations. The capital is Pyongyang, with a population of about 25 million. The country is known for its strict government control, military-first policy, and limited internet access. Kim Jong-un is the current leader, succeeding his father.
