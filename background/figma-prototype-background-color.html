Customize Figma Prototype Background Colors Easily

In Figma, you can easily customize your prototype's background color to enhance visual appeal and ensure branding consistency. To change the background color, select the frame or artboard, navigate to the properties panel, and choose a color from the palette or enter a hex code. Make sure it complements your design for a cohesive look.
