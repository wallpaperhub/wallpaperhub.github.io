Add Stunning Backgrounds to Your Instagram Story

To add a background to your Instagram story, open the app and tap on the camera icon to create a new story. Take a photo or upload an existing one. Next, tap on the 'Aa' text tool to add a color layer as your background. Adjust the transparency for more dimension. You can also use a solid color or gradient for a stylish look!
