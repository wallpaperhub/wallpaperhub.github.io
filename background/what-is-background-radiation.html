Understanding Background Radiation: What You Need to Know

Background radiation refers to the naturally occurring ionizing radiation present in the environment, originating from cosmic rays, terrestrial sources, and radon gas. This radiation is constantly around us, contributing to our overall exposure, which is typically low. Understanding background radiation is crucial for assessing health risks associated with potential exposure.
