Understanding Deferred Adjudication: What It Means

Deferred adjudication is a legal alternative to traditional sentencing where a defendant pleads guilty or no contest to a charge but does not receive a conviction. Instead, the court postpones judgment, allowing the individual to complete certain conditions (like probation). If fulfilled, charges can be dismissed, leading to a cleaner record.
