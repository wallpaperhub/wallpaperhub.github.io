Who Acquired Merrill Lynch? Bank of America Deal Explained

In 2008, Bank of America acquired Merrill Lynch during the financial crisis. This strategic purchase helped Bank of America expand its services and strengthen its investment banking and wealth management capabilities. The deal, valued at approximately $50 billion, was aimed at stabilizing the financial sector and enhancing customer offerings.
