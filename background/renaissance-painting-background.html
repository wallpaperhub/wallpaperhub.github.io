Renaissance Painting: A Journey into Masterpieces

Renaissance painting emerged in Italy during the 14th century, marking a shift towards realism and humanism. Artists like Leonardo da Vinci and Michelangelo emphasized perspective, anatomy, and light to create lifelike masterpieces. This period celebrated classical themes, innovative techniques, and a renewed interest in the natural world.
