Hannah in the Bible: Faith, Prayer, and Divine Blessing

Hannah, a notable figure in the Bible, is known for her deep faith and unwavering prayers. Featured in the First Book of Samuel, she struggled with infertility and fervently prayed for a child. God answered her prayers, leading to the birth of her son, Samuel, who became a great prophet. Hannah's story embodies hope, devotion, and the power of prayer.
