Doraemon: The Timeless Cat from the Future

Doraemon is a beloved robotic cat from the future, created by Fujiko F. Fujio. With his signature blue color, large round face, and magical pocket filled with futuristic gadgets, he helps his friend Nobita Nobi navigate life’s challenges. The stories promote friendship, imagination, and the importance of doing good deeds.
