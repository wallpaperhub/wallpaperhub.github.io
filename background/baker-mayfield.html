Baker Mayfield: The Rise of a Football Star

Baker Mayfield is an American football quarterback known for his competitive spirit and leadership. Born on April 14, 1995, he gained prominence during his college career at Oklahoma, winning the Heisman Trophy in 2017. Mayfield was selected as the first overall pick in the 2018 NFL Draft by the Cleveland Browns, leading them to their first playoff appearance in 26 years.
