Turkey Insights: All About This Fascinating Bird

Turkey is a fascinating bird, known for its distinctive fan-shaped tail and unique vocalizations. This large, ground-dwelling bird is native to North America and often associated with festive occasions like Thanksgiving. Wild turkeys are social creatures, living in flocks and displaying remarkable adaptability to various habitats.
