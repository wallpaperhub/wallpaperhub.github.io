How to Effortlessly Remove Background Noise in iMovie

To remove background noise in iMovie, start by selecting the clip you want to edit. Click on the "Audio" tab, then enable "Reduce Background Noise." Adjust the slider to find the right balance that minimizes unwanted sounds while retaining voice clarity. Preview the changes and make further adjustments if necessary.
