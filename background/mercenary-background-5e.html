Discover the Mercenary Background in D&D 5E

In Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition, a mercenary background offers characters a unique narrative, emphasizing their experience as soldiers for hire. This background provides skills in Athletics and Persuasion, along with proficiency in a gaming set. Players can create diverse personas, drawing on themes of loyalty, combat, and survival in any campaign.
