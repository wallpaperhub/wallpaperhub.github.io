Explore the Rich History of New Orleans

New Orleans, founded in 1718, is a vibrant city with a rich history shaped by French, Spanish, and African influences. Known for its unique culture, music, and cuisine, it has endured through wars, hurricanes, and cultural shifts. The historic French Quarter, jazz music, and Mardi Gras celebrations embody the city’s lively spirit.
