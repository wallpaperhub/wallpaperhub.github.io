US v. Nixon: Landmark Supreme Court Case Explained

The US v. Nixon case was a pivotal moment in American history, resulting from the Watergate scandal that questioned presidential authority. In 1974, the Supreme Court ruled that President Nixon must surrender tape recordings crucial to the investigation. This ruling emphasized the principle that no one, including the president, is above the law.
