Understanding Regulatory Signs: A Guide for Safe Driving

Regulatory signs are crucial elements of traffic management, providing essential information to ensure safe and efficient road use. They include speed limits, yield signs, and no parking indications, guiding drivers' behavior and promoting awareness. Compliance with these signs enhances road safety and minimizes accidents.
