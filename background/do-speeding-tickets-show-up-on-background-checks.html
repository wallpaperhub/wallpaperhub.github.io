Do Speeding Tickets Appear on Background Checks?

Speeding tickets generally do not show up on standard background checks, which typically focus on criminal records and employment history. However, if the background check is specifically for driving records, like those used by insurance companies or employers in transportation, they may be included. Always check local laws.
