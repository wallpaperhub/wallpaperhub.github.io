CPS Background Check: What It Includes

A CPS background check typically involves a review of criminal history, child abuse registry records, and other pertinent information to assess an individual's suitability for working with children. It may also include checks against national databases and interviews with references to ensure a comprehensive evaluation of the applicant's background.
