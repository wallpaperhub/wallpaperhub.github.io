Understanding Monarchy: Definition & Types

Monarchy is a form of governance where a single individual, the monarch, holds supreme authority, often hereditary. The monarch can be a king, queen, or emperor, and their powers may be absolute or constitutional, depending on the country's laws. This system emphasizes tradition, continuity, and formal leadership within a state's political structure.
