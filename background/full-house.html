Full House: The Ultimate Poker Combination

A "full house" refers to a winning hand in poker consisting of three cards of one rank and two cards of another rank. This powerful combination often leads to exciting gameplay and strategic decisions, captivating players at tables worldwide. Mastering the full house can enhance your poker skills and boost your chances of winning.
