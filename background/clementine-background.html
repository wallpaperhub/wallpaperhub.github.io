Explore the Sweet History of Clementines

Clementines are a beloved citrus fruit, known for their sweet, juicy segments and easy-to-peel skin. Originating from China, they gained popularity in the Mediterranean region. These seedless delights are often enjoyed fresh or in salads. Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, clementines are both a healthy snack and versatile ingredient.
