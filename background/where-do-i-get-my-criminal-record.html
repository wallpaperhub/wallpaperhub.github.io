How to Obtain Your Criminal Record Easily

To obtain your criminal record, start by contacting your local police department or state law enforcement agency. Many jurisdictions allow you to request a copy online, by mail, or in person. Additionally, you can check the court system where you reside. Be prepared to provide identification and pay any required fees.
