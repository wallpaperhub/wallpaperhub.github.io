The Unobserved Traits of Cosmic Microwave Background: What You Should Know

One characteristic not observed in the cosmic microwave background (CMB) is a uniform distribution of large-scale magnetic fields. The CMB is primarily isotropic, showcasing slight fluctuations in temperature, but does not exhibit significant evidence of magnetic influences, which is a notable absence in its properties and behavior.
