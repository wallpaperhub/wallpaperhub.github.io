Understanding Personas: The Key to Effective Marketing

A persona is a fictional representation of your ideal customer, created by gathering and analyzing data about your target audience. It includes demographic details, behaviors, motivations, and pain points, helping businesses tailor their marketing strategies. Understanding personas leads to more effective communication and engagement.
