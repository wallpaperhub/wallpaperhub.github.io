Fixing Greyed Out Background App Refresh

Background App Refresh allows apps to update content in the background, but sometimes it can appear greyed out. This typically occurs due to restrictions like low power mode, limited data usage settings, or if the device is connected to Wi-Fi only. To enable it, check your settings in the Privacy or General menus and adjust preferences accordingly.
