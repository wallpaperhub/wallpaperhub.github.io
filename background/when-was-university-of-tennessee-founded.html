History of the University of Tennessee: Founding Date

The University of Tennessee was founded in 1794 in Knoxville, making it one of the oldest public universities in the United States. Originally established as a small agricultural school, it has since evolved into a comprehensive institution, offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across various disciplines.
