Do Police Background Checks Include Internet History?

Police background checks generally focus on criminal records, employment history, and other direct behaviors related to the law. Internet history is typically not included unless it pertains to relevant criminal activity. Employers may conduct separate online searches for personal evaluations, but this is outside formal background checks.
