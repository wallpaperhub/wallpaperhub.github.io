Martin Luther King Jr.: The Value of Education in Social Change

Martin Luther King Jr. believed education was key to social change and empowerment. He advocated for equal educational opportunities for all, irrespective of race. Through his teachings, King inspired future generations to pursue knowledge and use it as a tool for justice, emphasizing that educated minds could lead to a more equitable society.
