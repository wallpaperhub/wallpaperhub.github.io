Bob Marley: The Reggae Legend Who Changed the World

Bob Marley, the legendary reggae musician, championed peace and love through his music. Born in Jamaica in 1945, he became a global icon with hits like "No Woman, No Cry" and "One Love." Marley's messages of unity resonate with fans today, as he inspires generations to embrace positivity and social change.
