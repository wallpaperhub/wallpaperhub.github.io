Explore the Magic of Figurative Language

Figurative language enriches writing by using creative expressions that go beyond literal meanings. It includes metaphors, similes, personification, and hyperbole, which help convey emotions and paint vivid images in readers' minds. Through these devices, writers can engage audiences, allowing them to experience the text more deeply.
