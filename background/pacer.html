Maximize Your Run: The Role of a Pacer

Pacer refers to a device or person that sets the pace for runners during races, ensuring they achieve their target times. In fitness training, a pacer is often used to maintain a steady speed, promoting endurance and efficiency. Utilizing a pacer can enhance performance, making it a valuable strategy for athletes and casual runners alike.
