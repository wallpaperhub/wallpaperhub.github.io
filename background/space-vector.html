Understanding Space Vector: A Simple Guide

Space vector refers to a mathematical representation combining direction and magnitude, often utilized in physics and engineering. It conveys key information about position, velocity, or force within a defined space. By understanding space vectors, one can analyze movement or forces more effectively, paving the way for innovations in technology and exploration.
