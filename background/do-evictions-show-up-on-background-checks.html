Do Evictions Appear on Background Checks?

Evictions can appear on background checks, potentially affecting your rental or housing applications. Landlords often use background checks to assess a tenant's rental history and financial responsibility. An eviction record may indicate risks, so addressing any past evictions proactively can help you secure housing more easily.
