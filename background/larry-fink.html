Larry Fink: Leader in Sustainable Finance

Larry Fink is the CEO of BlackRock, the world’s largest asset manager, overseeing over $8 trillion in assets. Known for his influence in finance and advocacy for corporate responsibility, Fink emphasizes sustainable investing and the importance of addressing climate change. His annual letters to CEOs frequently spotlight these issues.
