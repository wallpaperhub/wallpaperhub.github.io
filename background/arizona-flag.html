Discover the Iconic Arizona Flag: A Symbol of Heritage

The Arizona flag features a bold design with a copper star at the center, representing the state's copper industry. Surrounding the star are red and yellow rays symbolizing the setting sun, reflecting Arizona's vibrant sunsets. The blue field at the bottom embodies the sky. This flag captures the essence of Arizona's landscape and heritage.
