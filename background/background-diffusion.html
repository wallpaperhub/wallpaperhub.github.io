Understanding Background Diffusion: A Quick Guide

Background diffusion refers to the subtle spreading of light or sound through a medium, creating an ambient effect. This phenomenon can enhance various spaces, such as art installations and architectural designs, contributing to the overall atmosphere. Understanding background diffusion can enrich experiences in both visual and auditory contexts.
