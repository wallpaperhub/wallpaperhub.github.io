Effective Ways to Eliminate Background Noise on Your Microphone

To reduce background noise on your microphone, start by choosing a quiet location for recording. Utilize noise-canceling microphones and adjust levels in software settings to minimize pick-up. Use audio editing software to remove background noise after recording, and consider using pop filters for clearer sound.
