When Was Red Bull Invented? The Story Behind the Energy Drink

Red Bull was invented in 1984 by Dietrich Mateschitz and Chaleo Yoovidhya. It was inspired by a Thai energy drink called Krating Daeng, which translates to "Red Bull" in English. Mateschitz adapted the formula and branding for Western markets, launching the drink in Austria, paving the way for the global energy drink phenomenon.
