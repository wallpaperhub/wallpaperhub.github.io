Bad Bunny: The Rise of a Reggaeton Icon

Bad Bunny is a revolutionary Puerto Rican artist, blending reggaeton, trap, and Latin music to create infectious beats. Emerging from San Juan, he gained massive popularity with hits like "Soy Peor" and "DÁKITI." His unique style and fashion sense have made him a trendsetter, influencing a new generation of musicians and fans alike.
