Lockheed Martin: A Century of Aerospace Innovation

Lockheed Martin, founded in 1912, has evolved from a small aircraft manufacturer into a global leader in aerospace and defense. The company's history is marked by innovative designs, including the P-38 Lightning and the F-22 Raptor. Over the decades, it has merged with key firms, expanding its footprint and technology in national security.
