Discover the Rich Heritage of Samoan Tribes

The Samoan tribal background is rich with heritage, traditions, and community values. Each tribe, or "aiga," plays a crucial role in preserving customs, languages, and art forms. Samoan culture emphasizes familial connections and respects elders, evident in ceremonies and daily life. Through storytelling, dance, and crafts, they celebrate their identity.
