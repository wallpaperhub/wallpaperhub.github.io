Discover the Unique Texture of Kraft Paper

Kraft paper texture is characterized by its rustic and natural appearance, featuring a coarse, fibrous surface that exudes durability. Often a light brown hue, this eco-friendly paper is made from unbleached pulp, enhancing its organic feel. Ideal for packaging, crafts, and artistic projects, it offers a unique tactile experience.
