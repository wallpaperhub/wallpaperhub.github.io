Explore Yosemite National Park: A Journey Through History

Yosemite National Park, established in 1890, boasts a rich history tied to the preservation of natural beauty. Initially a protected area in the 1860s, it became America's first state park. John Muir's advocacy in the late 19th century played a crucial role in its designation as a national park, inspiring conservation efforts nationwide.
