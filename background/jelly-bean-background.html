Discover the Fun World of Jelly Beans

Jelly beans have a colorful and sweet history, originating in the United States in the 19th century. These candies, with their chewy texture and distinct flavors, became a favorite during the 1930s. They are often associated with holidays like Easter, enjoyed for their vibrant colors and playful nature, making them a beloved treat worldwide.
