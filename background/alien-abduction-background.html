Alien Abduction: Encounters Beyond Earth

Alien abduction refers to the phenomenon where individuals claim to have been kidnapped or taken aboard extraterrestrial spacecraft. These reports typically include descriptions of strange environments, encounters with aliens, and various examinations. The concept has captivated public imagination since the mid-20th century, inspiring numerous books, films, and discussions about the existence of intelligent life beyond Earth.
