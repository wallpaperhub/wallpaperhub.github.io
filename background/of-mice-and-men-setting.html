Of Mice and Men: Exploring Its Setting

The setting of "Of Mice and Men" is during the Great Depression in California's Salinas Valley. It highlights both the beauty of nature and the harsh realities of the time. The ranch serves as the primary backdrop, symbolizing hope and the American Dream, while also showcasing the loneliness and struggles faced by itinerant workers.
