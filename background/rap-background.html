Discover the Origins and Evolution of Rap

Rap originated in the 1970s in the Bronx, New York City, as a cultural expression of marginalized communities. Rooted in hip-hop culture, it combines rhythmic speech with beats, reflecting social issues, personal stories, and community pride. Pioneers like DJ Kool Herc and Grandmaster Flash laid the groundwork for what would become a global phenomenon.
