How Far Back Do Security Guard Background Checks Go?

Security guard background checks typically go back seven years, although some states allow up to ten years. Employers may check criminal history, employment records, and credit history. The depth of the check can depend on state laws, the hiring organization's policy, and the specific role. Always verify requirements in your area.
