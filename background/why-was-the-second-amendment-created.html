Understanding the Purpose of the Second Amendment

The Second Amendment was created to ensure the right of individuals to bear arms, emphasizing the importance of a well-regulated militia for security. It reflects the founders' desire to protect citizens from tyranny and enable self-defense. The amendment represents a balance between governmental authority and personal freedoms.
