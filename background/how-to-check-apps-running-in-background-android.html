Check Background Apps on Android: A Quick Guide

To check apps running in the background on your Android device, open the Settings app, scroll down, and tap on "Apps" or "Applications." From there, select "Running" or "App info." You’ll see a list of active apps, allowing you to manage them by stopping or uninstalling those you don’t need, improving performance and battery life.
