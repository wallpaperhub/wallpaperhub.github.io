Understanding DISA Background Check Levels

DISA background check levels vary based on the position and industry requirements. Common levels include standard checks, which cover criminal history and employment verification, and more comprehensive checks, which may include credit history and drug testing. Understanding these levels is crucial for employers in ensuring a safe hiring process.
