Where to Find Your Desktop Backgrounds

Desktop backgrounds are typically stored in specific folders dictated by the operating system. For Windows, default wallpapers can be found in the C:\Windows\Web\Wallpaper directory. On macOS, they are located in the /Library/Desktop Pictures folder. Users can also set custom backgrounds stored in personal directories.
