Understanding Police Brutality: Causes & Solutions

Police brutality is a critical issue that raises significant concerns about the misuse of power by law enforcement. This unjust violence often targets marginalized communities, leading to severe physical and psychological consequences. Society must address these systemic issues through reform, accountability, and advocacy for equitable treatment.
