How to Create a Transparent Background in DaVinci Resolve

In DaVinci Resolve, creating a transparent background is essential for professional video editing. You can achieve this by using the Alpha channel in the Color tab, making certain clips transparent. Import footage with alpha transparency or export your final video in formats like PNG or MOV to maintain transparency. Explore these features for stunning results!
