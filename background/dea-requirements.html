Essential DEA Requirements for Compliance

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) requirements outline the necessary criteria for individuals and organizations wishing to handle controlled substances. Key requirements include registration, compliance with federal and state laws, maintaining records, and ensuring proper storage and security of substances. Staying informed about regulations is crucial for compliance.
