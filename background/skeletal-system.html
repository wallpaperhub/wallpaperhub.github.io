Understanding the Skeletal System: A Quick Guide

The skeletal system is the framework of bones and cartilage that supports the body, protects vital organs, and facilitates movement. It consists of 206 bones in adults, providing structure and shape. Bone marrow within these bones produces blood cells, while joints allow for flexibility and mobility. Regular exercise aids in maintaining bone health.
