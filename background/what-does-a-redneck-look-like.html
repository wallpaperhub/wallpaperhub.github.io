What Does a Redneck Look Like? Unpacking the Stereotypes

A "redneck" typically embodies a rustic lifestyle, often associated with rural areas in the Southern United States. Commonly, they might be seen wearing faded jeans, flannel shirts, and baseball caps. Their interests often include hunting, fishing, and enjoying outdoor activities, reflecting a hardworking, down-to-earth mindset.
