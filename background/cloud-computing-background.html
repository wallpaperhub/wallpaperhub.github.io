Understanding Cloud Computing: A Brief Background

Cloud computing has revolutionized how businesses manage data and resources. Emerging in the 1960s, it gained momentum in the 2000s with the rise of the internet. By providing scalable solutions, it enables on-demand access to computing power, storage, and services, allowing organizations to innovate and optimize operations efficiently.
