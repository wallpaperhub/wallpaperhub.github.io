How to Darken Images for Impactful Photography

Darkening an image can enhance its mood and depth, making it more visually engaging. It is particularly useful for creating dramatic effects or highlighting specific subjects within a photo. Techniques such as adjusting brightness, contrast, or using filters can be applied to achieve the desired darkened effect, allowing for creativity in image editing.
