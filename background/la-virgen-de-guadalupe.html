La Virgen de Guadalupe: Symbol of Faith and Hope

La Virgen de Guadalupe, emblematic of Mexican culture and faith, is venerated as the patroness of Mexico and the Americas. Her image, depicting Mary with indigenous features, symbolizes unity and hope. Celebrated on December 12, she inspires millions, embodying compassion, strength, and divine protection for her followers.
