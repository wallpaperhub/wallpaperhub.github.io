When Was Football Invented? Discover Its Origins!

Football's origins trace back to ancient civilizations, with various forms of ball games played in China, Greece, and Mesoamerica. However, modern football, known as soccer in some regions, was formalized in the 19th century, particularly with the establishment of the English Football Association in 1863, which set the groundwork for today's game.
