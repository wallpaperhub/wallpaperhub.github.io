Discovering Houston: A Brief History

Houston's history began in 1836 when founders John Kirby Allen and Augustus Chapman Allen established the city near Buffalo Bayou. Initially a trading hub, it became a vital center for industry and commerce. The discovery of oil in the early 20th century accelerated its growth, leading to its current status as a diverse and vibrant metropolis.
