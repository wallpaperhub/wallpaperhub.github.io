Discover the Delicious History of Peanut Butter

Peanut butter, a beloved spread, has roots dating back to ancient civilizations that ground peanuts into a paste. Popularized in the 19th century, it gained fame with the invention of commercial peanut butter in the early 1900s. Today, it’s celebrated for its rich flavor, versatility, and nutritional benefits, appealing to all ages.
