Effective Background Noise Solutions for Tinnitus Relief

Background noise can be a helpful tool for managing tinnitus, a condition causing persistent ringing or buzzing in the ears. Many find that white noise, nature sounds, or soft music can mask disruptive noises, making it easier to relax and focus. This technique aims to distract the brain from tinnitus, providing symptomatic relief.
