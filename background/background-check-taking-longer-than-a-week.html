Why Is My Background Check Taking So Long?

When a background check takes longer than a week, it can be frustrating for candidates eager to secure a job. This delay can be due to various factors, such as the complexity of the applicant's history, verification of educational credentials, or a high volume of requests at the background check company. Communication is key; staying in touch with potential employers can help manage expectations during this waiting period.
