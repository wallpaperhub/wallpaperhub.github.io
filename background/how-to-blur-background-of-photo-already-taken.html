Blur Background of Photos: Quick Guide

To blur the background of a photo you've already taken, you can use photo editing software or mobile apps like Snapseed or Adobe Lightroom. Open your image, select the background area using selection tools, and apply a Gaussian blur or similar effect. Adjust the intensity to achieve the desired look, enhancing focus on your subject.
