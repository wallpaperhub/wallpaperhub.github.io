School Uniforms: Benefits and Importance

School uniforms are standardized clothing worn by students in educational institutions. They promote a sense of unity, reduce peer pressure, and can enhance focus on learning by minimizing distractions. Typically, uniforms consist of specific colors, styles, and logos that represent the school, fostering school spirit and identity.
