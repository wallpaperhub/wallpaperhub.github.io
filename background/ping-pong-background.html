Exploring the Rich History of Ping Pong

Ping pong, also known as table tennis, has a rich history dating back to the late 19th century in England. Initially a parlor game for the elite, it quickly gained popularity, evolving into a competitive sport. Today, it features fast-paced gameplay and international tournaments, captivating millions globally.
