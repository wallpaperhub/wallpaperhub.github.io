Understanding the Phi Beta Sigma Symbol: History and Meaning

The Phi Beta Sigma symbol holds deep significance for members of this historic fraternity, representing brotherhood, scholarship, and service. This emblematic logo features a blue and white design, symbolizing the ideals and values upheld by its members since the fraternity's founding in 1914. It unites brothers across campuses, encouraging a strong sense of community and commitment.
