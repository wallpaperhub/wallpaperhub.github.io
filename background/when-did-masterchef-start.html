When Did MasterChef Start? Exploring the Origins of the Culinary Show

MasterChef, the popular cooking competition show, first premiered in the UK on July 2, 1990. Created by Rebecca Bland and featuring judges with culinary expertise, the series has since inspired numerous international adaptations. Over the years, it has captivated audiences with its thrilling challenges and emotional moments.
