Gun Purchase Background Checks: What You Need to Know

When it comes to purchasing firearms in the United States, background check requirements vary by state. Some states mandate thorough background checks for all gun sales, while others have loopholes allowing private sales without checks. Understanding state laws is crucial for lawful gun ownership and ensuring public safety.
