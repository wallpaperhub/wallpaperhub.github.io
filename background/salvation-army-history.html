The History of The Salvation Army: A Legacy of Hope

The Salvation Army was founded in 1865 by William Booth in London, aiming to help the impoverished and marginalized. Initially a – "Christian Mission," it grew into a worldwide organization providing practical aid, spiritual support, and community programs. Its dedication to social justice has made it an enduring force for good across nations.
