Understanding Gamecock Heritage: History & Breeds

Gamecocks, or fighting cocks, are specially bred birds for the sport of cockfighting. Their history dates back to ancient civilizations, where they were revered for their strength and fighting prowess. Various breeds exist, each with unique characteristics, reflecting regional preferences and breeding techniques. Their culture is rich but controversial, as it raises ethical questions about animal welfare.
