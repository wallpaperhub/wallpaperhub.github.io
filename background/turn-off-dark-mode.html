How to Turn Off Dark Mode: Easy Guide

Turning off dark mode can enhance visibility and reduce eye strain in bright environments. To switch back to light mode, navigate to your device's settings, find the display options, and select 'Light Mode.' Don't forget to check individual apps as they may have their own settings! Enjoy better readability!
