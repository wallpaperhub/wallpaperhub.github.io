Celebrate St. Nicholas: The Legacy of Gift-Giving

St. Nicholas, known for his generosity and kindness, is the inspiration behind the modern-day Santa Claus. Born in the 4th century in Patara, he became the Bishop of Myra and performed many miracles, often secretly helping those in need. His legacy of gift-giving continues globally, celebrated on December 6th each year.
