Understanding Background Checks: Types & Differences

Not all background checks are the same. They vary based on the purpose, the depth of investigation, and the type of information being sought. Employment checks often include criminal records, credit history, and employment verification, while tenant screening may focus on rental history. Always tailor the check to your specific needs.
