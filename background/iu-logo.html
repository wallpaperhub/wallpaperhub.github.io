IU Logo: Significance and Design Explained

The IU logo, representing Indiana University, features a bold, crimson "I" entwined with a "U," symbolizing unity and tradition. This iconic design reflects the school's pride and heritage, commonly associated with its athletic teams and academic excellence. Recognizable and meaningful, the IU logo stands as a beacon for students and alumni alike.
