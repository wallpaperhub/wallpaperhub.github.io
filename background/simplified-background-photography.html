Simplified Background Photography: Focus on Your Subject

Simplified background photography focuses on using minimalistic and uncluttered backdrops to draw attention to the subject. By employing solid colors, soft gradients, or subtle textures, photographers can create a professional look without distractions. This technique enhances the visual appeal and keeps the viewer's focus where it belongs.
