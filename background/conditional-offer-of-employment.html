Understanding Conditional Offers of Employment: What You Need to Know

A conditional offer of employment is a job offer that is dependent upon specific conditions being met. These may include background checks, reference verification, or successful completion of a probation period. Such offers protect employers and ensure that ideal candidates are eligible based on professional qualifications and personal integrity.
