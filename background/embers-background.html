Embers: The Afterglow of Fire's Magic

Embers are the glowing remnants of a fire, offering warmth and light long after the flames have diminished. They symbolize transformation and the potential for new beginnings. In various cultures, embers are associated with nostalgia, storytelling, and the cyclical nature of life, reflecting both the end of a phase and the spark of what’s to come.
