El Paso Shooting: Tragedy and Impact

On August 3, 2019, a tragic mass shooting occurred at a Walmart in El Paso, Texas, leading to the deaths of 23 people and injuring many others. The shooter targeted a predominantly Hispanic community, prompting national conversations about gun control, immigration, and hate crimes. This devastating event highlights the urgent need for societal change.
