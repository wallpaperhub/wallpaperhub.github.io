Understanding Abigail Williams: The Girl Behind the Salem Witch Trials

Abigail Williams, a key figure in the Salem witch trials of 1692, was a young girl whose accusations fueled a notorious period of mass hysteria. Her claims led to the execution of several innocent people, significantly impacting the community. Abigail's story serves as a cautionary tale about the dangers of unchecked fear and deception.
