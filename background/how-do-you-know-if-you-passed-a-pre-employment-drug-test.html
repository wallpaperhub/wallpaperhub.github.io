How to Confirm Your Pre-Employment Drug Test Results

To know if you've passed a pre-employment drug test, you'll typically wait for the employer or testing facility to provide results. Some companies offer a follow-up call or email, usually within a week. If you haven’t heard back after the specified time, it’s appropriate to inquire about your results directly. Staying informed and proactive can help ease concerns.
