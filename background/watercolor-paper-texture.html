Choosing the Right Watercolor Paper Texture for Your Art

Watercolor paper texture plays a crucial role in how your artwork will turn out. Its surface can be rough, cold-pressed, or smooth, affecting the way paint interacts with the paper. Rough textures hold more water and pigment, creating depth, while smoother finishes facilitate fine details. Choosing the right texture can elevate your painting experience.
