AT&T: A Century-Long Journey in Telecommunications

AT&T's history dates back to 1885 when Alexander Graham Bell founded the American Telephone and Telegraph Company. Initially focused on telephone service, it expanded through acquisitions, becoming a telecommunications giant. In the late 20th century, AT&T underwent major restructuring and diversification, also playing a pivotal role in mobile technology and internet services.
