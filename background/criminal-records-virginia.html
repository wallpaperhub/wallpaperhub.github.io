Understanding Criminal Records in Virginia

Criminal records in Virginia detail an individual's history of arrests, charges, and convictions. These records can significantly impact various areas of life, including employment and housing opportunities. Individuals can request their own records through the Virginia State Police or review them online, aiding in understanding their legal standing.
