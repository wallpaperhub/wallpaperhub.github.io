Understanding TSA Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A TSA background check is a security screening process mandated by the Transportation Security Administration (TSA) for individuals seeking access to secure areas of airports and certain transportation facilities. It involves verifying identity, checking criminal history, and assessing eligibility for security clearance to ensure passenger safety.
