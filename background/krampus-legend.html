"Unveiling the Krampus Legend: The Dark Side of Christmas"

The Krampus legend is a captivating folklore figure from Central European Christmas traditions. Described as a horned, devilish creature, Krampus punishes naughty children, contrasting with Saint Nicholas’s kind gifts. This dark yet intriguing character serves as a reminder of moral behavior during the festive season, adding a unique twist to holiday tales.
