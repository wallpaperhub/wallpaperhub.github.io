Masters of the Universe: A Timeless Epic

Masters of the Universe is a popular toy and media franchise launched in the early 1980s by Mattel. It features heroic figures like He-Man battling against the evil Skeletor on the planet Eternia. The franchise has spawned animated series, comics, movies, and a dedicated fan base, becoming a timeless icon of pop culture.
