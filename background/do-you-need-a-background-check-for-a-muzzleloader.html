Do You Need a Background Check for Muzzleloaders?

While laws vary by state, generally, a background check is not required for purchasing a muzzleloader. Since they are classified differently than modern firearms, many jurisdictions apply different regulations. However, it's crucial to check local laws to ensure compliance and to promote responsible ownership.
