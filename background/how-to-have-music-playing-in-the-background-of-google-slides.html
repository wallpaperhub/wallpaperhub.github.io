How to Add Background Music to Google Slides

To play music in the background of Google Slides, first upload your audio file to Google Drive. Next, open your presentation and select the slide where you want the music to start. Click on "Insert," choose "Audio," and select your file. Adjust the playback options under the Format options menu to play across slides and loop if desired.
