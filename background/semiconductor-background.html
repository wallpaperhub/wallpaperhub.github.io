Understanding Semiconductors: The Backbone of Modern Electronics

Semiconductors are materials with electrical conductivity between conductors and insulators. They are crucial for modern electronics, found in devices like smartphones, computers, and solar cells. The semiconductor industry drives innovation, enabling advancements in technology and communication by controlling electrical currents with precision.
