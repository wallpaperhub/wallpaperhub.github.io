Twin Towers: A Symbol of Resilience

The Twin Towers, iconic symbols of New York City, stood as architectural marvels in Lower Manhattan. Completed in 1976, they were among the tallest buildings in the world. Tragically, they were destroyed in the September 11 attacks in 2001. Their legacy lives on through memorials and new construction, symbolizing resilience and hope.
