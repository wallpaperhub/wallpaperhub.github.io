Tobacco Through Time: A Brief History

Tobacco has a rich history that spans centuries, originating in the Americas where indigenous peoples cultivated it for ceremonial and medicinal purposes. In the 16th century, European explorers introduced tobacco to the Old World, triggering its popularity. By the 19th century, it became a global commodity, leading to health debates and regulations.
