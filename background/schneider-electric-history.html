Discover Schneider Electric's Transformation: From Steel to Smart Energy Solutions

Schneider Electric was founded in 1836 in France as a steel company. Over the decades, it transformed through acquisitions and innovations in energy management, automation solutions, and smart technology. The company has focused on sustainability and efficiency, becoming a global leader in promoting renewable energy and smart grids.
