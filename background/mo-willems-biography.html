Discover Mo Willems: Renowned Children's Author and Illustrator

Mo Willems is a celebrated author and illustrator known for his impactful children's books, including the beloved "Don't Let the Pigeon Drive the Bus!" and the "Elephant & Piggie" series. Born on February 11, 1968, in Chicago, Willems began his career as a writer and animator for "Sesame Street," earning multiple Emmy Awards. His whimsical storytelling and unique art style have captivated young readers worldwide, making him a prominent figure in children's literature.
