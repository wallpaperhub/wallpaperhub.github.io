IKEA's Remarkable Journey: From Small Beginnings to Global Home Furnishing Giant

Founded in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad in Sweden, IKEA began as a small mail-order company selling pens and wallets. Over the years, it evolved into a global leader in home furnishings, known for its modern designs, affordable prices, and do-it-yourself assembly furniture. The brand’s commitment to sustainability and innovation continues to shape its legacy.
