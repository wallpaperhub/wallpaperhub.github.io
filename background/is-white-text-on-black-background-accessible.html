Is White Text on a Black Background Accessible?

White text on a black background can be accessible, but it depends on the contrast ratio. Proper contrast enhances readability for most users, including those with visual impairments. However, some individuals may find high-contrast combinations uncomfortable or difficult to read. It's essential to consider user preferences and conduct usability testing.
