Edit Photo Backgrounds on iPhone: Quick Guide

To edit the background of a photo on your iPhone, start by opening the Photos app and selecting the image. Tap "Edit," then use the "Markup" tool for basic edits. For more advanced changes, download apps like Snapseed or PicsArt. These apps let you easily cut out backgrounds, replace them, and adjust colors for a perfect finish.
