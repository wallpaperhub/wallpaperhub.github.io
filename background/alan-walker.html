Discover Alan Walker: The Sound Behind "Faded"

Alan Walker is a British-Norwegian DJ and music producer best known for his hit single "Faded," which topped charts worldwide. With his signature hoodie and mask style, Walker blends electronic music with the emotional depth of pop. His unique sound and engaging visuals have garnered a massive global following.
