Understanding the History of Epilepsy: From Ancient Times to Today

Epilepsy is one of the oldest documented medical conditions, with mentions in ancient texts like the Babylonian Talmud and writings of Hippocrates. Initially attributed to supernatural forces, it was later recognized as a neurological disorder. Advances in understanding have evolved from trepanation in antiquity to modern treatments, including medications and surgery.
