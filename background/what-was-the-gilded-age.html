The Gilded Age: A Era of Prosperity and Inequality

The Gilded Age, spanning the late 19th century, was marked by rapid industrialization, economic growth, and significant social change in the United States. This era saw the rise of powerful tycoons, urbanization, and stark wealth disparities, while also laying the groundwork for labor movements and reforms that would follow.
