Explore the FFA Timeline: Key Events in Agricultural Education

The Future Farmers of America (FFA) experienced remarkable milestones, beginning in 1928 when it was founded. In 1950, it gained federal charter status. The National FFA Convention and Expo have been critical since the first assembly in 1926. The organization has evolved, embracing technology and diversity, continually shaping leaders in agriculture.
