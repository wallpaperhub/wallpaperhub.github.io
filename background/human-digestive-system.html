Understanding the Human Digestive System

The human digestive system is a complex network that processes food, absorbs nutrients, and eliminates waste. It begins in the mouth, where food is chewed and mixed with saliva. The esophagus carries food to the stomach, where acids break it down. Nutrient absorption occurs mainly in the small intestine, with waste exiting through the large intestine.
