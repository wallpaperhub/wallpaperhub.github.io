Discover the History of Romanticism: Key Figures & Ideas

Romanticism emerged in the late 18th century as a reaction against the Enlightenment's focus on reason. It celebrated individualism, nature, and emotion, profoundly influencing art, literature, and music. Key figures include Wordsworth, Shelley, and Beethoven, who sought to convey deeper truths through personal expression and the sublime beauty of the natural world.
