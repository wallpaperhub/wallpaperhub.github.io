Exploring the Roots of Early Childhood Education

Early childhood education dates back to ancient civilizations, where basic learning began at home or in community settings. Influential figures like Froebel and Montessori emphasized play-based learning and child-centered approaches in the 19th century. This foundation has evolved into diverse educational systems, shaping modern pedagogy.
