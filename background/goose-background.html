Understanding Goose Background: Fascinating Facts and Ecology

Geese are fascinating waterfowl known for their loud honking and strong migratory instincts. They typically feature a distinctive black neck, white cheeks, and a robust body. Found in various habitats, geese are social birds that often travel in families. Their presence greatly influences ecosystems, helping with the dispersal of seeds and maintaining wetland health.
