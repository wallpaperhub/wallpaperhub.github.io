Unlocking Background Knowledge for Effective Learning

Activating background knowledge is essential for learning. It connects new information to what learners already know, enhancing comprehension and retention. Techniques include asking open-ended questions, using graphic organizers, and encouraging personal reflections. These strategies help learners build a mental framework to integrate new concepts effectively.
