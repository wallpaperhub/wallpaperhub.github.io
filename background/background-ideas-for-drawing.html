Creative Background Ideas for Drawings

When choosing a background for your drawing, consider a dreamy forest with fantasy elements, a vibrant city skyline at sunset, or serene mountains reflecting in water. You can also explore abstract designs using bold colors or soft pastels for emotional impact. Let your imagination guide the setting to enrich your main subject!
