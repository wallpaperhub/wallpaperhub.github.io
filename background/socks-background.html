The Fascinating History and Style of Socks

Socks have a rich history, evolving from practical wear to fashion statements. Originally crafted from animal skins, they became popular in the Middle Ages with knitted wool. Today, socks come in various materials, styles, and patterns, catering to comfort and personal expression. They are essential for everyday life, sport, and formal occasions.
