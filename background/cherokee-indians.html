Discovering the Rich Heritage of the Cherokee Indians

The Cherokee Indians, one of the largest Native American tribes, have a rich history dating back thousands of years. Initially located in the southeastern United States, they faced dislocation during the Trail of Tears in the 1830s. Today, the Cherokee Nation preserves its culture through language, arts, and community traditions, showcasing resilience and heritage.
