Why Employers Can Refuse Your Application If You Skip Screening

Refusing a drug screening test or background check can lead to an employer deciding against hiring you. Many companies implement these checks to protect their workplace and ensure a safe environment. Candidates unwilling to comply may be viewed as a risk, reducing their chances of securing the job.
