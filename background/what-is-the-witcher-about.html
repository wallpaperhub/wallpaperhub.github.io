"The Witcher: Fantasy, Monsters, and Moral Dilemmas"

"The Witcher" is a fantasy series centered around Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a witcher. Set in a richly built world filled with magic, mythical creatures, and political intrigue, the story delves into Geralt's battles against both beasts and human conflicts, exploring themes of morality, destiny, and belonging.
