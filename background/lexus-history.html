Discover the Remarkable History of Lexus

Lexus, the luxury vehicle division of Toyota, was founded in 1989. Its mission was to create a high-end car that could rival European brands. The first model, the LS 400, was launched in 1989, setting new standards for comfort and reliability. Over the years, Lexus has expanded its lineup, introducing hybrid models and innovative technology, earning a reputation for luxury and durability.
