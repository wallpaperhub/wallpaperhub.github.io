Understanding Trauma: Healing and Resilience

Trauma can deeply affect individuals, altering their emotional, psychological, and physical well-being. It often stems from distressing experiences, such as accidents, abuse, or loss. Recovery requires time, support, and coping strategies, emphasizing the need for understanding and professional help to heal and rebuild resilience.
