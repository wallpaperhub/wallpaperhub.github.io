Do Unfounded CPS Cases Impact Your Record?

An unfounded Child Protective Services (CPS) case typically does not result in a permanent record on your criminal history. However, it may still be accessible in certain situations, such as during background checks for employment or custody hearings. It's essential to understand your rights and seek legal advice for the best course of action.
