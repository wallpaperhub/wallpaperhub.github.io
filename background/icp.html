Understanding ICP: Unearth the Musical Madness

ICP, or Insane Clown Posse, is a hip-hop duo known for their theatrical style and dedicated fanbase. Originating from Detroit, their unique blend of horrorcore and hip-hop challenges norms through wild performances, vibrant visuals, and deep storytelling. They have significantly impacted underground culture, creating a loyal community around their music.
