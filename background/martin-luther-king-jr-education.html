Education: The Foundation of Martin Luther King Jr.'s Legacy

Martin Luther King Jr. emphasized the importance of education in achieving equality and justice. He believed that education empowers individuals to challenge oppression and promotes social change. King himself was a highly educated man with a doctorate, advocating for the transformative power of knowledge in American society.
