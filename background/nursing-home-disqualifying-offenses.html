Nursing Home Disqualifying Offenses: What You Need to Know

Nursing home disqualifying offenses refer to specific criminal activities that can prevent individuals from working in long-term care facilities. These offenses usually include serious crimes such as abuse, neglect, exploitation, and violent behavior. Regulatory bodies assess applicants to ensure the safety and well-being of residents in care.
