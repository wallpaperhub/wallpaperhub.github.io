Exploring the Rich History of Manors

Manors have long been symbols of wealth and power, often set in sprawling estates with grand architecture. Originating in the medieval period, these residences hosted nobility and were central to agricultural economies. Their intriguing history includes lavish parties and significant social dynamics, capturing the essence of noble life.
