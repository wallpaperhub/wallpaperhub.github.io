Discover the Refreshing Benefits of Peppermint

Peppermint, a hybrid of watermint and spearmint, is cherished for its invigorating aroma and flavor. Native to Europe, it thrives in temperate regions and is often cultivated for culinary and medicinal uses. Rich in menthol, peppermint offers soothing effects, making it popular in teas, desserts, and natural remedies.
