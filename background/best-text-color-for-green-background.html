Best Text Color for Green Background: A Simple Guide

When choosing the best text color for a green background, high contrast is crucial for readability. White or light gray offer clarity and visibility, while black or dark blue can create a striking contrast. Consider softer greens with darker text for a cohesive, harmonious look. Test combinations to ensure legibility!
