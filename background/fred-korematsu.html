Remembering Fred Korematsu: A Civil Rights Pioneer

Fred Korematsu was an American civil rights activist known for resisting the internment of Japanese Americans during World War II. His courageous stand against the government’s unjust policies led to a landmark Supreme Court case, which initially upheld the internment. Today, Korematsu is celebrated for his fight for justice and civil liberties.
