Insights from the Book of Micah: Justice & Prophecy

Micah is a prophetic book in the Old Testament, emphasizing justice, mercy, and humility. It presents God’s messages through Micah, addressing social injustices and calling for true worship. Notable for the prophecy of the Messiah's birthplace, it challenges believers to act justly and demonstrate compassion in their daily lives.
