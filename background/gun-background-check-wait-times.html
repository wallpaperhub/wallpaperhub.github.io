Understanding Gun Background Check Wait Times

Gun background check wait times vary significantly based on factors like state regulations, the number of checks being processed, and the system used by the FBI. In many cases, these checks can take just a few minutes, but high traffic periods can extend wait times to days. It’s essential for buyers to be aware of potential delays when purchasing firearms.
