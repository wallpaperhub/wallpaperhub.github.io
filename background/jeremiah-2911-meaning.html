Understanding Jeremiah 29:11: God’s Plan for Your Future

Jeremiah 29:11 is a powerful reminder that God has a purpose for each of us. This verse emphasizes that God plans for our welfare and future, not for harm. It offers hope and assurance during challenging times, encouraging believers to trust in divine guidance and the promise of restoration and a brighter tomorrow.
