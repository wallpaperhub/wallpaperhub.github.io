Exploring the Colorful World of Monsters, Inc.

Monsters, Inc. is an animated film produced by Pixar and released by Disney in 2001. The story is set in a vibrant monster world where creatures generate energy from children's screams. The film follows Mike and Sulley, top "scarers," who embark on an adventure that challenges their perceptions and highlights the power of laughter over fear.
