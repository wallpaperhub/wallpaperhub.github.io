"Symbolism of the Eagle in the U.S. Flag"

The United States flag features a prominent bald eagle, a symbol of strength and freedom. This majestic bird embodies the spirit of the nation, representing courage and resilience. The eagle's association with the flag not only signifies independence but also reminds us of the values and ideals upon which the country was founded.
