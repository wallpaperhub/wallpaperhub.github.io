How a Misdemeanor Affects Employment Opportunities

A misdemeanor can potentially impact employment opportunities, especially in fields requiring background checks. Employers may view misdemeanors unfavorably, depending on the nature of the offense and the job's requirements. However, many companies consider the applicant's skills, experience, and overall character too, offering a chance for rehabilitation.
