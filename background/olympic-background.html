Discover the Rich History of the Olympics

The Olympic Games have a rich history that dates back to ancient Greece around 776 BC, celebrating athletic prowess and promoting peace among city-states. Revived in 1896, the modern Olympics unite nations every four years, showcasing diverse sports and cultures, and promoting values such as excellence, friendship, and respect.
