Understanding Hosanna: The Significance of Palm Sunday

On Palm Sunday, Christians commemorate Jesus’ triumphant entry into Jerusalem, where crowds welcomed Him with palm branches, symbolizing victory and peace. The use of palms has deep biblical roots, reflecting ancient customs. This day marks the beginning of Holy Week, leading to Easter, and invites reflection on Jesus' journey.
