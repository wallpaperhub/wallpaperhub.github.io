Unraveling Crime: The CSI Background Explained

CSI (Crime Scene Investigation) is a crucial field in forensic science. It involves the meticulous process of collecting, analyzing, and preserving evidence from crime scenes. Professionals, known as CSIs, use various scientific techniques to reconstruct events, identify suspects, and aid in legal proceedings. Their work ensures justice and enhances public safety.
