Coco Chanel: The Icon of Timeless Fashion

Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel was a revolutionary fashion designer who transformed women's style in the early 20th century. Known for her timeless designs, such as the Chanel No. 5 perfume and the classic Chanel suit, she championed simplicity, elegance, and luxury. Chanel's influence endures, shaping modern fashion and empowering women worldwide.
