Close Background Apps for Better Performance

To enhance your device's performance and battery life, it's essential to close all apps running in the background. Background apps consume resources, slowing down your device. Simply swipe them away or use your task manager to close unnecessary applications. This simple step can lead to faster processing and a smoother user experience.
