Accessible Design: White Text on Black Background

White text on a black background is popular for its high contrast, but it's essential to consider accessibility. Ensure sufficient contrast ratios (at least 4.5:1) for readability. Additionally, test visibility for users with visual impairments, and provide alternatives for disabled users to enhance website usability.
