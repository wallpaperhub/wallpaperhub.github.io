Mastering the Guild Artisan Background in D&D 5e

In Dungeons & Dragons 5e, a Guild Artisan is a character background that emphasizes craftsmanship and trade. Guild Artisans are skilled professionals who belong to a specific guild, which provides them with resources and connections. Players can choose from various artisan skills like smithing, brewing, or weaving, adding depth and roleplay opportunities.
