Essential Lyft Driver Requirements Explained

To become a Lyft driver, applicants must meet several requirements: be at least 21 years old, possess a valid driver's license and insurance, own a qualifying vehicle, and pass a background check. Additionally, drivers must have a smartphone capable of running the Lyft app. These criteria ensure safety and reliability for passengers.
