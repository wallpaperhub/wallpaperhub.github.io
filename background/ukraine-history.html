Discover the Fascinating History of Ukraine

Ukraine has a rich and complex history shaped by various influences, including its position between Europe and Asia. It emerged as a state in the 9th century with the Kievan Rus. Over centuries, it experienced invasions, partition, and struggles for independence. The 20th century brought significant events like the Holodomor and the 2014 Euromaidan protests, ultimately leading to its current fight for sovereignty.
