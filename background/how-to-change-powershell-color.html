How to Easily Change PowerShell Color Settings

To change PowerShell color, open the PowerShell window, right-click the title bar, and select "Properties." In the "Colors" tab, you can customize the screen text, screen background, and other colors. After adjusting, click "OK" to apply the changes. You can also use the command `Set-PSReadLineOption -Colors` for additional options.
