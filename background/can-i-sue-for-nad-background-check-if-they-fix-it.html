Can You Sue for Errors on Background Checks?

Yes, you can potentially sue if a background check contains incorrect information that harms you and the company fails to rectify it after being notified. The Fair Credit Reporting Act mandates that companies verify the accuracy of their reports. Consult a legal professional to explore your options.
