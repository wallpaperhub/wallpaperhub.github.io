"The Wendigo: Legends of a Cannibalistic Spirit"

The wendigo is a legendary creature from Algonquian folklore, often depicted as a malevolent spirit associated with winter, starvation, and greed. Described as gaunt with an insatiable hunger, it symbolizes the dangers of overindulgence and moral decay. Encounters with the wendigo are said to lead to madness and transformation.
