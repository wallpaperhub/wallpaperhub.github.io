Fascinating Facts About Jane Goodall

Jane Goodall, a renowned primatologist, revolutionized our understanding of chimpanzees. She began her groundbreaking research in Gombe, Tanzania, in 1960, observing tool use and social behaviors. Goodall's work emphasizes the importance of conservation and animal welfare, making her a global ambassador for environmental issues.
