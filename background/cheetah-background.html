Discover the Amazing World of Cheetahs

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, capable of reaching speeds up to 75 mph in short bursts covering distances up to 1,500 feet. With their distinctive black tear marks and slender bodies, these big cats are found primarily in Africa. They prefer open savannas and are known for their remarkable acceleration and hunting skills.
