Remove White Background in Photoshop: Step-by-Step Guide

To remove a white background in Photoshop, open your image and select the Magic Wand Tool. Click on the white area to select it, then press Delete. For more precision, use the Quick Selection Tool and refine the edges. Finally, save your image in PNG format to maintain a transparent background.
