Understanding Homework: Its Importance & Evolution

Homework background refers to the history and evolution of assignments outside the classroom. Initially used to reinforce learning, homework has adapted to modern educational theories. This practice varies widely across cultures and educational systems, influencing student engagement and performance. The role of technology has further transformed how assignments are given and completed.
