How to Change Background in Illustrator: A Step-by-Step Guide

Changing the background in Adobe Illustrator is a straightforward process. First, select the object or artwork whose background you want to alter. Go to the 'Object' menu, choose 'Arrange,' then 'Send to Back' or 'Bring to Front' as needed. Use the 'Rectangle Tool' to create a new background layer. Customize it with color or gradients for a fresh look.
