"The Vatican: A Timeline of Its Construction and Establishment"

The Vatican City, the smallest independent state in the world, traces its origins back to the early Christian community in Rome. The iconic St. Peter's Basilica was constructed between 1506 and 1626, marking the Vatican's architectural development. However, the Vatican was officially established as a sovereign entity in 1929 with the Lateran Treaty.
