Flour: A Culinary Staple Through the Ages

Flour, a staple ingredient in kitchens worldwide, has a rich history dating back thousands of years. Its origins trace to ancient civilizations, where grains were ground into powder for baking and cooking. Today, flour comes in various types, including all-purpose, whole wheat, and specialty flours, catering to diverse culinary needs.
