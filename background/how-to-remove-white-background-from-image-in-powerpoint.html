Effortlessly Remove White Backgrounds in PowerPoint

To remove the white background from an image in PowerPoint, select the image, go to the "Format Picture" tab, and click on "Remove Background." Adjust the selection area using the handles to highlight the part you want to keep. Click "Keep Changes" to finalize. This method effectively helps create a transparent background.
