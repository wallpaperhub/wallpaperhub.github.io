Heart in Black and White: Symbolism & Meaning

In the realm of symbolism, a heart depicted in black and white conveys deep emotions, blending love with mystery. The stark contrast often signifies the absence of color in a relationship, evoking feelings of nostalgia or longing. This visual representation can encapsulate the complexity of human connections.
