"Charles Melton: Rising Star of Hollywood"

Charles Melton is an American actor and model, best known for his role as Reggie Mantle in the TV series "Riverdale." Born on January 4, 1991, in Guam, he gained recognition for his charm and versatility. Beyond acting, Melton has made an impact in advocacy, particularly promoting Asian representation in Hollywood.
