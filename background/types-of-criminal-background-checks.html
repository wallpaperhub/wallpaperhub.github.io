Essential Types of Criminal Background Checks Explained

There are several types of criminal background checks, including local, state, and federal checks. Local checks look at records within a specific county, while state checks review statewide criminal history. Federal checks access criminal databases like the FBI's. Employment checks and tenant checks are also common, depending on the situation.
