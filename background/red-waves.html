Discover the Beauty of Red Waves

Red waves, a captivating phenomenon, occur when ocean waves receive specific light conditions, making them appear crimson. This natural occurrence adds beauty to coastal landscapes and can be observed during stunning sunsets. The interplay of sunlight and water creates awe-inspiring visuals, inviting photographers and nature enthusiasts alike.
