Stop Background Apps on Android: Boost Performance

To stop apps running in the background on Android, go to Settings > Apps. Here, select the app you want to stop and tap on 'Force Stop.' This will prevent the app from consuming resources and battery. You can also adjust background permissions by selecting 'Data usage' under the app settings. Regularly managing apps can enhance your device's performance.
