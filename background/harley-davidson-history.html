Harley-Davidson: A Legendary Motorcycle Journey

Harley-Davidson was founded in 1903 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, by William S. Harley and Arthur Davidson. Initially producing small engines, the company steadily grew, becoming iconic for its heavyweight motorcycles and a distinctive culture. With a storied legacy of innovation, rebellion, and Americana, Harley remains synonymous with freedom and adventure.
