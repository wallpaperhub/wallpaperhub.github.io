Essential Steps to Prepare for Your Background Check

To prepare for a background check, start by reviewing your own records, including credit reports and criminal history. Gather necessary documents like identification and proof of employment. Notify references and previous employers that they may be contacted. Lastly, ensure your social media presence is professional and appropriate.
