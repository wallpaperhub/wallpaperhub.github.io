Can You Drug Test a Minor Without Parental Consent?

Drug testing a minor typically requires parental consent, but laws can vary by state. In certain situations, such as school policies or health concerns, consent may not be necessary. Always consult local regulations and medical professionals to understand your legal obligations before proceeding with testing a minor.
