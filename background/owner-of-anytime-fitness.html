Discover the Owner Behind Anytime Fitness

Anytime Fitness is co-founded by Chuck Runyon and Dave Mortensen, who established the brand in 2002. The gym franchise has grown to include thousands of locations across the globe, focusing on convenience and accessibility for fitness enthusiasts. Their motto revolves around empowering members to seek healthier lifestyles anytime, anywhere.
