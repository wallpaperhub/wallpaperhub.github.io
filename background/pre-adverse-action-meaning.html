Understanding Pre-Adverse Action: What You Need to Know

Pre-adverse action refers to the notice provided to individuals when an employer intends to take adverse action based on their background check findings. This notice allows individuals to review the information and correct any inaccuracies before the formal decision is made, ensuring transparency and fairness in the hiring process.
