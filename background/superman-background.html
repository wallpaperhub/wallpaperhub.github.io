Discover Superman's Incredible Origin Story

Superman, the iconic superhero created by Jerry Siegel and Joe Shuster in 1938, hails from the fictional planet Krypton. His birth name is Kal-El, and he possesses extraordinary powers, including super strength, flight, and heat vision. Raised in Smallville by Jonathan and Martha Kent, he embodies truth, justice, and the American way.
