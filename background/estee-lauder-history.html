Discover the Rich History of Estée Lauder

Estée Lauder was founded in 1946 by Estée Lauder and her husband, Joseph. Starting with four products, the brand quickly gained popularity in the beauty industry. Lauder revolutionized marketing with personalized sales, free samples, and a focus on luxury. Today, it's a global leader in skincare, makeup, and fragrance.
