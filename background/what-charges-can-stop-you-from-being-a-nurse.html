Charges That Could Prevent You from Becoming a Nurse

Various charges can impede your path to becoming a nurse, particularly criminal offenses related to drug abuse, violence, or dishonesty. Felonies like theft or assault may result in disciplinary actions from nursing boards, while misdemeanors could also lead to licensing challenges. Always ensure your record is clean before pursuing nursing.
