Effortlessly Make Pictures Transparent in PowerPoint

To make a picture transparent in PowerPoint, select the image you want to modify. Go to the "Format" tab and click on "Color." Choose "Set Transparent Color" and click on the color in the image that you want to make transparent. This method is great for blending images into the background or creating unique visual effects.
