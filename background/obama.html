Barack Obama: Legacy and Impact of the 44th President

Barack Obama, the 44th President of the United States, served from 2009 to 2017. He made history as the first African American to hold the office. Notable for landmark policies like the Affordable Care Act, his presidency emphasized social change, diplomacy, and climate action. Obama remains a prominent voice in global issues today.
