How Much Caffeine is in a Large Dunkin' Iced Coffee?

A large Dunkin' iced coffee typically contains about 297 mg of caffeine, depending on the blend and how it's prepared. This amount may vary slightly based on factors such as the coffee type and sugar or milk added. It's a favorite choice for those needing a refreshing caffeine boost during hot days!
