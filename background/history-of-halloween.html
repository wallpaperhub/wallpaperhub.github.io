Unveiling Halloween: A Journey Through Spooktacular History

The history of Halloween dates back over 2,000 years to the ancient Celtic festival of Samhain, marking the end of the harvest season. Celebrated on October 31, it was believed that the boundary between the living and the dead blurred, allowing spirits to roam. Over centuries, Halloween evolved, mixing traditions from various cultures, culminating in the modern day celebration.
