The Fascinating Journey of Ceramics: A Historical Overview

The history of ceramics dates back to approximately 29,000 BC, with evidence of early pottery found in various regions. Initially used for storage, cooking, and ritualistic purposes, ceramics evolved through techniques like hand-building and wheel-throwing. Over centuries, cultures developed unique glazes and styles, showcasing ceramic art's rich legacy.
