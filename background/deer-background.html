All About Deer: Fascinating Facts & Insights

Deer have fascinated humans for centuries, known for their graceful presence in forests and grasslands worldwide. With over 90 species, they adapt to diverse habitats, showcasing a remarkable range of sizes and behaviors. From the agile white-tailed deer to the majestic moose, these herbivorous mammals play vital roles in ecosystems as grazers and prey.
