Optimize Your Designs: Background Mask in AutoCAD

Background mask in AutoCAD is a feature that allows users to apply a mask to specific areas of a drawing. This enhances presentation quality by obscuring unwanted details and emphasizing important elements. Ideal for creating presentations, LEED documentation, or any project where clarity is essential, it streamlines visual communication.
