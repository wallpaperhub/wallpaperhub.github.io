Gal Gadot: Hollywood's Wonder Woman

Gal Gadot, an Israeli actress and model, is best known for her iconic role as Wonder Woman in the DC Extended Universe. Rising to fame after her appearance in "Fast & Furious," Gadot's charm and strength made her a household name. Beyond acting, she’s an advocate for women’s rights and an inspiring role model worldwide.
