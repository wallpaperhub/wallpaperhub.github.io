Do Extras Get Credited in Film and TV?

Extras, also known as background actors, may not always receive credit for their work in film and television. This varies by production and contract stipulations. While some productions do credit extras, others may not. For aspiring actors, understanding this can help set expectations regarding recognition in the industry.
