Discover Galileo: The Father of Modern Science

Galileo Galilei, a pioneering astronomer and physicist of the Renaissance, revolutionized our understanding of the universe. With inventions like the telescope, he challenged existing beliefs, discovering moons of Jupiter and phases of Venus. His scientific method laid the groundwork for modern science, making him a key figure in history.
