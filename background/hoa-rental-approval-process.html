HOA Rental Approval Process: A Step-by-Step Guide

The HOA rental approval process involves submitting an application that includes tenant information and lease terms. The board reviews this application to ensure compliance with HOA rules. Approval may take several days to weeks, depending on board meetings. Clear communication between tenants, landlords, and the HOA is essential for a smooth process.
