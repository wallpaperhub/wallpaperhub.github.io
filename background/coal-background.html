Understanding Coal: History and Impact

Coal has been a crucial energy source for centuries, fueling industries and powering homes. Once seen as abundant, its environmental impact has raised concerns, leading to a shift toward renewable energy. Understanding coal's history, production, and role in climate change is vital for addressing future energy needs responsibly.
