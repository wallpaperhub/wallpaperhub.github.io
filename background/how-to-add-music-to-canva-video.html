Quick Guide: Adding Music to Your Canva Video

To add music to your Canva video, first, open your project and click on the “Audio” tab on the left sidebar. Browse through the available music tracks or upload your own by clicking “Upload.” Once you find the track you like, drag it onto your video timeline. Adjust the length and volume as needed, and preview your video to ensure everything syncs perfectly.
