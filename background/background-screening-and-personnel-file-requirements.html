Essential Background Screening & Personnel File Guidelines

Background screening is essential for ensuring workplace safety and suitability. Employers must verify candidates' credentials, criminal history, and references. Personnel file requirements include maintaining accurate records of hiring documents, performance evaluations, and disciplinary actions. This process helps uphold transparency and compliance with legal standards.
