Optimize Background App Refresh on Android

Background App Refresh on Android allows applications to update content even when they're not actively in use. This feature enhances user experience by ensuring that your apps are always ready with the latest information like messages, news, and social media updates. You can manage this feature through settings to optimize battery and data consumption.
