Was Elvis Presley of Indian Descent?

Elvis Presley, known as the King of Rock and Roll, had no documented Native American ancestry or Indian heritage. He was of primarily European descent, with roots traced to Scottish, Irish, and German heritage. Despite occasional claims, there's no credible evidence connecting him to Indian origins, making this a misconception.
