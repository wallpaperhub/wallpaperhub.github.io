Understanding the Autism Ribbon Color: Meaning & Significance

The autism ribbon color is a vibrant puzzle piece pattern, symbolizing the neurological diversity of individuals on the spectrum. Each color represents different aspects of autism, fostering awareness and understanding. Wearing or displaying the autism ribbon promotes acceptance and encourages dialogue about the unique experiences of those with autism.
