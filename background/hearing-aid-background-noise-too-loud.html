Why Is My Hearing Aid Amplifying Background Noise?

Hearing aids are designed to enhance sound but can sometimes amplify background noise, making hearing difficult. This issue can be frustrating for users in noisy environments. Adjusting settings, using noise-cancellation features, or consulting an audiologist can help improve clarity and reduce unwanted sound interference.
