Minnie Mouse: Timeless Icon of Joy

Minnie Mouse, an iconic Disney character, debuted in 1928 and has remained a beloved figure. With her signature polka-dots and bow, Minnie represents friendship and fun. Her stories often focus on love, adventure, and her goofy partner, Mickey Mouse. Over the decades, she has become a symbol of happiness and nostalgia for fans worldwide.
