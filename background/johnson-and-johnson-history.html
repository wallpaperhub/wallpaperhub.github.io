Johnson & Johnson: A Brief History of Innovation

Founded in 1886, Johnson & Johnson is an American multinational corporation known for its medical devices, pharmaceuticals, and consumer health products. It began with sterile surgical dressings and expanded to iconic brands like Band-Aid and Tylenol. With a commitment to innovation, health, and safety, it has become a household name worldwide.
