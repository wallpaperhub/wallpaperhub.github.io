Mastering the Art of Sky Painting

To paint a beautiful sky, start with a clean canvas. Use a flat brush and blend light blue, white, and hints of yellow for a daytime sky. For sunsets, mix orange, pink, and purple. Apply colors in smooth strokes, layering for depth. Don't forget to add clouds with a soft brush using white or light gray. Practice makes perfect!
