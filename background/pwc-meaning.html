Understanding PwC: What It Stands For

PwC, or PricewaterhouseCoopers, is a global professional services network offering audit, tax, and consulting services. As one of the "Big Four" accounting firms, it provides expertise to various sectors, helping organizations navigate complexities and enhance performance. Founded in 1998, it emphasizes trust, integrity, and innovation.
