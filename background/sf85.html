Understanding the SF-85: A Guide to Federal Employment Forms

The SF-85 form is essential for individuals seeking federal employment or security clearance. It collects crucial personal information to assess an applicant's trustworthiness and reliability. This form plays a vital role in background checks, ensuring candidates meet the security standards required for sensitive roles within government agencies.
