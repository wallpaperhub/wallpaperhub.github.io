Is Pluto a Planet? Unveiling the Dwarf Planet Debate

Pluto, once considered the ninth planet of our solar system, was reclassified by the International Astronomical Union in 2006 as a "dwarf planet." This change sparked debate among astronomers and the public alike. While some argue for its planetary status based on historical reasons, others support the criteria for defining a planet that Pluto does not meet.
