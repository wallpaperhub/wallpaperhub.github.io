Exploring Wentworth Miller: Actor, Advocate, & Icon

Wentworth Miller is a talented actor and screenwriter, best known for his role as Michael Scofield in the hit TV series "Prison Break." Born on June 2, 1972, in Chipping Norton, England, he gained fame for his compelling performances. Miller is also an advocate for mental health awareness and LGBTQ+ rights.
