How to Enable Dark Mode in Safari: A Quick Guide

To enable dark mode in Safari, open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad. Scroll down and tap on "Display & Brightness." Choose the "Dark" option to apply a system-wide dark theme. Safari will automatically adopt this setting. For macOS, go to System Preferences > General and select "Dark" to activate dark mode in Safari.
