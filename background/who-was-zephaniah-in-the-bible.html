Who Was Zephaniah? A Biblical Prophet Explained

Zephaniah was a prophet in the Hebrew Bible, belonging to the 7th century BC during King Josiah’s reign. He is known for his profound messages of impending judgment on Judah and the surrounding nations due to their sins, while also prophesying hope and restoration for the faithful. His book emphasizes God's sovereignty and justice.
