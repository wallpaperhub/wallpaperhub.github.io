Discover the Rich History of Rodeo

Rodeo originated in the 16th century, evolving from cattle ranching practices in Spain and Mexico. By the 19th century, it became a popular competitive sport in the American West, showcasing skills like bronc riding and roping. Today, rodeo reflects Western heritage, combining tradition, athleticism, and entertainment for audiences worldwide.
