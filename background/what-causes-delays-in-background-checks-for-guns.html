Understanding Delays in Gun Background Checks

Delays in background checks for gun purchases can stem from several factors. These include incomplete or inaccurate information provided by applicants, high volumes of requests during peak periods, and issues within state or federal databases. Administrative errors and the need for further investigation can also contribute to these delays.
