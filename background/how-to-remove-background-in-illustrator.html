Effortless Background Removal in Illustrator

To remove a background in Adobe Illustrator, use the Clipping Mask feature. First, select the object you want to keep, then go to 'Object' > 'Clipping Mask' > 'Make.' For complex backgrounds, try using the 'Image Trace' tool, adjusting settings to isolate your subject. Finally, delete or hide the background layer for a clean look.
