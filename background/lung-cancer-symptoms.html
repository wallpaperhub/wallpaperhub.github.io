Understanding Lung Cancer Symptoms

Lung cancer symptoms can be subtle or severe. Common signs include a persistent cough, chest pain, shortness of breath, and coughing up blood. Unexplained weight loss, fatigue, and frequent respiratory infections may also occur. Early detection is crucial; consult a healthcare provider if you experience these symptoms.
