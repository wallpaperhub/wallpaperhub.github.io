Does Divorce Show Up on a Background Check?

Yes, a divorce typically shows up on a background check, especially if the check includes public records. Divorce records are publicly accessible, which means potential employers or landlords can discover this information. However, it's essential to note that the details seen may vary depending on the type of background check conducted.
