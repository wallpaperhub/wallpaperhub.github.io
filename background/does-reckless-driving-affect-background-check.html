How Reckless Driving Impacts Background Checks

Reckless driving, an offense that reflects poor judgment, can significantly impact background checks. Employers and renters often consider driving records when assessing a candidate's trustworthiness and reliability. A reckless driving charge can raise red flags, potentially affecting job opportunities or leasing applications.
