How to Disable Verizon Digital Secure

To turn off Verizon Digital Secure, open the app and go to Settings. Tap on "My Subscription" and then select "Manage Subscription." From there, you can choose to cancel or turn off the service. Remember to confirm any changes. Restart your device if necessary to ensure changes take effect.
