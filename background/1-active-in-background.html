Maximize Device Efficiency: Understanding Active Background Processes

Being "active in background" refers to processes or applications running behind the scenes on devices. This feature is essential for multitasking, allowing apps to perform updates, notifications, or synchronize data without user intervention. It optimizes user experience but can affect battery life and performance. Manage background activity for efficiency.
