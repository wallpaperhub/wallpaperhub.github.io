What Happened to Obadiah in the Bible: A Prophetic Overview

In the Bible, Obadiah is a significant figure known primarily for the Book of Obadiah, which is a prophetic text in the Old Testament. He is often recognized for his message against Edom, a nation that descended from Esau. The prophecy foretells Edom's downfall due to its pride and betrayal of Israel, emphasizing divine judgment.
