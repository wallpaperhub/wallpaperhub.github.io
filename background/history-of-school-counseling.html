Discover the Evolution of School Counseling: A Historical Overview

School counseling has evolved significantly since its inception in the early 20th century, initially focusing on vocational guidance. Post-World War II, it expanded to encompass mental health and educational support, adapting to societal changes. Today, school counselors play a vital role in students' academic, emotional, and social development, promoting holistic well-being.
