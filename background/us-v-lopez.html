Understanding US v. Lopez: A Landmark Supreme Court Case

In "United States v. Lopez" (1995), the Supreme Court ruled that the Gun-Free School Zones Act exceeded Congress’s authority under the Commerce Clause. This landmark decision emphasized states' rights, limiting federal power over local issues. It marked a significant shift in constitutional law, affecting future federalism debates.
