Sofi Stadium: A Modern Marvel in Entertainment

Sofi Stadium, located in Inglewood, California, is a state-of-the-art venue that opened in 2020. Home to the NFL's Los Angeles Rams and Chargers, this multi-purpose stadium boasts a capacity of 70,000, expandable to 100,000 for major events. It features a unique, transparent roof and hosts concerts, sports, and entertainment events year-round.
