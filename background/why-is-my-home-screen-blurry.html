Why Is My Home Screen Blurry?

Having a blurry home screen can be frustrating. It may result from a few factors like low-resolution images, scaling issues, or software glitches. Check your display settings, ensure your images are high-resolution, and restart your device. If troubles persist, consider an update or seeking help from a technician.
