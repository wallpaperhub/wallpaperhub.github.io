How to Export Transparent Backgrounds in Affinity Designer

To export a design with a transparent background in Affinity Designer, begin by selecting the layers you wish to export. Go to the File menu and select 'Export'. In the export options, choose PNG format and check the 'Foreground' and 'Transparent background' options. Finally, click 'Export' to save your artwork with a clear background, ready for use in various projects.
