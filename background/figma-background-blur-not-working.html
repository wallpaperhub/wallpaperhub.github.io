Fixing Figma Background Blur Issues

If you're facing issues with Figma's background blur not working, it could be due to a few common reasons. Ensure you are using layers correctly and that the background blur effect is applied to the right layer. Check if the layer is not behind a solid color or an empty frame. Additionally, updating your Figma app may resolve glitches.
