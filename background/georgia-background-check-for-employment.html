Georgia Employment Background Checks: What You Need to Know

In Georgia, employers often conduct background checks as part of the hiring process. This may include verifying criminal records, employment history, and educational qualifications. It's essential for job seekers to be aware of their rights, including the Fair Credit Reporting Act, which governs how these checks must be conducted and reported.
