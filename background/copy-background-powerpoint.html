How to Copy Backgrounds in PowerPoint: A Step-by-Step Guide

Copying a background in PowerPoint is a simple yet effective way to enhance your presentations. To copy a background, right-click on the slide you want to duplicate, select "Format Background," and choose "Copy." You can then paste it onto another slide or throughout your presentation. This ensures consistent design and professionalism in your work.
