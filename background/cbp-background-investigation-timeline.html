Understanding the CBP Background Investigation Timeline

The CBP background investigation timeline typically spans several weeks to months, depending on various factors such as the complexity of the case and applicant response times. Key stages include the initial application review, background checks, interviews, and final adjudication. Candidates should prepare for potential delays in this thorough process.
