Discover the Rich History of Phoenix, Arizona

Phoenix, Arizona, founded in 1881, has a rich history rooted in Native American culture, particularly the Hohokam people, who lived in the region for centuries. The city blossomed with the arrival of railroads, transforming it into a thriving agricultural and urban hub. Today, Phoenix is known for its vibrant culture and desert landscape.
