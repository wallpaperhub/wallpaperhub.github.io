How to Change Your iPhone Background to White

To make the background white on your iPhone, go to Settings > Wallpaper. Here, you can choose a solid white wallpaper or tap “Choose a New Wallpaper” to access your photos or stock options. Select a white image and set it as the wallpaper for your home screen and lock screen to achieve a clean, minimalistic look.
