Understanding Halftone: The Art of Dot Photography

Halftone is a printmaking technique that uses dots of varying sizes and spacing to create a gradient effect and simulate continuous tones. Commonly applied in graphic art and print, it transforms images into a series of dots, allowing for effective color reproduction in both black and white and color prints.
