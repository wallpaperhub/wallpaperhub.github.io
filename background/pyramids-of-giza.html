Explore the Pyramids of Giza: Ancient Wonders Unveiled

The Pyramids of Giza are monumental structures located on the Giza Plateau near Cairo, Egypt. Built during the Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom, they serve as iconic funerary monuments for pharaohs. The Great Pyramid, built for Pharaoh Khufu, is the largest and one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, showcasing the incredible engineering prowess of ancient Egyptians.
