What Does a Pop Filter Remove?

A pop filter device is designed to reduce or eliminate plosive sounds—such as "P" and "B"—that can create unwanted noise during recording. It also effectively filters out sibilance, high-frequency hissing from "S" sounds, and helps prevent saliva droplets from reaching the microphone, ensuring cleaner audio quality for recordings.
