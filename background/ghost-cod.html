Discover the Delights of Ghost Cod: A Culinary Treasure

Ghost cod, also known as red cod or Mola mola, refers to a popular fish found in cold waters around New Zealand and Australia. Its firm, white flesh is highly sought after for its subtle flavor and versatility in dishes, from fish and chips to gourmet seafood platters. The ghost cod is a sustainable choice, supporting responsible fishing practices.
