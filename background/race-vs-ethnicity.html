Understanding Race vs. Ethnicity: Key Differences Explained

Race and ethnicity are often used interchangeably, but they refer to distinct concepts. Race relates to physical characteristics, such as skin color, while ethnicity relates to cultural factors, including nationality, language, and traditions. Understanding these differences fosters better communication and promotes inclusivity in diverse societies.
