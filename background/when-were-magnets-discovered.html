The Discovery of Magnets: A Historical Overview

Magnets have a long history, with their discovery dating back to ancient Greece around 600 BC. The Greeks first noticed the natural magnetic properties of lodestone, a type of magnetite. This remarkable mineral attracted iron, leading to early experiments and applications in navigation and various tools throughout the ages.
