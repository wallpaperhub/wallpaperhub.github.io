Tua Tagovailoa: Rising NFL Star

Tua Tagovailoa is an American football quarterback currently playing for the Miami Dolphins in the NFL. Born on March 2, 1998, in Hawaii, Tua gained fame at the University of Alabama, leading the Crimson Tide to a national championship in 2017. Known for his strong arm, accuracy, and resilience, he has become a promising star in professional football.
