Discovering the Secrets of the Ocean Floor

The ocean floor, or seabed, is a diverse and dynamic environment teeming with life. It consists of various geological formations, from sandy plains to rocky ridges and deep-sea trenches. Home to unique ecosystems, the ocean floor supports countless species and plays a crucial role in global climate regulation and carbon cycling.
