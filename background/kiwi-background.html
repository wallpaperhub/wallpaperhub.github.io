Discover the Fascinating Kiwi Background

Kiwi, a small, nutrient-rich fruit native to China, is loved worldwide for its vibrant green color and sweet-tart flavor. With origins dating back to the early 20th century, it was popularized in New Zealand. Today, kiwis are cultivated globally and are celebrated for their health benefits, including high vitamin C content and digestive health support.
