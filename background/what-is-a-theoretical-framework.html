Understanding Theoretical Frameworks in Research

A theoretical framework is a structured way of understanding how various concepts, theories, and variables relate to one another within a study or field of research. It provides a foundational basis for analyzing issues, guiding methodology, and interpreting results. By clearly articulating these relationships, it enhances academic rigor.
