Discovering the Corgi: A Brief History

Originating from Wales, corgis are beloved herding dogs known for their short legs and playful demeanor. Initially bred to herd cattle, these energetic canines have become cherished family pets. Their loyal and affectionate nature, combined with their unique appearance, has made them popular worldwide.
