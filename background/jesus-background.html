Explore the Life and Background of Jesus Christ

Jesus of Nazareth, a pivotal figure in Christianity, was born in Bethlehem around 4 BC and raised in Nazareth. A Jewish carpenter by trade, he began his ministry around age 30, promoting love, forgiveness, and the Kingdom of God. His teachings, crucifixion, and resurrection form the foundation of Christian faith, influencing billions worldwide.
