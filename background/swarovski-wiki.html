Swarovski: The Leader in Crystal Elegance

Swarovski is a leading Austrian manufacturer of crystal glass, known for its precision-cut crystals and luxury products. Founded in 1895 by Daniel Swarovski, the brand has become synonymous with elegance. Swarovski crystals are used in fashion, jewelry, and home décor, resulting in exquisite designs appreciated worldwide.
