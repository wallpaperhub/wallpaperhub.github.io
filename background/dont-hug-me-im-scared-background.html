"Exploring the Surreal World of Don't Hug Me I'm Scared"

"Don't Hug Me I'm Scared" is a British web series that blends puppetry and animation with dark humor and surreal storytelling. Created by Becky Sloan and Joseph Pelling, the series debuted in 2011 and gained a cult following for its unique approach to exploring themes like creativity, technology, and societal expectations. Each episode begins innocently but descends into chaos, revealing unsettling truths.
