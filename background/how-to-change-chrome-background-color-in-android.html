Change Chrome Background Color on Android Easily

To change the Chrome background color on Android, open the Chrome app and tap the three dots in the upper right corner. Go to "Settings," then "Theme." Here, you can select from Light, Dark, or System Default options. Choose your preferred theme, and the Chrome background will instantly update to reflect your choice.
