Understanding Invisible Text: Uses & Implications

Invisible text refers to text that is deliberately hidden from the viewer's eye, often used in web design and coding for various purposes, including SEO manipulation or to enhance user experience. While it can provide utility, misuse can lead to unethical practices and penalties from search engines, so it's important to use it judiciously.
