Do Employers Really Check Your Credit Score?

Employers often check credit scores during the hiring process, particularly for positions that involve financial responsibilities. A credit check allows employers to assess a candidate's reliability and financial habits. While it's not a universal practice, job seekers should be aware that poor credit may impact their job prospects.
