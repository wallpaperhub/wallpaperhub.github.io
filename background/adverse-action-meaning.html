Understanding Adverse Action: What It Means for You

Adverse action refers to any unfavorable decision or action taken against an individual or entity, often related to employment, credit, insurance, or housing. Examples include denying a job application, increasing interest rates, or refusing insurance. Understanding adverse actions is crucial for recognizing one's rights and potential recourse.
