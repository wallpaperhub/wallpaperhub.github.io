Understanding Autism: Raising Awareness for Inclusion

Autism awareness aims to promote understanding and acceptance of individuals with autism spectrum disorders (ASD). By fostering awareness, we can reduce stigma, encourage empathy, and support inclusive communities. Historically, misconceptions surrounding autism have hindered acceptance; awareness efforts strive to illuminate the unique strengths and challenges faced by those on the spectrum.
