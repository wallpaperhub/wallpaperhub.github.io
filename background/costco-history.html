Discover the Fascinating History of Costco

Costco was founded in 1983 in Seattle, Washington, by James Sinegal and Jeffrey Brotman. The company revolutionized retail with its membership-only warehouse model, offering bulk goods at low prices. Its success led to rapid expansion across the U.S. and internationally, including markets in Canada, Mexico, and Asia.
