Understanding Lens Flare: Effects & Techniques

Lens flare occurs when bright light hits the lens of a camera, creating dazzling streaks or spots. Often seen in photography and cinematography, it can add an artistic touch or a dramatic effect to an image. While some photographers embrace lens flare for its visual allure, others may prefer to reduce its occurrence for clarity.
