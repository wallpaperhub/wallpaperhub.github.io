All You Need to Know About Frenchie Background

French Bulldogs, affectionately known as "Frenchies," originated in the 19th century in Nottingham, England, as lace workers' companions. They gained popularity in France, leading to their name. These playful and affectionate dogs have a distinctive bat-like ear shape and a compact build, making them beloved family pets worldwide.
