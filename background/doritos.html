"Discover the Bold World of Doritos: Flavorful Tortilla Chips"

Doritos are a popular brand of flavored tortilla chips known for their bold taste and crunchy texture. Originally introduced in the 1960s, these snack chips come in various flavors, including Nacho Cheese and Cool Ranch. Perfect for parties, road trips, or a midday crunch, Doritos have become a staple in snack aisles around the world.
