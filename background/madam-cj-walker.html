Trailblazer of Beauty: Madam C.J. Walker

Madam C.J. Walker, born Sarah Breedlove in 1867, revolutionized the beauty industry with her line of haircare products for African American women. As one of the first female self-made millionaires in the U.S., she used her success to advocate for social and political change. Her legacy continues to inspire entrepreneurs today.
