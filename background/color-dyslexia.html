Understanding Color Dyslexia: Causes & Solutions

Color dyslexia, or color vision deficiency, affects an individual's ability to perceive colors accurately. This condition can lead to challenges in distinguishing between certain hues, making everyday tasks difficult. Awareness and understanding of color dyslexia can foster inclusivity and support for those affected, enhancing their quality of life.
