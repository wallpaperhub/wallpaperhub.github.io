Fascinating Facts About Spiders: Explore the World of Arachnids

Spiders belong to the class Arachnida and are known for their eight legs and ability to produce silk. With over 45,000 species, they play crucial roles in the ecosystem as predators, controlling insect populations. Their fascinating behaviors and unique adaptations, such as web-building or hunting tactics, highlight their evolutionary success.
