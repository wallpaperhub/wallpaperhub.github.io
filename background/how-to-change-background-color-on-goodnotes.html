Change Background Color in Goodnotes: A Quick Guide

To change the background color on Goodnotes, open your document and tap on the '...' menu at the top right. Select 'Background' from the options. You can choose a preset color or create a custom one. Once you've picked your color, hit 'Done' to apply the changes. Enjoy a personalized workspace!
