Discovering the Sweet History of Pan Dulce

Pan dulce, or "sweet bread," has roots in colonial Mexico, stemming from Spanish traditions. Over centuries, it evolved, incorporating indigenous ingredients and methods. Varieties like conchas and empanadas symbolize cultural identity, celebrated during festivities. Today, pan dulce remains a beloved staple in Mexican kitchens and bakeries worldwide.
