The Rich Tapestry of the Middle Ages

The Middle Ages, spanning from the 5th to the late 15th century, was a vibrant period marked by feudalism, the rise of kingdoms, and significant cultural shifts. This era saw the establishment of universities, gothic architecture, and the proliferation of chivalric ideals. It ended with the Renaissance, paving the way for modernity.
