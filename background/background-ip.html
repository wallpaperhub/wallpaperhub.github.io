Understanding Background IP: A Quick Guide

Background IP refers to intellectual property that is created independently of a specific project or agreement. It encompasses ideas, designs, and innovations developed before a collaboration. Understanding background IP is crucial for businesses to protect their assets and avoid potential legal conflicts over ownership and usage rights in collaborations.
