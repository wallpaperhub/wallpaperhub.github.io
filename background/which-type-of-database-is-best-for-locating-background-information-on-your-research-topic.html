Best Databases for Research Background Info

When seeking background information for your research topic, academic databases are invaluable. JSTOR, Google Scholar, and PubMed offer peer-reviewed articles, theses, and books. These databases curate reliable and scholarly sources, helping you gain a deep understanding of your topic while ensuring credibility in your research.
