"The Legacy of Morgan Stanley: A Historical Overview"

Morgan Stanley was founded in 1935 by Henry S. Morgan and Harold Stanley, emerging as a leader in the financial services industry. Initially a brokerage firm, it navigated through market changes, expanded globally, and diversified its services. Today, it stands as a powerhouse in investment banking, wealth management, and asset management.
