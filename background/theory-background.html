Understanding Theory Background: Key Concepts Explained

The theory background serves as the foundation of any research or study, encompassing key concepts, frameworks, and existing literature that contextualize the topic. Understanding this background allows researchers to situate their work within the broader scholarly dialogue, justifying their approach and methodology and highlighting gaps that their study aims to address.
