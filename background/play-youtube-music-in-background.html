How to Play YouTube Music in Background

To play YouTube Music in the background, simply open the app or the website, select your desired track or playlist, and then minimize the app. On mobile devices, you may need a YouTube Premium subscription to enable background play seamlessly. Enjoy your favorite tunes while multitasking or browsing!
