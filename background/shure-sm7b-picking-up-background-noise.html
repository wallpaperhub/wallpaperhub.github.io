"How to Reduce Background Noise with the Shure SM7B"

The Shure SM7B is renowned for its exceptional sound quality, but some users may notice it picking up background noise. This is often due to its sensitivity and the pickup pattern. To minimize unwanted sounds, use a proper microphone placement, isolation techniques, and consider using a pop filter or windscreen to enhance clarity in your recordings.
