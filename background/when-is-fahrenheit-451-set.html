When is Fahrenheit 451 Set? Understanding the Dystopian Timeline

Fahrenheit 451 is set in a dystopian future that revolves around a totalitarian regime where firemen burn books to suppress dissenting ideas. The story unfolds in a society obsessed with superficial entertainment and mindless consumerism, highlighting themes of censorship and the loss of critical thinking. The exact year is unspecified, emphasizing the timelessness of its warnings.
