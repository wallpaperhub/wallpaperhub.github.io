Exploring the Roots of Pop Punk: A Brief History

Pop punk emerged in the late 1970s, blending punk rock's raw energy with catchy melodies. Bands like Green Day and Blink-182 popularized the genre in the 1990s, characterized by angst-filled lyrics and a rebellious spirit. Today, pop punk continues to influence new artists while evolving within the music scene.
