Who Accused Rebecca Nurse of Witchcraft?

Rebecca Nurse, a respected member of Salem Village, was accused of witchcraft during the infamous Salem witch trials in 1692. Accusations came primarily from the younger girls in the community, notably Abigail Williams and Ann Putnam Jr. These young accusers claimed that Nurse's spirit harmed them, reflecting the hysteria pervasive at the time.
