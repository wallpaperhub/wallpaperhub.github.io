Reimagining Naruto: A Black Perspective

Black Naruto refers to the concept of reimagining the iconic character Naruto Uzumaki from the popular anime and manga series as a Black character. This creative reinterpretation engages fans in discussions about diversity in anime, representation, and cultural identity, highlighting the importance of inclusivity in storytelling.
