Discover Wiccan Symbols: Meanings & Images

Wiccan symbols are powerful images that hold deep meanings in the practice of Wicca. Common symbols include the pentacle, representing earth, air, fire, and water, and the triple goddess symbol, embodying maiden, mother, and crone. These images serve as tools for focus and intention, enhancing spiritual rituals and connections.
