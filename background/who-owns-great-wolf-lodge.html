Ownership of Great Wolf Lodge: Who's Behind the Brand?

Great Wolf Lodge is owned by Great Wolf Resorts, a subsidiary of the parent company, Blackstone Group. Founded in 1997, it has grown into a leading family entertainment brand, featuring indoor water parks and resort amenities. Great Wolf Lodge operates multiple locations across North America, catering to families seeking adventure and relaxation.
