Understanding VA Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A VA background check primarily looks for criminal history, including felonies and misdemeanors, financial stability through credit checks, and past employment verification. It may also review any relevant licenses or certifications. Additionally, it assesses character references to ensure a trustworthy candidate.
