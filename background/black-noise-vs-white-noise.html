Understanding Black Noise vs White Noise: Key Differences

Black noise and white noise are sound frequencies that differ in their applications and effects. White noise contains all audible frequencies with equal intensity, often used for masking background sounds. Black noise, on the other hand, consists of a complete absence of sound, promoting silence and calmness. Knowing their differences can help you choose the best auditory environment.
