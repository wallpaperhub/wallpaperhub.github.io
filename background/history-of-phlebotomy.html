Phlebotomy Through the Ages: An Ancient Medical Practice

Phlebotomy has ancient roots, tracing back to Egyptian practices where bloodletting was believed to heal ailments. The Greeks and Romans also utilized blood extraction for medical purposes. In modern times, phlebotomy evolved into a critical medical profession, focusing on safe blood collection for diagnostics and treatments, emphasizing hygiene and patient care.
