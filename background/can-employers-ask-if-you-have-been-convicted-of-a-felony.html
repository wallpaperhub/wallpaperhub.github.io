Can Employers Ask About Felony Convictions?

Yes, employers can ask if you have been convicted of a felony, but the legality may vary by state. Some jurisdictions limit this inquiry, requiring the question to be posed only after a conditional job offer. It's crucial to know your rights and understand how disclosure may impact your job prospects.
