Nike: A Brief History of the Brand

Nike was founded in 1964 by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman as Blue Ribbon Sports. The company became Nike, inspired by the Greek goddess of victory, in 1971. With its innovative products and clever marketing, including the iconic "Just Do It" slogan, Nike transformed sportswear, becoming a global leader in athletic footwear and apparel.
