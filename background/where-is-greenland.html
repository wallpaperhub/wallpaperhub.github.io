Discovering Greenland: Location & Facts

Greenland is the world’s largest island, located between the Arctic and Atlantic Oceans. It is situated east of Canada and northwest of Iceland. Despite its name, the majority of its landmass is covered in ice. Greenland is an autonomous territory within the Kingdom of Denmark, characterized by stunning landscapes and rich cultural heritage.
