Who Owns Inter Miami CF? David Beckham & Partners

Inter Miami CF, a Major League Soccer team, is primarily owned by David Beckham, the former professional soccer player and global ambassador for the sport. Alongside Beckham, the ownership group includes Jorge Mas, a prominent businessman, and other investors. This diverse ownership aims to elevate soccer in the U.S. and enhance Miami's sports culture.
