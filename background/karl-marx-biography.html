Karl Marx: The Visionary of Social Change

Karl Marx was a German philosopher born on May 5, 1818, in Trier. He co-authored "The Communist Manifesto" and authored "Das Kapital," laying the foundation for Marxism. His theories on capitalism, class struggle, and socialism have significantly influenced political thought. Marx died on March 14, 1883, in London, leaving a lasting legacy.
