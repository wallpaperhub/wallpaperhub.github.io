Nantucket Island: Your Ultimate Travel Guide

Nantucket Island is located off the coast of Massachusetts, about 30 miles (48 km) south of Cape Cod. It is part of the larger Nantucket County and lies in the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its charming cobblestone streets, beautiful beaches, and historic whaling heritage, Nantucket is a popular summer destination.
