Understanding the Reasons Behind 9/11

The events of 9/11 were rooted in a complex mix of geopolitics, religious extremism, and anti-American sentiment. Al-Qaeda sought to retaliate against U.S. foreign policies in the Middle East, particularly its support for Israel and military presence in Muslim countries. The attacks aimed to instill fear and provoke U.S. military responses.
