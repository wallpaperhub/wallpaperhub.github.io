What to Expect in a Background Check: Key Insights

Background checks typically reveal various aspects, including criminal history, employment verification, education credentials, and credit reports. They may also show public records like bankruptcies or liens, as well as driving records for some positions. Different employers may request different details depending on the role and industry.
