Discover the Origins of the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, a beloved franchise, was created in 1984 by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird. Originating as comic book characters, they became global icons through animated series, films, and merchandise. Each turtle—Leonardo, Michelangelo, Donatello, and Raphael—has distinct personalities and skills, thriving in a vibrant, action-packed world.
