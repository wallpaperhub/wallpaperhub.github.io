Change Background Color in Google Docs: A Simple Guide

To change the background color on Google Docs, go to File > Page setup. In the dialog box, look for the "Page color" section. Click on the color drop-down and choose your desired color. You can also customize a color using the “Custom” option. Finally, click “OK” to apply the changes to your document's background.
