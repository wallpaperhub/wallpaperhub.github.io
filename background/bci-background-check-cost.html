What You Need to Know About BCI Background Check Costs

The cost of a BCI (Bureau of Criminal Identification) background check varies based on the state and the type of check performed. Generally, fees range from $20 to $50. Additional costs may apply for processing or obtaining detailed reports. It's essential to check with local agencies for the most accurate cost information.
