Understanding Unattended Access: A Quick Guide

Unattended access refers to the ability to gain remote access to a computer or system without requiring the user to be present. This feature is commonly used for remote management and support, allowing technicians to troubleshoot or update systems efficiently. It enhances productivity by eliminating the need for physical presence.
