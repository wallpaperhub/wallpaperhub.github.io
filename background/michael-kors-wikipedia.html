Michael Kors: Iconic Fashion Designer & Brand

Michael Kors is a prominent American fashion designer known for his luxury accessories and ready-to-wear clothing. Founded in 1981, the Michael Kors brand offers a wide range of products, including handbags, watches, and footwear. With a global presence, Kors is renowned for his chic and modern designs, representing sophisticated American style.
