Cedar Point History: When Was It Built?

Cedar Point, located in Sandusky, Ohio, was established in 1870, originally as a beach resort. Over the years, it transformed into one of the most renowned amusement parks in the United States, known for its thrilling roller coasters and diverse attractions, continuously evolving to enhance visitor experiences.
