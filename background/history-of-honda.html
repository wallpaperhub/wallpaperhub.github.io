Discover the Fascinating History of Honda

Honda was founded in 1948 by Soichiro Honda and initially produced motorcycles. In 1959, it entered the U.S. market, quickly gaining popularity with models like the CB750. The company expanded into automobiles in the 1960s, becoming known for reliability and innovation. Today, Honda is a global leader in both sectors, known for its commitment to engineering excellence.
