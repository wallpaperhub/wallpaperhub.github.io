Exploring the Delicious History of Fries

Fries, originating from Belgium and France, have a rich history. They became popular in the late 19th century and were initially served with seafood. The culinary innovation of frying potatoes led to a beloved snack worldwide. Today, fries come in various styles and flavors, from crinkle-cut to loaded variations, making them a favorite for all ages.
