Mastering Hex Codes: Your Key to Perfect Color Selection

Hex codes are six-digit alphanumeric values used in web design and digital graphics to represent colors. Each code is preceded by a '#' symbol and indicates the levels of red, green, and blue (RGB) in a color. They provide a precise way to convey colors across different platforms and maintain consistency in design.
