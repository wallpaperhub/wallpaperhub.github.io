Cornelius Vanderbilt: The Rail Tycoon Who Transformed America

Cornelius Vanderbilt was an American business magnate known for his significant contributions to the transportation industry, particularly through railroads and shipping. He became one of the wealthiest individuals of his time by consolidating various shipping lines and revolutionizing the railroad industry, expanding trade and commerce in America.
