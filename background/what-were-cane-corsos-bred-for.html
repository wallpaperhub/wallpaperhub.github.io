Why Cane Corsos Were Bred: A Brief History

Cane Corsos were originally bred in Italy for various roles, including hunting, guarding, and as working dogs. Their robust build and protective nature made them excellent in hunting large game and serving as herders for livestock. Today, they continue to excel as loyal companions and formidable guardians.
