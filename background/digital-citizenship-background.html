Understanding Digital Citizenship: A Guide

Digital citizenship encompasses the responsible use of technology, ensuring individuals engage ethically while navigating the online landscape. It promotes digital literacy, online safety, and respectful communication. Understanding this concept is essential for fostering safe and positive online communities, empowering users to harness technology effectively.
