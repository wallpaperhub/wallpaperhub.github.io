Unlocking Learning: The Power of Prior Knowledge

Prior knowledge refers to the information and experiences individuals already possess before encountering new learning. It plays a crucial role in understanding and processing new concepts, as it allows learners to build upon what they know. This foundation enhances comprehension, retention, and the ability to connect ideas effectively.
