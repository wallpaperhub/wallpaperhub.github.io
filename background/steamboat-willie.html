Discover "Steamboat Willie": The Birth of Mickey Mouse

Steamboat Willie, released in 1928, is a landmark animated short film featuring Mickey Mouse. Directed by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks, it is celebrated for being one of the first synchronized sound cartoons. The charming adventures of Mickey aboard a steamboat captivated audiences and marked a significant milestone in the animation industry.
