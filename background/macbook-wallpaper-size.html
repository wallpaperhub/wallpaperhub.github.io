Perfect MacBook Wallpaper Sizes: A Quick Guide

When choosing a wallpaper for your MacBook, it's crucial to get the right size for a perfect fit. The ideal resolution is 2560x1600 pixels for the 13-inch MacBook Pro and 2880x1800 pixels for the 15-inch MacBook Pro. Always select images that match or exceed these dimensions to ensure clarity and vibrancy on your screen.
