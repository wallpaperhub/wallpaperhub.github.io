Does a Black Screen Really Save Battery Life?

A black screen can save battery life on OLED and AMOLED displays, as these screens turn off pixels to display black, consuming less power. However, on LCD screens, a black background doesn't significantly save battery since the backlight remains on. To maximize battery life, consider screen brightness and background settings.
