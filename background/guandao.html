Explore the Unique Guandao: History & Techniques

Guandao is a traditional Chinese pole weapon, featuring a curved blade resembling a sword attached to a long shaft. Historically used by Chinese infantry, it symbolizes strength and valor. The versatile design allows for slashing and thrusting motions, making it effective in combat. Today, guandao is popular in martial arts and weapon demonstrations.
