How to Become a Movie Extra: A Beginner's Guide

To be an extra in a movie, first research casting calls on websites like Backstage and Casting Networks. Prepare a simple headshot and resume without any acting experience required. Dress appropriately for your role and be ready to follow directions on set. Always arrive on time and maintain a positive attitude to enhance your chances for future gigs!
