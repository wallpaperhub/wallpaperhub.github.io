Discovering the Medieval Era: A Historical Journey

The medieval background encompasses a dynamic period from the 5th to the late 15th century characterized by feudalism, chivalry, and the rise of monarchies. This era saw the flourishing of arts and literature, the spread of Christianity, and significant events like the Crusades. Understanding this context is essential to grasping modern Western civilization's foundations.
