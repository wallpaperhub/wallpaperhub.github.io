Best Background Colors for Black Text: A Quick Guide

When choosing a background color for black text, high contrast is essential for readability. Light colors like white, light gray, or pastel shades provide excellent visibility while maintaining a clean look. Avoid overly bright colors that can cause strain. Always test combinations to find the best balance for your design.
