Discover the Iconic Arby's Logo: History & Design

Arby's logo is a distinctive feature that showcases the brand's identity. Introduced in 1964, the iconic cowboy hat symbolizes the restaurant's focus on roast beef sandwiches. The bold red color of the logo represents passion and appetite, making it easily recognizable. Over the years, the logo has evolved but remains true to its roots in Americana.
