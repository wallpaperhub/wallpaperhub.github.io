Exploring the Iconic Amazon Logo: Meaning & Design

The Amazon logo, with its iconic orange arrow connecting the letters 'A' and 'Z', symbolizes the vast range of products available on the platform, suggesting that customers can find everything from A to Z. The logo also features a subtle smile, emphasizing Amazon's commitment to customer satisfaction and a positive shopping experience.
