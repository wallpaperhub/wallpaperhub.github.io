Understanding the 1930s: A Decade of Change and Challenge

The 1930s were defined by the Great Depression, marked by economic hardship and widespread unemployment. Societal changes emerged, with families struggling to make ends meet while seeking new opportunities. This era also stimulated creativity in arts and culture, highlighted by the rise of prominent figures in literature, film, and music.
