Customize Your iPhone Home Screen: A Quick Guide

To change your iPhone home screen, long-press any app icon until they wiggle. You can drag apps to rearrange them or create folders by dragging one app over another. To add widgets, swipe right to the Today View, scroll down, and tap "Edit." Once you've customized your layout, press "Done" to save your changes.
