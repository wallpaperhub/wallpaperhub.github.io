When Did WWF Start? Discover the Origins of the World Wildlife Fund

The World Wildlife Fund (WWF) started on April 29, 1961. It was established by a group of passionate individuals, including Sir Julian Huxley, to promote wildlife conservation and address the growing threats to the world's natural environment. Since then, WWF has become a leading organization in global conservation efforts.
