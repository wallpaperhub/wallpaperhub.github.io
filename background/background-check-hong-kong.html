Essential Guide to Background Checks in Hong Kong

In Hong Kong, background checks are essential for employers to ensure a candidate's qualifications, integrity, and reliability. This process may include verifying educational credentials, employment history, and criminal records. With a growing emphasis on workplace safety and trust, conducting thorough background checks is critical for informed hiring decisions.
