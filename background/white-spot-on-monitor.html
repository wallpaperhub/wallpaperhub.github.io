Fixing White Spots on Your Monitor: Causes & Solutions

White spots on monitors can be frustrating, impacting viewing experiences. These spots often result from dead pixels, pressure marks, or screen damage. Regular cleanings can help, but if the issue persists, it may be time for professional repair or replacement. Understanding the cause can lead to better maintenance and prolonged monitor life.
