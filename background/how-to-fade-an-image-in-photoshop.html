Fade an Image in Photoshop: Step-by-Step Guide

To fade an image in Photoshop, first, open your image in the program. Select the layer you want to fade. Then, adjust the layer's opacity by moving the opacity slider in the Layers panel. To create a gradient fade, add a layer mask, use the Gradient Tool, and drag across the image to blend it seamlessly.
