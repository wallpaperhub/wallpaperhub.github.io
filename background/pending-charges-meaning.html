Understanding Pending Charges: A Simple Guide

Pending charges refer to transactions not yet completed or reflected in your account balance. These may include recent purchases, holds on funds, or payments initiated but not yet processed. Understanding pending charges is crucial for managing finances, as they can affect available funds and budgeting. Stay informed to avoid overdrafts.
