Unlocking the Power of Background Stories

Background stories are the rich narratives that give depth to characters, events, or places. They shape our understanding and emotional connection to a story, uncovering motivations and secrets. Whether in literature, film, or personal anecdotes, these stories reveal how past experiences influence the present, making them integral to storytelling.
