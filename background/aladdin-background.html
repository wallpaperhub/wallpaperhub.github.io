Discover the Magical World of Aladdin: His Story and Origins

Aladdin is a legendary character from the Middle Eastern folk tales of "One Thousand and One Nights" (Arabian Nights). His story, set in the fictional city of Agrabah, revolves around a young, impoverished man who discovers a magical lamp containing a genie. With the genie’s help, Aladdin overcomes challenges, finds love, and transforms his life.
