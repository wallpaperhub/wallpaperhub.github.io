Discover Fascinating Facts About Europe

Europe is a continent rich in history and culture. It comprises 44 countries and is home to over 740 million people. The European Union enhances economic and political cooperation among member states. Notably, Europe's landmarks include the Eiffel Tower, the Colosseum, and the Acropolis. Diverse languages and cuisines add to its allure.
