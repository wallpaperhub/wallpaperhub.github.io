The Best PowerPoint Background Colors for Impactful Presentations

Choosing the best background color for PowerPoint presentations is crucial for visual appeal and readability. Opt for light colors like pastel shades for a soft look, or dark colors for a bold statement. Contrast your text for clarity; white text on a dark background works well, while black text pairs best with light colors.
