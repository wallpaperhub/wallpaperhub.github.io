Discover the Nativity Scene: Bethlehem's Rich Heritage

Bethlehem, located in the West Bank, holds deep significance as the birthplace of Jesus Christ. This ancient city, with origins dating back to the 14th century BC, is steeped in rich Biblical history. Its notable landmarks include the Church of the Nativity, revered as a pilgrimage site, and the picturesque landscapes that echo the serene night of Jesus' birth.
