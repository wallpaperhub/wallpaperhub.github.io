Sonic Rings: The Iconic Collectibles Explained

Sonic rings are iconic collectibles in the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise, symbolizing speed and adventure. Originating from the 1991 game, these golden rings serve not only as currency but also as a protective shield against foes. Collecting them enhances gameplay, making Sonic's journeys even more exhilarating through vibrant worlds.
