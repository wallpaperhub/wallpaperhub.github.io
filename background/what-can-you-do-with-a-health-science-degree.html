Career Paths with a Health Science Degree

A health science degree opens doors to diverse careers in healthcare, research, and public health. Graduates can become healthcare professionals, laboratory technicians, wellness coordinators, or health administrators. With a focus on improving community health, this degree empowers individuals to make impactful changes in their communities.
