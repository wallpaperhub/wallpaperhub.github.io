Understanding Open Coffins: A Meaningful Farewell

An open coffin is a poignant expression of grief, allowing friends and family to pay their last respects. This practice often symbolizes acceptance and closure, enabling loved ones to confront loss directly. It invites reflection on cherished memories while emphasizing the cycle of life and the importance of shared experiences.
