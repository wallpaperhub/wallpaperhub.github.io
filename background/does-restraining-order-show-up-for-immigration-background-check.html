Do Restraining Orders Impact Immigration Background Checks?

Restraint orders may appear in immigration background checks, depending on the specifics of the situation. Immigration authorities often review criminal records, including arrests and convictions. A restraining order, while not a criminal conviction, can be included in background checks, potentially affecting immigration status.
