Why Did My iPhone Background Disappear? Fixes and Tips

Have you ever experienced your iPhone background disappearing unexpectedly? This can be alarming but often has simple fixes. Common reasons include accidental toggling of settings, glitches after an update, or issues with the wallpaper file. Restarting your device or reapplying the wallpaper can usually resolve the issue quickly!
