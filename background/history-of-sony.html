History of Sony: From Tape Recorders to PlayStation

Sony, founded in 1946 by Masaru Ibuka and Akio Morita, began as a small electronics shop in Tokyo. It revolutionized the music industry with the portable cassette player in the 1970s and the iconic Walkman in 1979. Over the decades, Sony expanded into gaming with PlayStation, film production, and a range of consumer electronics.
