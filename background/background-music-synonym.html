Essential Synonyms for Background Music

Background music, often referred to as "ambient sound" or "incidental music," plays a vital role in enhancing the atmosphere of various settings. It includes any music that isn't the main focus but underlines dialogues, events, or activities, creating an emotional backdrop that enriches experiences in films, videos, and live performances.
