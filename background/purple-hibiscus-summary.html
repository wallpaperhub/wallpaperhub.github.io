Discovering Freedom in "Purple Hibiscus" by Adichie

“Purple Hibiscus” by Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie tells the story of a Nigerian family dominated by a strict, abusive father. Narrated by 15-year-old Kambili, the novel explores themes of oppression, religious fervor, and the quest for voice and freedom. Through Kambili's awakening, we see the beauty and brutality of her world.
