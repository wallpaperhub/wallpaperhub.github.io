Corey Seager: Rising Star in MLB

Corey Seager is an American professional baseball shortstop, currently playing for the Texas Rangers in Major League Baseball (MLB). Known for his exceptional hitting ability and defensive skills, Seager made his MLB debut in 2015 with the Los Angeles Dodgers. He has won multiple accolades, including a World Series title and Rookie of the Year award.
