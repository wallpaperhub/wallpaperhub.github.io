Clarence Thomas: A Supreme Court Legacy

Clarence Thomas, the Supreme Court's longest-serving justice, has been a pivotal figure in American law since his appointment in 1991. Known for his originalist views, Thomas often advocates for limited government and staunchly defends individual rights. His controversial decisions and public statements spark ongoing debate about race and justice.
