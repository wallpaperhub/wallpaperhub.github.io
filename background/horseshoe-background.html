Lucky Horseshoe Background: Symbolism & History

Horseshoes have a rich history, often symbolizing good luck and protection. Traditionally made of iron and shaped like a U, they are thought to bring fortune to those who find them. In various cultures, horseshoes are hung above doorways to ward off evil spirits. Their unique shape and sturdy design make them iconic in equestrian life.
