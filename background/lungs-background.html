Understanding Lungs: Vital Organs for Health

Lungs are vital organs responsible for gas exchange, bringing in oxygen and expelling carbon dioxide. They are located in the thoracic cavity and protected by the ribcage. Comprised of millions of alveoli, lungs play a key role in respiratory health, influencing overall bodily functions and endurance during physical activities.
