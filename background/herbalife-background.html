Herbalife: A Global Leader in Nutrition and Wellness

Herbalife, founded in 1980 by Mark Hughes, is a global nutrition company specializing in weight management, nutritional supplements, and personal care products. The company promotes healthy lifestyles through its diverse range of products, designed to help individuals meet their dietary and fitness goals. Herbalife operates in over 90 countries.
