Yayoi Kusama: The Enduring Legacy of a Living Artist

Yayoi Kusama, the iconic Japanese artist known for her avant-garde work, is alive and continues to create. Born on March 22, 1929, she remains a vibrant figure in the art world, celebrated for her bold installations and polka dot patterns. Her influence and contributions to contemporary art are immense and ongoing.
