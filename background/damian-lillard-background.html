Damian Lillard: Basketball Star and Trailblazer

Damian Lillard, born on July 15, 1990, in Oakland, California, is an elite NBA player known for his scoring ability and leadership. A standout at Weber State University, he was selected by the Portland Trail Blazers as the sixth overall pick in the 2012 NBA Draft. Lillard has earned multiple All-Star selections and is renowned for his clutch performances.
