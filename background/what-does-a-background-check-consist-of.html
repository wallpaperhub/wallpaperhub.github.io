Understanding Background Checks: What They Include

A background check typically consists of verifying an individual's identity, checking criminal history, examining credit reports, and confirming employment and education qualifications. It may also include social media scrutiny and reference checks to ensure a comprehensive assessment of the person's history and background for employment or tenancy purposes.
