Essential Deposition Questions: Examples & Tips

Deposition questions typically cover a range of topics to gather evidence. Examples include inquiries about the deponent's background, expert opinions, observations related to the case, prior statements, and document reviews. Questions may also address the circumstances surrounding the incident in question and any relevant experiences.
