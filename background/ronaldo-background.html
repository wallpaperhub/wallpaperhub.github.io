Ronaldo: The Rise of a Football Legend

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal, grew up in a modest background. His father worked as a kit man, while his mother was a cook. Despite financial challenges, Ronaldo's talent in football emerged early, leading him to join Sporting Lisbon's youth academy. His dedication and hard work propelled him to global superstardom.
