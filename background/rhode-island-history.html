Discover Rhode Island's Rich History

Rhode Island, the smallest state in the U.S., was founded in 1636 by Roger Williams, who sought religious freedom. It was the first colony to declare independence from British rule in 1776. Known for its maritime history and industry, Rhode Island played a significant role during the American Revolution and became a key player in the Industrial Revolution.
