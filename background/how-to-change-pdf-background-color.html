Change PDF Background Color: Easy Step-by-Step Guide

To change the background color of a PDF, first, use a PDF editor like Adobe Acrobat or an online tool. Open your PDF and select the page or pages you want to modify. Look for a background option in the editing menu. Choose your desired color and apply it. Save your changes to preserve the new look of your PDF.
