GameStop: The Rise of Retail Investing

GameStop, a gaming and electronics retailer, gained monumental attention in early 2021 due to a short squeeze initiated by retail investors on platforms like Reddit. This phenomenon not only disrupted traditional market dynamics but also sparked a broader discussion about the power of collective action in finance. The event showcased the influence of social media on investing.
