Understanding Need-Based Financial Aid

Financial aid based on a family's financial background is known as need-based aid. This type of financial assistance helps students who have demonstrated financial need, ensuring they can access education regardless of their economic situation. It often includes grants, scholarships, work-study programs, and loans.
