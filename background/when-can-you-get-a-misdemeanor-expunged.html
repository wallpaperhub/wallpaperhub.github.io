How to Expunge a Misdemeanor: Eligibility & Process

To get a misdemeanor expunged, you typically must wait a certain period, often ranging from one to five years after completing your sentence, including probation and fines. Eligibility varies by state and offense type. Consult local laws or an attorney to determine your specific situation and ensure proper filing.
