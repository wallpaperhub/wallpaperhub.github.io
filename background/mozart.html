Mozart: The Genius of Classical Music

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, born in 1756, was a prolific and influential composer of the Classical era. Renowned for his operas, symphonies, and chamber music, he created masterpieces like "The Magic Flute" and "Symphony No. 40." His genius transcends time, leaving a lasting legacy that continues to inspire musicians and audiences worldwide.
