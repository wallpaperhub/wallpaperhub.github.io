Seamlessly Blend Two Images in Photoshop

To blend two images in Photoshop, start by opening both images in separate layers. Select the top layer and apply a layer mask. Use the Gradient Tool to create a smooth transition between the images by dragging from the area you want to reveal towards the area you want to hide. Adjust opacity for fine-tuning. Save your work!
