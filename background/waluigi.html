Waluigi: The Iconic Antagonist of the Mario Universe

Waluigi, the lanky, mustachioed counterpart of Luigi, made his debut in Mario Tennis. Known for his mischievous antics and distinctive purple attire, he often plays the role of an antagonist in various Mario games. With a flair for the dramatic and a unique personality, Waluigi has cultivated a dedicated cult following over the years.
