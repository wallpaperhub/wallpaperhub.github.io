What Disqualifies You from Being a Real Estate Agent in Florida?

In Florida, several factors can disqualify you from becoming a real estate agent. A felony conviction, especially involving fraud or theft, can bar you if it occurred within the last 15 years. Additionally, failing to pass the required state exams or providing false information during the licensing process will also disqualify you.
