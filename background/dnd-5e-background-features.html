D&D 5E Background Features Explained

In D&D 5E, background features provide unique traits that enhance character backstories and gameplay. These traits offer abilities like gathering information, finding shelter, or using contacts for help. They enrich role-playing experiences, enabling players to creatively interact with the world and deepen their characters’ narratives.
