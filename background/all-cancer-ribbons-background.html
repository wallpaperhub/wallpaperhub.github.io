Understanding Cancer Ribbons: Colors & Meanings

Cancer ribbons symbolize support and awareness for various types of cancer. Each color represents a different cancer type, from pink for breast cancer to blue for prostate cancer. Wearing these ribbons fosters solidarity, educates the public, and honors those affected. Understanding their meanings can help raise awareness and inspire action.
