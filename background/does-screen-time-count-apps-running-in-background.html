Does Background App Use Count Towards Screen Time?

Screen time does account for apps running in the background, but the extent depends on the device settings and operating system. Many smartphones measure active engagement and the time apps are in use, even if they're not in the foreground. Limiting background processes can help manage overall screen time effectively.
