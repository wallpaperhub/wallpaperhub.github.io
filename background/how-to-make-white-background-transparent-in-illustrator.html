How to Easily Make White Backgrounds Transparent in Illustrator

To make a white background transparent in Illustrator, start by selecting your image with the Selection Tool. Then, go to "Window" > "Image Trace" and check "Preview." Set the "Threshold" to detect the edges, and in the "Advanced" options, uncheck "White" to remove the white background. Expand the image to finalize.
