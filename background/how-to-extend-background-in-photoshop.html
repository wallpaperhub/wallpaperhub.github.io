Extend Your Background in Photoshop: A Quick Guide

To extend the background in Photoshop, start by selecting the area around your subject using the Lasso or Magic Wand tool. Then, copy the selection and paste it to a new layer. Use the Transform tool to stretch or reposition the background. Finally, utilize the Clone Stamp or Healing Brush to blend edges and create a seamless look.
