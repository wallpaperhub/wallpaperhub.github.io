Discover South Park: A Cultural Phenomenon

South Park, created by Trey Parker and Matt Stone, first aired in 1997 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Set in the fictional town of South Park, Colorado, the show is renowned for its satirical humor that tackles contemporary issues, politics, and social topics. Its unique animation style and bold storytelling set it apart in the world of adult animated television.
