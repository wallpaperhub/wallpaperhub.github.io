How to Add a Background in Premiere Pro: A Step-by-Step Guide

To add a background in Premiere Pro, start by importing your background image or video into the project panel. Drag it onto the timeline beneath your main video layer. Ensure the background layer is scaled and positioned correctly in the program monitor. You can also apply effects like blur to help your main content stand out.
