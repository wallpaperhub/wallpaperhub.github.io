Can Employers Conduct Background Checks Without Consent?

In most cases, employers cannot run a background check without the candidate's permission, especially for sensitive information. The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) mandates that employers obtain written consent before checking background records. Always read the fine print on job applications regarding background checks.
