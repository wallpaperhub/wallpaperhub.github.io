Unraveling Team Rocket: The Iconic Villains of Pokémon

Team Rocket, the infamous trio from the Pokémon universe, comprises Jessie, James, and the adorable Meowth. Known for their comical attempts to steal Pokémon, particularly Pikachu, they have become iconic villains throughout various Pokémon series. Their backstory includes a shared dream of team success, complicated pasts, and a quirky camaraderie.
