Essential CDL Background Check Requirements Explained

To obtain a Commercial Driver's License (CDL), applicants must undergo a thorough background check. Requirements may include a review of criminal history, driving records, and employment verification. Disqualifications can arise from serious offenses like DUI or felony convictions, ensuring only qualified individuals operate commercial vehicles.
