Zoom Background Best Practices for Professional Video Calls

To make the most of your Zoom background, choose a clean, uncluttered space to avoid distractions. Opt for professional settings or subtle patterns to maintain focus on you. Ensure good lighting and avoid bright colors that can be overwhelming. Test your background before meetings to ensure everything appears as intended.
