Discover the Rich History of the University of Arizona

Established in 1885, the University of Arizona (UA) is one of the oldest universities in the American Southwest. It began with a handful of students and has grown into a prestigious research institution known for its innovative programs, distinguished faculty, and commitment to inclusivity. UA has significantly contributed to advancements in science, technology, and the arts.
