Do Background Checks Call Previous Employers?

Yes, background checks often involve contacting previous employers to verify employment history, job titles, and tenure. This process helps employers confirm a candidate's qualifications and assess reliability. However, consent from the candidate is usually required before such checks can be conducted to comply with legal regulations.
