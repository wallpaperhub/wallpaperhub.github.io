Kaskus: Indonesia's Leading Online Community

Kaskus, founded in 1999, is Indonesia's largest online community and forum. Initially created as a space for Indonesian students abroad, it has evolved into a vibrant marketplace and discussion platform. Kaskus offers diverse content, including tech, lifestyle, and entertainment, sustaining engagement through user-generated discussions.
