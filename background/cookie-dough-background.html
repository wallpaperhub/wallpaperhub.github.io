Delicious Cookie Dough: A Sweet History

Cookie dough, a beloved treat, traces its origins to the early 20th century when bakers began tasting their mixtures before baking. This sweet blend of flour, sugar, butter, and eggs became increasingly popular, leading to innovations like edible cookie dough. Today, it’s enjoyed raw, baked, or as ice cream, captivating dessert lovers everywhere.
