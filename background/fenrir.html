Discover Fenrir: The Legendary Wolf of Norse Mythology

Fenrir, the monstrous wolf from Norse mythology, is a symbol of chaos and destruction. Born of the trickster god Loki, Fenrir is prophesied to bring about the end of the world during Ragnarök. His strength and ferocity are legendary, and he embodies the inevitable clash between fate and free will in myth.
