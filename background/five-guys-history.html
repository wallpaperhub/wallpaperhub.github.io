Five Guys: A Brief History of a Burger Icon

Five Guys was founded in 1986 by the Murrell family in Arlington, Virginia. Initially, it was a hot dog and burger joint catering to local customers. The brand quickly gained popularity for its customizable burgers and fresh, hand-cut fries. Today, Five Guys has expanded globally, offering its classic American diner experience.
