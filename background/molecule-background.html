Understanding Molecules: The Building Blocks of Matter

Molecules are the fundamental building blocks of matter, composed of atoms bonded together in fixed proportions. They can range from simple diatomic structures like O2 to complex organic compounds. Understanding molecular structure and interactions is crucial in various scientific fields, including chemistry, biology, and pharmacology.
