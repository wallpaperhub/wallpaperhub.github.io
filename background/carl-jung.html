Carl Jung: Exploring the Mind and Archetypes

Carl Jung was a Swiss psychiatrist and psychoanalyst who founded analytical psychology. He introduced concepts such as the collective unconscious, archetypes, and introversion/extroversion, shaping modern psychology and therapy. Jung’s work emphasizes the importance of the spiritual, personal growth, and self-actualization.
