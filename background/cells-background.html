Understanding Cell Background: The Building Blocks of Life

Cells serve as the fundamental units of life, each performing unique functions that contribute to the overall health and functionality of organisms. From the simple prokaryotic cells to the complex eukaryotic ones, their diverse structures and processes, including metabolism, reproduction, and responsiveness to stimuli, form the backbone of biological systems.
