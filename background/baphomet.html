Understanding Baphomet: Symbol of Duality in Occultism

Baphomet is a symbolic figure often associated with occultism and mysticism, portraying a goat-headed deity. It embodies the duality of existence, juxtaposing male and female, light and dark. Often misunderstood, Baphomet symbolizes enlightenment, wisdom, and the pursuit of knowledge while challenging conventional beliefs in religion and morality.
