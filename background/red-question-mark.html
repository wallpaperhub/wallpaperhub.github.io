Red Question Mark: What Does It Mean?

The red question mark symbolizes inquiry, curiosity, and a quest for knowledge. It often appears in digital interfaces to indicate help or troubleshooting options. This vibrant icon draws attention and encourages users to seek answers, embodying the spirit of exploration and the pursuit of understanding in both learning platforms and everyday tasks.
