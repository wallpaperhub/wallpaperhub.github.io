Arnold Schwarzenegger: From Bodybuilder to Hollywood Legend

Arnold Schwarzenegger is a multifaceted icon known for his remarkable journey from bodybuilding champion to Hollywood superstar and political figure. Born in Austria in 1947, he gained fame with films like "The Terminator" and "Predator." As California's governor, he championed fitness and environmental initiatives, inspiring millions worldwide.
