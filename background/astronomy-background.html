A Beginner's Guide to Understanding Astronomy

Astronomy, the study of celestial objects and phenomena, has roots in ancient civilizations who observed celestial patterns for navigation and farming. Modern astronomy employs advanced technology, from telescopes to space missions, to explore the universe's mysteries. This field merges science with philosophy, unveiling the cosmos' wonders and our place within it.
