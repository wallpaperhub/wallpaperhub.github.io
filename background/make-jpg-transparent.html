Make JPG Images Transparent: Easy Steps!

To make a JPG transparent, you need to convert it to a format that supports transparency, like PNG. Use photo editing software like Adobe Photoshop or online tools such as Canva or Pixlr. Simply open your JPG, use the magic wand or lasso tool to select the area to be made transparent, delete it, and save the image.
