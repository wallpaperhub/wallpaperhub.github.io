Understanding Background: Definition & Importance

Background refers to the context or setting in which an event occurs or a person exists. It can encompass various aspects, including cultural, historical, or personal factors that shape understanding. In art, background can also mean the part of an image that recedes into the distance, providing depth and relationship to the foreground.
