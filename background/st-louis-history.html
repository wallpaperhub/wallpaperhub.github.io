Discover St. Louis History: From Founding to Iconic Landmarks

St. Louis, founded in 1764, boasts a rich history marked by its strategic location on the Mississippi River. It flourished as a trade hub, played a key role in westward expansion, and witnessed significant events like the 1904 World's Fair. Its iconic Gateway Arch symbolizes both innovation and the spirit of exploration.
