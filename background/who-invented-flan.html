Who Invented Flan? Discover Flan's Origins

Flan, a delicious custard dessert, has a rich history that dates back to ancient Rome, where it was originally made with fish and eggs. Over centuries, it evolved through various cultures, particularly in Spain and Latin America. Today, flan is beloved worldwide for its creamy texture and caramel flavor.
