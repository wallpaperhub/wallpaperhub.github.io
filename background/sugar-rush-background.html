Understanding the Sugar Rush: Causes & Effects

A "sugar rush" refers to the burst of energy and excitement experienced after consuming a high-sugar food or drink. This phenomenon is often temporary, leading to a subsequent crash in energy levels. Understanding the sugar rush involves exploring its physiological effects, emotional responses, and cultural significance in various dietary habits.
