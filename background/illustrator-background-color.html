How to Set Background Color in Illustrator: A Quick Guide

When working in Illustrator, selecting the background color is crucial for visual clarity. You can easily set the background color by creating a rectangle that covers your entire artboard. This rectangle should be placed at the back of your layers. Choose your desired color from the color picker or swatches to enhance your design effectively.
