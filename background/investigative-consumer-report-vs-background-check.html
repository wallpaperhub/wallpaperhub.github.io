Investigative Consumer Reports vs Background Checks: What You Need to Know

Investigative consumer reports delve deeper than standard background checks, analyzing personal history, credit, and even social media activity. While background checks typically focus on criminal history and employment verification, investigative reports provide a comprehensive view, essential for informed decision-making in hiring or leasing.
