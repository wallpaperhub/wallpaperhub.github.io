How to Create Transparent Backgrounds in Krita

Krita offers a powerful feature for creating transparent backgrounds, enhancing your artwork's versatility. To achieve this, simply use the 'Add Alpha Channel' option under the Layers menu, enabling transparency. You can then easily erase or mask areas, allowing for creative designs that seamlessly integrate with various projects or presentations.
