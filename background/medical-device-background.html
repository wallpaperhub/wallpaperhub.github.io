Understanding Medical Devices: A Comprehensive Overview

Medical devices are essential tools in healthcare, aiding in diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment. They range from simple items like thermometers to complex technologies such as MRI machines. Their development is propelled by advancements in technology and the increasing need for effective healthcare solutions, ensuring better patient outcomes.
