Combatting Global Warming: Simple Steps for a Greener Future

Global warming is the long-term rise in Earth's temperatures due to human activities, primarily the burning of fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes. This leads to severe environmental changes, including melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather events, threatening ecosystems and human livelihoods worldwide.
