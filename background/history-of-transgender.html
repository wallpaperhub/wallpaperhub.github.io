"Exploring the Rich History of Transgender Identity"

The history of transgender individuals spans centuries, with evidence dating back to ancient cultures that recognized fluid gender identities. From the Two-Spirit people of Indigenous tribes to the hijra communities in South Asia, transgender identities have long been part of human diversity. Despite historical challenges, activists today continue to advocate for rights and recognition worldwide.
