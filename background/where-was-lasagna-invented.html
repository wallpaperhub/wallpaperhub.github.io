Where Was Lasagna Invented? Discover Its Origins!

Lasagna, a beloved dish made of layers of pasta, cheese, and sauce, is thought to have originated from Ancient Greece, where a similar dish called "Laganon" was created using layers of pasta and sauce. However, it was Italy, particularly Naples, that popularized modern lasagna in the 19th century, solidifying its place in Italian cuisine.
