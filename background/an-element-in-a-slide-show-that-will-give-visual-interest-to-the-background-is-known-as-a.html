Enhancing Slide Shows: The Power of Background Graphics

An element in a slide show that adds visual interest to the background is known as a "background graphic" or "dynamic background." These elements can include images, textures, or animations that enhance the overall aesthetic, making the presentation more engaging and appealing to the audience.
