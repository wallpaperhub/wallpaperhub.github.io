Understanding GPS: The Technology Behind Global Positioning

GPS (Global Positioning System) originated from U.S. military technology in the 1970s, aimed at enhancing navigation and logistics. It relies on a network of satellites that transmit signals to GPS receivers, enabling precise location tracking. Today, GPS is integral to various applications, including navigation, mapping, and outdoor activities.
