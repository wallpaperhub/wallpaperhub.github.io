Enhance Your Browsing with Safari Dark Mode

Safari dark mode is a feature designed to enhance your browsing experience by reducing eye strain and conserving battery life on devices. When activated, it changes the overall color scheme of the browser to darker tones, making it easier to read at night or in low-light environments. Enjoy a sleeker, stylish interface!
