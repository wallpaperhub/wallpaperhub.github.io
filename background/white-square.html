"The Meaning and Uses of the White Square"

A white square symbolizes simplicity, purity, and minimalism. Its clean lines and uniform shape evoke a sense of balance and calm. In art and design, it serves as a blank canvas for creativity, inviting exploration of color and form. Whether used in architecture or graphic design, the white square remains timeless and versatile.
