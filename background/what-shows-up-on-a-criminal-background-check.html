Understanding Criminal Background Checks: What's Included

A criminal background check typically reveals a person's criminal history, including arrests, convictions, and pending charges. It may also show sex offender status, civil judgments, and, in some cases, employment history. Variations in the extent of information may occur based on state laws and the type of check conducted.
