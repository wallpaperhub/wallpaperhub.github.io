Unveiling the Met Gala: A Night of Fashion and Charity

The Met Gala, officially known as the Costume Institute Gala, is an annual fundraising gala benefiting the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute in New York City. It marks the grand opening of the Costume Institute's annual fashion exhibit. Celebrities and fashion icons don extravagant attire, showcasing their creativity and individual style. The event's theme varies each year, influencing fashion trends and generating widespread media attention.
