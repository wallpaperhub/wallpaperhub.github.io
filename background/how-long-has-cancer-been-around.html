How Long Has Cancer Existed?

Cancer has existed for thousands of years, with the earliest documented cases dating back to ancient Egypt around 1600 BC. The term "cancer" originates from the Greek physician Hippocrates, who used the word "carcinos" to describe tumors. Over time, cancer has been recognized in various forms and has significantly impacted human health.
