Discovering the Legacy of Prada: Founder Mario Prada

Prada was founded by Mario Prada in 1913 in Milan, Italy, initially as a leather goods shop. His innovative designs and commitment to quality established the brand's reputation. After his passing, his granddaughter Miuccia Prada took the helm, revolutionizing the luxury fashion industry with her avant-garde style and strategic branding, making Prada a symbol of sophistication.
