Toilet Paper: A Brief History & Usage Essentials

Toilet paper, an essential household item, has a rich history dating back to ancient China in the 6th century. Initially made from rice straw and bamboo, it evolved into the soft, disposable rolls we know today. This ubiquitous product is not only practical but also plays a role in promoting hygiene and comfort worldwide.
