Creative Background Ideas for Eye-Catching Posters

When designing a poster, choosing the right background is crucial. It sets the tone and enhances the visibility of the main message. Opt for colors that align with the theme, considering both contrast and harmony. Patterns or images can add depth, but ensure they don’t overpower the text. A well-chosen background enhances focus and engagement.
