The English Flag: Symbol of Heritage and Pride

The English flag, known as the St. George's Cross, features a bold red cross on a white background. It symbolizes courage and valor, honoring England's patron saint, St. George. The flag is a prominent emblem at sporting events, national celebrations, and cultural festivities, representing English heritage and pride.
