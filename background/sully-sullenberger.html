Sully Sullenberger: Hero of the Hudson

Sully Sullenberger, an American hero and retired airline pilot, gained worldwide fame for successfully landing US Airways Flight 1549 on the Hudson River in 2009 after both engines failed. His quick thinking and professionalism prevented a catastrophe, earning him accolades and making him a symbol of leadership and calm under pressure.
