Sally Face: Unraveling the Haunting Secrets

Sally Face is a unique indie horror adventure game that explores the life of a boy named Sally, who wears a mask to conceal his disfigured face. Set in a mysterious apartment complex, players uncover dark secrets, navigate spooky environments, and build memorable character relationships. It's a tale of tragedy, friendship, and the paranormal.
