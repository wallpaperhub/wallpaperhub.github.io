Essential Pennsylvania Background Check Requirements for Healthcare Workers

In Pennsylvania, healthcare workers must undergo background checks to ensure patient safety. These checks typically include criminal history, child abuse clearance, and, for specific roles, additional federal checks. Healthcare facilities are responsible for verifying that workers comply with these regulations before employment.
