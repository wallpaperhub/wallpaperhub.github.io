How to Change Text Color on iPhone: A Quick Guide

To change text color on your iPhone, you can modify settings in various apps. In the Notes app, for instance, select the text, then tap the "Aa" option to choose a color. For accessibility options, go to Settings > Accessibility > Display & Text Size, and enable Color Filters for customizable text colors across your device.
