Handling Felony Questions on Job Applications

When applying for a job, many applications include a question about felony convictions. It's important to answer truthfully, as dishonesty can lead to disqualification. Consider the relevance of your conviction to the job, and if necessary, prepare to discuss it in a positive light, focusing on rehabilitation and growth.
