Gettysburg: The Turning Point of the Civil War

Gettysburg, a small town in Pennsylvania, is renowned for its pivotal role in the Civil War. The Battle of Gettysburg, fought from July 1 to 3, 1863, marked a turning point in the war, resulting in a significant Union victory. The site is now home to the Gettysburg National Military Park, preserving its rich history.
