Who Owns Liquid Death? Discover the Brand Behind the Canned Water

Liquid Death is owned by the company Liquid Death Mountain Water, founded by Mike Cessario in 2019. The brand, known for its unique canned mountain water, gained popularity for its unconventional marketing approach, targeting a younger audience while promoting sustainability. It has garnered investment from various venture capitalists, emphasizing its growth potential.
