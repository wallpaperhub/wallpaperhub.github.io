The Evolution of QVC: A Brief History

QVC, founded in 1986 by Joseph Segel, revolutionized home shopping with its live broadcasts, allowing viewers to shop while watching. It began as a cable retailer and quickly expanded into a multimedia platform, offering various products, including fashion, beauty, and home goods. Today, QVC remains a leader in the shopping network industry, blending entertainment and commerce.
