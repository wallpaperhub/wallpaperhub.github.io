Understanding Fiber Optics: The Future of Data Transmission

Fiber optics is a technology that uses thin strands of glass or plastic to transmit data as light signals. This method is advantageous due to its high speed, large bandwidth, and resistance to electromagnetic interference. Originally developed for telecommunications, fiber optics now plays a crucial role in internet connectivity, cable TV, and medical applications.
