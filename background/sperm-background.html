Essential Insights on Sperm Quality and Health

Sperm, the male reproductive cell, plays a crucial role in human reproduction. Each sperm cell is a unique genetic package, combining DNA from both parents to create new life. Produced in the testes, sperm undergo maturation in the epididymis before being transported during ejaculation. Understanding sperm health is vital for fertility.
