Cleveland Indians Legacy: A Rich Baseball History

The Cleveland Indians, now known as the Guardians, are a Major League Baseball team founded in 1901. They are based in Cleveland, Ohio, and have a rich history, including two World Series championships in 1920 and 1948. The team has evolved over the years, reflecting changes in culture and society while maintaining a deep connection to its passionate fanbase.
