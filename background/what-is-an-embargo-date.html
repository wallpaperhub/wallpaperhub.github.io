Understanding Embargo Dates: What You Need to Know

An embargo date is a predetermined deadline that restricts the release of information to the public until a specified time. Typically used in journalism, publishing, and business, embargoes ensure that all parties have equal access to news or materials at the same time, maintaining fairness and preparing for informed coverage.
