Explore the Beauty of Impressionism: Art That Captures Life

Impressionism is an art movement that emerged in the late 19th century, characterized by a focus on capturing the effects of light, color, and everyday scenes. Renowned artists like Monet, Degas, and Renoir challenged traditional techniques, opting for loose brushwork and vibrant palettes, creating lively representations of the world around them.
