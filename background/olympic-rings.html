Understanding the Olympic Rings: Unity in Sport

The Olympic Rings, designed by Pierre de Coubertin, symbolize the unity of the five continents: Asia, Africa, America, Europe, and Oceania. Each ring's color represents a different region, coming together to embody the spirit of the Olympic Games. The rings are a universal emblem of peace, competition, and international friendship.
