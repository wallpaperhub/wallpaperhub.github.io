Dark Mode on Chrome for iPhone: How to Enable It

Dark mode on Chrome for iPhone offers a sleek, visually appealing interface, reducing eye strain during nighttime browsing. To enable dark mode, simply go to your iPhone's settings, select Display & Brightness, and choose Dark. This not only enhances your browsing experience but also saves battery life, making it a popular choice among users.
