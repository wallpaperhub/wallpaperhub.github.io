"The Art and Meaning of Kiss Marks"

A kiss mark, also known as a lip print, is a romantic symbol made by pressing lips against a surface, often leaving behind a vibrant trace. These marks can convey affection, love, or desire, and are frequently used in art, greeting cards, and social media to express emotions. They capture a fleeting moment of intimacy, making them memorable.
