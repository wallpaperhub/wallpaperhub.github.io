How Much Do Background Actors Make? Insights & Earnings

Background actors, often referred to as extras, typically earn between $100 to $200 per day, depending on the production and location. Some may receive minimum wage, while others in unionized positions can earn higher pay. Factors affecting payment include experience, demand, and the scale of the project.
