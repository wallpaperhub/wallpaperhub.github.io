Key Factors Influencing Social Mobility Beyond Family Background

Education plays a larger role than family background in social mobility. The level of education influences job opportunities, income potential, and professional networks. A strong educational foundation can help individuals overcome socioeconomic barriers, enabling them to achieve upward mobility regardless of their origins.
