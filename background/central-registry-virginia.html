Virginia Central Registry: A Key Resource for Child Safety

The Central Registry in Virginia is a vital resource that maintains essential records for child abuse and neglect cases. It aids in ensuring the safety of children by allowing agencies to track histories for potential guardians and caretakers. This registry supports child protection policies and ensures informed decisions in sensitive situations.
