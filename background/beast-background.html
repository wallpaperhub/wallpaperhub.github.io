Understanding Beasts: Origins & Mythology

Beasts have long captivated human imagination, often depicted as powerful, mythical creatures. From ancient folklore to modern fantasy, these beings are characterized by their strength and primal nature. Their backgrounds vary, with some originating from dark legends, while others emerge as guardians of nature, embodying the wild spirit within.
