Understanding the History of Pollution: From Ancient Times to Today

Pollution has a long history, tracing back to ancient civilizations when smoke from fires and waste disposal led to health issues. The Industrial Revolution marked a significant increase in air and water pollution due to factories and urbanization. Modern awareness of pollution emerged in the 20th century, prompting regulations and global movements for environmental protection.
