Pong Game Background: The Birth of Arcade Gaming

Pong, developed by Atari in 1972, is often credited as one of the first arcade video games. The simplistic two-dimensional design features two paddles and a ball, captivating players with its competitive gameplay. Pong has significantly influenced the gaming industry, sparking interest that led to the evolution of modern video games.
