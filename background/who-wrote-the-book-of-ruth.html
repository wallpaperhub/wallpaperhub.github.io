Who Authored the Book of Ruth?

The Book of Ruth, found in the Hebrew Bible, is traditionally attributed to the prophet Samuel. However, scholarly opinion varies, with some suggesting that it may have been written by an anonymous author during the post-exilic period. The narrative follows Ruth, a Moabite woman, and her journey of loyalty and faithfulness.
