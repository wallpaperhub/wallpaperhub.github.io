Working in Schools with a Felony Record: What You Need to Know

Yes, you can work at a school with a felony, but opportunities may be limited. Many schools conduct background checks, and each state has different laws regarding employment in education. Certain felonies, especially those involving children, could disqualify you. It's essential to check local regulations and seek legal advice.
