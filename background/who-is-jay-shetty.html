Who is Jay Shetty? Exploring the Life of a Modern Monk

Jay Shetty is a British author, former monk, and internet personality known for his motivational content and mindfulness teachings. He gained fame through his viral videos on social media, sharing wisdom on relationships, mental health, and personal growth. His book "Think Like a Monk" delves into his experiences and insights on achieving peace and purpose.
