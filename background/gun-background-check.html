Understanding Gun Background Checks: What You Need to Know

A gun background check is an essential process that verifies an individual's eligibility to purchase a firearm. Conducted by licensed dealers, it examines criminal history, mental health status, and other factors to promote responsible gun ownership. These checks aim to enhance public safety and prevent firearms from falling into the wrong hands.
