Nitrogen: The Essential Element for Life & Industry

Nitrogen is a colorless, odorless gas that comprises about 78% of Earth's atmosphere. Essential for life, it is a key component of amino acids, proteins, and nucleic acids. In agriculture, nitrogen is a vital nutrient, and in industry, it is used in the production of fertilizers, explosives, and various chemical processes.
