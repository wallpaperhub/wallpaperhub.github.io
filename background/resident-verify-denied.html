What to Do When Resident Verify is Denied

When a resident verification is denied, it can lead to frustration and uncertainty. Reasons may include issues with background checks, income verification, or lacking necessary documentation. It's essential to review the denial carefully, address any discrepancies, and consider reaching out to the landlord for clarification or alternative solutions.
