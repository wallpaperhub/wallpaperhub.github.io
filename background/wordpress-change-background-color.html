Change Your WordPress Background Color Easily

Changing the background color in WordPress is easy! Navigate to your dashboard, click on "Appearance," and select "Customize." Look for the "Colors" or "Background" option. Here, you can pick a new color using the color picker, or enter a hex code. Save changes to see your new background live!
