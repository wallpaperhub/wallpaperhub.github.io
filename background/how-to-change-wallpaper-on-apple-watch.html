How to Change Wallpaper on Your Apple Watch

To change the wallpaper on your Apple Watch, press the Digital Crown to open the app menu, then select "Settings." Tap "Brightness & Text Size," scroll down to "Wallpaper," and choose either "Photo" or "Color." If you choose a photo, pick an image from your library, adjust it as needed, and set it as your new watch face.
