History of Grand Canyon University: A Journey Through Education

Grand Canyon University, founded in 1949 in Phoenix, Arizona, began as a private Christian college. It initially served as a Baptist institution, emphasizing education grounded in faith. Over the years, it evolved into a comprehensive university, offering diverse undergraduate and graduate programs, and became a leading online educational provider.
