Bojack Horseman: The Ultimate Guide

Bojack Horseman is an animated series that follows the life of a washed-up actor, Bojack, a horse in a world of anthropomorphic animals and humans. The show delves into themes like mental health, addiction, and the complexities of fame. With its dark humor and poignant storytelling, it captures the struggles of finding purpose and happiness.
