Explore the Unique World of Giraffes

Giraffes are majestic creatures native to Africa, known for their long necks and unique spotted coats. They inhabit savannas, grasslands, and open woodlands, where they gracefully browse on foliage from tall trees. As the tallest mammals on Earth, giraffes can reach heights of up to 18 feet, making them remarkable spectacles in the wild.
