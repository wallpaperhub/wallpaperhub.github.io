Can You Sue an Employer for Lying About Your Background Check?

Yes, you may have grounds to sue your employer if they lied about your background check, especially if it led to wrongful termination or damaged your reputation. Consult with an employment attorney to discuss your situation, as various state laws govern these cases. Document everything for a strong case.
