Effortlessly Remove Image Backgrounds on Mac

To remove the background from an image on a Mac, you can use built-in tools like Preview or Photos. In Preview, open your image, select the Markup tool, and then use the Instant Alpha feature to remove unwanted areas. Alternatively, use dedicated software like Adobe Photoshop for more advanced editing.
