Effortlessly Remove Backgrounds from Pictures on iPhone

To remove the background from a picture on your iPhone, open the Photos app and select your image. Tap 'Edit', then use the 'Markup' tool. Select the lasso or magnifying glass to highlight the subject you want to keep, then use the eraser to remove the background. Save your edited image for a clean look!
