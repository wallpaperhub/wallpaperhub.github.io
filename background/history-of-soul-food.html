Discover the Rich History of Soul Food

Soul food originated from the culinary traditions of African Americans in the Southern United States, shaped by the experiences of slavery and cultural heritage. Dishes like collard greens, fried chicken, and cornbread reflect resourcefulness and community. This cuisine celebrates family, resilience, and a rich history that continues to thrive today.
