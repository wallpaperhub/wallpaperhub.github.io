Do Speeding Tickets Affect Your Driving Record?

Yes, speeding tickets typically go on your driving record, impacting your insurance rates and driving privileges. Most states report tickets to the DMV, and they can remain on your record for several years. The duration varies by state and the severity of the violation. It's important to understand the consequences of receiving a ticket.
