The Fascinating History and Benefits of Eggs

Eggs are a crucial part of many diets, rich in proteins, vitamins, and essential fatty acids. Historically, eggs have been consumed for thousands of years, with chickens being domesticated over 8,000 years ago for their nutritious offerings. Eggs are versatile in cooking, serving various culinary traditions worldwide.
