Why Is My Background Check Taking So Long?

When a background check takes too long, it can lead to delays in the hiring process, increased anxiety for candidates, and potential loss of talent for companies. Various factors, such as incomplete information or high volumes of requests, can slow down the process. Understanding these reasons can help manage expectations and improve communication.
