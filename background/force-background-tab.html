Optimize Your Browsing: Force Background Tabs

Force background tab refers to the ability to run browser tabs in the background without interrupting their functionality. This feature allows users to keep their activities active, such as downloads, video streams, or web applications, even when they navigate away to other tabs. Users gain efficiency and multitasking capabilities, enhancing their overall browsing experience.
