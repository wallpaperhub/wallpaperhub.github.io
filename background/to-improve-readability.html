Boost Readability: Tips for Clearer Writing

To improve readability, focus on using short sentences and clear language. Break text into smaller paragraphs and use bullet points for lists. Choose a readable font and adequate spacing to enhance visual appeal. Additionally, consider the use of headings to guide the reader through the content, making it easier to scan and understand.
