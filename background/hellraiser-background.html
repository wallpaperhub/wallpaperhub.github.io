Hellraiser: Unveiling the Dark Legacy of the Cenobites

Hellraiser, a horror franchise created by Clive Barker, originated from his novella "The Hellbound Heart." The series revolves around the sinister Cenobites, led by the iconic Pinhead, who torment souls that opens the Lament Configuration puzzle box. Blending themes of pain and desire, Hellraiser explores the darkest aspects of human nature.
