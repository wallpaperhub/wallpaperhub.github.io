"Understanding the Role of a CFO: Key Insights"

The Chief Financial Officer (CFO) is a vital executive steering a company's financial health. Responsible for financial planning, risk management, record-keeping, and reporting, the CFO plays a strategic role in shaping business growth. They ensure compliance with regulations while optimizing financial performance and resources.
