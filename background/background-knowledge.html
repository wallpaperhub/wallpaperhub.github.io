Harnessing the Power of Background Knowledge

Background knowledge refers to the information and experiences that form a foundation for understanding new concepts. It plays a crucial role in learning by connecting prior experiences to new ideas. By integrating background knowledge, individuals can enhance comprehension, improve critical thinking, and foster better retention of information.
