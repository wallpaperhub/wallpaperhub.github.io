Mary Barra: Pioneering Leadership at General Motors

Mary Barra is the Chairperson and CEO of General Motors, making history as the first woman to lead a major global automaker. Under her leadership, GM has focused on innovation, electric vehicles, and sustainability. Barra's commitment to transforming the automotive industry has positioned GM as a key player in the race for a greener future.
