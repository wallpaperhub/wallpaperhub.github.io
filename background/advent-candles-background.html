Light Up the Holidays: The Meaning of Advent Candles

Advent candles hold significant meaning during the Christmas season, traditionally used in Advent wreaths to mark the weeks leading up to Christmas. Each candle symbolizes hope, peace, joy, and love, illuminated weekly. This cherished custom encourages reflection and anticipation, enhancing the festive atmosphere and spiritual preparation for Christ's birth.
