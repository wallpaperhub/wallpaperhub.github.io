The Great Gatsby: Exploring the American Dream

The Great Gatsby, written by F. Scott Fitzgerald, is set in the Roaring Twenties, a time of economic prosperity and cultural change in America. The novel captures the extravagant lifestyle of the wealthy East Egg residents and the contrasting struggles of the working class. Themes of ambition, love, and the American Dream are central to its poignant narrative.
