Author of "Esperanza Rising" - Pam Muñoz Ryan

The author of "Esperanza Rising" is Pam Muñoz Ryan. This poignant novel tells the story of Esperanza Ortega, a young girl who faces the challenges of migration and loss during the Great Depression. Through her journey from privilege to hardship, Esperanza learns about resilience, hope, and the importance of family.
