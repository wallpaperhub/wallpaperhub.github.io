Discover the Fascinating History of Cereal

Cereal has a rich history, originating from ancient grains cultivated by civilizations like the Egyptians and Chinese. Over time, cereals evolved into a staple food worldwide, with diverse forms from oats to cornflakes. Today, they're not just breakfast items but integral to various meals, providing essential nutrients and energy for daily activities.
