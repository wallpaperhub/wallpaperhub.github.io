Bauhaus School: Revolutionizing Art and Design

The Bauhaus School, founded in 1919 in Germany, revolutionized art and design education by merging craftsmanship with modern industrial techniques. It emphasized functional design, simplicity, and the unity of art and technology. This influential movement inspired countless architects, artists, and designers, leaving a lasting legacy on modern aesthetics.
