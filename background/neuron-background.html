Understanding Neuron Structure and Function

Neurons are specialized cells that transmit information throughout the body via electrical and chemical signals. They are fundamental to the nervous system, facilitating communication between the brain and various body parts. Composed of a cell body, dendrites, and an axon, they play critical roles in reflexes, sensations, and cognitive functions.
