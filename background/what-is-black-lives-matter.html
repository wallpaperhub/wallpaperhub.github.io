Understanding Black Lives Matter: A Movement for Justice

Black Lives Matter is a social movement advocating for the rights and dignity of Black individuals, primarily addressing systemic racism, police brutality, and racial inequality. Founded in 2013, it emphasizes the need for justice and equity, fostering awareness and action through protests, campaigns, and community engagement.
