How Long Does a No Contest Plea Affect Your Record?

A no contest plea typically remains on your criminal record indefinitely, as it is treated similarly to a guilty plea. However, the impact may vary by state. Some jurisdictions allow for expungement or sealing after a specific time, often contingent on fulfilling certain conditions like completing probation. Consult local laws for precise details.
