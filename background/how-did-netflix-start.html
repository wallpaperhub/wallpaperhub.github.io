How Netflix Revolutionized Entertainment: A Brief History

Netflix started in 1997 as a DVD rental service founded by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph in Scotts Valley, California. Customers could order DVDs online, which were then mailed directly to their homes. The innovative model shifted entertainment consumption, leading to the company's 2007 launch of streaming services, revolutionizing how people watch films and TV shows.
