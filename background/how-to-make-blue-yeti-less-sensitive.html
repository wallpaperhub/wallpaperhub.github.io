How to Reduce Sensitivity on Your Blue Yeti Microphone

To reduce the sensitivity of your Blue Yeti microphone, follow these steps: Adjust the gain knob to a lower setting for softer sounds. Utilize the cardioid pattern to focus on your voice while minimizing background noise. Use pop filters or foam windshields to lessen plosives, and position the mic further from your mouth during recording.
