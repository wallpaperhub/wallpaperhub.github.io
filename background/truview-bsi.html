Understanding Truview BSI: Measuring Burn Severity

Truview BSI, or Burn Severity Index, is a vital tool used to assess the impact of wildfires on ecosystems. By measuring factors like soil depth, vegetation types, and moisture levels, it provides detailed insights into burn severity. This information is crucial for restoration efforts and helps inform firefighting strategies in the future.
