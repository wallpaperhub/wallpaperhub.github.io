Finding Dory, released by Pixar in 2016, is a beloved animated film that continues the journey of the forgetful fish, Dory. Set in vibrant underwater locales, the film explores themes of friendship, family, and self-discovery. With stunning visuals and heartfelt storytelling, it captures the essence of marine life while promoting important messages about perseverance and belonging.
