Unveiling Pop Smoke: The Voice of Brooklyn's Drill Movement

Pop Smoke, an influential rapper from Brooklyn, emerged as a significant figure in the drill music scene before his untimely death in 2020. Known for his deep voice and unique sound, hits like "Dior" and "Welcome to the Party" showcased his talent and charisma. His posthumous album solidified his legacy, inspiring many artists.
