"Understanding the Iconic Amazon Logo: Meaning & Design"

The Amazon logo is instantly recognizable, featuring a bold, modern font with a curved arrow connecting the letters 'A' to 'Z'. This arrow symbolizes the company's motto of offering everything from A to Z, as well as a friendly smile for customer satisfaction. The logo's simplicity and versatility contribute to Amazon's strong brand identity.
