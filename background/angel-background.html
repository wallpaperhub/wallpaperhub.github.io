Discover the Fascinating Background of Angels

Angels are celestial beings often depicted in various cultures as messengers of God, protectors, and guides. Traditionally portrayed with wings and halos, they embody love, peace, and divine intervention. Their background spans religious texts and art, symbolizing hope and the connection between the divine and humanity.
