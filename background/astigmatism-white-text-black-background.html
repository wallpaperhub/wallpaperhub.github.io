Understanding Astigmatism: Why White Text on Black Background Helps

Astigmatism is a common vision condition caused by an irregularly shaped cornea or lens. This leads to blurred or distorted vision. People with astigmatism may find reading white text on a black background more comfortable due to reduced glare. Understanding this condition can help in seeking proper treatment and improving vision.
