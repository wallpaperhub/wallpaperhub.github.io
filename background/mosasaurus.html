Fascinating Facts About Mosasaurus: The Sea Monster

Mosasaurus was a dominant marine reptile during the Late Cretaceous period, reaching lengths up to 60 feet. With its elongated body, powerful tail, and sharp teeth, it hunted fish and other marine creatures in ancient seas. Often described as a "sea monster," it thrived as a top predator, showcasing incredible adaptations to its aquatic lifestyle.
