Understanding the OIG Exclusion List: A Quick Guide

The OIG Exclusion List is a critical tool maintained by the Office of Inspector General, identifying individuals and entities excluded from participating in federal healthcare programs. Being on this list can impact the ability to receive payments from Medicare and Medicaid. Regularly checking this list is essential for compliance and safeguarding public health.
