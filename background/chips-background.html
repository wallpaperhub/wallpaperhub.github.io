Discover the Crunchy World of Chips: History & Varieties

Chips, renowned as a popular snack worldwide, trace their origins back to the 19th century when they were created in Saratoga Springs, New York. Initially made from thinly sliced potatoes and deep-fried to a crisp, they have evolved into a variety of flavors and types, including tortilla and corn chips, catering to diverse palates.
