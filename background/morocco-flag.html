"Exploring the Symbolism of the Moroccan Flag"

The flag of Morocco features a red field with a green pentagram, symbolizing Islam and the country's connection to its historical roots. The red represents bravery and strength, while the green star signifies hope and peace. This iconic flag reflects Morocco's rich cultural heritage and vibrant history, making it a symbol of national pride.
