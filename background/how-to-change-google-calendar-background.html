Change Your Google Calendar Background in Easy Steps

To change the Google Calendar background, open your calendar and click on the “Settings” gear icon. Navigate to “Settings” and find the “Event settings” section. Here, you can customize colors for events and choose from the available background options. Save changes to see your new look instantly!
