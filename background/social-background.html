Understanding Social Background: Key Insights

Social background refers to the cultural, economic, and social conditions that shape an individual's life experiences and opportunities. It encompasses aspects like family, education, community, and socio-economic status. Understanding one’s social background is crucial for addressing inequalities and fostering inclusive environments in society.
