History of MAC Cosmetics: A Beauty Revolution

Founded in 1984 by Frank Toskan and Grazia Castronuovo in Toronto, MAC Cosmetics revolutionized the beauty industry with its professional makeup products. Originally aimed at professional artists, it quickly gained popularity for its vast color range and quality. In 1994, MAC launched its first Viva Glam campaign, promoting awareness for AIDS.
