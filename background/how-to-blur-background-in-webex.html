How to Easily Blur Background in Webex: Step-by-Step Guide

To blur your background in Webex, open the app and start or join a meeting. Click on the "Video" icon, then select "Change Virtual Background." Here, you'll find an option to blur your background. Choose it, and your background will become softly blurred, allowing for a more focused and professional appearance during your calls.
