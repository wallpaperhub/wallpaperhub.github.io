Understanding the Goth Subculture: A Deep Dive

Goth culture emerged in the late 1970s from the post-punk music scene in England, characterized by dark, melancholic aesthetics and themes. Its roots can be traced to literary and cinematic influences, including gothic literature and horror films. The subculture embraces individuality, self-expression, and often a fascination with the macabre.
