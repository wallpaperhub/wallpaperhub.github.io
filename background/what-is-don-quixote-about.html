Understanding Don Quixote: A Tale of Chivalry and Illusion

Don Quixote, written by Miguel de Cervantes, is a novel that follows the adventures of an aging nobleman who becomes convinced he is a knight. He sets off on quests to revive chivalry, accompanied by his loyal squire, Sancho Panza. Themes of reality versus illusion, heroism, and the struggle against societal norms are central to this timeless tale.
