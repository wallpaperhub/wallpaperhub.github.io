Understanding Background Check Timeframes

Background checks typically go back 7 to 10 years, but this can vary based on the type of check and local laws. For instance, criminal records may be searched more extensively, while employment history checks usually focus on a shorter timeframe. Always verify the specific laws that apply in your area for accurate information.
