Foreground vs Background: Understanding Visual Dynamics

In design and art, the term "vs background" refers to the contrast between the main subject and its surroundings. This interplay impacts visibility and focus, creating depth and context. Understanding how to balance foreground elements with their background can enhance visual storytelling and aesthetic appeal, making it essential for artists and designers alike.
