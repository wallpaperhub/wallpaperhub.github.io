Spider-Man: A Brief History of the Iconic Hero

Spider-Man, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. Peter Parker, a teenager bitten by a radioactive spider, gains superhuman abilities. The character has evolved through comic books, animated series, and blockbuster films, symbolizing resilience and responsibility.
