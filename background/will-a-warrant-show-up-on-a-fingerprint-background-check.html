Do Warrants Show Up on Fingerprint Background Checks?

When undergoing a fingerprint background check, active warrants typically do appear. This is because fingerprint checks pull up an individual’s criminal history, which includes pending warrants. However, the specifics may vary by jurisdiction and the type of background check being conducted, so it’s wise to confirm details with local authorities.
