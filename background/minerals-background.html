Essential Minerals: Nature's Building Blocks

Minerals are naturally occurring inorganic substances vital for life and the environment. They form through geological processes and can be found in various types, including metals, gemstones, and industrial minerals. Understanding their properties and distribution is essential for industries like construction, electronics, and agriculture, influencing global economies.
