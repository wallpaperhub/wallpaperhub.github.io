Discover the Origins of Coffee Beans

Coffee beans are the seeds of the Coffea plant, originating from tropical climates, primarily in regions along the Equator. They undergo a complex journey from cultivation to cup, including harvesting, processing, and roasting. Different varieties, like Arabica and Robusta, impart unique flavors and aromas, making each coffee experience distinct.
