Understanding Colossians: The Supremacy of Christ

Colossians is a New Testament letter by the Apostle Paul addressing the Colossian church. It emphasizes the supremacy of Christ, warns against false teachings, and encourages believers to live out their faith through love and good works. The letter highlights the transformative power of the Gospel and Christian unity.
