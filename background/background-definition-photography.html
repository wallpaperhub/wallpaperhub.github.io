Mastering Background in Photography: Key Techniques

Background definition photography focuses on how the background of a shot can influence the overall composition and mood of an image. It involves understanding depth of field, colors, and patterns in the background to enhance the subject. By managing elements behind the subject, photographers can tell richer stories and evoke emotions.
