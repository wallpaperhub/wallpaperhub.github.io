Understanding the Big 5 Personality Traits: OCEAN Explained

The Big Five Personality Traits, often referred to as OCEAN, encompass openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness, and neuroticism. These traits help in understanding human behavior and personality. They influence how we interact, work, and navigate relationships, shaping our unique patterns of thinking and feeling.
