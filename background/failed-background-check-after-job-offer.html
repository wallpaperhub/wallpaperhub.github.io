What to Do After a Failed Background Check

Receiving a job offer is exciting, but a failed background check can jeopardize your future with the company. Employers may withdraw the offer if discrepancies arise, often related to criminal records or employment history. It's essential to know your rights and check your background report to ensure accuracy before applying.
