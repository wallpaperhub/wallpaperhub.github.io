Exploring the Legacy of Internet Explorer

Internet Explorer, developed by Microsoft, was launched in 1995 as part of the Windows 95 operating system. It quickly gained popularity as a web browser due to its integration with the Windows platform. Over the years, it evolved through multiple versions, but faced challenges from competitors like Chrome and Firefox, leading to its eventual decline and replacement by Microsoft Edge in 2015.
