Understanding Jeremiah: The Weeping Prophet of the Bible

Jeremiah was a major prophet in the Bible, known for delivering God's messages during a turbulent period in Judah's history. He foretold the destruction of Jerusalem and called for repentance. Often referred to as the "weeping prophet," Jeremiah's profound lamentations reveal his deep compassion for his people and his anguish over their sins.
