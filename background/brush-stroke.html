Mastering Brush Strokes: Techniques for Artists

Brush strokes are the fundamental marks made by a brush on a surface, playing a crucial role in painting techniques. They can vary in size, shape, and style, influencing the texture and emotional impact of the artwork. Mastering different brush strokes allows artists to express their creativity and enhance their visual storytelling.
