Shipwright Background for D&D 5E: Crafting Adventures on the High Seas

In Dungeons & Dragons 5E, a shipwright is a skilled artisan specializing in the construction and repair of ships. These craftsmen possess knowledge of woodcraft, engineering, and sailing, making them invaluable in maritime settings. A shipwright can create everything from small vessels to grand galleons, contributing to naval adventures and trade routes.
