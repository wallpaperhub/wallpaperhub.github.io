Madness Combat: Chaos & Combat in Animation

Madness Combat is an iconic animated series created by Matt Jolly (aka Krinkels), originating from the Newgrounds platform in 2002. Blending dark humor and intense action, it follows a faceless protagonist known as Hank as he battles against various foes. The series is renowned for its unique art style, frenetic combat scenes, and immersive storytelling, building a dedicated fanbase over the years.
