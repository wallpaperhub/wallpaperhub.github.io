Change Your Windows 11 Lock Screen in Minutes

To change your lock screen on Windows 11, right-click on the desktop and select "Personalize." Click on "Lock screen" in the left menu. Here, you can select a background from Windows Spotlight, Picture, or Slideshow. Customize additional settings like the preview, and quick status features. Save your choice!
