Uncover the Mysteries of Light Strange Brigade

Light Strange Brigade is a thrilling cooperative action-adventure game set in the 1930s, where players join a band of misfit heroes. Tasked with uncovering the secrets of a supernatural foe in ancient tombs, they battle mummies, solve puzzles, and explore vast landscapes filled with danger and mystery. The blend of humor, history, and horror makes it a unique experience.
