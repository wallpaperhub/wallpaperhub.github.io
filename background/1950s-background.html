The 1950s: A Decade of Change and Culture

The 1950s was a transformative decade marked by post-war prosperity, the rise of the suburban lifestyle, and the birth of rock 'n' roll. Television became a household staple, influencing culture and identity, while the civil rights movement began to gain momentum, challenging social norms. This era encapsulated both conformity and rebellion.
