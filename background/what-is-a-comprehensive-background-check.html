What is a Comprehensive Background Check?

A comprehensive background check is a detailed investigation into an individual's history, including criminal records, employment verification, credit history, and personal references. It ensures employers or organizations can make informed decisions about hiring or granting access, contributing to a safer environment and reducing potential risks.
