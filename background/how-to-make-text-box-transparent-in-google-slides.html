Make Text Box Transparent in Google Slides

To make a text box transparent in Google Slides, select the text box you want to edit. Right-click and choose "Format options." In the sidebar, look for "Fill color," then select "Transparent." This will remove the background color, allowing images or other elements beneath to show through, creating a sleek look for your presentation.
