Understanding Bone Structure: The Essentials

Bone background refers to the study and understanding of the anatomy and physiology of bones, which form the human skeleton. This knowledge is crucial for medical and health fields, impacting areas like orthopedics, dentistry, and physical therapy. Bones support the body, protect organs, and enable movement through muscle attachment.
