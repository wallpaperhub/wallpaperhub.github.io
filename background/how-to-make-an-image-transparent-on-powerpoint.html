How to Easily Make Images Transparent in PowerPoint

To make an image transparent in PowerPoint, select the image, then go to the "Format" tab. Click on "Transparency" and adjust the slider to achieve your desired level of transparency. Alternatively, right-click the image, select “Format Picture,” and adjust the transparency in the Fill section. Preview and finalize as needed!
