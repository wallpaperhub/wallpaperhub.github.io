Illuminating Ideas: The Power of Spotlight Backgrounds

Spotlight background refers to the dramatic lighting technique often used in photography and videography to create focus on a subject. This approach uses a concentrated light beam that softens shadows and enhances features, adding depth to the scene. Common in theater and events, it captivates audiences by emphasizing key elements in a visually striking way.
