Aaron Hernandez: From NFL Stardom to Legal Infamy

Aaron Hernandez was an American football tight end who played for the New England Patriots. He gained notoriety both for his athletic prowess and legal troubles, including a murder conviction. His life was marked by a tragic spiral from NFL stardom to imprisonment, raising discussions about mental health and the pressures of fame.
