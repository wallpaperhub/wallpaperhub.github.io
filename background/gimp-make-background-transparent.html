Make Background Transparent in GIMP: A Quick Guide

To make a background transparent in GIMP, start by opening your image and selecting the layer. Use the 'Select' tool to highlight the area you want to keep. Next, go to 'Layer' > 'Transparency' > 'Add Alpha Channel' if not already added. Then, press 'Delete' to remove the selected area, making it transparent. Save your work in PNG format to preserve the transparency.
