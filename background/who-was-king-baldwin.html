Who Was King Baldwin IV of Jerusalem?

King Baldwin IV of Jerusalem, also known as Baldwin the Leper, ruled from 1174 to 1185. He was the son of Amalric I and became king at a young age. Despite battling leprosy, he was known for his military prowess and efforts to defend his kingdom against Saladin's forces. His reign is marked by valor and tragedy.
