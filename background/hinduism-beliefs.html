Explore Key Beliefs of Hinduism

Hinduism is a major world religion with core beliefs centered on Dharma (duty), Karma (action and consequence), and Moksha (liberation). It is a pluralistic faith that honors a variety of deities and recognizes the validity of diverse paths to truth. Rituals, meditation, and ethical living are integral aspects of this ancient tradition.
