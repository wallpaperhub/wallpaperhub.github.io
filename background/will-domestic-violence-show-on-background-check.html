Does Domestic Violence Appear on Background Checks?

Domestic violence can show up on background checks, particularly if there has been a criminal conviction or a protective order issued. Employers often conduct these checks to ensure workplace safety. However, the extent to which it appears depends on the type of background check and state laws governing privacy and expungement.
