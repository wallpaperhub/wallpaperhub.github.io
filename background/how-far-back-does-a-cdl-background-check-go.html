How Far Back Does a CDL Background Check Go?

A CDL background check typically goes back three to seven years, depending on the type of check being conducted. Employers often look into driving records, criminal histories, and previous employment. It's crucial for commercial drivers to maintain a clean record as it can significantly impact job opportunities.
