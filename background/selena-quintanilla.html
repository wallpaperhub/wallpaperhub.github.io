Celebrating Selena Quintanilla: The Queen of Tejano Music

Selena Quintanilla, the "Queen of Tejano," was a trailblazing singer, songwriter, and fashion icon who profoundly influenced Latin music. Born on April 16, 1971, her vibrant spirit and passionate performances captured hearts worldwide. Tragically, her life was cut short in 1995, but her legacy endures through timeless hits like "Como La Flor" and "Bidi Bidi Bom Bom."
