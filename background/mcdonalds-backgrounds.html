Discover the Rich History of McDonald's: From Stand to Global Icon

McDonald's, founded in 1940 by Richard and Maurice McDonald, began as a simple hamburger stand in San Bernardino, California. It revolutionized fast food with its assembly line cooking and value pricing. Over the decades, McDonald's has become a global icon, known for its Golden Arches and a diverse menu catering to various tastes.
