Can Employers Conduct Background Checks After Hiring?

Yes, an employer can run a background check after hiring, but they must comply with the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). If the employer finds issues, they may take action, which could include disciplinary measures or termination. Transparency and consent are crucial, so employers should inform employees of potential checks.
