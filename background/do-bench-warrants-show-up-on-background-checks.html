Do Bench Warrants Appear on Background Checks?

Bench warrants typically do show up on background checks, as they signify an active court order for arrest. Courts and law enforcement agencies maintain records of such warrants, which can be accessed during background screenings. It's crucial to address any outstanding warrants, as they could impact employment opportunities and other important areas of life.
