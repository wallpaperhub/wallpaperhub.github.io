"Choosing the Perfect Portrait Background"

A portrait background is essential for enhancing the subject's visual appeal in photography. Options include natural settings, studio backdrops, or textured surfaces. The right background complements the subject’s features, adds depth, and sets the mood, ensuring that the focus remains on the subject while providing context.
