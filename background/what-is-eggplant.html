Eggplant: A Nutritional Powerhouse & Culinary Delight

Eggplant, also known as aubergine, is a versatile vegetable belonging to the nightshade family. Recognized for its glossy purple skin and spongy texture, eggplant is a staple in various cuisines. It is rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making it a nutritious addition to dishes like ratatouille and baba ghanoush.
