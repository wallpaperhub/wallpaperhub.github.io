Understanding SWAT: Origins and Operations

SWAT, or Special Weapons and Tactics, originated in the 1960s as a police response to escalating violence and complex criminal situations. Comprised of specially trained officers, SWAT teams handle high-risk operations, including hostage rescues and armed confrontations. Their background includes rigorous training in tactics, weapons, and crisis negotiation.
