Causes of Red Flags in Gun Background Checks

A red flag on a background check for a gun can arise from various factors including criminal history, mental health issues, restraining orders, or substance abuse. Serious felonies, domestic violence convictions, or a history of violent behavior often lead to disqualification. Ensuring clear records is essential for legal firearm possession.
