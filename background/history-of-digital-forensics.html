History of Digital Forensics: Evolution & Impact

Digital forensics has evolved from simple data recovery efforts in the 1970s to a sophisticated field supporting crime investigation and cyber security. Initially focused on retrieving data from computers, it now encompasses a variety of devices and technologies, utilizing advanced methods to analyze digital evidence crucial for legal proceedings.
