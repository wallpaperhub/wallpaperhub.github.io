The Origins of Charcuterie: A Savory Tradition

Charcuterie traces its origins to ancient practices of preserving meat, with the term deriving from the French words for "cooked" (char) and "meat" (cuterie). It gained popularity in France, where artisans crafted a variety of cured meats and accompaniments, becoming a beloved culinary tradition across the globe.
