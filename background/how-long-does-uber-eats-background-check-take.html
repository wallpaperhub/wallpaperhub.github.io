How Long Does an Uber Eats Background Check Take?

Uber Eats background checks typically take between 3 to 10 days to complete. The process duration may vary based on various factors, including the applicant's location and the volume of applications being processed. It's crucial for prospective delivery drivers to be patient during this time, as thorough checks ensure safety for customers and drivers alike.
