California Fair Chance Act: Ensuring Second Chances in Employment

The California Fair Chance Act, enacted in 2018, aims to eliminate barriers for individuals with criminal records seeking employment. This law prohibits employers from asking about criminal history until after a job offer is made, ensuring fair consideration. By promoting second chances, it supports workforce diversity and reduces recidivism.
