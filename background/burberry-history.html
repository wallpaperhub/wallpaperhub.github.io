Discover the Iconic History of Burberry: From Trench Coats to Luxury Fashion

Burberry, founded in 1856 by Thomas Burberry in England, is renowned for its luxury fashion and innovative trench coats. Initially a clothing company serving the outdoors, it evolved into a global brand famous for its signature tartan pattern. Over the years, it has embraced modern fashion while honoring its rich heritage.
