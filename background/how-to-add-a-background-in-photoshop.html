How to Easily Add Backgrounds in Photoshop

To add a background in Photoshop, start by opening your image. Go to the Layers panel and create a new layer below your main image layer. Select the background layer, then use the Paint Bucket Tool or Gradient Tool to fill it with your desired color or image. Adjust opacity and blend modes for a seamless look!
