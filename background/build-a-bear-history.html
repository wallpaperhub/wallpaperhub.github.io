Discover the Fun History of Build-A-Bear Workshop

Build-A-Bear Workshop was founded in 1997 by Maxine Clark in St. Louis, Missouri. The concept allowed children to create personalized stuffed animals, choosing everything from stuffing to outfits. Over the years, it has expanded globally, adding popular licensed characters and interactive experiences, making it a beloved retail destination for kids and families.
