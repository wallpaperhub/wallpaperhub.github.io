Understanding Middle Ground in Art: Definition & Importance

Middle ground in art refers to the space between the foreground and background within a composition, often serving to create depth and perspective. It plays a crucial role in guiding the viewer's eye and emphasizing the relationship between various elements. Through careful manipulation, artists can achieve balance and enhance storytelling.
