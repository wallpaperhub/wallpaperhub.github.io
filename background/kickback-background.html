Understanding Kickbacks: A Deep Dive into Their Origins and Impact

Kickbacks have a dubious history, often tied to corruption and unethical practices in the business and political realms. Originating in the early 20th century, they involve secret payments to influence decisions favorably. While they can lead to short-term gains, kickbacks undermine integrity and trust, fostering an environment of bribery and moral decay.
