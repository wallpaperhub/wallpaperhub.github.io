Understanding Doctor Background: Education & Training

Doctors typically undergo extensive education and training, starting with a bachelor's degree followed by medical school, where they earn their MD or DO. Postdoctoral training, such as residencies and fellowships, hones their specialized skills. Additionally, ongoing education is crucial for staying updated with medical advancements.
