Discover the Fascinating History of Groundhog Day

Groundhog Day, celebrated on February 2, has its roots in ancient European traditions. It arrived in the U.S. via German settlers in Pennsylvania, who believed a groundhog's prediction of spring weather could determine the season's arrival. Today, it features Punxsutawney Phil and a fun annual celebration.
