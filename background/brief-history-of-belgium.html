A Brief History of Belgium: From Ancient Times to the Modern Era

Belgium's history dates back to the Roman Empire when it was inhabited by various tribes. After centuries of foreign rule, it gained independence from the Netherlands in 1830. The country became a key player during both World Wars and experienced significant industrial growth. Today, Belgium is known for its diversity, politics, and heritage.
