Play TikTok in the Background on iPhone: A Quick Guide

To play TikTok in the background on your iPhone, open the app and start playing a video. Swipe down from the top-right corner to access the Control Center. Then, tap the play button on the music widget to continue playing the audio even when you switch to another app. Enjoy your favorite TikTok sounds while multitasking!
