All About White Guinea Pigs: Traits & Care Tips

White Guinea Pigs are charming pets known for their friendly personalities and stunning appearance. These small rodents come in various shades of white, often with unique markings. They require a spacious cage, a balanced diet, and regular social interaction. Their gentle nature makes them ideal companions for kids and adults alike.
