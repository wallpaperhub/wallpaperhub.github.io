iCarly: The Ultimate Web Show Adventure

iCarly is a beloved teen sitcom that originally aired on Nickelodeon from 2007 to 2012. It follows Carly Shay, who creates her own web show with friends Sam and Freddie. The series blends humor, friendship, and the challenges of adolescence, captivating audiences with relatable storylines and iconic characters. Its revival in 2021 introduced a new generation to Carly’s adventures!
