When Was the American Heart Association Founded?

The American Heart Association (AHA) was founded in 1924 by a group of cardiologists in New York City. Its primary goal was to combat heart disease and promote cardiovascular health. Since then, the AHA has become a leading organization in medical research, advocacy, and education, focusing on improving heart health nationwide.
