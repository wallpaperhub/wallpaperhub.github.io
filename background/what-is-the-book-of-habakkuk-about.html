Understanding the Book of Habakkuk: A Prophetic Overview

The Book of Habakkuk is a prophetic text in the Old Testament, where the prophet Habakkuk dialogues with God about justice and suffering. He questions God's plan in light of Israel’s oppression and the rise of Babylon. Ultimately, it conveys themes of faith, trust in divine justice, and the assurance that God’s plans will prevail.
