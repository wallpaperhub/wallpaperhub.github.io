Delicious Cinnamon Rolls: A Sweet History

Cinnamon rolls, a beloved pastry, trace their origins to Scandinavian bakers in the late 19th century. The sweet, spiraled treat is made with a rich dough, aromatic cinnamon, and often topped with sticky icing. Enjoyed globally, they symbolize warmth and comfort, often served fresh from the oven during gatherings and celebrations.
