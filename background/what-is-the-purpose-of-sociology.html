Understanding Sociology: Its Purpose and Impact

The purpose of sociology is to study human society, social behavior, and social change. It examines how individuals interact within groups, the influence of culture, and the structures of institutions. By understanding these dynamics, sociology aims to address social issues, promote equality, and enhance community engagement.
