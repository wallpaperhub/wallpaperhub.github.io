Black and White Circle: Symbolism and Design Insights

A black and white circle symbolizes duality and balance, representing harmony between contrasting elements. This simple yet powerful design can evoke various meanings, from unity and infinity to the coexistence of opposites. Its minimalist aesthetic makes it a versatile motif in art, design, and branding.
