Discover the Fascinating History of Legoland

Legoland was first established in 1968 in Billund, Denmark, as a theme park showcasing Lego bricks' imaginative potential. The success of the park inspired a series of international expansions, making it a global brand. Today, Legoland parks blend entertainment with education, featuring rides, attractions, and miniatures built from millions of Lego bricks.
