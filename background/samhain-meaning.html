Samhain: Understanding Its Meaning and Significance

Samhain, celebrated on October 31st, is an ancient Celtic festival marking the end of the harvest season and the beginning of winter. It symbolizes the transition between the light and dark half of the year, where it’s believed that the veil between worlds grows thin, allowing spirits to cross over. The celebration involves feasting, bonfires, and honoring ancestors.
