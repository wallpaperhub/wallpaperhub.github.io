Understanding Your Native Background

Native background refers to the cultural, ethnic, and historical roots of a person or community. It encompasses traditional practices, languages, and beliefs passed down through generations. Understanding one's native background fosters a sense of identity and pride, and strengthens community ties, enabling individuals to connect with their heritage and preserve it for future generations.
