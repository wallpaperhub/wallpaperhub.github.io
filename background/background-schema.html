Understanding Background Schema: A Comprehensive Guide

Background schema refers to the underlying structure or framework that defines how data is organized, stored, and interpreted in various systems, including databases and programming environments. It provides a systematic way to represent relationships between data, enhancing efficiency and ensuring data integrity.
