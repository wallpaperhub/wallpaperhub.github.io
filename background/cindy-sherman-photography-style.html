Cindy Sherman: A Deep Dive into Her Iconic Photography Style

Cindy Sherman’s photography style is characterized by its conceptual depth and exploration of identity. Through self-portraiture, she adopts various personas, challenging societal norms and the role of women in media. Her work often blurs the lines between art and performance, inviting viewers to question reality and representation.
