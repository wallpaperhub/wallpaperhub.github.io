Understanding Backgrounds in Dungeons & Dragons

In Dungeons & Dragons (D&D), a background represents a character's history before adventuring. It provides unique traits, skills, and role-playing opportunities. Backgrounds enrich the story, helping players craft their characters' motivations and identities. Options include the acolyte, sage, or soldier, each offering distinct advantages.
