Understanding FedEx Background Check Disqualifiers

When applying for a position at FedEx, be aware of background check disqualifiers that may affect job eligibility. Key disqualifiers often include felony convictions, history of theft or fraud, and certain driving offenses. Drug-related charges and inconsistent employment history can also be problematic, as FedEx prioritizes safety and integrity.
