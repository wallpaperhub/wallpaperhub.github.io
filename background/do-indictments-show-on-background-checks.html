Do Indictments Show Up on Background Checks?

Indictments can show up on background checks, depending on the type of check conducted. For employers or individuals reviewing criminal history, an indictment may be visible if it has not been expunged. However, not all background checks capture every indictment, as it often depends on the specific services used and the jurisdictions involved.
