A Christmas Carol: Embrace the Spirit of Redemption

A Christmas Carol, written by Charles Dickens in 1843, is a novella that explores themes of redemption, compassion, and the spirit of Christmas. Set in Victorian London, it follows the transformation of Ebenezer Scrooge, a miserly man visited by the ghosts of Christmas Past, Present, and Yet to Come, which inspires him to embrace generosity.
