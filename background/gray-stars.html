Exploring the Mystery of Gray Stars

Gray stars, while not a common classification, refer to stars that appear gray due to their specific temperature and composition. They often emit a faint glow, giving them a subtle, muted appearance in the night sky. These celestial objects intrigue astronomers, prompting studies on stellar evolution and the life cycles of stars.
