Discover the Rich Background of Fire Emblem: Three Houses

Fire Emblem: Three Houses is set in the fantasy world of Fódlan, where three noble houses train at the Garreg Mach Monastery. Players take on the role of a professor, guiding students from the Black Eagles, Blue Lions, or Golden Deer. Choices made throughout the game shape personal relationships, alliances, and the fate of Fódlan itself.
