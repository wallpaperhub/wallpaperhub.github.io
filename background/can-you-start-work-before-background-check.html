Can You Start Working Before Your Background Check Clears?

Yes, you can start work before a background check is completed in some cases, but it depends on company policy and local laws. Many employers allow new hires to begin training or orientation processes while awaiting the results. However, there might be restrictions on certain responsibilities until the check clears.
