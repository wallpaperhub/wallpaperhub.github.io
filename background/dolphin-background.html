Fascinating Dolphin Facts: Understanding Their Background

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior and complex social structures. They belong to the cetacean family, which includes whales and porpoises. Commonly found in oceans and seas worldwide, these creatures communicate using a variety of sounds and display remarkable problem-solving skills.
