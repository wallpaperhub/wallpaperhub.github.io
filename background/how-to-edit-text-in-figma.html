Easy Steps to Edit Text in Figma

Editing text in Figma is straightforward. First, select the text layer you wish to edit. Double-click on the text or use the edit option in the right panel to enter the text editing mode. You can change font, size, color, and alignment from the properties panel. To apply changes, simply click outside the text area.
