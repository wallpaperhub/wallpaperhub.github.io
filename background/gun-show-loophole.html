Understanding the Gun Show Loophole

The "gun show loophole" refers to a legal gap that allows private sellers at gun shows to sell firearms without conducting background checks on buyers. This means individuals can purchase guns without the same scrutiny required of licensed dealers, raising concerns about public safety and the potential for firearms to fall into the wrong hands.
