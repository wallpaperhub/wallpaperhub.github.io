Understanding Background App Refresh: Benefits & Tips

Background app refresh allows apps on your device to update content and data while running in the background. This feature keeps your information fresh, enabling you to receive updates without having to open the app. However, it can consume battery and data. Adjust settings for optimal performance based on your preferences.
