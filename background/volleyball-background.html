Volleyball: A Brief History and Evolution

Volleyball originated in 1895 in Holyoke, Massachusetts, when William G. Morgan designed it as a recreational sport. Combining elements of basketball, tennis, and handball, it involves two teams separated by a net. Over the years, it evolved into an Olympic sport, gaining global popularity and diverse variations like beach volleyball.
