How to Change Your Instagram Story Background

To change the background on your Instagram story, open the app and swipe right to access the camera. Take a photo or upload one from your gallery. Tap the 'Aa' icon to add text, and then select the 'draw' option to pick a background color. Use the paintbrush tool to fill in the screen or create a gradient. Save and share!
