Understanding ISIS: Origins, Impact, and Global Response

ISIS, or the Islamic State of Iraq and Syria, is a militant group known for its extreme interpretation of Islam and brutal tactics. Emerging around 2013, ISIS gained global notoriety for its violent extremism, territorial control, and terrorist acts. The group's impact has resulted in widespread humanitarian crises and ongoing conflicts.
