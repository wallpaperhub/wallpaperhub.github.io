George Washington Carver: Pioneer of Agricultural Education

George Washington Carver, an innovative agricultural scientist, faced many hurdles in his early education due to racial discrimination. Despite these challenges, he pursued knowledge, eventually earning a master's degree in agricultural science from Iowa State Agricultural College. His dedication to education led to groundbreaking advancements in crop rotation and sustainable farming practices, benefitting countless farmers.
