Discover the Fat Albert Plane: U.S. Navy's Iconic Support Aircraft

The Fat Albert plane, a Lockheed C-130 Hercules, serves as the U.S. Navy's Blue Angels' support aircraft, delivering vital supplies and personnel to ensure smooth aerial performances. This iconic plane is named after a character from the famous cartoon series, symbolizing fun and teamwork in aviation.
