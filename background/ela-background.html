Understanding ELA Background: Essential Skills for Success

ELA (English Language Arts) background encompasses the skills and knowledge needed for effective communication through reading, writing, listening, and speaking. It emphasizes literacy development, critical thinking, and comprehension of diverse texts, preparing students for academic success and lifelong learning in today's diverse world.
