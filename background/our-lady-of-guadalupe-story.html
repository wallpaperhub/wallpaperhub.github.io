Discover the Inspiring Story of Our Lady of Guadalupe

Our Lady of Guadalupe is a beloved figure in Mexican culture, representing compassion and hope. The story began in 1531 when Juan Diego encountered a radiant lady on Tepeyac Hill. She revealed her identity as the Virgin Mary and requested a church to be built in her honor. Her image later appeared on Juan's tilma, solidifying her status as a symbol of faith and protection.
