Is Queen Still a Band? The Legacy Continues

Yes, Queen is still a band, although its lineup has changed since the passing of iconic frontman Freddie Mercury in 1991. The remaining members, Brian May and Roger Taylor, continue to perform and tour, often with Adam Lambert as the lead vocalist, under the name Queen + Adam Lambert, captivating fans worldwide with their enduring legacy.
