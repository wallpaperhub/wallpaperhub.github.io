Unfilled Roles in Your Business Plan: Identify Essential Positions

In a business plan, the section addressing roles that have not yet been filled typically resides in the "Management Team" or "Organizational Structure" section. This part outlines required positions, responsibilities, and the skills they need, highlighting gaps in the team essential for achieving business goals.
