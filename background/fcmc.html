Understanding FCMC: Key to US Monetary Policy

FCMC, or the Federal Open Market Committee, plays a crucial role in shaping US monetary policy. It meets regularly to set interest rates and guide actions that influence the economy's performance. By analyzing economic conditions, the FCMC aims to promote maximum employment and stable prices, guiding the nation through financial challenges.
