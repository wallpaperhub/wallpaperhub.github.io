How to Effectively Reduce Mic Background Noise

To reduce background noise on your mic, start by using a cardioid microphone, which picks up sound primarily from the front. Use pop filters and windshields for better clarity. Optimize your environment by closing windows and doors, or using soundproofing materials. Finally, software noise canceling can further enhance audio quality.
