Understanding Oxygen: The Essential Element for Life

Oxygen, a colorless and odorless gas, is essential for life on Earth. Found abundantly in the atmosphere, it comprises about 21% of the air we breathe. Produced mainly through photosynthesis in plants, oxygen supports cellular respiration in living organisms, playing a pivotal role in energy production and sustaining life.
