PUBG Background: Explore the Game's Environment and Features

PUBG, or PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, is a popular battle royale game that has reshaped the gaming landscape since its 2017 release. Players parachute onto an island and must scavenge for weapons and resources to survive against 99 others. Its stunning landscapes, intense gameplay, and strategic elements create a captivating environment for gamers.
