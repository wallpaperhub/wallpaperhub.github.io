Do You Need Coding Skills for Business Analytics?

Business analytics does not strictly require coding, but familiarity with programming languages can enhance analytical skills. Many tools used in analytics, like Excel or Tableau, allow for effective data analysis without coding. However, knowledge of SQL or Python can be advantageous for deeper insights and automation.
