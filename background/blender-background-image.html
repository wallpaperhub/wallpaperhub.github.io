Enhance Your Blender Projects with Background Images

Using a background image in Blender enhances your scenes and aids in composition. To set a background image, go to the properties panel, find the 'Background Images' section, and enable it. You can load an image from your device and adjust its position and transparency for optimal integration in your 3D environment.
