Vocal Harmony: Elevate Your Music Experience

Vocal harmony is the blending of vocal sounds to create a rich texture in music. It involves singers performing complementary melodies and chords, enhancing the emotional impact of a song. Ranging from simple two-part harmonies to complex arrangements, vocal harmony is essential across genres, elevating performances and creating a unique auditory experience.
