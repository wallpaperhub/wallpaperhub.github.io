Hello Kitty: The Iconic Cutie of Pop Culture

Hello Kitty, created by Sanrio in 1974, has become an iconic symbol of cuteness and positivity. Her charming design features a simple white cat with no mouth, dressed often in a bow. Originally aimed at children, Hello Kitty has garnered a massive fanbase across all ages, featuring in various merchandise, animated series, and even themed cafes.
