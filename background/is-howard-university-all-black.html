Is Howard University All Black? Facts & Insights

Howard University, founded in 1867, is not an all-Black institution but is historically significant within the Black community. It has a diverse student body, although it primarily serves African American students. Howard's mission focuses on academic excellence, cultural education, and the empowerment of the African American community.
