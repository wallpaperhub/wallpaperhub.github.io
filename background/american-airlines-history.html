Discover the Rich History of American Airlines

American Airlines, founded in 1930, has evolved into a major player in the aviation industry. The airline emerged from the merger of several small carriers and expanded its operations significantly during and after WWII. Known for its iconic logo and customer service, American Airlines now operates over 6,700 flights daily, serving more than 350 destinations worldwide.
