Understanding Restoration Background: Reviving Heritage

Restoration background refers to the visual and conceptual context that informs the revitalization of historical structures and environments. This process involves understanding the original design, materials, and cultural significance of a site, ensuring that any restoration work honors its heritage while integrating modern needs and sustainability.
