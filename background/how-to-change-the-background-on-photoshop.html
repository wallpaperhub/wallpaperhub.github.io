Change Background in Photoshop: A Quick Guide

To change the background in Photoshop, first open your image. Use the 'Quick Selection Tool' or 'Pen Tool' to select the subject. Next, inverse your selection to select the background. Press 'Delete' to remove it. Finally, add a new layer with your desired background, adjusting the placement as needed. Save your work!
