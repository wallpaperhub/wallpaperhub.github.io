How Long Does an ACD Stay on Your Record?

An Accumulated Conviction Data (ACD) typically remains on your record for a specified duration, often ranging from three to five years, depending on the jurisdiction and the severity of the offense. After this time, you may have options to expunge or seal your record, allowing for a fresh start in your personal and professional life.
