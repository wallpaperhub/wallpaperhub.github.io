Discovering the Art of Cave Pictures

Cave pictures, often found in ancient archaeological sites, reveal the creativity and beliefs of early humans. These artworks, usually depicting animals, human figures, and abstract symbols, provide valuable insights into prehistoric life. Studying them helps us understand our ancestors' environment, culture, and social structures.
