Self-Background Check for Gun Purchases: What You Need to Know

Yes, you can do a background check on yourself before purchasing a gun. This self-check helps identify any potential issues that may arise during the official background check process. You can obtain your criminal history report from local law enforcement or use online services, ensuring you comply with federal and state laws.
