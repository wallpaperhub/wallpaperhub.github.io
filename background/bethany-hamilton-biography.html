Inspiring Journey of Bethany Hamilton: Surfer & Shark Attack Survivor

Bethany Hamilton, born on February 8, 1990, in Hanalei, Hawaii, is a professional surfer known for her incredible resilience. At age 13, she lost her left arm in a shark attack but returned to surfing just a month later. Her inspiring journey of overcoming obstacles has made her a motivational speaker and author, sharing her story worldwide.
