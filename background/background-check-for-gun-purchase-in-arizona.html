Understanding Background Checks for Gun Purchases in Arizona

In Arizona, background checks for gun purchases are mandatory for sales conducted by licensed dealers. This process includes checking criminal history, mental health status, and any disqualifying factors. Private sales do not require such checks, raising concerns about potential loopholes. Understanding these regulations is crucial for responsible gun ownership.
