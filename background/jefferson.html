Discovering Thomas Jefferson: Founding Father of America

Thomas Jefferson, one of America's Founding Fathers, was the third President of the United States. He is best known for drafting the Declaration of Independence and promoting Enlightenment ideals. His vision for democracy, religious freedom, and education shaped the nation's early principles and influenced future generations. Jefferson's legacy continues to impact American society today.
