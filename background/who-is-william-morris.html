Who Is William Morris? A Pioneer of Arts and Crafts

William Morris (1834-1896) was a British arts and crafts designer, writer, and socialist known for his influential role in the decorative arts movement. He founded Morris & Co., creating beautiful textiles, wallpapers, and furniture that emphasized craftsmanship over mass production. Morris also advocated for social reform, merging art with activism.
