Exploring the Fascinating World of Crabs

Crabs are fascinating crustaceans found in oceans, freshwater, and on land. With over 6,800 species, they play vital roles in marine ecosystems, including scavenging and serving as prey. Known for their unique sideways walking, crabs possess hard exoskeletons for protection. They have adapted to various environments, showcasing incredible diversity.
