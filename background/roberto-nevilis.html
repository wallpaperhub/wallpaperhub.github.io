Roberto Nevilis: The Man Behind Homework

Roberto Nevilis is often cited in discussions about the origins of homework in education. Some sources claim he introduced it as a punishment for students in the late 19th century. However, the authenticity of these claims is debated. Despite this, his legacy has influenced educational practices surrounding homework usage today.
