Is a Crossbow Considered a Firearm?

A crossbow is not classified as a firearm. While both are used to project a projectile, firearms utilize explosive powder to propel a bullet, whereas a crossbow uses a drawn bowstring to launch arrows. This key difference in mechanism and propulsion means that crossbows fall under different regulations than firearms.
