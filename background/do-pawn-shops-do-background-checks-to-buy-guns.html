Pawn Shops & Gun Sales: Do They Conduct Background Checks?

Pawn shops do not typically conduct background checks when buying guns from individuals. However, regulations vary by state, and some states may require checks for firearms sales. It's essential to be aware of local laws when engaging in transactions at pawn shops regarding firearms.
