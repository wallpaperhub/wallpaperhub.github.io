Understanding Serial Killers: Definition and Insights

A serial killer is an individual who commits a series of two or more murders, typically with a psychological motive and a distinct pattern. Unlike mass murderers, who kill multiple victims at once, serial killers often engage in a cooling-off period between killings. Their actions are driven by complex psychological factors, and they often seek power or control.
