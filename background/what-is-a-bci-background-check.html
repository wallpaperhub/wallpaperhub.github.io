Understanding BCI Background Checks: Essential Insights

A BCI (Bureau of Criminal Identification) background check is a process used to verify an individual's criminal history in a particular state. It involves searching official government databases to identify any felonies, misdemeanors, or pending charges. Employers, organizations, and agencies often require this check for safety, security, or employment purposes.
