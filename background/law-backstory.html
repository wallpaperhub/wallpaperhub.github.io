Unveiling Law's Origins: A Historical Overview

The backstory of law traces its origins to ancient societies, where customs and traditions formed the basis of governance. As civilizations evolved, the need for structured legal frameworks emerged, leading to codifications like Hammurabi's Code and Roman Law. This progression laid the groundwork for modern legal systems, embodying principles of justice and order.
