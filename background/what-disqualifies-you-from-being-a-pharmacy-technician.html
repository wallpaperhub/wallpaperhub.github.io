Disqualifications for Pharmacy Technicians: What You Need to Know

Several factors can disqualify individuals from becoming pharmacy technicians. These include felony convictions related to controlled substances, lack of required education or certification, dishonesty or falsification of application materials, and failure to pass background checks or drug screening.
