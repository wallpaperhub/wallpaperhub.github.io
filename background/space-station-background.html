Understanding Space Stations: History & Impact

Space stations are habitable artificial satellites that orbit Earth, serving as platforms for scientific research and technology development. The International Space Station (ISS), a collaborative effort among multiple nations, has advanced our understanding of space environments, biology, and more since its launch in 1998.
