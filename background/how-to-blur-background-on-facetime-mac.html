How to Blur Background in FaceTime on Mac

To blur the background on FaceTime for Mac, open the FaceTime app and start or join a call. Click on the video button in the lower portion to access video settings. From there, choose "Blur Background." This feature creates a professional look by keeping your face in focus while softening the background distractions.
