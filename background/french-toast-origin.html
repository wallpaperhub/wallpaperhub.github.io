Discover the Origins of French Toast

French toast, a delicious breakfast favorite, traces its roots back to ancient Rome. The Romans soaked bread in a mixture of milk and eggs, frying it for a hearty meal. Over centuries, this simple dish evolved, gaining popularity across Europe. Today, French toast is enjoyed globally, showcasing various flavors and toppings.
