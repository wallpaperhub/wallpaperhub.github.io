Do Shotguns Require Background Checks? Understanding the Law

Yes, shotguns, like other firearms, typically require background checks when purchased from licensed dealers. These checks are mandated by federal law to ensure that buyers do not have criminal records or other disqualifying factors. However, regulations may vary by state, so it's essential to be aware of local laws as well.
