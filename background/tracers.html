Understanding Tracers: Unlocking Insights Across Fields

Tracers are valuable tools used in various fields such as science, engineering, and medicine. They are substances or devices that help track processes and movements by emitting detectable signals. In environmental studies, tracers reveal pollutant pathways, while in medical imaging, they aid in diagnosing diseases through enhanced visibility of specific organs and tissues.
