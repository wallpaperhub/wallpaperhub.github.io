Who Owns Frito-Lay? Discover the Parent Company!

Frito-Lay is owned by PepsiCo, one of the largest food and beverage companies in the world. Founded in 1961 through the merger of Frito Company and H.W. Lay & Company, Frito-Lay has become a leader in snack foods globally, offering popular brands like Doritos, Cheetos, and Lay's potato chips.
