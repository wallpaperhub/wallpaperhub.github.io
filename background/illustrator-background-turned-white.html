Fixing a White Background in Illustrator

When your Illustrator background unexpectedly turns white, it can disrupt your workflow. This often happens due to settings changes or document profile adjustments. To restore your desired background color, check the "Document Setup" and "Artboard" settings, or reset your workspace. Remember to save your work frequently!
