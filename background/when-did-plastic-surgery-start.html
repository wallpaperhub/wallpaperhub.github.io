History of Plastic Surgery: From Ancient Techniques to Modern Practice

Plastic surgery has ancient origins, dating back to around 800 B.C. in India, where early techniques for reconstructive surgery were practiced. The field evolved over centuries, with significant advancements made during the Renaissance. Modern plastic surgery as we know it began in the early 20th century, especially during World War I.
