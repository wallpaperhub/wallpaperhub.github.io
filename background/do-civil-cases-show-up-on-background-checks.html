Do Civil Cases Appear on Background Checks?

Yes, civil cases can show up on background checks, depending on the type of check being conducted. Employment checks, for example, may include civil litigation history, such as lawsuits or judgments against an individual. However, not all background checks cover civil cases, so it's important to understand the specific check being performed.
