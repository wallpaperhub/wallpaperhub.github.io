Hide Background Graphics in PowerPoint: Simple Tips

To hide background graphics in PowerPoint, go to the "Design" tab and click on "Format Background." Select the "Solid Fill" or "Gradient Fill" option to cover the background. If you want to hide only specific graphics, select them and press delete. You can also layer shapes to obscure unwanted images.
