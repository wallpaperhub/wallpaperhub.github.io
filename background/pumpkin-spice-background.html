Discover the Origins of Pumpkin Spice

Pumpkin spice has become a beloved seasonal flavor, especially in fall. Its origin can be traced to traditional spice mixes used in pumpkin pie, featuring cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. The trend gained popularity with coffee shops introducing pumpkin spice lattes, becoming a cultural phenomenon. Today, it's celebrated in various foods and drinks.
