Discovering the Rich History of Theatre

Theatre has a rich history that dates back to ancient Greece, where performances were held in open-air amphitheaters. Over centuries, it evolved across cultures, influencing literature and art. Today, theatre encompasses a wide range of genres, including drama, comedy, and musicals, serving as a vital form of cultural expression and entertainment.
