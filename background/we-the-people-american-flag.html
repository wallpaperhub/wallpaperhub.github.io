"We the People: The Significance of the American Flag"

The American flag, a symbol of unity and freedom, represents the ideals of democracy and “We the People.” It reminds us of the sacrifice made by countless individuals for our rights and liberties. Displaying the flag expresses patriotism and honors the diverse tapestry of individuals who contribute to our nation’s strength.
