Who Painted the Sistine Chapel? Discover Michelangelo's Masterpiece

The Sistine Chapel, a masterpiece of Renaissance art, was painted by Michelangelo Buonarroti between 1508 and 1512. This iconic work is renowned for its stunning frescoes, including the famous Creation of Adam, which showcases God's hand giving life to Adam. Michelangelo’s genius is evident in his use of vibrant colors and dynamic compositions.
