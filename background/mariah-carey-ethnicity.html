Understanding Mariah Carey's Ethnicity: A Cultural Blend

Mariah Carey is of mixed ethnicity, primarily African American and Venezuelan. Her father, Alfred Roy Carey, is of African American descent, while her mother, Patricia Hickey, has Irish ancestry. This diverse background has influenced her music and artistic expression, helping her connect with a wide range of audiences worldwide.
