Understanding Quantum Background: A Brief Overview

Quantum background refers to the fundamental principles and phenomena of quantum mechanics that underpin our understanding of the physical universe. This includes concepts like superposition, entanglement, and wave-particle duality. These aspects have profound implications in fields such as computing, cryptography, and quantum physics, leading to innovations that challenge classical paradigms.
