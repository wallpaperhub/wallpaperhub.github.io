History of Oreos: A Timeless Classic

Oreo cookies, introduced in 1912 by Nabisco, are a beloved sandwich cookie known for their rich cream filling and distinctive chocolate wafer. Originally named "Oreo Biscuit," they have become a global icon, featuring various flavors and shapes over the years. Oreos symbolize sweet indulgence and are enjoyed in countless desserts and snacks.
