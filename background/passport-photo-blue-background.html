How to Capture a Perfect Passport Photo with a Blue Background

When taking a passport photo, a blue background is often required for official purposes. Ensure that the background is a solid, light blue color without distractions. Proper lighting and framing are essential for capturing a clear image of your face. Dress in neutral colors and keep the focus on your expression to meet standards.
