Illinois Health Care Worker Registry: Your Source for Trusted Care

The Illinois Health Care Worker Registry is a vital resource for ensuring quality care in the health sector. It maintains a list of certified health care workers, including nursing assistants and home health aides. By keeping track of credentials, the registry promotes safety and accountability, ensuring that patients receive skilled and trusted care throughout the state.
