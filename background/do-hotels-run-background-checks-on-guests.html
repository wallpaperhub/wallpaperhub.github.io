Do Hotels Conduct Background Checks on Guests?

Hotels generally do not run background checks on guests before check-in. However, they may conduct basic identity verification and require a valid ID, especially for security reasons. Some hotels might flag or refuse service to individuals with suspicious behavior or a problematic history, but comprehensive checks are rare.
