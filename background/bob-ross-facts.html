Discover Fascinating Bob Ross Facts

Bob Ross, the iconic painter and host of "The Joy of Painting," inspired millions with his soothing voice and easy-going style. Did you know he was a veteran and a skilled airbrush artist? He painted over 30,000 canvases in his career and famously promoted the idea that "there are no mistakes, only happy accidents."
