Who Created XRP: The Origins of Ripple's Digital Asset

XRP was created by Ripple Labs Inc., a technology company founded in 2012. The brain behind XRP was its co-founder, Chris Larsen, along with Jed McCaleb. They aimed to facilitate faster and cheaper international money transfers using blockchain technology. XRP serves as the digital asset used within the Ripple network.
