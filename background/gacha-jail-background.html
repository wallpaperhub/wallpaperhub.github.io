Understanding Gacha Jail: A Gamer's Frustration

Gacha jail, a term used in gaming culture, refers to the frustration players experience when they are unable to acquire their desired characters or items through randomized "gacha" mechanics. This phenomenon often leads to a sense of being trapped in a cycle of spending and hope, impacting player enjoyment and overall engagement in the game.
