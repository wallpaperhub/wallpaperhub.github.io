Independence Day: Celebrating 15 August in India

August 15 marks India’s Independence Day, a pivotal moment in 1947 when the country gained freedom from British rule. This day symbolizes the struggles and sacrifices of countless freedom fighters and is celebrated nationwide with flag-hoisting ceremonies, parades, and cultural events, reflecting the spirit of unity and patriotism.
