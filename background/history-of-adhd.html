The Evolution of ADHD: A Historical Overview

ADHD, or Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, has roots tracing back to the early 20th century. Initially termed "minimal brain dysfunction," it evolved in the 1960s with increased awareness. By the 1980s, ADHD emerged as a recognized diagnosis, reflecting a greater understanding of behavioral, cognitive, and emotional challenges faced by individuals.
