MTV’s First Song: The Impact of “Video Killed the Radio Star”

The first song aired on MTV was "Video Killed the Radio Star" by The Buggles on August 1, 1981. This iconic moment marked the beginning of a new era in music, where video became a crucial medium for artists to showcase their work. The song's catchy tune and the revolutionary concept of a music video captivated audiences, setting the stage for MT's cultural impact.
