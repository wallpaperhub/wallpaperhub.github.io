How to Easily Make White Background Transparent in Photoshop

To make a white background transparent in Photoshop, start by opening your image. Use the Magic Wand Tool (W) to select the white area, adjusting the tolerance if needed. Once selected, hit Delete to remove the white background. To refine edges, use Select > Modify > Smooth/Feather. Save your image in PNG format to maintain transparency.
