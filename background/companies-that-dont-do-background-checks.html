Companies That Skip Background Checks

Some companies choose not to conduct background checks for various reasons, including a focus on inclusivity and the potential to cast a wider net for talent. This approach can help attract candidates who may have faced obstacles in traditional hiring processes, fostering diverse workplaces and innovative ideas, while also promoting a more forgiving hiring environment.
