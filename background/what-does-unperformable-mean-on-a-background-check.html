Understanding "Unperformable" on Background Checks

"Unperformable" in a background check indicates that certain records or information cannot be evaluated or verified. This could occur due to incomplete data, lack of consent, or issues accessing records. Employers may interpret unperformable results differently, potentially affecting hiring decisions. Ensuring accurate and complete information is vital to avoid complications.
