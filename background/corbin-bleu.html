Corbin Bleu: High School Musical Star and Multifaceted Entertainer

Corbin Bleu is an American actor, singer, and dancer known for his role as Chad Danforth in Disney's "High School Musical" series. He began his career in theatre and has showcased his incredible talent on stage and screen. Beyond acting, Corbin has released music and participated in various philanthropic efforts, making him a multifaceted entertainer.
