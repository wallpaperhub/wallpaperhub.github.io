Fascinating Facts About Amanda Gorman: A Young Poet's Journey

Amanda Gorman is a renowned poet and activist, known for her powerful voice and impactful works. Born on March 7, 1998, in Los Angeles, she became the youngest inaugural poet in U.S. history at 22. Gorman is a graduate of Harvard University and has published several poetry collections, inspiring audiences worldwide with her messages of hope and resilience.
