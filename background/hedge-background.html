Hedge background refers to the use of hedges, which are dense shrubs or trees, to create a natural barrier in gardens and landscaping. They provide privacy, reduce noise, and can serve as windbreaks. Hedges can also enhance the aesthetic appeal of a property while offering shelter for wildlife.
