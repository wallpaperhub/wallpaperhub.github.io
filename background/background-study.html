Understanding Background Studies: A Research Essential

A background study is a critical examination of existing research and literature that informs an investigation. It identifies gaps in knowledge, contextualizes the study, and supports the researcher's objectives. By reviewing prior work, researchers gain insights, enabling them to position their inquiry effectively within a broader academic discourse.
