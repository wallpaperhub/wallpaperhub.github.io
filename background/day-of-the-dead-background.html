Vibrant History of Day of the Dead: A Celebration of Life

The Day of the Dead, or Día de los Muertos, is a vibrant Mexican holiday celebrated on November 1st and 2nd. It honors deceased loved ones with colorful altars, sugar skulls, and marigolds. Families gather to reminisce, share stories, and celebrate the lives of those who have passed, blending indigenous traditions with Catholic beliefs for a unique cultural experience.
