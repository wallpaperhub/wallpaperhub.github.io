Stunning Cempasuchil Drawings: Capture the Spirit of Día de los Muertos

Cempasuchil, or marigold, is a vibrant flower deeply rooted in Mexican culture, particularly during Día de los Muertos. Drawing cempasuchil involves capturing its striking orange-yellow petals and intricate details. It symbolizes the beauty of life and death, making it a meaningful subject for artists and admirers alike.
