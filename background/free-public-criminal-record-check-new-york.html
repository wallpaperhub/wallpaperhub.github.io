Free Criminal Record Checks in New York: How to Access

In New York, you can access free public criminal record checks through the New York State Unified Court System's online database. This tool allows individuals to search for criminal cases involving specific names. Alternatively, local law enforcement can provide information on arrest records. Be aware of privacy laws when using these services.
