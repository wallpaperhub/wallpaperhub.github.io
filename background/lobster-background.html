Discover the Fascinating World of Lobsters

Lobsters are crustaceans found mostly in North Atlantic waters, thriving in rocky sea floors. Known for their large pincers and hard shells, they are a delicacy enjoyed globally. Historically, they were considered food for the poor due to their abundance, but today, they symbolize luxury, especially in seafood cuisine.
