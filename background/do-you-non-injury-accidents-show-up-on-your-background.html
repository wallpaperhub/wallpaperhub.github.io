Do Non-Injury Accidents Affect Your Background Check?

Non-injury accidents can appear on your background check, depending on the reporting agency and the nature of the check. Typically, insurance companies track these incidents, but they may not show up in standard background checks for employment. It's essential to understand how these records are handled by various institutions.
