Background YouTube on iPhone: Play Videos & Music Anywhere

To play YouTube in the background on your iPhone, the simplest method is to use Safari. Open YouTube in Safari, play your desired video, then switch to desktop mode. Press the home button or swipe up, and your video will continue playing while you use other apps. This way, enjoy music or podcasts seamlessly!
