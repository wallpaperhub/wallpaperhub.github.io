M.I.A.: The Iconic Artist Blending Music and Activism

M.I.A., or Mathangi "Maya" Arulpragasam, is a British-Tamil artist renowned for her eclectic music blending hip-hop, electronic, and world sounds. Her bold lyrics often tackle political and social issues, reflecting her activism and unique perspective as an immigrant. With hits like "Paper Planes," she continues to inspire and innovate.
