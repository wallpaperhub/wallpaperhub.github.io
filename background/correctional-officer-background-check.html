Essential Guide to Correctional Officer Background Checks

Correctional officer background checks are essential for ensuring the safety and integrity of correctional facilities. These checks typically include criminal history reviews, employment verification, and psychological evaluations. By thoroughly vetting candidates, the hiring process helps maintain a secure environment for both staff and inmates.
