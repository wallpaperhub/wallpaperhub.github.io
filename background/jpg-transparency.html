JPG Images and Transparency: What You Need to Know

JPG images do not support transparency natively, meaning any transparent areas will typically appear as solid backgrounds instead. For projects requiring transparency, consider using PNG format, which retains details clearly without a solid backdrop. This is essential for graphic design and web usage, where overlaying images is common.
