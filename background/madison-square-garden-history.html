Discover the Rich History of Madison Square Garden

Madison Square Garden, known as "The Garden," opened in 1968, though its origins trace back to 1879. Throughout its history, it has hosted countless iconic events, from legendary boxing matches to historic concerts. This landmark has become synonymous with sports and entertainment, deeply embedding itself in New York City's cultural fabric.
