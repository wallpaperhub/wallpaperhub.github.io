How to Change Your iPhone Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

Changing your iPhone wallpaper is a simple way to personalize your device. To do this, go to Settings, tap on Wallpaper, then select "Choose a New Wallpaper." Browse through dynamic, stills, or your photo library. Once you find the perfect image, set it for your lock screen, home screen, or both. Enjoy your fresh look!
