How to Easily Set Wallpaper on Your iPhone

Setting a wallpaper on your iPhone is easy and can personalize your device. To change your wallpaper, go to Settings, then tap on Wallpaper and select Choose a New Wallpaper. You can pick from Apple's stunning stock images or your own photos. Adjust and set it for your Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both!
