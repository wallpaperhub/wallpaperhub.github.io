How to Set a GIF as Wallpaper on iPhone

Yes, you can set a GIF as a wallpaper on your iPhone, but not directly. First, convert the GIF into a Live Photo using a compatible app. Once converted, go to Settings, choose Wallpaper, and select your Live Photo. This way, you can enjoy the motion of your favorite GIF as your wallpaper whenever you 3D touch the screen!
