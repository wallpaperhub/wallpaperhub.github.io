How to Change Your iPhone Home Screen Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

Changing the wallpaper on your iPhone's home screen is simple and can personalize your device! Start by going to Settings, tap on Wallpaper, then choose "Choose a New Wallpaper." You can select from Apple's default images or your own photos. Once you've picked the perfect image, set it as your home screen background and enjoy a fresh look!
