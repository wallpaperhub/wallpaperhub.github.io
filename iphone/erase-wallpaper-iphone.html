How to Erase Wallpaper on iPhone Easily

To erase wallpaper on your iPhone, go to Settings > Wallpaper. Tap 'Choose a New Wallpaper' and select 'Still' or 'Dynamic.' You can also delete a wallpaper by selecting 'None' to remove the current wallpaper from your home or lock screen. This process ensures a fresh look and personalized style.
