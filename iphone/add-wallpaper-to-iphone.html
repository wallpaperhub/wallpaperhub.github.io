How to Add Wallpaper to Your iPhone: A Quick Guide

Adding wallpaper to your iPhone is a simple way to personalize your device. Start by choosing an image from your Photos app, or you can download a new wallpaper from the internet. Go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. Select your desired image, set it as Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both, and tap Set. Enjoy the fresh look!
