Set a Video as Wallpaper on iPhone: A Quick Guide

Yes, you can set a video as your wallpaper on an iPhone. This feature allows for dynamic, moving backgrounds that can enhance your device's aesthetic. To do this, use the "Live Photo" option. Simply select a video, convert it to a Live Photo via apps or settings, and set this as your wallpaper for a lively experience.
