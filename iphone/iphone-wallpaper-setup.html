Customizing Your iPhone Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

Setting up your iPhone wallpaper is a simple way to personalize your device. Start by tapping on "Settings," then select "Wallpaper." Choose "Choose a New Wallpaper" to browse through stock images or your photos. You can set it for your Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both. Adjust the perspective effect if desired, and you're all set!
