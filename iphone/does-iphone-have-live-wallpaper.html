Yes, iPhones do support live wallpapers, which can add a dynamic touch to your home screen or lock screen. Users can choose from pre-installed live wallpapers or select from the Apple Store to personalize their device. To activate live wallpapers, simply 3D touch or long-press the screen to see the effect in action!
