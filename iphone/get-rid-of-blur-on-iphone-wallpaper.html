How to Eliminate Blur on iPhone Wallpaper

To get rid of the blur on your iPhone wallpaper, start by selecting high-resolution images, preferably 1920x1080 or higher. Go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper and pick your image. Ensure it fits properly. Avoid zooming in too much, which can cause pixelation. For best results, use images from reputable sources.
