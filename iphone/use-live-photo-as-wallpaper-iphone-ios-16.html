Set Live Photos as iPhone Wallpaper in iOS 16

To use a Live Photo as wallpaper on your iPhone running iOS 16, first, open the Photos app. Find your Live Photo, tap the share icon, and select “Use as Wallpaper.” Make sure to enable "Live Photo" option before setting it. Adjust the position, then tap “Set” to apply it as your lock screen or home screen wallpaper.
