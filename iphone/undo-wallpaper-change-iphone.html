How to Undo a Wallpaper Change on iPhone

To undo a wallpaper change on your iPhone, go to Settings > Wallpaper. Tap "Choose a New Wallpaper" to select your desired wallpaper from your photos or Apple’s libraries. Once chosen, tap "Set," then choose whether to apply it to your Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both. Your previous wallpaper will be restored!
