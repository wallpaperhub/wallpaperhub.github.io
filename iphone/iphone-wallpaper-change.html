Refresh Your iPhone: Quick Wallpaper Change Guide

Changing your iPhone wallpaper is a simple way to personalize your device. To do this, go to Settings, tap on Wallpaper, then choose "Choose a New Wallpaper." You can select from dynamic, stills, or photos from your library. Don’t forget to preview how it looks on your home and lock screens before you save. A fresh wallpaper refreshes your mood!
