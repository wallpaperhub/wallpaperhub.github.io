Customize Your iPhone Wallpaper: A Complete Guide

iPhone wallpaper settings let you personalize your device with stunning visuals. To change your wallpaper, go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. You can select from dynamic, stills, or your own photos. Customize the look further with options to set it as the Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both for a unique touch.
