How to Set Wallpaper on Your iPhone: A Quick Guide

Setting wallpaper on your iPhone is a simple way to personalize your device. To change your wallpaper, go to Settings, select "Wallpaper," then choose "Choose a New Wallpaper." You can select from Apple's dynamic, stills, or your own photos. Adjust the image, set it for the lock screen, home screen, or both, and enjoy your fresh look!
