Automatically Change Your iPhone Wallpaper: Here’s How!

Yes, iPhone wallpaper can change automatically through the use of the Dynamic Wallpaper feature or by setting up Live Photos. You can also create a photo shuffle in the Wallpaper settings, allowing your wallpaper to rotate through selected images at set intervals. This feature adds a fresh look to your device effortlessly.
