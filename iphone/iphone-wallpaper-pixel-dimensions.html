Ultimate Guide to iPhone Wallpaper Pixel Dimensions

When selecting iPhone wallpaper, it's important to consider pixel dimensions for optimal quality. For newer models like the iPhone 12 and later, the recommended resolution is 1284 x 2778 pixels. For older models, such as the iPhone 8, use 750 x 1334 pixels. Always ensure the wallpaper is well-sized to prevent stretching or pixelation.
