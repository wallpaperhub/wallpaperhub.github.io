How to Edit Your iPhone Wallpaper in Simple Steps

Customizing your iPhone wallpaper can refresh your device's look and feel. To edit your wallpaper, go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. Select from Apple's options or your own photos. Adjust the image, apply a filter, or change the perspective for a personalized touch. Save your new wallpaper to enjoy a fresh aesthetic!
