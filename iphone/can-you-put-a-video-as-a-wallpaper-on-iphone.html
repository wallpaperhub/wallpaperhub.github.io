How to Set a Video as Wallpaper on iPhone

Yes, you can set a video as your iPhone wallpaper by using a Live Photo. To do this, convert your video to a Live Photo using special apps, then save it. Go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper, and select your Live Photo. This will allow you to have a dynamic wallpaper that moves when you press on the screen!
