How to Easily Remove Wallpaper on Your iPhone

To remove wallpaper from your iPhone, navigate to 'Settings' > 'Wallpaper' > 'Choose a New Wallpaper.' Here, you can select a different image or simply tap 'None' to clear the current wallpaper. Make sure to preview the changes before saving. This process helps customize your device for a fresh look.
