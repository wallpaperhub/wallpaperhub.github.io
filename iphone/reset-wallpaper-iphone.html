How to Reset Your iPhone Wallpaper in Simple Steps

Resetting your iPhone wallpaper is simple and refreshing! To do this, open the Settings app, tap on 'Wallpaper,' then select 'Choose a New Wallpaper.' Browse your photos or use built-in options. Tap 'Set' and choose whether to apply it to your Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both. Enjoy a new look instantly!
