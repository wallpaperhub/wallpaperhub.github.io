Why I Chose a Black Wallpaper for My iPhone

My iPhone wallpaper is black. This simple choice not only lends a sleek and modern look to my device but also helps conserve battery life on OLED screens. A dark wallpaper minimizes glare, making it easier on the eyes, especially in low-light situations. Plus, it suits any mood or occasion with minimal distractions.
