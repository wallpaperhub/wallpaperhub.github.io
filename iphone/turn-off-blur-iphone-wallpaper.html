Turn Off Wallpaper Blur on iPhone

To turn off the blur effect on your iPhone wallpaper, go to Settings > Wallpaper. Choose the wallpaper you want to customize and select "Still" instead of "Perspective." This disables the motion blur, giving you a crisp, clear wallpaper. Enjoy a sharper look for your homescreen without distractions.
