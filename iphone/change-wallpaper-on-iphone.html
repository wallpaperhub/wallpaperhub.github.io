How to Change Wallpaper on iPhone: A Quick Guide

Changing your wallpaper on your iPhone is an easy way to personalize your device. Start by opening the Settings app, then tap on "Wallpaper." Choose "Select New Wallpaper" to pick from dynamic, stills, or your photos. Once you’ve made your choice, tap "Set," and customize it for your Home Screen, Lock Screen, or both!
