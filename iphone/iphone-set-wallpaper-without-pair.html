Change iPhone Wallpaper without Pairing: Quick Guide

Changing the wallpaper on your iPhone without pairing it to iTunes is simple. Go to the "Settings" app, tap "Wallpaper," and then "Choose a New Wallpaper." Select a photo from your library or use Apple's stock images. Adjust the position, then tap "Set," deciding if you want it on the lock screen, home screen, or both.
