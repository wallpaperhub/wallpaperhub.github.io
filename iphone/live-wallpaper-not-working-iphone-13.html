Fix Live Wallpaper Issues on iPhone 13

Is your live wallpaper not working on your iPhone 13? This can be frustrating, but there are several fixes you can try. First, ensure your wallpaper settings are correctly selected: go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. If that doesn’t help, try restarting your device or updating iOS to the latest version. If live wallpapers still won’t work, consider resetting your settings, as this may resolve any underlying issues.
