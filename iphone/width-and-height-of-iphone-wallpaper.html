Essential iPhone Wallpaper Size Guide: Width & Height

When setting an iPhone wallpaper, it's essential to consider the correct dimensions. The standard wallpaper size for most iPhone models is 1125 x 2436 pixels for iPhone X and later. For older models like the iPhone 7 and 8, the size is 750 x 1334 pixels. Always check your model to ensure a perfect fit!
