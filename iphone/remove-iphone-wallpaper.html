How to Remove iPhone Wallpaper in Simple Steps

To remove an iPhone wallpaper, go to Settings, then tap Wallpaper. Select "Choose a New Wallpaper" and select "None" or "Standard" to revert to the default. You can also choose a new image from your photos or from Apple’s wallpapers. Confirm changes to achieve your desired look on the home and lock screens.
