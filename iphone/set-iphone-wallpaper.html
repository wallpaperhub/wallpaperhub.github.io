Stylish iPhone Wallpaper: Set It Up Easily

Setting a captivating wallpaper on your iPhone is simple and enhances your device's look. To do so, go to Settings > Wallpaper > Choose a New Wallpaper. You can select from Apple’s stunning images or your own photos. Adjust the image as needed, and tap 'Set' to choose whether you want it for your Home screen, Lock screen, or both!
