How to Change Wallpaper on iPhone 14: A Quick Guide

To change the wallpaper on your iPhone 14, open the Settings app and select "Wallpaper." Tap "Choose a New Wallpaper" to browse through Apple’s dynamic, stills, or your own photos. Once you pick an image, you can set it as your lock screen, home screen, or both. Don’t forget to tap "Set" to apply your new wallpaper!
