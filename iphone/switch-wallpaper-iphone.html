How to Easily Switch iPhone Wallpapers

Switching wallpapers on your iPhone is simple and personalized. To change, go to Settings, select Wallpaper, then choose ‘Choose a New Wallpaper’. Browse through Apple's options or select an image from your library. You can set it as your Lock Screen, Home Screen, or both. Embrace creativity with every glance!
