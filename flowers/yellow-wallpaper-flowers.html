Exploring the Symbolism of Yellow Wallpaper Flowers

Yellow wallpaper flowers symbolize the struggle for identity and freedom within domestic confines. In literature, particularly in "The Yellow Wallpaper" by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, these flowers reflect the protagonist's mental state. Their vibrant yet entangled nature echoes the claustrophobia of societal expectations on women.
