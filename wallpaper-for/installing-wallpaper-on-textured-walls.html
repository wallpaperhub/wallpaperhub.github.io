How to Successfully Install Wallpaper on Textured Walls

Installing wallpaper on textured walls can be challenging but rewarding. Start by cleaning the surface thoroughly to remove dust and dirt. Apply a heavy-duty wallpaper adhesive or use the paste method for better adhesion. Smooth out bubbles with a wallpaper brush, ensuring the pattern aligns properly. Consider using thicker wallpaper for better coverage on irregular surfaces to achieve a flawless finish.
