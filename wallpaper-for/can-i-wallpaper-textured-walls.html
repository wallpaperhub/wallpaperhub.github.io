Wallpapering Textured Walls: Tips & Techniques

Yes, you can wallpaper textured walls, but preparation is key. Start by cleaning the surface and repairing any damage. A thicker wallpaper or a wallpaper liner can help hide imperfections. Ensure you use adhesive suitable for textured surfaces for the best results, and consider the pattern to ensure it adheres well.
