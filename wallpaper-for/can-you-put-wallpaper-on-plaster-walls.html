Wallpapering Plaster Walls: Tips & Techniques

Yes, you can put wallpaper on plaster walls, but preparation is key. Ensure the surface is clean, dry, and smooth. If the plaster is too rough, consider sanding it down or applying a primer. Use appropriate adhesive for best results, and be mindful of moisture levels to prevent peeling.
