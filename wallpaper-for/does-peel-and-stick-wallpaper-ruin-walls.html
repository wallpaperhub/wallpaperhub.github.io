Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Damage Walls?

Peel and stick wallpaper is designed to be removed without damaging walls, making it a popular choice for renters. However, proper application and removal techniques are crucial. If applied to textured or dirty surfaces, or if left on too long, it may cause paint removal or adhesive residue. Always test a small area first.
