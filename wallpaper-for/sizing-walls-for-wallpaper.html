Perfectly Size Your Walls for Wallpaper Installation

When sizing walls for wallpaper, it's essential to measure the height and width to determine the total square footage. Subtract any doors and windows from the total area. Always buy extra wallpaper to account for matching patterns and waste during installation. Proper sizing ensures a seamless and professional finish.
