Can You Use Wallpaper on Textured Walls?

Wallpaper can be applied over textured walls, but achieving a smooth finish can be challenging. It's crucial to choose the right type of wallpaper and adhesive. To minimize imperfections, you might need to apply a lining paper first or opt for textured wallpaper that complements your wall. Always ensure the surface is clean and dry for best results.
