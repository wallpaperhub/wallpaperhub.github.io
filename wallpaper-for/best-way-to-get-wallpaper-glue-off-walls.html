How to Easily Remove Wallpaper Glue from Walls

To effectively remove wallpaper glue from walls, start by soaking the glue with warm water using a sponge. Allow it to sit for a few minutes to loosen the adhesive. Gently scrape with a plastic scraper or putty knife. For stubborn spots, use a mixture of vinegar and water or a commercial adhesive remover. Rinse and wipe clean.
