Tips for Hanging Wallpaper on Textured Walls

Hanging wallpaper on textured walls can transform your space but requires a few extra steps. Start by cleaning the wall surface and filling in deep textures. Use a heavy-duty adhesive on thicker wallpaper to ensure it adheres well. Smooth out air bubbles with a wallpaper brush and allow the wallpaper to cure for a lasting finish.
