Does Wallpaper Damage Walls?

Wallpaper can cause damage to walls, especially if it's not applied or removed properly. Poor adhesion may lead to peeling, while adhesive residue can harm paint or drywall when removed. There's also the risk of moisture retention, leading to mold growth. Proper preparation and removal techniques can minimize potential wall damage.
