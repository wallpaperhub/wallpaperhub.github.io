How to Fix Sticky Walls After Removing Wallpaper

After removing wallpaper, it's common for walls to feel sticky due to adhesive residue. To resolve this, start by cleaning the walls with warm water and a mild detergent to remove any leftover glue. If necessary, you can use a wallpaper adhesive remover. Once clean, allow the surface to dry completely before repainting or applying new wallpaper.
