Mastering Wallpaper on Uneven Walls: Tips & Tricks

Applying wallpaper on uneven walls can be a challenge, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a beautiful finish. Start by preparing the surface—fill in gaps and smooth out rough areas. Use thick wallpaper to better conceal imperfections, and consider using a paste-the-wall option for easier application. Embrace creativity with patterns that distract from unevenness!
