Wallpapering Textured Walls: Tips & Tricks

Putting wallpaper on textured walls can be challenging, but with the right preparation, it can yield stunning results. Start by cleaning the walls and filling in any deep grooves or holes. Use a heavy-duty adhesive for better grip. Opt for thicker wallpaper, as it can mask imperfections and enhance the overall look of your space.
