Peel and Stick Wallpaper on Textured Walls: A Guide

Peel and stick wallpaper can be applied to textured walls, but the results may vary. Textured surfaces can affect adhesion and may lead to bubbling or lifting. For the best outcome, choose thicker, more durable wallpaper and consider prepping the surface by smoothing out prominent textures where possible for a flat application.
