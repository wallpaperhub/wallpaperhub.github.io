Wallpapering on Orange Peel Walls: A Guide

Yes, you can put wallpaper on orange peel walls, but it's essential to prepare the surface first. Start by cleaning the wall thoroughly and then consider using a wallpaper primer to smooth out the texture. This will help the wallpaper adhere better and prevent future peeling. Choose a thicker wallpaper for better coverage and lasting results.
