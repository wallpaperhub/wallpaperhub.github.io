Essential Steps to Prep Walls for Perfect Wallpapering

Preparing walls for wallpaper is essential for achieving a flawless finish. Start by cleaning the walls to remove dust and grime. Repair any cracks or holes with spackle, then sand the surface until smooth. Apply a primer to help the wallpaper adhere better. Finally, ensure the walls are dry and level before hanging the wallpaper for best results.
