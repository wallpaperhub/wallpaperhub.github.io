Prep Your Walls for Peel and Stick Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

To prep walls for peel and stick wallpaper, start by ensuring the surface is clean, dry, and smooth. Remove any dust, grease, or old wallpaper. Patch holes and imperfections with spackle and sand down any rough spots. Finally, wipe the walls with a damp cloth to ensure maximum adhesion, making your project hassle-free and successful.
