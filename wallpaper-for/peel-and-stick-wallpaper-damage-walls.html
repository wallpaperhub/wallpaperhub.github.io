Is Peel and Stick Wallpaper Damaging Your Walls?

Peel and stick wallpaper can be a stylish and convenient option for home decor. However, removing it may cause damage to walls, potentially leading to paint peel or drywall issues. To minimize damage, carefully follow removal instructions, use a heat source if necessary, and take your time. Always test a small section first.
