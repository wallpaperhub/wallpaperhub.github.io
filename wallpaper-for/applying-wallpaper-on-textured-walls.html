How to Easily Apply Wallpaper on Textured Walls

Applying wallpaper on textured walls requires preparation for a smooth finish. Begin by cleaning the surface and repairing any major imperfections. Use a heavy-duty wallpaper adhesive that can grip the uneven texture. Apply wallpaper with a smoothing tool to eliminate bubbles. Choose a thicker wallpaper design for better coverage.
