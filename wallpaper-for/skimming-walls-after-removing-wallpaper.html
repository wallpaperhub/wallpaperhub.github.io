How to Skim Walls After Removing Wallpaper

Skimming walls after removing wallpaper is essential for achieving a smooth finish. Begin by cleaning the wall to eliminate residues and defects. Apply a skim coat of joint compound using a broad knife, working in small sections. Once dry, sand the surface to ensure it's even and prepares it for painting or new wallpaper. This process enhances aesthetics and longevity.
