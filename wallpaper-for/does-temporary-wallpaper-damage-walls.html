Does Temporary Wallpaper Damage Walls? Myths & Facts

Temporary wallpaper is a popular choice for renters and DIY enthusiasts, offering a stylish and versatile way to decorate. Generally, when applied and removed correctly, it doesn't damage walls. However, some adhesives can leave residue or remove paint. Always check compatibility with your wall type before application for best results.
