Does Wallpaper Damage Walls? Find Out Here!

Wallpaper can potentially damage walls when removed improperly or if it's poorly installed. The adhesive can pull off paint or drywall, leading to costly repairs. However, high-quality wallpaper and professional installation minimize the risk. Regular maintenance and proper removal techniques can help protect the integrity of your walls.
