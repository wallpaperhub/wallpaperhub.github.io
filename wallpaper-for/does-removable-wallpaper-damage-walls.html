Does Removable Wallpaper Damage Walls? Find Out!

Removable wallpaper is designed to be safe for walls, and it typically does not cause damage when applied and removed properly. However, the condition of the underlying paint and wall surface matters. Some materials may peel or damage paint upon removal. Always test a small area first to ensure your walls remain intact.
