The Symbolism of "The Yellow Wallpaper": Imagery Insights

The imagery in "The Yellow Wallpaper" embodies the narrator's mental entrapment and deteriorating sanity. The wallpaper itself, with its chaotic patterns, symbolizes societal constraints and the domestic sphere that confines women. As the narrator obsesses over the yellow wallpaper, it transforms from a mere decorative element into a manifestation of her struggles.
