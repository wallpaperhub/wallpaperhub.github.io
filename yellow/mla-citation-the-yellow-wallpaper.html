The Yellow Wallpaper: A Guide to MLA Citation

To cite "The Yellow Wallpaper" by Charlotte Perkins Gilman in MLA format, include the author's name, title in italics, publisher, and year of publication. For example: Gilman, Charlotte Perkins. *The Yellow Wallpaper*. Dover Publications, 2005. Make sure to adjust based on the edition you are referencing, including page numbers if applicable.
