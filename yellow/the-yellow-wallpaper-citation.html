The Yellow Wallpaper: Understanding Its Themes and Impact

The Yellow Wallpaper, a short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, explores themes of mental illness and female oppression in the 19th century. Through the narrator's descent into madness, symbolized by the wallpaper, Gilman critiques the traditional treatment of women's mental health. This poignant commentary remains relevant in discussions of women's rights today.
