The Yellow Wallpaper: Publisher Insights

The Yellow Wallpaper, a seminal work by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, was first published in 1892 in "The New England Magazine." This influential short story explores themes of mental health and gender roles through the experiences of a woman confined to a room. Its poignant critique of societal norms remains relevant today.
