"Unlocking the Secrets of The Yellow Wallpaper Plot"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" follows a woman's descent into madness as she is confined to a room for rest cure treatment. The plot begins with her initial confinement, where she becomes obsessed with the room's yellow wallpaper. As the story progresses, her mental state deteriorates, culminating in a powerful climax where she identifies with the trapped woman in the wallpaper, leading to her ultimate breakdown.
