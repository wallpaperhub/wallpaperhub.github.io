The Symbolism of The Yellow Wallpaper: A Deep Dive

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," Charlotte Perkins Gilman employs rich figurative language to convey themes of mental illness and feminism. The wallpaper itself symbolizes the narrator's confinement, with its chaotic patterns reflecting her deteriorating mind. Metaphors and similes enhance the emotional intensity, illustrating her struggle for freedom.
