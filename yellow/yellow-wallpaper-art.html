Exploring the Symbolism of Yellow Wallpaper Art

Yellow wallpaper art represents the intersection of psychological struggle and aesthetic beauty. Often associated with the feminist movement, its intricate patterns reflect themes of confinement and freedom. This art form captivates audiences, inviting deep reflection on mental health and societal norms, making it a powerful visual statement.
