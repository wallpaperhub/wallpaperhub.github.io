Exploring the Theme of Yellow Wallpaper: A Feminist Perspective

The yellow wallpaper in Charlotte Perkins Gilman's short story symbolizes the oppressive forces of patriarchy and mental illness. It reflects the protagonist's descent into madness as she is confined and silenced, mirroring her struggles for autonomy. The chaotic patterns represent her trapped psyche and desire for freedom, making it a powerful feminist critique.
