The Yellow Wallpaper: Character Analysis & Interpretation

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," the main character's descent into madness is a poignant reflection of societal oppression. Confined by her husband, John, she becomes obsessed with the yellow wallpaper in her room, which symbolizes her entrapment. Her struggle for identity and freedom culminates in a powerful critique of gender roles and mental illness.
