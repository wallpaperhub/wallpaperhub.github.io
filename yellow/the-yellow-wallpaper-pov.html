The Yellow Wallpaper: A Journey Through Madness

The Yellow Wallpaper by Charlotte Perkins Gilman is narrated through a first-person perspective, capturing the descent into madness of the protagonist. Her confinement in a room with oppressive yellow wallpaper reflects her struggle against societal norms and mental illness, illustrating her increasing obsession and isolation.
