Exploring the Setting of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The setting of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is a secluded, inherited mansion where the protagonist is confined to a room for rest cure. The room features peeling yellow wallpaper that becomes increasingly significant as her mental state deteriorates. The isolation and oppressive atmosphere reflect the broader societal constraints on women during the late 19th century.
