Exploring the Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper

The yellow wallpaper in Charlotte Perkins Gilman's short story symbolizes the oppression of women in the 19th century. It reflects the protagonist's struggle against societal constraints and mental illness. As she becomes increasingly obsessed with it, the wallpaper embodies her descent into madness, illustrating the dangers of confinement and the quest for freedom.
