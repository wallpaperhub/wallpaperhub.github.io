Unraveling Themes in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The theme of "The Yellow Wallpaper" revolves around the oppression of women and the detrimental effects of societal expectations on mental health. It highlights the struggle for identity and self-expression, reflecting the consequences of confinement and isolation. The wallpaper itself symbolizes the entrapment of the protagonist's mind and spirit.
