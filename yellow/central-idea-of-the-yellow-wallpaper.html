Understanding "The Yellow Wallpaper": A Journey to Freedom

The central idea of "The Yellow Wallpaper" revolves around the struggles of a woman who faces oppression in a patriarchal society. Confined to a room with yellow wallpaper, her mental health deteriorates as she obsesses over the wallpaper's patterns. This symbolizes her fight against societal constraints and her quest for personal freedom.
