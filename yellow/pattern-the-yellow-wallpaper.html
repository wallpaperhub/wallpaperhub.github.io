Understanding the Symbolism in The Yellow Wallpaper

The "Pattern" in "The Yellow Wallpaper" symbolizes the protagonist's mental struggle and societal constraints. As she obsesses over the wallpaper's chaotic design, it reflects her confinement and gradual descent into madness. The wallpaper becomes a representation of her fight for freedom against patriarchal oppression in a stifling environment.
