Unraveling the Secrets of The Yellow Wallpaper

"The Yellow Wallpaper," a short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, delves into the oppressive realities faced by women in the 19th century. It tells the tale of a woman confined to a room, suffering from postpartum depression and gradually losing her grip on reality. The narrative symbolizes the struggle for identity and freedom.
