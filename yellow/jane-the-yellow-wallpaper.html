"Exploring 'The Yellow Wallpaper': A Dive into Madness and Gender"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" by Charlotte Perkins Gilman is a groundbreaking short story that explores themes of mental health, gender roles, and oppression. It tells the tale of a woman confined to a room by her husband, where she obsesses over the wallpaper. This descent into madness symbolizes the struggles women face in a patriarchal society.
