Discover the Hidden Meaning Behind the Old Yellow Wallpaper

The "Old Yellow Wallpaper" symbolizes the struggle of women against societal norms and mental illness. In Charlotte Perkins Gilman's short story, a woman's descent into madness is mirrored by the grotesque wallpaper in her confinement. This vivid narrative explores themes of oppression, identity, and the quest for freedom.
