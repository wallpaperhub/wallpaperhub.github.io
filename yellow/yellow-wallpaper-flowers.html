Explore the Symbolism of Yellow Wallpaper Flowers

Yellow wallpaper flowers symbolize both beauty and entrapment in literature. They're often depicted as vibrant yet suffocating, reflecting the inner turmoil of characters like the narrator in Charlotte Perkins Gilman's "The Yellow Wallpaper." The flowers' intricate patterns mirror societal constraints faced by women.
