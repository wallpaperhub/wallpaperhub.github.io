The Symbolism Behind The Yellow Wallpaper: A Deep Dive

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," the wallpaper itself symbolizes the constraints imposed on women and their mental health in the 19th century. As the protagonist's obsession with the wallpaper grows, it reflects her descent into madness caused by societal repression. The trapped woman behind the pattern symbolizes the struggle for freedom.
