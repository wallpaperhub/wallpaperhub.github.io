"Exploring 'The Yellow Wallpaper': A Study of Madness and Freedom"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" is a haunting short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, exploring themes of mental illness and female oppression. Through the perspective of a woman confined to a nursery, it delves into the impact of isolation and societal repression on her psyche. The unsettling narrative symbolizes the struggle for identity and freedom.
