The Haunting Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper Room

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," a woman's descent into madness is depicted through her obsession with the wallpaper in her room. Confined to a nursery by her husband for rest, she becomes increasingly fixated on the grotesque patterns, symbolizing her struggle against oppression. The room transforms from a space of care into a prison, illustrating her desperate fight for freedom and identity.
