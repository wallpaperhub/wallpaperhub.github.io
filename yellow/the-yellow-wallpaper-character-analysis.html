The Yellow Wallpaper: A Deep Dive into Character Analysis

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," the unnamed narrator epitomizes the struggles of women in a patriarchal society. As her mental health deteriorates, the wallpaper symbolizes her confinement and the societal expectations stifling her creativity. Her eventual rebellion underscores themes of freedom and self-identity, making her a powerful representation of resistance.
