Understanding "The Yellow Wallpaper": A Critical Analysis

The thesis of "The Yellow Wallpaper" delves into the themes of mental illness, gender roles, and the oppressive nature of societal expectations. Through the protagonist’s descent into madness, the story illustrates how forced confinement and lack of autonomy can lead to a spiraling decline in one's mental health, highlighting the need for self-expression and freedom.
