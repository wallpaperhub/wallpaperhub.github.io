"Themes in The Yellow Wallpaper: Mental Health & Women's Oppression"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" explores themes of mental illness, gender roles, and the oppression of women. Through the protagonist's descent into madness, the story critiques the patriarchal society that confines women, showcasing the struggle for self-identity and liberation. The wallpaper symbolizes the entrapment and the quest for freedom.
