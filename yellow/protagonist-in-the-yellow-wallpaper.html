Understanding the Protagonist in The Yellow Wallpaper

The protagonist in "The Yellow Wallpaper" is a woman diagnosed with a mental illness and prescribed the "rest cure." Confined to a room by her husband, she becomes obsessed with the room's yellow wallpaper, which she believes conceals a trapped woman. Her descent into madness reflects the struggles of women against societal norms.
