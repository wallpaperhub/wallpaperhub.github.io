The Power of Symbols in The Yellow Wallpaper

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," symbols play a crucial role in conveying themes of mental illness and gender oppression. The wallpaper itself symbolizes the constraints imposed on women, with its intricate patterns reflecting the narrator's entrapment. The barred windows signify her isolation, while the figure she imagines in the wallpaper represents her struggle for freedom and identity.
