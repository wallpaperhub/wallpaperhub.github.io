The Yellow Wallpaper: A Psychoanalytic Exploration

The Yellow Wallpaper delves into the psychological deterioration of a woman confined by societal norms. Through her descent into madness, it reflects themes of identity, repression, and the quest for autonomy. The wallpaper symbolizes her struggle and the constraints placed on women, revealing the profound impacts of mental health and isolation.
