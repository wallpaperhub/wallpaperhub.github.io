"The Yellow Wallpaper: A Visual and Literary Exploration"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" explores the themes of madness, isolation, and gender roles through the lens of art. The wallpaper itself becomes a symbol of the protagonist's mental decline, intricately woven with patterns that reflect her entrapment. Artists have embraced this imagery, creating powerful visual interpretations that captivate and provoke thought.
