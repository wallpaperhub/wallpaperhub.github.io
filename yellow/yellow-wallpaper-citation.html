The Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper: An Analysis

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," Charlotte Perkins Gilman explores themes of mental illness and societal oppression through the protagonist's descent into madness. The wallpaper itself becomes a symbol of her confinement, reflecting her struggles against a patriarchal society that stifles her creativity and autonomy.
