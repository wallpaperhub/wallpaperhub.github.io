Understanding the Symbolism of Plain Yellow Wallpaper

The "plain yellow wallpaper" evokes a haunting atmosphere, symbolizing confinement and mental struggle. Often associated with Charlotte Perkins Gilman's classic story, it represents a woman's descent into madness as she is trapped in a domestic space. The wallpaper becomes a mirror of her psyche, blurring reality with haunting imagery.
