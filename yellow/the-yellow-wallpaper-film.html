The Yellow Wallpaper: A Haunting Psychological Thriller

The Yellow Wallpaper is a psychological thriller film that dives deep into themes of mental illness, isolation, and gender roles. Adapted from Charlotte Perkins Gilman's haunting short story, it follows a woman's descent into madness as she grapples with her oppressive surroundings and societal expectations, pushing the boundaries of reality.
