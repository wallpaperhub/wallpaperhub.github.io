Understanding the Main Character in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The main character in "The Yellow Wallpaper" is a woman suffering from postpartum depression. Confined to a room by her husband, she becomes obsessed with the yellow wallpaper. As her mental state deteriorates, she begins to identify with the trapped woman she perceives in the patterns, symbolizing her struggle for freedom.
