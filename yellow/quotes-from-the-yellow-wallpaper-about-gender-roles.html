Understanding Gender Roles in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," Charlotte Perkins Gilman vividly explores gender roles through the narrator's confinement and mental decline. Quotes highlight the oppressive nature of a patriarchal society, exemplified by her husband’s dominance and the stifling domestic expectations. The wallpaper itself symbolizes her struggle against these constraints.
