Understanding the Theme of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" explores themes of mental health, gender roles, and the oppression of women in the 19th century. The wallpaper symbolizes the protagonist's confinement and the struggle for self-identity. As her mental state deteriorates, the story critiques societal norms and the stifling effects of isolation and patriarchy.
