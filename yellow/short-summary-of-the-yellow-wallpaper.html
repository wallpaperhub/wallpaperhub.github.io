Understanding "The Yellow Wallpaper": A Brief Overview

The Yellow Wallpaper tells the story of a woman suffering from postpartum depression, confined to a room by her physician husband for a "rest cure." Isolated and tormented by the room's yellow wallpaper, she descends into madness, obsessively analyzing the patterns as she identifies with a trapped woman within. The tale explores themes of mental illness and societal oppression.
