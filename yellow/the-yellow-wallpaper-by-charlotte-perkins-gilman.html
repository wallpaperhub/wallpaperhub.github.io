The Yellow Wallpaper: A Journey of Madness and Freedom

The Yellow Wallpaper by Charlotte Perkins Gilman explores the descent into madness of a woman prescribed the "rest cure" for her mental health. Confined to a room with disturbing yellow wallpaper, she becomes obsessed with its patterns, symbolizing her struggle against societal oppression and the quest for self-identity.
