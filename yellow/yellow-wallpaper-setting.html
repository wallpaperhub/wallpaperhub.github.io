Unraveling the Symbolism of "The Yellow Wallpaper" Setting

The setting of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is primarily a secluded mansion, where the protagonist is confined to a room for rest and recuperation. The wallpaper's intricate, yellow patterns become a focal point, reflecting her descent into madness. As she observes the wallpaper's chaotic design, it symbolizes her struggle for identity and freedom in a patriarchal society.
