The Haunting Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper Room

The yellow wallpaper room symbolizes the constraints of women’s mental health in the 19th century. Its haunting patterns represent entrapment, mirroring the protagonist's struggle for freedom. As she becomes obsessed with the wallpaper, it reflects her descent into madness, challenging societal norms and exploring identity and autonomy.
