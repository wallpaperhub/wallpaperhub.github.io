The Symbolism of The Yellow Wallpaper Drawings

The yellow wallpaper drawings represent the protagonist's descent into madness as she becomes increasingly obsessed with the wallpaper's chaotic patterns. These sketches reflect her struggles, emotions, and ultimately her desire for freedom. Each stroke reveals layers of her confinement and longing, encapsulating the story’s themes of mental health and gender roles.
