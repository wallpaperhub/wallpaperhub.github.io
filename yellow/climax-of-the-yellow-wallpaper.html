Climax of "The Yellow Wallpaper": A Turning Point

The climax of "The Yellow Wallpaper" occurs when the narrator, having become increasingly obsessed with the wallpaper, finally tears it down in a desperate bid for freedom. This moment symbolizes her rebellion against the oppressive forces in her life, culminating in her complete mental breakdown and assertion of autonomy as she believes she has liberated the woman trapped within the wallpaper.
