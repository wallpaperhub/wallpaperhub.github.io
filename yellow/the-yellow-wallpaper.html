"The Yellow Wallpaper: A Deep Dive into Madness and Feminism"

"The Yellow Wallpaper," a captivating short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, explores themes of mental health and female oppression. Narrated by a woman confined to a room for rest, she descends into madness as she fixates on the room's yellow wallpaper. This powerful tale critiques societal norms of the 19th century and highlights the struggles of women.
