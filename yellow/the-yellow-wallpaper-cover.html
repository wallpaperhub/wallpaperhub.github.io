The Captivating Design of "The Yellow Wallpaper" Cover

The cover of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is iconic, featuring intricate patterns of yellow and green that symbolize the narrator's mental state. The wallpaper embodies themes of confinement and psychological struggle, reflecting the societal constraints faced by women in the 19th century. It's a haunting visual representation of oppression and a quest for identity.
