Understanding the Narrator in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The narrator of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is an unnamed woman suffering from postpartum depression, confined to a nursery by her physician husband. Her descent into madness is captured through her journal entries as she obsesses over the wallpaper. This confinement symbolizes the broader oppression of women in the 19th century.
