Exploring the Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper

The "Yellow Wallpaper" is a powerful short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman that explores mental health and societal oppression. It follows a woman's descent into madness, confined to a room where the yellow wallpaper becomes a symbol of her struggles. This narrative artfully critiques gender roles and the treatment of women in the 19th century.
