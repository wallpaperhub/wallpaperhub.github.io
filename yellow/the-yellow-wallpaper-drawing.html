Unraveling the Significance of the Yellow Wallpaper Drawing

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," the drawing symbolizes the struggle for freedom and identity. The intricate patterns reflect the narrator's deteriorating mental state as she becomes obsessed with the wallpaper's design. It serves as a representation of her confinement, both physically and mentally, ultimately leading to her quest for liberation.
