Exploring Themes in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The themes in "The Yellow Wallpaper" explore mental health, gender roles, and the oppression of women in the 19th century. The wallpaper itself symbolizes the confinement and struggles of the protagonist, reflecting her descent into madness. This powerful narrative critiques societal norms and advocates for self-identity and freedom.
