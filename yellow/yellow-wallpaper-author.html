Discover the Impact of "The Yellow Wallpaper" by Charlotte Perkins Gilman

The author of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is Charlotte Perkins Gilman, an influential American feminist writer. Published in 1892, this short story explores themes of mental health, gender roles, and the oppression of women in the 19th century. Gilman's work remains relevant today, sparking discussions about women's rights and mental health awareness.
