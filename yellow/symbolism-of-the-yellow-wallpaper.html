Understanding the Symbolism of the Yellow Wallpaper

The yellow wallpaper in Charlotte Perkins Gilman's story symbolizes the oppression of women in the 19th century. It represents the protagonist's struggle against confinement and her quest for freedom. The wallpaper's chaotic pattern mirrors her declining mental state and the societal constraints that entrap her. Ultimately, it becomes a symbol of her rebellion.
