A Haunted Room: Exploring the Setting of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The setting of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is a secluded, ancestral mansion where the narrator is confined by her husband for rest cure therapy. The room, adorned with faded yellow wallpaper, symbolizes her entrapment and mental decline. The isolation and oppressive environment intensify her struggle against societal norms and her deteriorating mental state.
