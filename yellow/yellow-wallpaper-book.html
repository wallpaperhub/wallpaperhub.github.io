Discover the Significance of the Yellow Wallpaper

The "Yellow Wallpaper" is a short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman that explores themes of mental illness, gender roles, and societal oppression. Through the journal of an unnamed woman, locked away in a room with yellow wallpaper, it delves into the descent into madness and the quest for self-identity amidst restrictive norms.
