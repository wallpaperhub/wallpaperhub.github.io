Point of View in The Yellow Wallpaper: Analysis

The short story "The Yellow Wallpaper" is told from the first-person point of view. The narrator's personal experiences and thoughts are conveyed through her journal entries, providing readers with intimate access to her deteriorating mental state. This perspective enhances the themes of confinement and identity as she battles with societal expectations.
