Exploring the Themes of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The poem "The Yellow Wallpaper" explores themes of mental illness, confinement, and a struggle for self-identity. Through vivid imagery, the protagonist’s descent into madness unfolds, symbolized by the chaotic yellow wallpaper. As she unravels, the poem critiques societal norms and sheds light on women's oppression, evoking empathy and reflection.
