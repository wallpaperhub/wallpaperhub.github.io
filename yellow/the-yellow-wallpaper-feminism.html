Feminism and Madness in The Yellow Wallpaper

The Yellow Wallpaper explores the theme of feminism through the lens of mental illness and societal repression. The protagonist's descent into madness mirrors the struggles faced by women, highlighting the oppressive forces of patriarchy. The story serves as a powerful critique of gender roles, urging liberation and self-expression.
