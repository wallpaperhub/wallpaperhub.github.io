Exploring the Themes in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" explores themes of mental illness, gender roles, and societal oppression. The story illustrates the descent into madness of a woman confined by her husband, representing a critique of 19th-century attitudes towards women's mental health. The wallpaper symbolizes her struggle and desire for freedom as she battles against repression.
