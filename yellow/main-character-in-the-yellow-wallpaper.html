Exploring the Main Character in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The main character in "The Yellow Wallpaper" is an unnamed woman whose descent into madness reflects her struggle against societal oppression. Confined to a room for "rest," she becomes obsessed with the wallpaper's pattern, projecting her frustrations and eventual identity crisis onto it, symbolizing her desire for freedom and self-expression.
