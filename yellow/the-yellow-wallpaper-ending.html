The Symbolic Ending of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The ending of "The Yellow Wallpaper" reveals a profound transformation in the narrator as she finally breaks free from the confines of her oppressive environment and societal expectations. By tearing down the wallpaper, she symbolizes her rebellion against the patriarchal restraints and reclaiming her identity, even as it hints at her descent into madness.
