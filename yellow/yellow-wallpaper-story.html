Unraveling the Mystery of the Yellow Wallpaper

The "Yellow Wallpaper" is a haunting short story by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, exploring themes of mental health and societal oppression. As a woman confined to a room by her husband for "rest cure," she becomes obsessed with the wallpaper's patterns, reflecting her descent into madness and illuminating the struggles against patriarchal control.
