"Unlocking Mary: A Deep Dive into The Yellow Wallpaper"

"The Yellow Wallpaper" by Charlotte Perkins Gilman follows Mary, a woman trapped in a stifling environment due to the "rest cure." As her mental health deteriorates, she becomes obsessed with the wallpaper in her room, symbolizing her struggle against oppression and a quest for identity. The story is a powerful critique of 19th-century gender roles.
