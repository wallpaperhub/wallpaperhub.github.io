Understanding the Ending of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

The ending of "The Yellow Wallpaper" is a striking culmination of the protagonist's descent into madness. As she tears down the wallpaper, she identifies with the trapped woman she perceives within its patterns. This act of rebellion symbolizes her struggle against oppressive societal norms and ultimately challenges the barriers of mental health.
