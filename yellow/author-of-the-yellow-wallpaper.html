Discover the Author of "The Yellow Wallpaper"

Charlotte Perkins Gilman is the author of "The Yellow Wallpaper," a seminal work published in 1892. This feminist short story explores themes of mental illness, societal oppression, and the struggle for identity through the descent of its protagonist into madness, symbolized by the room's eerie wallpaper.
