The Portrait of a Woman in "The Yellow Wallpaper"

In "The Yellow Wallpaper," the protagonist is a woman confined to a room as part of a prescribed treatment for her mental health. As she descends into madness, her fixation on the wallpaper symbolizes her struggle against oppression and her search for identity. Her characterization reflects the themes of feminism and mental illness in a patriarchal society.
