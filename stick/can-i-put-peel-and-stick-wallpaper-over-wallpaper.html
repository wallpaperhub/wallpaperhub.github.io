Can You Apply Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Existing Wallpaper?

Yes, you can put peel and stick wallpaper over existing wallpaper, provided the underlying surface is smooth, clean, and free of any texture. Stripping away the old wallpaper may offer a better finish, but if you're in a pinch, ensure the old wallpaper is firmly adhered to avoid bubbling or peeling of the new layer.
