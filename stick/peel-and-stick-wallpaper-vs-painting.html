Peel and Stick Wallpaper vs. Painting: Which Is Best?

Peel and stick wallpaper offers a versatile, easy-to-apply alternative to traditional painting. Unlike paint, which requires prep work and drying time, peel and stick is an instant transformation, removable, and often reusable. Choose patterns and textures for a unique look or go solid for a clean finish without the mess of painting.
