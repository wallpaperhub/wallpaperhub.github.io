Apply Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Old Wallpaper

Yes, you can put peel and stick wallpaper over old wallpaper, provided the existing wallpaper is smooth and well-adhered. Make sure to clean the surface to remove dust and debris, and check for any peeling edges. If the old wallpaper is textured or damaged, it's best to remove it first for a better finish.
