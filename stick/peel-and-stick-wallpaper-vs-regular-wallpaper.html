Peel and Stick Wallpaper vs Regular Wallpaper: Which is Best?

Peel and stick wallpaper offers a hassle-free alternative to traditional wallpaper. It's easy to install, removable, and often reusable, making it ideal for renters or temporary decor. On the other hand, regular wallpaper may require paste and tools for installation, but it often provides a more luxurious finish and durability.
