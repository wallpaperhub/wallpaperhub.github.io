Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Really Last?

Peel and stick wallpaper can last anywhere from 5 to 15 years, depending on the quality of the material, the application process, and the environment it's placed in. Proper surface preparation and careful installation are key for durability. It's a great option for renters or those looking to update decor easily.
