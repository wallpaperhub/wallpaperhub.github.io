Can You Use Peel and Stick Wallpaper in a Bathroom?

Peel and stick wallpaper can be a fantastic option for bathrooms, as it is water-resistant and easy to apply. Ensure the surface is smooth and clean for the best adherence. With various stylish designs available, you can create a fresh look without the commitment of traditional wallpaper. Just be mindful of high humidity areas!
