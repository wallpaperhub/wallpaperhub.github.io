Using Glue on Peel and Stick Wallpaper: A Guide

Yes, you can use glue on peel and stick wallpaper, but it's not always necessary. Most peel and stick products come with an adhesive backing designed for easy installation and repositioning. If edges lift or if you're applying in high moisture areas, a wallpaper adhesive can provide extra security and durability.
