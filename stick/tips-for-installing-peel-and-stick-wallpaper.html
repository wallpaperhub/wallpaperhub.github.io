Easy Tips for Installing Peel and Stick Wallpaper

To install peel and stick wallpaper, start with a clean, dry surface. Measure and cut your wallpaper to fit, leaving some extra at the edges. Peel back the backing slowly while aligning the wallpaper, smoothing out air bubbles with a flat tool. Trim excess at the top and sides for a polished finish.
