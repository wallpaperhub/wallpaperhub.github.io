Transform Your Space with Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Applying peel and stick wallpaper is a simple and effective way to transform your space without the hassle of traditional wallpaper. Before starting, ensure your walls are clean and smooth. Measure and cut the wallpaper to fit your surfaces, then peel back the backing and stick. Smooth out any bubbles with a plastic smoother. Enjoy your new look!
