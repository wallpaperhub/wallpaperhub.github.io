Applying Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Existing Wallpaper

Yes, you can put peel and stick wallpaper over existing wallpaper, provided the underlying wallpaper is smooth, clean, and free from damage. Ensure proper adhesion by testing a small section first. Keep in mind that textured or heavily patterned wallpapers might affect the final look of your new peel and stick design.
