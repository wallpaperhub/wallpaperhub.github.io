Tips for Smooth Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation

Peel and stick wallpaper can be a fantastic choice for easy home decor updates, but wrinkles could be a concern during application. To achieve a smooth finish, start by cleaning the wall and using a wallpaper smoothing tool to gently push out air bubbles. If wrinkles persist, use a heat source like a hairdryer on low to help relax the material.
