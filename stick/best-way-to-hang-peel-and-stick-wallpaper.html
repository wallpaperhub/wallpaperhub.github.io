How to Hang Peel and Stick Wallpaper Like a Pro

To hang peel and stick wallpaper effectively, start with a clean, smooth wall surface. Measure and cut the wallpaper to fit, allowing for a little extra at the edges. Peel back a corner, stick it to the wall, and slowly peel off more backing while smoothing it down with a plastic smoother. Use a utility knife for trimming edges neatly.
