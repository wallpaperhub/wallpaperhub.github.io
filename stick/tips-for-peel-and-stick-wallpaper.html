Essential Tips for Peel and Stick Wallpaper Success

When applying peel and stick wallpaper, start with a clean, smooth surface for better adhesion. Measure and cut carefully, leaving extra for adjustment. Begin from the top and work downwards, smoothing out air bubbles with a smoothing tool or credit card. If bubbles persist, pierce them with a small pin to release air. Finally, trim excess edges for a neat finish.
