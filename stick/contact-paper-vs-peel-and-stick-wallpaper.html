Contact Paper vs. Peel and Stick Wallpaper: What's Best for You?

Contact paper and peel and stick wallpaper are popular DIY solutions for home decor. Both are adhesive options for transforming spaces, but they serve different purposes. Contact paper is great for smaller projects like lining shelves or covering furniture, while peel and stick wallpaper offers bold patterns for larger wall areas. Easy to remove and install, both allow for quick updates without the commitment of traditional wallpaper.
