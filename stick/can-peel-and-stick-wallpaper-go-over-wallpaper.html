Can You Apply Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Existing Wallpaper?

Peel and stick wallpaper can be applied over existing wallpaper, but it's essential to ensure the surface is clean, smooth, and free of any texture. If your wallpaper has a glossy finish, consider lightly sanding it for better adhesion. This method is a quick way to refresh a space without the hassle of removing old wallpaper.
