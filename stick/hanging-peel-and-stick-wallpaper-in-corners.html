How to Hang Peel and Stick Wallpaper in Corners

Hanging peel and stick wallpaper in corners can be a game-changer for any room. Start by measuring the corner area and cutting the wallpaper accurately, ensuring you leave a bit of extra for adjustments. Start from the center of the corner and work your way out, smoothing as you go to avoid bubbles. Use a straight edge for clean lines, and don't hesitate to trim excess paper for a seamless finish.
