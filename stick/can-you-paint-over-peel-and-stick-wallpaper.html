Painting Over Peel and Stick Wallpaper: Tips and Tricks

Yes, you can paint over peel and stick wallpaper! However, it's crucial to prepare the surface properly. Clean the wallpaper and apply a primer designed for vinyl surfaces to ensure better paint adhesion. Once primed, you can use high-quality latex paint to achieve the desired look. Keep in mind that texture and pattern may show through, so layering may be necessary.
