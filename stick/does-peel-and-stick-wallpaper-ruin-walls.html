Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Damage Your Walls?

Peel and stick wallpaper can be a great rental-friendly design option, but concerns about wall damage are common. Generally, it won't ruin walls if applied properly and removed carefully. However, surfaces should be clean, dry, and smooth. Always check manufacturer guidelines for specific recommendations to avoid peeling paint or residue.
