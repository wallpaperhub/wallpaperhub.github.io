Peel and Stick Wallpaper vs. Contact Paper: Which is Better?

Peel and stick wallpaper and contact paper are popular home decor options, but they serve different purposes. Peel and stick wallpaper offers a decorative solution for walls, providing a wide range of designs and textures. In contrast, contact paper is often used for surfaces like shelves and countertops, offering a protective layer. Both are easy to apply and remove, making them ideal for renters or DIY enthusiasts.
