Is Peel and Stick Wallpaper Ideal for Bathrooms?

Peel and stick wallpaper can be a great choice for bathrooms. It's water-resistant, easy to apply, and removable, making it perfect for quick updates. Additionally, it’s available in various designs to match your decor. However, ensure the surface is clean and dry for the best adhesion to prevent peeling in humid conditions.
