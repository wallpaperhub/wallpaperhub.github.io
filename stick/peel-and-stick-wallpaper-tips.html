Top Tips for Peel and Stick Wallpaper Success

When applying peel and stick wallpaper, clean the surface thoroughly for better adhesion. Start at the top and work your way down, smoothing out air bubbles with a flat tool. Measure and cut accurately, allowing a bit of overlap. If you need to reposition, do so quickly before the adhesive sets. Enjoy a fresh, stylish look!
