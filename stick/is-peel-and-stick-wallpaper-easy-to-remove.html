Easy Removal: Peel and Stick Wallpaper Guide

Peel and stick wallpaper is generally easy to remove, making it a popular choice for renters and DIY enthusiasts. To remove, simply peel it off slowly from one corner. If any adhesive residue remains, it can usually be cleaned with warm soapy water. Just ensure the underlying wall surface is in good condition to avoid damage.
