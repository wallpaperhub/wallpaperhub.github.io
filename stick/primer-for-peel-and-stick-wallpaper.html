Top Tips for Using Primer with Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Primer is essential for peel and stick wallpaper, ensuring a smooth and lasting application. It creates a barrier between the wall and wallpaper, enhancing adhesion and preventing peeling. Properly primed surfaces also allow for easier removal in the future. For best results, select a primer suitable for your wall type and follow the manufacturer's instructions.
