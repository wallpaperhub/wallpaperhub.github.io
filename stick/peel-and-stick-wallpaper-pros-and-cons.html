Peel and Stick Wallpaper: Pros and Cons Explained

Peel and stick wallpaper offers easy installation and removal, making it great for renters. It's cost-effective and comes in a variety of designs. However, it may not adhere well on textured surfaces and can peel over time, particularly in humid areas. Consider both durability and aesthetics before choosing.
