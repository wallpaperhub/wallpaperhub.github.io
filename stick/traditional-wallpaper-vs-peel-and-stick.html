Traditional Wallpaper vs. Peel and Stick: Which is Best?

Traditional wallpaper requires adhesive, professional installation, and can be time-consuming to remove. In contrast, peel and stick wallpaper is user-friendly, allowing for easy application and removal without damaging walls. It’s perfect for renters and DIY enthusiasts seeking flexibility in design, offering a wide range of patterns and colors.
