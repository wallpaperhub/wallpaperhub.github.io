Peel and Stick Wallpaper vs. Contact Paper: What’s the Difference?

Peel and stick wallpaper and contact paper are similar but serve different purposes. Peel and stick wallpaper is primarily designed for décor, providing a stylish look with removable adhesive. Contact paper, on the other hand, is more utilitarian, often used for covering surfaces like countertops and shelves. Both are easy to apply and remove.
