Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Remove Paint?

Peel and stick wallpaper is generally safe for painted surfaces and won't damage the paint when applied or removed properly. However, older or low-quality paint jobs may be more susceptible to peeling. To minimize risks, ensure your walls are clean, smooth, and paint is well-adhered before applying wallpaper.
