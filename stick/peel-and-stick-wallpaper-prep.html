Essential Guide to Peel and Stick Wallpaper Prep

Before applying peel and stick wallpaper, ensure your walls are clean, dry, and smooth. Remove any old wallpaper, fill in holes, and sand rough patches. Use a level to draw guide lines for accurate alignment. Measure and cut panels carefully, allowing for pattern matching. Prepping properly leads to a flawless finish!
