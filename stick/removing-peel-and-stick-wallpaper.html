Easy Guide to Removing Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Removing peel and stick wallpaper is a straightforward process. Start by gently lifting one corner with a utility knife. Slowly peel off the wallpaper, taking care to pull at a 45-degree angle to avoid tearing. For stubborn residues, use a heat gun or steamer to soften the adhesive. Finish by cleaning the wall with soap and water.
