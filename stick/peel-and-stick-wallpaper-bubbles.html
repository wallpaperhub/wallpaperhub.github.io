Fixing Bubbles in Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Peel and stick wallpaper is a fantastic way to upgrade your space, but bubbles can ruin the look. To fix this, gently lift the wallpaper and smooth it out with a squeegee. Use a pin to pop any stubborn bubbles and press the area to ensure adhesion. With proper care, your walls can look flawless and stylish.
