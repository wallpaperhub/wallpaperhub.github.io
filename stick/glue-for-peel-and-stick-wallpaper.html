Essential Guide to Glue for Peel and Stick Wallpaper

When applying peel and stick wallpaper, you may wonder about the need for glue. While most products are designed for easy application without additional adhesive, certain situations call for extra security. If walls are textured or wallpaper is lifting, using a removable wallpaper adhesive can ensure a strong bond and enhance durability.
