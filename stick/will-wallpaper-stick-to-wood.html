Will Wallpaper Adhere to Wood Surfaces? Discover the Best Tips!

Wallpaper can stick to wood surfaces, but the success depends on various factors. The wood should be clean, smooth, and dry for optimal adhesion. Consider using a primer or wallpaper adhesive suitable for porous surfaces to enhance stickiness. Additionally, test a small area first to ensure a proper bond.
