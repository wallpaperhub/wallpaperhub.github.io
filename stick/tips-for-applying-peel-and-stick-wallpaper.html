Top Tips for Perfect Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation

Applying peel and stick wallpaper can transform your space easily! Start with clean, smooth walls for better adhesion. Measure and cut wallpaper strips accurately, leaving a bit extra for adjustments. Use a straight edge to align patterns and smooth it out with a squeegee. Take your time, and you'll achieve a professional look!
