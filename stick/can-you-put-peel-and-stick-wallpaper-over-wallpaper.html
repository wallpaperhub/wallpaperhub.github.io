"Can You Apply Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Existing Wallpaper?"

Yes, you can put peel and stick wallpaper over existing wallpaper, but it's essential to consider a few factors. Ensure that the underlying wallpaper is smooth and well-adhered to the wall surface. Any texture or peeling can affect the stickiness and durability of the new wallpaper. Test a small section first to achieve the best results.
