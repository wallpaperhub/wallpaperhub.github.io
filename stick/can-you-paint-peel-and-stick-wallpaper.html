Painting Peel and Stick Wallpaper: Tips & Tricks

Yes, you can paint peel and stick wallpaper, but there are some crucial steps to ensure a successful outcome. First, select a high-quality paint and prepare the surface by cleaning it. Use a primer for better adhesion, and apply thin coats to prevent peeling. Always test a small area first!
