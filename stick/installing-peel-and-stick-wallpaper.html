Easy Guide to Installing Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Installing peel and stick wallpaper is an easy and affordable way to transform your space without the mess of traditional wallpaper. Start by measuring and cutting the wallpaper to fit your walls. Clean the surface thoroughly, then peel the backing and stick it, smoothing out air bubbles along the way for a seamless look. Enjoy your new decor!
