How to Fix Bubbling in Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Peel and stick wallpaper can bubble due to improper application, moisture, or wall imperfections. To prevent bubbling, ensure the surface is clean and dry before applying. If bubbles appear, try to smooth them out with a flat tool, or use a pin to release trapped air. Proper installation is key to a flawless finish.
