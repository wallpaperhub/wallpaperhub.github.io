Wallpaper vs. Peel and Stick: Which is Right for You?

When choosing between traditional wallpaper and peel-and-stick options, consider installation, durability, and design. Traditional wallpaper requires glue and skill, while peel-and-stick is user-friendly and removable. Peel-and-stick offers flexibility for renters or those who frequently update decor, while wallpaper often gives a more permanent, sophisticated look.
