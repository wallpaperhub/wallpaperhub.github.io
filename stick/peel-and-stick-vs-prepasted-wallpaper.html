Peel and Stick vs. Prepasted Wallpaper: Which to Choose?

Peel and stick wallpaper is a convenient choice for those who want an easy, mess-free installation. Simply peel off the backing and apply to the wall. In contrast, prepasted wallpaper requires being wet to activate the adhesive before hanging. Both options have their pros, but peel and stick is ideal for renters and quick updates.
