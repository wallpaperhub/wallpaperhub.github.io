Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Damage Paint?

Peel and stick wallpaper is designed to be removable, making it a popular choice for renters and DIY enthusiasts. Generally, it won't damage painted surfaces if removed properly. However, factors like paint quality and wall texture can affect results. Always test a small section first to ensure safe removal.
