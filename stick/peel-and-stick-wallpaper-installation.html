Easy Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation Tips

Peel and stick wallpaper installation is a simple, DIY-friendly process that transforms any room. Start by cleaning the wall surface to ensure proper adhesion. Measure and cut the wallpaper to fit your wall. Peel off the backing, align it carefully, and smooth it down using a plastic smoother to eliminate bubbles. Enjoy your refreshed space!
