How to Fix Peeling Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Peel and stick wallpaper can sometimes come off due to inadequate surface preparation, humidity, or poor adhesive quality. To resolve this, ensure your walls are clean and dry before application. If your wallpaper is peeling, reposition it or use additional adhesive to secure it. Regular maintenance can prolong its lifespan and keep your space looking fresh.
