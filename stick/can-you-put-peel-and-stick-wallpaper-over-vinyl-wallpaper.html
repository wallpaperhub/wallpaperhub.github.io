Can You Apply Peel and Stick Wallpaper Over Vinyl?

Yes, you can put peel and stick wallpaper over vinyl wallpaper, but it's essential to ensure proper adhesion. Clean the vinyl surface thoroughly to remove dust and grease, then apply the peel and stick wallpaper. Ensure no bubbles or wrinkles form during installation for a smooth finish. Test a small area first if you're unsure.
