Can Wallpaper Stick to Plywood? Tips & Tricks

Yes, wallpaper can stick to plywood, but proper preparation is key. Make sure the surface is clean, smooth, and free of dust. Using a primer designed for wood can improve adhesion. Opt for a high-quality wallpaper adhesive to ensure a lasting bond. Always test a small area first to confirm compatibility.
