Troubleshooting Wallpaper: Why It Won’t Stick

If your wallpaper won’t stick, it could be due to surface preparation, moisture, or improper adhesive use. Ensure the walls are clean, dry, and primed before application. Remove any old coverings and ensure the room is well-ventilated. Using the right paste or adhesive will also improve adhesion.
