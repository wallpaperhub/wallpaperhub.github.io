Non-Woven Wallpaper vs. Peel and Stick: Which is Best?

Non-woven wallpaper offers durability and breathability, making it a favorite for long-term home décor. In contrast, peel and stick wallpaper provides easy application and removal, ideal for renters or temporary updates. Both options add style, but your choice depends on your commitment and desired look.
