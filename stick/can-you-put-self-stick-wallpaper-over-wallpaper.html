Layering Self-Stick Wallpaper: Can You Apply Over Existing Wallpaper?

Yes, you can put self-stick wallpaper over existing wallpaper, but it’s important to ensure the surfaces are smooth, clean, and dry. If the original wallpaper is peeling or textured, this may affect the adherence and finish of the new wallpaper. For best results, consider removing the old wallpaper or using a primer.
