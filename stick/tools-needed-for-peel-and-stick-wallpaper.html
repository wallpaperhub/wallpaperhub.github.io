Essential Tools for Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation

To install peel and stick wallpaper successfully, ensure you have the right tools: a clean cloth for prep, a level to ensure straight placement, a utility knife for trimming edges, a smoothing tool to eliminate bubbles, and a ruler or measuring tape for accurate cuts. These essentials will make your project seamless!
