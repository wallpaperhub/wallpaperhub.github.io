Easy Guide to Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation

To apply peel and stick wallpaper, start by ensuring your wall is clean, smooth, and dry. Measure and cut the wallpaper to fit your desired area. Peel off the backing and align the wallpaper with your wall, smoothing it out from the center to the edges to avoid bubbles. Trim any excess at the top and bottom. Enjoy your new look!
