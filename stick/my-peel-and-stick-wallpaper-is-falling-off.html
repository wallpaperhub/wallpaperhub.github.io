How to Fix Falling Peel and Stick Wallpaper

If your peel and stick wallpaper is falling off, there are a few steps you can take to fix the issue. First, ensure the surface is clean and dry before reapplying. Consider using wallpaper adhesive for extra hold. If your wallpaper has been exposed to moisture, that could be the culprit. Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for best results.
