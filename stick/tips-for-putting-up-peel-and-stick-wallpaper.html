Essential Tips for Applying Peel and Stick Wallpaper

To successfully apply peel and stick wallpaper, start by preparing a clean, smooth surface. Measure and cut the wallpaper to fit your space, leaving a little extra at the edges. Peel the backing, and apply gently, smoothing out air bubbles with a credit card. Work from the center outward for best results. Trim excess edges for a polished finish.
