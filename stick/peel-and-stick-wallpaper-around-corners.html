Mastering Peel and Stick Wallpaper Around Corners

Peel and stick wallpaper is an excellent solution for adding flair to your space, even around corners. Start by measuring your corner carefully and trimming the wallpaper to fit. Gently peel off the backing and press it into place, smoothing out any bubbles. For a polished look, use a seam roller to ensure a seamless finish.
