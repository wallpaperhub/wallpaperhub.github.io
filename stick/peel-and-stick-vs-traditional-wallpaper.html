Peel and Stick vs. Traditional Wallpaper: Which is Better?

Peel and stick wallpaper offers a user-friendly option for home decor—easy to apply and remove without damaging walls, perfect for renters. Traditional wallpaper, while more durable and elegant, often requires glue and professional installation. Choose peel and stick for a quick refresh or traditional for long-lasting beauty.
