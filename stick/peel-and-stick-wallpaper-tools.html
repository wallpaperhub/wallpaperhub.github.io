Top Tools for Peel and Stick Wallpaper Installation

Peel and stick wallpaper tools are essential for a smooth, hassle-free application. Key tools include a straightedge and utility knife for precise cuts, a smoothing tool to eliminate bubbles, and a measuring tape for accurate measurements. A level helps ensure your wallpaper is straight, while a clean cloth aids in surface preparation for perfect adhesion.
