Peel and Stick Wallpaper vs. Paste: Which is Best?

Peel and stick wallpaper offers an easy, mess-free application, perfect for renters and DIYers. It allows for quick updates without glue or paste. In contrast, traditional paste wallpaper usually provides longevity and durability but requires more preparation and tools. Choose based on your need for permanence versus convenience.
