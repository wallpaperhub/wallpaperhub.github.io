Can You Use Peel and Stick Wallpaper in a Shower? Find Out!

Peel and stick wallpaper can add charm to bathrooms, but it's not ideal for use in a shower. The high humidity and water exposure can cause the adhesive to weaken, leading to peeling and damage. If you want to create a unique look, consider using it in dry areas instead, such as accent walls or behind sinks.
