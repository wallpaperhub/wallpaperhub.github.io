Can Peel and Stick Wallpaper Adhere to Wood Surfaces?

Peel and stick wallpaper can adhere to wood surfaces, but proper preparation is key. Ensure the wood is clean, dry, and smooth to maximize adhesive effectiveness. For rough or textured woods, consider sanding the surface beforehand. While it offers a temporary solution and ease of removal, the longevity of adhesion may vary based on wood type and conditions.
