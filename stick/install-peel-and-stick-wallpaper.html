Quick Guide to Installing Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Installing peel and stick wallpaper is an easy and transformative DIY project. Start by preparing your wall by cleaning and smoothing it. Cut the wallpaper into manageable strips and peel off the backing. Align the top edge and press down, smoothing out bubbles as you go. Finally, trim any excess, and enjoy your new look!
