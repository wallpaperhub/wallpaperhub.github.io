How to Fix Bubbles in Peel and Stick Wallpaper

Bubbles in peel and stick wallpaper can be a common issue, resulting from improper application or air pockets trapped under the surface. To fix it, gently lift the wallpaper, smooth out the bubbles with a squeegee or a flat edge, and reapply. Ensuring a clean, dry surface before installation helps prevent this problem.
