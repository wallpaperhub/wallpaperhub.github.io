Spoonflower Peel and Stick Wallpaper: Easy Installation Guide

To install Spoonflower peel and stick wallpaper, start by ensuring your walls are clean and dry. Measure and cut the wallpaper to size. Peel back the backing a few inches at a time, starting from a corner, and press it against the wall. Smooth out any bubbles with a flat tool. Trim excess if needed. Enjoy your new look!
