Effortless Guide to Installing Peel and Stick Wallpaper

To install peel and stick wallpaper, start by measuring the area and cutting the wallpaper to size. Clean the wall surface thoroughly to ensure good adhesion. Begin applying the wallpaper from the top, smoothing down to eliminate air bubbles. Use a utility knife for precise cuts around outlets. Enjoy your new look!
