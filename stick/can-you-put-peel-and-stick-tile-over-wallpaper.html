Installing Peel and Stick Tile Over Wallpaper: What You Need to Know

Yes, you can put peel and stick tile over wallpaper, but it's crucial to ensure the wallpaper is smooth, clean, and firmly adhered to the wall. Any bumps or loose edges may lead to an uneven surface or tiles peeling off. For the best results, consider removing the wallpaper to achieve a flat base.
