Prepasted vs. Peel and Stick Wallpaper: Which Is Best?

Prepasted wallpaper is pre-coated with adhesive, requiring only water for activation, making it easier to apply. On the other hand, peel and stick wallpaper features a self-adhesive backing that can be removed and repositioned easily without water. Both options offer unique benefits for quick and stylish room transformations.
