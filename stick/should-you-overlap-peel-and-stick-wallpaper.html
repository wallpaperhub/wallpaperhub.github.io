Should You Overlap Peel and Stick Wallpaper? Expert Tips

Overlapping peel and stick wallpaper can lead to unsightly seams and uneven textures. It's generally advisable to apply wallpaper edge-to-edge for a seamless look. Proper alignment is important to avoid bubbles and ensure a professional finish. For best results, consider pre-trimming edges before installation.
