Does Peel and Stick Wallpaper Damage Walls? Find Out!

Peel and stick wallpaper is generally safe for walls and can be removed without causing damage. However, its impact may depend on wall texture and paint type. On smooth, sealed surfaces, it usually adheres well and removes cleanly. It's best to test a small area first, especially if the paint is old or vulnerable.
