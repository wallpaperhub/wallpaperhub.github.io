Find Your Current Wallpaper Location in Windows 10

In Windows 10, your current wallpaper is typically located in the C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Themes folder. You can also find it in the Settings app under Personalization > Background. For downloaded or custom wallpapers, be sure to save them in an easily accessible folder for quick changes.
