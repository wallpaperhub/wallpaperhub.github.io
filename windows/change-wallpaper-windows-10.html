How to Change Wallpaper in Windows 10: A Quick Guide

Changing your wallpaper in Windows 10 is easy! Right-click on your desktop and select "Personalize." In the Settings menu, choose "Background" to upload an image or select a preset. You can adjust the positioning and choose between a picture, solid color, or slideshow. Refresh your desktop with a new look in just a few clicks!
