Fixing Your Windows Wallpaper Black Screen Issue

Having a Windows wallpaper that frequently goes black can be frustrating. This issue may arise due to display settings, power-saving features, or problematic software. To fix it, check your personalization settings, update your graphics drivers, and disable any potential power-saving modes that might override your wallpaper preferences.
