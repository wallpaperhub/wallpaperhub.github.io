Discover the Origins of Windows XP's Bliss Wallpaper

Windows XP's iconic default wallpaper, known as "Bliss," was captured by photographer Charles O'Rear in 1996. It features a lush green hillside under a vibrant blue sky, located in Sonoma County, California. The image has become synonymous with nostalgia for the operating system, representing simplicity and serenity in technology.
