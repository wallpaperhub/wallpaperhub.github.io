Where to Find Your Windows 10 Current Wallpaper

In Windows 10, the current wallpaper is stored in the "C:\Users\\AppData\Roaming\Microsoft\Windows\Themes" directory. The wallpaper files are typically in JPG format. You can easily change your desktop background by right-clicking on the desktop, selecting "Personalize," and browsing to the desired image location.
