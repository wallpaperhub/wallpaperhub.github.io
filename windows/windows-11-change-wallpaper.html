How to Change Your Wallpaper in Windows 11 Easily

Changing the wallpaper on Windows 11 is a simple process that personalizes your desktop experience. Right-click on the desktop and select "Personalize." From there, click on "Background," where you can choose a picture, solid color, or slideshow. Browse your files, select your favorite image, and enjoy a refreshed look!
