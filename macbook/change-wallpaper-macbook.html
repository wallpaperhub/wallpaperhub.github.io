How to Change Your MacBook Wallpaper in Minutes

Changing the wallpaper on your MacBook is a simple way to personalize your workspace. To do this, navigate to System Preferences, then select "Desktop & Screen Saver." From there, you can choose from Apple's default images or upload your own. Enjoy a fresh look every time you open your laptop!
