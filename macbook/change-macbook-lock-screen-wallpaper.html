How to Change Your MacBook Lock Screen Wallpaper Easily

Changing your MacBook lock screen wallpaper is quick and easy! To do this, go to System Preferences, click on "Desktop & Screen Saver," then select the "Screen Saver" tab. Choose your favorite image from the options available or upload a new one. Enjoy a fresh look every time your Mac locks!
