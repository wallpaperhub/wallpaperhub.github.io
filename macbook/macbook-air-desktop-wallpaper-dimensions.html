Perfect Desktop Wallpaper Dimensions for MacBook Air

When choosing desktop wallpaper for a MacBook Air, it's crucial to consider the display dimensions. The MacBook Air typically features a resolution of 1440 x 900 pixels for the 13-inch model. High-resolution images that fit these dimensions will ensure your wallpaper looks crisp and enhances the visual appeal of your desktop. Use a ratio of 16:10 for optimal results.
