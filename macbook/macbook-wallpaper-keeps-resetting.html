Stop Your MacBook Wallpaper from Resetting: Quick Fixes

Are you frustrated because your MacBook wallpaper keeps resetting? This issue can stem from system updates, iCloud settings, or user preferences. Try turning off wallpaper syncing in iCloud, ensuring that your desktop picture settings are set correctly, and checking if any applications are interfering with your preferences.
