Easy Steps to Change Your MacBook Wallpaper

Changing your MacBook wallpaper is a simple way to personalize your device. To do this, right-click on your desktop and select "Change Desktop Background." You can choose from Apple's default images or your own photos. Experiment with different styles to refresh your workspace and inspire creativity!
