Best Paint Options for Covering Wallpaper

When selecting paint to cover wallpaper, choose a high-quality primer first to ensure good adhesion. Then, opt for a matte or eggshell finish latex paint for even coverage. Darker wallpapers may require a tinted primer. Always test a small area first to ensure the final color meets your expectations and covers effectively.
