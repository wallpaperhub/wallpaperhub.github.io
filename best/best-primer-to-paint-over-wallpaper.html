The Best Primer for Painting Over Wallpaper: A Quick Guide

When painting over wallpaper, using the right primer is key to achieving a smooth finish. Look for a high-quality, stain-blocking primer like Zinsser Gardz or KILZ Adhesion. These primers adhere well to the wallpaper, prevent peeling, and ensure that the paint color stays vibrant. Always test in a small area first!
