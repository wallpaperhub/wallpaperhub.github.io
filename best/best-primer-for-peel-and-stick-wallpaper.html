Top Primer Picks for Peel and Stick Wallpaper

When selecting the best primer for peel and stick wallpaper, choose a high-quality, water-based primer for enhanced adhesion. Ensure the surface is clean and smooth before application. Primers like Zinsser Bulls Eye 1-2-3 or Benjamin Moore’s Fresh Start are excellent choices. They create a durable base that helps wallpaper stick better and reduces the risk of peeling.
