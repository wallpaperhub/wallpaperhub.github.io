Expert Tips for Painting Over Wallpaper

Painting over wallpaper can refresh your space without the hassle of removal. Start by cleaning the wallpaper and repairing any damage. Use a primer to ensure adhesion and prevent old patterns from showing through. Choose high-quality paint and apply at least two coats for a smooth finish. Remember to use a paint suitable for the type of wallpaper.
