Top Primer Choices for Painting Over Wallpaper

When painting over wallpaper, using the best primer is essential for achieving a smooth finish. Look for a high-quality, stain-blocking primer, such as a latex-based or oil-based option. These primers adhere well to the wallpaper, prevent peeling, and ensure the final coat of paint appears vibrant and even. Proper application will enhance durability and appearance.
