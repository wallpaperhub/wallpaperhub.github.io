Top Tips for Easily Removing Wallpaper Borders

To remove a wallpaper border effectively, start by using a utility knife to carefully cut the edge where the border meets the wall. Then, apply a wallpaper removal solution or a mixture of warm water and fabric softener to loosen the adhesive. Allow it to sit for a few minutes, gently peeling the border away. For stubborn areas, repeat the process.
