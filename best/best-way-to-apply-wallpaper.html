Top Tips for Perfect Wallpaper Application

To apply wallpaper effectively, start by prepping the walls: clean them thoroughly and patch any holes. Choose the right adhesive based on the wallpaper type—paste the wall or the paper as needed. Measure and cut the wallpaper accurately, then use a smoother to eliminate air bubbles during application. Lastly, trim excess at the edges for a neat finish.
