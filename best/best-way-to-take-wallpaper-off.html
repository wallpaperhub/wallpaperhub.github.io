The Easiest Way to Remove Wallpaper

To effectively remove wallpaper, start by gathering essential tools: a wallpaper scraper, sponge, bucket, and wallpaper removal solution. Begin by scoring the wallpaper with a utility knife, then apply the solution to break down the adhesive. Let it soak, then use the scraper to gently peel off the wallpaper, working from the top down. Clean any residue with water afterward.
