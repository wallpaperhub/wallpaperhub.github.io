How to Effectively Cover Wallpaper: Tips and Tricks

To cover wallpaper effectively, start by cleaning the surface to remove dust and grease. Use a primer to ensure better adhesion of the new layer. Consider using peel-and-stick wallpaper for easy application or opt for fabric or paint for a modern touch. Measure and cut accurately for a smooth finish.
