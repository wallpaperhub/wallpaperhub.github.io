The Ultimate Guide to Wallpaper Removal

To effectively remove wallpaper, start by scoring the surface with a utility knife. Then, apply a wallpaper removal solution or a mix of vinegar and water using a sponge. Allow it to soak for a few minutes to loosen the glue. Gently peel the wallpaper off, and clean any residue with warm, soapy water for a smooth finish.
