Effective Wallpaper Cleaning Tips

To clean wallpaper effectively, start by dusting with a soft cloth or vacuum with a brush attachment. For stains, mix warm water with a gentle soap. Use a sponge to dab the solution on the stain, avoiding excess moisture. For tougher stains, consider using a specialized wallpaper cleaner, rinsing the sponge frequently. Always test a small area first!
