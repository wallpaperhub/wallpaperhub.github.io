Easy Guide to Applying Peel and Stick Wallpaper

To apply peel and stick wallpaper effectively, start by preparing the walls: clean and ensure they’re smooth. Measure and cut the wallpaper into manageable sections. Peel the backing slowly, aligning the top edge with your guide. Use a smoothing tool to eliminate bubbles, working from center to edges. Trim excess for a perfect finish.
