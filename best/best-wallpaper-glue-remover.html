Top Wallpaper Glue Remover: Quick & Effective Solutions

When tackling stubborn wallpaper glue, the best remover options include vinegar, commercial adhesive removers, and a DIY mixture of water and baking soda. Vinegar effectively breaks down adhesive, while commercial products can target tough spots. Always test on a small area first to avoid damage to your walls!
