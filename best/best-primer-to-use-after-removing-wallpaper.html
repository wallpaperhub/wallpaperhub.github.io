Best Primer Options After Wallpaper Removal

After removing wallpaper, it's essential to use a high-quality primer for a smooth painting surface. Look for a stain-blocking primer, such as Zinsser BIN or KILZ, to effectively cover any residual adhesive and prevent stains. These primers not only promote better paint adhesion but also ensure a uniform finish for your walls.
