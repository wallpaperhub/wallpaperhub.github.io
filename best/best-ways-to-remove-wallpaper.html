Top Methods to Easily Remove Wallpaper

Your best options for wallpaper removal include using a wallpaper scraper or steamer for easy peeling. For stubborn areas, a mixture of water and vinegar can loosen the adhesive. Always start at the seams, work in sections, and use a damp cloth to remove residue afterward. Protect your floors and keep the area ventilated!
