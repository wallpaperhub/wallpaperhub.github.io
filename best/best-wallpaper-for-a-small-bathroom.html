Top Wallpaper Ideas for Small Bathrooms

For a small bathroom, consider light-colored wallpapers to create an illusion of space. Patterns like vertical stripes or small florals can add charm without overwhelming. Waterproof options, such as vinyl wallpaper, are practical. Opt for glossy finishes to reflect light, making the area feel brighter and larger.
