Top Wallpaper Removal Techniques for a Smooth Finish

When it comes to removing wallpaper, steam removal is often the best method. Using a wallpaper steamer, you can soften the adhesive, making it easier to peel off without damaging the walls. For stubborn areas, a mixture of warm water and vinegar can also work wonders. Remember to test a small patch first for best results!
