Transform Your Space: Wallpaper Tips for a Bigger Room

To make a room appear bigger, consider wallpaper with light colors and subtle patterns. Large, vertical stripes can create an illusion of height, while reflective or glossy finishes can enhance light. Opt for wallpapers that blend seamlessly with the trim and ceiling to eliminate visual boundaries, expanding the space visually.
