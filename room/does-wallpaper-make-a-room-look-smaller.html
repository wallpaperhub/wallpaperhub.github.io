Does Wallpaper Make a Room Look Smaller? Discover the Truth!

Wallpaper can indeed make a room appear smaller, especially if it's a dark color or a busy pattern. Lighter colors and designs that reflect light can give the illusion of more space. The scale of patterns plays a role too; smaller patterns can make a room feel cozier, while larger designs can engulf the space. Always consider your room's natural light.
