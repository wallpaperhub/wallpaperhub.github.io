The Yellow Wallpaper Room: A Journey into Madness

The yellow wallpaper room symbolizes oppression, mental illness, and the struggle for autonomy. In Charlotte Perkins Gilman's short story, a woman confined to this room due to her "nervous condition" descends into madness. The wallpaper, intricately patterned and unsettling, mirrors her deteriorating mental state and highlights the suffocating societal norms of the time.
