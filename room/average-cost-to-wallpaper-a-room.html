Average Cost to Wallpaper a Room: A Quick Guide

The average cost to wallpaper a room typically ranges from $400 to $1,200, depending on the room size, wallpaper type, and labor costs. DIY projects can save money, but professional installation ensures a seamless finish. Always factor in additional expenses like materials and removal of old wallpaper for an accurate estimate.
