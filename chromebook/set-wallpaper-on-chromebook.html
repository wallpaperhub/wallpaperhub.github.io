Set Wallpaper on Chromebook: Easy Guide

To set wallpaper on your Chromebook, right-click on the desktop and select "Set wallpaper." You'll be taken to the wallpaper settings, where you can choose from pre-installed images or upload your own from local storage. Simply click on your preferred image to apply it, and enjoy a personalized desktop experience!
