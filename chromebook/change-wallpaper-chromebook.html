How to Change Wallpaper on Your Chromebook: A Quick Guide

Changing the wallpaper on your Chromebook is simple and enhances your personalization. Right-click on the desktop, select "Set wallpaper," and choose from the default options or upload your own image. Customize it further by selecting the "Wallpapers" app for more choices. Keep your background fresh!
