Perfect MacBook Pro Wallpaper Dimensions Guide

When designing a wallpaper for your MacBook Pro, consider the screen dimensions for optimal fit and quality. The common resolutions are 2560 x 1600 for the 13-inch model and 2880 x 1800 for the 15-inch version. Creating wallpapers at these dimensions ensures your images look crisp without any distortion or stretching.
