Revamp Your Bathroom: Wallpaper Over Tile

Transforming your bathroom with wallpaper over tile is an innovative way to refresh your space. Choose moisture-resistant wallpaper to ensure durability in high humidity. Proper surface preparation is key: clean and smooth the tiles before application. This DIY project not only enhances aesthetics but also adds a personal touch to your bathroom decor.
