Using Wallpaper in Bathrooms: A Stylish Guide

Yes, you can use wallpaper in a bathroom, but it's essential to choose moisture-resistant types. Vinyl wallpapers are ideal because they're easy to clean and won't warp in humid conditions. Consider using wallpaper on an accent wall or above the wainscoting to add personality while ensuring longevity and style in your bathroom decor.
