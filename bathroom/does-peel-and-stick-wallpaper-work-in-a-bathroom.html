Peel and Stick Wallpaper in Bathrooms: What You Need to Know

Peel and stick wallpaper can work in a bathroom, but it’s essential to choose a high-quality, moisture-resistant option. Bathrooms have higher humidity levels, which can cause the adhesive to weaken or the wallpaper to peel. Proper surface preparation and venting can enhance durability and longevity, making it a stylish, temporary decor choice.
