Can You Use Peel and Stick Wallpaper in Bathrooms?

Yes, peel and stick wallpaper can be used in a bathroom, provided it's made from moisture-resistant materials. It's a great way to add personality to the space. Consider using it on accent walls or instead of traditional tiles for easy installation and removal. Always check the manufacturer's guidelines for optimal durability.
